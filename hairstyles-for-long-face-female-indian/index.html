Hairstyles For Long Face Female Indian Tips Tricks And Ideas

Written by Petter Sep 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are a long-faced Indian woman looking for the perfect hairstyle, you have come to the right place. Long faces can be tricky to style, but with the right cut and some styling tricks, you can rock any look you desire. In this post, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for long face female Indian, including how to achieve them, tips for maintenance, and more.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles for Long Face Female Indian
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's essential to know your face shape. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back from your face and examine your features in the mirror. Pay attention to the shape of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead is wider than your jawline and chin, you have an oval face shape. If your jawline is narrow, and your forehead and cheekbones are wide, you have a heart-shaped face. If your jawline is square, and your cheekbones and forehead are equally wide, you have a square face shape. Finally, if your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.

Hairstyles for Long Face Female Indian

Now that you know your face shape let's look at some of the best hairstyles for long face female Indian.

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a perfect hairstyle for long face female Indian. It adds volume and texture to your hair, making your face appear shorter. A bob that falls just below your chin will give you a more balanced look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften your face and make it appear shorter. These bangs should be cut diagonally and styled to one side of your face. They work well with most hairstyles, including long, wavy hair.

3. Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers work well with long hair and can help balance out a long face. These layers should be cut around your face, starting at your chin or cheekbones. They add volume and dimension to your hair, making your face appear shorter.

4. Short Pixie Cut

If you are looking for a bold and daring look, a short pixie cut might be perfect for you. This hairstyle adds volume to the top of your head, making your face appear shorter. It's also easy to maintain and style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you have chosen your hairstyle let's look at how to style it.

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. If you are using a curling iron or a straightener, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first.
  4. Style your hair according to your chosen hairstyle.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use styling products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hairline and make your face appear longer.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make your face appear longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too high, such as high ponytails or buns, as they can elongate your face even more.

Can I wear a middle part if I have a long face?

Yes, you can wear a middle part if you have a long face. However, it's best to pair it with face-framing layers or side-swept bangs to balance out your features.

Do I need to use styling products every day?

No, you don't need to use styling products every day. However, using a light styling product, such as a mousse or a texturizing spray, can help add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Female Indian

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping your scalp of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

With these tips, tricks, and hairstyle ideas, you can rock any look you desire. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style, and always take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
