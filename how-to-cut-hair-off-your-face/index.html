How To Cut Hair Off Your Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 08, 2023 · 7 min read

Cutting hair off your face can be a daunting task, especially if you don't have any experience with cutting hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a fresh, new look without having to go to a salon. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to cut hair off your face. Here's a table of contents to help you navigate:

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • How to Cut Hair Off Your Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Hair Off Your Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Hair Off Your Face

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather a few tools:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips or hair ties to section off your hair
  • Mirror

How to Cut Hair Off Your Face

Follow these steps to cut your hair off your face:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it. This will make it easier to work with.
  2. Section off the hair that you want to cut off your face. Use clips or hair ties to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.
  3. Comb the hair that you want to cut straight down. This will ensure that you get an even cut.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers where you want to cut it. Remember that hair will bounce up a little after it's cut, so hold it a little longer than you want it to be.
  5. Cut the hair in a straight line. Make sure you're cutting perpendicular to the hair, not at an angle. You can always go back and trim more if you need to.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 with the rest of the hair that you want to cut off your face.
  7. Check your work in the mirror. Make sure that both sides are even.
  8. If you need to make any adjustments, repeat steps 4-7 until you're happy with the length.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Hair Off Your Face

Here are a few tips to keep in mind while cutting your hair:

  • Start with a little bit of hair and work your way up. It's easier to cut more hair than to try to fix a mistake from cutting too much.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Don't rush. Take your time and make sure you're cutting straight.
  • If you have curly hair, cut your hair while it's dry. Curly hair can shrink up when it dries, making it difficult to cut evenly.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair when it's wet?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry because wet hair can stretch and you might end up cutting it too short. If you have curly hair, cut it when it's dry to avoid shrinkage.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Can I cut my own hair if I have layers?

If you have layers, it can be more difficult to cut your own hair. It's best to go to a salon to get a professional cut.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair Off Your Face

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Now that you know how to cut hair off your face, you can save money and achieve a fresh, new look without having to go to a salon. Just remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and follow our tips for a successful cut.
