Short Shaggy Haircuts For Fine Wavy Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 23, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have fine wavy hair, then you know how challenging it can be to find a haircut that complements your hair type. Fortunately, short shaggy haircuts are a great option for those with fine wavy hair. In this post, we’ll discuss everything you need to know about short shaggy haircuts for fine wavy hair, including how to get the perfect cut, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

Short shaggy haircuts are characterized by their choppy layers and textured ends. They’re perfect for those with fine wavy hair because they add volume and movement to the hair. The layers are cut in a way that creates a messy, undone look, which is great for those who want a low-maintenance style.

How to get the perfect cut

The key to getting the perfect short shaggy haircut for fine wavy hair is to find a stylist who has experience working with this hair type. When you’re at the salon, be sure to communicate your hair goals and ask for their advice on what will work best for your hair type. It’s also important to bring in pictures of styles you like so that your stylist can get a better idea of what you’re looking for.

Step by step

The following is a step-by-step guide to getting a short shaggy haircut for fine wavy hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into 4 parts.
  3. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up, cutting the hair in small sections.
  4. Use texturizing scissors to create choppy layers.
  5. Continue cutting until you’ve achieved your desired length and texture.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short shaggy haircut for fine wavy hair:

  • Make sure your stylist is experienced with this hair type.
  • Communicate your goals and bring in pictures of styles you like.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Opt for a shorter cut if you have thin or fine hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short shaggy haircuts for fine wavy hair:

Q: Will a short shaggy haircut work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a short shaggy haircut can work for all face shapes. However, the length and layers may need to be adjusted to flatter your face shape.

Q: How often should I get my short shaggy haircut trimmed?

A: It’s recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your haircut.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight with a short shaggy haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair straight with a short shaggy haircut. However, the texture and layers may not be as prominent as when you wear your hair wavy.

Hair Care

To keep your short shaggy haircut looking its best, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of your haircut.

In conclusion, a short shaggy haircut is a great option for those with fine wavy hair. By finding a stylist who has experience working with this hair type, communicating your goals, and following the tips and hair care advice in this post, you can achieve a low-maintenance, stylish haircut that complements your hair type.
