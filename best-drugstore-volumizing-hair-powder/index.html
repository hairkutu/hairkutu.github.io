The Best Drugstore Volumizing Hair Powder For Fuller Thicker Hair

Written by Frank Apr 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of limp, lifeless hair that lacks volume and thickness? Look no further than the best drugstore volumizing hair powder. Not only is it affordable and easily accessible, but it can also give your hair the boost it needs for a fuller, more voluminous look. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about volumizing hair powder, including how to use it, tips for getting the best results, and frequently asked questions.

Description

Volumizing hair powder is a styling product that helps create volume and texture in hair. It's usually a fine, powdery substance that you sprinkle onto your roots and massage into your scalp. The powder works by absorbing excess oil and moisture, which can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. As a result, your hair appears fuller and thicker with added body and bounce.

How to Use Volumizing Hair Powder

Using volumizing hair powder is easy and straightforward. Here's how:

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Shake the volumizing hair powder onto your roots, focusing on the areas where you want more volume.
  3. Massage the powder into your scalp using your fingers or a brush. This will help distribute the product and create lift.
  4. Style your hair as desired, using a brush or your fingers to create texture and volume.

Tips for Getting the Best Results

To get the most out of your volumizing hair powder, try these tips:

  • Use the right amount of product. Too much powder can make your hair look dull and powdery, while too little won't give you the volume you want. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.
  • Focus on your roots. The roots are where you want the most lift and volume, so concentrate the powder there rather than on the ends of your hair.
  • Use a brush. A brush can help distribute the powder evenly and create more lift than using your fingers alone.
  • Try different application techniques. You can apply the powder directly to your roots or sprinkle it onto your brush and then apply it to your hair. Experiment to see what works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best drugstore volumizing hair powder?

Some of the best drugstore volumizing hair powders include Batiste Volumizing Hair Powder, Not Your Mother's Plump for Joy Body Building Dry Shampoo, and Bumble and Bumble Pret-a-Powder Tres Invisible Nourishing Dry Shampoo.

Can I use volumizing hair powder on wet hair?

No, it's best to use volumizing hair powder on dry hair. Wet hair can dilute the powder and make it less effective.

How often should I use volumizing hair powder?

You can use volumizing hair powder as often as needed, but be careful not to overuse it. Too much powder can make your hair look dull and powdery, so use it sparingly and only when you need an extra boost of volume.

Hair Care

While volumizing hair powder can be a great way to add volume and thickness to your hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove excess oil and dirt.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to minimize damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With the best drugstore volumizing hair powder and proper hair care, you can achieve fuller, thicker hair that looks and feels amazing. Try it out today and see the difference for yourself!
