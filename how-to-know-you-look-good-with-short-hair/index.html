How To Know You Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Luffy Apr 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering cutting your hair short but unsure if it will suit you? Short hair can be a big change, but it can also be a refreshing and stylish look. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to know if you look good with short hair. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in determining whether short hair will suit you is to consider your face shape. Short hair generally looks best on those with oval, heart-shaped, and square faces. If you have a round face, a short haircut can still work as long as it is layered and styled in a way that flatters your face shape.

Next, think about your hair texture. Short hair can work for those with straight, wavy, or curly hair. However, it is important to consider the maintenance required for your hair type. If you have curly hair, for example, you may need to spend more time styling it to avoid frizz.

You can also try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to see how you would look with short hair. This can give you a good idea of whether the style will suit you before making the big chop.

Step by Step

If you are still unsure whether short hair will suit you, follow these steps:

  1. Find inspiration from celebrities or online hair galleries.
  2. Book a consultation with a trusted hairstylist who can offer advice and guidance.
  3. Consider trying a shorter haircut gradually by going for a lob (long bob) or a shoulder-length cut before going for a pixie cut or a bob.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques to see what works best for you.
  5. Take good care of your hair by using quality products and getting regular trims to maintain the shape and style.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when deciding if short hair will suit you:

  • Think about your personal style and whether a short haircut will complement it.
  • Consider your lifestyle and whether a shorter style will be practical for you.
  • Think about the season and whether a shorter haircut will be comfortable and suitable for the weather.
  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair:

Will short hair make me look older?

No, short hair can actually make you look younger and more refreshed. It all depends on the cut and styling.

Can short hair work for all face shapes?

Short hair generally looks best on those with oval, heart-shaped, and square faces. Those with round faces can still pull off a short haircut if it is layered and styled in a way that flatters their face shape.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape and style. It is also important to use quality products that are suited for your hair type. If you have curly hair, for example, use products that are designed to enhance and define your curls. Additionally, protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great look for many people. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can determine whether a shorter haircut will suit you. Remember to consult with a trusted hairstylist and take good care of your hair to maintain a healthy and stylish look.
