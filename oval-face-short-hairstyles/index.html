Oval Face Short Hairstyles How To Choose The Perfect Style For You

Written by Frank Jul 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. Whether you prefer short hair, long hair, or something in between, there's a style out there that will flatter your features.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for an Oval Face

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for an oval face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in the type of hairstyle that will look best on you. If you have thick, coarse hair, you may want to choose a style that will help to thin it out. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, you may want to choose a style that will add volume and texture.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still requires some upkeep. If you're someone who doesn't have a lot of time to spend styling your hair, you may want to choose a low-maintenance style that can be easily tousled with some product.

Consider Your Face Shape

While oval faces are versatile, there are still some styles that will flatter your features more than others. A good rule of thumb is to choose a style that will balance out the proportions of your face. For example, if you have a long face, you may want to choose a style with some volume on top to help shorten the appearance of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for an Oval Face

Now that you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your oval face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition

Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair. Use a conditioner that is appropriate for your hair texture.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair. Avoid rubbing it vigorously, as this can cause damage.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your hair. This could be a mousse, pomade, or styling cream, depending on your hair texture and the style you're going for.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Focus on the roots to create volume.

Step 5: Finish with Product

Once your hair is dry, apply a finishing product to help hold the style in place. This could be a hairspray, wax, or gel.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for an Oval Face

Short hair can be a lot of fun, but it does require some maintenance. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair looking great:

Get Regular Trims

To keep your style looking fresh, it's important to get regular trims. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Use the Right Products

Using the right products for your hair texture and style can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Talk to your stylist about the best products to use for your specific style.

Protect Your Hair

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to help prevent damage. You should also avoid pulling your hair too tight, as this can cause breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best short hairstyles for oval faces?

Some of the best short hairstyles for oval faces include pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and layered cuts.

Can oval faces pull off bangs?

Yes, oval faces can pull off bangs. However, it's important to choose a style that will flatter your features. Side-swept bangs are a good choice for oval faces.

How often should I wash my short hair?

How often you wash your short hair will depend on your hair texture and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go a few days between washes.

Conclusion: Rock Your Oval Face Short Hairstyle with Confidence

With so many short hairstyles to choose from, there's no reason why you can't find the perfect style for your oval face. Whether you're looking for something edgy or something more classic, there's a style out there that will flatter your features and give you the confidence to rock your new look.
