How To Put Hair Back Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a guy who's looking for a new hairstyle? Putting your hair back is a great option that can make you look more put together and professional. In this article, we'll show you how to put hair back guys, step by step. We'll also provide some tips, answer frequently asked questions, and give you some hair care advice. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Hair Back Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Put Hair Back Guys

There are a few different ways you can put your hair back as a guy. Here are some options:

  • Ponytail
  • Man bun
  • Half-up, half-down
  • Slicked back

Each of these styles has its own unique look and feel. Choose the one that best fits your personal style and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to put your hair back in each of these styles:

Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather all of your hair in your hands at the back of your head.
  3. Secure your hair with a hair tie or elastic band.
  4. Adjust the ponytail to your desired height and tightness.

Man Bun

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather all of your hair in your hands at the top of your head.
  3. Twist your hair into a bun shape.
  4. Secure the bun with a hair tie or elastic band.
  5. Adjust the bun to your desired tightness and shape.

Half-Up, Half-Down

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Section off the top half of your hair, from your temples to the back of your head.
  3. Secure the top section with a hair tie or elastic band.
  4. Leave the bottom section of your hair loose.

Slicked Back

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair.
  3. Using a comb, slick your hair back away from your face.
  4. Adjust the style to your desired shape and hold.

Tips for Putting Hair Back Guys

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when putting your hair back:

  • Use a hair tie or elastic band that matches your hair color.
  • Don't pull your hair back too tightly, as this can cause tension headaches.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that looks best on you.
  • Use a hair gel or pomade that provides a strong hold, especially for slicked back styles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about putting hair back for guys:

Can guys with short hair put their hair back?

Yes, guys with short hair can put their hair back using a slicked back style or by using a hair accessory like a headband.

How do I prevent my hair from falling out of a ponytail?

Use a hair tie or elastic band that's tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.

Can I put my hair back if I have curly hair?

Yes, guys with curly hair can put their hair back using any of the styles mentioned in this article. However, you may need to use a stronger hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care Advice

Putting your hair back can be a great way to switch up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Don't put your hair back too often, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you now know how to put hair back guys. Experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair, and you'll have a great look that lasts.
