How To Style Medium Length Hair Updos A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Apr 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Medium length hair can be styled in a variety of ways, and updos are one of the most popular. Whether you have a special occasion or just want a new look, updos are a great way to switch up your hairstyle. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to style medium length hair updos, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Medium Length Hair Updos
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair Updos
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Medium Length Hair Updos

How to Style Medium Length Hair Updos

Medium length hair is perfect for updos because it’s long enough to be styled, but not so long that it’s difficult to manage. Here are the basic steps to styling medium length hair updos:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

It’s important to start with clean, dry hair when styling updos. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be more difficult to style and hold the shape.

2. Choose your updo

There are many different types of updos to choose from, including buns, braids, and twists. Choose the style that best suits your hair type and the occasion.

3. Gather your hair

Gather your hair into a ponytail at the nape of your neck or high on your head, depending on the style you’ve chosen.

4. Secure with pins or elastic

Use bobby pins or an elastic band to secure your hair in place. Make sure to tuck in any loose ends so that your updo looks neat and polished.

5. Finish with hairspray

To keep your updo in place all day, finish with a spritz of hairspray.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling a classic braided updo for medium length hair:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Shampoo and condition your hair as normal, then blow dry it until it’s completely dry.

2. Part your hair

Create a deep side part on one side of your head.

3. Create a French braid

Starting at the part, create a French braid by gathering small sections of hair and crossing them over each other. As you braid, add more hair to each section until you reach the nape of your neck.

4. Secure with pins

Use bobby pins to secure the end of the braid to your head.

5. Twist and pin

Take the remaining hair and twist it into a bun at the nape of your neck. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

6. Finish with hairspray

Spritz your updo with hairspray to keep it in place all day.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair Updos

Here are some tips to help you style medium length hair updos:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and grip to your hair before styling.
  • Don’t be afraid to use hair extensions to add length and fullness to your updo.
  • Experiment with different braids and twists to create unique updos.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.

FAQs

Can I style a medium length hair updo on my own?

Yes, with practice and patience, anyone can style a medium length hair updo on their own.

Do I need special tools to style a medium length hair updo?

No, you don’t need any special tools to style a medium length hair updo. Bobby pins, elastics, and hairspray are all you need.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair Updos

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or other styling products, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can style beautiful and unique updos for medium length hair. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
