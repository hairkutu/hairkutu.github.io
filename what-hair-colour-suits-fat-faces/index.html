What Hair Colour Suits Fat Faces Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hair colour that suits your face shape? If you have a round or chubby face, it can be challenging to choose the right hair colour that complements your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this blog post, we'll be discussing what hair colours suit fat faces and how you can achieve that perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Choose the Right Hair Colour
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Choosing the right hair colour is essential when it comes to enhancing your facial features. If you have a round or chubby face, you should opt for hair colours that create an illusion of length and slimness. Some of the best hair colours for fat faces include warm and natural shades such as caramel, honey blonde, and chocolate brown. These colours add depth and dimension to your hair, making your face appear slimmer and longer.

How To Choose the Right Hair Colour

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you have a round or chubby face. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hair colour:

  • Consider your skin tone. Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining the right hair colour for you. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm hair colours such as caramel and honey blonde. If you have cool undertones, go for cool hair colours such as ash blonde and platinum.
  • Think about your natural hair colour. If you want a natural look, choose a hair colour that is only a few shades lighter or darker than your natural hair colour.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hair colour that requires minimal upkeep.
  • Consult a hairstylist. If you're unsure about which hair colour suits you best, consult a hairstylist who can guide you in the right direction.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect hair colour for your fat face:

  1. Decide on the hair colour you want to go for.
  2. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and natural hair colour.
  3. Consult a hairstylist to determine the best hair dye for your hair type.
  4. Prepare your hair for dyeing by washing it with a clarifying shampoo.
  5. Apply the hair dye according to the instructions on the box.
  6. Leave the dye on for the recommended time.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  8. Apply a colour-safe conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes.
  9. Rinse your hair with cool water again.
  10. Dry and style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you maintain your hair colour and keep your fat face looking slim and radiant:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools, which can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Invest in a good-quality dry shampoo to keep your hair looking fresh between washes.
  • Consider getting a regular trim to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair colours for fat faces:

  • Can I dye my hair if I have a chubby face? Yes, you can dye your hair regardless of your face shape. Just make sure to choose a hair colour that complements your features.
  • What hair colours suit fat faces? Warm and natural shades such as caramel, honey blonde, and chocolate brown are some of the best hair colours for fat faces.
  • How do I choose the right hair colour for my skin tone? If you have warm undertones, opt for warm hair colours such as caramel and honey blonde. If you have cool undertones, go for cool hair colours such as ash blonde and platinum.

Hair Care Tips

Maintaining your hair colour can be challenging, but with the right hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and radiant. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your hair colour:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair colour from fading.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause your hair colour to fade faster.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb when detangling your hair to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight, as this can cause your hair colour to fade.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Now that you know what hair colour suits fat faces, it's time to get creative and find the perfect hair colour for you. Remember to consider your skin tone, natural hair colour, and lifestyle when choosing a hair colour, and don't be afraid to consult a hairstylist for guidance. With the right hair colour and hair care tips, you can rock any hairstyle with confidence!
