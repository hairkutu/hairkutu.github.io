How To Cut Long Curly Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man with long, curly hair, you may be looking for ways to keep your locks looking their best. One of the most important things you can do is learn how to cut your hair properly. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cutting long curly men's hair, from start to finish. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced barber, these tips will help you achieve the perfect cut every time.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Long Curly Men's Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Long Curly Men's Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Long Curly Men's Hair

How to Cut Long Curly Men's Hair

Before you get started, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to invest in a set of clippers if you plan on doing any trimming or fading.

When it comes to cutting curly hair, it's important to remember that less is more. Start with a conservative cut and work your way up if needed. Curly hair tends to shrink as it dries, so keep this in mind when cutting.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help to remove any product buildup and make your hair easier to work with.
  2. Towel dry your hair and comb it out. You want to remove any tangles or knots before you start cutting.
  3. Using your comb, separate your hair into sections. Start at the top and work your way down.
  4. Take a small section of hair and twist it. This will help you see any uneven ends that need to be trimmed.
  5. Using your scissors, trim the ends of the twisted hair. Start with a small cut and work your way up if needed.
  6. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way down the head.
  7. If you want to do any trimming or fading, use your clippers to create a clean line.
  8. Once you've finished cutting your hair, use your spray bottle to mist your hair with water. Scrunch your curls to encourage them to form.
  9. Style your hair as desired using your favorite product.

Tips for Cutting Long Curly Men's Hair

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when cutting long curly men's hair:

  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Curly hair tends to shrink as it dries, so it's best to cut it when it's dry.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Start with a conservative cut and work your way up if needed.
  • Be patient. Cutting curly hair can take some time, but the end result is worth it.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting long curly men's hair:

Do I need to use clippers?

No, clippers are not necessary for cutting long curly men's hair. However, they can be helpful if you want to create a clean line or do any trimming or fading.

How often should I cut my hair?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how you want it to look. As a general rule, you should aim to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Hair Care for Long Curly Men's Hair

Now that you know how to cut your long curly men's hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons.

By following these tips, you can keep your long curly men's hair looking its best. Happy cutting!
