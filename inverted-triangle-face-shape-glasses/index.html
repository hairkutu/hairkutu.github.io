Inverted Triangle Face Shape Glasses The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect pair of glasses for your face shape? If you have an inverted triangle face shape, you may find it challenging to find glasses that flatter your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll give you the lowdown on inverted triangle face shape glasses, including how to find the right frames, styling tips, and hair care advice.

What is an Inverted Triangle Face Shape?

Before we dive into glasses, let's first define what an inverted triangle face shape is. This face shape is characterized by a broad forehead and narrow chin. The cheekbones may also be prominent. Essentially, the face shape resembles an upside-down triangle, hence the name.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, here's a quick way to find out:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a lipstick or washable marker and trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the shape you've drawn.

If the shape resembles an upside-down triangle, then you have an inverted triangle face shape.

What Kind of Glasses Suit Inverted Triangle Face Shape?

When choosing glasses for an inverted triangle face shape, the goal is to balance out the proportions of the face. Here are some tips to keep in mind when shopping for frames:

  • Look for frames that are wider at the bottom than at the top. This will help balance out the broad forehead.
  • Avoid frames that are too top-heavy or have embellishments on the top. This will only draw attention to the forehead.
  • Frames with a curved or round edge can soften the angles of the face.
  • Choose frames that sit low on the nose to balance out the forehead.
  • Frames with a narrow bridge can help elongate the nose and balance out the face.

Styling Tips for Inverted Triangle Face Shape Glasses

Now that you know what kind of frames to look for, let's talk about how to style them:

  • Experiment with bold colors and prints to add some personality to your look.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, as this will only make the forehead appear larger.
  • Try hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as a chin-length bob or side-swept bangs. This will balance out the proportions of the face.
  • Accessorize with statement earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from the forehead.

Hair Care Tips for Inverted Triangle Face Shape

Finally, let's talk about how to take care of your hair when you have an inverted triangle face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tight, as this will only emphasize the broad forehead.
  • Try adding volume to the crown of the head to balance out the proportions of the face.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering one for your face shape.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add movement and texture to the hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about inverted triangle face shape glasses:

What kind of glasses should I avoid if I have an inverted triangle face shape?

Avoid glasses that are too top-heavy, have embellishments on the top, or are too narrow at the bottom.

Can I wear oversized glasses if I have an inverted triangle face shape?

Yes, oversized glasses can work for an inverted triangle face shape, as long as they have a wider bottom and sit low on the nose.

What kind of hairstyles should I avoid if I have an inverted triangle face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, as this will only make the forehead appear larger.

In Conclusion

With the right glasses, styling, and hair care, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your inverted triangle face shape. Remember to look for frames that balance out the proportions of your face, experiment with bold colors and prints, and avoid hairstyles that add height to the forehead. With these tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect glasses for your face shape!
