How To Cut Women s Hair With Thinning Scissors

Written by Aprilia Aug 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting women's hair with thinning scissors is a great technique to create a seamless and natural look. When used correctly, thinning scissors can remove bulk and add texture to any hairstyle. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut women's hair with thinning scissors step by step.

Description

Thinning scissors are a type of haircutting scissors that have teeth on one or both blades. These teeth can vary in size and spacing, allowing hair to be removed in a controlled and precise manner. Thinning scissors are commonly used to thin out thick hair or create a textured look.

How to

Before using thinning scissors, it is essential to have the right tools and equipment. You will need:

  • Thinning scissors
  • Regular haircutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Spray bottle with water

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry the hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 2: Section the hair

Use a comb to section the hair into manageable sections. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the hair with regular scissors

Use regular haircutting scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. This will remove any bulk and create a base for the haircut.

Step 4: Use thinning scissors

Take the thinning scissors and hold them vertically. Use them to cut into the hair, making sure to only cut a small amount at a time. This will create a textured and natural look.

Step 5: Blend the hair

Use the regular scissors to blend the hair and remove any harsh lines. This will create a seamless and natural look.

Step 6: Repeat on each section

Repeat steps 3-5 on each section of hair until the entire head is complete.

Tips

  • Use the thinning scissors sparingly to avoid over-thinning the hair.
  • Make sure to only cut a small amount of hair at a time with the thinning scissors.
  • Use the regular scissors to blend the hair and create a seamless look.
  • Practice on a mannequin head before cutting a client's hair.

FAQ

What is the difference between thinning scissors and regular scissors?

Thinning scissors have teeth on one or both blades, which allows hair to be removed in a controlled and precise manner. Regular scissors do not have teeth and are used to cut hair to a specific length.

Can thinning scissors be used on all hair types?

Thinning scissors can be used on most hair types, but they are particularly useful for thick hair.

How often should thinning scissors be sharpened?

Thinning scissors should be sharpened every 6-12 months, depending on how often they are used.

Hair Care

After cutting the hair with thinning scissors, it is important to take care of the hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can help to keep the hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting women's hair with thinning scissors is a great technique to add texture and create a natural look. By following these steps and tips, you can master the art of using thinning scissors and create beautiful haircuts for your clients.
