How To Cut Hair With Scissors Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying for haircuts or don't have the time to go to a salon? Cutting your own hair with scissors can be a cost-effective and convenient solution. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to cut your hair with scissors yourself. We'll also share some tips and tricks to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions. So, let's get started!

How to

Step 1: Gather Supplies

The first step is to gather all the necessary supplies. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before cutting it. Wet hair is easier to cut, and you can see the natural fall of your hair.

Step 3: Part Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair in the middle. Bring the hair to the front and clip it to hold it in place.

Step 4: Trim the Ends

Start by trimming the ends of your hair. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair evenly, making sure to cut straight across. Repeat this process around your head, making sure to trim the same amount of hair from each section.

Step 5: Layer Your Hair

To layer your hair, take a small section of hair from the top of your head and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, making sure to cut more from the longer side. This will create layers in your hair. Repeat this process around your head, taking small sections of hair and cutting at an angle.

Step 6: Blend Your Hair

Once you have layered your hair, blend the layers by taking small sections of hair and cutting vertically into them. This will help to create a natural, blended look.

Step 7: Check Your Work

After you have finished cutting your hair, check your work in a mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is even and looks the way you want it to.

Tips

Here are some tips to make cutting your hair with scissors easier:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need to. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.
  • Use hair clips to hold your hair in place while you cut. This will make it easier to see what you're doing.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take a break if you need one.

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the length and style of your hair. On average, you should get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. If you're trying to grow your hair, you may be able to go longer between trims.

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, you should use hair cutting scissors to cut your hair. Regular scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve the look you want.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair with scissors can be a great way to save money and time on haircuts. However, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use hair masks and treatments to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great between haircuts.

Now that you know how to cut your hair with scissors yourself, you can save money and time on haircuts. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at cutting your own hair in no time!
