How To Cut Short Hair Straight At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short hair is an easy and low-maintenance hairstyle that can look chic and stylish. However, regular visits to the hair salon can be expensive and time-consuming. That's why learning how to cut short hair straight at home can be a game-changer for your budget and beauty routine. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut your hair straight at home with simple tools and techniques.

How to Cut Short Hair Straight at Home

Cutting your hair straight at home involves some preparation, patience, and precision. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need some essential tools, including:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle

Make sure your scissors are sharp and clean to avoid damaging your hair or giving yourself split ends.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's easier to cut your hair when it's clean and dry, so start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. Use a towel to remove excess water and let your hair air dry or blow-dry it until it's completely dry.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using a comb and clips. Start by parting your hair in the middle or on one side, and then create horizontal sections from the bottom up. Secure each section with clips to keep it out of the way while you cut the rest of your hair.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair Straight

Start cutting your hair straight by trimming the ends of each section. Hold the scissors at a slight angle and cut small sections at a time. Keep checking the length of your hair by using a mirror or by feeling it with your fingers. Make sure both sides are even and symmetrical.

Step 5: Blend Your Hair

After you've cut all the sections, blend your hair by combing it and checking for any uneven parts or chunks. Use your scissors to trim any excess hair or create layers if you want. Remember to cut less than you think you need, as you can always go back and trim more if necessary.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you've cut and blended your hair, it's time to style it. You can use a straightening iron, a curling iron, a blow dryer, or any other tool that suits your hair type and desired style. Apply some hair styling products, such as hair spray, mousse, or serum, to enhance your hair's texture and hold.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Straight at Home

Cutting your hair straight at home requires some skills and patience, but it can be a fun and rewarding experience. Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with small sections and cut less than you think you need.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head or ask someone to help you.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet or damp, as it may appear longer than it actually is.
  • Be patient and take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what suits you best.

FAQs About Cutting Short Hair Straight at Home

How often should I cut my short hair?

It depends on your hair growth rate and desired style. Generally, you should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Can I cut my hair straight without any experience?

Yes, you can. Cutting your hair straight at home requires some practice and patience, but it's doable with the right tools and techniques. Start with small sections and take your time to avoid making mistakes.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

Don't panic. If you make a mistake, you can always go back and fix it. Use your scissors to trim the excess hair or create a new shape. If you're unsure how to proceed, seek advice from a professional hairstylist.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Cutting your hair straight at home is just one part of your hair care routine. To keep your short hair healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat or chemicals on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and pollution.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to hydrate your hair and scalp.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut your short hair straight at home like a pro. Remember to be patient, take your time, and have fun experimenting with different styles and lengths. Happy cutting!
