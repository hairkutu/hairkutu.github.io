How To Make Curly Hair Straight For Guys Naturally

Written by Aprilia Sep 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Curly hair is a blessing and a curse – it looks great, but it can be difficult to manage. If you’re a guy with curly hair, you might be wondering how to make it straight. There are a lot of options out there, but most involve harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair. Luckily, there are also natural ways to straighten curly hair. In this article, we’ll go over how to make curly hair straight for guys naturally.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Curly Hair Straight for Guys Naturally
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Straightening Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how to make curly hair straight for guys naturally:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start with clean hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won’t weigh your hair down. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly.

2. Apply Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is great for hair – it moisturizes, conditions, and can even help straighten curly hair. Apply a small amount of coconut oil to your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair. Don’t pull or tug on your hair, as this can cause damage.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a hair dryer on the cool setting to dry your hair. Use a brush to help straighten your hair as you dry it.

5. Apply Aloe Vera Gel

Aloe vera gel is another natural ingredient that can help straighten curly hair. Apply a small amount of aloe vera gel to your hair, focusing on the ends.

6. Comb Your Hair Again

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair again. This will distribute the aloe vera gel evenly throughout your hair.

7. Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry completely. Don’t use a hair dryer or any other heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair.

8. Apply Argan Oil

Argan oil is a great natural ingredient for hair. Apply a small amount of argan oil to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help smooth and soften your hair.

9. Comb Your Hair One Last Time

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair one last time. This will ensure that your hair is smooth and straight.

10. Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to add some hold and texture.

Tips for Straightening Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for straightening curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your hair – don’t pull or tug on your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down.

FAQs

Can I use a straightening iron to straighten my curly hair?

While a straightening iron can be effective, it can also damage your hair over time. If you do use a straightening iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using it too often.

How often should I straighten my curly hair?

You should avoid straightening your hair too often, as this can damage your hair over time. Try to limit straightening to once a week or less.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a t-shirt or microfiber towel to gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking great – whether you decide to keep it curly or straighten it out.
