How To Cut Hair With Clippers Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to give yourself or someone else a fresh haircut, learning how to cut hair with clippers fade is a great skill to have. A fade is a popular haircut style that involves gradually cutting the hair shorter towards the bottom, resulting in a smooth transition from longer to shorter hair. In this article, we'll go over the steps you need to take to create a perfect fade with clippers.

Description

A fade is a type of haircut style that involves cutting the hair shorter towards the bottom, resulting in a smooth transition from longer to shorter hair. It is a great way to add some style to your hair while keeping it low maintenance. Clippers are a popular tool for creating fades because they allow for precise and consistent cutting.

How to Cut Hair with Clippers Fade

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, clipper guards in various sizes, a comb, scissors, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want for your fade. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to shorter guards until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin by using the longest guard to cut the hair on the top of your head. Work your way down to the sides and back, gradually switching to shorter guards as you go. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to cut it.

Step 4: Blend the Fade

The key to a good fade is blending the different lengths of hair together seamlessly. Use a shorter guard to blend the hair between the longer and shorter sections. Use a comb to lift the hair and the clippers to cut it.

Step 5: Define the Hairline

Use scissors to define the hairline around the ears and neck. This will give the fade a more polished look.

Step 6: Clean Up

Use the clippers without a guard to clean up any stray hairs around the hairline and sideburns.

Tips

  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair before cutting. This will make it easier to cut and prevent the hair from flying around.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure a smooth transition between lengths.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles.

FAQ

How long does a fade haircut last?

A fade haircut can last anywhere from 2 to 4 weeks before it starts to lose its shape.

Can you do a fade haircut at home?

Yes, you can do a fade haircut at home with the right tools and techniques.

What is the difference between a low fade and a high fade?

A low fade starts at the bottom of the hairline and gradually gets shorter towards the top. A high fade starts higher up on the head and gets shorter towards the top.

Hair Care

To maintain your fade haircut, it's important to keep it clean and hydrated. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims will also help keep the shape of the fade.
