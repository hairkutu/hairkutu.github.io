How To Wear A Fedora With Short Hair

Written by Robby Jul 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you wondering how to wear a fedora with short hair? Fedoras can be a stylish and versatile addition to any outfit, but it can be challenging to wear them with short hair. However, with the right styling and attitude, you can pull off this classic look. In this article, we’ll show you how to wear a fedora with short hair, step by step.

Description

A fedora is a type of hat with a wide brim and a creased crown. It is typically made of wool, felt, or straw and is often associated with classic Hollywood movies and the fashion of the 1920s and 1930s. Fedoras are a stylish and versatile accessory that can be worn with casual or formal outfits.

How to

Here’s how to wear a fedora with short hair:

Step 1: Choose the right fedora

The first step in wearing a fedora with short hair is to choose the right fedora. Look for a fedora that fits your head comfortably and complements your face shape. If you have a round face, choose a fedora with a wider brim to balance out your features. If you have a longer face, opt for a fedora with a shorter brim.

Step 2: Style your hair

Before putting on your fedora, style your hair in a way that complements the hat. Short hair can be styled in many ways, such as slicking it back, adding volume with mousse, or leaving it natural. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Step 3: Position the fedora

Place the fedora on your head at a slight angle, with the brim tilted downward over your forehead. The front of the hat should be about an inch above your eyebrows, and the back of the hat should rest on the crown of your head. Adjust the hat until it feels comfortable and secure.

Step 4: Own the look

Wearing a fedora with short hair requires confidence and attitude. Own the look and wear the hat with pride. Remember, fashion is about expressing yourself and having fun.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for wearing a fedora with short hair:

  • Choose a fedora in a color that complements your outfit.
  • Avoid wearing a fedora with a formal suit, as it is a more casual accessory.
  • Experiment with different styles of fedoras, such as fedoras with a wider brim or a different texture.
  • Accessorize with a scarf or pair of sunglasses for a complete look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a fedora with short hair:

Can I wear a fedora with very short hair?

Yes, you can wear a fedora with very short hair. Just make sure to choose a fedora that fits your head comfortably and complements your face shape. You may need to adjust the hat more frequently to ensure it stays in place.

Can I wear a fedora with curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a fedora with curly hair. Just make sure to style your hair in a way that complements the hat and adjust the hat accordingly. You may need to use more pins or clips to secure the hat in place.

Hair Care

When wearing a fedora with short hair, it’s important to take care of your hair. Wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it soft and healthy. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. If you’re going to be wearing a fedora for an extended period, use a dry shampoo to freshen up your hair.

Now that you know how to wear a fedora with short hair, it’s time to try out this classic look. Remember to choose the right fedora, style your hair, position the hat correctly, and own the look with confidence. With these tips, you’ll be rocking a fedora in no time.
