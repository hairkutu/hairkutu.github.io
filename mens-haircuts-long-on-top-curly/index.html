Mens Haircuts Long On Top Curly A Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by Eveline Mar 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man with curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the right haircut that suits your style. However, with the current trend of mens haircuts long on top curly, you are in luck! Long curly hair has become increasingly popular in recent years, with many men embracing their natural curls and flaunting them with pride. This guide will take you through the process of getting the perfect long haircut for curly hair, including how to style and care for your locks.

Description

The mens haircuts long on top curly look is all about having long hair on the top of the head, with shorter sides and back. The length on top can vary from a few inches to shoulder-length, depending on your preference. This style is perfect for men with thick, curly hair, as it allows them to showcase their natural texture while keeping the hair manageable and easy to style.

How to Get the Look

If you are ready to get the mens haircuts long on top curly style, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Find the Right Barber

Before you can get the perfect long haircut for curly hair, you need to find a barber who specializes in this type of cut. Look for a barber who has experience working with curly hair and can give you the advice you need to achieve the right look.

Step 2: Choose Your Length

The length of your hair on top will depend on your preference and your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the length shorter, while men with oval or square faces can go for a longer length.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

The sides and back of the hair should be cut short, usually with clippers. This will create a contrast with the longer hair on top and give you a more polished look.

Step 4: Style Your Curls

Once you have your long haircut for curly hair, it's time to style your curls. Use a curl-defining product to enhance your natural texture and create a more defined look. You can also use a diffuser on your hairdryer to help enhance the curls and reduce frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Haircut for Curly Hair

Now that you have your perfect long haircut for curly hair, it's essential to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry, so make sure you use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your locks hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. Try to wash your hair every two to three days, or as needed.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly will help keep it healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about mens haircuts long on top curly:

Q: Can I get the long on top curly look if I have straight hair?

A: The long on top curly look is designed for men with curly hair, so if you have straight hair, you may not be able to achieve the same look. However, you can still try a longer haircut and add some texture with a texturizing product.

Q: Do I need to use special products for my long curly hair?

A: Yes, it's essential to use products designed for curly hair, such as curl-defining products and moisturizing shampoos and conditioners. These products will help keep your curls healthy and defined.

Q: How often should I style my long curly hair?

A: It's up to you how often you style your hair, but keep in mind that over-styling can damage your hair. Try to limit heat styling and opt for air-drying whenever possible.

Hair Care for Mens Haircuts Long on Top Curly

Overall, mens haircuts long on top curly are a great way to showcase your natural curls and create a stylish, on-trend look. With the right barber, the right products, and proper maintenance, you can achieve the perfect long haircut for curly hair and look your best every day.
