Wedding Hairstyles For Mid Length Curly Hair How To Look Stunning On Your Big Day

Written by Eveline Aug 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your wedding day can be a daunting task, especially if you have mid-length curly hair. But don't worry, we've got you covered! Here, we'll share some beautiful and easy wedding hairstyles for mid-length curly hair that will make you look stunning on your big day. Plus, we'll provide some tips on how to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.

  • How to Choose the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle for Mid Length Curly Hair
  • Step by Step: Wedding Hairstyles for Mid Length Curly Hair

  • Hair Care Products for Mid Length Curly Hair

Description

Mid-length curly hair can be challenging to style for a wedding, but it's also incredibly versatile. You can wear it down, half up, or in a beautiful updo. The key is to find a hairstyle that complements your face shape, dress style, and overall wedding theme.

When choosing a wedding hairstyle for mid-length curly hair, consider the following factors:

  • Your face shape
  • Your dress style
  • Your wedding theme
  • Your hair length and texture

How to Choose the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle for Mid Length Curly Hair

Here are some tips on how to choose the perfect wedding hairstyle for your mid-length curly hair:

  • Consider your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a hairstyle that adds height to your crown, like a half-up, half-down style. If you have a square face, try a soft updo or loose waves that frame your face.
  • Match your dress style. If you have a bohemian-style dress, opt for loose waves or a messy bun. If you have a more classic dress, try a sleek updo or a polished half-up, half-down style.
  • Think about your wedding theme. If you're having a beach wedding, go for a more relaxed, undone hairstyle. If you're having a formal wedding, choose a more sophisticated updo.
  • Experiment with different styles. Before your wedding day, try different hairstyles to see what works best for your hair length and texture. Take photos of each style to see how it looks on camera.

Step by Step: Wedding Hairstyles for Mid Length Curly Hair

Here are some step-by-step instructions for wedding hairstyles for mid-length curly hair:

Half-Up, Half-Down Style

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from each side of your head and twist them backward, securing them in the back with a bobby pin.
  3. Take another section of hair from each side and repeat the twisting process, securing them in the back with a bobby pin.
  4. Continue this process until you've twisted all of your hair and secured it in the back.
  5. Loosen the twists to create a more relaxed, bohemian look.

Messy Bun

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a loose ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a messy bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few face-framing pieces of hair to create a more relaxed look.

Sleek Updo

  1. Start by smoothing your hair with a flat iron or blow dryer.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a low bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Add some extra shine with a hair serum or spray.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curly Hair

Curly hair can be more prone to dryness and breakage, so it's important to take good care of it leading up to your wedding day. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

FAQs

What should I do if my curls are frizzy?

If your curls are frizzy, try using a leave-in conditioner or a curl-defining product to tame them. You can also try scrunching your hair with a towel or a t-shirt to help define your curls.

How can I make my curls last all day?

To make your curls last all day, use a curl-enhancing product before styling your hair. You can also use a hairspray or a hair oil to help set your curls in place.

Hair Care Products for Mid Length Curly Hair

Here are some hair care products that are perfect for mid-length curly hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl-enhancing product
  • Hairspray
  • Hair oil

With these wedding hairstyles for mid-length curly hair and hair care tips, you'll be able to rock your curls on your big day. Remember to experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel the most beautiful and confident. Congratulations and enjoy your special day!
