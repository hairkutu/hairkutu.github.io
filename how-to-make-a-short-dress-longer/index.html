How To Make A Short Dress Longer Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jun 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having to pass on a cute dress because it's too short? Or maybe you already have a favorite dress that you wish was just a little bit longer? Don't worry, there are several ways to make a short dress longer. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect length.

How to Make a Short Dress Longer

Before we get started, it's important to note that some dresses may not be able to be lengthened. If the dress is too short or the material is too delicate, it may not be possible to add length without altering the overall look of the dress. However, if the dress has a little bit of extra material or you're willing to get creative, there are several ways to make it longer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some simple steps you can take to make your short dress longer:

  1. Add a hemline: One of the easiest ways to make a dress longer is to add a hemline. You can do this by sewing a strip of fabric to the bottom of the dress.

  2. Layer it: Another way to add length is to layer the dress over leggings, tights, or even another skirt. This will not only add length but also give the dress a new look.

  3. Use a slip: If the dress is too see-through, you can add a slip underneath. This will not only make the dress longer but also make it more appropriate for different occasions.

  4. Add lace: If you're feeling crafty, you can add lace to the bottom of the dress. This will not only add length but also give the dress a feminine touch.

  5. Accessorize: You can also accessorize the dress by adding a belt or a scarf. This will not only add length but also give the dress a more polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to make a short dress longer:

  • Choose the right fabric: Some fabrics, like silk or chiffon, may be too delicate to add length. Choose a fabric that can handle alterations.

  • Match the color: If you're adding a hemline or lace, make sure the fabric and color match the dress.

  • Get creative: Don't be afraid to get creative with your alterations. You may find a unique way to add length that works best for your dress.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a short dress longer:

  • Can I use a safety pin to make the dress longer?

    • While you can use a safety pin to temporarily make a dress longer, it's not a permanent solution. The pin may come undone or damage the fabric.


  • Can I just wear the dress as is?

    • It's up to personal preference, but if you feel uncomfortable or self-conscious in a short dress, it's best to add length.


  • What if I don't know how to sew?

    • You can always take the dress to a tailor or seamstress to have it altered.

Hair Care

Now that you know how to make a short dress longer, it's time to think about hair care. When wearing a dress, it's important to have a hairstyle that complements the look. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Choose a style that suits the dress: If you're wearing a dress with a high neckline, consider a hairstyle that puts the focus on the neckline, like an updo or a side-swept style.

  • Use the right products: If you're going for a sleek look, use a smoothing serum or cream. If you're going for a voluminous look, use a volumizing mousse or spray.

  • Don't forget about accessories: You can also accessorize your hair with a headband, scarf, or barrette.

With these tips, you'll be able to make any short dress longer and have a hairstyle that complements the look.
