How To Cut Hair With Scissors And Comb A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank May 21, 2023 · 6 min read

Learning how to cut hair with scissors and comb is an essential skill for anyone looking to save money on haircuts or start a career in hairstyling. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve the perfect haircut with just a pair of scissors and a comb.

How To:

The tools you'll need to cut hair with scissors and comb are a quality pair of hair cutting scissors, a fine-toothed comb, and hair clips to section off the hair. Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry, and you're working in a well-lit area with a mirror.

Step 1: Section and Comb the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into workable sections using hair clips. Comb through each section carefully to remove any tangles or knots, ensuring the hair is smooth and even.

Step 2: Cut the Hair with Scissors

Using your scissors, start cutting the hair in small sections, working from the back of the head to the front. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length.

Step 3: Use the Comb to Check for Evenness

After cutting each section, use the comb to check for evenness. Comb through the hair and make any necessary adjustments to ensure the length is even.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the sections, use the scissors to blend the hair together. This helps to create a more natural-looking finish and avoids any harsh lines.

Step 5: Finish with Styling

After cutting the hair, style it as desired using your favorite products. This could be a simple blow-dry or something more elaborate like curling or straightening the hair.

Tips:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. Cheap scissors can lead to split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Use the comb to keep the hair in place and ensure an even cut.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections to avoid making any mistakes.
  • Work in a well-lit area with a mirror to ensure you can see what you're doing.

FAQ:

Can I cut my own hair with scissors and comb?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with scissors and comb. It may take some practice, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.

Can I use household scissors to cut hair?

No, it's not recommended to use household scissors to cut hair. Hair cutting scissors are specifically designed to make clean cuts without damaging the hair.

What is the best way to maintain my haircut?

To maintain your haircut, use quality hair products and visit a professional stylist for regular trims.

Hair Care:

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy hair. This includes regular washing and conditioning, using quality hair products, and protecting the hair from heat and environmental damage. Consult with a professional stylist for personalized hair care advice.

Cutting hair with scissors and comb is a simple yet effective way to achieve a professional-looking haircut. With the right tools and techniques, you can save money on haircuts and feel confident in your new look. Remember to take your time, work in a well-lit area, and use quality hair cutting scissors for the best results.
