How To Curl Ends Of Hair With Flat Iron The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of using the same old curling iron to achieve those perfect curls but ending up with heat damage and split ends? It's time to switch to a flat iron for curling your hair ends. In this guide, we will show you how to curl ends of hair with flat iron in a few easy steps.

Description

Flat irons are versatile hair styling tools that can be used to create not only straight but also curly hairstyles. Curling your hair ends with a flat iron is a quick and easy way to add some volume and texture to your hair without damaging it. With a few simple techniques, you can achieve beautiful bouncy curls that will last all day.

How to Curl Ends of Hair with Flat Iron

Follow these simple steps to curl your hair ends with a flat iron:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before curling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. You can also add some volumizing mousse to add some texture to your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair ends.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Flat Iron

Set your flat iron to a medium heat setting. If you have thin hair, use a lower heat setting, and if you have thick hair, use a higher heat setting.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair Ends

Take a small section of your hair and clamp the flat iron around it near the ends. Twist the flat iron away from your face and pull it down towards the ends of your hair. Hold it in place for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process for all the sections of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have curled all the sections of your hair, finish with a light hold hairspray to set the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you curl your hair ends with a flat iron:

  • Start with a low heat setting and gradually increase it if needed.
  • Use a comb to detangle your hair before curling it.
  • Twist the flat iron away from your face to create a natural-looking curl.
  • Use a small section of hair to achieve tighter curls and a larger section for looser curls.
  • Always use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

FAQ

Can I curl my hair ends with a flat iron if I have short hair?

Yes, you can curl your hair ends with a flat iron even if you have short hair. Just make sure to use a smaller section of hair to achieve tighter curls.

How long will the curls last?

The curls will last all day if you use a hairspray to set them in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent heat damage and split ends. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to curl ends of hair with flat iron, go ahead and give it a try. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve beautiful bouncy curls that will turn heads.
