How Do You Know If A Hair Colour Will Suit You

Written by Eveline Aug 25, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair colour but not sure if it will suit you? Choosing a hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you are trying a new shade for the first time. In this article, we will explore how to know if a hair colour will suit you and provide tips on how to choose the perfect shade.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know If A Hair Colour Will Suit You
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hair Colour
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Coloring

How to Know If A Hair Colour Will Suit You

There are a few factors to consider when choosing a hair colour:

Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair colour. Warm skin tones generally look best with warm hair colours like golden blonde, copper or rich browns. Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colours like ash blonde, platinum blonde or cool brunettes.

Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair colour will suit you. Blue and green eyes tend to look best with cool hair colours, while brown and hazel eyes look great with warm hair colours.

Your Natural Hair Color

If you are considering a drastic change in hair colour, it is important to consider your natural hair colour. Going too far from your natural shade can sometimes look unnatural and not suit your skin tone or eye colour.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to help determine which hair colour will suit you:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

There are three main skin tones: warm, cool and neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins in your wrist. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If you can't determine whether they are green or blue, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Determine Your Eye Color

Look at your eye colour to determine whether you have warm or cool undertones. Blue and green eyes tend to have cool undertones, while brown and hazel eyes tend to have warm undertones.

Step 3: Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

If you have a light natural hair colour, you can usually go for a lighter shade without any issues. If you have a dark natural hair colour, it might be best to stay within a few shades of your natural colour.

Step 4: Try A Virtual Hair Color App

If you are still unsure of which hair colour will suit you, try using a virtual hair colour app. These apps allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hair colours to see which one looks best on you.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hair Colour

Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hair colour:

Start with Semi-Permanent Hair Colour

If you are trying a new hair colour for the first time, start with a semi-permanent hair colour. This will allow you to test the colour without committing to a permanent change.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair colour. If you have a busy schedule, you might want to choose a low-maintenance hair colour that doesn't require frequent touch-ups.

Consult A Professional

If you are still unsure of which hair colour will suit you, consult a professional hair stylist. They can help you choose the perfect shade based on your skin tone, eye colour and natural hair colour.

FAQs

  • Can I dye my hair if I have a sensitive scalp?
    Yes, there are hair dyes specifically designed for people with sensitive scalps. Look for hair dyes that are ammonia-free and contain natural ingredients.
  • How often should I touch up my hair colour?
    This depends on the type of hair colour you choose. Semi-permanent hair colour usually lasts 4-6 weeks, while permanent hair colour can last up to 8 weeks.
  • Can I dye my hair if I have highlights?
    Yes, you can dye your hair if you have highlights. However, it is important to choose a hair colour that complements your existing highlights.

Hair Care After Coloring

After coloring your hair, it is important to take care of your locks to keep your hair healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo

Use a color-safe shampoo to help maintain your hair colour. These shampoos are formulated to be gentle on dyed hair and prevent fading.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade faster.

Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will help your hair look vibrant and shiny.

Choosing a new hair colour can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your skin tone, eye colour and natural hair colour, you can choose a colour that will suit you perfectly. Don't be afraid to consult a professional if you are still unsure of which hair colour to choose. And remember to take care of your hair after coloring to keep it healthy and vibrant.
