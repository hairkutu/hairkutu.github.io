How To Cut Long Hair With Clippers Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the salon for a simple haircut? Do you want to take matters into your own hands and try cutting your own hair with clippers? Cutting long hair with clippers may seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and efficiently. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut long hair with clippers yourself.

The Tools You Will Need

Before you begin cutting your hair with clippers, you will need to gather the necessary tools:

  • Clippers with guard sizes ranging from 1-4
  • Comb
  • Haircutting scissors
  • Hair clip or elastic band to section hair
  • Mirror
  • Cape or towel to protect clothing

How to Cut Long Hair with Clippers Yourself

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It is important to start with clean, dry hair. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly before you begin cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a hair clip or elastic band to section your hair. Start by dividing your hair in half, from ear to ear, and clip the top section of hair on top of your head. This will allow you to work on the bottom section of hair first.

Step 3: Choose the Right Clipper Guard Size

Choose a clipper guard size based on how much hair you want to cut off. If you want to take off a lot of hair, start with a larger clipper guard size and work your way down to a smaller size. If you want to trim your hair without taking off too much length, start with a smaller clipper guard size.

Step 4: Begin Cutting Your Hair

Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up towards the crown of your head. Use the clippers to cut the hair in a straight line, moving from the back of your head to the front. Be sure to use the comb to lift the hair and guide the clippers.

Step 5: Blend Your Hair

After you have cut the bottom section of hair, unclip the top section and blend it with the bottom section. Use the same clipper guard size you used on the bottom section and cut the hair in a straight line, again moving from the back to the front of your head.

Step 6: Use Haircutting Scissors to Trim the Ends

Once you have blended your hair with the clippers, use the haircutting scissors to trim any ends that look uneven or need further shaping. Start at the front of your head and work your way to the back, taking small sections of hair at a time.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure your hair is even. If you notice any areas that need further trimming, go back and make adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair with Clippers Yourself

  • Start with a larger clipper guard size and work your way down to a smaller size to avoid taking off too much hair.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and guide the clippers for a more even cut.
  • Trim small sections of hair at a time with the haircutting scissors for precision.
  • Use a mirror to check your work and make adjustments.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. With the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair can be easy and cost-effective.

What clipper guard size should I use?

The clipper guard size you use will depend on how much hair you want to cut off. Start with a larger clipper guard size and work your way down to a smaller size if you want to take off more hair.

Should I use haircutting scissors to trim my hair?

Yes, using haircutting scissors to trim your hair will allow you to achieve a more precise cut and shape your hair as desired.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

After cutting your hair with clippers, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair, and avoid using hot styling tools too often. To maintain your haircut, it is recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Now that you know how to cut long hair with clippers yourself, you can save time and money by cutting your own hair at home. Follow these steps and tips for a professional-looking cut that you can be proud of!
