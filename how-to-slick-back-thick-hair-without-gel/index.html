How To Slick Back Thick Hair Without Gel

Written by Bardi Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of using gel to slick back your thick hair? Do you want a more natural look? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will show you how to slick back thick hair without gel. From the basics to the advanced techniques, we have got you covered.

How to Slick Back Thick Hair Without Gel

Slicking back thick hair without gel can seem like a difficult task. But it is actually quite simple. All you need is some water and a good quality pomade or hair wax. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any product buildup and make your hair easier to work with.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Use a towel to dry your hair. Do not rub your hair vigorously as this can cause damage. Instead, gently pat your hair dry.

Step 3: Apply Pomade or Hair Wax

Take a small amount of pomade or hair wax and rub it between your palms. Apply it to your hair, starting at the back and working your way forward. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to slick back your hair. Start at the front and work your way back. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to go.

Step 5: Add More Product (Optional)

If you have very thick hair, you may need to add more product to keep it in place. Take a small amount of pomade or hair wax and apply it to the areas that need more hold. Comb your hair again to distribute the product evenly.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide for slicking back thick hair without gel:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a small amount of pomade or hair wax to your hair.
  4. Comb your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  5. Add more product if necessary.

Tips for Slicking Back Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you slick back your thick hair without gel:

  • Use a high-quality pomade or hair wax.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Comb your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  • Use a towel to dry your hair instead of a blow dryer.
  • Do not use too much product as this can make your hair look greasy.

FAQs

Can I slick back thick hair without gel?

Yes, you can slick back thick hair without gel. All you need is some water and a good quality pomade or hair wax.

What is the best product to use for slicking back thick hair?

The best product to use for slicking back thick hair is a pomade or hair wax. Look for a product that is high-quality and has a good hold.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy?

To prevent your hair from looking greasy, use a small amount of product and avoid applying too much. Also, make sure to wash your hair regularly to remove any product buildup.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

If you slick back your hair regularly, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any product buildup.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product.
  • Do not use a blow dryer too often as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to slick back thick hair without gel, you can achieve a natural and stylish look without using any harsh chemicals. Follow these simple steps and tips for a sleek and polished hairstyle that will turn heads.
