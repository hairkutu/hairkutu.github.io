How Much Do Curly Hair Perms Cost

Written by Frank Jul 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're tired of your straight hair and want to add some curls, then a perm may be the solution for you. However, before getting a perm, you may be wondering how much it will cost. In this article, we'll discuss the cost of curly hair perms, how to get one, and some tips for maintaining your curls.

Description

A curly hair perm is a chemical treatment that curls your hair permanently. It's a great option for people who have naturally straight hair and want to add some texture and volume to their hair. Perms are a popular option for women with fine hair who want to add some body to their locks. Additionally, perms are also a great option for people who want to style their hair with less heat.

How to Get a Curly Hair Perm

Getting a curly hair perm is a process that requires a few steps. First, you need to find a reputable hair salon that specializes in perms. Next, you will need to schedule a consultation with the stylist to discuss the type of perm you want and the overall process. During the consultation, the stylist will examine your hair to determine its texture and condition.

Once the stylist has determined the type of perm that is best for your hair, they will begin the process. The process includes washing your hair with a special perm solution, applying the curling rods, and then applying another solution to set the curls. The process can take anywhere from 2-4 hours, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Step by Step Guide on How to Get a Curly Hair Perm

If you're interested in getting a curly hair perm, follow these simple steps:

  1. Find a reputable hair salon that specializes in perms
  2. Schedule a consultation with the stylist
  3. Determine the type of perm that is best for your hair
  4. Wash your hair with a special perm solution
  5. Apply the curling rods
  6. Apply another solution to set the curls
  7. Wait for the solution to set for the recommended time
  8. Rinse your hair with water
  9. Apply a neutralizing solution
  10. Rinse your hair again
  11. Dry and style your hair

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

After getting a curly hair perm, it's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your curls:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair
  • Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair to define your curls
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

FAQ

How Much Do Curly Hair Perms Cost?

The cost of a curly hair perm can vary depending on the salon and location. On average, a curly hair perm can cost anywhere from $100 to $300. However, some salons may charge more if you have longer or thicker hair.

How Long Does a Curly Hair Perm Last?

A curly hair perm can last anywhere from 2-6 months, depending on how well you take care of your curls. Factors that can affect the longevity of your perm include your hair type, the products you use, and how often you wash your hair.

Can I Get a Curly Hair Perm If I Have Colored Hair?

Yes, you can get a curly hair perm if you have colored hair. However, it's important to let your stylist know that you have colored hair so they can adjust the perm solution accordingly. Additionally, it's a good idea to wait a few weeks after coloring your hair before getting a perm.

Hair Care

If you're considering getting a curly hair perm, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid using heat styling tools, and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Overall, a curly hair perm can be a great way to add some texture and volume to your hair. With the right care and maintenance, your curls can last for months and make you feel confident and beautiful.
