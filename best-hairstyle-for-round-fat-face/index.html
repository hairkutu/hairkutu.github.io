The Best Hairstyle For Round Fat Face Your Ultimate Guide

Written by William Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round fat face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging. You want a hairstyle that can slim down your face, make it look longer, and draw attention away from your cheeks. Fortunately, there are various hairstyles that can flatter your face shape. In this blog post, we'll give you the ultimate guide on the best hairstyle for round fat face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round fat face has full cheeks and a rounded chin, which can make you look younger and cuter. However, it can also make your face look wider and less defined. To balance out your face shape, you need a hairstyle that can add height and length to your face. The best hairstyle for round fat face should be one that creates angles and draws attention upwards.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for round fat face is to determine your face shape. Start by measuring the widest part of your face, which is usually around your cheeks. If the width is almost the same as the length, then you have a round face shape. Once you've identified your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that can flatter your face shape.

Step by step

Here are the steps on how to choose the best hairstyle for round fat face:

  1. Look for hairstyles that can add height and length to your face. This includes hairstyles with layers, volume, and height on top.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. Short hairstyles can make your face look wider, while long hairstyles can make your face look rounder.
  3. Consider hairstyles with side-swept bangs or layers that frame your face. This can create angles and draw attention away from your cheeks.
  4. Experiment with different hairstyles to see what works best for you. Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles and consult with your hairstylist for recommendations.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hairstyle for round fat face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or sleek. This can make your face look wider and rounder.
  • Consider hairstyles with volume on top or layers around your face. This can add height and length to your face.
  • Experiment with different hair colors and highlights to create dimension and depth.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with headbands, hair clips, or hats to draw attention away from your cheeks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for round fat face:

  • What is the best hairstyle for round fat face with glasses? Hairstyles with side-swept bangs or layers that frame your face can complement glasses and draw attention away from your cheeks.
  • Can I have a pixie cut with a round fat face? Yes, you can have a pixie cut with a round fat face. However, make sure that the cut is not too short and has some volume on top to balance out your face shape.
  • What hair color is best for round fat face? Hair colors with highlights or lowlights can create dimension and depth, making your face look longer and slimmer. Avoid hair colors that are too dark or flat.

Hair Care

Aside from choosing the best hairstyle for round fat face, it's also important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hair products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray and avoiding excessive heat styling.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for round fat face is one that can add height and length to your face. Consider hairstyles with layers, volume, and height on top, and avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. Experiment with different hairstyles and consult with your hairstylist for recommendations. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain a healthy lifestyle for healthy, beautiful hair.
