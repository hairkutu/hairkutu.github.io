How To Put Your Hair Down For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a guy with long hair, you may be wondering how to put your hair down without looking messy or unkempt. Putting your hair down can be a great way to change up your look and show off your luscious locks. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide to help you put your hair down in a way that looks stylish and put-together.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Start with Clean Hair
  • Step 2: Choose Your Part
  • Step 3: Brush Your Hair
  • Step 4: Apply Product (Optional)
  • Step 5: Let Your Hair Down
  • Tips for Putting Your Hair Down
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to putting your hair down is to start with clean hair. If your hair is dirty or greasy, it will be more difficult to style and may not look as good. So, make sure to wash your hair with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner before you begin.

Step 2: Choose Your Part

Next, decide where you want to part your hair. This will determine how your hair falls and can make a big difference in your overall look. If you're not sure where to part your hair, try experimenting with different parts to see what looks best on you.

How to Part Your Hair

To create a part, use a comb or your fingers to separate your hair along your scalp. You can create a middle part, a side part, or a diagonal part, depending on your preference. Once you've created your part, use the comb to smooth down any flyaways or bumps.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair

Before you let your hair down, it's important to brush it out to remove any tangles or knots. This will help your hair fall smoothly and look more polished. Use a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush to gently brush through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 4: Apply Product (Optional)

If you want to add some extra hold or texture to your hair, you can apply a styling product like wax, pomade, or gel. This is totally optional, but can help keep your hair in place and give it a more defined look. Just be careful not to use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Down

Finally, it's time to let your hair down! Use your fingers to gently loosen the hair around your part and let it fall naturally. You can use your hands to smooth down any bumps or flyaways, or you can leave your hair slightly tousled for a more relaxed look. And that's it - you've successfully put your hair down!

Tips for Putting Your Hair Down

  • Experiment with different parts to find the one that looks best on you.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or paddle brush to gently brush out any tangles or knots.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product if you want to add hold or texture to your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Leave your hair slightly tousled for a more relaxed look.

FAQs

Can guys with short hair put their hair down?

Yes, guys with short hair can still put their hair down, although it may not look as dramatic as with longer hair. To put your short hair down, simply brush it back or to one side using a comb or your fingers.

What if my hair is too thick to put down?

If your hair is very thick or heavy, it may be more difficult to put it down. In this case, you may want to consider thinning out your hair or getting a haircut that is more conducive to letting your hair down.

Will putting my hair down damage it?

Putting your hair down should not damage it, as long as you are gentle when brushing and styling it. However, if you frequently use heat styling tools or chemical treatments, this can damage your hair over time.

Hair Care for Long Hair

If you have long hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for long hair:

  • Wash your hair with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or paddle brush to gently brush out any tangles or knots.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Minimize your use of chemical treatments like hair dye and bleach.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

By following these tips, you can keep your long hair looking fabulous and put your hair down with confidence!
