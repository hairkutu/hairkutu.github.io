Easy Hairstyles For School For Medium Hair

Written by William May 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same hairstyle every day to school? Do you want to switch up your look without spending hours in front of the mirror? Look no further! Here are some easy hairstyles for school for medium hair that will make you the envy of your classmates.

Description

Medium hair can be tricky to style, but it doesn't have to be. With a little creativity and the right tools, you can create a variety of hairstyles that are perfect for school. From braids to buns, there are plenty of options to choose from.

How to

To create these easy hairstyles, you'll need a few basic tools: a brush or comb, hair elastics, bobby pins, and hairspray. Depending on the style, you may also need a curling iron or straightener.

1. Half-Up Half-Down

This classic hairstyle is perfect for medium-length hair. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair elastic. You can leave the bottom half straight or add some loose curls for a more polished look.

2. Braided Crown

This elegant hairstyle is surprisingly easy to create. Simply braid two sections of hair on either side of your head and secure them at the back with bobby pins. You can also add some flowers or other hair accessories for a more bohemian vibe.

3. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a staple hairstyle for school. To create this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy, effortless look.

Step by step

1. Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair elastic.
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to add some curls or waves to the bottom half of your hair.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair on one side of your head and braid it all the way down to the end.
  3. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  4. Bring the braids around to the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins.
  5. Add hair accessories if desired.

3. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair elastic.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few strands to create a messy look.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make styling your medium hair even easier:

  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Invest in some cute hair accessories to add some extra flair to your hairstyles.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques.
  • Practice makes perfect! The more you practice, the easier it will be to create these hairstyles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for school for medium hair:

Q: How can I make my hairstyles last all day?

A: Use plenty of hairspray to hold your style in place, and consider using a hair primer or setting spray to help it last even longer.

Q: Can I create these hairstyles without any tools?

A: Yes! You can create many of these hairstyles using just your fingers and some hair elastics.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these easy hairstyles for school for medium hair, you'll never have to worry about a bad hair day again. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these styles are sure to turn heads. So grab your tools and get styling!
