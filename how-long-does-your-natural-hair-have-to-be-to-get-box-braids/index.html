How Long Does Your Natural Hair Have To Be To Get Box Braids

Written by Aprilia Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Box braids are a popular protective hairstyle that can be worn by women with natural hair. They are a great way to protect your hair from damage while also giving you a chic look. However, before you get box braids, you may be wondering how long your hair needs to be before you can get them. In this article, we will answer that question and provide you with some tips for maintaining your box braids once you have them.

Table of Contents

  • How To Get Box Braids
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Box Braids
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Box Braids

How to Get Box Braids

Box braids are a type of protective hairstyle that involves braiding your hair into small, square-shaped sections. The braids can be any length and can be styled in a variety of ways. To get box braids, you will need to visit a hair salon or stylist who specializes in braiding. They will be able to braid your hair into the desired style and length.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you are interested in getting box braids, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Choose the length and style of braids you want.
  2. Cleanse and condition your hair before the appointment.
  3. Make an appointment with a stylist who specializes in box braids.
  4. Arrive at the appointment with your hair detangled and ready to braid.
  5. Sit still while the stylist braids your hair into the desired style.
  6. Pay for the service and leave the salon with your new box braids.

Tips for Maintaining Box Braids

Once you have your box braids, it is important to take care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your box braids:

  • Keep your scalp clean and moisturized by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid scratching your scalp, as this can cause damage to the braids.
  • Wear a satin or silk scarf or bonnet when sleeping to prevent frizzing and tangling.
  • Avoid using heavy oils on your hair, as this can weigh down the braids and cause them to loosen.
  • Be gentle when styling your braids, as pulling or tugging can cause them to break or become loose.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting box braids:

1. How long does my hair need to be to get box braids?

Your hair should be at least three to four inches long to get box braids. If your hair is shorter than this, the braids may not stay in place and may fall out.

2. How long do box braids last?

Box braids can last anywhere from four to eight weeks, depending on how well you take care of them. If you properly maintain your braids, they can last up to two months.

3. Can I wash my hair with box braids?

Yes, you can wash your hair with box braids. However, it is important to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner and to avoid scrubbing your scalp too vigorously.

Hair Care for Box Braids

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when you have box braids:

  • Wash your hair once a week with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Moisturize your scalp daily with a lightweight oil, such as coconut or jojoba oil.
  • Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your braids and cause them to loosen.
  • Wear a satin or silk scarf or bonnet when sleeping to prevent frizzing and tangling.

Now that you know how long your hair needs to be to get box braids, you can decide if this style is right for you. Remember to take care of your braids to ensure they last as long as possible.
