Does Darker Hair Color Make You Look Older

Written by Christine Jun 06, 2023 · 6 min read

As we age, many of us start to wonder whether we should stick with our natural hair color or make a change. One question that often comes up is whether darker hair color makes you look older. In this article, we’ll explore this question and provide some tips for those who are considering a hair color change.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that as we age, we should stick with lighter hair colors to avoid looking older. However, this is not necessarily true. While lighter hair colors can be flattering, darker hair colors can also be very chic and sophisticated. The key is to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and style.

How to

If you’re considering a darker hair color, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider your skin tone. If you have cool-toned skin, you may want to opt for a darker hair color with cool undertones, such as espresso or jet black. If you have warm-toned skin, a darker hair color with warm undertones, such as chestnut or dark chocolate, may be more flattering.

It’s also important to consider your natural hair color. If you have very light blonde hair, going too dark too quickly can be a shock to your system. Instead, consider gradually going darker over a few salon visits to allow your hair to adjust to the new color.

Step by Step

If you’ve decided to go for a darker hair color, here are some steps you can take:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best shade for your skin tone and natural hair color.
  2. Make sure to use high-quality hair color products to ensure long-lasting results.
  3. Take good care of your hair after coloring to prevent damage and maintain your new color.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you look your best with a darker hair color:

  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and style.
  • Consider gradually going darker if you have very light blonde hair.
  • Use high-quality hair color products to ensure long-lasting results.
  • Take good care of your hair after coloring to prevent damage and maintain your new color.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about darker hair color and looking older:

Does darker hair color make you look older?

Not necessarily. The key is to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and style.

What are some darker hair color options?

Some popular darker hair color options include espresso, jet black, chestnut, and dark chocolate.

Should I consult with a professional stylist before coloring my hair?

Yes, it’s always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best shade for your skin tone and natural hair color.

Hair Care

To maintain your new darker hair color, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

In conclusion, darker hair color does not necessarily make you look older. The key is to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and style, and to take good care of your hair after coloring. By following these tips, you can rock a chic and sophisticated darker hair color at any age.
