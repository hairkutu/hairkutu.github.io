How To Make Short Hair Wavy Without Heat

Written by Bardi Apr 29, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of always using heat tools to achieve the perfect beachy waves on your short hair? Not only can using heat tools be damaging to your hair, but it can also be time-consuming. Luckily, there are plenty of ways to achieve natural-looking waves without using any heat. In this article, we will go over how to make short hair wavy without heat, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Short Hair Wavy Without Heat
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Wavy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Make Short Hair Wavy Without Heat

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to note that the technique you use to make your hair wavy without heat will depend on your hair type and texture. The following methods work best for those with straight or slightly wavy hair. If you have naturally curly hair, you can try these methods, but your results may vary.

Method 1: Braid Your Hair

One of the easiest ways to make your hair wavy without heat is to braid it. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Divide your hair into sections
  3. Braid each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends
  4. Secure the ends of your braids with small hair ties
  5. Leave your braids in overnight, or for at least 6-8 hours
  6. Take out the hair ties and gently unravel your braids
  7. Run your fingers through your hair to separate the waves

For tighter waves, braid your hair into smaller sections. For looser waves, braid larger sections of hair.

Method 2: Twist Your Hair

Another easy way to make your hair wavy without heat is to twist it. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Divide your hair into sections
  3. Twist each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends
  4. Secure the ends of your twists with small hair ties
  5. Leave your twists in overnight, or for at least 6-8 hours
  6. Take out the hair ties and gently unravel your twists
  7. Run your fingers through your hair to separate the waves

For tighter waves, twist your hair into smaller sections. For looser waves, twist larger sections of hair.

Method 3: Use a Salt Spray

If you want to enhance your hair's natural texture and add some waves, you can use a salt spray. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Spray a salt spray evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage waves
  4. Leave your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to separate the waves

Remember, a little goes a long way with salt spray. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the three methods for making your short hair wavy without heat, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve perfect waves:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair and comb out any tangles
  3. Decide which method you want to use to create your waves
  4. Follow the steps outlined in the method you chose
  5. Leave your hair to dry overnight, or for at least 6-8 hours
  6. In the morning, take out the hair ties or unravel your twists
  7. Use your fingers to separate the waves and create your desired look

Remember, practice makes perfect! It may take a few tries to get the hang of creating natural-looking waves without heat, so don't get discouraged if your first attempt doesn't turn out how you hoped.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wavy Hair

Once you've achieved the perfect waves, you'll want to make sure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your wavy hair:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to help extend the life of your waves
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the waves
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and help your waves stay intact overnight

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use these methods on all hair types?

These methods work best for those with straight or slightly wavy hair. If you have naturally curly hair, you can try these methods, but your results may vary.

How long will my waves last?

Your waves should last for at least a few days, depending on how well you maintain them. Using a dry shampoo and avoiding excessive touching can help extend the life of your waves.

Will these methods damage my hair?

No, these methods are heat-free and should not cause any damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Now that you have beautiful, natural-looking waves, you'll want to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for wavy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for wavy hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz

By following these tips, you can keep your wavy hair looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.

Now that you know how to make short hair wavy without heat, it's time to give it a try! Whether you choose to braid, twist, or use a salt spray, you're sure to love your natural-looking waves.
