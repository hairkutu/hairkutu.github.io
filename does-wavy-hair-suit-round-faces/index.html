Does Wavy Hair Suit Round Faces Find Out In 2023

Written by Eveline Mar 30, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you wondering if wavy hair suits round faces? If so, you're not alone. Many people with round faces are hesitant to try wavy hair because they're not sure if it will flatter their face shape. In this article, we'll explore whether wavy hair is a good choice for those with round faces, how to style it, and what hair care tips you should keep in mind.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Wavy Hair for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Description

Before we dive into whether wavy hair suits round faces, let's take a moment to define what a round face is. A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with equal width and length. Those with round faces typically have full cheeks and a rounded jawline. If you have a round face, you may want to consider hairstyles that elongate your face and create the illusion of more angular features.

How to Style Wavy Hair for Round Faces

Wavy hair can be a great choice for those with round faces, provided you style it correctly. The key to making wavy hair work for a round face is to create volume and height at the crown of your head. This will help elongate your face and balance out your features. Here are a few tips for styling wavy hair if you have a round face:

1. Use the Right Products

When it comes to styling wavy hair, the right products can make all the difference. Look for volumizing mousses, sprays, or serums that will give your hair some lift and texture. You may also want to consider using a curling wand or iron to define your waves.

2. Create Volume at the Crown

To create height at the crown of your head, try backcombing or teasing the hair at your roots. This will give your hair some lift and make your face appear more elongated. You can also try using a volumizing powder or dry shampoo to add some texture and volume to your roots.

3. Avoid Heavy Bangs

If you have a round face, you may want to avoid heavy bangs that cut across your forehead. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a middle part that frames your face. This will help create the illusion of a longer face and balance out your features.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're ready to try wavy hair with a round face, here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you style your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, then blow dry your hair with a round brush to create some volume.

2. Add Texture

Once your hair is dry, add some texture with a volumizing mousse or spray. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.

3. Create Waves

Using a curling wand or iron, create loose waves throughout your hair. Start at the mid-lengths of your hair and wrap small sections around the barrel of the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds, then release.

4. Tease at the Crown

Tease the hair at the crown of your head to create some height and volume. Use a fine-toothed comb to gently backcomb your hair at the roots.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light-hold hairspray to keep your waves in place. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair

Once you've styled your wavy hair, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining wavy hair:

1. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

When you're brushing your hair, use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush. This will help prevent tangles and minimize breakage.

2. Avoid Heat Damage

Try to avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and make your hair look frizzy. If you do need to use a curling iron or straightener, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness and frizz, so it's important to keep it hydrated. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my wavy hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your wavy hair straight if you have a round face. Just be sure to add some volume at the crown of your head to create the illusion of a longer face.

Q: What kind of bangs should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: If you have a round face, you may want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that cut straight across your forehead. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a middle part that frames your face.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Whether you have a round face or not, it's important to take care of your wavy hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining wavy hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Less Often

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness, so try to wash your hair less often to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

2. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

As mentioned earlier, use a wide-toothed comb to brush your hair to prevent tangles and breakage.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims every six to eight weeks will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

So, does wavy hair suit round faces? The answer is yes! With the right styling techniques and hair care tips, wavy hair can be a great choice for those with round faces. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
