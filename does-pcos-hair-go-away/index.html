Does Pcos Hair Go Away Understanding Pcos Hair Loss And What You Can Do About It

Written by Christine May 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our blog where we discuss all things related to hair care. If you're reading this, you may be wondering about the connection between PCOS and hair loss. In this article, we'll answer the question, "does PCOS hair go away?" and provide you with tips and advice on how to manage PCOS hair loss.

What is PCOS?

PCOS or Polycystic Ovary Syndrome is a hormonal disorder that affects women of reproductive age. It's estimated that 1 in 10 women have PCOS, making it a common condition. Women with PCOS have high levels of androgens or male hormones, which can cause a variety of symptoms including hair loss, acne, weight gain, and irregular periods.

How Does PCOS Cause Hair Loss?

PCOS hair loss occurs due to the excess production of androgens, which can cause hair follicles to shrink and produce finer, thinner hair. This type of hair loss is called androgenic alopecia, and it's the most common type of hair loss in women.

Does PCOS Hair Go Away?

Unfortunately, PCOS hair loss doesn't go away on its own. However, there are treatments available that can help manage the symptoms of PCOS hair loss. The key is to identify the underlying cause of your hair loss and address it accordingly.

How to Manage PCOS Hair Loss

If you're experiencing hair loss due to PCOS, there are several things you can do to manage the condition:

1. Consult with a Doctor or Dermatologist

It's important to consult with a doctor or dermatologist if you're experiencing hair loss. They can help you identify the underlying cause of your hair loss and provide you with the appropriate treatment options.

2. Medication

There are medications available that can help manage PCOS hair loss. These include oral contraceptives, anti-androgen medications, and topical solutions like minoxidil.

3. Lifestyle Changes

Lifestyle changes can also help manage PCOS hair loss. These include regular exercise, a healthy diet, and stress management techniques like yoga or meditation.

4. Hair Care

Proper hair care can also help manage PCOS hair loss. Use gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools, and consider using hair growth products like biotin supplements or hair growth serums.

FAQs

Q: Is PCOS hair loss permanent?

A: PCOS hair loss is not necessarily permanent, but it can be a chronic condition that requires ongoing management.

Q: Can PCOS hair loss be reversed?

A: While PCOS hair loss cannot be completely reversed, it can be managed with appropriate treatment and lifestyle changes.

Q: Does PCOS hair loss affect all women with PCOS?

A: No, not all women with PCOS experience hair loss. It's estimated that about 50% of women with PCOS experience some form of hair loss.

Final Thoughts

PCOS hair loss can be a frustrating and challenging condition to manage, but it's important to remember that there are treatment options available. Consult with a doctor or dermatologist, make lifestyle changes, and take proper care of your hair to manage PCOS hair loss. With the right approach, you can minimize the impact of PCOS on your hair and overall well-being.
