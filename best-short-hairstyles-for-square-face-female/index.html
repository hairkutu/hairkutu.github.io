Best Short Hairstyles For Square Face Female

Written by William Apr 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a square face, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want something that will soften your features and complement your face shape. Short hairstyles can be a great option for square faces, but it's important to choose the right one. In this article, we'll explore the best short hairstyles for square face females.

Description

A square face has a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and wide cheekbones. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a square face is to soften these features and create balance. Short hairstyles can achieve this by drawing attention away from the jawline and forehead.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a square face, it's important to consider the length and shape of the cut. Ideally, you want a cut that falls just below the chin or above the shoulders. This length will help to elongate the face and create balance. The shape of the cut should also be soft and rounded, with layers to add movement and volume.

Step by Step

1. Determine the length you want for your hair.

2. Choose a soft and rounded shape with layers for movement and volume.

3. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your stylist for inspiration.

4. Ask your stylist for their opinion on what will work best for your face shape.

5. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for a square face, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

- Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin

- Opt for soft layers to add movement and volume

- Consider side-swept bangs to soften your forehead

- Play around with different parts to find what works best for you

- Add volume at the crown to elongate your face

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with a square face?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a soft, layered pixie cut that adds volume to the crown.

Q: Should I avoid short hairstyles altogether if I have a square face?

A: No, but it's important to choose the right cut that complements your face shape.

Q: Can I add highlights to my short hair?

A: Yes, highlights can add dimension to your hair and help to soften your features.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle for your square face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best short hairstyles for square face females, it's time to book your appointment with your stylist. With the right cut and some TLC, you can rock a short hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
