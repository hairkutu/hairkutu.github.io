Hair Styles For Wavy Hair Medium Length

Written by Luffy Apr 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Welcome to our guide on hair styles for wavy hair medium length! If you have wavy hair and are looking for some inspiration for your next haircut, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll show you some of the best hair styles for wavy hair medium length, along with tips on how to achieve them and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Wavy Hair Medium Length
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Wavy Hair Medium Length
  • Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair Medium Length
  • FAQs About Hair Styles for Wavy Hair Medium Length
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair Medium Length

How to Style Wavy Hair Medium Length

Styling wavy hair medium length can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of looks. Some of the most popular hair styles for wavy hair medium length include:

  • Layered cuts
  • Long bobs
  • Beach waves
  • Half-up half-down styles
  • Braids

When styling wavy hair medium length, it’s important to use products that enhance your natural waves without weighing them down. Look for lightweight mousses or gels that define curls and add volume. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Wavy Hair Medium Length

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling wavy hair medium length:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a lightweight mousse or gel to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment, scrunching your hair as you go.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand or curling iron to define any loose curls or waves.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair Medium Length

To keep your wavy hair medium length looking its best, here are a few tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush that can break up your curls.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

FAQs About Hair Styles for Wavy Hair Medium Length

What is the best haircut for wavy hair medium length?

Some of the best haircuts for wavy hair medium length include layered cuts, long bobs, and shaggy styles.

How do I enhance my natural waves?

You can enhance your natural waves by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, applying a lightweight mousse or gel, and scrunching your hair as it dries.

What should I avoid when styling wavy hair medium length?

Avoid using a brush to detangle your hair, washing your hair too often, and using heat styling tools too often.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair Medium Length

To keep your wavy hair medium length looking its best, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, rather than a regular towel that can cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it hydrated and healthy.

We hope you found this guide on hair styles for wavy hair medium length helpful! With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful, healthy hair.
