Best Short Hairstyles For Thin Hair 2019

Written by Petter Aug 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thin hair, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face and makes your hair look thicker. Short haircuts can be the solution to this problem, and in 2019, there were a few styles that were particularly popular. In this article, we will explore the best short hairstyles for thin hair in 2019 and give you some tips on how to style them.

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for thin hair because they create the illusion of thickness and volume. There are many different styles to choose from, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags. The key is to find a style that works with your face shape and hair texture.

How to

To achieve the best results with a short hairstyle for thin hair, it's essential to use the right styling products and techniques. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style some of the most popular short haircuts for thin hair:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that is perfect for thin hair. Here's how to style a pixie cut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to the ends of your hair to create a messy, tousled look.

Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on thin hair. Here's how to style a bob haircut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a sleek look.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair serum to the ends of your hair to add shine.

Shag Haircut

A shag haircut is a layered hairstyle that looks great on thin hair. Here's how to style a shag haircut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair.
  5. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray to the ends of your hair to create a messy, tousled look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short haircuts for thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume at the roots.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for thin hair:

What is the best short haircut for thin hair?

There are many great short haircuts for thin hair, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags. The best one for you will depend on your face shape and hair texture.

Can you add volume to thin hair?

Yes, you can add volume to thin hair by using the right styling products and techniques. A volumizing mousse and a round brush are essential tools for creating volume at the roots.

How often should you wash thin hair?

Thin hair should be washed every other day to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils. Using a dry shampoo can also help to refresh your hair between washes.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a beautiful, voluminous look for your thin hair. Whether you choose a pixie cut, bob, or shag, remember to use the right products and techniques to keep your hair looking its best.
