How Long Does It Take To Cut Curtain Bangs

Written by Eveline Apr 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're thinking of getting curtain bangs, you might be wondering how long it takes to cut them. Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that can give you a trendy and youthful look. In this article, we'll go over how long it takes to cut curtain bangs, and we'll also provide you with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are longer in the middle and shorter on the sides. They are called "curtain" bangs because they frame your face like a pair of curtains. This style has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's easy to see why. Curtain bangs can make your face appear more symmetrical, and they can also help to soften your features.

How to Cut Curtain Bangs

If you're thinking of cutting your own curtain bangs, you might be wondering how to do it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. Wet hair can be difficult to work with, and it can also cause your bangs to appear shorter than you intended.

Step 2: Section off your bangs.

Use a comb to section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Make sure the section is even on both sides of your face.

Step 3: Cut your bangs.

Start by cutting your bangs slightly longer than you want them to be. You can always go back and trim them later if they're too long. Use a pair of sharp scissors and cut straight across.

Step 4: Texturize your bangs.

Use a pair of thinning shears to texturize your bangs. This will help to give them a more natural look and prevent them from appearing too heavy.

Step 5: Style your bangs.

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to style your bangs. Brush them to the side and use the blow dryer to give them a slight curl.

Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curtain bangs:

  • Start by cutting your bangs slightly longer than you want them to be.
  • Use a pair of sharp scissors.
  • Texturize your bangs with thinning shears.
  • Style your bangs with a round brush and a blow dryer.
  • Consider getting your bangs cut by a professional.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

How long does it take to cut curtain bangs?

The length of time it takes to cut curtain bangs can vary depending on the skill of the person cutting them. It usually takes between 15 to 30 minutes.

Can I cut my own curtain bangs?

Yes, you can cut your own curtain bangs, but it's recommended to have them cut by a professional to avoid any mistakes.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Overall, cutting curtain bangs can be a fun and easy way to change up your hairstyle. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect look in no time.
