How To Cut Curly Hair Side Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 01, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have curly hair and are wondering whether you should get side bangs or not, the answer is – definitely yes! Side bangs can add a whole new dimension to your curly hair, making it look more stylish and feminine. However, cutting curly hair side bangs can be a bit tricky. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of cutting curly hair side bangs, step by step.

How To

Before we start, there are a few things you should keep in mind. Firstly, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Secondly, use sharp scissors and a fine-tooth comb. Thirdly, always cut your hair when it’s dry as curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut curly hair side bangs:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Comb all your hair back and section off the hair you want to cut for your side bangs.

Step 2: Comb the Sectioned Hair

Comb the sectioned hair forward and hold it between your fingers.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Cut the hair at a slight angle, starting from the outer corner of your eyebrow and ending at the bottom of your cheek.

Step 4: Check the Length

After cutting, check the length of the side bangs. If they’re too long, you can trim them a bit more.

Step 5: Style the Bangs

Style your side bangs using a hair dryer and a round brush.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting curly hair side bangs:

  • Always cut your hair when it’s dry as curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries.
  • Start by cutting less hair and then gradually increase the amount.
  • If you’re unsure about cutting your own hair, seek help from a professional stylist.
  • Use sharp scissors and a fine-tooth comb.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly hair side bangs:

Q: Can I cut my curly hair side bangs myself?

A: Yes, you can cut your curly hair side bangs yourself. However, it’s always advisable to seek help from a professional stylist if you’re unsure.

Q: How often should I trim my side bangs?

A: You should trim your side bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I style my curly hair side bangs in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your curly hair side bangs in different ways, such as sweeping them to the side or curling them with a curling iron.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair healthy and shiny, make sure you follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Comb your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

In conclusion, cutting curly hair side bangs can be a bit tricky, but with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a stylish and feminine look. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article and take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
