How To Cut Short Messy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your unruly, messy hair? Do you want to achieve a trendy, short hairstyle? Look no further! In this guide, we will show you how to cut short messy hair in the comfort of your own home. Follow our step-by-step instructions and achieve a fresh and stylish new look.

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Short Messy Hair

How to Cut Short Messy Hair

Cutting short messy hair can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily at home. Before you begin, it's important to have a vision of the hairstyle you want to achieve. Look for inspiration in magazines or on social media, and choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.

What You Will Need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Hair cutting cape or towel
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before cutting. Comb your hair and remove any tangles. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up. Cover yourself with a hair cutting cape or towel.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want to cut your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to measure the length and mark it with a clip.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting your hair, starting from the back and working your way to the front. Use the comb to hold your hair in place and cut it at the desired length. Use your fingers to lift the hair and cut it at an angle for a messy look.

Step 4: Check the Length

After cutting each section, release it and check the length in the mirror. Adjust the length if needed.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Blend the layers by cutting the top layer shorter than the bottom layer. Use your fingers to lift the hair and cut it at an angle.

Step 6: Cut the Bangs

If you have bangs, comb them straight down and cut them at the desired length.

Step 7: Trim the Sides

Use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair for a cleaner look. Use a longer guard for a messy look.

Step 8: Finish with Detailing

Use scissors to detail the hair around the ears and neck.

Tips for Cutting Short Messy Hair

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut gradually to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a spray bottle to keep your hair damp and easier to cut.
  • Use scissors instead of clippers for a more natural, messy look.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as it may look uneven or choppy.
  • Take breaks and step away from the mirror to avoid overcutting.

FAQs

How often should I cut my short messy hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Can I cut my short messy hair myself?

Yes, cutting your short messy hair at home is possible with the right tools and techniques. However, if you're unsure or nervous, it's best to visit a professional hairstylist.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my short messy hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Hair grows back quickly, and you can always visit a professional hairstylist to fix the mistake.

Hair Care After Cutting Short Messy Hair

After cutting your short messy hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the style and health of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and avoid using too much heat or styling products. Visit a professional hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Cutting short messy hair can be a fun and creative experience. With the right tools, techniques, and inspiration, you can achieve a stylish and trendy new look. Follow our step-by-step guide and enjoy your new short and messy hairstyle.
