Men s Hairstyles Short On Sides Longer On Top A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is trendy, stylish, and low-maintenance? Look no further than the classic men's hairstyle short on sides longer on top. This versatile haircut is perfect for all hair types and can be customized to suit your unique style. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get the Men's Hairstyle Short on Sides Longer on Top
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

  5. Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

How to Get the Men's Hairstyle Short on Sides Longer on Top

The first step in getting this stylish haircut is to find a good stylist who has experience cutting men's hair. Once you have found a stylist you trust, show them a picture of the hairstyle you want, or describe it in detail. The basic idea is to keep the sides short while leaving the top longer, creating a contrast in length.

The length of the hair on the sides can vary depending on your preference. Many men opt for a fade, where the hair gradually gets shorter from the temple to the nape of the neck. Others prefer a more drastic contrast, with a buzz cut or undercut on the sides.

Once you have the basic cut, you can customize it to suit your style. You can add texture with a razor or scissors, or keep it slick and polished with a comb and pomade.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you have your new haircut, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect short on sides longer on top hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Pomade, wax, or clay are the best options for this style.
  3. Using a comb, comb your hair back and to the side, creating volume on the top.
  4. Use your fingers to tousle the hair on top, creating texture.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray or grooming spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you have your new hairstyle, you want to make sure it looks great every day. Here are some tips for maintaining your short on sides longer on top hairstyle:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs About Men's Hairstyles Short on Sides Longer on Top

Q: Is the short on sides longer on top hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit any face shape. Your stylist can help you choose the best length and style for your face.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut to maintain this style?

A: It's recommended to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for trims and touch-ups.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for formal events?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. For a formal event, consider slicking your hair back or parting it to the side for a more polished look.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you're well on your way to achieving the perfect men's hairstyle short on sides longer on top. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a messy, textured style, this haircut is sure to turn heads and keep you looking stylish and trendy.
