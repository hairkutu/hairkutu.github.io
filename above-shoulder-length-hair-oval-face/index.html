Above Shoulder Length Hair For Oval Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're lucky! This is considered the most versatile face shape, and almost any hairstyle will look good on you. However, if you're considering going for above shoulder length hair, there are still some things to keep in mind. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about above shoulder length hair for oval face shapes, including how to style it, what to avoid, and how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Above Shoulder Length Hair for Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Above Shoulder Length Hair

Description

When it comes to above shoulder length hair, there are a few things to keep in mind for an oval face shape. First, you want to make sure that your hair frames your face nicely, without adding too much volume at the sides. This can be achieved with layers that start at the chin and blend into the rest of your hair. You also want to consider your hair texture â if you have fine hair, a blunt cut may not be the best choice, as it can make your hair look flat.

How to Style Above Shoulder Length Hair for Oval Face

When it comes to styling your above shoulder length hair, the possibilities are endless. Here are a few ideas:

Beachy Waves

Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves throughout your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to add volume and definition.

Sleek and Straight

For a chic and polished look, straighten your hair with a flat iron. Apply a heat protectant spray before styling to prevent damage.

Braids

Braids are a great way to add some interest to your hair. Try a simple braid down the back, or a more intricate style like a fishtail or Dutch braid.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here's how to achieve the beachy waves look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Prep your hair with a heat protectant spray.
  3. Section your hair into manageable pieces.
  4. Curl each section with a curling iron or wand.
  5. Once all sections are curled, use your fingers to gently separate the curls.
  6. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Look

Here are some tips to keep your above shoulder length hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and avoid split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage over time.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage.

FAQs

What face shape is best for above shoulder length hair?

Almost any face shape can pull off above shoulder length hair, but it's especially flattering for oval face shapes.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail with above shoulder length hair?

Yes, you can! A low ponytail or a messy bun can look great with this length of hair.

What's the best way to add volume to my above shoulder length hair?

Try using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo at the roots to add volume and lift.

Hair Care Tips for Above Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can be damaging.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock above shoulder length hair with confidence!
