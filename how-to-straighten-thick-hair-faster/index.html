How To Straighten Thick Hair Faster

Written by Christine Apr 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have thick hair that takes forever to straighten? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with straightening thick hair, but there are some tips and tricks you can use to make the process faster and easier. In this article, we'll go over some of the best ways to straighten thick hair quickly and efficiently. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Straighten Thick Hair Faster
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips for Straightening Thick Hair

How to Straighten Thick Hair Faster

The first step to straightening thick hair faster is to use the right tools. A high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates is essential for achieving smooth, straight hair. You'll also want to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair from the heat of the flat iron.

Another tip for straightening thick hair faster is to work in small sections. Trying to straighten large sections of hair can be time-consuming and may not give you the results you're looking for. Instead, divide your hair into small sections and work on one section at a time.

Finally, you can speed up the straightening process by blow-drying your hair before using the flat iron. This will help to remove excess moisture and make your hair easier to straighten.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to straightening thick hair faster:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual, using products designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the flat iron.
  4. Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.
  5. Starting with the bottom section, use a blow dryer to dry your hair until it is about 80% dry.
  6. Using a flat iron with ceramic plates, work on one small section of hair at a time. Clamp the flat iron down on the hair near the roots and slowly pull it down to the ends.
  7. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working from the bottom to the top of your head.
  8. Once you have straightened all of your hair, apply a small amount of hair serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

Tips for Straightening Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips for straightening thick hair:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates to achieve the best results.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before using the flat iron.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat of the flat iron.
  • Work in small sections to achieve the best results.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about straightening thick hair:

  • Is it better to straighten thick hair when it's wet or dry? It's best to straighten thick hair when it's dry to avoid damage and achieve the best results.
  • How often should I straighten my thick hair? It's best to limit the amount of heat styling you do on your hair to avoid damage. Try to straighten your hair no more than once a week.
  • What's the best way to prevent frizz when straightening thick hair? Using a hair serum can help to prevent frizz and add shine to your hair.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your thick hair healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your thick hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can straighten your thick hair faster and more efficiently. Remember to use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates, work in small sections, and use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve smooth, straight hair in no time!
