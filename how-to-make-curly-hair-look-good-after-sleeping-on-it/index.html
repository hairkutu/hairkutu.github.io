How To Make Curly Hair Look Good After Sleeping On It

Written by Frank Aug 16, 2023 · 8 min read

Waking up with curly hair that looks like a mess can be a common problem for many people. However, there are ways to make your hair look good even after sleeping on it. In this article, we’ll discuss the best tips and tricks for taming your curly hair in the morning and achieving a beautiful, defined, and healthy look. We’ll cover everything from how to refresh your curls to how to protect them while sleeping. So, let's get started!

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The best way to make your curly hair look good after sleeping on it is to refresh it in the morning. This can be done in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Firstly, wet your hair by using a spray bottle or running it under the shower. This will help to activate your curls and refresh your hair.

Step 2: Apply Leave-in Conditioner

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will add moisture and help to define your curls. Use a small amount of leave-in conditioner and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair using a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. This will help to remove excess water and enhance your curls. Avoid using a regular towel as it can cause frizz and damage your curls.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair that suits your hair type and desired look. This can be a gel, mousse, or cream. Use a small amount of product and distribute it evenly throughout your hair. Scrunch your hair again to ensure the product is evenly distributed.

Step 5: Diffuse Your Hair

Finally, diffuse your hair using a hairdryer with a diffuser attachment. This will help to dry your hair gently and enhance your curls. Use a low heat and low speed setting to avoid damaging your curls.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you refresh your curly hair in the morning:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Step 2: Apply Leave-in Conditioner

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product that suits your hair type and desired look. Distribute it evenly and scrunch your hair again.

Step 5: Diffuse Your Hair

Diffuse your hair using a hairdryer with a diffuser attachment on a low heat and low speed setting.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your curly hair look good after sleeping on it:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and protect your curls while sleeping.
  • Pineapple your hair before sleeping. This involves gathering your hair at the top of your head and securing it with a scrunchie or hair tie. This will help to protect your curls and maintain their shape while sleeping.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair in the morning as this can cause frizz and damage your curls. Instead, use your fingers to gently separate your curls.
  • Use a hair oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you use styling products. Generally, washing your curly hair once or twice a week is enough to keep it healthy and clean.

Q: Can I use a regular towel to dry my curly hair?

A: No, it’s best to avoid using a regular towel as it can cause frizz and damage your curls. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to scrunch your hair.

Q: What is the best styling product for curly hair?

A: The best styling product for curly hair depends on your hair type and desired look. However, a gel or cream can help to define your curls and control frizz.

Hair Care

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to add moisture and nourishment.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons as they can cause damage to your curls.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your curly hair look good even after sleeping on it. Remember to take care of your hair and use products that suit your hair type and desired look. With a little bit of effort and patience, you can achieve beautiful, defined, and healthy curls every day.
