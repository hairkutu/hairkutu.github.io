Best Haircuts For Oval Shaped Faces Tips Styles And Hair Care

Written by Luffy Mar 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for the best haircuts for oval shaped faces? Look no further! We have gathered some of the most popular and stylish haircuts for your oval shaped face that will make you stand out in the crowd. Whether you have long, short, curly, or straight hair, there is a perfect hairstyle for everyone. Here is a complete guide for you to choose the best haircut for your oval face shape.

Description of Oval Shaped Faces

Oval shaped faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape with balanced proportions. The forehead and jawline are well balanced, and the cheekbones are slightly wider than the forehead. This face shape is ideal for any haircut as most styles will suit oval shaped faces.

How to Determine if You Have an Oval Shaped Face?

To determine if you have an oval shaped face, measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If the measurements are almost equal, and your face has an elongated shape, then you have an oval shaped face.

Step by Step Guide: Best Haircuts for Oval Shaped Faces

1. Long Layers

Long layers are the perfect haircut for oval shaped faces. They add volume and texture to your hair, and the layers frame your face beautifully. You can either keep your hair straight or curl it for a more elegant look.

2. Pixie Cut

Pixie cuts are a bold and daring haircut for oval shaped faces. They highlight your cheekbones and jawline and give your face a more defined look. This haircut is perfect for those who want to make a statement.

3. Bob Cut

Bob cuts are a classic and timeless haircut for oval shaped faces. They are versatile and can be styled in different ways to suit your personality. You can choose a short bob or a longer bob, depending on your preference.

4. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a great way to add some personality to your haircut. They frame your face and draw attention to your eyes. This haircut is perfect for those who want to add some edge to their look.

5. Wavy Lob

A wavy lob is a stylish and trendy haircut for oval shaped faces. It is a longer version of the bob and has loose waves that add volume and texture to your hair. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low maintenance look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To maintain your haircut, it is essential to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution.

FAQs

1. Can oval shaped faces pull off any haircut?

Yes, oval shaped faces can pull off any haircut as long as it complements their face shape and personality.

2. What is the most popular haircut for oval shaped faces?

The most popular haircut for oval shaped faces is the long layered haircut.

3. Can oval shaped faces have bangs?

Yes, oval shaped faces can have bangs. Side swept bangs are a great option for oval shaped faces.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for your oval shaped face is easy if you know what to look for. From long layers to a pixie cut, there are many options to choose from. Remember to maintain your haircut with a proper hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
