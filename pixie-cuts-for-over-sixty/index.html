Pixie Cuts For Over Sixty A Guide To A Stylish And Low Maintenance Look

Written by Eveline Jul 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are over sixty and looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, then a pixie cut may be the perfect option for you. Pixie cuts are short and sassy, and they can give you a youthful and vibrant look. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about pixie cuts for over sixty, including how to style them, how to care for them, and some common FAQs.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Get a Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut
  • FAQs about Pixie Cuts for Over Sixty
  • Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Description

Pixie cuts are short haircuts that are typically no longer than two inches in length. They are named after the mischievous and playful pixies of folklore, and they can be styled in many different ways. Pixie cuts are perfect for women over sixty who want a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style and looks great.

How to Get a Pixie Cut

To get a pixie cut, you should first find a stylist who has experience with short haircuts. You should also bring pictures of pixie cuts that you like to your appointment to help your stylist understand the look you are going for. Your stylist will then use scissors or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length.

Choosing the Right Pixie Cut for You

When choosing a pixie cut, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a pixie cut with longer layers to create the illusion of length. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a pixie cut with shorter layers to add volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut

Styling a pixie cut is easy and takes only a few minutes. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your pixie cut:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  5. Use a flat iron to smooth any frizz or flyaways.
  6. Apply a finishing serum or hairspray to set your style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Maintaining your pixie cut is easy and requires minimal effort. Here are some tips to keep your pixie cut looking great:

  • Get regular trims every four to six weeks to maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Use a dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as adding a side part or sweeping your bangs to the side.

FAQs about Pixie Cuts for Over Sixty

What face shapes are best suited for a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts are versatile and can be adapted to suit any face shape. However, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a pixie cut with longer layers to create the illusion of length. If you have a square face, you may want to choose a pixie cut with softer, more rounded edges to soften your features.

Can I color my hair if I have a pixie cut?

Yes, you can color your hair if you have a pixie cut. However, because pixie cuts are so short, the color will be more noticeable and will require more frequent touch-ups.

Can I wear a pixie cut if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use more product to tame your curls and create a smooth, sleek look.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Caring for your pixie cut is easy and requires minimal effort. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry or brittle.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, pixie cuts are a great option for women over sixty who want a low-maintenance and stylish haircut. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a youthful and vibrant look that is easy to maintain. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your pixie cut looking great and your hair healthy and strong.
