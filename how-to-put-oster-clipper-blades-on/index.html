How To Put Oster Clipper Blades On A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a barber or simply someone who likes to groom their own hair, you know the importance of having a good clipper. And one of the most essential parts of any clipper is the blade. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of putting Oster clipper blades on your clipper.

How to Put Oster Clipper Blades On

Before we dive into the steps, here's a quick list of the things you'll need:

  • Oster clipper blades
  • Your clipper
  • A cleaning brush
  • Lubricating oil

Step 1: Clean Your Clipper

Before you put on your new Oster clipper blade, it's important to clean your clipper. Use a cleaning brush to remove any hair or debris that may be stuck in the blades or around the clipper.

Step 2: Remove the Old Blade

If you're replacing an old blade, you'll need to remove it first. To do this, simply press down on the blade release button on your clipper and slide the blade off.

Step 3: Attach the New Blade

Take your new Oster clipper blade and slide it onto the clipper. Make sure it clicks into place and is securely attached.

Step 4: Lubricate the Blade

To ensure your new blade works smoothly and lasts longer, it's important to lubricate it. Apply a few drops of lubricating oil to the blade and turn on the clipper. Let it run for a few seconds to distribute the oil evenly.

Step 5: Test the Blade

Before you start using your clipper, it's important to test the blade to make sure it's working properly. Turn on the clipper and run it over your hand. The blade should cut smoothly and evenly.

Tips for Putting Oster Clipper Blades On

  • Always make sure your clipper is turned off before attaching or removing the blade.
  • Keep your clipper and blades clean to ensure they work properly and last longer.
  • Use a quality lubricating oil to keep your blades in good condition.
  • If you're having trouble attaching the new blade, double-check that you have the right size and type for your clipper.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace my Oster clipper blade?

It depends on how often you use your clipper and how well you maintain it. Generally, it's a good idea to replace your blade every 6-12 months or when it starts to show signs of wear and tear.

Can I use any Oster clipper blade on my clipper?

No, you'll need to make sure you have the right size and type of blade for your clipper. Check your clipper's manual or contact the manufacturer to find out which blades are compatible.

Hair Care with Oster Clipper Blades

Using Oster clipper blades can make grooming your hair easier and more efficient. However, it's important to take care of your hair as well. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair after using your clipper.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to put Oster clipper blades on your clipper with ease and keep your hair looking great.
