How To Cut Your Hair Pixie Style A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a dramatic change in your hairstyle, a pixie cut might be just the thing! Pixie cuts are short, low-maintenance hairstyles that can give you a fresh new look. Here's a step by step guide on how to cut your hair pixie style.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that's typically cut above the ears and close to the head. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be styled in a variety of ways. Pixie cuts can be very flattering on many face shapes and can be tailored to your personal style.

How to Cut Your Hair Pixie Style

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather some supplies. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start with the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up. Use your scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Work your way through the back section, cutting small sections of hair at a time.

Next, move on to the sides of your hair. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up. Cut the hair to your desired length. Work your way through the side sections, cutting small sections of hair at a time.

Finally, move on to the top section of your hair. Use your comb to lift a small section of hair straight up. Cut the hair to your desired length. Work your way through the top section, cutting small sections of hair at a time.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is even.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect pixie cut:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter. You can always cut more hair off, but you can't add it back once it's gone.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time. This will help you achieve a more even cut.
  • Use a comb to lift your hair straight up before cutting it. This will help ensure that your hair is cut evenly.
  • Consider going to a professional hairstylist if you're not confident in your ability to cut your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair pixie style if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can definitely cut your hair pixie style if you have curly hair. Just make sure to work with a hairstylist who has experience cutting curly hair.

Q: Will a pixie cut work with my face shape?

A: Pixie cuts can be very flattering on many face shapes. However, it's a good idea to consult with a hairstylist to find the best pixie cut for your face shape.

Q: How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: Pixie cuts require regular trims to maintain their shape. You should plan on getting your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your hair pixie style, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, which can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

Cutting your hair pixie style can be a fun and exciting change. By following these steps and taking good care of your hair, you can rock a fabulous pixie cut!
