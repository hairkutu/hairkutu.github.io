How Long Is Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Robby May 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Shoulder length hair is a popular hairstyle for many people, but how long is it exactly? In this article, we'll answer that question and provide some tips on how to take care of your shoulder length hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Measure Shoulder Length Hair
  3. Step by Step Guide to Measuring Shoulder Length Hair
  4. Tips for Taking Care of Shoulder Length Hair
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Description

Shoulder length hair is hair that falls to the shoulders when it is straight. This length is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. It is also a great length for those who want to try out different haircuts and styles without committing to a shorter or longer length. Shoulder length hair can be worn by people of all ages, genders, and hair types.

How to Measure Shoulder Length Hair

Measuring shoulder length hair is easy. Simply take a measuring tape and measure from the tip of your shoulder to the ends of your hair. The length you get is your shoulder length hair measurement.

What if my hair is curly?

If your hair is curly, it can be a bit more difficult to measure. The best way to get an accurate measurement is to straighten your hair first and then measure from the tip of your shoulder to the ends of your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Measuring Shoulder Length Hair

Here is a step by step guide to measuring your shoulder length hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would.
  2. Straighten your hair using a flat iron or blow dryer.
  3. Take a measuring tape and measure from the tip of your shoulder to the ends of your hair.
  4. The length you get is your shoulder length hair measurement.

Tips for Taking Care of Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your shoulder length hair:

  • Use shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is shoulder length hair the same length for everyone?

No, shoulder length hair can vary slightly depending on a person's height and build. However, it generally falls between 12-16 inches in length.

Can shoulder length hair be styled in different ways?

Yes, shoulder length hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can wear it straight, curly, or in a variety of updos and braids.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Proper hair care is important for keeping your shoulder length hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Blow dry your hair on a low or medium setting to prevent heat damage.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly as it can cause breakage and damage.

Now that you know how long shoulder length hair is and how to take care of it, you can rock this versatile and stylish hairstyle with confidence!
