How Do I Get My Hair To Stop Falling Out After Bleaching

Written by Christine Jun 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of seeing clumps of hair falling out after bleaching? Bleaching is a chemical process that can cause significant damage to your hair, leading to hair breakage and hair loss. However, if you take the necessary steps to care for your hair after bleaching, you can prevent further damage and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we will discuss how to get your hair to stop falling out after bleaching.

Table of Contents

  • How to Stop Hair from Falling Out After Bleaching
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Prevent Hair Loss After Bleaching
  • Tips for Healthy Hair After Bleaching
  • FAQs: Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Loss After Bleaching
  • Hair Care Products for Bleached Hair

How to Stop Hair from Falling Out After Bleaching

The first step to stop hair fall after bleaching is to take care of your hair. After bleaching, your hair becomes dry, fragile and prone to breakage. Therefore, you need to provide your hair with the necessary nourishment and moisture to prevent hair loss. Here are a few things you can do:

1. Use a Protein Treatment

Protein treatments are essential to strengthening your hair after bleaching. They help to rebuild the hair's structure and prevent further breakage. You can use a protein treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Deep Conditioning

Deep conditioning is crucial for bleached hair. It helps to moisturize and nourish your hair, preventing it from becoming dry and brittle. You can deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and strong.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can further damage your bleached hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers. If you must use heat styling, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and set the temperature to the lowest setting possible.

Step-by-Step Guide to Prevent Hair Loss After Bleaching

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to prevent hair loss after bleaching:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent further damage to your hair. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

Step 2: Apply a Protein Treatment

Apply a protein treatment to your hair to strengthen it and prevent further breakage. Leave the treatment on for 20-30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 3: Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair to moisturize and nourish it. Leave the conditioner on for 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers. If you must use heat styling, use a heat protectant spray and set the temperature to the lowest setting possible.

Step 5: Use a Hair Growth Treatment

Use a hair growth treatment to stimulate hair growth and prevent hair loss. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and vitamin E.

Tips for Healthy Hair After Bleaching

Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy after bleaching:

1. Trim Your Hair

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and prevent further breakage.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. Avoid using a brush, as it can cause further breakage.

3. Wear Protective Styles

Wear protective styles like braids, twists, and buns to protect your hair from further damage.

FAQs: Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Loss After Bleaching

1. Can hair loss from bleaching be reversed?

Yes, hair loss from bleaching can be reversed with proper hair care. You need to provide your hair with the necessary nourishment and moisture to prevent further hair loss.

2. How long does it take for hair to stop falling out after bleaching?

It can take several weeks for your hair to stop falling out after bleaching. However, with proper hair care, you can prevent further hair loss.

3. Can I bleach my hair again if it's falling out?

No, you should not bleach your hair again if it's falling out. Bleaching can cause significant damage to your hair, leading to hair breakage and hair loss. You need to take care of your hair and allow it to recover before bleaching it again.

Hair Care Products for Bleached Hair

Here are a few hair care products that are essential for bleached hair:

1. Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent further damage to your hair.

2. Protein Treatment

Use a protein treatment to strengthen your hair and prevent further breakage.

3. Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner to moisturize and nourish your hair.

4. Hair Growth Treatment

Use a hair growth treatment to stimulate hair growth and prevent hair loss.

By following these steps and using the right hair care products, you can stop your hair from falling out after bleaching. Remember to be patient, as it takes time for your hair to recover from the damage caused by bleaching. With proper hair care, you can promote healthy hair growth and prevent further hair loss.
