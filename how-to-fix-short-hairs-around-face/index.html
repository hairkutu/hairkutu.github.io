How To Fix Short Hairs Around Face A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have short hairs around your face and you're looking for ways to fix them, then you're in the right place. Short hairs can be frustrating, especially when they stick out and ruin your hairstyle. But don't worry, we've got you covered with this complete guide on how to fix short hairs around your face.

Description

Short hairs around the face are often the result of breakage, split ends, or new growth. They can be difficult to manage and can ruin the overall look of your hairstyle. In this guide, we'll show you how to fix short hairs around your face and prevent them from coming back.

How to Fix Short Hairs Around Face

If you're wondering how to fix short hairs around your face, there are several ways to do it. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Use a Toothbrush

One of the easiest ways to fix short hairs around your face is to use a toothbrush. Spray some hairspray on the toothbrush and gently brush the short hairs in the direction you want them to go. This will help them stay in place and blend in with the rest of your hair.

2. Try Hair Wax

Hair wax is another great option for fixing short hairs around your face. Apply a small amount of hair wax to your fingertips and gently work it into the short hairs. This will help smooth them down and keep them in place.

3. Use a Flat Iron

If you have a flat iron, you can use it to fix short hairs around your face. Simply clamp the flat iron onto the short hairs and gently pull it down in the direction you want them to go. This will help smooth them out and keep them in place.

4. Trim Them

If your short hairs are too long to manage, you may need to trim them. Use a pair of sharp scissors and carefully trim the short hairs to the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven results.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know how to fix short hairs around your face, here's a step by step guide to help you get the best results:

Step 1: Clean Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help remove any buildup or dirt that may be weighing down your hair and making it difficult to manage.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help create a smooth base for fixing your short hairs.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a small amount of hairspray, hair wax, or serum to your hair. This will help hold your hairstyle in place and make it easier to manage your short hairs.

Step 4: Fix Your Short Hairs

Use a toothbrush, flat iron, or scissors to fix your short hairs. Choose the method that works best for your hair type and the length of your short hairs.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your style in place and prevent your short hairs from sticking out.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you fix short hairs around your face:

  • Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair
  • Be gentle when fixing your short hairs to avoid breakage and damage
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while you sleep
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage

FAQ

Q: Can I use a toothbrush to fix short hairs on my hairline?

A: Yes, a toothbrush is a great tool for fixing short hairs on your hairline. Simply spray some hairspray on the toothbrush and brush the short hairs in the direction you want them to go.

Q: Can I use hair wax on wet hair?

A: It's best to use hair wax on dry hair. Applying wax to wet hair can dilute the product and make it less effective.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to prevent short hairs and other hair problems. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair after shampooing to keep it hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to support healthy hair growth

With these tips and techniques, you can easily fix short hairs around your face and achieve a smooth, polished hairstyle. Remember to be gentle and take good care of your hair to prevent future hair problems.
