Men s Haircut For Curly Hair 2020 A Guide

Written by Frank May 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. It can give you a unique and distinctive look, but it can also be difficult to manage and style. In 2020, we saw a lot of new trends and styles for men's haircut for curly hair. In this article, we will discuss how to get the perfect haircut for your curly hair, step by step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care. Let's dive in!

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Men's haircut for curly hair in 2020 was all about embracing natural curls and texture. This year, we saw a lot of short and medium-length haircuts that emphasized the natural volume and bounce of curly hair. Some of the most popular styles include the curly fringe, messy curls, and undercut with textured curls.

When it comes to haircuts for curly hair, it's important to find a stylist who understands the unique needs of curly hair. Curly hair requires a different approach than straight hair, and a stylist who specializes in curly hair will know how to work with your hair's natural texture.

How to

If you're thinking about getting a new haircut for your curly hair, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Look for inspiration: Before you go to the salon, do some research and find some styles that you like. Look for pictures of men with similar hair texture and face shape as you.
  • Find a good stylist: As mentioned earlier, finding a stylist who specializes in curly hair is important. Look for reviews and ask for recommendations from friends or family members.
  • Communicate with your stylist: When you're at the salon, make sure to communicate your preferences and concerns with your stylist. They can give you suggestions based on your hair type and face shape.

Step by step

Here are the steps to getting a men's haircut for curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair: Make sure to wash your hair before your appointment. This will help your stylist to see your hair's natural texture.
  2. Consult with your stylist: Talk to your stylist about your preferences and what kind of style you're looking for.
  3. Trim your hair: Your stylist will start by trimming your hair to remove any split ends and to create a shape that complements your face.
  4. Style your hair: Your stylist will then style your hair using products that enhance your natural curls and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your curly hair looking great:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair: A diffuser will help to enhance your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Don't wash your hair every day: Curly hair tends to be dry, so avoid washing it every day. Instead, wash it every 2-3 days and use a co-wash in between.
  • Use products designed for curly hair: Look for products that are specifically formulated for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing creams, gels, and leave-in conditioners.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircut for curly hair:

Q: What are some popular styles for men's haircut for curly hair in 2020?

A: Some of the most popular styles include the curly fringe, messy curls, and undercut with textured curls.

Q: Do I need to use a special shampoo for curly hair?

A: Yes, it's a good idea to use a shampoo that's specifically designed for curly hair. These shampoos are formulated to moisturize and define curls, while also preventing frizz.

Q: Can I still straighten my hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair if you have curly hair. However, it's important to use a heat protectant spray and to avoid using the highest heat setting on your straightener.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Moisturize your hair: Curly hair tends to be dry, so make sure to use a moisturizing conditioner and leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage curly hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser to enhance your natural curls.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: The sun's rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

With these tips and tricks, you can get the perfect men's haircut for curly hair in 2020 and keep your curls looking great all year round!
