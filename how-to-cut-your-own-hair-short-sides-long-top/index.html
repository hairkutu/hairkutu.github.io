How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Sides Long Top In Relaxed English Language

If you're looking for a trendy hairstyle that's easy to maintain, you might want to try the short sides long top haircut. This hairstyle has been popular for a while and it's not going away anytime soon. Instead of going to a salon, you can save money and cut your own hair at home. This article will give you step-by-step instructions on how to cut your own hair short sides long top in a relaxed English language.

Description

The short sides long top haircut is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. The sides are trimmed short, while the top is left longer. This creates a contrast that can be styled in many different ways. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance look that's still stylish.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Sides Long Top

Cutting your own hair can be a bit intimidating, especially if you're not used to it. However, with a bit of patience and practice, you can achieve a great short sides long top haircut. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air-dry. This will make it easier to cut and style.

2. Use clippers to trim the sides of your hair. Start with the highest guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the sides are even.

3. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair. Start with small sections and work your way across your head. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut in a straight line. Keep the length longer at the front and gradually taper it towards the back.

4. Blend the sides and the top together by using scissors to cut the hair around the crown of your head. Make sure the transition is smooth and even.

5. Style your hair as desired using pomade or gel. You can experiment with different styles, such as a slicked-back look or a messy quiff.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

- Invest in a good pair of scissors and clippers. This will make the process easier and give you better results.

- Take your time and don't rush the process. It's better to cut a little bit at a time than to take off too much.

- Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure your hair is even.

- If you're not sure about a certain technique, watch tutorials online or ask a friend for help.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair short sides long top with just scissors?

A: It's possible, but it will be more difficult to achieve the desired look. Clippers are better for cutting the sides short, while scissors are better for blending the top and sides together.

Q: How often should I cut my hair short sides long top?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style starts to look messy. Generally, you should aim to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair short sides long top, you can experiment with different styles and save money on salon visits. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and have fun with it!
