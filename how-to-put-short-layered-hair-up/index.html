How To Put Short Layered Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 12, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of leaving your short layered hair down all the time? Putting your hair up can be a great way to switch up your look and keep your hair out of your face. However, if you have short, layered hair, you may be wondering how to put it up without it looking messy or falling out. In this guide, we’ll show you how to put short layered hair up in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Put Short Layered Hair Up
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Putting Short Layered Hair Up
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Short Layered Hair

How to Put Short Layered Hair Up

There are several ways to put short layered hair up, depending on the length of your hair and the style you want to achieve. Here are a few popular options:

  • Ponytail
  • Bun
  • Half-up, half-down
  • Braids

In this guide, we’ll focus on the ponytail and bun styles.

Bun

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know a few ways to put short layered hair up, let’s go through the steps for each style.

Ponytail Steps

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and hold it with one hand.
  3. With your other hand, use a hair tie to secure your hair in a ponytail.
  4. If you have any shorter layers that won’t stay in the ponytail, use bobby pins to secure them in place.

Bun Steps

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Twist your ponytail around the hair tie to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to tuck in any loose ends.

Tips for Putting Short Layered Hair Up

Putting short layered hair up can be a bit tricky, but these tips can help:

  • Use bobby pins to secure any shorter layers that won’t stay in the ponytail or bun.
  • Use hair elastics that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Tease your hair at the crown before putting it up to add volume.
  • Add a decorative hair clip or headband to dress up your ponytail or bun.

FAQs

Can you put short layered hair in a bun?

Yes, you can put short layered hair in a bun. To do this, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

How do you keep short layered hair in a ponytail?

To keep short layered hair in a ponytail, use bobby pins to secure any shorter layers that won’t stay in the ponytail. You can also try using a hair tie and wrapping it around the ponytail several times to make it more secure.

Hair Care for Short Layered Hair

Short layered hair can be a fun and versatile style, but it does require some maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.
  • Use a lightweight, volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Consider using a texturizing spray to add some definition to your layers.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily put your short layered hair up in a variety of styles. Whether you’re going for a casual or formal look, there’s a perfect updo for every occasion.
