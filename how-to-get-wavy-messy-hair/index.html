How To Get Wavy Messy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 03, 2023 · 7 min read
You’ve seen it on Instagram, Pinterest, and all over the red carpet. The wavy, messy hair look is everywhere, and for good reason – it’s effortless, chic, and suits almost everyone. But how do you achieve those perfectly imperfect waves? In this post, we’ll take you through the steps to get wavy messy hair, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Wavy Messy Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for the Perfect Wavy Messy Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Wavy Messy Hair

How to Get Wavy Messy Hair

Wavy messy hair is all about creating a natural, undone look. To achieve this, you’ll need to start with the right products and tools. Here’s what you’ll need:
  • Texturizing spray
  • Hair oil or serum
  • Curling wand or iron
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your products and tools ready, let’s get started on creating those perfect waves:
  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry about 80%. You don’t want it to be soaking wet, as this can make it harder to curl.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. This will help give your hair that messy, undone look.
  3. Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to help protect them from the heat of the curling wand.
  4. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling wand, holding for about 5-8 seconds. Don’t worry about making perfect curls – the messier, the better.
  5. Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through the curls and break them up. This will give you that effortless, beachy look.
  6. Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for the Perfect Wavy Messy Hair

- Experiment with different curling wand sizes to find the perfect wave for your hair length and texture. - Don’t worry about making every curl perfect – the beauty of wavy messy hair is in the imperfections. - Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair before and after curling. - If you have naturally wavy or curly hair, you can skip the curling wand and simply enhance your natural texture with a texturizing spray and a little scrunching. - Don’t be afraid to play around with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve wavy messy hair with short hair? A: Absolutely! Just use a smaller curling wand or iron and focus on curling the ends of your hair. Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain this style? A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Generally, you can go 2-3 days between washes and refresh your waves with a little texturizing spray. Q: Can I achieve this look without using heat? A: You can try braiding your hair while it’s damp and leaving it to dry overnight for a similar effect.

Hair Care for Wavy Messy Hair

To keep your wavy messy hair looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips: - Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. - Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy. - Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair. - Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your waves and make them look greasy. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and help your waves look bouncier and healthier. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect wavy messy hair look in no time. Remember, the key is to embrace the imperfections and have fun with it!
