How To Get The Wet Look Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 02, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a sleek and sexy hairstyle that's perfect for a night out, then the wet look hairstyle might be just what you need. This edgy yet sophisticated style has been a favorite of celebrities and fashion icons alike, and it's surprisingly easy to achieve. In this article, we'll show you how to get the wet look hairstyle in just a few simple steps.

Description

The wet look hairstyle is a popular trend that involves styling your hair to look wet, shiny, and slicked back. It's a great way to add a touch of glamour to your look, and it's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're going out for a night on the town or you just want to add some edge to your everyday look, the wet look hairstyle is a great choice.

How to Get the Wet Look Hairstyle

Getting the wet look hairstyle is actually quite simple. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

The first step to achieving the wet look hairstyle is to start with clean, damp hair. You can either wash your hair and let it air dry, or you can wet it down with a spray bottle. Make sure your hair is damp, but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Apply a styling gel or pomade

Next, you'll need to apply a styling gel or pomade to your hair. This will help to give your hair that slick, wet look. Be sure to use a product that's specifically designed for this type of hairstyle, as other products may not give you the desired effect.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a comb, comb your hair back away from your face. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair as you comb it back. You may want to use a hair dryer on a low setting to help set the style in place.

Step 4: Finish with a shine spray

Finally, finish off your wet look hairstyle by using a shine spray. This will help to give your hair a glossy, polished finish. Be sure to hold the spray bottle a few inches away from your head to avoid over-saturating your hair.

Tips for Getting the Wet Look Hairstyle

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect wet look hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality styling gel or pomade for the best results.
  • Don't overdo it with the product – a little goes a long way.
  • Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  • Experiment with different shine sprays to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the wet look hairstyle:

Q: Can I achieve the wet look hairstyle with short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve the wet look hairstyle with short hair. Simply apply a small amount of product to your hair and comb it back.

Q: Will the wet look hairstyle damage my hair?

A: No, the wet look hairstyle shouldn't damage your hair if you use the right products and don't overdo it with the styling.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the wet look hairstyle with confidence and style. Whether you're going out on the town or just need a quick and easy hairstyle for everyday wear, the wet look is a great choice. So go ahead and try it out – you might just fall in love with this edgy and sophisticated style!
