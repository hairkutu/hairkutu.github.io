Description

Men's hairstyle short sides longer top has been a popular choice among men for years. This style features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on the top. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many ways, making it perfect for any occasion. It's also easy to maintain and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, this style can work for you.

How to Style

Styling men's hairstyle short sides longer top is relatively easy. To achieve this style, you will need a good quality hair clipper, scissors, and a comb. Start by trimming the hair on the sides and back with the hair clipper. Then, use scissors to cut the hair on top to your desired length. You can leave the hair longer on the crown and shorter on the front, or vice versa. The key is to create a contrast between the short sides and the longer top.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style men's hairstyle short sides longer top:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to make it clean and easy to manage.
  2. Use a towel to dry your hair and remove any excess water.
  3. Start trimming the hair on the sides and back of your head with a hair clipper. Use a number 2 or 3 guard to keep the hair short.
  4. Use scissors to cut the hair on top to your desired length. Keep the hair longer on the crown and shorter on the front.
  5. Use a comb to style your hair. You can slick it back, create a part, or leave it messy and textured.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add shine and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintenance

To maintain men's hairstyle short sides longer top, you should trim the hair on the sides and back every 2-3 weeks. You can also trim the hair on top if it starts to get too long. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hairstyle short sides longer top:

  • Is this style suitable for all face shapes? Yes, this style can be adapted to suit different face shapes. Talk to your hairstylist to find the best version of this style for your face.
  • What hair types work best for this style? This style works best for straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you may need to use more hair product to keep it under control.
  • Can I style this hairdo for formal occasions? Yes, this style can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Use a comb to create a slicked-back look for formal events.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking great, you should follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. You should also avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it. If you use a hair dryer, make sure to use a low heat setting. Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair gently. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as it can cause breakage.

Men's hairstyle short sides longer top is a timeless look that never goes out of style. It's perfect for any occasion and can be styled in many ways. With a little bit of maintenance and care, you can keep your hair looking great all year round. So, why not give this style a try and see how it can transform your look?
