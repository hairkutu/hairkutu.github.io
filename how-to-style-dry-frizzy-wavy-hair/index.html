How To Style Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Wavy hair is beautiful, but when it's dry and frizzy, it can be a challenge to style. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling dry frizzy wavy hair. We'll cover how to style it, step by step instructions, some tips to make it easier, frequently asked questions, and finally, some hair care tips to help prevent dry and frizzy hair in the first place.

How to Style Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair

The first step to styling dry frizzy wavy hair is to start with a good wash and conditioning routine. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for dry, frizzy hair to help add moisture and manageability. Once you've washed and conditioned your hair, you can follow these simple steps to style it:

  1. Towel dry your hair gently. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel, as this can cause more frizz.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or moisturizing cream to your hair. This will help to add moisture and manageability to your hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Be gentle and start at the ends, working your way up to the roots.
  4. Apply a curl defining cream or gel to your hair. This will help to enhance your natural waves and reduce frizz.
  5. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your waves to form. Avoid using a brush or comb, as this can cause more frizz.
  6. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help to reduce frizz and enhance your waves.

Once your hair is dry, you can finger comb it to add volume and shape. You can also use a light hold hairspray to help keep your waves in place.

Step by Step Instructions

If you're more of a visual learner, here's a step by step guide to styling dry frizzy wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, wet hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or moisturizing cream to your hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  4. Apply a curl defining cream or gel to your hair.
  5. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your waves to form.
  6. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  7. Finger comb your hair to add volume and shape.
  8. Use a light hold hairspray to help keep your waves in place.

Tips to Make it Easier

Styling dry frizzy wavy hair can be a challenge, but here are some tips to make it easier:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause more frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or old t-shirt to dry your hair, as they are gentler on your hair and cause less frizz.
  • Apply products to your hair when it's still wet, as this will help to distribute the product evenly and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause more frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to help reduce frizz and prevent breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I wash my dry frizzy wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. In general, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use a hair mask on my dry frizzy wavy hair?

A: Yes, using a hair mask once a week can help to add moisture and manageability to your hair.

Q: Can I brush my dry frizzy wavy hair?

A: It's best to avoid brushing your hair, as this can cause more frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Preventing dry and frizzy hair starts with a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help keep your hair healthy and hydrated:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for dry, frizzy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your dry frizzy wavy hair with ease. Remember to be gentle with your hair, avoid using hot tools, and use products specifically formulated for dry, frizzy hair. With a little patience and practice, you can achieve beautiful, healthy, and manageable wavy hair.
