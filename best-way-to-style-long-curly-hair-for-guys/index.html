The Best Way To Style Long Curly Hair For Guys

Written by Oliver Jun 10, 2023 · 8 min read

Long curly hair can be a challenge, but it can also be a blessing if styled correctly. In this article, we will go over the best way to style long curly hair for guys. From how to, step by step, tips, FAQ, and hair care, we’ve got you covered. Read on to find out more.

How to Style Long Curly Hair for Guys

The first step to styling long curly hair is to identify your hair type. This will help you determine which products to use and what style will work best for you. Once you know your hair type, you can start experimenting with different styles.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo that is designed for curly hair. This will help to keep your hair clean and moisturized, which is essential for healthy hair. After washing, apply a conditioner that is designed for curly hair. This will help to detangle your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply Products

Apply a styling product to your hair. This can be a gel, mousse, or cream. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair to ensure that your curls are well defined. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to style your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up to the top. This will help to avoid tangles and knots. Once you have styled your hair, let it air dry or use a diffuser to dry it.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Long Curly Hair for Guys

Step 1: Identify Your Hair Type

The first step to styling long curly hair is to identify your hair type. This will help you determine which products to use and what style will work best for you. There are three types of curly hair: wavy, curly, and kinky. Wavy hair has loose curls, curly hair has tighter curls, and kinky hair has tight, coiled curls.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo that is designed for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair clean and moisturized, which is essential for healthy hair. After washing, apply a conditioner that is designed for your hair type. This will help to detangle your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 3: Apply Products

Apply a styling product to your hair. This can be a gel, mousse, or cream. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair to ensure that your curls are well defined. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair for Guys

Here are some tips for styling long curly hair for guys:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid tangles and knots.
  • Apply product evenly throughout your hair to ensure that your curls are well defined.
  • Avoid using a brush as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser to dry your hair to avoid damage.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs About Styling Long Curly Hair for Guys

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and how often you style it. Generally, it is recommended to wash your hair every two to three days.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it is important to use a heat protectant to avoid damage.

Q: How do I prevent frizz?

A: Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush, and avoid touching your hair too much.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair for Guys

Here are some tips for hair care for long curly hair for guys:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can dry out your hair.
  • Avoid using a brush as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.

In conclusion, styling long curly hair for guys can be a challenge, but it is also an opportunity to experiment with different styles. By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve healthy and well-defined curls.
