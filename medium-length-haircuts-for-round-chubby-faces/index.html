Medium Length Haircuts For Round Chubby Faces

Written by Daniel Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of constantly searching for the perfect haircut that flatters your round, chubby face? Look no further, because we have compiled a list of the best medium length haircuts that will make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve these haircuts, as well as tips on how to maintain them.

Description

Medium length haircuts are perfect for those who want to embrace their round, chubby faces. These haircuts typically fall between the shoulders and collarbone, and can be styled in a variety of ways. They are also great for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair.

How to

Before getting a medium length haircut, it is important to consult with your hairstylist to determine which style will work best for your face shape. Once you have decided on a haircut, follow these steps to achieve the look:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair.
  4. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or hairspray, to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the most popular medium length haircuts for round, chubby faces:

The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a popular medium length haircut that falls just above the shoulders. It is a great haircut for those with round, chubby faces because it elongates the face and draws attention away from the cheeks. Here is how to achieve the lob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair in the middle.
  3. Use a straightener to create loose waves.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to add volume.

The Shag

The shag is a layered medium length haircut that is perfect for those with round, chubby faces because it adds texture and movement to the hair. Here is how to achieve the shag:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair in the middle.
  3. Use a razor to create layers throughout your hair.
  4. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or hairspray, to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips on how to maintain your medium length haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about medium length haircuts for round, chubby faces:

What is the best medium length haircut for a round, chubby face?

The lob and shag are both great medium length haircuts for round, chubby faces because they add texture and movement to the hair.

How do I style my medium length haircut?

You can style your medium length haircut by using hot styling tools, such as a curling iron or straightener, and applying a styling product, such as mousse or hairspray, to hold the style in place.

How often should I visit my hairstylist to maintain my medium length haircut?

You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your medium length haircut.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy, shiny locks. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these medium length haircuts, tips, and hair care advice, you'll be sure to find the perfect style for your round, chubby face. Embrace your natural beauty and feel confident in your new haircut!
