Short Hairstyle Make You Look Younger How To Achieve It

Written by Aprilia Aug 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair has always been a popular choice among women of all ages. However, did you know that a short hairstyle can also make you look younger? Yes, that's right! In this article, we will share with you how a short hairstyle can help you achieve a youthful look, how to achieve it, step by step, tips, FAQ, and hair care. Read on to find out more.

Table of Contents

  • Why Short Hairstyle Makes You Look Younger
  • How to Achieve a Youthful Look with Short Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Why Short Hairstyle Makes You Look Younger

A short hairstyle can make you look younger for several reasons. Firstly, it can give the illusion of a smaller face, which is associated with youthfulness. Secondly, it can make your hair look thicker and fuller, which is also a sign of youthfulness. Finally, it can highlight your facial features, which can make you look more vibrant and fresh.

How to Achieve a Youthful Look with Short Hair

If you want to achieve a youthful look with short hair, there are several things you can do. Firstly, choose a short hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture. Secondly, opt for a haircut that highlights your best features, such as your cheekbones or eyes. Finally, consider adding some layers or texture to your hair to give it more volume and movement.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to achieve a youthful look with short hair:

  1. Choose a short hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Opt for a haircut that highlights your best features, such as your cheekbones or eyes.
  3. Add some layers or texture to your hair to give it more volume and movement.
  4. Use styling products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and definition to your hair.
  5. Style your hair in a way that complements your face shape and highlights your best features.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles:

Q: Will a short hairstyle suit me?

A: Short hair can suit most face shapes and hair types. However, it's important to choose a style that complements your features and hair texture.

Q: Will a short hairstyle make me look younger?

A: Yes, a short hairstyle can make you look younger by giving the illusion of a smaller face, making your hair look thicker and fuller, and highlighting your facial features.

Q: How often should I get my short hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your short hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Now that you know how a short hairstyle can make you look younger, why not try it out for yourself? Remember to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture, and follow our tips for maintaining and caring for your short hair.
