How To Wear A Baseball Cap With Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Baseball caps are a great accessory for any casual outfit, but for girls with curly hair, it can be a challenge to wear one without ruining their curls. In this article, we will show you how to wear a baseball cap girl with curly hair, step by step. From choosing the right cap to styling your hair, we have got you covered.

Table of Content

  • Prep Your Hair
  • Put on the Cap
  • Style Your Hair
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Choose the Right Cap

When it comes to choosing the right baseball cap, you want to look for one that fits your head snugly but not too tightly. A cap that is too tight can cause your curls to flatten, while a cap that is too loose can make your hair look messy. Look for caps that have an adjustable strap or snapback closure so you can adjust the size to fit your head perfectly.

Also, consider the material of the cap. A cotton or wool cap can cause your hair to frizz, so it's best to choose a cap made of silk or satin. These materials will not only protect your curls but also add a touch of elegance to your outfit.

Prep Your Hair

Before putting on your cap, you want to prep your hair to ensure it stays in shape all day. Start by applying a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to lock in moisture and prevent frizz. Then, use a diffuser on your hairdryer to dry your curls gently. This will help to maintain the shape of your curls and prevent them from getting flat.

Put on the Cap

Now that your hair is prepped, it's time to put on the cap. Start by tilting the cap slightly to the back of your head to avoid flattening your curls. Then, gently pull your hair through the back of the cap, making sure to tuck any loose strands inside the cap. Adjust the cap to your liking, and you're good to go.

Style Your Hair

If you want to add a touch of style to your hair while wearing a baseball cap, there are a few options. One is to create a half-up, half-down hairstyle by pulling the top half of your hair into a ponytail or bun. This will not only show off your curls but also add some volume to your hair.

Another option is to wear your hair in a side braid or low ponytail. These styles will keep your hair out of your face and prevent it from getting tangled under the cap.

Tips and Tricks

  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair inside the cap.
  • Choose a cap with a wide brim to protect your face from the sun.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Can I wear a baseball cap with wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to wear a baseball cap with wet hair as it can cause your hair to become flat and frizzy.

How do I prevent my curls from getting flattened under the cap?

Start by tilting the cap slightly to the back of your head and pulling your hair through the back of the cap. This will help to maintain the shape of your curls.

What can I do if my hair gets tangled under the cap?

Try wearing your hair in a side braid or low ponytail to keep it from getting tangled. You can also use a detangling spray or comb to remove any knots.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair to keep your curls moisturized and frizz-free. Avoid using heat tools on your hair, and instead, air dry your curls or use a diffuser on your hairdryer.

By following these tips, you can wear a baseball cap girl with curly hair and look stylish and chic all day long.
