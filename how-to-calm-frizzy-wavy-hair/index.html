How To Calm Frizzy Wavy Hair In 2023 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy, unruly hair every day? Do you struggle to style your wavy hair because it always seems to have a mind of its own? If so, you're not alone. Many people with wavy or curly hair struggle to tame their locks, especially when humidity levels are high or the weather is unpredictable. Fortunately, there are several steps you can take to calm frizzy wavy hair and achieve the sleek, smooth look you desire. In this guide, we'll provide you with all the tips, tricks, and techniques you need to master the art of taming frizzy wavy hair.

How to Calm Frizzy Wavy Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step in calming frizzy wavy hair is to use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates are harsh detergents that can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry, brittle, and prone to frizz. Instead, look for a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated specifically for wavy or curly hair. These products will help to hydrate your hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and hydrate your hair. Look for a leave-in conditioner that is designed for wavy or curly hair, as these products are formulated to help define your curls and reduce frizz. Apply the conditioner to damp hair, focusing on the ends, and then comb through with a wide-toothed comb.

Step 3: Use a Microfiber Towel or T-Shirt to Dry Your Hair

Instead of using a regular towel to dry your hair, use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt. Regular towels can be too harsh on your hair and can cause frizz and breakage. Microfiber towels and t-shirts are gentler on your hair and will help to absorb excess water without causing damage.

Step 4: Apply a Frizz-Fighting Serum or Oil

To help reduce frizz and add shine to your hair, apply a frizz-fighting serum or oil. Look for a product that is lightweight and non-greasy, as heavy oils can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Apply the serum or oil to the ends of your hair, and then work your way up to the roots.

Step 5: Use a Diffuser Attachment on Your Hair Dryer

To help define your curls and reduce frizz, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. A diffuser attachment helps to distribute heat evenly and gently, which can help to reduce frizz and enhance your natural curls.

Step 6: Avoid Heat-Styling Tools

Heat-styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can be damaging to your hair, causing it to become dry, brittle, and prone to frizz. Instead of using these tools, embrace your natural texture and work with what you've got.

Tips for Calming Frizzy Wavy Hair

In addition to the steps outlined above, there are several other tips and tricks you can use to calm frizzy wavy hair. Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair

Frequently Asked Questions About Calming Frizzy Wavy Hair

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have frizzy wavy hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with wavy or curly hair find that washing their hair every other day works best, while others prefer to wash their hair every few days. Experiment to find the washing frequency that works best for you.

Q: Can I use a hair mask to help calm frizzy wavy hair?

A: Yes! Hair masks can be a great way to hydrate and nourish your hair, which can help to reduce frizz. Look for a hair mask that is formulated specifically for wavy or curly hair, and use it once a week for best results.

Q: Is it okay to blow-dry my hair if I have frizzy wavy hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to use a diffuser attachment and to avoid using high heat. High heat can be damaging to your hair and can make frizz worse.

Hair Care Tips for Calming Frizzy Wavy Hair

In addition to the tips outlined above, there are several other hair care tips you can use to keep your frizzy wavy hair under control. Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a month to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and flat irons
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated

By following these tips and techniques, you can calm frizzy wavy hair and achieve the sleek, smooth look you desire. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for you, and don't be afraid to seek out advice from a professional stylist if you need additional help. With a little patience and perseverance, you can tame your frizzy wavy hair and feel confident and beautiful every day.
