Tip #1: Choose a hairstyle that softens your jawline

One of the main characteristics of a square face is a prominent jawline. To soften your facial features, choose a hairstyle that adds volume around your face. Consider hairstyles with layers or side-swept bangs that cover your forehead and temples. This will create a softer, more rounded appearance.

Tip #2: Avoid flat hairstyles

Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear flat, as this will only emphasize your angular features. Instead, choose hairstyles with texture and movement, such as messy curls or a textured crop. This will add dimension and balance to your face shape.

Tip #3: Add volume to your hair

Adding volume to your hair is a great way to balance out a square face shape. Consider hairstyles with height and volume at the crown of your head, such as a pompadour or a quiff. This will elongate your face and create a more oval appearance.

How to Choose a Hairstyle for Square Face Male Indian

Choosing the right hairstyle for your square face can be challenging, but it's not impossible. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

The first step is to determine your face shape correctly. Stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or an eyeliner pencil. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a square face shape.

Step 2: Consider your hair type and texture

Once you know your face shape, consider your hair type and texture. Do you have straight or curly hair? Is your hair thick or thin? These factors will affect the type of hairstyle that will suit you best.

Step 3: Look for inspiration

Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, or even celebrities with square face shapes. Save pictures of hairstyles that you like and show them to your hairstylist.

Step 4: Consult with a hairstylist

Consult with a hairstylist who can help you choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair type. Your hairstylist can also give you advice on how to maintain your hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's essential to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Use a styling product, such as gel or mousse, to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a square face male Indian with short hair?

The best hairstyle for a square face male Indian with short hair is a textured crop or a buzz cut. These styles add volume and dimension to your hair, creating a more balanced appearance.

What is the best hairstyle for a square face male Indian with long hair?

The best hairstyle for a square face male Indian with long hair is a layered cut with side-swept bangs. This style softens your facial features and adds volume and movement to your hair.

Can I wear a middle part with a square face?

Avoid wearing a middle part if you have a square face, as this will only emphasize your angular features. Instead, consider a side part or a messy part.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your square face male Indian can make a significant difference in your appearance. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. Consult with a hairstylist who can guide you in choosing the perfect hairstyle for you. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your appearance.
