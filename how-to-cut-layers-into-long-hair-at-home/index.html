How To Cut Layers Into Long Hair At Home

Written by Robby May 29, 2023 · 6 min read

Long hair has always been a symbol of beauty and femininity. However, long hair can often look flat and lifeless, especially if it lacks layers. Layers can add movement, texture and dimension to long hair. But, going to a salon to get layers can be expensive. In this article, we’ll guide you on how to cut layers into long hair at home.

Description

Layering long hair is a simple process that requires some basic tools and techniques. You can cut your hair at home without any professional help. Cutting your hair at home can be fun and easy, and you can experiment with different lengths and angles to create a unique look.

How to Cut Layers into Long Hair at Home

Here are the steps to cut layers into long hair at home:

Step 1: Gather the Tools

You will need sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Use a comb to divide your hair into four sections: top, sides, and back. Clip each section separately.

Step 4: Start Cutting the Hair

Start with the bottom section of your hair. Hold the hair between two fingers and cut diagonally upwards. Cut small sections at a time and check the length frequently.

Step 5: Move to the Next Section

Unclip the next section and repeat the process. Make sure to cut the hair at the same angle as the previous section to create a uniform look.

Step 6: Cut the Top Section

Cut the top section of your hair using the same technique. Hold the hair between two fingers and cut diagonally upwards.

Step 7: Blend the Layers

Blend the layers by running your fingers through your hair. Check the length of the layers and trim any uneven parts.

Step 8: Check the Back

Check the back of your hair in the mirror. Trim any uneven parts and make sure the layers are even.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. Layers can be styled straight, wavy, or curly.

Tips

  • Start with small sections and cut slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Always cut diagonally upwards to create layers.
  • Check the length frequently to avoid any mishaps.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid an uneven look.
  • Make sure to blend the layers for a natural look.

FAQ

Can I cut layers into long hair at home?

Yes, you can cut layers into long hair at home. All you need is a sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

What is the best way to cut layers into long hair?

The best way to cut layers into long hair is to start with small sections and cut diagonally upwards. Check the length frequently and blend the layers for a natural look.

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, it is not recommended to cut your hair with regular scissors. Use a sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, make sure to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Apply a hair serum or oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to cut layers into long hair at home, go ahead and experiment with different lengths and angles to create a unique look that suits your personality.
