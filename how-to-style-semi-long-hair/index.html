How To Style Semi Long Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 23, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have semi long hair, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that's easy, versatile, and stylish. Luckily, there are many options for those with this hair length. From braids to updos to beachy waves, there's a style for every occasion. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to style semi long hair so you can look your best every day.

Description

Semi long hair is hair that falls between the shoulders and the mid-back. It's a versatile length that can be worn in many different styles, from loose waves to sleek updos. However, styling semi long hair can be challenging, as it's not quite long enough to pull back into a ponytail or bun without some pieces falling out. Additionally, it's not short enough to wear in a simple bob or pixie cut. But with the right tools and techniques, you can create a variety of beautiful styles that will make the most of your semi long hair.

How to Style Semi Long Hair

Step One: Start With Clean, Dry Hair

The first step to styling semi long hair is to start with clean, dry hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair, then towel dry it gently. Avoid rubbing your hair too vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz. Once your hair is dry, you can begin styling it.

Step Two: Choose Your Style

There are many different styles you can choose from when it comes to semi long hair. Some popular options include:

  • Beachy waves
  • Braids
  • Updos
  • Straight and sleek

Think about the occasion and your personal style when choosing your hair style. For example, if you're going to a formal event, an updo may be the best choice. If you're going for a casual look, beachy waves or braids may be more appropriate.

Step Three: Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, you'll need to prep it with some products. Use a heat protectant spray if you're going to be using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener. If you're going for a wavy or curly look, apply a texturizing spray or mousse to help hold the curls in place. For an updo, you may want to use a volumizing spray or mousse to add some extra volume.

Step Four: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is prepped, it's time to start styling. Depending on the look you're going for, you may need to use different tools and techniques. Here are some tips for each style:

Beachy Waves

To create beachy waves, start by dividing your hair into sections. Use a curling iron to curl each section, holding the iron at an angle to create a loose wave. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently loosen the curls and create a more natural look. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Braids

Braids are a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways. To create a simple braid, divide your hair into three sections and cross them over each other, alternating the sections as you go. For a more complex braid, try a fishtail braid or a french braid. Finish with a hairspray to hold the braid in place.

Updos

To create an updo, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun, securing it with bobby pins. For a more complex updo, try a braided bun or a chignon. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Straight and Sleek

To create a straight and sleek look, start by blow drying your hair with a round brush to add volume. Once your hair is dry, use a straightener to smooth out any frizz or waves. Finish with a shine serum to add some extra shine.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Invest in good quality hair products
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a stylist or friend
  • Be gentle with your hair to avoid damage and breakage

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my semi long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go a few days between washes. Use your own judgement and listen to your hair.

Q: How can I add volume to my semi long hair?

A: Use a volumizing spray or mousse when styling your hair. You can also try blow drying your hair upside down to add some extra volume.

Q: Can I dye my semi long hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your semi long hair. However, be aware that dyeing your hair can cause damage and breakage. Use a gentle dye and follow the instructions carefully.

Hair Care

To keep your semi long hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools too often. Use a heat protectant spray when you do use hot tools, and be gentle when brushing your hair to avoid breakage. Additionally, make sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your semi long hair in a variety of beautiful and fashionable ways. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask for help from a stylist or friend. With a little practice, you'll be able to create stunning hairstyles that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
