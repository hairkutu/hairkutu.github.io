Why Is My Hair Curly Sometimes And Wavy Others

Written by Eveline Sep 02, 2023 · 7 min read

If you are someone who has naturally curly or wavy hair, you may have noticed that your hair can sometimes be more curly or more wavy depending on the day. In this article, we will explore the reasons behind why your hair can be curly sometimes and wavy others. We will also provide tips and advice on how to care for your hair to promote healthy and consistent curls or waves.

Table of Contents

  • Genetics
  • Humidity
  • Product Usage
  • Heat Styling
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Genetics

One of the main reasons why your hair can be curly sometimes and wavy others is due to genetics. Your hair type is determined by the shape of the hair follicle. If your hair follicle is oval-shaped, your hair will be wavy. If your hair follicle is more circular, your hair will be curly. However, there are different degrees of waviness and curliness, and this can vary from day to day depending on other factors.

Humidity

Humidity can play a big role in how curly or wavy your hair is on any given day. When the air is humid, the moisture in the air can cause your hair to absorb the moisture, which can make it more curly. On the other hand, when the air is dry, your hair may be less curly or wavy.

Product Usage

The products you use on your hair can also affect the curliness or waviness of your hair. For example, if you use products that are designed to enhance curls, your hair may be more curly. If you use products that are designed to smooth hair, your hair may be less curly or wavy. It's important to choose products that are designed for your hair type and to follow the instructions on the label.

Heat Styling

Heat styling can also affect the curliness or waviness of your hair. If you use a curling iron or a flat iron, you can create curls or waves that are more consistent than your natural hair. However, heat styling can also damage your hair if you don't use heat protectant products or if you use too much heat. It's important to use heat styling tools sparingly and to protect your hair from heat damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips on how to care for your hair to promote healthy and consistent curls or waves:

  • Use products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use heat protectant products when you do use heat styling tools.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.

FAQs

Can I change my hair type?

No, you cannot change your hair type. Your hair type is determined by genetics.

Can I make my hair more curly or more wavy?

You can enhance your curls or waves by using products that are designed to promote curls or waves. You can also use heat styling tools to create curls or waves. However, it's important to use these tools sparingly and to protect your hair from heat damage.

Why does my hair look different every day?

There are many factors that can affect the way your hair looks on any given day, including humidity, product usage, and heat styling. It's normal for your hair to look different from day to day.

How can I promote healthy hair?

You can promote healthy hair by using products that are designed for your hair type, avoiding heat styling tools, and deep conditioning your hair regularly. It's also important to avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.

Overall, there are many factors that can affect the curliness or waviness of your hair. By understanding these factors and taking good care of your hair, you can promote healthy and consistent curls or waves.
