Short Hair For Square Face 2021 How To Rock The Look

Written by Frank Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a square-shaped face, you might feel like short haircuts are not for you. But fear not, there are plenty of trendy and stylish options that will flatter your face shape. In this article, we will explore the best short haircuts for square faces in 2021. Whether you're looking for a bob, a pixie cut, or something in between, we've got you covered. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

First, let's talk about what a square-shaped face looks like. Typically, square faces have a strong jawline and a forehead that is similar in width to the jawline. The cheekbones are also usually wide and prominent. When it comes to choosing a short haircut for a square face, the goal is to soften the angles and create some roundness. This can be achieved with layers, bangs, and volume.

How to

When considering a short haircut for a square face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid styles that emphasize the squareness of your face, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create movement and softness.

Some popular short haircuts for square faces include:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Layered bob with side-swept bangs
  • Textured lob (long bob)
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Curly bob

Step by Step

Now, let's go through the steps of getting a short haircut for a square face:

  1. Find inspiration photos: Look for hairstyles that you like and that flatter your face shape. Save them to show your stylist.
  2. Consult with your stylist: Explain what you're looking for and ask for their recommendations. They can help you choose a style that will work best for your hair type and face shape.
  3. Cut and style: Sit back and let your stylist work their magic! They will cut and style your hair to create the desired look.
  4. Product and maintenance: Ask your stylist for product recommendations and tips on how to maintain your new haircut.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for rocking a short haircut with a square face:

  • Experiment with side parts and side-swept bangs to create softness around the face.
  • Consider adding layers to create movement and volume.
  • Try a curly or wavy style to add texture and soften the angles of the face.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to create volume and roundness.
  • Accessorize with earrings or statement necklaces to draw attention away from the jawline.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my square face look even more square?

A: It depends on the style. Blunt bobs or straight-across bangs can emphasize the squareness of the face, but hairstyles with layers, volume, and movement can soften the angles and create a more rounded appearance.

Q: What hair type is best for a short haircut with a square face?

A: Short haircuts can work for all hair types, but some styles may be better suited for certain hair types. For example, a curly bob may work better for someone with naturally curly hair, while a layered bob may work better for someone with fine hair.

Hair Care

After getting a short haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short haircut with a square face like a pro. Remember to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type, and to take care of your hair after the cut. Happy styling!
