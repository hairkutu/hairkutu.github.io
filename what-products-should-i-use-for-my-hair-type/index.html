What Products Should I Use For My Hair Type

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right hair products can be overwhelming, especially if you're not sure what products are best for your hair type. Using the wrong products can lead to damage, breakage, and frizz. In this article, we'll discuss what products you should use for your hair type and provide tips for healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Understanding Your Hair Type
  • What Products Should I Use?
  • How to Use Hair Products
  • Healthy Hair Tips
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding Your Hair Type

The first step in choosing the right hair products is understanding your hair type. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each hair type has its own unique characteristics and requires a different approach to hair care.

Straight Hair

Straight hair is characterized by its lack of curl or wave. It tends to be shiny and can become oily easily. Straight hair is prone to becoming limp and lifeless, so it benefits from volumizing products.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair has a slight curl or wave to it. It tends to be thicker than straight hair and can become frizzy in humid weather. Wavy hair benefits from products that enhance its natural texture.

Curly Hair

Curly hair is characterized by its tight, springy curls. It tends to be dry and prone to frizz. Curly hair benefits from products that provide moisture and define curls.

Coily Hair

Coily hair has the tightest curls of all the hair types. It tends to be very dry and requires a lot of moisture to stay healthy. Coily hair benefits from products that provide moisture and define curls.

What Products Should I Use?

Now that you understand your hair type, it's time to choose the right products. Here are some general guidelines for each hair type:

Straight Hair

If you have straight hair, look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners to add body and bounce. Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair. Opt for light mousses or sprays instead.

Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, look for products that enhance your natural texture. Try using a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, and use a lightweight styling cream or gel to define your waves.

Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, look for products that provide moisture and define curls. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and follow up with a curl-defining cream or gel. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your curls.

Coily Hair

If you have coily hair, look for products that provide maximum moisture. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and follow up with a leave-in conditioner or curl cream. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools that can cause damage.

How to Use Hair Products

Using the right hair products is important, but so is using them correctly. Here are some tips for using hair products:

  • Shampoo your hair no more than twice a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair every time you shampoo to add moisture and prevent breakage.
  • Apply styling products to damp hair, not wet or dry hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.

Healthy Hair Tips

In addition to using the right products, there are other things you can do to keep your hair healthy:

  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a combination of hair types?

If you have a combination of hair types, you may need to use different products on different parts of your hair. For example, if you have wavy hair on top and curly hair on the bottom, use a curl-defining product on the bottom and a texturizing product on the top.

Can I use the same products as my friends with similar hair types?

While it's tempting to use the same products as your friends, keep in mind that everyone's hair is unique. What works for one person may not work for another. Experiment with different products until you find what works for your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. As a general rule, shampoo your hair no more than twice a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Can I use natural products on my hair?

Yes, natural products can be great for your hair. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and aloe vera.

Do I need to use the same brand for all my hair products?

No, you don't need to use the same brand for all your hair products. Mix and match products from different brands until you find what works best for your hair.
