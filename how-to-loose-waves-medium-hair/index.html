How To Loosen Waves In Medium Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have medium-length hair with waves that are too tight or just not your style, don't fret. With the right technique and products, you can easily loosen and soften your waves for a more relaxed look. In this article, we'll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to loosen waves in medium hair, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Loosen Waves in Medium Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Loosen Waves in Medium Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about some of the products and tools you'll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Detangling brush
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair dryer with diffuser attachment
  • Sea salt spray or texturizing spray

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to loosen waves in your medium hair:

  1. Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair as usual, then gently towel dry.
  2. Using a wide-tooth comb, gently detangle any knots or tangles in your hair.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  4. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to a low or medium heat setting.
  5. Using the diffuser, dry your hair in sections, scrunching your hair up towards your scalp as you go.
  6. Once your hair is dry, apply a sea salt spray or texturizing spray to your hair, scrunching it up again to create more waves.
  7. Using a detangling brush, brush through your hair to soften the waves and create a more relaxed look.
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place, if desired.

Tips for Loosening Waves in Medium Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect relaxed wave:

  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to blot your hair dry after washing, as regular towels can create frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil before blow-drying to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • If you have naturally tight curls, try using a curl-defining cream or mousse before blow-drying to loosen your curls.
  • Experiment with different heat settings on your hair dryer to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about loosening waves in medium hair:

Can I use a straightener to loosen my waves?

While a straightener can be used to create loose waves, it can also damage your hair if not used correctly. We recommend using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer instead.

How often should I use a texturizing spray?

It depends on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. If you have oily hair, you may only need to use a texturizing spray once or twice a week. If you have dry hair, you may need to use it more frequently.

Will sea salt spray make my hair feel crunchy?

Not necessarily. Look for a sea salt spray that also contains moisturizing ingredients to prevent your hair from feeling dry or crunchy.

Hair Care Advice for Looser Waves

To keep your waves looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and frizz.

With these simple tips and techniques, you can easily loosen waves in your medium hair for a more relaxed, carefree look. Experiment with different products and techniques until you find the ones that work best for you, and always take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
