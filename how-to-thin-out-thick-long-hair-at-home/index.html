How To Thin Out Thick Long Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 01, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have thick long hair, you may have trouble styling it and keeping it manageable. Thinning out your hair can help make it more manageable and easier to style. Going to a salon to get your hair thinned out can be expensive, but luckily, you can do it at home with the right tools and techniques. In this article, we’ll teach you how to thin out thick long hair at home.

Description

Thinning out thick long hair can help make it more manageable and easier to style. It involves removing some of the bulk from your hair without significantly changing the length. You can thin out your hair at home with the right tools and techniques.

How to Thin Out Thick Long Hair at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

To thin out your hair at home, you’ll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Make sure your scissors are sharp and clean.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start thinning out your hair, wash it with shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and comb it out.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start by separating the top layer of your hair and clipping it away.

Step 4: Begin Thinning Out Your Hair

Take a section of your hair and hold it between your fingers. Slide your fingers down the length of your hair until you reach the point where you want to start thinning it out.

Using your scissors, carefully cut into your hair at a 45-degree angle. Make sure not to cut too much hair at once.

Continue cutting small sections of hair at a time until you have thinned out the entire section.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Tips

- Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

- Don’t cut too much hair at once. It’s better to cut small sections at a time.

- Take breaks if you need to. Thinning out your hair can be a time-consuming process.

FAQ

Can I thin out my hair if it’s curly?

Yes, you can thin out curly hair. However, it’s important to be careful and not cut too much hair at once.

Will thinning out my hair make it look thinner?

Thinning out your hair should not make it look thinner. It should just make it more manageable and easier to style.

How often should I thin out my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how thick it is. Generally, you should aim to thin out your hair every 3-6 months.

Hair Care

Thinning out your hair can help make it more manageable, but it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy.

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

- Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.
