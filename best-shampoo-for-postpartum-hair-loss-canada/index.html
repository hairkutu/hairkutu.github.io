Best Shampoo For Postpartum Hair Loss Canada

Written by Bardi Apr 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Postpartum hair loss is a common condition that affects many women after giving birth. During pregnancy, estrogen levels rise, causing hair to remain in the growth phase for longer periods. However, after childbirth, estrogen levels drop, causing hair to shift into the shedding phase, resulting in hair loss.

If you are experiencing postpartum hair loss, the right shampoo can help reduce hair shedding and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we will discuss the best shampoos for postpartum hair loss in Canada.

How to Choose the Right Shampoo for Postpartum Hair Loss

Choosing the right shampoo for postpartum hair loss can be overwhelming, given the variety of options available in the market. However, there are some key features to look out for when selecting a shampoo for postpartum hair loss:

  • Ingredients: Look for shampoos with ingredients that promote hair growth, such as biotin, keratin, and collagen.
  • Sulfate-free: Sulfates can strip the natural oils from the hair, making it dry and brittle. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and gentle on the hair and scalp.
    • pH-balanced: A pH-balanced shampoo helps maintain the natural pH level of the scalp, preventing excessive hair shedding and breakage.

Top Shampoos for Postpartum Hair Loss in Canada

1. Nioxin System 2 Cleanser Shampoo

Nioxin System 2 Cleanser Shampoo is a popular choice for postpartum hair loss. It contains biotin, keratin, and collagen, which promote hair growth and strengthen the hair shaft. The shampoo is sulfate-free and pH-balanced, making it gentle on the hair and scalp.

2. OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo

OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo is another excellent option for postpartum hair loss. It contains biotin and collagen, which improve hair density and volume. The shampoo is sulfate-free and pH-balanced, making it suitable for all hair types.

3. Renpure Biotin & Collagen Shampoo

Renpure Biotin & Collagen Shampoo is a natural and affordable option for postpartum hair loss. It contains biotin and collagen, which promote hair growth and thickness. The shampoo is sulfate-free, paraben-free, and pH-balanced, making it gentle on the hair and scalp.

How to Use Shampoo for Postpartum Hair Loss

To get the best results from your postpartum hair loss shampoo, follow these steps:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly.
  2. Apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp.
  3. Massage your scalp gently for a few minutes.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water.
  5. Repeat if necessary.

Tips for Managing Postpartum Hair Loss

Aside from using the right shampoo, there are other things you can do to manage postpartum hair loss:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and vitamin D.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles, as they can pull on the hair and cause breakage.
  • Limit the use of heat styling tools, such as hair dryers and flat irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends, which can lead to hair breakage.

FAQs

1. When does postpartum hair loss start?

Postpartum hair loss typically starts around three months after giving birth and can last up to a year.

2. How much hair loss is normal postpartum?

It is normal to lose up to 100 hairs per day postpartum. However, if you notice excessive shedding or bald patches, consult a healthcare provider.

3. Can postpartum hair loss be permanent?

Postpartum hair loss is usually temporary and resolves on its own. However, in rare cases, it can lead to permanent hair loss. If you are concerned about your hair loss, consult a healthcare provider.

Hair Care for Postpartum Hair Loss

Postpartum hair loss can be frustrating, but with the right hair care routine and products, you can promote healthy hair growth and reduce hair shedding. Choose a shampoo that is sulfate-free, pH-balanced, and contains hair growth-promoting ingredients like biotin and collagen. Additionally, eat a balanced diet, avoid tight hairstyles and heat styling tools, and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and strong.
