How To Cut Kid Boy Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As a parent, you may have found yourself in a situation where you need to cut your kid boy's hair at home. Whether it's due to convenience or cost savings, cutting your kid boy's hair at home is a great option. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut kid boy hair at home. We will also provide you with tips on how to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Cut Kid Boy Hair at Home

Before we start, here are some things you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Cape or towel
  • Spray bottle with water

Step 1: Prepare Your Child and Tools

Before you start cutting your kid boy's hair, make sure your child is comfortable and willing to sit still. You should also prepare your tools by cleaning and sanitizing them.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Choose the right clipper guard based on the length you want to cut your kid boy's hair. Use a longer guard for a longer length and a shorter guard for a shorter length.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the sides and back of your kid boy's hair first. Use the clipper guard and start at the bottom, moving upwards. Go against the direction of hair growth for a closer cut.

Once you're done with the sides and back, move on to the top of the head. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it in a straight line.

Step 4: Clean Up

Use the clippers without the guard to clean up the edges around the ears and neck. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Tips for Cutting Kid Boy Hair at Home

  • Use a cape or towel to catch hair clippings and make clean up easier.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter if needed.
  • Use the spray bottle to dampen hair and make it easier to cut.
  • Take breaks if your child is getting restless.

FAQ

Can I Cut My Kid Boy's Hair with Scissors Only?

Yes, you can cut your kid boy's hair with scissors only. However, it may be more difficult to achieve an even cut.

What if I Mess Up?

If you mess up, don't worry. Hair grows back and you can always take your kid boy to a professional to fix any mistakes.

How Often Should I Cut My Kid Boy's Hair?

It depends on how fast your kid boy's hair grows and the length you prefer. On average, you should cut your kid boy's hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your kid boy's hair, make sure to wash it with shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep hair soft and manageable. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down hair.

We hope this step-by-step guide on how to cut kid boy hair at home was helpful. Remember to take your time and be patient. With practice, you'll be able to give your kid boy a great haircut at home.
