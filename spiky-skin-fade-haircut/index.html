Spiky Skin Fade Haircut The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Jul 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle that can make you stand out from the crowd? Then, look no further than spiky skin fade haircut! This trendy hairstyle has been gaining immense popularity among men of all ages and is a perfect choice for those who want a modern and low-maintenance look.

Description

The spiky skin fade haircut is a combination of a short buzz cut on the sides that gradually fades into longer hair on the top. The hair on the top is styled into spikes using hair gel or wax, which adds texture and volume to the hair. This haircut is ideal for men with thick and straight hair, but it can also work for those with wavy or curly hair.

How to Get a Spiky Skin Fade Haircut?

Getting a spiky skin fade haircut is easy, but it's always best to visit a professional hairstylist who can guide you through the process. Here are the steps involved in getting a spiky skin fade haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting a spiky skin fade haircut is to decide on the length of your hair. Generally, the hair on the top should be about 2-3 inches long, while the sides should be buzzed short. However, you can adjust the length according to your personal preference.

Step 2: Create a Fade

The next step is to create a fade on the sides. This involves gradually trimming the hair shorter as you move towards the bottom. Your hairstylist will use clippers or scissors to create a smooth transition from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once the fade is done, it's time to style your hair into spikes. Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your fingertips and run them through your hair, creating spikes that stand up. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Spiky Skin Fade Haircut

Here are some tips to keep your spiky skin fade haircut looking fresh:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or wax to hold the spikes in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Consider using a hair serum or oil to add shine to your hair.

FAQ

1. Is a spiky skin fade haircut suitable for all hair types?

A spiky skin fade haircut works best for men with straight or thick hair, but it can also work for those with wavy or curly hair.

2. How often should I wash my hair with a spiky skin fade haircut?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

3. Can I style my spiky skin fade haircut differently?

Yes, you can style your spiky skin fade haircut in different ways, such as creating a messy look or slicking your hair back.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

With the right care and maintenance, your spiky skin fade haircut can last for weeks and give you a stylish and edgy look. So, go ahead and try this trendy hairstyle and see how it can transform your appearance!
