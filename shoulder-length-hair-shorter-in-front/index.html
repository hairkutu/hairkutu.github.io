Shoulder Length Hair Shorter In Front A Trendy And Chic Hairstyle

Written by Bardi Aug 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both trendy and chic, you might want to consider shoulder length hair shorter in front. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add more volume and texture to their hair while still keeping the length. In this blog post, we'll show you how to achieve this look and give you some tips on how to take care of your shoulder length hair.

Description

Shoulder length hair shorter in front is a hairstyle that features shorter layers in the front of the hair, gradually getting longer towards the back. This creates a flattering and face-framing effect that draws attention to your best features. It's a versatile hairstyle that works well with all hair types and face shapes, making it a popular choice among women of all ages.

How to

If you're interested in getting shoulder length hair shorter in front, here are the steps you can follow:

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

The first step is to consult with a hairstylist who has experience in cutting this type of hairstyle. They will be able to assess your hair type, face shape, and personal style to create a look that suits you best.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before your appointment, make sure to wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier for the hairstylist to work with and get a better idea of how it falls naturally.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

The hairstylist will then section your hair and start cutting the shorter layers in the front. They will gradually work towards the back, blending the layers seamlessly into the longer hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is done, you can style your hair as desired. You can use a curling iron or a straightener to add texture and volume to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you decide to get shoulder length hair shorter in front:

  • Regular trimming is essential to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create more volume at the roots.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

1. Can shoulder length hair shorter in front work for any hair type?

Yes, shoulder length hair shorter in front can work for any hair type, whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair.

2. Is this hairstyle low maintenance?

It depends on your hair type and how you style it. If you have naturally straight hair, it may require less maintenance compared to curly or wavy hair.

3. Will this hairstyle suit my face shape?

Yes, shoulder length hair shorter in front is a versatile hairstyle that can suit any face shape. However, it's always best to consult with a hairstylist to get a personalized recommendation.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your shoulder length hair shorter in front. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day or as needed, depending on your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water and opt for lukewarm water instead to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools and if possible, let your hair air dry.

With proper care and maintenance, your shoulder length hair shorter in front can look stunning and chic all year round. So, go ahead and try out this trendy hairstyle for a fresh and stylish look.
