Short Haircut For Square Face Male How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Apr 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read
Are you a male with a square face and struggling to find the right haircut? Look no further! In this blog post, we will give you tips and tricks on how to get the perfect short haircut for square face male. We will cover everything from the best hairstyles to hair care tips to ensure your hair always looks its best. So, let's dive in!

Description

A square face has a strong and angular jawline, with a forehead and cheekbones that are roughly the same width. The best short haircuts for square face male should soften the sharpness of the face shape and enhance its natural features. The right haircut can make all the difference in your appearance, making you look more attractive and confident.

How to

Getting the perfect short haircut for square face male is not rocket science, but it does require some knowledge and preparation. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Know Your Face Shape

Before you choose a haircut, it's essential to know your face shape. A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and prominent cheekbones. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a haircut that complements your features.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle for a square face male should add volume to the top of the head and soften the sharp angles of the face. Some of the best short haircuts for square face male include the buzz cut, the crew cut, the side part, and the textured crop.

Step 3: Find a Good Barber

Finding a good barber who understands your face shape and hair type is essential. You want someone who can give you expert advice and create a haircut that suits you. Look for a barber who specializes in men's haircuts and has experience working with square face shapes.

Step 4: Maintain Your Haircut

Once you've got the perfect short haircut for square face male, it's essential to maintain it. Regular trims every four to six weeks will keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Use high-quality hair products that suit your hair type, and follow a good hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for square face male: - Avoid hairstyles that are too short on the sides or top, as they can make your jawline look even more prominent. - Add texture to your hair to soften the sharp angles of your face. - Use hair products that add volume to the top of your head, such as mousse or a texturizing spray. - Experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Can you suggest some short haircuts for square face male? A: The buzz cut, the crew cut, the side part, and the textured crop are some of the best short haircuts for square face male. Q: Can I style my short haircut for square face male differently for different occasions? A: Yes, you can experiment with different styling techniques, such as slicking your hair back or adding some texture for a more casual look.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut for square face male looking its best, follow these hair care tips: - Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. - Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. - Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection. In conclusion, getting the perfect short haircut for square face male is easy when you know your face shape, choose the right hairstyle, find a good barber, and maintain your haircut. Follow our tips and tricks, experiment with different hairstyles, and take good care of your hair to look your best at all times.
