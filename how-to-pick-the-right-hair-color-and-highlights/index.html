How To Pick The Right Hair Color And Highlights

Written by Frank May 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the perfect hair color and highlights can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed. But don't worry, we're here to help! In this post, we'll guide you through the process of picking the right hair color and highlights for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. We'll also provide some tips for maintaining your new hair color and highlights. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Pick the Right Hair Color and Highlights
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Pick the Right Hair Color and Highlights

Before you start browsing hair color options, it's important to consider your skin tone and eye color. This will help you determine which hair colors and highlights will complement your natural features the best. Here are some tips:

  • If you have warm undertones (yellow or golden), opt for warm hair colors such as golden blonde or auburn.
  • If you have cool undertones (pink or blue), choose cool hair colors such as ash blonde or black.
  • If you have a neutral undertone, you can pull off both warm and cool hair colors.
  • Consider your eye color as well. If you have blue eyes, warm hair colors will make them pop. If you have brown eyes, cool hair colors will complement them.

Once you've determined your skin tone and eye color, it's time to consider your personal style. Do you prefer a natural look or something more bold? Are you willing to commit to regular touch-ups or do you want something low-maintenance? Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • If you want a natural look, opt for hair colors that are close to your natural shade. You can add subtle highlights for dimension.
  • If you want something bold, consider bright colors such as red or purple. You can also opt for dramatic highlights such as blonde or platinum.
  • If you want something low-maintenance, choose a hair color that is close to your natural shade. This will make regrowth less noticeable.
  • If you don't mind regular touch-ups, choose a hair color that is drastically different from your natural shade. This will require more maintenance but can make a big impact.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when picking the right hair color and highlights:

  1. Determine your skin tone and eye color.
  2. Consider your personal style and maintenance preferences.
  3. Browse hair color options and choose a few that you like.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which hair color and highlights will work best for you.
  5. Book an appointment and get your new hair color and highlights!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider the season. Warm hair colors such as red and copper are great for fall, while cool hair colors such as platinum and ash blonde are perfect for summer.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new! Hair color can always be changed if you don't like the result.
  • Invest in quality hair care products to maintain your new hair color and highlights.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as this can cause the color to fade.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about picking the right hair color and highlights:

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting with a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose the right hair color and highlights and ensure that the dye is applied correctly.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my hair color?

A: This depends on the hair color and highlights you choose and how fast your hair grows. Some hair colors require touch-ups every 4-6 weeks, while others can last up to 12 weeks.

Q: Will hair dye damage my hair?

A: While hair dye can cause some damage to your hair, using quality hair care products and avoiding excessive heat styling can help minimize damage. Be sure to follow your hairstylist's recommendations for maintaining your new hair color and highlights.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining your new hair color and highlights:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve the color.
  • Avoid using excessive heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as this can cause the color to fade.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your new hair color and highlights look their best for as long as possible!
