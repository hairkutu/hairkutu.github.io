How To Make Natural Dreadlocks With Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 24, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to make natural dreadlocks with long hair, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll take you through the entire process of creating your own dreads, from start to finish. We'll cover everything from how to prepare your hair, to the different methods you can use to create your dreads, to how to care for your new hairstyle once it's in place. So, let's get started!

How to Prepare Your Hair for Dreadlocks

Before you can start creating your dreads, you'll need to prepare your hair. This involves washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or oils. You'll also want to avoid using any conditioner, as this can make it harder for the dreadlocks to form.

Once you've washed your hair, you'll need to let it dry completely. You can either air dry your hair or use a blow dryer if you're short on time.

Different Methods for Creating Dreadlocks

The Twist and Rip Method

The twist and rip method is one of the most popular methods for creating dreadlocks. To use this method, you'll need to section your hair into small, even sections. Then, you'll twist each section in one direction and then rip it apart in the opposite direction. This creates knots in the hair that will eventually form into dreadlocks.

The Backcombing Method

The backcombing method involves teasing the hair backwards, towards the scalp. This creates knots in the hair that will eventually form into dreadlocks. To use this method, you'll need to section your hair into small, even sections and then backcomb each section until it's knotted.

The Freeform Method

The freeform method is the most natural method for creating dreadlocks. With this method, you simply let your hair do its thing and form dreadlocks on its own. This method is great if you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle, but it can take longer for the dreadlocks to form.

Tips for Creating Natural Dreadlocks with Long Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you're creating your dreadlocks:

  • Be patient. Creating dreadlocks can take time, especially if you're using the freeform method.
  • Use a crochet hook to tighten up loose hairs and make your dreadlocks more uniform.
  • Avoid using wax or other products in your hair, as this can make it harder for the dreadlocks to form.
  • Keep your hair clean and dry, as wet hair can cause your dreadlocks to unravel.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create dreadlocks?

The amount of time it takes to create dreadlocks can vary depending on the method you're using and the length of your hair. It can take anywhere from a few hours to a few months.

How often should I wash my dreadlocks?

It's important to keep your dreadlocks clean, but you don't want to wash them too often. Aim to wash your hair once a week or every other week.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Once your dreadlocks are in place, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a residue-free shampoo to avoid buildup in your hair.
  • Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can cause your dreadlocks to unravel.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using rubber bands or hair ties, as these can cause damage to your hair.

Follow these tips and you'll be well on your way to creating natural dreadlocks with long hair. Good luck!
