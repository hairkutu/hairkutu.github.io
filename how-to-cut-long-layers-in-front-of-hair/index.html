How To Cut Long Layers In Front Of Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 16, 2023 · 8 min read

Cutting long layers in the front of your hair can be a great way to add dimension and movement to your hairstyle. Whether you have long, medium, or short hair, this technique can work for you. In this step-by-step guide, we’ll walk you through how to cut long layers in front of hair, including tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect look.

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Long Layers
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Cut Long Layers in Front of Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. If you’re not confident in your cutting skills, it’s always best to consult with a professional stylist.

Here are the basic steps for cutting long layers in front of hair:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and blow-dry your hair as you normally would. Brush out any tangles or knots, and make sure your hair is smooth and straight.

2. Section off the front of your hair.

Use a comb to create a triangle-shaped section of hair at the front of your head. The base of the triangle should be at the crown of your head, and the point should be at the center of your forehead. Clip the rest of your hair back out of the way.

3. Decide on the length of your layers.

Decide how long you want your layers to be. For a subtle look, keep your layers long and try to blend them in with the rest of your hair. For a more dramatic look, go shorter and create more distinct layers.

4. Cut the first layer.

Take a small section of hair from the front of the triangle, and hold it between your fingers. Point cut into the hair, starting at the length you’ve decided on for your layers. This will create a soft, blended effect. Repeat this process for the rest of the hair in the triangle section.

5. Blend the layers.

Once you’ve cut the first layer, take another small section of hair from the triangle and use it as a guide to blend the layers together. Cut the hair at the same length as the first layer, but use a point-cutting technique to create a softer edge.

6. Check your work.

Release the rest of your hair and check your layers in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that the layers are even and blended.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you want to see the process in action, here’s a step-by-step guide to cutting long layers in front of hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the front of your hair.
  3. Decide on the length of your layers.
  4. Cut the first layer.
  5. Blend the layers.
  6. Check your work.

For a more detailed explanation of each step, see the previous section.

Tips for Cutting Long Layers

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting long layers in front of hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair to create a more natural, blended look.
  • Use a point-cutting technique to create softer, more natural-looking layers.
  • When cutting the first layer, use your fingers as a guide to create a diagonal line that follows the shape of your face.
  • Don’t be afraid to make small, incremental cuts to achieve the desired length.
  • Always check your work in the mirror, and make any necessary adjustments before moving on to the next section.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long layers in front of hair:

Q: Can I cut long layers in front of hair on my own?

A: While it’s possible to cut your own hair, we recommend consulting with a professional stylist if you’re not confident in your cutting skills.

Q: How often should I cut my hair to maintain long layers?

A: It’s generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and maintain your layers.

Q: Will cutting long layers in front of hair damage my hair?

A: As long as you’re using the right tools and techniques, cutting long layers in front of hair shouldn’t damage your hair. However, it’s always important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair after cutting long layers in front:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your layers and keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, layered hair that looks great from every angle.
