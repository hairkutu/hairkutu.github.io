How To Do 60 S Flip Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a fun and retro hairstyle to try out? Look no further than the 60's flip! This classic look has been making a comeback in recent years and is a great way to add some vintage flair to your look. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect 60's flip in just a few simple steps.

Description:

The 60's flip is a hairstyle characterized by flipped-out ends that curve away from the face. This style was popularized by icons such as Brigitte Bardot and Mary Tyler Moore and has since become a staple of vintage fashion. It's a fun and playful look that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How To:

To achieve the perfect 60's flip, you'll need a few key tools:

  • Round brush
  • Hairspray
  • Flat iron

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Begin by washing and blow-drying your hair. The 60's flip works best with clean, dry hair, so make sure your locks are free from any dirt or oil.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, separate your hair into two sections: top and bottom. Clip the top section out of the way so you can work on the bottom section first.

Step 3: Curl Your Ends

Take a small section of hair from the bottom and curl the ends upward using a flat iron. Hold the flat iron in place for a few seconds to ensure the curl sets.

Step 4: Flip Your Hair Outward

Using a round brush, flip the curled section of hair outward. Start at the root and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to create a smooth curve. Hold the brush in place for a few seconds to ensure the flip stays in place.

Step 5: Repeat on the Top Section

Once you've finished the bottom section, unclip the top section and repeat the curling and flipping process.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've flipped out all of your ends, finish the look with a spritz of hairspray. This will help hold the style in place and prevent any unwanted frizz or flyaways.

Tips:

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect 60's flip:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • For added volume, tease your roots before flipping out your ends.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ:

Here are a few frequently asked questions about the 60's flip:

1. Can I achieve this look on short hair?

Yes! The 60's flip works well on both short and long hair. Just adjust the size of your curls and flips accordingly.

2. Do I need to use a specific type of flat iron?

No, any flat iron should work for this style. Just make sure it's heated up to the appropriate temperature.

3. How long will the style last?

This depends on your hair type and how well you've set the style with hairspray. Generally, the 60's flip should last for a few hours to a full day.

Hair Care:

While the 60's flip is a fun and playful style, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many hot tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent tangles and knots.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 60's flip in no time. Whether you're heading to a retro-themed party or just want to add some vintage flair to your everyday look, this style is sure to turn heads.
