How To Cut Hair With Clippers Only A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of spending a lot of money at the hair salon? Do you want to save money by cutting your hair at home? Well, you're in luck because cutting hair with clippers only is easier than you think! In this post, we'll show you how to cut hair with clippers only and give you some tips and tricks to make it a success.

How to Cut Hair with Clippers Only

Before we get started, let's talk about the tools you'll need. You'll need hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You can buy a set of hair clippers at your local beauty supply store or online. Make sure to choose a set that comes with different guard sizes for different lengths of hair.

Step 1: Choose the Right Guard Size

Start by choosing the right guard size. The guard size will determine the length of the hair you're cutting. For example, if you want a shorter haircut, use a smaller guard size. If you want a longer haircut, use a larger guard size. If you're not sure which guard size to use, start with a longer guard size and work your way down.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting. This will make it easier to cut and will also help you see the natural shape of your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair. Start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom section. Use hair clips to hold the top section in place.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair with the clippers. Begin at the back of your head and work your way up. Use the comb to lift the hair and run the clippers over it. Make sure to keep the clippers at a 90-degree angle to your head to get an even cut.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

After you've cut the back of your head, move on to the sides. Use the same technique to cut the sides. Make sure to blend the hair between the back and sides to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 6: Cut the Top Section

Now it's time to cut the top section of your hair. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and at the same length.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

After you've cut your hair, style it to your liking. You can use a hair dryer, styling products, or even a flat iron to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers Only

Here are some tips to make cutting hair with clippers only a success:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if you're not sure which guard size to use.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and run the clippers over it to get an even cut.
  • Blend the hair between the back and sides to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Use the scissors to cut the top section of your hair to the desired length.
  • Style your hair to your liking after you've cut it.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair with clippers only:

Can I cut my hair with clippers without a guard?

We don't recommend cutting your hair with clippers without a guard. This can result in uneven cuts and even injury.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers only?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. You can cut your hair with clippers every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Do I need to oil my clippers?

Yes, you should oil your clippers before and after each use. This will help to keep them running smoothly and extend their lifespan.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair with Clippers Only

After you've cut your hair with clippers only, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care after cutting your hair with clippers only:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair for at least a week after cutting it.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Cutting hair with clippers only is a great way to save money and achieve a fresh new look. With the right tools and techniques, you can cut your hair at home like a pro. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips for a successful haircut every time!
