How To Cut Your Own Short Hair Female Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to save some money and cut your own short hair at home, you're in the right place. With the help of YouTube tutorials, you can achieve a salon-worthy look from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting your own short hair, step-by-step.

Description

Cutting your own hair can seem daunting, especially if you've never done it before. But with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a great haircut at home. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know to cut your own short hair, from choosing the right tools to styling your new 'do.

How to

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you begin cutting your hair, you'll need to gather a few essential tools. These include:

  • Haircutting scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle with water

Make sure your tools are clean and in good condition before you start cutting your hair.

Step 2: Wash and dry your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll want to wash and dry it. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get an even cut. Once your hair is dry, comb it straight and smooth.

Step 3: Section your hair

Using sectioning clips, divide your hair into four sections: top, sides, and back. Clip each section out of the way, leaving only the bottom section loose.

Step 4: Start cutting

Begin by cutting the bottom section of your hair. Take small sections (about 1 inch wide) and cut straight across. Use the comb to ensure that each section is even.

Once you've finished cutting the bottom section, release the next section and repeat the process. Continue cutting each section until you've finished the entire head.

Step 5: Blend the hair

After you've finished cutting all the sections, it's time to blend the hair. Use your comb to blend the hair between the different sections, creating a seamless transition.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to style it. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to create volume and shape. You can also use styling products to add texture and hold.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. It's easier to take off more hair than to add it back.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so give yourself a rest if you need it.
  • Use the right tools. Haircutting scissors are designed for cutting hair, while regular scissors can damage the hair.
  • Be patient. Cutting your own hair can take longer than you expect, especially if it's your first time.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your own curly hair. However, it's important to use the right techniques to ensure that you get an even cut. You may want to consider watching tutorials specifically for curly hair before you begin.

Do I need to wet my hair before cutting it?

Yes, it's best to cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair is easier to work with and will give you a more accurate cut.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. Depending on the severity of the mistake, you may be able to fix it yourself or you may need to visit a professional stylist. In the future, start with small cuts and work your way up to reduce the risk of mistakes.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using heat protectant products when styling, and avoiding over-processing your hair with chemicals or heat. If you have any questions about how to care for your hair, consult a professional stylist.

With the help of YouTube tutorials and the right tools, you can cut your own short hair at home. Follow these steps and tips for a great haircut every time.
