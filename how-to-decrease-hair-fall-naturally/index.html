How To Decrease Hair Fall Naturally A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 15, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of seeing your hair fall out in clumps every time you brush or wash it? Hair fall is a common problem that affects millions of people worldwide, and it can be caused by a variety of factors such as stress, hormonal changes, and nutritional deficiencies.

If you're looking for natural ways to combat hair fall and promote healthy hair growth, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss some simple yet effective tips to help you decrease hair fall naturally.

Table of Contents

  • How to Decrease Hair Fall Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Useful Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Decrease Hair Fall Naturally

There are several natural remedies that you can use to decrease hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Here are some of the most effective ones:

Eat a Healthy Diet

The foods you eat can have a significant impact on the health of your hair. Eating a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein can help keep your hair strong and healthy. Foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, vitamin B12, and biotin are especially beneficial for hair health.

Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes every day. You can also use a scalp massager or a brush with soft bristles to help improve blood flow to your scalp.

Use Natural Oils

Natural oils such as coconut oil, olive oil, and argan oil can help nourish and strengthen your hair. Apply a small amount of oil to your hair and scalp and leave it on for a few hours or overnight before washing it out with a gentle shampoo. Oiling your hair once or twice a week can help improve its texture and reduce hair fall.

Use Herbal Remedies

Herbs such as amla, brahmi, and neem are known for their hair-strengthening properties. You can use these herbs in the form of powders, oils, or hair masks to help reduce hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Mix a tablespoon of your chosen herb with a carrier oil such as coconut or olive oil and apply it to your hair and scalp. Leave it on for a few hours before washing it out with a gentle shampoo.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you decrease hair fall naturally:

  1. Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein.
  2. Massage your scalp for a few minutes every day to improve blood circulation.
  3. Use natural oils such as coconut oil, olive oil, and argan oil to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  4. Use herbal remedies such as amla, brahmi, and neem to help reduce hair fall and promote healthy hair growth.
  5. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as hair dyes and styling products.
  6. Avoid using hot styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners.
  7. Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids and ponytails that can pull on your hair.
  8. Get enough sleep and manage your stress levels to promote overall health and well-being.
  9. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  10. Visit a dermatologist if you experience severe hair fall or other hair-related issues.

Useful Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you decrease hair fall naturally:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair and cause hair fall.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQs

1. How much hair fall is normal?

It's normal to lose between 50 and 100 hairs a day. However, if you notice excessive hair fall or thinning of your hair, it's best to consult a dermatologist.

2. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you're stressed, your body produces the hormone cortisol, which can affect the health of your hair.

3. Can nutritional deficiencies cause hair fall?

Yes, nutritional deficiencies such as iron deficiency and vitamin B12 deficiency can cause hair fall. Eating a healthy, balanced diet can help prevent nutritional deficiencies.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, strong hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as hair dyes and bleaches.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after washing it as it can cause hair breakage.

By following these natural remedies and hair care tips, you can decrease hair fall naturally and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient and consistent in your hair care routine, and you'll soon see the results you've been looking for.
