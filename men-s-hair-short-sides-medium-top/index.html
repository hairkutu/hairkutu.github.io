Men s Hair Short Sides Medium Top The Perfect Style For 2023

Written by Petter Jul 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, the men's hair short sides medium top style is the way to go. This classic haircut has been around for decades and is still popular today, thanks to its clean, modern look. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Get the Men's Hair Short Sides Medium Top Style

Description

The men's hair short sides medium top style is a classic haircut that features short hair on the sides and a slightly longer length on the top. The sides are usually cut with clippers, while the top is left long enough to style in various ways. This style is popular because it's easy to maintain and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How to Get the Men's Hair Short Sides Medium Top Style

If you're interested in getting the men's hair short sides medium top style, the first step is to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in this cut. When you meet with your stylist, be sure to bring a picture of the style you want so they can get a better idea of what you're looking for.

Choosing the Right Length

When it comes to the length of your hair, there are a few things to consider. The sides should be cut short, usually with a #2 or #3 clipper guard, while the top can be left longer. The exact length of the top will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to keep the top shorter to create more height and lengthen your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the men's hair short sides medium top style:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Sit in the barber's chair and explain the style you want.
  3. The barber will start by using clippers to cut the sides of your hair, usually with a #2 or #3 clipper guard.
  4. Next, the barber will use scissors to cut the top of your hair to the desired length.
  5. The barber will then blend the sides and top together using clippers and scissors.
  6. Finally, the barber will style your hair using a product of your choice.

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Hair Short Sides Medium Top Style

Here are some tips for maintaining your men's hair short sides medium top style:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.
  • Try different hairstyles to keep your look fresh and interesting.

FAQ

Is the men's hair short sides medium top style suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the men's hair short sides medium top style can be adapted to suit all face shapes, although the length of the top may need to be adjusted to flatter your face shape.

What kind of product should I use to style my hair?

The type of product you use will depend on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. A pomade, wax, or gel can help you achieve a sleek, polished look, while a matte paste or clay can give you a more textured, natural look.

Hair Care Tips for the Men's Hair Short Sides Medium Top Style

To keep your men's hair short sides medium top style looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good-quality hairbrush to help distribute your hair's natural oils and prevent tangling.

Overall, the men's hair short sides medium top style is a timeless haircut that can be adapted to suit any man's style. With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve a clean, modern look that will never go out of fashion.
