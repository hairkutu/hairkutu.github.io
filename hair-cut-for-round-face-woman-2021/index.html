Hair Cut For Round Face Woman 2021 A Guide To The Perfect Style

Are you a woman with a round face looking for the perfect haircut in 2021? Look no further. This article will guide you through the best haircuts for your face shape, how to achieve them, tips for maintaining your new style, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for a round face, it's all about creating the illusion of length and angles. A few key things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline as they can make your face appear wider
  • Choose a cut with layers and texture to add movement and dimension
  • Consider a side-swept fringe to create angles and draw attention to your eyes
  • Opt for a length that falls below the chin to elongate the face

Some popular haircuts for round face women in 2021 include:

  • Long layers with face-framing angles
  • A textured bob with a side-swept fringe
  • An asymmetrical lob (long bob) with layers
  • A layered shag with side-swept bangs

Step by Step Guide to Achieving Your New Style

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut for your face shape, it's time to bring it to life. Here's a step by step guide to achieving your new style:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to ensure the chosen haircut is right for your hair type and texture
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products that suit your hair type and texture
  3. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement
  5. Style your hair as desired, using a curling iron or straightener if necessary
  6. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil for added shine and hold

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Maintaining your new haircut is essential to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

  • Regularly trim your hair to avoid split ends and maintain the shape of your cut
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair
  • Invest in quality hair products that suit your hair type and texture
  • Consider changing up your style with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips

FAQ

What hairstyles should round face women avoid?

Round face women should avoid hairstyles that add width to their face, such as blunt cuts that end at the jawline, full bangs, and center parts.

Can round face women wear short hair?

Absolutely. Round face women can wear short hair as long as it's styled in a way that creates length and angles. Textured bobs, pixie cuts with volume, and asymmetrical cuts are all great options.

What hair color looks best on round face women?

When it comes to hair color, there's no one size fits all answer. However, highlights and balayage can help create dimension and movement in round face women's hair.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Women

Aside from regular haircuts and maintenance, there are some additional hair care tips that can help keep your hair looking its best:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while you sleep
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your hairline and lead to breakage
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your round face in 2021 and beyond. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
