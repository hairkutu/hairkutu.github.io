How To Style Hair For Chubby Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Aug 14, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a chubby face, you may find it difficult to style your hair in a way that flatters your facial features. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a look that is both flattering and stylish. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to style hair for chubby face.

How to Style Hair for Chubby Face

The right haircut can make a huge difference when it comes to styling hair for chubby face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Choose a Flattering Haircut

When choosing a haircut, look for styles that elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts can all help to create a more flattering look. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or pixie cuts.

2. Embrace Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Consider adding waves or curls to your hair, or try a messy, tousled style. Avoid overly sleek or straight styles, as these can make your face appear wider.

3. Play with Partings

Changing your parting can also help to create a more flattering look. Try a deep side part, which can help to elongate your face, or a middle part with volume at the crown, which can create the illusion of height.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style hair for chubby face:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Towel-dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

2. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to add volume and lift at the roots. Once your hair is dry, use a cool shot of air to set the style.

3. Add Texture

Using a curling iron or a straightener, add texture to your hair. Create loose waves or curls, or add volume and texture to the crown of your head. Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and hold.

4. Choose Your Parting

Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering one for your face shape. A deep side part can help to elongate your face, while a middle part with volume at the crown can create height.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Hair for Chubby Face

Here are some additional tips for styling hair for chubby face:

1. Avoid Overly Sleek Styles

Straight, sleek styles can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for styles with volume and texture.

2. Don't Overdo the Layers

While layers can help to create a more flattering look, too many layers can add volume to the sides of your face. Opt for long layers that start below your chin.

3. Add Height at the Crown

Creating height at the crown of your head can help to elongate your face. Consider adding volume with a volumizing mousse or by backcombing your hair.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a chubby face?

The best haircut for a chubby face is one that elongates your face and draws attention away from your cheeks. Consider long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

How can I make my face look slimmer with my hair?

To make your face look slimmer with your hair, choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Should I avoid bangs if I have a chubby face?

Not necessarily. Side-swept bangs can be a flattering choice for those with a chubby face, as they help to create a more oval-shaped face. Avoid blunt bangs, however, which can add volume to the sides of your face.

Hair Care for Chubby Face

Caring for your hair is important for maintaining a healthy, shiny look. Here are some tips for hair care for chubby face:

1. Use Products for Your Hair Type

Using products that are formulated for your hair type can help to keep your hair healthy and strong. Whether you have curly, straight, or wavy hair, choose products that are designed to work with your hair type.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Using heat tools can damage your hair over time. To protect your hair from heat damage, use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or straightener.

3. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or even less often if possible.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

In conclusion, styling hair for chubby face can be a challenge, but with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a look that is both flattering and stylish. Follow these tips and tricks to create a hairstyle that accentuates your best features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
