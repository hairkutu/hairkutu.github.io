How Many Mm Is A 3 Clipper Guard

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to haircuts, clipper guards are essential tools for barbers and hairstylists. These guards come in different sizes, each with a corresponding length in millimeters. One of the most commonly used clipper guards is the #3 guard. But how many mm is a #3 clipper guard, exactly? In this blog post, we'll discuss everything you need to know about the #3 clipper guard and its length in millimeters.

Description

A clipper guard is a detachable comb that is attached to the blades of a hair clipper. It is used to control the length of the hair that is cut. The lower the number of the guard, the shorter the hair will be. The #3 clipper guard is the third guard in the series, and it is commonly used for buzz cuts and crew cuts.

How to Use a #3 Clipper Guard

Using a #3 clipper guard is pretty straightforward. Here's how:

  1. Attach the #3 clipper guard to your clipper.
  2. Turn on the clipper and run it through the hair in the direction of the hair growth.
  3. Repeat until you have reached the desired length.

It's important to note that the length of the hair will depend on the starting length of the hair and the type of clipper used.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use a #3 clipper guard:

  1. Start with dry, clean hair.
  2. Attach the #3 clipper guard to your clipper.
  3. Start at the back of the head and work your way up. Run the clipper through the hair in the direction of the hair growth.
  4. Repeat until you have reached the desired length.
  5. Move to the sides of the head, following the same process.
  6. Trim around the ears and the neckline using a smaller clipper guard or trimmer.
  7. Blend the hair using a comb and scissors if necessary.
  8. Finish with a styling product if desired.

Remember to clean your clipper and the clipper guard after use to maintain its performance and longevity.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a #3 clipper guard:

  • Start with a longer guard if you're not sure about the length.
  • Use a comb to guide the clipper for a more even cut.
  • Don't apply too much pressure with the clipper to avoid a choppy cut.
  • Take your time and work in small sections for a more precise cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the #3 clipper guard:

What is the length of a #3 clipper guard?

A #3 clipper guard has a length of 9.5mm or 3/8 of an inch.

What hairstyles can you achieve with a #3 clipper guard?

A #3 clipper guard is commonly used for buzz cuts, crew cuts, and other short hairstyles. It can also be used for blending and tapering.

Can you use a #3 clipper guard for long hair?

No, a #3 clipper guard is not recommended for long hair as it will only cut off a small amount of hair. It is best used for short haircuts.

Hair Care

After a haircut using a #3 clipper guard, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and avoid split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking and feeling its best after a #3 clipper guard haircut.

Conclusion

A #3 clipper guard is a versatile tool that can be used for a variety of short hairstyles. It has a length of 9.5mm or 3/8 of an inch, making it perfect for buzz cuts, crew cuts, and other short haircuts. By following the proper techniques and taking care of your hair, you can achieve a stylish and well-maintained look with a #3 clipper guard.
