What Color Hair Looks Good With Brown Eyes A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hair color to complement your brown eyes? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll provide tips and tricks on what hair colors look best with brown eyes. From warm tones to cool shades, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Brown eyes are versatile and can look good with a variety of hair colors. However, some colors can enhance the natural beauty of brown eyes more than others. The right hair color can make your eyes pop and bring out their warm, rich tones. In this guide, we’ll explore which hair colors look best with brown eyes and how to achieve them.

How to

When choosing a hair color to complement your brown eyes, it’s important to consider your skin tone as well. Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors, while cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors. Here are some hair color options to consider:

Warm Hair Colors

Warm hair colors complement brown eyes by bringing out their golden and amber tones. Some warm hair color options include:

  • Golden blonde
  • Caramel brown
  • Copper red
  • Auburn

Cool Hair Colors

Cool hair colors provide a striking contrast to brown eyes and can make them appear more intense. Some cool hair color options include:

  • Platinum blonde
  • Ash brown
  • Jet black
  • Burgundy

Step by Step

If you’re unsure which hair color to choose, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a color that complements your skin tone and eye color. If you decide to dye your hair at home, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a hair dye that complements your skin tone and eye color.
  2. Do a strand test to ensure you’re not allergic to the dye.
  3. Apply the dye evenly to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down.
  4. Leave the dye on for the recommended time, then rinse it out thoroughly.
  5. Apply a conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.

Tips

  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create dimension.
  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair color vibrant.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and fade the color.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

FAQ

What’s the best hair color for brown eyes and fair skin?

If you have fair skin and brown eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde and caramel brown can complement your features.

What’s the best hair color for brown eyes and olive skin?

If you have olive skin and brown eyes, cool hair colors like ash brown and burgundy can complement your features.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it’s important to do a patch test before dyeing your hair. Apply a small amount of the dye on your skin and wait 24 hours to see if you have a reaction.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what hair color looks good with brown eyes, it’s time to make a change! Whether you choose warm or cool tones, the right hair color can enhance your natural beauty and make your brown eyes shine.
