What Is My Hair Type African American Quiz

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to the ultimate guide to understanding your hair type as an African American. One of the most common questions that people ask when it comes to their hair is, "What is my hair type?" Your hair type refers to the natural texture and curl pattern of your hair, and as an African American, it can be tough to figure out. This guide will provide you with all the information you need to know about your hair type and how to determine it with an African American hair quiz.

Description

Knowing your hair type is vital for ensuring that you're using the right products and techniques to take care of your hair. There are several hair types, and each requires a specific regimen to maintain its health and beauty. The hair types include:

  • Type 1: Straight hair
  • Type 2: Wavy hair
  • Type 3: Curly hair
  • Type 4: Coily hair

If you're an African American, your hair lies within the type 3 and type 4 categories, which can be further broken down into subtypes. Understanding your hair subtype is essential in helping you choose the right hair products and styles.

How to

The best way to determine your hair type is by taking an African American hair quiz. The quiz will ask you a series of questions that will help determine your hair type and subtype. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to take a hair quiz:

  1. Find an African American hair quiz online.
  2. Answer the questions honestly and to the best of your knowledge.
  3. Submit your answers.
  4. Your hair type and subtype will be revealed.

Once you know your hair type and subtype, you can find products and styles that work best for your hair.

Step by step

Here are the steps to take an African American hair quiz:

  1. Search for "African American hair quiz" on Google.
  2. Choose a quiz that looks reliable and trustworthy.
  3. Start answering the questions provided in the quiz.
  4. Be honest and provide accurate answers.
  5. Submit your answers and wait for the results.

Once you get your results, take some time to read about your hair type and subtype so that you can understand what products and styles will work best for your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you take an African American hair quiz:

  • Make sure you're taking the quiz from a reliable source.
  • Be honest when answering the questions.
  • Take note of your hair's thickness, porosity, and elasticity.
  • Read up on your hair type and subtype to find the right hair products and styles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about African American hair type:

What is the difference between hair type and hair texture?

Your hair type refers to the shape of your hair strands, while your hair texture refers to the overall look and feel of your hair, such as its thickness and porosity.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how often you use styling products. Generally, washing your hair once or twice a week is enough for most people.

What are some good hair products for African American hair?

Some good hair products for African American hair include leave-in conditioners, hair oils, and deep conditioning treatments.

Hair Care

Once you know your hair type and subtype, you can choose the right products and styles to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for taking care of African American hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and always use a heat protectant when you do use them.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and strong.

By taking an African American hair quiz, you can gain a better understanding of your hair type and subtype, which will help you take better care of your hair. Remember to always use the right hair products and styles to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
