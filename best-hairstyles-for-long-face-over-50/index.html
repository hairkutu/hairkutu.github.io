Best Hairstyles For Long Face Over 50 In 2023

Written by William Sep 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair and face shape may change, and we need to find hairstyles that flatter our features. For women over 50 with long faces, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that complements their facial structure. In this article, we will share with you the best hairstyles for long face over 50 that will make you look fabulous and youthful.

  • Best Hairstyles for Long Face over 50
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for long face over 50, it's essential to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape will help you choose the most flattering hairstyle for your features. Here are the steps to determine your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin.
  4. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and long. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Long Face over 50

Now that you know your face shape let's look at the best hairstyles for long face over 50:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and movement to your hair, making your face appear wider. They also help to soften the angles of a long face shape. You can add layers to any length of hair, from shoulder-length to long locks.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to balance the length of a long face shape. They draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, creating the illusion of width. You can pair side-swept bangs with long layers or a bob haircut.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can be a great option for women over 50 with long faces. A chin-length or shoulder-length bob can help to shorten the length of your face and create the illusion of width. You can add layers or bangs to a bob haircut to customize the look to your liking.

4. Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle, a pixie cut may be the perfect choice. A short haircut can help to balance the length of a long face shape and highlight your facial features. You can add texture or side-swept bangs to a pixie cut to create a more personalized look.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you know the best hairstyles for long face over 50, here are some tips for styling your hair:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

A volumizing shampoo can help to add body and fullness to your hair, making it appear thicker and more youthful. Look for a shampoo with ingredients like keratin, collagen, or biotin.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can add volume and lift to your roots, creating the illusion of width. Use a round brush to smooth out the ends and create a polished look.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have curly or wavy hair, embrace your natural texture. You can use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and create a more youthful look.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Long Face over 50

1. Can I Wear Short Hair with a Long Face?

Yes, you can wear short hair with a long face. A short haircut can help to balance the length of your face and highlight your facial features.

2. Should I Avoid Middle Parts?

Women with long faces should avoid middle parts as they can make the face appear longer. Instead, opt for a side part or side-swept bangs to create the illusion of width.

3. Can I Color My Hair?

Yes, you can color your hair. Adding highlights or lowlights can add depth and dimension to your hair, making it appear thicker and more youthful.

Hair Care Tips for Women over 50

1. Use a Heat Protectant

When using heat styling tools like a flat iron or curling iron, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Look for a product that contains ingredients like argan oil or keratin.

2. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, depending on the length and texture of your hair.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Look for a product that contains ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or avocado oil.

In conclusion, the best hairstyles for long face over 50 are long layers, side-swept bangs, bob haircuts, and pixie cuts. Remember to consider your face shape when choosing a hairstyle, and use our styling tips to create a youthful and flattering look. Don't forget to take care of your hair with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments to keep it healthy and vibrant.
