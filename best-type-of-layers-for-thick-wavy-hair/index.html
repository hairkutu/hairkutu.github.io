The Best Type Of Layers For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Eveline Apr 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unmanageable, frizzy hair? Do you have thick, wavy hair that you just can't seem to tame? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with finding the right hairstyle for their hair type. But don't worry, we're here to help. In this article, we'll discuss the best type of layers for thick wavy hair, and provide you with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Layers are a great way to add volume and texture to thick, wavy hair. They can also help to reduce frizz and make your hair more manageable. But not all layers are created equal. The best type of layers for thick wavy hair are long layers. These layers are typically cut at an angle, and are longer in the front than in the back. This helps to create a flattering, face-framing effect, while still allowing your hair to retain its natural shape and texture.

How to

If you're considering getting long layers for your thick wavy hair, it's important to find a stylist who is experienced with this type of cut. They will be able to assess your hair type and texture, and determine the best length and angle for your layers. You should also bring in pictures of styles that you like, so that your stylist can get a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step by Step

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced with long layers for thick wavy hair
  2. Bring in pictures of styles that you like
  3. Discuss the best length and angle for your layers with your stylist
  4. Sit back and relax while your stylist works their magic
  5. Enjoy your new, more manageable hairstyle!

Tips

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's wet
  • Apply a frizz-reducing serum or cream to your hair after washing it
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair and cause it to become more frizzy
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Q: Will long layers work for all types of thick wavy hair?

A: While long layers can work well for many people with thick wavy hair, it's important to find a stylist who can assess your hair type and texture to determine if this is the best option for you.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, manageable hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and nourished
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on a daily basis
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and getting the right type of layers for your thick wavy hair, you can achieve a beautiful, manageable hairstyle that you'll love showing off.
