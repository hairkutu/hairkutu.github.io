Which Glasses Suit My Face Online A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver May 26, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for the perfect pair of glasses to suit your face online? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to find the right style for you. However, with the right guidance, finding the perfect pair of glasses for your face shape can be a breeze. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to choose the perfect glasses for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Popular Glasses Styles
  • Tips for Choosing Glasses Online
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

Determining Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the perfect pair of glasses, it's important to determine your face shape. The following are the most common face shapes:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Oval
  • Diamond

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from forehead to chin. Based on these measurements, you can determine your face shape.

Round Face

If you have a round face, your face width and length are approximately the same, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face. To elongate your face and make it appear slimmer, choose glasses with angular frames such as square or rectangle frames.

Square Face

If you have a square face, your face width and length are approximately the same, and your jawline is the most prominent feature of your face. To soften your angular features, choose glasses with round or oval frames.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, your forehead is the widest part of your face, and your jawline is the narrowest. To balance out your features, choose glasses with bottom-heavy frames such as aviator or cat-eye frames.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, your face length is greater than your face width, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face. Lucky for you, almost any glasses style will suit your face shape.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are approximately the same width. To balance out your features, choose glasses with oval or round frames.

Popular Glasses Styles

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the perfect glasses style. The following are popular glasses styles:

  • Aviator
  • Rectangle
  • Round
  • Square
  • Cat-eye

Aviator glasses feature a teardrop-shaped lens and thin metal frames. Rectangle glasses have a rectangular shape and are perfect for those with round or oval faces. Round glasses have circular lenses and are perfect for those with square or heart-shaped faces. Square glasses have a square shape and are perfect for those with round faces. Cat-eye glasses have an upswept frame and are perfect for those with heart-shaped faces.

Tips for Choosing Glasses Online

Choosing glasses online can be tricky, but with these tips, you'll find the perfect pair in no time:

  • Use the virtual try-on feature to see how glasses look on your face
  • Read reviews to see how glasses fit on other people
  • Choose a reputable website with a good return policy
  • Consider the size of the glasses and how they will fit on your face
  • Choose a style that complements your face shape and personal style

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if glasses will fit my face?

Check the frame measurements to ensure they will fit your face. You can also use the virtual try-on feature to see how glasses will look on your face.

Can I return glasses if they don't fit?

Yes, most reputable online glasses retailers have a return policy. Make sure to read the policy before purchasing glasses.

What if I don't like the glasses I ordered?

Most online glasses retailers have a return policy that allows you to return glasses if you don't like them. Make sure to read the policy before purchasing glasses.

Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

If you wear glasses, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it doesn't interfere with your glasses. The following are hair care tips for glasses wearers:

  • Choose hairstyles that won't interfere with your glasses
  • Avoid using hair products that will transfer to your glasses
  • Keep your glasses clean to avoid transferring dirt and oil to your hair

Now that you know how to choose the perfect glasses for your face shape, it's time to start shopping online. With our guide, you'll find the perfect pair in no time.
