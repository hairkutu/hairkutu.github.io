The Best Volumizer For Big Bouncy Hair

Written by Petter May 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair? Do you dream of big, bouncy curls or voluminous waves? Look no further than the best volumizer on the market! In this article, we'll dive into everything you need to know about adding volume to your hair, from how to use it to our top product recommendations.

What is a Volumizer?

A volumizer is a hair product that adds body and fullness to your hair. It typically comes in the form of a spray, mousse, or powder, and is applied directly to your roots or throughout your hair. Volumizers work by lifting your hair away from your scalp, creating the illusion of more hair and more volume.

How to Use a Volumizer

Using a volumizer is easy! Simply apply the product to your roots, either directly from the bottle or by spraying it onto your hands and then rubbing it into your scalp. For extra volume, you can also apply it throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Then, blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on lifting your roots away from your scalp as you dry.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Volumizer:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair as normal, then towel dry.
  2. Apply a quarter-sized amount of volumizer to your roots, either directly from the bottle or by spraying it onto your hands and then rubbing it into your scalp.
  3. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends for extra volume.
  4. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on lifting your roots away from your scalp as you dry.
  5. Style your hair as desired, and enjoy your voluminous locks!

Tips for Using a Volumizer

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a volumizer:

  • Start with a small amount of product, and add more as needed.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections, focusing on one section at a time.
  • Use a round brush to lift your roots away from your scalp as you dry.
  • Finish with a blast of cool air to set your style.

FAQ about Volumizers

What type of hair is best for volumizers?

Volumizers work best on fine, limp hair that needs a boost. However, they can be used on any hair type to add body and fullness.

Can I use a volumizer every day?

Yes, you can use a volumizer every day. However, be sure to wash your hair regularly to avoid product buildup.

Can I use a volumizer with other styling products?

Yes, you can use a volumizer with other styling products. However, be sure to apply the volumizer first, before any other products.

  2. Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray
  3. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Dryspun Texture Spray

Hair Care Tips for Voluminous Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when trying to achieve voluminous hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look flat.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup.
  • Avoid heavy conditioners, which can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and be gentle to avoid breakage.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools.

With these tips and our top product recommendations, you'll be well on your way to achieving big, bouncy, voluminous hair!
