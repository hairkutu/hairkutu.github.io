Long On Top Short On Sides Haircut Curly The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jun 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both stylish and practical, the long on top short on sides haircut curly is the perfect choice. This versatile cut can work for any hair type and face shape, and it's easy to maintain. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of your curls.

Description

The long on top short on sides haircut curly is a style that features long hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair on top can be left to flow freely, or it can be styled into a variety of different looks, such as a quiff, pompadour, or messy fringe. The sides and back are typically buzzed or faded, creating a clean and modern look.

How to Get the Long on Top Short on Sides Haircut Curly

The first step in getting the long on top short on sides haircut curly is to find a good stylist who has experience working with curly hair. They will be able to help you choose the right length and style for your face shape and hair type. Once you've decided on the style, the stylist will use clippers to buzz the sides and back to the desired length, while leaving the top longer. They may also use scissors to trim the top and create layers that will help enhance your curls.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the long on top short on sides haircut curly:

  1. Choose a stylist with experience working with curly hair.
  2. Decide on the length and style of your haircut.
  3. Have the stylist buzz the sides and back to the desired length.
  4. Have the stylist trim the top with scissors to create layers that will enhance your curls.
  5. Style your hair as desired using a curl-enhancing product.

Tips for Styling the Long on Top Short on Sides Haircut Curly

Styling curly hair can be tricky, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a variety of different looks with your long on top short on sides haircut curly. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your hairstyle:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser to enhance your curls.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Try different styling techniques, such as twisting, scrunching, or braiding, to create different textures and looks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short on sides haircut curly:

Q: Will this hairstyle work for all hair types?

A: Yes, the long on top short on sides haircut curly can work for any hair type, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often do I need to get this haircut?

A: This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly the buzzed sides and back start to grow out. You may need to get a touch-up every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this haircut. You can use a styling product to create a sleek look, or you can leave some curls loose for a more casual look.

Hair Care for the Long on Top Short on Sides Haircut Curly

To keep your long on top short on sides haircut curly looking its best, it's important to take good care of your curls. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your curls.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the long on top short on sides haircut curly with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a casual and carefree vibe, this haircut is sure to turn heads.
