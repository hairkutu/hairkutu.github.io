What Hairstyle Suits Round Chubby Face A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 22, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your round chubby face? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this guide, we will provide you with tips and ideas on how to choose the best hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Whether you have short or long hair, there is a hairstyle out there that will enhance your features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Description

Round chubby faces have soft and curved features, which can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds length to your face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline. It's essential to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only accentuate the roundness.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for a round chubby face, there are a few things to consider:

1. Length

Long hair can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. If you have short hair, consider adding layers to create the illusion of length.

2. Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to balance out your features and make your face appear longer. Opt for side-swept bangs that fall below your eyebrows.

3. Volume

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only accentuate the roundness. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a messy bun or a high ponytail.

Step by Step Guide to Hairstyles for Round Chubby Face

1. Long Layers

If you have long hair, consider adding long layers that frame your face. This will create the illusion of length and slim down your face. To style, blow-dry your hair and use a round brush to add volume to the top of your head. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

If you have short hair, consider adding side-swept bangs that fall below your eyebrows. This will balance out your features and make your face appear longer. To style, blow-dry your hair and use a flat iron to create a sleek look. Finish with a shine serum to add shine and hold the style in place.

3. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a great way to add volume to the top of your head and create the illusion of length. To style, gather your hair into a high ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Pull out a few strands to create a messy look and finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Hairstyles for Round Chubby Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling hairstyles for round chubby face:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that add volume to the top of your head, such as mousse or volumizing spray. Avoid products that add volume to the sides of your face, such as thickening spray.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this will only accentuate the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. Try different hairstyles and see how they make you feel. The most important thing is to feel confident and beautiful in your hair.

FAQ

1. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round chubby face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round chubby face. Just make sure to add volume to the top of your head to create the illusion of length.

2. Should I avoid short hair if I have a round chubby face?

No, you don't have to avoid short hair if you have a round chubby face. Just make sure to add layers and side-swept bangs to create the illusion of length.

3. How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Round Chubby Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when styling hairstyles for round chubby face:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

When styling your hair with heat tools, use a heat protectant to prevent damage and breakage.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and dry it out. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every two days.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When brushing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that suit round chubby faces. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds length to your face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline. With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
