Short Black Hairstyles For Round Fat Faces How To Rock Your Look In 2023

Written by Petter Jun 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round, fat face? Look no further! In this article, we'll show you how to rock short black hairstyles that not only flatter your face shape but also make a statement in 2023. From bobs to pixies, we've got you covered.

How to Choose the Right Short Black Hairstyle for Your Round Fat Face

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for a round, fat face, it's all about creating balance. You want a hairstyle that will elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Tip #1: Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that will help to elongate your face.

Tip #2: Choose Layers Wisely

Layering can be a great way to add volume and texture to your hair, but be careful not to go overboard. Too many layers can create a rounder shape, so opt for longer layers that will help to elongate your face.

Tip #3: Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in how your hairstyle flatters your face shape. For example, if you have thin hair, a pixie cut may not be the best option as it can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for a bob with layers to add volume and texture.

Short Black Hairstyles for Round Fat Faces

Now that you know what to look for when choosing a short black hairstyle for your round, fat face, let's take a look at some of our top picks for 2023:

The Blunt Bob

The blunt bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's perfect for round, fat faces as it creates a sleek, elongated look. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist for a straight cut that falls just above your shoulders.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a daring hairstyle that can be incredibly flattering on round, fat faces. It's important to choose the right length and texture for your face shape, so consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect pixie cut for you.

The Asymmetrical Bob

The asymmetrical bob is a fun and edgy hairstyle that can be customized to flatter your face shape. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair shorter on one side and longer on the other to create an asymmetrical look.

FAQ: Short Black Hairstyles for Round Fat Faces

Q: Can I still wear a short hairstyle if I have a round, fat face?

A: Absolutely! It's all about finding the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Short hairstyles can be incredibly flattering on round, fat faces when done correctly.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on the length and style of your hair, as well as your personal preference. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the health and shape of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Black Hairstyles

Now that you've found the perfect short black hairstyle for your round, fat face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

Tip #1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Short hair can be prone to dryness, especially if you have a lot of layers. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip #2: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or every three days if possible.

Tip #3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Also, try to limit your use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you'll be rocking your short black hair with confidence and style in 2023.
