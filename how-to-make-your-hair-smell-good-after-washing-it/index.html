How To Make Your Hair Smell Good After Washing It

Written by Frank Jun 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone who loves the feeling of freshly washed hair, but doesn't love the smell of your shampoo or conditioner, you're not alone. Many people struggle with the scent of their hair after washing it, and it can be frustrating to try and find a solution. Luckily, there are a few easy tricks you can use to make your hair smell good after washing it. Here's how:

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Smell Good After Washing It
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step

1. Use a Scented Shampoo and Conditioner

If you're looking for a quick and easy solution, try switching to a scented shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain essential oils or natural fragrances like lavender, rose, or peppermint. These scents can help to mask any unwanted smells and leave your hair smelling fresh and clean.

2. Apply Perfume or Body Spray to Your Hair

If you're looking for a stronger scent, you can apply perfume or body spray to your hair after washing it. Just be careful not to overdo it, as too much perfume can be overwhelming. Spray the perfume or body spray onto your hairbrush and then brush it through your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a great option for those who want to freshen up their hair without having to wash it. Look for a dry shampoo that contains a pleasant scent, like coconut or vanilla, and apply it to your roots. Not only will it help to absorb any excess oil, but it will leave your hair smelling great.

4. Rinse Your Hair with Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is a natural remedy that can help to remove any unwanted odors from your hair. Mix one part apple cider vinegar with two parts water and pour it over your hair after washing. Let it sit for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cold water. The vinegar smell will disappear once your hair is dry, leaving your hair smelling fresh and clean.

5. Use Essential Oils

Essential oils are a great way to add a natural, pleasant scent to your hair. Mix a few drops of your favorite essential oil with a carrier oil like coconut or jojoba oil and apply it to your hair. You can also add a few drops of essential oil to your shampoo or conditioner for an added scent boost.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you make your hair smell good after washing it:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent any buildup of oils or dirt that can cause unwanted odors.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can contribute to a less-than-pleasant scent.
  • Avoid smoking or spending time in smoky environments, as the smell can linger in your hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush that will help to distribute natural oils throughout your hair, keeping it healthy and smelling fresh.

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people should aim to wash their hair at least once a week. If you have very oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

What causes bad smells in hair?

Bad smells in hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including sweat, bacteria, and environmental factors like smoke or pollution.

Hair Care

Remember, taking care of your hair is an important part of keeping it smelling great. Be sure to use high-quality hair care products that are gentle on your hair and scalp, and avoid using excessive heat or styling products that can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair smell good after washing it and enjoy the feeling of freshly washed hair without any unwanted odors.
