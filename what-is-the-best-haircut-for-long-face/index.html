What Is The Best Haircut For Long Face In 2023

Written by Frank Mar 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a long face, you may be wondering what the best haircut is for you. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for long faces in 2023. We'll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to the best haircuts and styling tips. Let's get started!

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead and a narrow chin. The length of the face is longer than the width. If you have a long face, you want to choose a haircut that will balance out your features and make your face appear shorter. You also want to avoid haircuts that will make your face look even longer.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into the best haircuts for long faces, it's important to determine your face shape. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead is the widest part of your face and your jawline is narrow, you have a long face shape.

Best Haircuts for Long Faces

The best haircuts for long faces are those that add width to the face and create balance. Here are some of the best haircuts for long faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great haircut for long faces because they add volume and movement to the hair. This helps to create the illusion of width in the face. Make sure to avoid blunt cuts, as these can make your face appear even longer.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften a long face. They create the illusion of width in the forehead area and help to balance out the length of the face. Avoid straight across bangs, as these can make your face appear even longer.

3. Wavy Bob

A wavy bob is a great haircut for long faces because it adds volume and movement to the hair. This helps to create the illusion of width in the face. Make sure to avoid straight and blunt cuts, as these can make your face appear even longer.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a great haircut for long faces because it adds volume and texture to the hair. This helps to create the illusion of width in the face. Make sure to avoid a cut that is too short, as this can make your face appear even longer.

Styling Tips

Once you have the right haircut, it's important to style it in a way that flatters your face shape. Here are some styling tips for long faces:

1. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair can help to create the illusion of width in the face. You can do this by using a volumizing mousse or by blow drying your hair upside down.

2. Avoid Center Parts

A center part can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for a side part, which can help to create the illusion of width in the face.

3. Play with Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help to create the illusion of width in the face. You can do this by using a texturizing spray or by curling your hair.

FAQ

Q. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long face?

A. Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a long face. Just make sure to add volume to your hair to create the illusion of width in the face.

Q. Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a long face?

A. No, you don't have to avoid short haircuts if you have a long face. Just make sure to choose a cut that adds volume and texture to the hair.

Q. Can I wear bangs if I have a long face?

A. Yes, you can wear bangs if you have a long face. Just make sure to choose side-swept bangs that help to create the illusion of width in the forehead area.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for long faces:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

When styling your hair with heat tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, the best haircut for long faces is one that adds width to the face and creates balance. Long layers, side-swept bangs, a wavy bob, and a pixie cut are all great haircuts for long faces. Remember to style your hair in a way that flatters your face shape and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
