Hairstyles To Cover Up Thinning Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 10, 2023 · 10 min read

Thinning hair can be a source of insecurity for many people. While there are many reasons why hair can thin, including genetics, aging, and medical conditions, there are a number of hairstyles that can help to cover up thinning areas and give you the appearance of fuller, thicker hair. In this article, we’ll take a look at some of the most popular hairstyles to cover up thinning hair, as well as offer tips on how to care for your hair and prevent further thinning.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Volume
  3. Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Products for Thinning Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle to cover up thinning hair, there are a few key factors to consider. First, you’ll want to think about the overall shape of your face and the areas where your hair is thinning. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to opt for a style that adds height and volume to the crown of your head, while those with a square face may want to choose a style that softens their jawline.

Additionally, you’ll want to consider the texture of your hair. If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, you may be able to create volume and hide thinning areas simply by letting your hair air-dry and using a bit of styling product. Those with straight hair may need to use hot tools like a curling iron or flat iron to create volume and texture.

Some popular hairstyles for covering up thinning hair include:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for those with thinning hair, as it can help to create the illusion of thicker hair by keeping the hair short and close to the head. This style works particularly well for those with straight or wavy hair, and can be customized to suit a variety of face shapes.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for those with thinning hair, as it can add volume and texture to the hair. This style works particularly well for those with straight or wavy hair, and can be customized to suit a variety of face shapes.

3. Layered Cut

A layered cut can be a great option for those with thinning hair, as it can help to create the illusion of thicker hair by adding volume and texture. This style works particularly well for those with straight or wavy hair, and can be customized to suit a variety of face shapes.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Volume

If you’re looking to create volume and texture in your hair to help cover up thinning areas, there are a few steps you can follow:

1. Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for thinning hair. This will help to nourish your hair and create a healthy base for styling.

2. Apply a Volumizing Product

Next, apply a volumizing product to your hair, such as a mousse or root-lifting spray. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and separate your hair at the roots. This will help to create volume and texture in your hair.

4. Use Hot Tools

If needed, use hot tools like a curling iron or flat iron to create additional texture and volume in your hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle and styling your hair to create volume and texture, there are a few things you can do to maintain healthy, thick hair:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to nourish your hair and prevent further thinning. Be sure to include plenty of protein, iron, and omega-3 fatty acids in your diet.

2. Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling your hair with hot tools, chemical treatments, and tight hairstyles can damage your hair and lead to further thinning. Try to limit your use of these styling techniques and opt for gentler, more natural styles instead.

3. Use Gentle Hair Products

Using hair products that are gentle and free from harsh chemicals can help to nourish your hair and prevent further thinning. Look for products that are designed specifically for thinning hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can wearing a wig help cover up thinning hair?

A: Yes, wearing a wig can be a great option for those with thinning hair. Just be sure to choose a wig that looks natural and fits well.

Q: Can stress cause hair thinning?

A: Yes, stress can contribute to hair thinning. If you’re experiencing hair loss, it’s important to talk to your doctor to rule out any underlying medical conditions.

Q: Are there any supplements that can help promote hair growth?

A: Yes, there are a number of supplements that can help promote hair growth, such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron. Talk to your doctor before taking any supplements to make sure they’re safe for you.

Hair Care Products for Thinning Hair

If you’re looking for hair care products to help promote healthy, thick hair, there are a number of options available:

1. Nioxin System Kit

The Nioxin System Kit is a three-step system designed to promote healthy hair growth and prevent further thinning. The kit includes a shampoo, conditioner, and scalp treatment.

2. Rogaine

Rogaine is a topical treatment that can help to promote hair growth and prevent further thinning. It’s available over-the-counter and is applied directly to the scalp.

3. Viviscal

Viviscal is a hair growth supplement that contains a blend of vitamins, minerals, and marine extracts designed to promote healthy hair growth and prevent further thinning.

Whether you’re looking for a new hairstyle to cover up thinning hair or simply want to promote healthy, thick hair, there are a number of options available. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can achieve the healthy, beautiful hair you’ve always wanted!
