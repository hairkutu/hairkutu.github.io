How To Make Hair Fluffy

Written by Aprilia Jun 09, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to add volume to your hair without using any harsh chemicals or hot tools, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair fluffy using simple techniques that you can do at home. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Hair Fluffy
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Make Hair Fluffy

If you want to make your hair fluffy, you'll need to focus on creating volume at the root. Here are some techniques that you can use:

1. Blow Dry Upside Down

One of the easiest ways to create volume at the root is to blow dry your hair upside down. This technique will lift your hair away from your scalp and create natural volume. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Flip your head upside down.
  3. Blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the back.
  4. Use a round brush to lift your hair away from your scalp as you blow dry.

Once you've finished blow drying, flip your head back up and style your hair as desired.

2. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair can create instant volume at the root. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with dry hair.
  2. Take a small section of hair at the crown of your head.
  3. Hold the section straight up and backcomb it with a fine-toothed comb.
  4. Spray the teased section with hairspray to hold the volume.
  5. Repeat with additional sections as desired.

Keep in mind that teasing your hair too much can cause damage, so use this technique sparingly.

3. Use Velcro Rollers

Velcro rollers are an easy and gentle way to create volume at the root. Here's how to use them:

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Section your hair into 1-2 inch sections.
  3. Roll each section of hair onto a velcro roller.
  4. Blow dry your hair on low heat until it's completely dry.
  5. Remove the rollers and style your hair as desired.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair fluffy:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair upside down or use velcro rollers to create volume at the root.
  3. Tease your hair at the crown for extra volume.
  4. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture.
  5. Style your hair as desired.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the volume.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair fluffy:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup that could weigh your hair down.
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners that could flatten your hair.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to create natural waves and volume.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute natural oils and create shine.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making hair fluffy:

How can I make my hair fluffy without heat?

You can make your hair fluffy without heat by using velcro rollers or braiding your hair overnight to create natural waves and volume.

Can I make my hair fluffy if I have fine hair?

Yes, you can make your hair fluffy even if you have fine hair. Try using a volumizing mousse or spray and blow drying your hair upside down to create lift at the root.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can cause breakage and damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein and vitamins to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to make your hair fluffy, it's time to try these techniques at home. With a little practice, you'll be able to create voluminous, bouncy hair that lasts all day!
