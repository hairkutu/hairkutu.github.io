Can Microneedling Help Regrow Hair Here s All You Need To Know

Written by Oliver Mar 26, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're dealing with hair loss, you're not alone. Hair loss is a common problem that affects millions of people worldwide. Fortunately, there are many hair restoration treatments available in the market, including microneedling.

In this blog post, we'll explore the question of "can microneedling help regrow hair?" and provide you with all the information you need to know to make an informed decision about this treatment option.

What is Microneedling?

Microneedling is a cosmetic treatment that involves using a device called a dermaroller to create tiny punctures in the skin. These punctures stimulate the production of collagen and elastin, two proteins that are essential for healthy skin.

Microneedling is a popular treatment for a variety of skin concerns, including wrinkles, acne scars, and hyperpigmentation. However, recent studies have shown that microneedling may also be effective for hair restoration.

How Does Microneedling Help Regrow Hair?

The theory behind microneedling for hair restoration is that the tiny punctures created by the dermaroller stimulate the production of growth factors, which can help promote hair growth. Additionally, microneedling may help increase blood flow to the scalp, which can also promote hair growth.

Studies have shown that microneedling can be an effective treatment for hair loss. In one study, participants who received microneedling treatments saw a significant increase in hair growth after 12 weeks.

How to Perform Microneedling for Hair Restoration

If you're interested in trying microneedling for hair restoration, it's important to do it safely and correctly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Scalp

Before performing microneedling, it's important to cleanse your scalp thoroughly. Use a gentle shampoo and warm water to remove any dirt or debris.

Step 2: Apply Numbing Cream

Microneedling can be a little uncomfortable, so it's a good idea to apply a numbing cream to your scalp before the treatment. Follow the instructions on the cream carefully.

Step 3: Use a Dermaroller

Use a dermaroller with 0.25mm-0.5mm needles to create tiny punctures in your scalp. Roll the dermaroller over your scalp in different directions, being careful not to apply too much pressure.

Step 4: Apply Hair Growth Serum

After microneedling, apply a hair growth serum to your scalp. This can help nourish your hair follicles and promote hair growth.

Step 5: Repeat the Treatment

For best results, repeat the microneedling treatment once a week for several weeks. You should start to see results after about 12 weeks.

Tips for Microneedling for Hair Restoration

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your microneedling treatment for hair restoration:

  • Use a high-quality dermaroller
  • Be consistent with your treatments
  • Don't apply too much pressure
  • Use a hair growth serum after microneedling
  • Be patient – it may take several weeks to see results

FAQs About Microneedling for Hair Restoration

Is Microneedling Safe for Hair Restoration?

Yes, microneedling is generally considered safe for hair restoration when done correctly.

How Long Does It Take to See Results from Microneedling for Hair Restoration?

It may take several weeks to see results from microneedling for hair restoration. Most people start to see results after about 12 weeks.

Can Microneedling Cause Hair Loss?

No, microneedling should not cause hair loss when done correctly.

Final Thoughts

Microneedling can be an effective treatment for hair restoration. By stimulating the production of growth factors and increasing blood flow to the scalp, microneedling can help promote hair growth. If you're interested in trying microneedling for hair restoration, be sure to do it safely and correctly for the best results.
