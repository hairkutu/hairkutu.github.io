How To Cut Your Own Hair Pixie Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying expensive salon prices to maintain your pixie undercut? Why not try cutting your own hair? With a little patience and the right tools, you can achieve a stylish and edgy pixie undercut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we will show you how to cut your own hair pixie undercut in a relaxed and easy-to-follow language.

Description

A pixie undercut is a trendy and stylish haircut that involves short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. It can be a challenging haircut to maintain, especially if you have to visit the salon frequently. Cutting your own hair pixie undercut can be a cost-effective and convenient solution to this problem.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Pixie Undercut

Cutting your own hair requires some preparation and the right tools. Here is what you will need:

  • Clippers with different guard lengths
  • Scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • Clips to section your hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own hair pixie undercut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use the comb to part your hair down the middle and clip up the top section.
  2. Use the clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back, starting with the longest guard length and working your way down to the shortest.
  3. Use the scissors to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. Take small sections of hair and cut at a slight angle, working your way up towards the top section.
  4. Release the top section of hair and use the scissors to cut it to the desired length. You can use the comb to hold the hair straight and cut it in a straight line or at an angle for a more edgy look.
  5. Trim any stray hairs and check the length and symmetry of your haircut in the mirror.
  6. Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the hair around your ears and neckline.
  7. Style your hair as desired using gel or pomade.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair Pixie Undercut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect pixie undercut:

  • Start with longer guard lengths when using the clippers and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use the scissors to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top for a seamless look.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises after it dries.
  • Take breaks and step away from the mirror to avoid overcutting.
  • Watch video tutorials or ask a friend to help you if you're not confident in cutting your own hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about cutting your own hair pixie undercut:

  • How often should I cut my hair pixie undercut?
  • It depends on how fast your hair grows, but every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.
  • Can I cut my hair pixie undercut wet?
  • It's best to cut your hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises after it dries.
  • What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?
  • Don't panic! Hair grows back, and you can always visit a salon to fix any mistakes.
  • Do I need to use all the tools listed?
  • No, you can use the tools you're most comfortable with.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Undercut

Maintaining a pixie undercut requires some hair care efforts. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, or use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Trim any split ends regularly to keep your hair healthy.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Cutting your own hair pixie undercut can be challenging, but it can also be a fun and rewarding experience. Follow this guide, and you'll be rocking a stylish and edgy haircut in no time!
