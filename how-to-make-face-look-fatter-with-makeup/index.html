How To Make Your Face Look Fatter With Makeup

Written by Oliver Jul 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having a thin-looking face? Do you want to add some fullness to your cheeks and jawline? If so, then you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll show you how to make your face look fatter with makeup. We’ll cover everything from the basics to advanced techniques, so whether you’re a beginner or a pro, you’ll find something useful here. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Makeup can be a powerful tool in enhancing your natural features. By using certain techniques, you can create the illusion of a fuller face. This is especially useful for those who have a thin face or want to balance out their facial proportions. In this article, we’ll show you how to use makeup to make your face look fatter.

How To

The first step in making your face look fatter with makeup is to choose the right products. You’ll need a few key items, including:

  • Foundation
  • Concealer
  • Blush
  • Contour powder or cream
  • Highlighter

Once you have your products, you can start applying them to your face. Here are the basic steps:

Step By Step

  1. Start by applying foundation all over your face. This will create a smooth base for the rest of your makeup.
  2. Next, use a concealer to cover any blemishes or dark circles under your eyes. This will help brighten up your face and make it look more awake.
  3. Now it’s time to add some color to your cheeks. Use a blush that matches your skin tone, and apply it to the apples of your cheeks. This will give your face a healthy, youthful glow.
  4. To create the illusion of a fuller face, you’ll need to contour your cheekbones. Use a contour powder or cream that is a few shades darker than your skin tone, and apply it to the hollows of your cheeks. Blend it out well to avoid any harsh lines.
  5. Finally, use a highlighter to add some shimmer to your face. Apply it to the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and your cupid’s bow. This will help create the illusion of a fuller face by drawing attention to the high points of your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your face look fatter with makeup:

  • Use a fluffy brush to apply your blush, and start with a small amount. You can always add more if needed.
  • When contouring, make sure to blend well. You don’t want any harsh lines or patches of color.
  • Choose a highlighter that complements your skin tone. If you have fair skin, go for a champagne or pearl shade. If you have medium or dark skin, try a gold or bronze shade.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your face look fatter with makeup:

Can I use bronzer instead of contour powder?

Yes, you can use bronzer to contour your cheeks. Just make sure to choose a shade that is a few shades darker than your skin tone, and blend it out well.

What if I have oily skin?

If you have oily skin, use a matte foundation and powder to avoid any shine. You can also use a matte blush and contour powder to avoid adding any extra shine to your face.

Hair Care

In addition to using makeup, you can also add fullness to your face by changing your hairstyle. Try adding some layers or waves to your hair, or go for a voluminous updo. This will help create the illusion of a fuller face.

So, there you have it – our guide to making your face look fatter with makeup. With the right products and techniques, you can create a full, youthful look that complements your natural beauty. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
