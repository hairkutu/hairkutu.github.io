How To Keep Flat Ironed Hair From Frizzing Tips And Tricks

Written by William Aug 18, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may be familiar with the struggles of trying to keep your hair straight and frizz-free. Flat ironing your hair is a great way to achieve a sleek, straight look, but it can be frustrating when your hair starts to frizz up shortly after styling. In this blog post, we’ll share some tips and tricks for how to keep flat ironed hair from frizzing.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Keep Flat Ironed Hair from Frizzing

There are a few things you can do before and after flat ironing your hair to help prevent frizz:

1. Use a heat protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it’s important to protect your hair from damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before flat ironing to help minimize damage and frizz.

2. Choose the right temperature

Make sure you’re using the right temperature setting on your flat iron. If your hair is fine or damaged, use a lower temperature setting to avoid further damage and frizz. If your hair is thick or coarse, you may need a higher temperature setting to effectively straighten your hair.

3. Start with clean, dry hair

Flat ironing wet or damp hair can cause damage and frizz. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a flat iron.

4. Use a high-quality flat iron

Investing in a high-quality flat iron can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels after styling. Look for a flat iron with ceramic plates, which will help distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz.

5. Don’t touch your hair after styling

Once you’ve finished flat ironing your hair, avoid touching it as much as possible. The oils and moisture from your hands can cause frizz and undo all of your hard work.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide for how to keep flat ironed hair from frizzing:

1. Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed to smooth and hydrate.

2. Apply a heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from damage.

3. Blow dry your hair

Use a blow dryer to completely dry your hair before using a flat iron.

4. Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.

5. Use a high-quality flat iron

Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates to straighten each section of hair.

6. Avoid touching your hair

Once you’ve finished flat ironing your hair, avoid touching it as much as possible.

Tips for Keeping Flat Ironed Hair from Frizzing

Here are a few additional tips for keeping your flat ironed hair frizz-free:

1. Use a silk or satin pillowcase

Switching to a silk or satin pillowcase can help reduce frizz while you sleep.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz.

3. Avoid using too much product

Using too much hair product can weigh your hair down and cause frizz. Use a small amount of product and focus on applying it to the ends of your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I flat iron my hair every day?

A: Flat ironing your hair every day can cause damage and dryness. Try to limit heat styling to every other day or less.

Q: Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?

A: No, using a flat iron on wet hair can cause damage and frizz. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a flat iron.

Hair Care for Frizz-Free Hair

Preventing frizz starts with a good hair care routine. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy and frizz-free:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Look for products that are designed to hydrate and nourish your hair without weighing it down.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and cause frizz. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray.

3. Avoid over-washing your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair every other day or less.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your flat ironed hair frizz-free and looking its best. Remember to take good care of your hair and avoid over-styling to keep it healthy and strong.
