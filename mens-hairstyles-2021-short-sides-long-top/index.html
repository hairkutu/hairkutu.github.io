Mens Hairstyles 2021 Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, then the mens hairstyles 2021 short sides long top is the perfect choice for you. This haircut has been in trend for a while now and is still going strong. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Description

The mens hairstyles 2021 short sides long top is a haircut that features short sides and a long top. The sides are usually faded or tapered, while the top is left longer and can be styled in various ways. This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn by men of all ages and hair types. It is a great choice for those looking for a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut.

How to Get the Mens Hairstyles 2021 Short Sides Long Top

The first step to getting the mens hairstyles 2021 short sides long top is to find a good barber or hairstylist. You'll want to make sure that they have experience with this particular haircut and can give you the look you're going for.

When you go to the barber, be sure to bring a picture of the hairstyle you want. This will help them understand what you're looking for and give them a clear idea of how to cut your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the mens hairstyles 2021 short sides long top:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Next, section off the top of your hair and clip it up out of the way.
  3. Use clippers to fade the sides and back of your hair. You can choose how short you want the sides to be, but a typical length is around a #2 guard.
  4. Blend the sides and top together with scissors or clippers.
  5. Release the top section of your hair and use scissors to cut it to the desired length.
  6. Style your hair using your preferred product, such as pomade or wax.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Hairstyles 2021 Short Sides Long Top

To keep your mens hairstyles 2021 short sides long top looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips to follow:

  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Use a high-quality hair product to style your hair and hold it in place.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • When you do wash your hair, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

1. Is the mens hairstyles 2021 short sides long top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can be worn by men with all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily.

2. What face shapes does this hairstyle suit?

The mens hairstyles 2021 short sides long top suits a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and round faces.

3. Can I style my hair in different ways with this haircut?

Yes, the mens hairstyles 2021 short sides long top is versatile and can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Hairstyles 2021 Short Sides Long Top

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the mens hairstyles 2021 short sides long top with confidence and style. So go ahead and book that appointment with your barber, and get ready to turn heads with your new haircut!
