How To Know If You Look Good With Platinum Blonde Hair

Written by Frank Sep 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Platinum blonde hair is a bold and daring look that can transform your appearance. But before you take the plunge and dye your hair, it's important to consider whether this shade will suit your skin tone, facial features, and personal style. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks to help you determine if platinum blonde hair is the right choice for you.

How to Know if You Look Good with Platinum Blonde Hair

If you're considering platinum blonde hair, there are several factors to take into account:

Skin Tone

Platinum blonde hair can look stunning on individuals with cool skin tones, such as those with pink or blue undertones. However, if you have a warm skin tone with yellow or peach undertones, this shade may clash with your complexion.

Eye Color

If you have light-colored eyes, such as blue or green, platinum blonde hair can create a striking contrast and enhance your features. However, if you have dark brown eyes, this shade may wash out your complexion and make your eyes appear dull.

Facial Features

Platinum blonde hair can accentuate certain facial features, such as high cheekbones and a defined jawline. However, if you have a round face or softer features, this shade may not be as flattering.

Personal Style

Platinum blonde hair is a statement look that requires confidence and a bold fashion sense. If you prefer a more understated or natural appearance, this shade may not align with your personal style.

Ultimately, the best way to determine if platinum blonde hair is right for you is to consult with a professional stylist. They can assess your skin tone, eye color, facial features, and personal preferences to recommend the most flattering shade of blonde for your unique look.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you've decided to take the plunge and try platinum blonde hair, follow these steps for a successful transformation:

Step 1: Choose a Professional Stylist

While at-home hair dye kits may seem like a cost-effective option, platinum blonde hair is a tricky and complex shade to achieve. It's best to seek the expertise of a professional stylist who can ensure your hair is properly bleached and toned to avoid damage or uneven coloring.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before your appointment, be sure to wash and condition your hair to remove any buildup or oil. Avoid using any styling products or heat tools to prevent damage to your hair. It's also a good idea to bring in photos of your desired shade and style to show your stylist.

Step 3: Bleach Your Hair

To achieve platinum blonde hair, your stylist will need to bleach your hair to remove any natural pigment. This process can take several hours and may require multiple sessions, depending on your hair's thickness and texture. Be sure to follow your stylist's instructions for aftercare, such as avoiding washing your hair for a few days and using a deep conditioning treatment.

Step 4: Tone Your Hair

After bleaching your hair, your stylist will apply a toner to neutralize any remaining yellow or orange tones and create a bright, white shade. This step is crucial for achieving a true platinum blonde color. Again, be sure to follow your stylist's aftercare instructions.

Step 5: Maintain Your Color

Platinum blonde hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. This may include using a purple shampoo to prevent brassiness, scheduling regular touch-up appointments with your stylist, and using a deep conditioning treatment to nourish your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Platinum Blonde Hair

To keep your platinum blonde hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a purple shampoo once a week to prevent brassiness
  • Avoid using heat tools or styling products that can damage your hair
  • Schedule touch-up appointments with your stylist every 4-6 weeks
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water, which can turn your hair green

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I dye my hair platinum blonde at home?

We strongly recommend seeking the expertise of a professional stylist to achieve platinum blonde hair. This shade requires complex bleaching and toning processes that can be difficult to achieve on your own.

Will platinum blonde hair damage my hair?

Bleaching your hair can cause damage and dryness, but a professional stylist can help minimize the risk by using high-quality products and following proper aftercare procedures.

How long will my platinum blonde hair last?

Your platinum blonde hair may require touch-up appointments every 4-6 weeks to maintain its color and vibrancy.

Hair Care for Platinum Blonde Hair

Platinum blonde hair requires regular maintenance and care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils
  • Avoid using heat tools or styling products that can damage your hair
  • Schedule regular touch-up appointments with your stylist to maintain your color
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water, which can turn your hair green

By following these tips and tricks, you can determine if platinum blonde hair is the right choice for you and maintain your bold new look for months to come.
