Mens Hair Fade 2020 A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Daniel Sep 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Welcome to our guide on mens hair fade 2020! If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, the fade is the way to go. This hairstyle has been trending for a while now, and it's still going strong in 2020. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect fade, step by step. We'll also give you some tips on how to maintain your new look and answer some frequently asked questions about the mens hair fade.

Description

The mens hair fade is a haircut that features a gradual transition from short to long hair. The hair is cut short at the bottom and gradually gets longer as it goes up the sides and back of the head. The top of the hair can be left long or cut to a shorter length. The result is a clean and modern look that is easy to maintain.

How to Get the Perfect Mens Hair Fade 2020

Step 1: Choose Your Fade Style

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to decide on the type of fade you want. There are different types of fades, including low fade, high fade, mid fade, and skin fade. Each type of fade has a different level of hair length difference between the top and bottom of the head. The low fade has a small difference, while the skin fade has no hair at the bottom.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting. This will make it easier to cut and style. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Use a clipper to trim the hair at the bottom of your head to the desired length. Make sure the clipper guard is set to the right length.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the hair at the sides and back of your head. Use a clipper to create a gradual transition from short to long hair. Move the clipper up the head, using different guard sizes to create the fade. Blend the hair between the different lengths to create a smooth transition.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length. You can leave it long or cut it short. Use scissors or a clipper to trim the hair. Blend the top with the sides and back to create a seamless look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair. Apply the product to your hands and run them through your hair. Style your hair to your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Hair Fade 2020

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens hair fade:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

It's recommended that you visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and maintained.

Hair Care

Maintaining your hairstyle is important to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health.

We hope you found this guide on mens hair fade 2020 helpful. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect fade and maintain it for a long time. Remember to visit your barber regularly and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
