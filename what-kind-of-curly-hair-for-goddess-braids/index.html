What Kind Of Curly Hair For Goddess Braids

Written by Bardi Sep 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will make you feel like a goddess? Then you should try out goddess braids! These beautiful braids are perfect for any occasion, whether you're going to a wedding or just running errands. But before you can start braiding, you need to know what kind of curly hair to use. In this article, we'll discuss the different types of curly hair for goddess braids and give you some tips for maintaining your braids once they're done.

Description

Goddess braids are a type of African braiding style that involves creating large, intricate braids that are usually styled in an updo. These braids are perfect for anyone who wants a low-maintenance yet glamorous hairstyle. They can be done on any hair type, but they look particularly stunning on curly hair.

How To

If you want to try out goddess braids on your curly hair, here's how to do it:

  1. Choose your hair extensions. There are several types of hair extensions to choose from, including synthetic hair, human hair, and kanekalon hair. Synthetic hair is the most affordable option, but it doesn't last as long as human hair. Kanekalon hair is a synthetic hair that looks and feels like real hair. Human hair is the most natural-looking option, but it's also the most expensive.
  2. Wash and condition your hair. It's important to start with clean, moisturized hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a deep conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  3. Cornrow your hair. Cornrowing your hair will create a base for your goddess braids. Make sure to cornrow your hair tightly so that the braids don't slip out.
  4. Add the extensions. Take a strand of hair extensions and fold it in half. Place the folded end of the extension against your scalp and braid it into your cornrow. Braid the extension along with your natural hair until you reach the end.
  5. Continue braiding. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you have braided all of your hair.
  6. Style your braids. Once you've finished braiding, you can style your braids in any way you like. You can wear them in an updo or let them hang loose.

Step by Step

If you're still not sure how to do goddess braids on your curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose your hair extensions

Decide on the type of hair extensions you want to use. Synthetic hair is the most affordable, while human hair is the most natural-looking.

Step 2: Wash and condition your hair

Clean and moisturize your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and a deep conditioner. This will help keep your curls hydrated.

Step 3: Cornrow your hair

Cornrow your hair tightly to create a base for your goddess braids. Make sure to cornrow your hair in a pattern that will allow you to create the braids you want.

Step 4: Add the extensions

Take a strand of hair extensions and fold it in half. Place the folded end of the extension against your scalp and braid it into your cornrow. Braid the extension along with your natural hair until you reach the end.

Step 5: Continue braiding

Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you have braided all of your hair. Make sure to braid tightly to prevent the braids from slipping out.

Step 6: Style your braids

Once you've finished braiding, you can style your braids in any way you like. You can wear them in an updo or let them hang loose.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your goddess braids:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly to prevent dryness and itching.
  • Wear a scarf or bonnet at night to prevent your braids from getting frizzy.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Don't leave your braids in for too long. Take them out after 6-8 weeks to prevent damage to your natural hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about goddess braids:

What kind of hair extensions should I use?

You can use any type of hair extensions, but synthetic hair is the most affordable option. Human hair is the most natural-looking but it's also the most expensive.

How long do goddess braids last?

Goddess braids can last up to 6-8 weeks with proper maintenance.

Can I do goddess braids on short hair?

Yes, you can do goddess braids on short hair. You may need to use shorter hair extensions to create the braids.

Are goddess braids easy to maintain?

Yes, goddess braids are relatively low-maintenance. Just make sure to moisturize your scalp regularly and avoid heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair while you have goddess braids. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly to prevent dryness and itching.
  • Wear a scarf or bonnet at night to prevent your braids from getting frizzy.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Don't leave your braids in for too long. Take them out after 6-8 weeks to prevent damage to your natural hair.

In conclusion, goddess braids are a beautiful and versatile hairstyle for curly hair. You can use any type of hair extensions, but synthetic hair is the most affordable option. Make sure to take care of your hair while you have braids, and don't leave them in for too long. With proper maintenance, your goddess braids will look stunning for weeks!
