Short Sides Medium Top Fade A Modern Hairstyle For Men

Written by Oliver Apr 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

The short sides medium top fade is a popular hairstyle among men. This haircut features short hair on the sides and a longer top that gradually fades into the shorter sides. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways, making it perfect for any occasion, from casual to formal. In this article, we will guide you on how to get and maintain a short sides medium top fade, as well as provide you with tips and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

The short sides medium top fade is a modern hairstyle that suits men with different face shapes and hair types. It's characterized by short hair on the sides that gradually fades into the longer hair on top. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it typically falls between 2 to 4 inches. The fade can also be customized to suit your preferences, from a high fade that starts at the temples to a low fade that starts at the nape of the neck. This hairstyle is easy to maintain and style, making it perfect for men who are always on the go.

How to Get a Short Sides Medium Top Fade

Getting a short sides medium top fade requires a skilled barber or hairstylist. Here's how to get the cut:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

Determine how long you want the hair on top to be. It's best to bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to show your barber or hairstylist.

Step 2: Choose the Fade Type

Decide on the type of fade you want. You can choose from a high fade, mid fade, or low fade, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on the Sides

The barber or hairstylist will start by cutting the hair on the sides. They will use clippers and scissors to achieve the desired length and fade.

Step 4: Cut the Hair on Top

Once the sides are cut, the barber or hairstylist will cut the hair on top to the desired length. They will use scissors to create texture and volume.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Finally, the barber or hairstylist will style the hair on top using a pomade, wax, or gel, depending on the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining a Short Sides Medium Top Fade

Here are some tips for maintaining your short sides medium top fade:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and fade.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Style your hair using a hair dryer and a brush to achieve the desired volume and texture.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

What face shape is best for a short sides medium top fade?

The short sides medium top fade suits different face shapes, including round, oval, and square faces.

Can I style my short sides medium top fade in different ways?

Yes, you can style your short sides medium top fade in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

Can I get a short sides medium top fade if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can get a short sides medium top fade if you have curly hair. However, it's best to consult with your barber or hairstylist to determine the best cut and style for your hair type.

Hair Care for Short Sides Medium Top Fade

To keep your short sides medium top fade looking its best, it's essential to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner at least twice a week to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair dryer and a brush to style your hair, as this can add volume and texture.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and fade.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short sides medium top fade like a pro. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
