How To Slick Back Long Hair Mens A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle, slicking back your long hair is the way to go. It's a versatile hairstyle that can work for both formal and casual occasions. In this article, we will show you how to slick back long hair mens, step-by-step, along with some tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

How to Slick Back Long Hair Mens

Slicking back long hair is all about using the right products and techniques. Here are the things you'll need:

  • A comb or brush
  • A hair dryer
  • A styling product, such as pomade, wax, or gel

Now, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any buildup and make your hair easier to style. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Take a small amount of your chosen styling product and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Apply it evenly through your hair, starting from the back and working your way forward. Make sure to distribute the product evenly to avoid clumps or greasy spots.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a hair dryer to dry your hair while combing it backwards with a comb or brush. This will help to set the style and create the slicked back look. Be sure to use the cool setting on your hair dryer to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Back

Once your hair is dry, use a comb or brush to slick it back. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down to the nape of your neck. Use a firm hand and make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to go.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To set your style and make it last all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Tips for Slicking Back Long Hair Mens

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that is designed for your hair type.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go to help set the style.
  • Use a comb or brush with firm bristles to help smooth your hair back.
  • If you have a receding hairline or thinning hair, avoid slicking your hair straight back, as this can make your hairline look more pronounced.

FAQs

Q: Can I slick back my long hair if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can. However, you may need to use a stronger hold product to keep your curls in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I slick it back?

A: It's best to avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, try washing it every 2-3 days, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to freshen up your hair.

Q: Can I slick back my hair if it's oily?

A: Yes, you can. However, you may need to use a clarifying shampoo to remove excess oil before styling your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Slicked Back Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your slicked back hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can clog your pores and cause breakouts.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to slick back your long hair like a pro. Whether you're heading to a formal event or just want to add some sophistication to your everyday look, this classic hairstyle is sure to impress. So grab your comb, your favorite styling product, and get slicking!
