How To Hide Clip In Hair Extensions In Thin Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thin hair can be a challenge when it comes to wearing clip in hair extensions. You want to add volume and length to your hair, but you don't want the clips to show through. In this article, we will show you how to hide clip in hair extensions in thin hair.

Description

If you have thin hair, you may have tried clip in hair extensions before and found that the clips show through. This can be frustrating and make you feel self-conscious. However, with the right technique, you can hide clip in hair extensions in thin hair and achieve a natural look.

How to

Follow these steps to hide clip in hair extensions in thin hair:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Then, use a comb to create a part where you want to place the clip in hair extensions.

Step 2: Tease your hair

Take a small section of hair where you want to place the clip in hair extensions and tease it using a comb. This will create a base for the clip to hold onto and prevent it from slipping.

Step 3: Clip in the hair extensions

Clip in the hair extensions, making sure that the clips are secure and not visible. Start from the bottom of your hair and work your way up.

Step 4: Blend the hair extensions

Once the hair extensions are clipped in, use a brush to blend them with your natural hair. This will create a seamless look and prevent the clips from showing through.

Step 5: Style your hair

You can style your hair as desired, whether it's straightening, curling or adding volume. The hair extensions should blend in with your natural hair and look natural.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you hide clip in hair extensions in thin hair:

  • Choose hair extensions that match your hair color and texture
  • Use hair extensions that are the same length as your natural hair
  • Tease your hair to create a base for the clips to hold onto
  • Clip in the hair extensions in layers to create a more natural look
  • Use a brush to blend the hair extensions with your natural hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding clip in hair extensions in thin hair:

Can I wear clip in hair extensions if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can wear clip in hair extensions if you have thin hair. You just need to follow the right technique to hide the clips and achieve a natural look.

How many clip in hair extensions do I need?

The number of clip in hair extensions you need depends on the volume and length you want to achieve. Typically, 5-7 clip in hair extensions are enough for a full head.

How long can I wear clip in hair extensions?

You can wear clip in hair extensions for up to 8 hours at a time. It's important to remove them before sleeping to avoid damaging your natural hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your clip in hair extensions to ensure they last and look good. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your clip in hair extensions with a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your clip in hair extensions
  • Store your clip in hair extensions in a cool, dry place
  • Use a comb to detangle your clip in hair extensions gently

By following these tips, you can hide clip in hair extensions in thin hair and achieve a natural look. With the right technique and care, clip in hair extensions can be a great way to add volume and length to your hair.
