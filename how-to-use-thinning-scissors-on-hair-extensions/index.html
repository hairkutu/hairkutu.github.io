How To Use Thinning Scissors On Hair Extensions

Written by Robby Sep 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thinning scissors are a great tool to use on hair extensions if you want to add texture and reduce bulk. However, it's important to use them correctly to avoid damaging your extensions. In this article, we'll show you how to use thinning scissors on hair extensions and provide some tips on how to maintain your extensions to keep them looking great.

Description

Thinning scissors are designed to remove hair without creating a blunt edge. They have a serrated edge that cuts some hair and leaves some hair uncut, creating a soft, textured look. They are commonly used to thin out thick hair or add texture to hair that is too uniform.

How to Use Thinning Scissors on Hair Extensions

Before using thinning scissors on your hair extensions, make sure that they are completely dry and brushed out. Start with small sections of hair and make sure that you don't cut too much at once. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 2: Hold the Scissors Correctly

Hold the thinning scissors with the serrated edge pointing upwards. This will help you to blend the hair and create a softer look.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Start at the middle of the hair section and work your way down to the ends, cutting small sections at a time. Make sure that you don't cut too much hair at once, as this can create an uneven look. Remember to keep the scissors at an angle to create a more natural look.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair, use a comb to blend it in with the rest of the hair. This will ensure that there are no harsh lines and that the hair looks natural.

Tips

  • Don't use thinning scissors on wet hair, as this can create an uneven look.
  • Make sure that you don't cut too much hair at once, as this can create an uneven look.
  • Use a comb to blend the hair after cutting it to create a natural look.
  • Practice on a small section of hair before cutting your entire head of hair.

FAQ

Can thinning scissors be used on all types of hair extensions?

Yes, thinning scissors can be used on all types of hair extensions. However, it's important to use them correctly to avoid damaging the extensions.

How often should I use thinning scissors on my hair extensions?

You should only use thinning scissors on your hair extensions when necessary. Overuse can lead to damage and breakage.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair extensions to keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Brush your hair extensions regularly to prevent tangles.
  • Wash your hair extensions with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair extensions, as this can damage them.
  • Store your hair extensions in a cool, dry place to prevent tangling and damage.

By following these tips and using thinning scissors correctly, you can keep your hair extensions looking great and add texture and dimension to your hair.
