How To Cut A Messy Layered Bob

Written by Bardi Sep 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your same old boring haircut? Do you want to try something new and trendy? If so, a messy layered bob might just be the perfect haircut for you! In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of cutting a messy layered bob. We will also share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect haircut.

Description

A messy layered bob is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. This haircut is perfect for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair. It is also a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style and maintain.

How to Cut a Messy Layered Bob

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It is also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp.

Step 1: Divide your hair into sections

Start by dividing your hair into sections. Use hair clips to secure the top section of your hair. Then, divide the bottom section into two parts, one on each side of your head.

Step 2: Cut the bottom section

Start by cutting the bottom section of your hair. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Repeat this process on the other side of your head. Make sure to cut the hair at an angle to create layers.

Step 3: Cut the top section

Release the top section of your hair and divide it into small sections. Hold each section of hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Make sure to cut the hair at an angle to create layers.

Step 4: Blend the layers

Once you have cut all the sections of your hair, blend the layers together by cutting the ends of your hair at an angle. This will create a messy, layered look.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once you have cut your hair, style it according to your preference. You can use a hair straightener or curling iron to add some texture to your hair. You can also use hair styling products like mousse or hair spray to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy layered bob:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends
  • Cut your hair when it is damp to make it easier to cut and style
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Blend the layers together to create a natural look

FAQ

Q: Is a messy layered bob suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a messy layered bob can be customized to suit different hair types and textures.

Q: Can I cut my own hair?

A: It is possible to cut your own hair, but it is recommended to have a professional stylist cut your hair to avoid any mistakes.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It is recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Cutting a messy layered bob is a great way to refresh your look and add some texture and volume to your hair. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect haircut. Don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny!
