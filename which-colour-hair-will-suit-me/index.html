Which Colour Hair Will Suit Me

Written by Oliver Apr 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering changing your hair color but not sure which one would suit you? Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we will guide you on how to determine which hair color will suit you best.

Description

Before we dive into the different hair colors, let's understand how to determine our skin tone. Skin tone plays a vital role in determining which hair color will suit you best. There are two main skin tones, warm and cool. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones.

How to

Now that you know your skin tone, let's look at the different hair colors that will suit you.

For Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm skin tones, the following hair colors will complement your skin tone:

  • Warm browns
  • Golden blonde
  • Copper red
  • Caramel

These hair colors will bring out the warmth in your skin tone and make you look radiant.

For Cool Skin Tones

If you have cool skin tones, the following hair colors will complement your skin tone:

  • Ash blonde
  • Platinum blonde
  • Cool brown
  • Deep red

These hair colors will bring out the cool undertones in your skin and make you look fresh and youthful.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can follow to determine which hair color will suit you best:

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Research hair colors that complement your skin tone
  3. Consult a hair stylist for their professional opinion if you're still unsure
  4. Choose a shade that complements your lifestyle and personality

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider the maintenance of the hair color you choose
  • Choose a hair color that matches your eye color
  • Try on wigs or use a virtual hair color app to see how the color looks on you before committing
  • Consider your natural hair color when choosing a hair color

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair color:

How do I know if I have warm or cool skin tones?

You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear green, you have warm skin tones, and if they appear blue, you have cool skin tones.

Can I dye my hair a color that doesn't complement my skin tone?

Yes, you can dye your hair any color you like. However, it may not complement your skin tone, and you may not feel as confident with the color.

How often do I need to touch up my hair color?

The frequency of touch-ups depends on the hair color you choose and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should touch up your hair color every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your hair color, it's essential to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid over-washing your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools too often

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these guidelines and tips, you'll be sure to find a hair color that complements your skin tone and personality.
