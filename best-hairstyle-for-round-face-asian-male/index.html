Best Hairstyle For Round Face Asian Male

Written by Petter May 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, there are so many options out there that it can be hard to decide which one is best for you. If you're an Asian male with a round face, it can be even more challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that will complement your features. In this article, we'll discuss the best hairstyles for round face Asian males, including how to style them and tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Hair Care
  • FAQs

Description

First of all, it's important to understand what a round face is. A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded jawline. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of more angular features.

For Asian males with round faces, there are several hairstyles to choose from that will flatter your features. These include short haircuts, medium-length hairstyles, and longer hairstyles. The key is to avoid hairstyles that add more fullness to your face or make it appear rounder than it already is.

How to Style

The best hairstyles for round face Asian males are those that create height and volume at the top of the head while keeping the sides and back relatively short. This creates the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape.

Short haircuts, such as a buzz cut or crew cut, are great options for those who prefer low-maintenance hairstyles. These styles can be easily styled with a bit of pomade or hair wax to add texture and volume.

For those who prefer longer hairstyles, a layered cut with a side part can be a great option. This style creates height at the top of the head while keeping the sides and back shorter. It can be styled with a bit of mousse or hair gel to add volume and texture.

Step by Step Guide

If you're looking to style your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide for a simple, yet stylish look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a small amount of mousse or hair gel to your hair
  3. Blow-dry your hair while using a brush to create volume at the top of your head
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a bit of pomade or hair wax to add texture
  5. Style your hair with your fingers or a comb, creating a side part if desired
  6. Finish with a bit of hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips for Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. This means using a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type and using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

It's also important to avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair follicles and lead to breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and limit your use to a few times a week.

FAQs

What are the best haircuts for round face Asian males?

The best haircuts for round face Asian males are those that create height and volume at the top of the head while keeping the sides and back relatively short. This includes short haircuts, medium-length hairstyles, and longer hairstyles with layers and a side part.

How do I style my hair to make my face look less round?

To make your face look less round, you want to create height and volume at the top of your head while keeping the sides and back shorter. This can be achieved with a layered cut, a side part, and a bit of styling product to add texture and volume.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's recommended to wash your hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Over-washing can strip the hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage.

How do I keep my hair healthy?

To keep your hair healthy, be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type, use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized, and limit your use of heat styling tools.

Overall, the best hairstyle for round face Asian males is one that creates height and volume at the top of the head while keeping the sides and back shorter. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a stylish and flattering look that complements your features.
