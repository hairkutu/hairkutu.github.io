How To Make Your Hair Curly If It s Naturally Wavy

Written by Luffy Aug 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have naturally wavy hair, you may have been wondering how to make it curly. Don't worry, you're not alone! Many women with wavy hair want to achieve a curly look, but aren't sure how to go about it. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for getting those luscious curls you've been dreaming of.

How to

There are a few different methods you can try to make your hair curly. The best method for you will depend on your hair type, the tools you have available, and your personal preference. Here are some popular methods:

Method 1: Using a Curling Iron

One of the easiest ways to make your hair curly is by using a curling iron. Here's how:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds.
  5. Release the curl and repeat with the rest of your hair.
  6. Once you've curled all of your hair, use a light-hold hairspray to set the curls.

Using a curling iron is a quick and easy way to get curly hair, but it can be damaging if you use it too often. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and only use the curling iron once or twice a week.

Method 2: Using Heatless Methods

If you want to avoid heat damage, there are several heatless methods you can try:

  1. Twist your hair into small sections and secure with bobby pins. Leave the twists in overnight and remove in the morning for natural-looking curls.
  2. Use flexi rods or perm rods to create curls. Simply wrap small sections of hair around the rods and leave them in for several hours or overnight.
  3. Braid your hair and leave the braids in overnight. In the morning, unravel the braids for loose waves.

Heatless methods take a bit more time and effort, but they are much healthier for your hair. Plus, they can create beautiful, natural-looking curls.

Step by Step

Now that you know the methods, let's go over the step-by-step process for making your hair curly:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray (if using heat).
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Choose your method (curling iron, flexi rods, braids, etc.) and start curling.
  5. Set the curls with hairspray (if using heat).

That's it! With a little practice, you'll be able to create beautiful curls in no time.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use a light-hold hairspray to set the curls without weighing them down.
  • Don't brush or comb your curls too much, as this can cause them to frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to keep your curls looking healthy and defined.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about making your hair curly:

Can I make my hair curly without heat?

Yes! There are several heatless methods you can try, such as using flexi rods, braiding your hair, or twisting it into sections and securing with bobby pins.

Will curling my hair damage it?

If you use a curling iron too often without using a heat protectant spray, it can cause damage. However, if you use it sparingly and take proper precautions, you should be able to avoid damage.

How can I make my curls last longer?

Use a light-hold hairspray to set the curls, and try not to touch or brush them too much. You can also sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help preserve the curls overnight.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant spray when using heat tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create beautiful, curly hair even if you have naturally wavy locks. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
