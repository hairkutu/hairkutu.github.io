Long On Top Short On Sides Haircut Tutorial

Written by William Jul 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our haircut tutorial on the long on top short on sides hairstyle! This popular men's haircut has been around for a while now and has remained a classic look. The style is versatile and can be customized to suit your personality and lifestyle. In this tutorial, we will show you how to achieve the perfect long on top short on sides haircut in a step-by-step process. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The long on top short on sides haircut is a style where the hair on the top of the head is left long, while the hair on the sides and back is cut short. This haircut is versatile and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. It is perfect for men who want a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle that can be easily styled in different ways.

How to

To achieve the long on top short on sides haircut, all you need is a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you are doing this at home, we recommend having someone to help you with the back and sides.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect long on top short on sides haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it is slightly damp.
  2. Using scissors, start by cutting the hair on top to the desired length. Use a comb to hold the hair in place and cut it in small sections.
  3. Using clippers, start cutting the hair on the sides and back. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you achieve the desired length.
  4. Blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top using scissors or clippers. Use a comb to hold the hair in place and blend it in a diagonal direction.
  5. Cut the hair around the ears and the neckline using clippers or scissors.
  6. Check the length and adjust it accordingly. Use your fingers to feel for any unevenness or longer strands of hair.
  7. Style your hair using your preferred hair product. You can slick it back, create a side part, or leave it messy and tousled.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect long on top short on sides haircut:

  • Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head.
  • Start with a longer guard when cutting the hair on the sides and back to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use scissors to achieve a more textured and layered look.
  • Experiment with different hair products to achieve different styles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short on sides haircut:

  • What face shape does this haircut suit? This haircut suits most face shapes, especially oval and square faces.
  • Can I achieve this haircut with curly hair? Yes, this haircut can be achieved with curly hair. However, you may need to adjust the length and style to suit your hair type.
  • How often do I need to maintain this haircut? This haircut can be maintained every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and tidy.

Hair Care

To keep your long on top short on sides haircut looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair when styling it.
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and style.

That's it! We hope this tutorial has helped you achieve the perfect long on top short on sides haircut. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
