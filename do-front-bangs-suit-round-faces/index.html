Do Front Bangs Suit Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Are you thinking of getting front bangs but wondering if they would suit your round face shape? Look no further because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about front bangs and how they can flatter round faces. Let's dive in!

Description

Front bangs, also known as fringe, can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some personality to your look. However, not all bangs are created equal, and some styles may not flatter your face shape. Round faces, in particular, can look wider with the wrong type of bangs. But don't worry, because there are ways to make front bangs work for round faces.

How to Determine If Front Bangs Suit Round Faces

The first step in determining if front bangs would suit your round face is to understand your face shape. A round face is characterized by its width and lack of angles. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, giving the face a circular appearance.

When it comes to bangs, the goal is to create angles and balance out the width of the face. Side-swept bangs or asymmetrical bangs can work well for round faces because they create diagonal lines that break up the circular shape. However, blunt bangs that fall straight across the forehead can make a round face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide on Getting Front Bangs for Round Faces

If you've determined that front bangs would suit your round face, here's a step-by-step guide on how to get them:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist about the best type of bangs for your face shape.
  2. Decide on the length and thickness of your bangs. Thicker bangs can create a more dramatic look, but thinner bangs may be easier to manage.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs in a diagonal or asymmetrical shape to create angles and balance out your face shape.
  4. Style your bangs using a blow dryer, round brush, and/or flat iron to achieve the desired look.

Tips for Styling Front Bangs on Round Faces

Now that you've got your front bangs, here are some tips on styling them:

  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to lay.
  • If you have thicker bangs, consider using a flat iron to smooth them out and create a sleeker look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, such as pinning your bangs back or adding volume with a teasing comb.
  • Maintain your bangs by getting regular trims to prevent split ends and keep them looking fresh.

FAQ

What other types of bangs can work for round faces?

In addition to side-swept and asymmetrical bangs, curtain bangs can also flatter round faces. These bangs are parted down the middle and frame the face on both sides, creating a slimming effect.

Can bangs make a round face look even rounder?

Yes, blunt bangs that fall straight across the forehead can make a round face look wider. It's important to choose a type of bangs that creates angles and balance out the width of the face.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Front Bangs on Round Faces

Once you've got your front bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your front bangs on a round face:

  • Wash your bangs regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent buildup and keep them looking clean.
  • Blow dry your bangs immediately after washing to prevent them from drying in a wonky direction.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent oil buildup and frizz.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock front bangs on your round face with confidence. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and choose a style that flatters your unique face shape. Happy styling!
