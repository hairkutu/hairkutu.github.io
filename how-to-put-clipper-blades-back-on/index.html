How To Put Clipper Blades Back On A Relaxing Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a proud owner of a clipper, then you know the importance of maintaining it to ensure its longevity. One of the most crucial parts of maintaining your clipper is knowing how to put clipper blades back on. This simple task can save you money and time, and we're here to guide you through it.

How to Put Clipper Blades Back On

Before we dive into the step-by-step process, let's first discuss the tools you'll need:

  • A screwdriver (usually included with the clipper)
  • Clipper oil
  • A brush or cloth

Step 1: Cleaning

Before attaching the blades, make sure to clean them. Using a brush or cloth, remove any hair or debris from the blades. Then, apply a few drops of clipper oil to the blades and turn on the clipper to let it distribute the oil.

Step 2: Removing the Blades

Using the screwdriver, remove the screws holding the blades in place. Be sure to keep the screws in a safe place to avoid losing them.

Step 3: Aligning the Blades

Align the blades with the clipper's teeth. Ensure that the blade's teeth are facing the front of the clipper and that the alignment tongue fits into the groove on the clipper's body.

Step 4: Attaching the Blades

Once the blades are aligned, gently tighten the screws. Be sure not to over-tighten, as this can damage the blades or clipper.

Step 5: Testing

Turn on the clipper and test the blades to ensure they're working correctly. If they're not, double-check that they're aligned correctly and the screws aren't too tight.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when putting clipper blades back on:

  • Use clipper oil to lubricate the blades before reattaching them.
  • Be gentle when tightening the screws to avoid damaging the clipper or blades.
  • Double-check the alignment of the blades before tightening the screws.
  • Test the clipper after reattaching the blades to ensure they're working correctly.

FAQ

Can I use any screwdriver to remove the blades?

It's best to use the screwdriver that came with the clipper to ensure the correct size and fit. Using the wrong screwdriver can damage the screws or clipper.

How often should I put clipper blades back on?

You should remove and clean your clipper blades after every use to maintain their performance. If you notice a decrease in performance or the blades become dull, it's time to remove and sharpen them.

Can I use water to clean the blades?

No, it's best to avoid water when cleaning clipper blades as it can cause rust and damage to the blades. Use a brush or cloth and clipper oil to clean them.

Hair Care

Proper clipper maintenance is essential to ensure healthy and effective haircuts. By regularly putting clipper blades back on, you'll avoid damaging the clipper or blades, resulting in a smoother and more precise cut.

Additionally, using clipper oil to lubricate the blades will reduce friction and prevent hair from getting caught in the blades, minimizing the risk of injury or discomfort.

Overall, putting clipper blades back on is a simple task that can have a significant impact on your clipper's performance and lifespan. By following these steps and tips, you'll be well on your way to maintaining your clipper and getting the best haircuts possible.
