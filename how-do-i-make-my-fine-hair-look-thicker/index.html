How Do I Make My Fine Hair Look Thicker

Written by Christine May 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of fine hair that always looks limp and lifeless? Do you wish you could have thick, voluminous locks? If so, you're not alone! Many people with fine hair struggle to make it look fuller and more voluminous. But don't worry, there are plenty of tips and tricks you can use to make your fine hair look thicker. In this article, we'll go over some of the best ways to achieve thicker-looking hair.

Table of Contents

  • How To Make Fine Hair Look Thicker
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips For Thicker-Looking Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips For Fine Hair

How To Make Fine Hair Look Thicker

There are several ways to make fine hair look thicker, including:

  • Choosing the right haircut
  • Using volumizing products
  • Blow-drying your hair upside down
  • Using a diffuser
  • Teasing your hair
  • Using hair extensions

Choosing the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make a huge difference in how thick your hair looks. Layers can add movement and texture to your hair, making it look fuller. A blunt cut, on the other hand, can make your hair look flat and lifeless. Talk to your stylist about the best haircut for your hair type and face shape.

Using Volumizing Products

There are plenty of volumizing products on the market that can help give your hair a boost. Look for products that are specifically designed for fine hair, such as volumizing shampoos and mousses. These products can help lift your hair at the roots and add volume.

Blow-Drying Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can help add volume and lift to your roots. Start by flipping your head upside down and blow-drying your hair until it's about 80% dry. Then, flip your head back up and finish blow-drying your hair as usual.

Using a Diffuser

A diffuser attachment on your blow dryer can also help add volume to your hair. A diffuser helps distribute the heat evenly and can help reduce frizz, which can make your hair look thinner.

Teasing Your Hair

Teasing your hair can help add volume and lift to your roots. Start by sectioning off the top layer of your hair and teasing the roots with a comb. Be careful not to tease your hair too much, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Using Hair Extensions

If you're looking for a more drastic change, hair extensions can help add length and volume to your hair. Clip-in extensions are a great option for those who want to try out extensions without committing to a more permanent solution.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your fine hair look thicker:

  1. Start by choosing the right haircut for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help lift your hair at the roots.
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots before blow-drying your hair.
  4. Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.
  5. Flip your head back up and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to finish drying your hair.
  6. Tease the roots of your hair to add volume and lift.
  7. Use hair extensions for a more dramatic change.
  8. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips For Thicker-Looking Hair

Here are some additional tips for making your fine hair look thicker:

  • Avoid using heavy conditioners or hair masks, as these can weigh down your hair.
  • Try using a dry shampoo in between washes to help absorb excess oil and add volume.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help smooth and strengthen your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with gentle bristles to avoid causing damage or breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as these can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Can I Make My Hair Look Thicker Without Using Products?

Yes! Choosing the right haircut and blow-drying your hair upside down can both help add volume and lift to your hair without using any products.

Can I Tease My Hair Every Day?

No. Teasing your hair too much can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit teasing to once or twice a week.

Will Hair Extensions Damage My Hair?

Clip-in hair extensions should not cause damage to your hair if they are applied and removed properly. However, more permanent solutions like tape-in or sew-in extensions can cause damage if they are not installed correctly or if they are left in for too long.

Hair Care Tips For Fine Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of fine hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this can help prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can also cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your fine hair look thicker and more voluminous. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair type and style.
