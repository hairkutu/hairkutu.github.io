39 How To Style Short Hair Over 50 For Fresh Hairstyle

Written by Eveline Jul 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read
Table of Content - Introduction - How to Style Short Hair Over 50 - Step by Step Guide - Tips for Styling Short Hair Over 50 - Frequently Asked Questions - Hair Care Tips for Short Hair Over 50 - Conclusion Introduction As we age, our hair tends to lose its lustre and volume. When it comes to women over 50, many believe that long hair is the only option to look stylish and trendy. However, short hair can be just as chic and flattering. In this article, we will explore how to style short hair over 50, step by step guide, tips, frequently asked questions and hair care tips. How to Style Short Hair Over 50 The first step to styling short hair over 50 is to choose the right cut that flatters your face shape and hair texture. A layered bob, pixie cut or a shaggy cut are some popular options. Once you have your haircut, it's time to style it. Step by Step Guide 1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. 2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots. 3. Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and lift to your hair. 4. Use a flat iron to straighten any frizzy or unruly strands. 5. Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair. 6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place. Tips for Styling Short Hair Over 50 - Invest in good quality hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type. - Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you. - Use accessories like headbands, clips or scarves to add a touch of glamour to your hair. - Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair. Frequently Asked Questions Q. Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or bun with short hair? A. Yes, you can. Use bobby pins and hair ties to create a mini bun or ponytail. You can also use a headband to add some style. Q. Will short hair make me look older? A. No, it won't. Short hair can actually make you look younger and more stylish. Q. How often should I get my hair cut? A. It depends on your hair growth and maintenance. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks. Hair Care Tips for Short Hair Over 50 - Use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage and breakage. - Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished. Conclusion Styling short hair over 50 can be easy and fun with the right cut and styling techniques. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a messy and carefree style, there are many options to choose from. Don't be afraid to experiment and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair with proper maintenance and hair care products.
