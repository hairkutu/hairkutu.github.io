Description

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of settings, from casual to formal. It's a great option for those with medium length hair who want to keep their hair out of their face while still looking stylish. In this blog post, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect messy bun for medium length hair.

How to

To create a messy bun for medium length hair, you will need a few key tools:

  • Hair Elastic
  • Bobby Pins
  • Dry Shampoo

Follow these steps to create the perfect messy bun:

Step by step

  1. Start by spraying your hair with dry shampoo to add texture and volume.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and tie it into a loose ponytail with a hair elastic.
  3. Twist the ponytail until it starts to coil around itself.
  4. Wrap the twisted ponytail around the hair elastic in a circular motion until the entire ponytail is wrapped around the elastic.
  5. Secure the bun with bobby pins to keep it in place.
  6. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a relaxed, messy look.
  7. Spray your bun with hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect messy bun:

  • Don't be afraid to use bobby pins to secure your bun in place.
  • Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a more relaxed and effortless look.
  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair before creating the bun.

FAQ

Q: Can I create a messy bun with short hair?

A: It's possible, but you may need to use more bobby pins to secure your hair in place.

Q: How do I keep my messy bun from falling out?

A: Use plenty of bobby pins to secure your bun in place, and finish with a spritz of hairspray.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of buildup.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

By following these hair care tips and using the right tools and techniques, you can create the perfect messy bun for your medium length hair.
