What Should I Pack For 60 Degree Weather A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 14, 2023 · 6 min read

Heading somewhere with a climate of around 60 degrees Fahrenheit? It can be challenging to pack for this kind of weather since it’s not too hot nor too cold. In this article, we’ll cover what items you should pack for 60 degree weather to ensure that you stay comfortable throughout your trip.

Description

60 degrees Fahrenheit is considered mild weather, but it can still be chilly if you’re not prepared. Packing the right clothes and accessories can make a big difference in how comfortable you feel during your trip. You want to make sure you have the right layers to keep you warm but also be able to remove them if needed.

How to Pack for 60 Degree Weather

Packing for 60 degree weather is about finding the perfect balance between warmth and coolness. Here are some tips to help you pack for this type of climate:

Step 1: Check the Weather

The first thing you should do before you start packing is to check the weather forecast for the place you’re visiting. This will give you an idea of what to expect and help you pack accordingly.

Step 2: Choose Light Layers

Since 60 degree weather can be warm during the day and chilly at night, it’s important to pack light layers. This includes long-sleeved shirts, cardigans, light jackets, and scarves. These items can be easily layered and removed as needed.

Step 3: Don’t Forget About Pants

It’s easy to get caught up in packing tops and forget about pants. Pack a variety of pants such as jeans, leggings, and khakis that can be worn with different tops.

Step 4: Bring Comfortable Shoes

Comfortable shoes are a must in any climate. For 60 degree weather, pack closed-toe shoes such as sneakers, loafers, or boots.

Step 5: Consider Accessories

Accessories such as hats, gloves, and sunglasses are essential for any trip. They can protect you from the sun, wind, and cold.

Tips for Packing for 60 Degree Weather

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when packing for 60 degree weather:

  • Stick to neutral colors to make it easier to mix and match your outfits.
  • Choose fabrics that are easy to wash and dry.
  • Bring a small umbrella or rain jacket in case of unexpected rain.
  • Always pack a few extra items in case the weather is colder than expected.

FAQ

What should I wear in 60 degree weather?

In 60 degree weather, it’s best to wear light layers such as long-sleeved shirts, cardigans, light jackets, and scarves. Pack a variety of pants such as jeans, leggings, and khakis that can be worn with different tops. Don’t forget to pack comfortable, closed-toe shoes.

What kind of jacket should I wear in 60 degree weather?

A light jacket such as a denim jacket, bomber jacket, or windbreaker is perfect for 60 degree weather. It’s important to choose a jacket that can easily be removed if the weather gets warmer.

Should I pack shorts for 60 degree weather?

It’s best to avoid packing shorts for 60 degree weather since it can be chilly. Stick to pants and light layers instead.

Hair Care in 60 Degree Weather

Humidity can cause hair to become frizzy and unmanageable. To prevent this, pack a leave-in conditioner and a hair serum to keep your hair moisturized. If you’re going to be spending a lot of time outdoors, consider wearing a hat to protect your hair from the sun and wind.

Packing for 60 degree weather doesn’t have to be difficult. By following these tips, you can ensure that you have everything you need to stay comfortable during your trip.
