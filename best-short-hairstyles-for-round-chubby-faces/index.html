Description

Having a round chubby face can make it difficult to choose the right hairstyle that complements your facial features. Short hairstyles have been known to work best for round chubby faces as they help create an illusion of a slimmer face. In this article, we will be discussing the best short hairstyles for round chubby faces, how to choose the right one, and how to style and maintain them.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for Round Chubby Faces

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round chubby face, it is important to consider the length, texture, and volume of the hair. The ideal length for a short hairstyle for a round chubby face is above the chin to create a slimming effect. It is also important to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to the hair to balance out the roundness of the face. Hairstyles with layers and side-swept bangs are great options for round chubby faces.

Some of the Best Short Hairstyles for Round Chubby Faces Include:

  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob Cut
  • Layered Bob
  • Textured Shag
  • Side-Swept Bangs

Step by Step Guide to Style Short Hairstyles for Round Chubby Faces

Pixie Cut:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Dry your hair with a towel and apply a hair styling product to add volume and texture.
  3. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume to the roots.
  4. Use a flat iron to create texture and style your hair into a pixie cut.
  5. Finish off with a hair spray to hold the style in place.

Bob Cut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Dry your hair with a towel and apply a hair styling product for texture and volume.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the roots.
  4. Use a flat iron to create texture and style your hair into a bob cut.
  5. Finish off with a hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips to Maintain Short Hairstyles for Round Chubby Faces

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your short hairstyle.
  • Apply a hair styling product for volume and texture.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the roots.
  • Use a flat iron to create texture and style your hair.
  • Finish off with a hair spray to hold the style in place.

FAQs

Q. Can I wear a pixie cut with a round chubby face?

A. Yes, a pixie cut is a great option for round chubby faces as it adds volume and texture to the hair, creating a slimming effect.

Q. What is the best short hairstyle for a round chubby face?

A. The best short hairstyle for a round chubby face is a bob cut or a pixie cut, both of which add volume and texture to the hair, creating a slimming effect.

Q. How often should I visit the hairstylist to maintain my short hairstyle?

A. It is recommended to visit the hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your short hairstyle.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Round Chubby Faces

When caring for short hairstyles for round chubby faces, it is important to use a hair styling product that adds volume and texture to the hair. It is also important to wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny. Additionally, it is recommended to visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your short hairstyle.

With the right short hairstyle, you can accentuate your best facial features and create a slimmer appearance. Use these tips and tricks to choose the best short hairstyle for your round chubby face and style and maintain it with ease.
