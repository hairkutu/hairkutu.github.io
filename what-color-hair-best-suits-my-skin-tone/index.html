What Color Hair Best Suits My Skin Tone

Written by Oliver Jun 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are unsure of what shade will complement your skin tone. Your hair color can make or break your look, so it's important to choose wisely. In this article, we will guide you on what color hair best suits your skin tone.

Description

Your skin tone is determined by the amount of melanin in your skin. It can be classified into three categories: warm, cool, and neutral. Your skin tone will determine whether you have warm or cool undertones. Warm undertones have yellow, peach, or golden hues, while cool undertones have pink, red, or blue hues. Neutral undertones have a mix of both warm and cool undertones.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

There are a few ways to determine your skin tone:

1. Look at Your Veins

Check the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you may have neutral undertones.

2. Hold Up Different Colors

Hold up different colors of clothing, jewelry, or makeup to your face. If gold looks better on you, you have warm undertones. If silver looks better on you, you have cool undertones. If both look good, you have neutral undertones.

3. Check Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can give a clue about your skin tone. Warm undertones usually have hair with red, orange, or golden hues. Cool undertones usually have hair with ash, blue, or violet hues.

Step by Step Guide on What Color Hair Best Suits Your Skin Tone

1. Warm Undertones

If you have warm undertones, you should choose hair colors with warm hues such as golden blonde, caramel, honey, or copper. These colors will complement your skin tone and bring out the warmth in your complexion. Avoid cool tones such as platinum blonde, ash brown, or black, as they can wash you out.

2. Cool Undertones

If you have cool undertones, you should choose hair colors with cool hues such as platinum blonde, ash brown, or black. These colors will contrast your skin tone and make your complexion look brighter. Avoid warm tones such as golden blonde, copper, or red, as they can clash with your skin tone.

3. Neutral Undertones

If you have neutral undertones, you are lucky as you can pull off both warm and cool hair colors. You can experiment with different shades such as ash blonde, strawberry blonde, or chocolate brown. Just make sure to choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your eye color. Choose a hair color that complements your eye color.
  • Consult a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose the right hair color based on your skin tone and features.
  • Choose a hair color that matches your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance hair color.

FAQ

1. Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin. Just make sure to choose a hair dye that is safe for sensitive skin and do a patch test before applying it to your hair.

2. How often should I touch up my hair color?

It depends on the type of hair color you have. If you have a permanent hair color, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks. If you have a semi-permanent hair color, you should touch up your roots every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting experience. By following our guide, you can find a hair color that complements your skin tone and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
