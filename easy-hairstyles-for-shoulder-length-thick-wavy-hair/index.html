Easy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Bardi May 16, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have shoulder length thick wavy hair, you might find it challenging to style it every day. However, there are plenty of easy hairstyles that you can try to change up your look. In this article, we’ll show you step by step how to achieve some of the most popular hairstyles for shoulder length thick wavy hair.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a classic hairstyle that can be achieved with shoulder length thick wavy hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it’s slightly damp.
  2. Apply a sea salt spray to your hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create texture.
  3. Use a curling wand to curl small sections of your hair, alternating the direction of the curls.
  4. Brush out the curls with your fingers to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Beachy waves are perfect for a casual day out or a beach vacation.

Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that can be done in minutes. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by pulling your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

A messy bun is perfect for a lazy day or a day when you’re running late.

Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a simple yet elegant look that can be done in minutes. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair.
  2. Pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Use a curling wand to curl the bottom half of your hair.
  4. Brush out the curls with your fingers to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

A half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for a date night or a formal event.

Braided Crown

A braided crown is a bohemian-inspired hairstyle that can be done with shoulder length thick wavy hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and braid it.
  3. Bring the braid over your head and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

A braided crown is perfect for a music festival or a bohemian-themed party.

Fishtail Braid

A fishtail braid is a trendy hairstyle that can be done with shoulder length thick wavy hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and divide it into two sections.
  3. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of one section and bring it over to the inside of the other section.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

A fishtail braid is perfect for a day at the office or a night out with friends.

Ponytail

A ponytail is a classic hairstyle that can be done in seconds. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair back and securing it with a hair tie.
  2. Use a curling wand to curl the ends of your hair.
  3. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

A ponytail is perfect for a day at the gym or a day when you want to keep your hair out of your face.

Top Knot

A top knot is a stylish and practical hairstyle that can be done with shoulder length thick wavy hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by pulling your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun in place with bobby pins.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

A top knot is perfect for a day at the office or a night out with friends.

Twisted Bun

A twisted bun is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that can be done with shoulder length thick wavy hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by pulling your hair into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and twist it.
  4. Bring the twisted section of hair over the bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

A twisted bun is perfect for a wedding or a formal event.

Loose Curls

Loose curls are a romantic and effortless hairstyle that can be done with shoulder length thick wavy hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it’s slightly damp.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Use a curling wand to curl small sections of your hair, holding the wand vertically.
  4. Brush out the curls with your fingers to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Loose curls are perfect for a date night or a special occasion.

Straight Hair

If you want to switch up your look, you can straighten your shoulder length thick wavy hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it’s slightly damp.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten small sections of your hair.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Straight hair is perfect for a day at the office or a night out with friends.
