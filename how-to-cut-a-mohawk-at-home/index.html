How To Cut A Mohawk At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're feeling adventurous and want to try a new hairstyle, a mohawk might be just what you need. A mohawk is a bold and edgy look that is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd. But, if you're not ready to go to a salon or barbershop to get it done, you can do it at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a mohawk at home in a few easy steps.

Description

A mohawk is a hairstyle that is characterized by a strip of hair that runs down the middle of the head, while the sides are shaved or cut short. It can be styled in different ways, such as spiked up, slicked back, or even braided. Mohawks have been around for decades, and they continue to be a popular choice among both men and women.

How to Cut a Mohawk at Home

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, clippers, a comb, and some hair clips. Once you have everything, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section off the hair that you want to keep for the mohawk. This should be a strip of hair that runs from the front of your head to the back. Use hair clips to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Shave or Cut the Sides

Using clippers or scissors, shave or cut the sides of your head. The length of the sides is up to you, but they should be shorter than the hair in the middle. If you're using clippers, start with a longer guard and work your way down until you get the desired length.

Step 4: Trim the Middle Section

Using scissors, trim the hair in the middle section. The length of the hair in the middle is also up to you, but it should be longer than the sides. Start with small cuts and work your way up until you get the desired length.

Step 5: Style Your Mohawk

Once you've cut your hair, it's time to style your mohawk. You can use hair gel, wax, or hairspray to spike up the hair in the middle. You can also comb it back or braid it. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.

Tips

- Start with a longer length and work your way down. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
- Use sharp scissors and clippers to avoid uneven cuts.
- Don't rush the process. Take your time and make sure you're happy with the results.
- Ask for help if you're not confident in cutting your hair alone.

FAQ

Can I cut a mohawk with long hair?

Yes, you can cut a mohawk with long hair. However, you'll need to trim the sides to create the strip of hair in the middle.

Do I need to shave the sides of my head?

No, you don't have to shave the sides of your head. You can cut them short instead. However, shaving will create a more dramatic look.

How long does a mohawk last?

A mohawk can last for several weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. You'll need to trim it periodically to maintain the style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, make sure you take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Cutting a mohawk at home is easy if you follow these steps. With a little bit of practice, you can achieve a stylish and edgy look that will turn heads. So, go ahead and give it a try!
