Can You Get Butterfly Locs With Short Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are someone with short hair and wondering whether you can get butterfly locs, you are not alone. Butterfly locs are a popular protective hairstyle that involves wrapping your hair around crochet locs. This hairstyle is gaining popularity due to its unique style and versatility. If you are wondering whether you can get butterfly locs with short hair, the answer is yes, you can. Here is everything you need to know about getting butterfly locs with short hair.

Description

Butterfly locs are a type of faux locs that are created using the crochet method. The hair is wrapped around the crochet hair, creating a butterfly effect. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, protective hairstyle that can last for several weeks.

How to Get Butterfly Locs with Short Hair

Getting butterfly locs with short hair is possible, but it requires some preparation. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

Before getting butterfly locs, you need to determine the length of hair you want. If you have short hair, you can still get butterfly locs, but you need to choose a length that will allow you to wrap your hair around the crochet locs. You can choose a length that is slightly longer than your natural hair length to ensure that you have enough hair to wrap around the crochet hair.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before getting butterfly locs, you need to prepare your hair. This involves washing and conditioning your hair to ensure that it is clean and moisturized. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and make it easier to work with.

Step 3: Install the Crochet Locs

Once your hair is prepared, you can start installing the crochet locs. This involves using a crochet needle to weave the locs into your hair. You should start at the back of your head and work your way to the front. You can also use a latch hook to pull your natural hair through the locs, making it easier to wrap your hair around the locs.

Step 4: Wrap Your Hair

Once the crochet locs are installed, you can start wrapping your hair around the locs. You should take a small section of hair and wrap it around the loc, securing it with a hair tie. You should continue wrapping your hair around the locs until all your hair is wrapped.

Tips for Getting Butterfly Locs with Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when getting butterfly locs with short hair:

  • Choose a length that will allow you to wrap your hair around the crochet locs
  • Prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair
  • Start at the back of your head and work your way to the front when installing the crochet locs
  • Use a latch hook to pull your natural hair through the locs
  • Wrap your hair tightly around the locs to ensure that they stay in place
  • Protect your hair at night by wrapping it in a silk scarf or bonnet

FAQ

Can I get butterfly locs with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can get butterfly locs with relaxed hair. However, you should ensure that your hair is healthy and strong enough to withstand the weight of the locs.

How long do butterfly locs last?

Butterfly locs can last for several weeks, depending on how well you take care of them. You should avoid washing your hair too often and use a dry shampoo to keep your scalp clean.

How do I take care of my butterfly locs?

You should take care of your butterfly locs by moisturizing your scalp regularly and avoiding excessive manipulation. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Butterfly Locs

Here are some hair care tips to help you take care of your butterfly locs:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly
  • Avoid excessive manipulation
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your scalp clean
  • Protect your hair at night by wrapping it in a silk scarf or bonnet

In conclusion, getting butterfly locs with short hair is possible. You just need to choose the right length, prepare your hair, and follow the steps outlined above. With proper care, your butterfly locs can last for several weeks and keep your hair looking stylish and healthy.
