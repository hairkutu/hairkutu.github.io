Mens Hairstyle Long Top Faded Sides 2023 Hair Trend

Description

The mens hairstyle long top faded sides is a popular hair trend in 2023. It's a versatile look that combines a longer top with shorter sides that gradually fade into the skin. This hairstyle is suitable for men of all ages and face shapes, and it can be styled in various ways depending on personal preference.

How to Get the Mens Hairstyle Long Top Faded Sides

The mens hairstyle long top faded sides requires a skilled barber or hairdresser to achieve. The first step is to determine the length of the hair on the top and the sides. The top should be left longer, while the sides should gradually become shorter.

Tools You Need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair Gel or Pomade

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Ask your barber to leave the hair on top long and to gradually fade the sides into the skin.
  3. Use clippers to trim the sides, starting with a longer guard and gradually moving to a shorter one.
  4. Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and the top.
  5. Apply hair gel or pomade to the hair on top and style it as desired.
  6. Use a comb to create a clean part or to style the hair in a pompadour or quiff.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining the Look

  • Use a high-quality gel or pomade to keep the hair on top in place.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy or textured look.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the fade and to keep the hair on top trimmed and styled.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to the hair on top for a more dramatic look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the mens hairstyle long top faded sides suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the mens hairstyle long top faded sides is suitable for all face shapes. However, the length of the hair on top and the style may need to be adjusted depending on the individual's face shape.

How often should I visit my barber to maintain the look?

You should visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the fade and to keep the hair on top trimmed and styled.

Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

Yes, the hair on top can be styled in various ways, such as a messy or textured look, a pompadour, or a quiff.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Hairstyle Long Top Faded Sides

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep the hair on top healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out the hair and scalp.
