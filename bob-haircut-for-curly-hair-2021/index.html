Bob Haircut For Curly Hair 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 18, 2023 · 8 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on the bob haircut for curly hair in 2021! If you’re looking for a chic and trendy hairstyle that’s easy to maintain and perfect for curly locks, the bob haircut might be just what you need. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about the bob haircut for curly hair, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice. Let’s get started!

Description

The bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that’s been around for decades, but it’s always evolving to keep up with the latest trends. In 2021, the bob haircut is all about embracing your natural curls and texture. This means that the traditional straight bob is out, and the curly bob is in. The curly bob is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type, whether you have loose waves or tight corkscrew curls.

How to Get the Bob Haircut for Curly Hair

The first step to getting the bob haircut for curly hair is to find a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair. This is important because curly hair requires a different cutting technique than straight hair. You’ll want to look for a stylist who knows how to cut curly hair dry, which means they’ll cut your hair while it’s in its natural, curly state. This will ensure that your curls are evenly distributed and your haircut looks balanced.

Once you’ve found a stylist, it’s time to decide on the length and shape of your bob. The length of your bob will depend on your face shape and personal style, but a good rule of thumb is to have it hit just above your shoulders. As for the shape, you can choose from a classic bob, an angled bob, a layered bob, or a curly shag. Your stylist can help you decide which shape will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Styling the Bob Haircut for Curly Hair

Styling the bob haircut for curly hair is easy and can be done in just a few steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for curly hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them hydrated.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to further moisturize and define your curls. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel. This will help to enhance your natural curls and give your hair some hold.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, dry your hair while scrunching it upwards to encourage your curls to form. Be sure to use a low heat and speed setting to avoid frizz.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Oil

Finish off your look by applying a hair oil to your ends to add shine and prevent frizz.

Tips for Maintaining the Bob Haircut for Curly Hair

To keep your bob haircut looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips to follow:

  • Refresh your curls between washes with a curl refreshing spray.
  • Protect your hair while sleeping by wrapping it in a silk scarf or sleeping on a silk pillowcase.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep your bob looking sharp.

FAQs

Can I get a bob haircut if my curls are tight?

Yes, you can still get a bob haircut even if your curls are tight. Just be sure to work with a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair and knows how to work with your hair type.

How do I know which bob shape is right for me?

Your stylist can help you determine which bob shape will work best for your face shape and hair type. It’s important to choose a shape that flatters your features and complements your natural texture.

Can I wear my bob straight?

Yes, you can wear your bob straight if you’d like. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using heat tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Bob Haircut for Curly Hair

When it comes to caring for your bob haircut for curly hair, it’s important to use products that are designed for curly hair. This includes sulfate-free shampoos, moisturizing conditioners, and curl-enhancing styling products. Additionally, be sure to protect your hair from heat and avoid over-styling to prevent damage.

And there you have it – our ultimate guide to the bob haircut for curly hair in 2021! We hope you found this guide helpful and are inspired to try out this trendy and stylish hairstyle for yourself.
