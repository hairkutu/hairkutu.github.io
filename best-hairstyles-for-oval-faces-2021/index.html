Best Hairstyles For Oval Faces 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you one of the lucky ones with an oval face? If so, congratulations! You have a versatile and balanced facial structure that can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which style to try next. In this article, we'll share the best hairstyles for oval faces in 2021, along with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is defined by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and high cheekbones. This facial structure is considered the most versatile because it can pull off a variety of hairstyles. The key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we'll share the best hairstyles for oval faces in 2021.

How To

The first step in achieving the perfect hairstyle for your oval face is to identify your hair texture and length. Whether you have curly, straight, or wavy hair, there is a hairstyle that will work for you. Next, consider your personal style, as well as your lifestyle. Do you prefer a low-maintenance style or are you willing to spend more time on your hair?

Step by Step

Here are some popular hairstyles for oval faces in 2021:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for oval faces because they add volume and movement to your hair. This style works best for those with long hair and can be styled with waves or curls for added texture.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for oval faces. This style can be worn straight or with loose waves for added texture. The length of the bob can vary, from chin-length to shoulder-length.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle, a pixie cut is a great choice. This style works well for oval faces because it accentuates your cheekbones and jawline. You can add layers or texture to this style for added dimension.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to frame your face and add dimension to your hair. This style works well for oval faces because it softens your features and adds texture to your hair. You can wear your bangs straight or with loose waves.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your oval face:

  • Consider your hair texture and length when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style and lifestyle.
  • Consult with a hairstylist for personalized recommendations.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What hairstyles should oval faces avoid?

Oval faces can pull off a variety of hairstyles, but there are a few styles to avoid. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer. Additionally, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as this can accentuate the length of your face.

Can oval faces pull off short hair?

Absolutely! Oval faces can pull off a variety of hair lengths, including short hair. A pixie cut or bob can be a great choice for those with oval faces.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and using a heat protectant before styling with hot tools. Additionally, consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you're sure to find the perfect look for your oval face in 2021. Remember to embrace your natural beauty and have fun experimenting with different styles!
