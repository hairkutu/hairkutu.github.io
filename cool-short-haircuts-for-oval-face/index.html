Cool Short Haircuts For Oval Face Top 10 Trendy Styles In 2023

Written by Petter Jul 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that flatters your oval-shaped face, you're in luck. There are plenty of cool short haircuts that can make you look stylish and chic. From pixie cuts to bob hairstyles, there are many options to choose from. In this article, we'll take a look at the top 10 trendy styles for cool short haircuts for oval face in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care
  • Top 10 Cool Short Haircuts for Oval Face

Description

Oval face shape is one of the most versatile face shapes that can pull off a variety of haircuts. It has a balanced proportion with a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a slightly rounded chin. A cool short haircut can complement the oval face shape by highlighting its best features and creating an illusion of definition.

How to

Before choosing a cool short haircut, consider your hair type, texture, and lifestyle. Short haircuts require regular maintenance and styling, so make sure you have the time and patience to keep up with it. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut that suits your face shape and personality.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when getting a cool short haircut for oval face:

  • Research different short hairstyles that flatter oval face shape
  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and lifestyle
  • Bring reference pictures to show your hairstylist what you want
  • Maintain your short haircut by getting regular trims and using hair products that suit your hair type and texture

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a cool short haircut for oval face:

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a short haircut
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type and texture to maintain your short haircut
  • Get regular trims to keep your short haircut looking fresh and stylish

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cool short haircuts for oval face:

  • What is the best short haircut for oval face? The best short haircut for oval face depends on your personal style, hair type, and texture. Some popular options include pixie cuts, bob hairstyles, and layered haircuts.
  • Can I pull off a short haircut if I have an oval face? Yes, oval face shape is one of the most versatile face shapes that can pull off a variety of haircuts, including short haircuts.
  • How do I maintain a short haircut? Short haircuts require regular maintenance and styling. Get regular trims and use hair products that suit your hair type and texture to maintain your short haircut.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a cool short haircut:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Get regular trims to keep your short haircut looking fresh and healthy

Top 10 Cool Short Haircuts for Oval Face

Here are the top 10 trendy styles for cool short haircuts for oval face in 2023:

  1. Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  2. Bowl cut with blunt bangs
  3. Bob haircut with layers
  4. Short shag haircut
  5. Asymmetrical bob
  6. Pixie cut with long bangs
  7. Textured bob haircut
  8. Choppy layered haircut
  9. Curly bob haircut
  10. Side-parted short haircut

These cool short haircuts for oval face are perfect for anyone who wants to look stylish and chic. Choose the one that suits your personality and lifestyle, and consult with your hairstylist to get the perfect haircut for you. Remember to maintain your short haircut by getting regular trims and using hair products that suit your hair type and texture.
