Best Haircuts For Round Square Faces A Guide For 2023

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round square face? Look no further! This guide will give you all the information you need to choose the best haircut for your face shape in 2023. From how to determine your face shape to tips for hair care, we've got you covered.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best haircut for your round square face is determining your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back so that your face is fully visible. Take note of the widest part of your face, the length of your face, and the shape of your jawline.

If the widest part of your face is in the middle and your jawline is rounded, you likely have a round square face shape. This means that your face is not quite round and not quite square, but a combination of both. Other face shapes include oval, heart, diamond, and square.

Best Haircuts for Round Square Faces

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the best haircut for your round square face. The key is to choose a style that will add length to your face and create angles at your jawline. Here are some of the best haircuts for round square faces:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round square faces because they add length and movement to your hair. Ask your stylist to cut long, face-framing layers that start at your cheekbones and angle down to your shoulders.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and create angles at your jawline. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting at your eyebrows and gradually getting longer towards the sides of your face.

Bob with Angled Layers

A bob with angled layers can help create the illusion of a longer face and add angles to your jawline. Ask your stylist to cut your hair into a bob that falls just below your chin, with longer layers in the front that angle towards your jawline.

Asymmetrical Pixie Cut

An asymmetrical pixie cut can help elongate your face and add angles to your jawline. Ask your stylist to cut one side of your hair shorter than the other, with longer layers in the front that angle towards your jawline.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you've chosen the best haircut for your round square face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Use a straightener or curling iron to add texture and dimension.
  • Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different partings to create the illusion of a longer face.

Tips for Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the best haircut for round square faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round square face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a bob that falls at your jawline. These styles can make your face appear rounder and less angular.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round square face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to create volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face. You can also experiment with different partings to create the illusion of a longer face.

Do I have to follow these guidelines for choosing a haircut?

No, these guidelines are simply suggestions based on what typically looks best for round square faces. Ultimately, you should choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to choose the best haircut for your round square face in 2023. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
