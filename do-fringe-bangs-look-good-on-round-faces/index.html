Do Fringe Bangs Look Good On Round Faces

Description

Are you thinking of getting fringe bangs but you have a round face? You might be wondering if this hairstyle would look good on you. The answer is yes! Fringe bangs can look great on round faces, as long as you choose the right style and length.

How to

When choosing fringe bangs for a round face, you want to make sure they create angles and lengthen the face. Opt for side-swept bangs that start at the temples and gradually get longer towards the cheekbones. This will create a diagonal line that gives the illusion of a longer face.

Another option is to go for curtain bangs, which are parted down the middle and frame the face on both sides. These bangs create vertical lines that elongate the face and draw attention to the eyes.

It's important to avoid blunt bangs that are cut straight across the forehead, as they can make the face appear rounder. Additionally, short bangs that stop above the eyebrows can also make the face look shorter and wider.

Step by Step

If you've decided to go for fringe bangs, here's how to style them:

  1. Wash and blow dry your hair, making sure your bangs are wet.
  2. Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs, pulling them to one side for side-swept bangs or straight down the middle for curtain bangs.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair serum or pomade to your bangs to tame any frizz.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves in your bangs.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling fringe bangs for a round face:

  • Keep your bangs trimmed regularly to prevent them from becoming too long and weighing down your face.
  • Add some texture to your bangs with a texturizing spray or by using a crimping iron.
  • Accessorize your bangs with a cute headband or hair clip to add some extra flair.

FAQ

Q: Will fringe bangs make my round face look wider?

A: If you choose the right style of bangs, they can actually make your face look longer and more angular. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs and opt for side-swept or curtain bangs instead.

Q: Can I style my fringe bangs differently depending on the occasion?

A: Absolutely! You can wear your bangs straight and sleek for a formal event, or add some volume and texture for a more casual look.

Hair Care

To keep your fringe bangs looking their best, make sure to wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can cause damage and frizz. Additionally, consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

With the right style and care, fringe bangs can look great on round faces. So go ahead and try out this trendy hairstyle!
