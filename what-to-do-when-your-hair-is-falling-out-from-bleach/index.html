What To Do When Your Hair Is Falling Out From Bleach

Written by Oliver Apr 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss after bleaching your hair? Are you worried about the damage that bleach might have caused to your hair? Don't worry! In this article, we'll discuss what to do when your hair is falling out from bleach. We'll give you tips on how to prevent further damage and how to repair the damage that has already been done.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

If you're experiencing hair loss after bleaching your hair, the first thing you should do is stop using any hair products that contain bleach. This includes hair dyes, hair bleach, and hair lighteners. You should also avoid using any heat styling tools, such as hair straighteners, curling irons, and hair dryers.

The next step is to start using a deep conditioning treatment on your hair. You can use a store-bought deep conditioner or make your own by mixing equal parts of olive oil and honey. Apply the conditioner to your hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Another great way to repair the damage caused by bleach is to use a hair mask. You can make your own hair mask by mixing equal parts of avocado and coconut oil. Apply the mask to your hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step by Step

Here are the steps you should follow if you're experiencing hair loss after bleaching your hair:

  1. Stop using any hair products that contain bleach
  2. Avoid using any heat styling tools
  3. Start using a deep conditioning treatment on your hair
  4. Make your own hair mask by mixing equal parts of avocado and coconut oil
  5. Apply the hair mask to your hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out

Tips

Here are some tips to help you prevent further damage and repair the damage that has already been done:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or a scarf

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair loss after bleaching:

Q: Can hair loss from bleach be reversed?

A: Yes, hair loss from bleach can be reversed by following the steps outlined in this article.

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back after bleach damage?

A: It can take several months for hair to grow back after bleach damage. However, following the tips in this article can help speed up the process.

Hair Care

Here are some general tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner after every wash
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or a scarf
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends

By following the tips outlined in this article, you can prevent further damage and repair the damage that has already been done to your hair. Remember to be patient, as it can take time for your hair to recover from bleach damage. With proper care and attention, your hair will be healthy and beautiful once again!
