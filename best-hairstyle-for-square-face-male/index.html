Best Hairstyle For Square Face Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 05, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re a man with a square face shape, finding the perfect hairstyle can be daunting. It’s important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your facial structure. In this article, we’ll discuss the best hairstyle for square face male, including how to achieve the look, tips, and hair care. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyle for Square Face Male
  • Tips for Styling Square Face Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Square Face Hair

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before delving into the best hairstyle for square face male, it’s important to determine your face shape. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine which facial shape you have.

The most common face shapes include oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Best Hairstyle for Square Face Male

The best hairstyle for square face male is one that softens the angles of the face and adds texture. We recommend the following:

1. Short Buzz Cut

A short buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that works well for square face men. It adds texture and dimension to the hair while also minimizing the angles of the face.

2. Textured Crop

A textured crop is a trendy hairstyle that has become increasingly popular among square face men. It involves keeping the hair short on the sides and longer on top with a textured finish.

3. Side-Parted Hairstyle

A side-parted hairstyle is a classic look that works well for square face men. It involves keeping the hair short on the sides and longer on top with a side part. This style adds texture and dimension to the hair while also softening the angles of the face.

4. Quiff

A quiff is a stylish hairstyle that works well for square face men. It involves keeping the hair short on the sides and longer on top with a voluminous finish. This style adds height and texture to the hair while also softening the angles of the face.

Tips for Styling Square Face Hair

Here are some tips for styling square face hair:

  • Use a lightweight styling product to add texture and volume to the hair.
  • Avoid slicked-back hairstyles, as they can accentuate the angles of the face.
  • Consider getting a fringe or side-swept bangs to soften the forehead.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for square face male:

What hairstyles should square face men avoid?

Square face men should avoid hairstyles that accentuate the angles of the face, such as slicked-back hairstyles and center parts.

What hair length is best for square face men?

Square face men can rock any hair length, but shorter hairstyles tend to work better as they minimize the angles of the face.

Should square face men get a beard?

A beard can help balance out the proportions of a square face, but it’s not necessary. Square face men can still look great without a beard.

Hair Care for Square Face Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy square face hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a nourishing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair and scalp.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for square face male, it’s time to experiment with different looks and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair with regular washing and styling, and don’t be afraid to try new things. Happy styling!
