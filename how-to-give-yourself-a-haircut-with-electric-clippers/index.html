How To Give Yourself A Haircut With Electric Clippers

Written by Daniel May 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon or barbershop for a haircut? Do you want to save money and time by giving yourself a haircut? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to give yourself a haircut with electric clippers. So, let's get started.

Description

Electric clippers are an essential tool for anyone who wants to give themselves a haircut. They are easy to use and come with different attachments to achieve different lengths of hair. With the right technique, you can give yourself a professional-looking haircut in no time.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair with electric clippers, here are some things you need:

  • Electric clippers
  • Attachment combs
  • Scissors
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape
  • Hairbrush or comb

Once you have gathered all the necessary tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Attachment Comb

The first step is to choose the right attachment comb for the length of hair you want to achieve. The attachment combs come in different sizes, and each size corresponds to a different length of hair. Start with a longer attachment comb and work your way down to shorter ones.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting it. This will make it easier to cut and give you a better result.

Step 3: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Start by cutting the top of your hair. Use the scissors to cut the longer hair first, then use the electric clippers with the attachment comb to blend in the shorter hair. Start at the front and work your way towards the back, using the mirror to check your progress.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back of Your Hair

Next, cut the sides and back of your hair. Use the electric clippers without an attachment comb to cut the hair around your ears and the back of your neck. Use the mirror to check your progress and make sure both sides are even.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair by using different attachment combs to achieve different lengths. Start with a longer attachment comb and work your way down to shorter ones to create a smooth transition between the different lengths of hair.

Step 6: Clean Up the Hairline

Use the electric clippers without an attachment comb to create a defined hairline around your ears and the back of your neck. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you have cut your hair, style it as desired using a hairbrush or comb.

Tips

  • Start with a longer attachment comb and work your way down to shorter ones.
  • Use the mirror to check your progress and make sure both sides are even.
  • Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.
  • Wash and dry your hair before you start cutting it.
  • Use a towel or cape to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair with electric clippers without an attachment comb?

A: Yes, you can. However, it is recommended to use an attachment comb to achieve a more even cut.

Q: Can I use electric clippers to trim my beard?

A: Yes, you can. Use the attachment comb to achieve your desired beard length.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Trim your hair regularly to maintain your desired length. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to give yourself a haircut with electric clippers, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home.
