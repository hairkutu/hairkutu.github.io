How To Cut Men s Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top

Written by Frank Jul 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and edgy haircut, then look no further than the shaved sides long top style. This haircut is perfect for men who want to make a statement with their hair. In this article, we will show you how to cut men's haircut shaved sides long top in a relaxed English language. We will also provide you with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect cut.

Description

The shaved sides long top haircut is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your personal style. The sides and back are shaved or cut very short, while the top is left longer. This haircut can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or spiked up. This style is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance but stylish haircut.

How to Cut Men's Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you start cutting, you will need to gather your tools. You will need a pair of hair clippers with different guard sizes, a comb, scissors, and hair styling products.

Step 2: Decide on the length

The first step in cutting a shaved sides long top haircut is to decide on the length. Use the clippers with the appropriate guard size to trim the sides and back of the head. The top should be left longer, so use scissors to trim the hair to the desired length.

Step 3: Blend the hair

Once you have trimmed the sides and back, you will need to blend the hair. This can be done by using the clippers without a guard and carefully blending the hair between the long top and the shaved sides.

Step 4: Style the hair

Once you have cut the hair, it's time to style it. Use a comb and hair styling products to create the desired look. The shaved sides long top haircut can be styled in a variety of ways, so experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect shaved sides long top haircut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a comb to section off the hair and make sure you are cutting evenly.
  • Blend the hair carefully to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

How often should I get a shaved sides long top haircut?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. Generally, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh.

What face shape is best for a shaved sides long top haircut?

The shaved sides long top haircut is a versatile style that can be customized to suit any face shape. However, it looks particularly good on men with square or oval faces.

Hair Care

To keep your shaved sides long top haircut looking its best, you should take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You should also get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Now that you know how to cut men's haircut shaved sides long top, you can experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you. With a little bit of practice and some patience, you can achieve a stylish and edgy haircut that will turn heads wherever you go.
