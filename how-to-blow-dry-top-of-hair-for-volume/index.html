How To Blow Dry Top Of Hair For Volume The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Mar 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you ever feel like your hair is lacking in volume and just looks flat and lifeless? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with achieving volume in their hair, especially at the top. But fear not, because we have the ultimate guide on how to blow dry the top of your hair for volume.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Blow Dry Top of Hair for Volume
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Blow drying the top of your hair for volume is a great way to add some oomph to your hair and make it look fuller and more voluminous. It can be a bit tricky to get right, but with the right technique and some practice, you'll be a pro in no time.

How to Blow Dry Top of Hair for Volume

To start, you'll want to make sure that your hair is clean and towel-dried. You don't want your hair to be dripping wet, but it should still be damp.

Next, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots. This will help to give your hair some lift and make it easier to style.

Now it's time to start blow drying. Begin by flipping your head upside down and using your fingers to lift the roots of your hair. This will help to create volume at the top.

Use a round brush to help create even more volume. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends of your hair. Make sure to use the brush to lift the hair as you dry it.

Once your hair is dry, flip your head back up and use your fingers to tousle your hair and create even more volume.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you blow dry the top of your hair for volume:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots
  3. Flip your head upside down and use your fingers to lift the roots of your hair
  4. Use a round brush to create even more volume
  5. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends of your hair
  6. Use the brush to lift the hair as you dry it
  7. Once your hair is dry, flip your head back up and use your fingers to tousle your hair and create even more volume

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve maximum volume when blow drying the top of your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair in sections to ensure that you don't miss any spots
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help add volume
  • Don't be afraid to use a lot of product to help hold your style and add volume

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about blow drying the top of your hair for volume:

How long does it take to blow dry the top of your hair for volume?

It typically takes around 10-15 minutes to blow dry the top of your hair for volume, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Can you blow dry the top of your hair for volume if you have short hair?

Yes, you can still blow dry the top of your hair for volume if you have short hair. Just use a smaller round brush and be sure to lift the roots of your hair as you dry it.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair

Now that you know how to blow dry the top of your hair for volume, you can say goodbye to flat, lifeless hair and hello to voluminous locks. With a little practice and the right technique, you'll be able to achieve the perfect amount of volume every time.
