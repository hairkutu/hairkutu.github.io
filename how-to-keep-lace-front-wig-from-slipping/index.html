How To Keep Lace Front Wig From Slipping

Written by Oliver Jul 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are a wig wearer, you know that there is nothing more frustrating than a wig that constantly slips off your head. This is especially true when it comes to lace front wigs. However, there are several steps you can take to ensure that your lace front wig stays securely in place. In this blog post, we will discuss how to keep lace front wig from slipping, so you can feel confident and comfortable in your wig.

How to Keep Lace Front Wig from Slipping

Here are some tips on how to keep lace front wig from slipping:

1. Use Wig Tape or Adhesive

One of the most effective ways to keep your lace front wig from slipping is to use wig tape or adhesive. Wig tape is a double-sided tape that you can use to secure your wig to your scalp. Adhesive is a glue-like substance that you can apply to your scalp and the inside of the wig cap. Both wig tape and adhesive are available at most beauty supply stores.

2. Secure Your Wig with Bobby Pins

If you don't want to use wig tape or adhesive, you can also secure your wig with bobby pins. Simply place the wig cap over your head and use bobby pins to secure it in place. Make sure to place the bobby pins in areas where they won't be visible.

3. Use a Wig Grip Band

A wig grip band is a stretchy band that you can wear underneath your wig cap. The band helps to keep your wig in place by providing a non-slip surface for the wig to grip onto. Wig grip bands are available in different colors and sizes, so you can choose one that matches your skin tone.

4. Adjust the Wig Cap

If your wig cap is too loose, your wig will likely slip off your head. To prevent this, make sure to adjust the wig cap so it fits snugly on your head. You can do this by tightening the straps on the wig cap or by using wig clips to secure the cap in place.

5. Tie Your Hair Underneath the Wig

If you have long hair, make sure to tie it up and secure it underneath the wig cap. This will help to prevent your hair from getting tangled in the wig and causing it to slip off your head.

Step by Step Guide on How to Keep Lace Front Wig from Slipping

Here is a step by step guide on how to keep lace front wig from slipping:

Step 1: Clean Your Scalp

Before applying your wig, make sure to clean your scalp thoroughly. This will help to remove any dirt or oil that could prevent the wig from sticking to your head.

Step 2: Apply Wig Tape or Adhesive

If you are using wig tape or adhesive, apply it to your scalp and the inside of the wig cap. Make sure to follow the instructions on the packaging carefully.

Step 3: Put on the Wig

Place the wig cap over your head and adjust it so that it fits snugly. Make sure that the wig is positioned correctly on your head.

Step 4: Secure the Wig

If you are using bobby pins, secure the wig to your scalp by placing the pins in areas where they won't be visible. If you are using a wig grip band, place the band over your head and position it so that it covers your hairline.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

Once your wig is securely in place, you can style it as desired. You can use heat-styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, to create your desired look.

Tips for Keeping Your Lace Front Wig in Place

Here are some additional tips for keeping your lace front wig in place:

1. Use a Wig Stand

When you're not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand. This will help to maintain its shape and prevent it from getting tangled.

2. Avoid Excessive Heat

Excessive heat can damage your wig and cause it to lose its shape. Avoid exposing your wig to high temperatures, such as those from hair dryers or straightening irons.

3. Clean Your Wig Regularly

Regular cleaning will help to keep your wig looking its best. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for cleaning your wig.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my lace front wig in the shower?

A: No, you should not wear your lace front wig in the shower. Water can damage the wig and cause it to lose its shape.

Q: How long will my lace front wig last?

A: The lifespan of a lace front wig depends on how well it is cared for and how often it is worn. With proper care, a lace front wig can last for several months.

Hair Care for Lace Front Wigs

Here are some tips for caring for your lace front wig:

1. Use a Wig Brush

Use a wig brush to detangle your wig. Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots.

2. Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo to clean your wig. Avoid using hot water, as this can damage the wig.

3. Avoid Sleeping in Your Wig

Avoid sleeping in your wig, as this can cause it to tangle and lose its shape.

By following these tips, you can keep your lace front wig securely in place and looking its best. Whether you're wearing your wig for fashion or medical reasons, these tips will help you feel confident and comfortable in your wig.
