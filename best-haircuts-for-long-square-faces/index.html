Best Haircuts For Long Square Faces

Are you a long square-faced person looking for the perfect haircut? You're in luck! This article will provide you with the best haircuts for long square faces, as well as tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long square face is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, which can make it difficult to find the right haircut. However, there are many hairstyles that can flatter a long square face, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and soft waves. These haircuts can help to soften the jawline and create a more balanced look.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a long square face, it's important to consider the length of your hair. Longer hair can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. Additionally, layers can help to add volume and movement to your hair, making it appear fuller and more luscious.

Step by Step

Here are the best haircuts for long square faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for long square faces as they help to soften the jawline and create movement in the hair. For best results, ask your stylist to add layers starting from your cheekbones and going down to the ends of your hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create a more balanced look for long square faces. Ask your stylist to create long, side-swept bangs that start at the crown of your head and taper down to the ends of your hair.

3. Soft Waves

Soft waves can help to add volume to your hair and create a more feminine look. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose, beachy waves throughout your hair.

Tips

When styling your hair, consider using volumizing products to add volume and movement to your hair. Additionally, avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or angular, as they can accentuate the squareness of your face.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a long square face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair straight if you have a long square face. However, consider adding some layers or side-swept bangs to soften your features.

Hair Care

When caring for your hair, consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Additionally, avoid heat styling your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. Finally, consider using a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.

In conclusion, there are many haircuts that can flatter a long square face. Consider trying long layers, side-swept bangs, or soft waves to soften your features and create a more balanced look. Remember to always take care of your hair by using deep conditioning treatments and avoiding heat styling too often.
