Best Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50

Written by Robby Apr 10, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hairstyles should evolve to complement our changing features. For women over 50 with round faces, it's important to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape while still reflecting your personal style. In this article, we'll explore the best hairstyles for round faces over 50, including how to achieve them, tips for maintenance, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your Desired Look
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs About Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50
  • Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round faces over 50, let's first determine whether you have a round face shape. To do this, follow these simple steps:

  1. Take a photo of yourself facing the camera with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face on the photo.
  3. Compare the shape to the following face shapes: oval, square, heart, and round.

If your face shape is round, you'll notice that your face is almost as wide as it is long, with a rounded chin and soft features.

Best Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's explore the best hairstyles for round faces over 50:

1. Shoulder-Length Layers

Shoulder-length layers are a great option for women over 50 with round faces because they elongate the face without drawing attention to the chin. This hairstyle works well with both straight and curly hair, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and confident hairstyle that works well for round faces over 50. This cut draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones, while still maintaining a flattering shape around the chin. To keep the look modern, consider adding layers or highlights.

3. Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is a classic hairstyle that works well for women of all ages, including those with round faces over 50. The bangs help to create a vertical line, which elongates the face, while the bob adds volume and movement. Consider adding subtle highlights to add depth and dimension.

4. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for women over 50 with round faces because they create vertical lines and add movement to the hair. This hairstyle works well with both straight and curly hair, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

5. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for women over 50 with round faces because they draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, while still maintaining a flattering shape around the chin. This hairstyle works well with both short and long hair, and can be easily styled with a round brush and blow dryer.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your Desired Look

To achieve your desired look, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair and use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and movement.
  3. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add additional texture and dimension.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray to maintain your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To maintain your hairstyle, follow these simple tips:

  • Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50

Q: Can women over 50 pull off a pixie cut?

A: Absolutely! A pixie cut can be a great option for women over 50 who want to embrace a bold and confident hairstyle.

Q: What is the best way to style a bob with bangs?

A: To style a bob with bangs, use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and movement. Finish with a light hold hairspray to maintain your desired look.

Q: Can women over 50 wear long hair?

A: Yes! Long hair can be a great option for women over 50, especially when styled with layers and movement.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50

1. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, especially as you age. To protect your hair from the sun, consider wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

As we age, our hair becomes more prone to dryness and breakage. To keep your hair healthy and hydrated, consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

3. Choose a Hairstyle That Complements Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you're a busy mom or career woman, you may want a low-maintenance style that is easy to maintain.

In conclusion, there are many great hairstyles for round faces over 50. By considering your face shape, personal style, and lifestyle, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and reflects your unique personality.
