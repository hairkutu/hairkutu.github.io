How To Make My Straight Hair Really Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to add some curls to your locks? It can be a challenge to make straight hair really curly, but it's not impossible. In this article, we'll give you step-by-step instructions on how to make your straight hair really curly.

Description

Having straight hair can sometimes be boring, and you might want to switch things up by adding some curls. Whether you want big bouncy curls or tight ringlets, there are several methods you can use to achieve your desired look.

How to

Method 1: Using a Curling Iron

To create curls using a curling iron, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant product.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  3. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel, starting from the root and working your way down.
  4. Hold the hair in place for about 10-15 seconds, then release it from the curling iron.
  5. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.
  6. Once all sections are curled, use your fingers to separate the curls and create a more natural look.
  7. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Method 2: Using Hair Rollers

To create curls using hair rollers, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant product.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  3. Take one section of hair and wrap it around a hair roller, starting from the root and working your way down.
  4. Secure the roller in place with a clip or pin.
  5. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.
  6. Leave the rollers in your hair for at least 30 minutes or until your hair is completely dry.
  7. Remove the rollers and use your fingers to separate the curls.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Step-by-Step

Now that you know the methods, here's a step-by-step guide to making your straight hair really curly:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant product.
  2. Choose the method you want to use (curling iron or hair rollers).
  3. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  4. Start curling your hair using your chosen method.
  5. Once all sections are curled, use your fingers to separate the curls and create a more natural look.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to make your straight hair really curly:

  • Use a heat protectant product to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly to avoid damaging it.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your curls in place.
  • Try using different sized rollers or curling iron barrels to create different types of curls.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making straight hair really curly:

Can I make my straight hair really curly without using heat?

Yes, you can use non-heat methods such as braiding your hair or using flexi rods to create curls without using heat.

How long will my curls last?

It depends on your hair type and the method you used to create your curls. Generally, your curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Can I make my curls last longer?

Yes, you can make your curls last longer by using a strong hold hairspray, sleeping with a silk scarf or bonnet on, and avoiding touching your hair too much.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair when trying to make it really curly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your curls too much to prevent frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your straight hair really curly, it's time to experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid damaging it with too much heat.
