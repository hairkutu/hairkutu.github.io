How Long Is A Size 4 Haircut

Written by Petter Jul 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering getting a size 4 haircut but aren't sure how long it will be? Look no further, as we break down everything you need to know about this popular haircut length.

Description

A size 4 haircut refers to the length of the guard that is used on the clippers. This guard leaves hair at a length of 1/2 inch or 12.7mm. Keep in mind that the length may vary slightly depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

How to

If you're interested in getting a size 4 haircut, the first step is to find a stylist or barber who is comfortable with using clippers. Once you've found someone you trust, communicate clearly about the length you want and any specific styling preferences you have.

The process typically involves trimming the hair evenly all around the head using the clippers with the size 4 guard attached. If you want a more layered look or have specific styling requests, be sure to communicate that with your stylist or barber.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step breakdown of what to expect during a size 4 haircut:

  1. Consult with your stylist or barber about the length and style you want.
  2. The stylist or barber will prepare the clippers with a size 4 guard attached.
  3. Starting at the bottom of the head, the stylist or barber will use the clippers to trim the hair evenly all around the head.
  4. If you have any specific requests, communicate them with your stylist or barber at this time.
  5. The stylist or barber will finish the haircut by blending any uneven areas and shaping the hairline.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering a size 4 haircut:

  • Be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist or barber about the length and style you want.
  • If you're unsure about the length, it's always better to start with a longer guard and work your way shorter.
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a style.
  • Remember that hair grows back, so don't be afraid to try something new.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about size 4 haircuts:

Is a size 4 haircut short?

A size 4 haircut is considered a medium length, as it leaves hair at a length of 1/2 inch or 12.7mm.

Will a size 4 haircut suit me?

The length and style of a haircut should be chosen based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences. It's best to consult with a stylist or barber to determine the best haircut for you.

How often should I get a size 4 haircut?

The frequency of haircuts depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain it. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care

After getting a size 4 haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

Overall, a size 4 haircut can be a versatile and low-maintenance option for those who want a medium-length style. By communicating clearly with your stylist or barber and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy your new haircut for weeks to come.
