How To Cut Curly Hair Without Layers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 30, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right haircut. Layers can often create frizz and make your hair look more voluminous than you’d like. But don’t worry! In this guide, we’ll show you how to cut curly hair without layers, so you can achieve a sleek and polished look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Curly Hair Without Layers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

If you’re looking for a low-maintenance haircut that will show off your natural curls, cutting your hair without layers may be the perfect solution. This technique involves cutting your hair straight across at the ends, rather than creating layers that add volume and movement.

Without layers, your hair will appear longer and more even, and you’ll be able to achieve a sleek and polished look with minimal effort. Plus, cutting curly hair without layers can help reduce frizz and make your curls look more defined.

How to Cut Curly Hair Without Layers

Before you start cutting your hair, it’s important to gather the right tools. For cutting curly hair, you’ll need a sharp pair of scissors, a spray bottle with water, and a wide-tooth comb.

To begin, wash and condition your hair as you normally would, and let it air dry until it’s about 80% dry. This will help you see the natural shape and movement of your curls, so you can cut your hair to suit your unique texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use clips to secure each section, so you can work on one section at a time.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair

Take one section of hair and use your wide-tooth comb to comb through any tangles. Then, use your scissors to cut your hair straight across at the ends. Be sure to cut slowly and carefully, so you don’t accidentally cut off too much hair.

Repeat this process for each section of hair, until you’ve cut all of your hair straight across at the ends.

Step 3: Check for Evenness

Once you’ve cut all of your hair, use your comb to check for evenness. Comb through each section of hair and make sure that the ends are all the same length.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After you’ve finished cutting your hair, it’s time to style it! Use your fingers to scrunch your curls and create volume, or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair and enhance your curls.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Cutting curly hair can be tricky, so here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Always cut your hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean cuts and prevent split ends.
  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time, and check for evenness after each cut.
  • If you’re not comfortable cutting your own hair, consider going to a professional stylist who has experience cutting curly hair.

FAQs

Can you cut curly hair without layers?

Yes! Cutting curly hair without layers is a great way to achieve a sleek and polished look without adding extra volume or frizz.

What tools do I need to cut curly hair?

To cut curly hair, you’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a spray bottle with water, and a wide-tooth comb.

How do I prevent frizz when cutting curly hair?

To prevent frizz when cutting curly hair, make sure to cut your hair when it’s dry, use sharp scissors, and cut your hair straight across at the ends.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

After cutting your hair without layers, it’s important to take good care of your curls to keep them looking healthy and defined. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your hair’s natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair when it’s damp, to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

By following these tips and cutting your hair without layers, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that shows off your beautiful curls. Good luck!
