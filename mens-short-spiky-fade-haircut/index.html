Mens Short Spiky Fade Haircut A Trendy Look In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are looking for a trendy hairstyle that will make you stand out in a crowd, then the mens short spiky fade haircut is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity over the years, and in 2023, it is all set to become one of the most sought-after hairstyles for men. In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting a mens short spiky fade haircut, with tips on how to maintain it and answer some frequently asked questions about the hairstyle.

Description

The mens short spiky fade haircut is a combination of short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on the top that is styled into spikes. The sides and back are faded gradually, creating a seamless transition between the shorter and longer hair. The hairstyle looks edgy and stylish, making it perfect for men who want a modern and bold look.

How to Get a Mens Short Spiky Fade Haircut

The mens short spiky fade haircut can be achieved by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting the haircut, it is important to consult with your hairstylist. They will help you determine if the hairstyle is suitable for your face shape and hair type. You can also discuss the length of the spikes and the level of fade you prefer.

Step 2: Preparation

The hairstylist will start by washing your hair and drying it with a towel. They will then comb it out and section it for cutting.

Step 3: Cutting

The hairstylist will use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair. They will gradually fade the hair from shorter to longer as they move up towards the top of your head. They will then cut the longer hair on the top to your desired length and style it into spikes using a styling product.

Step 4: Finishing

The hairstylist will finish the haircut by trimming any stray hairs and applying a finishing product to add shine and hold to the spikes.

Tips for Maintaining a Mens Short Spiky Fade Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens short spiky fade haircut:

  • Use a styling product to hold the spikes in place.
  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer on high heat as it can damage your hair.

FAQ

Can anyone get a mens short spiky fade haircut?

The mens short spiky fade haircut is suitable for most hair types and face shapes. However, it is best to consult with your hairstylist before getting the haircut to ensure it suits you.

What styling products should I use for a mens short spiky fade haircut?

You can use a gel, pomade or wax to style the spikes. Choose a product that provides a strong hold and is suitable for your hair type.

Can I style the spikes differently?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles for the spikes. You can try a messy or textured look, or a more polished and sleek look, depending on the occasion.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, make sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools. You can also use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent split ends.

The mens short spiky fade haircut is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that is perfect for men who want to make a bold statement. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can maintain the look and keep your hair healthy and shiny. So, go ahead and try this popular hairstyle in 2023!
