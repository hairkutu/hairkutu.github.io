Best Hairstyle For Oval Face Shape Boy

Written by Oliver Jun 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a boy with an oval face shape and trying to find the perfect hairstyle to suit your features? Look no further than this guide on the best hairstyle for oval face shape boy. We've curated the top styles that will complement your face shape and leave you feeling confident and stylish.

Description

Before we delve into specific hairstyles, let's discuss the characteristics of an oval face shape. An oval face shape is longer than it is wide and has a slightly rounded jawline. This shape is considered the most versatile and can suit a variety of hairstyles.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for an oval face shape boy, it's essential to consider the length and texture of your hair. The most flattering styles for this face shape are ones that keep the hair off the forehead and showcase the natural features of the face.

Short Hair

A classic buzz cut or crew cut is an excellent option for a boy with an oval face shape. These styles highlight the natural features of the face and can be easily maintained with regular trims. For a more edgy look, consider a textured crop or a short fringe that falls just above the eyebrows.

Medium Hair

A medium-length hairstyle, such as a side-swept fringe or layered cut, can work well with an oval face shape. These styles add volume and texture to the hair while still keeping it off the forehead. Avoid styles that are too long or cover the face, as they can make the face appear narrower.

Long Hair

Long hair can also work well with an oval face shape, as long as it's styled correctly. A layered cut with face-framing layers can add dimension and movement to the hair. A side-parted style or slicked-back style can also work well, as they showcase the natural features of the face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on achieving a classic buzz cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use clippers to trim the hair to a uniform length
  3. Blend the sides and back with a slightly longer guard to create a tapered look
  4. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs on top and blend with the sides
  5. Finish with a styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to add texture and hold

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for an oval face shape boy, consider the following tips:

  • Keep the hair off the forehead to showcase the natural features of the face
  • Avoid styles that cover the face or make it appear narrower
  • Add volume and texture to the hair to create dimension
  • Choose a style that complements your personal style and personality
  • Maintain regular trims to keep the style looking fresh

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for oval face shape boy:

What hairstyles should I avoid with an oval face shape?

Avoid styles that cover the face or make it appear narrower, such as long bangs or a center part.

Can I still have long hair with an oval face shape?

Yes, as long as it's styled correctly. Opt for face-framing layers and avoid styles that cover the face.

How often should I get a trim?

To keep the style looking fresh, aim for a trim every four to six weeks.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle wet hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage and damage
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

Now that you have a better understanding of the best hairstyle for oval face shape boy, you can confidently choose a style that complements your features and personality. Remember to consider the length and texture of your hair, keep the hair off the forehead, and maintain regular trims to keep the style looking fresh.
