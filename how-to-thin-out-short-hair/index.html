How To Thin Out Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you have short hair that feels too thick and heavy? Thin hair can look and feel more manageable and easy to style. In this article, we’ll show you how to thin out short hair in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Thin Out Short Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Thinning Out Short Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Thinned Out Short Hair

How to Thin Out Short Hair

Thinning out short hair can be done with a few simple steps. Here are some ways to do it:

  • Use thinning shears or scissors to cut some of the hair strands. Thinning shears are designed to remove some hair while leaving the rest of the hair intact.
  • Try a razor to create a choppy or textured look. This technique can add volume and dimension to short hair.
  • Use a clipper to trim some of the hair near the roots. This method can help create a more even and natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to thin out short hair:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. Use a brush or a comb to remove any tangles.
  2. Select the hair section that you want to thin out. You can choose to thin out the entire head of hair or just a specific section.
  3. Use thinning shears or scissors to cut the hair strands. Start at the bottom of the hair section and work your way up. Be careful not to remove too much hair at once.
  4. Check the hair section to see if it has the desired thickness. If not, repeat step 3 until you achieve the desired thickness.
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 for other sections of the hair.
  6. Check the hair in the mirror to see if it has the desired look. Make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Apply styling products as desired.

Tips for Thinning Out Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when thinning out short hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up. This will help you avoid removing too much hair at once.
  • Use sharp scissors or shears. Dull scissors can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut if you are unsure about how to thin out your hair.
  • Be patient and take breaks as needed. Thinning out hair can be a time-consuming process.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about thinning out short hair:

How often should I thin out my short hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it is recommended to thin out short hair every 6-8 weeks.

Can I thin out my short hair at home?

Yes, you can. However, it is recommended to seek advice from a professional hairstylist if you are unsure about how to do it.

Will thinning out my short hair make it look thinner?

No, thinning out your short hair will not make it look thinner. It can help create a more manageable and easy-to-style look.

Hair Care for Thinned Out Short Hair

After thinning out your short hair, it is essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons too often.
  • Apply hair oil or serum to keep the hair hydrated and healthy-looking.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the thinned-out look.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a thinned-out short hair look that is easy to style and manage. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
