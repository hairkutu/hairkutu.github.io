How To Cut Face Framing Long Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 06, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, cutting face framing long bangs can be a great way to freshen up your look. In this guide, we'll go through the steps to achieve this hairstyle, as well as provide tips for maintaining it.

Description

Face framing long bangs are a popular hairstyle that can suit a variety of face shapes and hair types. They typically start at the crown of the head and extend down to the eyebrows or just below, framing the face and adding a youthful and edgy touch to any haircut. This hairstyle can be achieved with a simple trim or a more layered approach, depending on your preference.

How to Cut Face Framing Long Bangs

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You'll also need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors and a comb.

Step 1: Section off your hair

Start by sectioning off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. This typically includes the hair at the crown of your head, extending down to just above your eyebrows.

Step 2: Comb out the section

Use a comb to separate the section of hair you want to cut and comb it out so that it's smooth and free of tangles.

Step 3: Cut the bangs

Starting at the center of your forehead, use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to avoid making mistakes. Continue cutting until you've reached your desired length.

Step 4: Check for symmetry

Once you've cut your bangs, check for symmetry by comparing both sides. If one side is longer than the other, trim it until both sides are even.

Step 5: Blend the bangs

To create a more natural look, use your scissors to blend the bangs into the rest of your hair. This can be done by gently snipping into the ends of the hair, creating a feathered look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect face framing long bangs:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.
  • Use a comb and your fingers to help guide your scissors while cutting.
  • Don't cut too much at once, as it's easier to trim more hair than to add it back.
  • Consider layering your bangs to add more texture.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you cut.

FAQ

How often should I trim my face framing long bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their length and shape.

Can I cut my own face framing long bangs?

Yes, it's possible to cut your own bangs at home. However, it's important to take your time and be cautious, as mistakes can be difficult to fix.

Hair Care

To keep your face framing long bangs looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using heat protectant products when styling with heat tools. Additionally, be sure to schedule regular trims to maintain the shape of your bangs.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve the perfect face framing long bangs and keep your hair looking great. Happy styling!
