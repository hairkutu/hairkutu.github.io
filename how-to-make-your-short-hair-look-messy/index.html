How To Make Your Short Hair Look Messy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 15, 2023 · 7 min read

Do you want to achieve that effortless, messy hair look but you have short hair? Don't worry, you can still rock that messy hair look even with short hair! In this post, we will guide you on how to make your short hair look messy. Check out our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care tips to help you achieve that perfectly messy hair look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Short Hair Look Messy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Perfectly Messy Hair Look
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Short, Messy Hair

How to Make Your Short Hair Look Messy

When it comes to achieving that messy hair look, the key is to create texture and volume. Here are some ways to make your short hair look messy:

  • Use texturizing spray or dry shampoo to create volume and texture
  • Scrunch your hair with your hands to create a messy, tousled look
  • Tease your hair at the roots to add volume
  • Create loose waves or curls with a curling iron or flat iron
  • Mess up your hair with your fingers to create a natural, undone look

With these simple tips, you can easily transform your short hair into a perfectly messy hairstyle. Now, let's move on to the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve your desired messy hair look:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Then, blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.

Step 2: Apply texturizing spray or dry shampoo

Apply texturizing spray or dry shampoo to your hair to create texture and volume. Focus on the roots and ends of your hair.

Step 3: Tease your hair

Tease your hair at the crown to add more volume. Use a teasing comb or a fine-toothed comb to backcomb your hair.

Step 4: Create loose waves

Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls. Don't worry if your curls aren't perfect, messy curls are the goal.

Step 5: Mess up your hair

Use your fingers to mess up your hair and create a natural, undone look. Scrunch your hair and tousle it with your hands.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finish your messy hairstyle with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfectly Messy Hair Look

Here are some tips for achieving a perfectly messy hair look:

  • Use the right products: Use texturizing spray or dry shampoo to create volume and texture in your hair.
  • Tease your hair: Teasing your hair at the roots will add more volume and create a fuller look.
  • Don't overdo it: The key to a messy hairstyle is to make it look natural and effortless. Don't overdo it with styling products or teasing.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles: Try different messy hairstyles to find the one that works best for your hair type and face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I achieve a messy hairstyle with short hair?

Absolutely! Short hair can still be styled into a messy hairstyle. Just use texturizing spray and tease your hair at the roots to create volume.

What products should I use to achieve a messy hairstyle?

Use texturizing spray or dry shampoo to create volume and texture in your hair. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Hair Care Tips for Short, Messy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short, messy hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a perfectly messy hairstyle even with short hair. Have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and don't be afraid to try new things!
