How To Volume Hair With Straightener

Voluminous hair has always been a popular trend, and achieving it is not always easy. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can easily achieve the desired volume. In this article, we will discuss how to volume hair with straightener, step by step, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

How to Volume Hair with Straightener

Volumizing hair with a straightener may sound counterintuitive, but it is an effective way to achieve bouncy, voluminous hair. Follow these steps to achieve voluminous hair with a straightener:

Step 1: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start straightening your hair, it is essential to apply a heat protectant. Heat protectants create a barrier between your hair and the heat, preventing damage and breakage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections before you start straightening it. This will make it easier to straighten your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Straighten Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp the straightener at the root. Slowly glide the straightener down your hair, pulling it away from your head. To add volume, twist the straightener as you glide it down your hair. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have straightened your hair, finish with a light hold hairspray. This will help to hold the volume in place and prevent it from falling flat.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to volumizing hair with a straightener:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Apply heat protectant
  3. Section your hair into small sections
  4. Take a small section of hair and clamp the straightener at the root
  5. Glide the straightener down your hair
  6. Twist the straightener as you glide it down your hair to add volume
  7. Repeat the process on all sections of your hair
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray

Tips for Volumizing Hair with Straightener

To achieve the best results when volumizing hair with a straightener, follow these tips:

  • Use a high-quality straightener with adjustable heat settings
  • Start with clean and dry hair
  • Apply a heat protectant to prevent damage and breakage
  • Section your hair into small sections to ensure even straightening
  • Twist the straightener as you glide it down your hair to add volume
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to hold the volume in place

FAQs

Can I use a straightener to add volume to my hair?

Yes, you can use a straightener to add volume to your hair by twisting it as you glide it down your hair.

What kind of hairspray should I use to hold the volume in place?

Use a light hold hairspray to hold the volume in place without making your hair stiff or crunchy.

Hair Care Tips for Voluminous Hair

To maintain your voluminous hair, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming oily
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as it is less damaging than a brush

Now that you know how to volume hair with straightener, you can achieve bouncy, voluminous hair in no time. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care tips to achieve the best results.
