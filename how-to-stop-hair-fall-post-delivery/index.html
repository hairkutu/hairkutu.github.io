How To Stop Hair Fall Post Delivery Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Mar 29, 2023 · 7 min read

Post delivery, many new mothers experience hair fall due to hormonal imbalances and stress. This can be a frustrating experience, but there are ways to combat it. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair fall post delivery with tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Stop Hair Fall Post Delivery
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Stop Hair Fall Post Delivery

The first step to stopping hair fall post delivery is to identify the root cause. Hormonal imbalances, stress, and nutritional deficiencies are common culprits. Once you have identified the cause, you can take steps to address it.

Here are some ways to stop hair fall post delivery:

1. Get Enough Nutrients

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help prevent hair fall. Make sure to include foods that are high in protein, iron, and biotin. These nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water is important for overall health, including hair health. Aim to drink at least 8 glasses of water per day to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Manage Stress

Stress can cause hair fall, so it is important to manage stress levels. Engage in activities that help you relax, such as yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.

4. Consult a Doctor

If your hair fall is severe, it may be a good idea to consult a doctor. They can help identify any underlying medical conditions that may be causing your hair fall and recommend appropriate treatment.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions to stop hair fall post delivery:

1. Identify the Cause

The first step is to identify the cause of your hair fall. This can be done by consulting a doctor or a hair specialist.

2. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, especially those that are good for hair health, such as protein, iron, and biotin.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water per day to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

4. Manage Stress

Engage in activities that help you manage stress levels, such as yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.

5. Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and bleach. Use gentle hair care products that are free of sulfates and parabens.

6. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair and cause it to fall out.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to stop hair fall post delivery:

  • Massage your scalp with warm oil to increase blood circulation and promote hair growth.
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly, as this can cause breakage and hair fall.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement, such as biotin or iron, to promote hair growth.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage and hair fall.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this is gentler on your hair and causes less breakage.

FAQs

1. How long does postpartum hair loss last?

Postpartum hair loss can last up to 6 months after delivery, but it usually resolves on its own within a year.

2. Can postpartum hair loss be prevented?

Postpartum hair loss can be prevented by following a healthy diet, staying hydrated, managing stress levels, and using gentle hair care products.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and prevent hair fall:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can stop hair fall post delivery and enjoy healthy, beautiful hair.
