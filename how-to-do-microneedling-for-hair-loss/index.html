How To Do Microneedling For Hair Loss A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are experiencing hair loss, you are not alone. Hair loss is a common issue that affects both men and women. Fortunately, there are several treatments available to help combat hair loss, one of which is microneedling. In this article, we will discuss how to do microneedling for hair loss, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips to ensure optimal results.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to do Microneedling for Hair Loss
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Microneedling for Hair Loss
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips After Microneedling

Description

Microneedling is a minimally invasive procedure that involves puncturing the skin with small needles to stimulate collagen production. Microneedling has been used for many years to improve the appearance of acne scars, fine lines, and wrinkles. Recently, microneedling has gained popularity as a treatment for hair loss. Microneedling for hair loss involves creating small punctures in the scalp to stimulate hair growth.

How to do Microneedling for Hair Loss

Microneedling for hair loss is a simple procedure that can be done at home or in a clinic. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Clean your scalp

Before microneedling, it is essential to clean your scalp thoroughly. Use a gentle shampoo and warm water to remove any dirt or oil on your scalp. Dry your hair using a clean towel or a hairdryer.

Step 2: Prepare the microneedling device

Choose a microneedling device with needles that are at least 0.5mm long. Sterilize the device by soaking it in alcohol for a few minutes. Once the device is sterilized, you can start microneedling.

Step 3: Microneedle your scalp

Start microneedling your scalp by dividing your hair into several sections. Use the microneedling device to make small punctures on your scalp, focusing on the areas where you experience hair loss. Be gentle and avoid applying too much pressure, as this can cause pain or bleeding.

Step 4: Apply hair growth serum

Once you have microneedled your scalp, apply a hair growth serum to the affected areas. The serum should contain ingredients such as minoxidil, biotin, and keratin, which can help stimulate hair growth. Massage the serum into your scalp gently.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to do microneedling for hair loss:

  1. Clean your scalp using a gentle shampoo and warm water
  2. Sterilize the microneedling device by soaking it in alcohol for a few minutes
  3. Divide your hair into several sections
  4. Gently make small punctures on your scalp using the microneedling device
  5. Apply a hair growth serum containing minoxidil, biotin, and keratin to the affected areas
  6. Massage the serum into your scalp gently
  7. Repeat the process once a week for optimal results

Tips for Microneedling for Hair Loss

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing microneedling for hair loss:

  • Choose a microneedling device with needles that are at least 0.5mm long
  • Be gentle and avoid applying too much pressure when microneedling to avoid pain or bleeding
  • Apply a hair growth serum containing minoxidil, biotin, and keratin to the affected areas after microneedling
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after microneedling
  • Repeat the process once a week for optimal results

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about microneedling for hair loss:

Is microneedling safe for hair loss?

Yes, microneedling is a safe and effective treatment for hair loss when done correctly. However, it is essential to follow the instructions carefully to avoid pain or bleeding.

How often should I do microneedling for hair loss?

You should do microneedling for hair loss once a week for optimal results. However, you should avoid microneedling too frequently, as this can cause damage to your scalp.

How long does it take to see results from microneedling for hair loss?

You may start seeing results from microneedling for hair loss after several weeks to a few months, depending on the severity of your hair loss. However, it is essential to be patient and consistent with the treatment.

Hair Care Tips After Microneedling

After microneedling for hair loss, it is essential to take good care of your hair to ensure optimal results. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after microneedling
  • Avoid using hair products containing harsh chemicals
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood flow and stimulate hair growth
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause damage

By following these tips and instructions on how to do microneedling for hair loss, you can help stimulate hair growth and combat hair loss effectively. Remember to be patient and consistent with the treatment to achieve optimal results.
