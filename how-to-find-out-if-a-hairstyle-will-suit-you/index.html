How To Find Out If A Hairstyle Will Suit You

Written by Robby Jul 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking of getting a new hairstyle but not sure if it will look good on you? Finding the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, but with a little research and some expert advice, you can find the perfect look that suits your face shape and personality. Here are some tips on how to find out if a hairstyle will suit you.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. The four main face shapes are oval, round, square, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. Look for the widest point of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face.

If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off almost any hairstyle. Round faces look best with hairstyles that add height and length. Square faces look great with hairstyles that soften the angles of the face. Heart-shaped faces look best with hairstyles that add width at the chin.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Do you have straight, wavy, curly, or kinky hair? Is your hair thick or thin? These factors can affect how a hairstyle looks on you.

Straight hair looks best with hairstyles that add volume and texture. Wavy hair looks great with hairstyles that enhance the natural texture. Curly hair looks best with hairstyles that accentuate the curls. Kinky hair looks great with hairstyles that showcase the natural texture.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Before you head to the salon, look for inspiration for your new hairstyle. Browse through magazines, Pinterest, and Instagram for ideas. Look for hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair type. Save pictures of the styles you like and bring them to your hairstylist.

Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist

The best way to find out if a hairstyle will suit you is to consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can assess your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle to recommend the best hairstyle for you. They can also make suggestions for color and length that will complement your features.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Consider the amount of time you are willing to spend styling your hair
  • Choose a style that matches your personality
  • Don't be afraid to try something new
  • Keep in mind your hair's natural texture and color
  • Consider your lifestyle and how your hairstyle will fit into it

FAQs

Q: What should I do if I'm not happy with my new hairstyle?

A: If you're not happy with your new hairstyle, talk to your hairstylist. They may be able to make adjustments or suggest a different style that better suits you.

Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle?

A: Short hairstyles can look great on almost anyone, but it's important to consider your face shape and hair type before making the cut.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type and avoid over-styling with heat tools. Schedule regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, finding the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can find the perfect look that suits you. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
