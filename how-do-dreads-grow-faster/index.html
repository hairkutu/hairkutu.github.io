How Do Dreads Grow Faster Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby May 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're wondering how to make your dreadlocks grow faster, you're not alone. Many people with dreadlocks are looking for ways to speed up the process. There are several things you can do to encourage faster hair growth and help your dreadlocks grow longer and stronger. In this article, we'll explore some of the best tips and tricks for growing your dreads faster.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow Dreads Faster
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Dreads Faster
  • Tips for Growing Dreads Faster
  • FAQs About Growing Dreads Faster

How to Grow Dreads Faster

The first thing you need to understand about growing dreads faster is that it's a gradual process. You won't see results overnight, but if you follow these tips consistently, you'll notice a difference in the length and thickness of your hair over time.

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

One of the most important things you can do to promote hair growth is to eat a healthy, balanced diet. Make sure you're getting plenty of vitamins and minerals, especially those that are known to support healthy hair growth, such as vitamin B, vitamin C, and biotin. Incorporate foods like spinach, sweet potatoes, avocados, and nuts into your diet.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water is essential for healthy hair growth. When your body is dehydrated, your hair can become dry and brittle, which can lead to breakage and slow growth. Aim to drink at least eight glasses of water a day.

3. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help increase blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Dreads Faster

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before you start the dreadlock process, make sure your hair is clean and completely dry. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue from your hair.

2. Choose Your Method

There are several methods for creating dreadlocks, including backcombing, twisting, and using a dread perm. Choose the method that works best for your hair type and lifestyle.

3. Be Patient

As we mentioned earlier, growing dreadlocks is a gradual process. Don't expect to see results overnight. It can take several months or even years for your hair to fully mature into dreadlocks.

Tips for Growing Dreads Faster

1. Use Natural Oils

Using natural oils like coconut oil, jojoba oil, and castor oil can help moisturize your hair and scalp, which can promote healthy hair growth. Apply a small amount of oil to your hair and scalp regularly.

2. Protect Your Hair

Avoid using heat styling tools and harsh chemicals on your hair, as these can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. Wear a hat or headwrap to protect your hair from the sun and wind.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down the growth of your dreadlocks. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

FAQs About Growing Dreads Faster

1. Can I use hair growth supplements to speed up the process?

While hair growth supplements may help promote healthy hair growth, they are not a magic solution for growing dreadlocks faster. The best way to promote hair growth is to eat a healthy diet and take care of your hair.

2. Will washing my hair too often slow down the growth process?

No, washing your hair regularly will not slow down the growth process. In fact, keeping your hair and scalp clean is essential for healthy hair growth.

3. Can I use hair extensions to make my dreadlocks look longer?

Yes, you can use hair extensions to add length to your dreadlocks. However, keep in mind that extensions can be heavy and may pull on your natural hair, which can cause breakage and slow down the growth process.

Hair Care Tips for Growing Dreads Faster

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

While it's important to keep your hair and scalp clean, overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week.

2. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent hair breakage and tangles, which can promote healthy hair growth. Silk is also gentle on your hair and can help prevent frizz.

3. Protect Your Hair While Swimming

Chlorine and saltwater can be harsh on your hair and cause damage and breakage. Wear a swim cap or apply a leave-in conditioner before swimming to protect your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help your dreadlocks grow faster and healthier. Remember to be patient and consistent, and your hair will thank you.
