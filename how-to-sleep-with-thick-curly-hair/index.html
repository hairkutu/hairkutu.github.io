How To Sleep With Thick Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Apr 07, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Do you have thick curly hair that always seems to get tangled and frizzy when you wake up in the morning? Sleeping with curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques, you can wake up with beautiful, defined curls. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to sleep with thick curly hair.

Table of Content

  • How to Sleep with Thick Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Sleeping with Thick Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

How to Sleep with Thick Curly Hair

The first step to sleeping with thick curly hair is to prepare your hair before bed. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently comb through your hair. This will remove any knots or tangles and make it easier to style in the morning.

2. Apply Leave-in Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to your hair to keep it moisturized throughout the night. This will also help to prevent frizz and breakage.

3. Style Your Hair

Style your hair in a way that will preserve your curls overnight. You can try a pineapple, a braid, or a satin bonnet. We will discuss these styles in more detail in the next section.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to sleep with thick curly hair:

1. Pineapple

To do the pineapple, gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie or a hair tie. This will create a loose ponytail that resembles a pineapple. Make sure that the scrunchie is not too tight, as this can cause dents in your curls. Cover your hair with a satin bonnet or a silk scarf to protect it while you sleep.

2. Braid

To do a braid, divide your hair into sections and braid each section. You can do one big braid or multiple braids, depending on your hair length and thickness. Make sure to use a hair tie or a scrunchie to secure the end of each braid. Cover your hair with a satin bonnet or a silk scarf to protect it while you sleep.

3. Satin Bonnet

A satin bonnet is a great way to protect your curls while you sleep. Simply place the bonnet over your hair and adjust it to fit comfortably. Make sure that the bonnet is made of satin or silk, as these materials will not cause friction or static.

4. Silk Scarf

You can also use a silk scarf to protect your curls while you sleep. Simply wrap the scarf around your head and tie it in a knot or a bow. Make sure that the scarf is made of silk or satin, as these materials will not cause friction or static.

Tips for Sleeping with Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for sleeping with thick curly hair:

1. Use Satin or Silk Sheets

Sleeping on satin or silk sheets can help to prevent frizz and breakage. These materials are smooth and gentle on your hair, unlike cotton or polyester.

2. Avoid Cotton Pillowcases

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and tangles in your hair. If you cannot switch to satin or silk sheets, at least switch to a satin or silk pillowcase. This will help to protect your curls while you sleep.

3. Sleep on Your Back

Sleeping on your back can help to prevent your hair from getting tangled or flattened. If you tend to move around in your sleep, try to secure your hair in a pineapple or a braid to keep it in place.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and your lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you exercise or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Q: Can I sleep with wet curly hair?

A: It is not recommended to sleep with wet hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you go to bed.

Q: How can I refresh my curls in the morning?

A: You can refresh your curls in the morning by spritzing them with water or a leave-in conditioner. You can also use a diffuser or a hair dryer on a low heat setting to revive your curls.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick curly hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to comb through your hair. This will prevent breakage and tangles.

2. Use a Leave-in Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to your hair to keep it moisturized and prevent frizz.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you must use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can sleep with thick curly hair and wake up with beautiful, defined curls. Remember to always take care of your hair and protect it while you sleep.
