Can You Keep Your Hair During Chemo

Written by Frank Jun 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Going through chemotherapy can be a daunting experience, and one of the most common concerns for patients is hair loss. Losing your hair during chemo can be particularly distressing, as it is a visible sign of your illness. However, the good news is that there are ways to minimize hair loss during chemo, and in some cases, it is possible to keep your hair. In this blog post, we will explore the various options and tips for keeping your hair during chemo.

Table of Contents

  • How-To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How-To

The first step in keeping your hair during chemo is to talk to your doctor. They will be able to advise you on the type of chemotherapy you will be receiving and the likelihood of hair loss. They may also be able to recommend treatments that can help minimize hair loss.

One of the most effective ways to keep your hair during chemo is to use a cold cap. A cold cap is a tight-fitting cap that is worn during chemotherapy sessions. It cools the scalp, which reduces blood flow to the hair follicles and helps to protect them from the effects of chemotherapy.

Step-by-Step

If you decide to use a cold cap, here is a step-by-step guide to using it:

  1. Wash your hair the day before your chemo session, using a gentle shampoo.
  2. Put on the cold cap before your chemotherapy begins. It should be tight-fitting but not uncomfortable.
  3. Wear the cold cap throughout your chemotherapy session and for a few hours afterward.
  4. Repeat the process for each chemo session.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair during chemo:

  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free from harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as hair dryers and flat irons, as these can cause damage to your hair.
  • Avoid coloring or perming your hair during chemo, as this can cause additional damage.

FAQ

Q: Will I definitely lose my hair during chemo?

A: Not everyone loses their hair during chemo. The likelihood of hair loss depends on the type of chemotherapy you are receiving.

Q: Is using a cold cap uncomfortable?

A: The cold cap can be uncomfortable for some people, but it is generally well-tolerated. Most people find that the discomfort is outweighed by the benefit of keeping their hair.

Q: How long will I need to use the cold cap?

A: You will need to wear the cold cap throughout your chemo session and for a few hours afterward.

Hair Care

If you do experience hair loss during chemo, it is important to take care of your scalp and any remaining hair. Here are some tips for hair care during chemo:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos or conditioners that can further irritate your scalp.
  • Gently wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools or chemical treatments on your hair.
  • Wear a soft cap or scarf to protect your scalp from the sun and cold weather.

In conclusion, it is possible to keep your hair during chemo with the help of a cold cap and other hair care measures. Talk to your doctor about your options and take care of your hair and scalp during this challenging time.
