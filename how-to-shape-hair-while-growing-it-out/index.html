How To Shape Hair While Growing It Out Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Aug 12, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of the awkward stage of growing out your hair? Do you want to know how to shape your hair while it grows to avoid looking like a hot mess? Well, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll provide you with tips and tricks on how to shape your hair while growing it out, so you can look fabulous at every stage of the process.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Shape Hair While Growing It Out
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Growing Hair

How to Shape Hair While Growing It Out

When growing out your hair, it’s essential to keep it looking neat and tidy, so it doesn’t look like you’ve given up on your appearance. Here’s how to shape your hair while it grows:

  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain a healthy look.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands and hair elastics to keep hair away from your face.
  • Style your hair in different ways to keep it fun and interesting.
  • Use hair products like mousse, hairspray, and gel to keep hair in place.

By following these simple steps, you can shape your hair and make it look great while it grows.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to shape your hair while growing it out:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt and oil.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.
  3. Apply a styling product like mousse or gel to your hair to give it some hold.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls for a different look.
  6. Finish with hairspray or another styling product to keep your hair in place.

Repeat these steps every time you wash your hair, and you’ll have a perfectly shaped hairdo while it grows out.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you shape your hair while growing it out:

  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use hair accessories like clips, pins, and headbands to keep hair out of your face.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on how to style your growing hair.
  • Be patient and don’t get discouraged if your hair takes longer to grow than expected.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking fabulous at every stage of the growing process.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaping hair while growing it out:

How often should I get a trim while growing out my hair?

It’s recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

What hairstyles are best for growing out hair?

Hairstyles that can be easily pinned up, like buns and braids, are great for growing out hair because they keep it off your face and neck.

What can I do to make my hair grow faster?

Eating a healthy diet, taking vitamins, and avoiding heat styling can help your hair grow faster.

Hair Care for Growing Hair

When growing out your hair, it’s essential to take care of it to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some hair care tips for growing hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great while it grows out.

Conclusion

Growing out your hair can be a time-consuming process, but with the right tips and tricks, you can shape your hair and make it look great at every stage. By following our step-by-step guide and hair care tips, you’ll have healthy, beautiful hair that’s ready for any style or occasion.
