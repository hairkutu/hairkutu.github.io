Short Hair Styles For Round Faces 2020 The Ultimate Guide

If you have a round face, you might feel like it limits your hairstyle options. However, there are plenty of short hair styles for round faces that can flatter your features and make you feel confident and stylish. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best short hair styles for round faces in 2020, as well as tips for caring for and styling your hair.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

How to Choose the Best Short Hair Style for Your Round Face

When it comes to choosing a short hair style for a round face, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Look for styles that add height and volume to the crown of your head, which can elongate your face and balance out its roundness.
  • Avoid styles that fall flat against your face, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider haircuts that incorporate layers or angles, which can create flattering lines and movement.
  • Think about your hair texture and thickness, as certain styles may work better for different hair types.

Some of the best short hair styles for round faces in 2020 include:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hair style that can work well for round faces. To make it more flattering, consider adding volume at the crown and keeping the sides shorter. This can create a more angular look that balances out the roundness of your face.

Bob Cut

A bob cut can also be a great option for round faces, as long as it incorporates layers or angles. Consider a chin-length bob with choppy layers, which can create movement and texture while elongating your face.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut can add visual interest and create flattering lines for a round face. Consider a shorter cut on one side and longer layers on the other, or an angled bob with longer pieces in the front.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Once you've chosen a short hair style that works well for your round face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. If your hair is naturally straight, consider using a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and movement.
  3. If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, consider using a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.
  4. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the crown and roots to create volume.
  5. If desired, use a straightening or curling iron to create additional texture or waves.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair Styles for Round Faces

To keep your short hair style looking its best, consider these tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your style.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling, as this can damage your hair and make it look dry and frizzy.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.

FAQs About Short Hair Styles for Round Faces

Here are some commonly asked questions about short hair styles for round faces:

Can I wear bangs with a short hair style for a round face?

Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Consider side-swept bangs or longer, wispy bangs that can create a more angular look.

What if I have thin hair?

Consider a shorter cut with layers, which can create volume and movement. You can also use a volumizing spray or mousse to add fullness to your hair.

Can I still wear a ponytail or updo with a short hair style?

Yes, but you may need to use bobby pins or other styling tools to keep your hair in place. Consider a half-up, half-down style or a messy bun for a relaxed, casual look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair Styles for Round Faces

When it comes to caring for your short hair style, consider these tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and short hair styles for round faces in 2020, you can feel confident and stylish no matter what your face shape is. Experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you!
