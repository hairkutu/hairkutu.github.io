How To Do An Undercut On Long Hair

Written by Frank May 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, an undercut on long hair might be just what you need. This style involves shaving or trimming the hair on the sides or back of your head, while leaving the top long and flowing. In this article, we'll go over the steps to achieve this look, along with some tips and tricks for maintaining it.

Table of Contents

  • What is an Undercut?
  • How to do an Undercut on Long Hair

What is an Undercut?

An undercut is a hairstyle that involves shaving or trimming the hair on the sides or back of your head, while leaving the top long and flowing. This style has been popular for both men and women since the 1920s, and it has recently made a comeback in the fashion world.

How to do an Undercut on Long Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, comb, and hair ties or clips to hold the hair in place. It's also a good idea to have a mirror or someone to help you with the back of your head.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to achieve an undercut on long hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it.
  2. Section off the top of your hair and tie it up or clip it out of the way.
  3. Choose the length you want for the undercut and use the clippers to shave the sides and back of your head.
  4. Blend the shaved area with the longer hair on top using scissors or clippers.
  5. Release the top section of your hair and style as desired.

Tips for Maintaining an Undercut

Here are some tips for maintaining your undercut:

  • Get regular trims to keep the undercut looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercuts:

  • Can I still tie my hair up with an undercut?
  • Yes, you can still tie your hair up even with an undercut. Just make sure to leave enough hair on top to tie it up.
  • Do I need to use hair products to style my undercut?
  • It's up to you! Some people prefer to use hair products to add texture and hold to their undercut, while others prefer a more natural look.

Hair Care for an Undercut

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair with an undercut:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these steps and tips, you'll be able to achieve an undercut on long hair and maintain it with ease. Whether you're going for a bold and edgy look or just want to switch things up, an undercut is a great way to do it. So go ahead and give it a try!
