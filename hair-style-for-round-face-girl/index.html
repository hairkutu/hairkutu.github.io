Best Hair Styles For Round Face Girls

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Having a round face can be both a blessing and a curse when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. While many styles can accentuate your features, others can make your face look even rounder. In this article, we’ll go over some of the best hair styles for round face girls, tips for maintaining your hair’s health, and answer some frequently asked questions about hair care.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. Here’s how:

Step 1:

Pull your hair back away from your face and stand in front of a mirror.

Step 2:

Using a washable marker or lipstick, trace the outline of your face onto the mirror.

Step 3:

Step back and look at the shape you’ve drawn. If the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all relatively similar, you likely have a round face shape.

Best Hair Styles for Round Face Girls

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to find the perfect hairstyle to accentuate your features. Here are some of the best hair styles for round face girls:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Opt for layers that start at your chin or below to create a slimming effect.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can help create angles on a round face shape. Make sure they start at the arch of your eyebrow and sweep to the side to create a slimming effect.

3. Bob Hair Cut

A bob hair cut is a great option for round face girls. Opt for a longer bob that falls just above your shoulders, and add layers around the face to create angles.

4. Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob can help add dimension to a round face shape. Opt for a bob that’s shorter in the back and longer in the front to create a slimming effect.

5. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can help elongate a round face shape. Opt for a cut that’s longer on top and shorter on the sides to create angles.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair’s Health

Now that you’ve found the perfect hair style for your round face, it’s important to maintain your hair’s health. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Before using any hot tools on your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Try using a hair mask once a week to keep your locks nourished.

3. Trim Regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

Q: What hair color looks best on round face girls?

A: While it ultimately comes down to personal preference, warm hair colors such as honey blonde and copper can help create a slimming effect on round face shapes.

Q: Can short hair look good on a round face?

A: Yes! Short hair can look great on a round face. Just make sure to add layers and angles to create dimension.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It ultimately depends on your hair type and texture. Those with oily hair should wash their hair every other day, while those with dry hair can wash once or twice a week.

Conclusion

There are many hair styles that can accentuate the features of a round face shape. From long layers to asymmetrical bobs, finding the perfect style for you is all about experimenting and finding what you feel most confident in. Remember to take care of your hair’s health with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments, and don’t be afraid to try something new!
