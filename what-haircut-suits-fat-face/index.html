What Haircut Suits Fat Face A Comprehensive Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Aug 04, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round or "fat" face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty, but you also want something that is easy to maintain and fits your lifestyle. In this guide, we will explore the different haircuts that suit a fat face and offer tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking its best.

  2. How to Choose the Right Haircut
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

  6. Hair Care Tips for a Fat Face

Description

A fat face is one that is round or chubby. This face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. When choosing a haircut for a fat face, you want to create the illusion of a more oval or elongated face shape. This can be achieved by adding height and volume to the crown of the head, creating layers around the face, and avoiding styles that emphasize the width of the face.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

There are several factors to consider when choosing a haircut for a fat face. These include your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Consider your face shape. A fat face benefits from hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of the head and create vertical lines to elongate the face.
  • Choose a style that flatters your features. If you have a strong jawline, a chin-length bob can be very flattering. If you have a high forehead, consider bangs to balance out your features.
  • Think about your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a shorter cut can add volume and make your hair look thicker. If you have thick hair, consider a layered cut to lighten the weight of your hair.
  • Consider your personal style. Your haircut should reflect your personality and lifestyle. If you are low maintenance, choose a style that is easy to maintain and doesn't require a lot of styling.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to choosing a haircut for a fat face:

  1. Start by determining your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking at your face in the mirror.
  2. Research different hairstyles that flatter your face shape. Look for styles that add height and volume to the crown of the head and create vertical lines to elongate the face.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional stylist can help you choose a style that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.
  4. Consider your lifestyle. Choose a style that is easy to maintain and fits your lifestyle.
  5. Experiment with different styles. Don't be afraid to try something new and see how it looks on you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have chosen a haircut for your fat face, it is important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that emphasize the width of your face. Instead, choose styles that add height and volume to the crown of the head.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and minimize breakage.
  • Consider investing in a quality hair dryer and styling tools to make styling your hair easier.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for fat faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a fat face?

Avoid hairstyles that emphasize the width of your face. This includes blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and straight, blunt cuts.

What hairstyles are best for a fat face?

The best hairstyles for a fat face add height and volume to the crown of the head and create vertical lines to elongate the face. This includes layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and graduated bobs.

Can I wear my hair long if I have a fat face?

Yes, you can wear your hair long if you have a fat face. However, it is important to add layers and volume to the crown of the head to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face.

Hair Care Tips for a Fat Face

In addition to choosing the right haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for a fat face:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel comfortable and reflects your personal style.
