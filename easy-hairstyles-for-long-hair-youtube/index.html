Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair Youtube

Written by Eveline Apr 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are looking for some easy hairstyles for long hair, you have come to the right place. YouTube is an excellent source for hairstyle tutorials that can help you create a variety of looks. In this article, we will show you some of the best hairstyles for long hair that you can find on YouTube, along with tips and tricks for achieving them.

Description

Long hair is beautiful and versatile, but it can also be challenging to style. However, with the help of YouTube tutorials, you can learn how to create easy and stunning hairstyles that will suit any occasion. Whether you are looking for a simple everyday look or a fancy updo for a special event, there is a hairstyle tutorial on YouTube that can guide you through the process.

How to

Creating easy hairstyles for long hair is all about finding the right tutorials and following the steps carefully. Here are some tips for getting the most out of YouTube hairstyle tutorials:

  • Choose a tutorial that matches your hair length and texture.
  • Watch the tutorial all the way through before you start styling.
  • Have all the necessary tools and products ready before you begin.
  • Take your time and follow the steps carefully.
  • Practice the hairstyle a few times to perfect it.

Step by Step

Here are some easy hairstyles for long hair that you can find on YouTube:

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for casual occasions. Here's how to create it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a cute and easy way to style your long hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it into a half-up ponytail.
  2. Tease the ponytail to create volume.
  3. Wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to hide it.
  4. Let the rest of your hair fall down.
  5. Curl the loose hair to create soft waves.
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. French Braid

The French braid is a classic and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for formal occasions. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and divide it into three even sections.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Add a small section of hair from the right side to the right section.
  6. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  7. Add a small section of hair from the left side to the left section.
  8. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  9. Repeat steps 5-8 until you reach the nape of your neck.
  10. Finish the braid with a regular three-strand braid and secure it with an elastic band.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for creating easy hairstyles for long hair:

  • Use hair accessories to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different braids, twists, and knots to create unique looks.
  • Don't be afraid to ask a friend or family member for help if you are having trouble with a hairstyle.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for long hair:

Q: Are there any easy hairstyles for long hair that don't require heat?

A: Yes, there are many heatless hairstyles that you can create using braids, twists, and buns. Check out YouTube for tutorials on heatless hairstyles.

Q: How can I make my long hair look thicker?

A: You can use volumizing products, such as mousse or dry shampoo, to add volume to your hair. You can also try teasing your hair at the roots or using hair extensions to create a fuller look.

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily. However, if you exercise frequently or have an oily scalp, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your long hair healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for taking care of your long hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these easy hairstyles for long hair YouTube tutorials and tips, you can create a variety of stunning looks that will suit any occasion. Whether you prefer a casual messy bun or an elegant French braid, there is a hairstyle tutorial on YouTube that can help you achieve the look you want.
