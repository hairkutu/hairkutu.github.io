What Is The Best Hairstyle For Over 60 With Glasses

Written by Aprilia May 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair texture and thickness change, and with the addition of glasses, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. In this blog post, we will discuss what is the best hairstyle for over 60 with glasses, along with how to achieve it, step by step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Best Hairstyle for Over 60 with Glasses
  • Step by Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining the Best Hairstyle for Over 60 with Glasses
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Advice for Over 60 with Glasses

Description

The best hairstyle for over 60 with glasses is one that flatters your face shape, complements your glasses, and is easy to maintain. Short hairstyles are the most popular choice for women over 60 with glasses, as they are low maintenance and can be styled in various ways. However, if you prefer long hair, there are still plenty of options to choose from.

How to Achieve the Best Hairstyle for Over 60 with Glasses

The first step in achieving the best hairstyle for over 60 with glasses is to consult with your hairstylist. They can help you choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and glasses. Your hairstylist can also advise you on how to maintain your hair at home and recommend products that will help you achieve your desired look.

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, a short, layered bob can help elongate your face. If you have an oval face, a long bob with layers can add volume to your hair. If you have a square face, a side-swept fringe can soften your features.

Complement Your Glasses

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your glasses. If you have thick frames, a short, layered hairstyle can help balance out your face. If you have thin frames, a longer hairstyle can add volume to your hair. It's also essential to consider the color of your glasses when choosing a hairstyle. If you have colorful frames, a simple, elegant hairstyle can help bring attention to your glasses.

Step by Step Instructions

To achieve the best hairstyle for over 60 with glasses, follow these simple steps:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and glasses.
  2. Maintain your hair at home by washing and conditioning regularly and using products recommended by your hairstylist.
  3. Style your hair daily using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron, depending on your desired look.
  4. Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Tips for Maintaining the Best Hairstyle for Over 60 with Glasses

Here are some tips to help you maintain the best hairstyle for over 60 with glasses:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly using products recommended by your hairstylist.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Try to air dry your hair when possible to avoid heat damage.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most popular hairstyle for women over 60 with glasses?

The most popular hairstyle for women over 60 with glasses is a short, layered bob.

Can women over 60 with glasses wear long hair?

Yes, women over 60 with glasses can wear long hair. However, it's essential to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and glasses.

What products should I use to maintain my hair?

You should use products recommended by your hairstylist, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products.

Hair Care Advice for Over 60 with Glasses

Here are some general hair care tips for women over 60 with glasses:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly using products recommended by your hairstylist.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Try to air dry your hair when possible to avoid heat damage.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for over 60 with glasses is one that flatters your face shape, complements your glasses, and is easy to maintain. Consult with your hairstylist to choose a hairstyle that suits your needs, and follow these simple tips to keep your hair looking its best.
