How To Flatten Thick Hair For Wig

Written by Oliver Aug 06, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of getting a wig that doesn't sit well on your head because your hair underneath is too thick? You are not alone. This is a common problem faced by many people. The good news is that there are ways to flatten thick hair for wig. In this blog post, we will be discussing how to do just that. Let’s get started.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Flatten Thick Hair for Wig
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the process of flattening thick hair for wig, let’s first understand what it means. Thick hair is hair that has a lot of volume and is difficult to manage. When you wear a wig, the thickness of your hair can be a problem because it can make the wig look bulky and unnatural. By flattening your hair, you can create a smooth base for your wig to sit on. This will make your wig look more natural and comfortable to wear.

How to Flatten Thick Hair for Wig

Flattening thick hair for wig requires some preparation. Here are the things you will need:

  • Flat iron
  • Hair clips
  • Hair tie
  • Hair spray

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to flatten thick hair for wig:

  1. Wash your hair and blow-dry it until it's completely dry.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using the hair clips.
  3. Take one section of hair and tie the rest of your hair away from it.
  4. Using your flat iron, straighten the section of hair from the roots to the ends.
  5. Repeat the process with the other sections of your hair.
  6. Once you have straightened all the sections, use a hair tie to tie your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  7. Apply some hair spray to keep your hair in place.
  8. Put on your wig and adjust it to your head.
  9. Style your wig as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you flatten your thick hair for wig:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair from the flat iron.
  • Don't straighten your hair too often as it can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a high-quality wig that fits well on your head.
  • Don't use too much hair spray as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about flattening thick hair for wig:

Q. Can I use a hair straightener instead of a flat iron?

A. Yes, you can use a hair straightener to flatten your hair for wig. However, a flat iron is more effective as it has wider plates that can straighten more hair at once.

Q. How long will my hair stay flat?

A. Your hair will stay flat until you wash it again. However, it may start to lose its flatness after a few hours, especially if you have thick hair.

Q. Can I use this method on curly hair?

A. Yes, you can use this method on curly hair. However, it may take longer to straighten curly hair than straight hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you are flattening it for wig. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can cause build-up and damage to your hair.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Flattening thick hair for wig may seem like a daunting task, but it's actually quite simple. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a smooth base for your wig to sit on. Follow the steps in this guide, and you'll be on your way to a natural-looking wig in no time.
