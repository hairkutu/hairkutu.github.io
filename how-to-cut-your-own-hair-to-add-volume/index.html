How To Cut Your Own Hair To Add Volume

Written by Petter Jul 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for an easy way to add volume to your hair, cutting it yourself might just be the solution you're looking for. Not only is it a cost-effective option, but it also allows you to have complete control over the length and style of your hair. In this article, we'll be sharing a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair to add volume, along with some tips and tricks to ensure a successful outcome.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools, such as sharp scissors, hair clips, and a comb. It's also recommended to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair tends to be longer and can result in uneven cuts.

Step 1: Divide Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you don't miss any areas.

Step 2: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair, hold it between your fingers, and cut off the desired length. It's important to cut small sections at a time to avoid any mistakes.

Step 3: Move to the Next Section

Once you've finished cutting one section, move on to the next one and repeat the process. Make sure to check for any uneven areas and adjust accordingly.

Step 4: Finish the Back

Cutting the back of your hair can be tricky, but it's not impossible. Use a mirror to see the back of your head and take small sections of hair to cut. It's better to cut less hair than more, as it's easier to fix any mistakes.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to ensure a successful haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends
  • Cut small sections at a time
  • Take breaks if you feel tired
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Use a comb to ensure an even cut

FAQ

Can I cut my hair when it's wet?

It's not recommended to cut your hair when it's wet, as wet hair tends to be longer and can result in uneven cuts.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair growth.

Can I add layers to my hair when cutting it myself?

Yes, you can add layers to your hair when cutting it yourself. However, it's important to start with small sections and check for any uneven areas before moving on to the next section.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain healthy hair growth. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Use a hair mask once a week
  • Get regular trims
  • Avoid brushing wet hair

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair stays healthy and voluminous after cutting it yourself.

Conclusion

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a voluminous and healthy look. Remember to take your time, cut small sections at a time, and check for any uneven areas before moving on to the next section. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut your own hair like a pro in no time.
