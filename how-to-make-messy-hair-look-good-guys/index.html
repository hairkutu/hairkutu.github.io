How To Make Messy Hair Look Good Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Messy hair is a trend that never goes out of style. Not only does it give a relaxed and effortless look, but it also adds a bit of edge to your appearance. However, making messy hair look good can be a tricky task, especially for guys. In this guide, we will show you how to achieve the perfect messy hairstyle that looks great and suits your personality.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Messy Hair Look Good Guys
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Messy Hair

How to Make Messy Hair Look Good Guys

The first step to making your messy hair look good is finding the right style that suits your face shape and hair type. The key to achieving a great messy hairstyle is to have a cut that allows you to have some length and texture in your hair.

You can go for a layered haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. Another option is to have a fade cut that gives you a clean and sharp look at the back and sides while keeping the top longer.

To style your messy hair, you can use a pomade, a wax, or a clay that provides a strong hold and adds texture to your hair. Start by applying a small amount of the product on your hand and rub it between your palms. Then, apply it to your hair, starting from the roots and moving towards the ends.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to making your messy hair look good:

  1. Start with clean and towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax on your hand and rub it between your palms.
  3. Apply the product to your hair, starting from the roots and moving towards the ends.
  4. Use your fingers to create texture and volume in your hair. Scrunch and twist your hair to create a messy look.
  5. If you want to add more volume, use a blow dryer on low heat while scrunching your hair with your fingers.
  6. Finish your look with a hairspray that provides a strong hold.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your messy hair:

  • Don't wash your hair too often as it can strip your hair of natural oils that keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it in shape and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair and make it look dry and frizzy.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve a messy hairstyle with short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve a messy hairstyle with short hair. Just use a strong-hold product and create texture and volume with your fingers.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out regularly, you may need to wash your hair more often. However, it's best to avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of natural oils.

Hair Care for Messy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your messy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and helps to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can dry out your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair and prevent damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach and hair dyes that can damage your hair and make it look dry and frizzy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollutants by wearing a hat or using a hair serum that contains UV protection.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a great messy hairstyle that looks good and suits your personality. Remember to find the right style that suits your face shape and hair type and use the right products to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
