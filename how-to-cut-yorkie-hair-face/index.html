How To Cut Yorkie Hair Face Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Yorkies are lovely and adorable pets that require proper grooming to keep them healthy and happy. Among the essential grooming activities, cutting the hair on the face is crucial to maintain their charming appearance. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut Yorkie hair face, including tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Yorkie Hair Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Yorkie Hair Face
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Yorkie Hair Care

How to Cut Yorkie Hair Face

Cutting Yorkie hair face requires a specific set of tools and techniques to achieve the desired results. Before you start cutting, ensure that you have the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Thinning scissors
  • Clipper
  • Comb and brush
  • Styptic powder (in case of bleeding)

Once you have the tools, follow the steps below:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Brush and comb your Yorkie's hair to remove any tangles and mats.
  2. Use the clipper to trim the hair around the eyes, nose, and ears. Be careful not to hurt your dog.
  3. Use the scissors to trim the hair around the face, including the cheeks and chin. Use the thinning scissors to blend the hair and create a natural look. Cut the hair slowly and in small sections.
  4. Trim the hair on the top of the head, leaving it slightly longer than the hair on the sides and back of the head.
  5. Check for any uneven hair and trim it accordingly.
  6. Clean up the hair around the face and ears, and wipe any loose hair with a damp cloth.

Tips for Cutting Yorkie Hair Face

Cutting your Yorkie's hair face requires patience, attention to detail, and practice. Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with clean and dry hair, as wet hair can be challenging to cut and style.
  • Use sharp scissors and thinning scissors for a precise cut.
  • Take breaks if needed to avoid stressing your dog.
  • Use treats and positive reinforcement to make the grooming experience enjoyable for your pet.
  • Ask for help from a professional groomer if you are not confident in your skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions that pet owners ask about cutting Yorkie hair face:

How often should I cut my Yorkie's hair face?

You should cut your Yorkie's hair face every four to six weeks to maintain a healthy and clean appearance.

Can I use human scissors to cut my Yorkie's hair face?

No, you should avoid using human scissors as they are not designed for pet grooming and can cause injury or uneven cuts. Use sharp and high-quality pet scissors instead.

What should I do if my Yorkie's hair face is bleeding?

If you accidentally cut your dog's skin and cause bleeding, apply styptic powder to the affected area to stop the bleeding. If the bleeding persists, seek veterinary care immediately.

Yorkie Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your Yorkie healthy and happy. Here are some tips to maintain your dog's hair:

  • Brush and comb your dog's hair regularly to remove tangles and mats.
  • Bathe your dog every four to six weeks using a mild shampoo designed for pets.
  • Trim your dog's hair every four to six weeks to maintain a clean and healthy appearance.
  • Check your dog's ears and eyes regularly and clean them if necessary.
  • Visit a professional groomer every three months to ensure your dog's hair is in good condition.

By following these tips and techniques, you can cut your Yorkie hair face like a pro and maintain your dog's charming appearance. Remember to be patient and gentle with your pet and seek professional help if needed.
