How To Stop Humidity From Frizzing Hair Tips Faq And Hair Care

Written by Oliver Mar 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Humidity can be the worst enemy of those who have naturally curly or wavy hair. It can cause hair to frizz, making it difficult to manage and style. If you’re tired of dealing with frizzy hair caused by humidity, read on to learn some tips and tricks to help you prevent it.

  • How to Stop Humidity from Frizzing Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips to Prevent Frizz
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

How to Stop Humidity from Frizzing Hair

The first step to preventing frizz caused by humidity is to understand what causes it. When the air is humid, it contains a lot of moisture. This moisture is absorbed by the hair, causing the hair shaft to expand. As a result, the cuticles on the hair strands become raised, causing the hair to look frizzy.

To prevent frizz caused by humidity, you need to keep your hair moisturized. Moisturizing your hair helps to keep the cuticles on the hair strands smooth, preventing them from becoming raised and causing frizz. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair moisturized:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start with a good shampoo and conditioner that is designed to moisturize your hair. Look for products that contain natural oils, such as coconut oil or argan oil, that can help to hydrate your hair.

2. Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help lock in moisture. Look for a product that is lightweight and won’t weigh down your hair.

3. Use a Hair Oil

Apply a hair oil to the ends of your hair to help seal in moisture. Argan oil or jojoba oil are good options that can help to tame frizz and add shine to your hair.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to prevent frizz caused by humidity:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help lock in moisture.

Step 3: Apply Hair Oil

Apply a hair oil to the ends of your hair to help seal in moisture.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as usual, but avoid using a lot of heat. Heat can cause hair to become dry, which can lead to frizz.

Step 5: Use a Humidity-Resistant Hairspray

Finish off with a humidity-resistant hairspray to help keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.

Tips to Prevent Frizz

Here are some additional tips to help prevent frizz caused by humidity:

1. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more prone to frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day.

2. Use a Microfiber Towel

Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. Microfiber towels are gentler on the hair and can help to prevent frizz.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause hair to become dry and brittle, which can lead to frizz. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

4. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are gentler on the hair and can help to prevent frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can I prevent frizz without using any products?

A: Yes, you can prevent frizz by keeping your hair moisturized and avoiding heat styling. However, using moisturizing products can help to enhance the results.

Q: Can I use a regular towel to dry my hair?

A: While you can use a regular towel to dry your hair, it can be more prone to causing frizz. A microfiber towel is a gentler option that can help to prevent frizz.

Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help to prevent split ends, which can lead to frizz.

3. Avoid Brushing Your Hair When It’s Dry

Brushing your hair when it’s dry can cause it to become frizzy. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.

By following these tips and tricks, you can prevent frizz caused by humidity and keep your hair looking smooth and silky.
