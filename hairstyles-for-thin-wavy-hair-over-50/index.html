Hairstyles For Thin Wavy Hair Over 50 Tips And Ideas

Written by Luffy Sep 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thin wavy hair and you are over 50, you might be looking for some inspiration to switch up your hairstyle. While it's true that thin hair can be challenging to style, there are plenty of hairstyles out there that can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. In this article, we will share some tips and ideas for hairstyles for thin wavy hair over 50.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Thin Wavy Hair
  • Hairstyles for Thin Wavy Hair Over 50
  • Tips for Styling Thin Wavy Hair

How to Style Thin Wavy Hair

If you have thin wavy hair, it's important to choose the right products and styling techniques to make your hair look its best. Here are some tips:

  • Start with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Use a round brush to curl your hair and create waves.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Hairstyles for Thin Wavy Hair Over 50

Here are some hairstyles that work well for thin wavy hair over 50:

1. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that can work wonders for thin wavy hair. Opt for a chin-length or shorter bob that is cut with layers to add volume and movement to your hair.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and stylish choice for women with thin wavy hair. This short and layered hairstyle can make your hair look fuller and thicker.

3. Layered Lob

A layered lob is a longer version of a bob that falls just above the shoulders. This hairstyle is perfect for women who want to keep some length while adding movement and volume to their hair.

4. Shag

A shag is a layered and textured hairstyle that looks great on thin wavy hair. This hairstyle can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous, while also adding a touch of edginess to your look.

5. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can be a great addition to any of the above hairstyles. They can help frame your face and add some depth to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

Tips for Styling Thin Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling thin wavy hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Use a round brush to curl your hair and create waves.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thin wavy hair over 50?

The best haircut for thin wavy hair over 50 is one that is layered and textured. A bob, pixie cut, layered lob, or shag can all work well for thin wavy hair.

How can I make my thin wavy hair look fuller?

You can make your thin wavy hair look fuller by using volumizing products, blow drying your hair upside down, and using a round brush to create waves. You can also consider getting a haircut with layers to add volume and movement to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep your thin wavy hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for thin hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your thin wavy hair over 50. Whether you prefer a bob, pixie cut, layered lob, or shag, there are plenty of options out there that can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.
