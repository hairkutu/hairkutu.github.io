Hair Cut Shorter In Back Longer In Front A Guide To The Trendy Hairstyle

Written by Luffy Jul 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy hairstyle that will give you a chic and edgy look, then the hair cut shorter in back longer in front is the perfect style for you. This hairstyle is also known as an angled bob or graduated bob and has been popular for years. In this article, we will discuss how to get this hairstyle, tips for maintaining it, and answer frequently asked questions about it.

Description

The hair cut shorter in back longer in front is a hairstyle where the hair is cut at an angle, with the back of the hair being shorter than the front. This creates a graduated effect that is both stylish and modern. This hairstyle can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, making it a versatile option for many women.

How to Get the Hair Cut Shorter in Back Longer in Front

The first step in getting this hairstyle is to find a professional hairstylist who specializes in this cut. Once you have found a stylist, you should discuss the style you want and ask for their advice on how to customize the cut to suit your face shape and hair texture.

The stylist will start by sectioning off the hair and cutting the back of the hair shorter than the front. They will then gradually blend the hair to create a seamless transition from the shorter back to the longer front. Depending on your hair texture, the stylist may also add layers to create movement and texture in the hair.

Step by Step

  1. Find a professional hairstylist who specializes in this cut
  2. Discuss the style you want and ask for their advice on how to customize the cut
  3. Section off the hair and cut the back of the hair shorter than the front
  4. Gradually blend the hair to create a seamless transition from the shorter back to the longer front
  5. Add layers to create movement and texture in the hair

Tips for Maintaining the Hair Cut Shorter in Back Longer in Front

To maintain this hairstyle, you should visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks for a trim. This will help to keep the cut looking fresh and prevent split ends. You should also use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools, as this will help to prevent damage to your hair.

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you can enhance your hair's texture by using a curl-enhancing product. This will help to create defined curls or waves and add volume to your hair.

FAQ

1. Can anyone get the hair cut shorter in back longer in front?

Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, making it a versatile option for many women.

2. Will this hairstyle suit my face shape?

Your hairstylist can help you customize the cut to suit your face shape. Generally, this hairstyle suits oval, round, and heart-shaped faces.

3. Can I style my hair differently with this cut?

Yes, you can style your hair differently with this cut. You can straighten it, curl it, or create waves to change up your look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, you should use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. You should also avoid using heat tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage. If you do use heat tools, make sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using the highest heat setting.

You should also avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye. If you do want to color your hair, make sure to go to a professional hairstylist who can use high-quality products that won't damage your hair.

Conclusion

The hair cut shorter in back longer in front is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. With the help of a professional hairstylist, you can achieve this stylish and modern look. By following the tips for maintaining this hairstyle and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
