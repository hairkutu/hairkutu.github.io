Best Medium Length Hair For Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 18, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, but you also want something that is easy to maintain and style. That's where medium length hair comes in. Medium length hair is the perfect balance between short and long hair, making it a versatile option for those with round faces. In this article, we'll explore the best medium length hair for round faces, and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Best Hairstyles for Round Faces

When it comes to medium length hair for round faces, there are a few styles that work particularly well. These styles help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the roundness. Here are some of the best hairstyles for round faces:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for those with round faces. The layers help to create movement and dimension, while also drawing attention away from the roundness of the face. To style, use a round brush to blow dry your hair, and then use a curling iron to add loose waves.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for those with round faces. The bangs help to create a diagonal line across the face, which helps to elongate the face. To style, blow dry your hair with a round brush, and then use a flat iron to straighten your bangs.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular style for medium length hair, and they work particularly well for those with round faces. The waves help to create movement and texture, while also drawing attention away from the roundness of the face. To style, use a curling iron to create loose waves, and then use a sea salt spray to add texture.

How to Style Medium Length Hair for Round Faces

Styling medium length hair for round faces is all about creating balance. You want to create a hairstyle that helps to elongate the face, while also adding volume and texture. Here are some tips for styling medium length hair for round faces:

Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair is an important step in styling medium length hair for round faces. Use a round brush to create volume at the roots, and then use a flat brush to smooth out the ends. This will help to create a sleek, polished look.

Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair is key to creating balance for round faces. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume at the roots, and then use a round brush to create lift and movement.

Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can also help to create balance for round faces. Use a headband or hair clip to create a diagonal line across your face, which will help to elongate your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair for Round Faces

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling medium length hair for round faces:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots
  4. Blow dry your hair with a round brush
  5. Use a flat brush to smooth out the ends
  6. Use a curling iron to add loose waves
  7. Apply a sea salt spray to add texture
  8. Use a headband or hair clip to create a diagonal line across your face
  9. Finish with a light hold hairspray

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair for Round Faces

Maintaining medium length hair for round faces is easy if you follow a few simple tips:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims are important for maintaining healthy hair. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Use Heat Protectant

Heat protectant is essential for protecting your hair from damage caused by styling tools. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream before using a curling iron or flat iron.

Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help to keep it healthy and moisturized. Use a deep conditioning mask or treatment to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

FAQs About Medium Length Hair for Round Faces

What is the best length for medium length hair for round faces?

The best length for medium length hair for round faces is just below the collarbone. This length helps to elongate the face, while also providing enough length to create a variety of styles.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A ponytail can be a great option for those with round faces, but it's important to create volume at the crown of your head to help elongate your face. You can also add a headband or hair clip to create a diagonal line across your face.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair for Round Faces

Here are some additional hair care tips for medium length hair for round faces:

Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or every three days if possible.

Avoid Heat Damage

Heat damage can cause your hair to become dry and brittle, so it's important to use heat styling tools sparingly. If you do use a curling iron or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray or cream.

Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help to promote healthy hair growth. Be sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein to keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know the best medium length hair for round faces, it's time to try out some new styles. Remember to follow our tips for styling and hair care to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
