How To Curl My Hair Properly A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Mar 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Curling your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you’re new to it. But with the right tools and techniques, anyone can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls. In this article, we’ll walk you through the step-by-step process of how to curl your hair properly, as well as share some tips and FAQs to help you get the look you want.

Table of Contents

  • How to Curl Your Hair Properly
  • Tips for Curling Your Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Curl Your Hair Properly

Before you start curling your hair, you’ll need to gather a few tools:

  • Curling iron
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Sectioning clips
  • Hairspray

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using sectioning clips, separate your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl will be. Clip each section out of the way.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Start with the bottom section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding the iron vertically. Hold for 5-10 seconds, then release. Repeat until all sections are curled.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, spray hairspray all over your head to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you curl your hair like a pro:

  • Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel for even heat distribution.
  • Start with a lower heat setting and gradually increase if needed.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Don’t touch the curls until they’ve cooled down. This will help them set and last longer.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should I hold the curling iron on my hair?

You should hold the curling iron on your hair for 5-10 seconds, depending on how tight you want the curls to be. If you hold it longer, the curls will be tighter.

What’s the best way to curl short hair?

For short hair, use a curling wand with a small barrel. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the wand, holding for 5-10 seconds.

How can I make my curls last longer?

Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place. Don’t touch or brush the curls until they’ve cooled down. You can also sleep with your hair in a loose bun to help the curls last longer.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and defined.

With these tips and techniques, you can curl your hair like a pro and keep your curls looking their best. Happy curling!
