How To Style Short Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia May 03, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have short curly hair, you know that styling it can be a challenge. But fear not, because we’ve got you covered. In this blog post, we’ll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to style short curly hair to perfection. From the right products to use, to the best hairstyles for your hair type, we’ve got everything you need to know. So, without further ado, let’s get started!

How to Style Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair can be tricky, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful, defined look. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to styling your short curly hair is to wash and condition it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy. Gently towel dry your hair, but don’t rub it too hard as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner or Curl Cream

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place. Be sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser Attachment on Your Blow Dryer

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to enhance your natural curls and prevent frizz. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with your fingers as you dry it.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is dry, it’s time to style it. You can either wear your curls down or pull them back in a ponytail or bun. If you’re looking for a more polished look, try using a curling iron to define your curls even more.

Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your short curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet, and avoid using a brush as this can cause frizz.
  • Don’t touch your hair too much, as this can cause it to frizz.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep your curls hydrated and healthy. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Q: What’s the best hairstyle for short curly hair?

A: The best hairstyle for short curly hair is one that works with your natural curls. Try a bob or a pixie cut, or experiment with different updos and braids.

Hair Care

Taking care of your short curly hair is crucial to achieving a beautiful, healthy look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause heat damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to style your short curly hair like a pro. Remember to embrace your natural curls and have fun experimenting with different styles and products. Happy styling!
