How To Spike My Hair With Gel A Step By Step Guide

In this year 2023, spiky hairstyles are still a popular trend for both men and women. One way to achieve this look is by using hair gel. In this article, we will show you how to spike your hair with gel in a relaxed language. We will cover the basics of how to apply the gel, how to style your hair, and how to care for it afterwards.

How to Spike My Hair with Gel

Spiking your hair with gel can be a simple process if you follow the right steps. Here are the basic steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Gel

Choosing the right gel is important to achieve the desired look. Look for a gel that is specifically designed for spiking hair. These gels are usually stronger and provide a better hold. You can also look for gels that are alcohol-free to avoid drying out your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It is important to start with clean and dry hair. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or oil. Dry your hair with a towel or blow dryer until it is completely dry.

Step 3: Apply Gel to Your Hair

Apply a small amount of gel to your fingertips and rub it between your hands. Start from the back of your head and work your way to the front. Make sure to evenly distribute the gel throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers, style your hair in the desired direction. You can also use a comb or brush to create a more defined look. Make sure to spike your hair evenly and avoid clumping the gel in one area.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Dry

Allow your hair to dry completely before touching it. This will ensure that the gel sets and holds your hair in place. You can also use a blow dryer on a low setting to help speed up the drying process.

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to spike your hair with gel:

Step 1: Choose the Right Gel

As mentioned earlier, it is important to choose the right gel for spiking your hair. Look for a gel that is strong and provides a good hold. You can also look for gels that are alcohol-free to avoid drying out your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or oil. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry. You can also use a blow dryer on a low setting to help dry your hair.

Tips for Spiking Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when spiking your hair:

  • Use a small amount of gel to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Start with clean and dry hair to ensure the gel holds properly.
  • Use a comb or brush to create a more defined look.
  • Try different spiking techniques to find the one that works best for you.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much once it is spiked to avoid ruining the style.

FAQs

Can I spike my hair with any type of gel?

No, it is important to use a gel that is specifically designed for spiking hair. These gels are usually stronger and provide a better hold.

How long will my spiked hair last?

Your spiked hair can last for several hours or even a full day, depending on the amount of gel used and how well it holds. Avoid touching your hair too much to help it last longer.

How do I remove the gel from my hair?

You can remove the gel from your hair by washing it with shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all the gel.

Hair Care After Spiking Your Hair

After spiking your hair, it is important to take care of it to avoid damage. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any dirt or oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much gel, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant when using a blow dryer or other heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

By following these tips, you can maintain a healthy and stylish look even after spiking your hair with gel.

Spiking your hair with gel can be a fun and easy way to change up your hairstyle. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can achieve a trendy and modern look that will turn heads. Just remember to take care of your hair afterwards to keep it healthy and looking its best.
