How To Get Loose Curls For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a guy looking to switch up your hairstyle with some loose curls? You're in luck! In this article, we'll be discussing how to get loose curls for guys. We'll cover everything from how to prep your hair to tips for maintaining your curls. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Curling Your Hair
  • Cooling Your Curls
  • Styling Your Curls
  • Must-Have Hair Products

Prepping Your Hair

The key to getting great curls is starting with clean, dry hair. Before you start curling, make sure you've washed and conditioned your hair. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair.

Next, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Curling Your Hair

Now it's time to start curling! We recommend using a curling iron with a barrel size of 1-1.5 inches for loose curls. Start by sectioning your hair into 1-2 inch sections.

Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting at the base of the hair near your scalp. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl.

Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to curl each section in the same direction for a uniform look.

Cooling Your Curls

Once you've curled all of your hair, it's time to cool your curls. Use a cool shot button on your blow dryer or simply let your curls cool on their own for a few minutes. This will help set your curls and prevent them from falling out too quickly.

Styling Your Curls

Now that your curls are set, it's time to style them! If you want more volume, try flipping your head upside down and gently scrunching your curls. You can also use a hair pick to lift your curls at the roots.

If you prefer a more relaxed look, simply run your fingers through your curls to loosen them up a bit.

Must-Have Hair Products

When it comes to maintaining your curls, it's important to use the right products. Here are some must-have hair products for guys with loose curls:

  • Curl-defining cream
  • Lightweight leave-in conditioner
  • Hair oil or serum
  • Anti-frizz spray

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

To keep your curls looking great, follow these tips:

  • Avoid shampooing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can break up your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Refresh your curls throughout the day with a curl-refreshing spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can guys with short hair get loose curls?

A: Yes! While it may be more difficult to achieve loose curls with short hair, it's definitely possible. Use a smaller curling iron and focus on curling the ends of your hair.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: This depends on your hair type and how well you've prepped and styled your hair. On average, loose curls can last anywhere from 1-3 days.

Hair Care for Curls

Finally, it's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for hair care for guys with curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep your curls moisturized.

There you have it - a complete guide on how to get loose curls for guys! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect head of curls. Whether you want a more polished or relaxed look, there's a curling technique that will work for you. Happy styling!
