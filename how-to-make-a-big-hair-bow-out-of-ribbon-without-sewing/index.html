How To Make A Big Hair Bow Out Of Ribbon Without Sewing

Written by Daniel Aug 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a fun and easy way to jazz up your hairstyle, look no further than a big hair bow made out of ribbon. And the best part? You don't even need to know how to sew to make one! In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to create your own hair bow using just a few simple materials.

Table of Contents

  • Materials
  • How To Make a Big Hair Bow Out of Ribbon Without Sewing
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Materials

Before we get started, you'll need to gather a few materials:

  • Ribbon (at least 2 inches wide)
  • Scissors
  • Hot glue gun and glue sticks
  • Hair clip or elastic band

How to Make a Big Hair Bow Out of Ribbon Without Sewing

Now that you have your materials, let's get started on making your hair bow:

Step 1: Cut the Ribbon

Start by cutting three pieces of ribbon: two that are 12 inches long and one that is 4 inches long.


Step 2: Create the Loops

Take one of the 12-inch pieces of ribbon and create a loop by bringing the two ends together and overlapping them by about an inch. Use your hot glue gun to secure the two ends together. Repeat with the second 12-inch piece of ribbon.


Step 3: Make the Bow

Take the two loops you just created and place them on top of each other, with the glue seams facing down and the loops forming an "X" shape. Use your hot glue gun to attach the two loops together in the center.


Step 4: Add the Center Ribbon

Take the 4-inch piece of ribbon and wrap it around the center of the bow, covering up the spot where the loops are attached together. Use your hot glue gun to secure the ends of the 4-inch ribbon to the back of the bow.


Step 5: Attach the Clip or Elastic Band

Finally, attach a hair clip or elastic band to the back of the bow using your hot glue gun. Allow the glue to dry completely before wearing your new hair accessory!


Tips

  • Experiment with different colors and patterns of ribbon to create a unique look.
  • If you don't have a hot glue gun, you can use fabric glue or even a strong adhesive tape instead.
  • If you're using an elastic band, make sure it's the right size for your head so that the bow stays in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a different type of ribbon?

A: Yes! You can use any type of ribbon you like, as long as it's at least 2 inches wide.

Q: Can I make a smaller or larger hair bow?

A: Absolutely! You can adjust the size of your hair bow by cutting your ribbon to different lengths.

Hair Care

When wearing a big hair bow, it's important to take extra care when brushing and styling your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up. If you're using an elastic band to secure your hair bow, make sure it's not too tight and doesn't pull on your hair too much.

With these simple steps, you can create your own big hair bow out of ribbon without sewing. Have fun experimenting with different colors and patterns, and enjoy your new hair accessory!
