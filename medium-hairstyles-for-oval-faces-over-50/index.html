Medium Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 50

Written by Robby May 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner, and it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that suits our face shape and age. For women over 50 with an oval face shape, medium hairstyles are an excellent option. Medium hairstyles are versatile and easy to manage, making them the perfect choice for women who want a low-maintenance style that looks great.

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the ideal face shape because it is balanced and symmetrical. With an oval face shape, the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all in proportion. Women with this face shape can experiment with different hairstyles and lengths without worrying about unflattering angles.

How to Choose the Right Medium Hairstyle for Your Oval Face

Choosing the right medium hairstyle for your oval face can be a daunting task. The key is to find a style that accentuates your best features and complements your face shape. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect medium hairstyle for your oval face:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Texture

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your hair texture. Do you have straight, curly, or wavy hair? Knowing your hair texture will help you choose a hairstyle that works with your hair type.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

The next step is to consider your lifestyle. Do you have a busy schedule that requires low-maintenance hairstyles? Or do you have more time to spend on your hair? Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle.

Step 3: Consult with a Hairstylist

Consulting with a hairstylist is always a good idea. A professional hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Step by Step Guide to Medium Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 50

Here are some step by step guides to help you achieve some of the best medium hairstyles for oval faces over 50:

1. Shoulder-Length Bob

The shoulder-length bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to shoulder-length with layers. You can add bangs to soften the look and create a more youthful appearance.

2. Layered Lob

The layered lob is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn straight or wavy. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to just above the shoulders with long layers. This style adds volume and movement to thin hair.

3. Shaggy Layers

The shaggy layers hairstyle is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance style that looks great. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to shoulder-length with choppy layers.

Tips for Styling Medium Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 50

Here are some tips for styling your medium hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add volume and texture to thin hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and shape to your hair.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can women over 50 wear long hair?

Yes, women over 50 can wear long hair. However, long hair can be challenging to manage and may require more maintenance.

2. Can women over 50 wear bangs?

Yes, women over 50 can wear bangs. Bangs can soften the look and create a more youthful appearance.

Hair Care for Medium Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 50

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

In conclusion, medium hairstyles are a great option for women over 50 with an oval face shape. With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age.
