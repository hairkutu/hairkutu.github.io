How To Cut Your Own Short Curly Hair

Written by William Apr 06, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the hair salon? Do you want to learn how to cut your own short curly hair? In this article, we will show you step-by-step how to cut your hair at home. We will also provide tips on how to care for your hair after the cut.

Description

Curly hair can be tricky to cut, especially if you have short hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut at home. Cutting your own hair can also save you time and money.

How To

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to use hair clips to section off your hair.

Step-by-Step

1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. Do not use any styling products.

2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.

3. Use hair clips to section off your hair. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up.

4. Take a small section of hair and twist it tightly. Cut the ends with your scissors, being careful not to cut too much.

5. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way around your head.

6. Once you have finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check the back. Make any necessary adjustments.

7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

1. Start with small sections of hair. You can always cut more, but you cannot put hair back once it has been cut.

2. Use a mirror to check the back of your hair. It can be difficult to see all angles without a mirror.

3. If you are unsure about cutting your hair, start with a small trim. You can always cut more later.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair when it is wet?

A: We recommend cutting your hair when it is dry. Wet hair can be longer and may shrink as it dries, resulting in an uneven cut.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. We recommend cutting your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

3. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

4. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape.

Conclusion

Cutting your own short curly hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut at home. Remember to start with small sections of hair and use a mirror to check the back. After cutting your hair, take care of it to maintain its shape and health.
