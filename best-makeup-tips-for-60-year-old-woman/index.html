Best Makeup Tips For 60 Year Old Woman

Written by Eveline Jul 11, 2023 · 8 min read

As women age, their skin changes and so does their makeup routine. At 60, it's important to choose makeup products that enhance natural beauty and make you feel confident. In this article, we will discuss the best makeup tips for a 60-year-old woman.

Table of Content

  • How to Choose the Right Makeup Products
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Applying Makeup
  • Makeup Tips for a 60-Year-Old Woman
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for a 60-Year-Old Woman

How to Choose the Right Makeup Products

As you age, your skin becomes more delicate and sensitive. Therefore, it's essential to choose makeup products that are gentle on the skin. Here are some tips on how to choose the right makeup products:

1. Choose a Foundation with SPF

A foundation with SPF will not only provide coverage but also protect your skin from harmful UV rays. Look for a foundation with a lightweight formula that won't settle into fine lines and wrinkles.

2. Opt for Creamy Formulas

Creamy formulas are perfect for mature skin as they hydrate and add moisture, which is essential for healthy-looking skin. Look for cream-based blushes, eyeshadows, and lipsticks.

3. Use Eye Creams

As you age, your skin around the eyes gets thinner, making it more prone to wrinkles and fine lines. Using an eye cream before applying makeup will keep the area hydrated and prevent makeup from creasing.

Step-by-Step Guide to Applying Makeup

Now that you know how to choose the right makeup products, it's time to learn how to apply them correctly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Cleanse and Moisturize Your Face

Before applying makeup, cleanse your face thoroughly and apply a moisturizer. This will create a smooth canvas for makeup application.

2. Apply a Primer

A primer helps to smooth out your skin's texture and create a flawless finish. Apply a primer before foundation to ensure your makeup stays put throughout the day.

3. Apply Foundation

Use a foundation brush or sponge to apply foundation evenly over your face. Start at the center of your face and blend outwards for a natural finish.

4. Apply Concealer

Apply concealer under your eyes to cover dark circles and any blemishes. Use a concealer brush or your finger to blend the product into your skin.

5. Apply Blush

Apply cream blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend upwards towards your temples. This will give you a natural, healthy glow.

6. Apply Eyeshadow

Choose a neutral eyeshadow shade and apply it to your eyelids. Use a blending brush to blend outwards for a natural look.

7. Apply Mascara

Use a mascara wand to apply mascara to your lashes. Wiggle the wand from the roots to the tips for voluminous lashes.

8. Apply Lipstick

Choose a cream-based lipstick and apply it to your lips. Use a lip brush for precise application.

Makeup Tips for a 60-Year-Old Woman

Here are some makeup tips for a 60-year-old woman:

1. Embrace Your Natural Beauty

As you age, it's important to embrace your natural beauty. Use makeup to enhance your best features rather than trying to cover up imperfections.

2. Use Light-Reflecting Products

Light-reflecting products such as highlighters can help to give your skin a youthful glow.

3. Avoid Heavy Eye Makeup

Heavy eye makeup can make your eyes look smaller and draw attention to fine lines and wrinkles. Stick to neutral eyeshadow shades and avoid heavy eyeliner.

4. Keep Your Makeup Fresh

Makeup products can expire, so it's important to keep them fresh. Replace your makeup every six months to avoid using outdated products that can cause skin irritation.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I still wear bold lipstick at 60?

Absolutely! Bold lipstick can add a pop of color and make you feel confident. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

2. Do I need to wear eyeshadow?

No, you don't need to wear eyeshadow if you don't want to. However, a neutral eyeshadow shade can help to brighten up your eyes.

3. What's the best way to apply concealer?

Use a concealer brush or your finger to apply concealer under your eyes. Blend the product into your skin for a natural finish.

Hair Care Tips for a 60-Year-Old Woman

As you age, your hair also changes. Here are some hair care tips for a 60-year-old woman:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free.

2. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims will keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Embrace Your Natural Hair Color

Embrace your natural hair color and avoid harsh dyes that can damage your hair.

In conclusion, these makeup and hair care tips can help a 60-year-old woman look and feel confident. Remember to choose products that are gentle on the skin, embrace your natural beauty, and keep your makeup and hair fresh.
