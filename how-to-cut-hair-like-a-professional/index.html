How To Cut Hair Like A Professional A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Aug 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of spending hundreds of dollars at the salon for a simple haircut? If you want to learn how to cut hair like a professional, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair like a pro, as well as some tips and FAQs. Let’s get started!

How to Cut Hair Like a Professional

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, here are some essential tools you’ll need to cut hair like a pro:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Spray bottle

Step 1: Prep the Hair

Start by washing the hair and towel-drying it. Use a comb to detangle the hair and section it into four parts, using hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Cutting the Back

Start with the back of the hair. Take one section and comb it straight down. Use your fingers to measure the length you want to cut and use the scissors to cut straight across. Repeat this process for each section in the back.

Step 3: Cutting the Sides

Next, move on to the sides of the hair. Use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Cutting the Top

Finally, move on to the top of the hair. Take one section at a time and use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle for a layered look.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Once you’ve cut all the hair to the desired length, use the clippers to trim the hair around the ears and the nape of the neck. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a more polished look.

Tips for Cutting Hair Like a Professional

Here are some tips to help you cut hair like a pro:

  • Invest in high-quality scissors and clippers.
  • Start with a longer guard when using the clippers and work your way down.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle for a layered look.
  • Take your time and don’t rush the process.
  • Practice on a mannequin head before cutting someone’s hair.

FAQ About Cutting Hair Like a Professional

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair like a pro:

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, we recommend that you practice on a mannequin head before attempting to cut your own hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you’re going for. Generally, it’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always trim a little more hair off to fix the mistake. If the mistake is severe, you may need to go to a professional to have it fixed.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the style.
  • Use hair masks and oils to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can learn how to cut hair like a professional. Remember to take your time and practice, and don’t be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
