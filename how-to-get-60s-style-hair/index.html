How To Get 60S Style Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to add some retro flair to your hairstyle, why not try a 60s-inspired look? This era was known for its iconic hairstyles that are still popular today. In this post, we will show you how to get 60s style hair, including tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get 60s Style Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining 60s Style Hair
  • FAQs

How to Get 60s Style Hair

There are a few different 60s hairstyles to choose from, but some of the most iconic ones include:

  • The bouffant
  • The beehive
  • The pixie cut
  • The shag
  • The long, straight hair with a center part

Each of these styles requires a different technique, but they all share some common elements. Here are the basic steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help give your hair the volume it needs for a 60s-inspired style.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair while it's still damp. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair from roots to ends.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots. Make sure to direct the heat upwards to help create the desired style.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Using a fine-tooth comb, tease the crown of your hair to create height and volume. Work in small sections and be gentle to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 5: Smooth Your Hair

Smooth the top layer of your hair with a paddle brush to create a polished finish. You can also use hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining 60s Style Hair

Once you've achieved your desired 60s-inspired hairstyle, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause it to become flat and limp.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes and add volume.
  • Invest in a good quality hairspray to help hold your style in place.
  • Consider using hot rollers or a curling iron to add extra volume and texture to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve 60s style hair with short hair?

A: Yes! The pixie cut was a popular 60s hairstyle, so if you have short hair, you can still achieve a retro look with the right styling techniques.

Q: Can I wear a 60s-inspired hairstyle to work?

A: Absolutely! There are many 60s hairstyles that can be adapted for a professional setting. Consider a sleek, low ponytail or a polished bob.

Hair Care for 60s Style Hair

To keep your 60s-inspired hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Invest in a good quality brush and comb to prevent tangles and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a fabulous 60s-inspired hairstyle that is sure to turn heads. Whether you opt for a bouffant, beehive, or long, straight hair, you'll be channeling your inner retro goddess in no time!
