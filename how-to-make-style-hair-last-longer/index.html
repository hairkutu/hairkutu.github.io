How To Make Style Hair Last Longer Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 09, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’ve ever spent hours styling your hair, only to have it fall flat within a few hours, you know how frustrating it can be. Luckily, there are several tips and tricks you can use to make your hair style last longer. In this article, we’ll share our top tips for keeping your hair looking great all day long.

How to Make Style Hair Last Longer

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, it’s important to understand why your hair may not be holding its style. There are several factors that can contribute to this, including:

  • Humidity
  • Oily hair
  • Product buildup
  • Using the wrong products
  • Not using enough product
  • Not letting your hair cool down after styling

Now that we know what could be causing your hair to fall flat, let’s talk about how to prevent it from happening.

Step-by-Step Guide to Make Style Hair Last Longer

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Before you even start styling your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. If your hair is oily or dirty, it’s more likely to fall flat. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup from your hair, and make sure it’s completely dry before styling.

Step 2: Use the right products.

Using the right products is key to making your hair style last longer. Use a heat protectant before styling to prevent damage from hot tools. If you’re using a curling iron, use a holding spray to help the curls stay in place. If you’re styling your hair straight, use a smoothing serum to prevent frizz.

Step 3: Use enough product.

Using too little product can cause your hair to fall flat. Make sure you’re using enough product to hold your style in place. If you’re using a holding spray or mousse, apply it generously to your hair.

Step 4: Let your hair cool down.

After you’ve finished styling your hair, let it cool down before touching it. This will help your style set and prevent it from falling flat too quickly. If you’re using hot tools, let your hair cool down completely before applying any product.

Tips to Make Style Hair Last Longer

Tip 1: Sleep with a silk scarf or pillowcase.

Sleeping on a silk scarf or pillowcase can help prevent frizz and keep your hair looking great. Silk is gentle on your hair, so it won’t cause any breakage or damage.

Tip 2: Avoid touching your hair.

The more you touch your hair, the more likely it is to fall flat. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible, and if you need to adjust your style, use a comb or brush instead of your hands.

Tip 3: Use dry shampoo.

If your hair starts to look oily throughout the day, use a dry shampoo to absorb the oil and refresh your style. This will help your hair look clean and fresh, even if you haven’t washed it in a few days.

FAQs About Making Style Hair Last Longer

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily it gets. Some people can go a few days without washing their hair, while others may need to wash it every day. Use your best judgement and listen to your hair.

Q: Can I use hairspray to make my hair style last longer?

A: Yes, hairspray can help hold your style in place. Just make sure you’re not using too much, as this can cause your hair to look stiff and unnatural.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Lastly, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients

By following these tips, you can make your hair style last longer and keep your hair healthy and strong. Happy styling!
