How To Braid Your Own Hair Mens Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 21, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that's both stylish and practical? Braids are a great option for men, and they're easy to do yourself with a little practice. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to braid your own hair mens, including some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Table of Contents

  • How to Braid Your Own Hair Mens
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Braid Your Own Hair Mens

Braids are a versatile hairstyle that work for any hair type or length. Whether you're looking for an edgy look or something more classic, braids can be customized to suit your personal style. Here's how to braid your own hair mens:

Choose the Right Type of Braid

Before you start braiding, you'll need to decide what type of braid you want. There are many different styles to choose from, including:

  • French braid
  • Dutch braid
  • Cornrows
  • Fishtail braid

Each type of braid has a slightly different technique, so be sure to choose the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin braiding, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is too oily or greasy, the braids may not hold well. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help your hair stay hydrated.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you're ready to braid, follow these steps:

Step 1: Divide Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into three sections, holding one in each hand and using your fingers to separate the middle section.

Step 2: Cross the Sections

Take the right section and cross it over the middle section, then take the left section and cross it over the new middle section. Repeat this process, alternating sides, until you reach the end of your hair.

Step 3: Secure the End

When you reach the end of your hair, secure it with an elastic band or hair tie. You may also want to use a small amount of hair wax or gel to help keep the braids in place.

Tips for Braiding Your Own Hair Mens

Braiding your own hair can be tricky at first, but with a little practice, you'll be a pro in no time. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Start with a simple braid, like a French braid, before moving on to more complex styles.
  • Use a mirror to help you see what you're doing.
  • Take your time and be patient – braiding can be a slow process.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to braid your own hair?

The time it takes to braid your own hair depends on the length and complexity of the braid. Simple braids can take as little as 10-15 minutes, while more complex styles may take an hour or more.

Can I braid my hair if it's short?

Yes, you can still braid your hair if it's short. Just be sure to choose a braid style that works well with shorter hair, like a French braid or cornrows.

Hair Care for Braids

Once you've braided your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and clean. Here are some tips for hair care with braids:

  • Wash your hair regularly – about once a week is recommended.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the braids.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on the braids, as this can cause them to come loose.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to braid your own hair mens in no time. So why not try out a new braided hairstyle today?
