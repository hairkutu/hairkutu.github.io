How To Make Messy Wavy Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

If you have short hair, you might think that you're limited in terms of styling options. But that's not the case! Messy wavy hair is a great look for short hair, and it's surprisingly easy to achieve. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create messy wavy short hair, as well as provide tips and tricks for maintaining your new style.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Messy Wavy Short Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Messy Wavy Short Hair

Messy wavy short hair is a style that looks effortless, but there are a few things you can do to make sure your hair looks its best. Here are some tips:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray
  • Use a curling iron or wand
  • Use a texture spray or sea salt spray

By following these steps, you can create a messy wavy look that will last all day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Clean and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with your usual shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry. You want to make sure your hair is completely dry before using any heat styling tools.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant Spray

Before using any heat styling tools, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help prevent damage from the heat.

Step 3: Use a Curling Iron or Wand

Section your hair and use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Start at the top of your head and work your way down, curling small sections of hair at a time. Be sure to curl away from your face for a more natural look.

Step 4: Use a Texture Spray or Sea Salt Spray

Once you've curled your hair, use a texture spray or sea salt spray to add volume and texture. Spray the product onto your hair, scrunch it up with your hands, and tousle it to create a messy, wavy look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect messy wavy short hair:

  • Use a smaller curling iron or wand for tighter waves
  • Use a larger curling iron or wand for looser waves
  • Don't overdo it with the texture spray or sea salt spray. A little goes a long way.
  • To create more volume, tease your roots with a comb or brush before adding any product.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to make messy wavy short hair:

How long will the style last?

The style should last all day with the help of a texture spray or sea salt spray.

Do I need to use a curling iron or wand?

A curling iron or wand will give you the best results, but you can also achieve a similar look with a flat iron or braids.

Can I create this style with naturally curly hair?

Absolutely! Follow the same steps, but skip the curling iron or wand.

Hair Care

To maintain your messy wavy short hair, be sure to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner. Also, avoid using too much heat on your hair and be sure to use a heat protectant spray when you do use heat styling tools. Finally, trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a beautiful messy wavy short hair style that's perfect for any occasion. Have fun experimenting with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you!
