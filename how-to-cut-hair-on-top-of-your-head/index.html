How To Cut Hair On Top Of Your Head A Guide For Beginners

Written by Bardi Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting your own hair can be an intimidating task, especially when it comes to the top of your head. But with some basic knowledge and the right tools, you can achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll go over how to cut hair on top of your head, step by step.

How to Cut Hair on Top of Your Head

Before we dive into the specifics of cutting hair on top of your head, let's go over some basic tools you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clipper
  • Mirror
  • Hair ties or clips

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair thoroughly and letting it air dry or blow drying it. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair looks clean and healthy.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair ties or clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Starting with the top section, use your scissors to trim your hair to the desired length. Start with small snips and work your way up to avoid cutting too much at once. Use a comb to lift your hair and cut it straight across.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Moving on to the side sections, use your clipper to blend the hair from the top section into the sides. Start with a longer guard and work your way down, using an upward motion to create a gradual fade.

Step 5: Trim the Back

Finally, tackle the back section by using your clipper to trim the hair to the desired length. Use a handheld mirror to check for any missed spots.

Tips for Cutting Hair on Top of Your Head

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair on top of your head:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down gradually.
  • Use a comb to lift and cut your hair to ensure an even length.
  • Take breaks if you need to rest your arms or adjust your grip.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check for any missed spots.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair on top of my head with just scissors?

Yes, you can use scissors to cut your hair on top of your head. However, using a clipper can make the process faster and more precise.

How often should I cut my hair on top of my head?

This depends on personal preference and hair type. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

Don't panic! If you accidentally cut too much or create an uneven length, you can always trim it down or even it out later. It's better to take your time and go slowly than to rush and make a mistake.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your newly cut hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

Cutting your own hair on top of your head can be a daunting task, but with some practice and patience, you can achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut at home. Remember to use the right tools, take your time, and follow these step-by-step instructions to create a look you'll love.
