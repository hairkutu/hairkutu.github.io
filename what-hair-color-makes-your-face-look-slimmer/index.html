What Hair Color Makes Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by Christine Aug 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for an easy way to make your face look slimmer, changing your hair color can be a great option. But with so many hair colors out there, how do you know which one will work best for you? In this article, we'll explore what hair color makes your face look slimmer, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to keep your new hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Slimming Hair Color
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The hair color that makes your face look slimmer is one that creates contrast between your hair and skin tone. This contrast draws attention away from the roundness of your face and towards the angles created by the contrast. The best hair colors for creating this contrast are dark colors like black, espresso, and dark chocolate, as well as cool-toned colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and cool brown. These colors create the illusion of depth and dimension, which helps to slim down your face.

How to Achieve Slimming Hair Color

If you're ready to try a new hair color to slim down your face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone. This can be done by consulting with a professional hair stylist or using online color matching tools. Once you've chosen your color, it's time to prep your hair for the dyeing process.

Prepping Your Hair

Before dyeing your hair, it's important to prep it properly to ensure the color comes out evenly and lasts as long as possible. Here's how to prep your hair for dyeing:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue that may interfere with the dyeing process.
  2. Deep condition your hair to restore moisture and prevent damage.
  3. Wait at least 24 hours before dyeing your hair to give your scalp time to recover from the washing and conditioning process.

Step by Step Guide

Once you've prepped your hair, it's time to dye it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Put on gloves to protect your hands from the dye.
  2. Mix the dye according to the instructions on the package.
  3. Apply the dye to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time, usually 30-45 minutes.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear.
  6. Apply a color-safe conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes.
  7. Rinse your hair again with cool water to seal the cuticle and lock in the color.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep your new hair color looking its best:

  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade faster.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q: Will any dark or cool-toned hair color work for slimming my face?

A: While dark and cool-toned colors are generally the best for creating contrast and slimming down your face, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone. Consulting with a professional hair stylist or using online color matching tools can help you find the perfect color for you.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home, or do I need to go to a salon?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's always best to consult with a professional hair stylist. They can help you choose the right color for your skin tone and hair type, as well as ensure that the dyeing process is done safely and effectively.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove buildup and keep your scalp healthy.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know what hair color makes your face look slimmer, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to choose a color that complements your skin tone, prep your hair properly before dyeing, and take good care of your new hair to keep it looking its best.
