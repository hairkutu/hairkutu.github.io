Best Hairstyles For Thick Coarse Wavy Hair

Written by Daniel Sep 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When you have thick coarse wavy hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will show off your natural texture without being too difficult to manage. Luckily, there are plenty of great hairstyles out there that can work for your hair type. Here are some of the best styles to consider:

Description

Thick coarse wavy hair is characterized by its volume, texture, and tendency to frizz. This hair type can be challenging to manage, but with the right styling products and techniques, it can create stunning looks. Thick coarse wavy hair is also prone to dryness, so taking care of it with hydrating products is essential.

How to

To get the best hairstyles for thick coarse wavy hair, you'll need to start with the right cut. A layered cut can help to reduce bulk and add movement to your hair. You can also consider adding bangs to your cut to frame your face and add dimension to your style.

When it comes to styling, it's important to use products that will enhance your natural texture. A curl-enhancing cream or mousse can help to define your waves and reduce frizz. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner.

2. Apply curl-enhancing cream or mousse to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition.

5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

-Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can dry out your hair and cause damage.

-Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

-Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my thick coarse wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant and not to do it too often.

Q: How often should I wash my thick coarse wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair's needs, but generally, washing your hair every other day or every few days is sufficient.

Hair Care

When caring for thick coarse wavy hair, it's essential to use products that will add moisture and enhance your natural texture. Here are some products to consider:

-A hydrating shampoo and conditioner

-A curl-enhancing cream or mousse

-A diffuser attachment for your blow dryer

-A deep conditioning treatment

-A light hold hairspray

By using the right products and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, manageable hairstyles for your thick coarse wavy hair.
