Best Hairstyle For Long Oval Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of scrolling through endless photos of hairstyles that just don't seem to work with your long oval face shape? Look no further! In this guide, we will discuss the best hairstyles for long oval face shapes, along with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Long oval face shapes are characterized by a narrow chin and forehead, with the length of the face being longer than the width. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to create balance and proportion.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for your long oval face shape is to determine your face shape. Once you have established that you have a long oval face shape, you can start considering the different styles that will best complement your features.

When choosing a hairstyle, it is important to consider your hair type and texture, as well as your lifestyle and personal style. You want to choose a style that not only flatters your face shape but also fits your overall aesthetic.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle for your long oval face shape, let's dive into some of the best styles to try.

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for those with long oval face shapes as they help to create movement and dimension. The layers help to break up the length of the face and add volume to the hair.

To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that start at the chin or collarbone. You can also add some face-framing layers to help balance out your features.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for long oval face shapes. They help to create balance by drawing attention away from the length of the face and towards the eyes.

To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long, side-swept bangs that start at the eyebrow and angle down towards the cheekbone.

3. Bob

A bob is a versatile hairstyle that works well with long oval face shapes. The length of the bob can be customized to flatter your face shape and features.

To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a chin-length bob that is slightly longer in the front. You can also add layers or bangs to create additional dimension.

Tips and Tricks

When styling your hair, there are a few tips and tricks to keep in mind to help you achieve the perfect look.

1. Use Products

Using the right products can help you achieve the perfect style for your long oval face shape. For example, if you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse to add some lift and body. If you have curly hair, use a curl-defining cream to help define your curls.

2. Use Heat Tools

Heat tools such as curling irons and straighteners can help you create a variety of styles that flatter your face shape. For example, using a curling iron to create loose waves can help add volume and dimension to your hair.

3. Experiment with Parting

Experimenting with different parting styles can help you create a look that flatters your face shape. For example, a middle part can help to create balance and symmetry, while a side part can help to create dimension and movement.

FAQ

What if I have thick hair?

If you have thick hair, consider getting layers to help thin out your hair and create movement. You can also try a bob or lob to help manage your hair.

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, consider getting a shorter cut to help create the illusion of thickness. You can also try adding layers or using volumizing products to help add some lift and body.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your hair, it is important to use products that are tailored to your hair type and texture. You should also avoid using heat tools too often and be sure to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle for your long oval face shape. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
