Best Goatee Styles For Round Face

Written by Oliver Jul 22, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have a round face, choosing the right facial hair style can be a bit tricky. A goatee is a classic and versatile style that can work well with a round face shape. In this article, we will discuss the best goatee styles for round face and provide tips on how to maintain them.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Style a Goatee for Round Face
  • Step by Step Guide on How to Trim a Goatee
  • Tips for Maintaining a Goatee
  • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
  • Hair Care Tips for Goatee

Description

A goatee is a type of beard style that involves growing hair on the chin and keeping the rest of the face clean-shaven. It is a popular style among men of all ages and can be adapted to suit different face shapes. For those with a round face, a goatee can help create the illusion of a longer and more defined jawline. However, not all goatee styles are suitable for a round face. To look your best, you need to choose a style that complements your face shape.

How to Style a Goatee for Round Face

When it comes to styling a goatee for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid styles that make your face look rounder or fuller. Instead, opt for styles that create the illusion of length and angularity. Here are some of the best goatee styles for round face:

1. Classic Goatee

The classic goatee is a timeless style that works well with a round face. It involves growing a small patch of hair on the chin and keeping the rest of the face clean-shaven. To create a more angular look, you can shape the goatee into a square or triangle shape.

2. Van Dyke Goatee

The Van Dyke goatee is a popular style that combines a mustache with a goatee. It involves growing a small patch of hair on the chin and a separate mustache. This style works well with a round face as it creates the illusion of length and angularity.

3. Anchor Goatee

The anchor goatee is a more complex style that involves shaping the goatee into an anchor shape. It involves growing a small patch of hair on the chin and a soul patch directly below the lower lip. This style works well with a round face as it creates the illusion of length and angularity.

Step by Step Guide on How to Trim a Goatee

Once you have decided on a goatee style, it is important to keep it well-groomed. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to trim a goatee:

Step 1: Grow the Goatee

Allow the hair to grow for at least a week before trimming it. This will give you enough hair to work with and help you achieve a more defined shape.

Step 2: Define the Outline

Using a trimmer, define the outline of the goatee by trimming the hair around the chin. Use a comb to help guide the trimmer and create a more even look.

Step 3: Trim the Hair

Using a pair of scissors, trim the hair on the goatee to your desired length. You can also use a trimmer to create a more even look.

Step 4: Shape the Goatee

Using a razor, shape the goatee into your desired shape. Be sure to take your time and make small adjustments as needed.

Step 5: Clean Up

Once you are happy with the shape of your goatee, use a razor or trimmer to clean up any stray hairs around the edges.

Tips for Maintaining a Goatee

Here are some tips for maintaining a goatee:

  • Trim your goatee regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep the hair soft and moisturized.
  • Wash your goatee regularly to prevent any buildup of dirt or oil.
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on your goatee as this can damage the hair.
  • Consider using a beard comb or brush to help keep your goatee looking neat and tidy.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the best goatee style for a round face?

The best goatee styles for a round face are the classic goatee, Van Dyke goatee, and anchor goatee. These styles create the illusion of length and angularity, which can help balance out a round face.

How often should I trim my goatee?

You should trim your goatee regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy. How often you need to trim it will depend on how fast your hair grows, but once a week is usually sufficient.

Can I use regular shampoo on my goatee?

You can use regular shampoo on your goatee, but it is best to use a shampoo that is specifically designed for facial hair. Regular shampoo can be too harsh and strip the hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and damage.

Hair Care Tips for Goatee

Here are some hair care tips for goatee:

  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep the hair soft and moisturized.
  • Wash your goatee regularly to prevent any buildup of dirt or oil.
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on your goatee as this can damage the hair.
  • Consider using a beard comb or brush to help keep your goatee looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and styles, you can choose the best goatee style for your round face and maintain it for a sharp, stylish look. Remember to take care of your facial hair and keep it trim and neat for the best results.
