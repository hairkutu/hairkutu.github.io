How To Cut Men s Medium Long Hair With Scissors

Written by Petter Aug 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting men's medium long hair with scissors can be a daunting task, especially if you are not a professional hair stylist. However, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a great haircut at home. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's medium long hair with scissors.

Description

Men's medium long hair refers to hair that falls between the shoulders and chin. This type of hair is versatile and can be styled in various ways, including messy, slicked back, and textured. However, to achieve a great hairstyle, it is essential to start with a good haircut.

How To

Before you start cutting men's medium long hair with scissors, you will need the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle

Once you have all the tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair and let it dry completely. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of the hair.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair into four parts: top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section. Comb the hair straight down and cut it in a straight line. Make sure to cut small sections at a time to ensure precision.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Move to the side sections and comb the hair straight down. Cut the hair in a diagonal line, starting from the back and moving towards the front. Make sure to blend the back and side sections seamlessly.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move to the top section and comb the hair straight up. Cut the hair in a straight line, starting from the back and moving towards the front. Make sure to blend the top section with the side sections.

Step 6: Check for Unevenness

After you have cut all sections, check for unevenness and trim any stray hairs.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Style the hair as desired using hair products.

Tips

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Cut small sections at a time for precision.
  • Blend the back and side sections seamlessly.
  • Trim any stray hairs after cutting.
  • Use hair products to style the hair.

FAQ

Can I cut men's medium long hair with regular scissors?

It is recommended to use sharp hair cutting scissors for best results. Regular scissors may not be sharp enough and can result in uneven cuts.

How often should I cut men's medium long hair?

It is recommended to cut men's medium long hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the desired length and style.

Can I cut men's medium long hair myself?

While it is possible to cut men's medium long hair yourself, it is recommended to visit a professional hair stylist for best results.

Hair Care

After cutting men's medium long hair, it is essential to take care of the hair to maintain its health and style. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash the hair and avoid using hot styling tools frequently. Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
