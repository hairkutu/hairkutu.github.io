The Most Popular Hairstyle For 2021

Written by William May 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for the latest hairstyle trend of 2021? Look no further because we have the answer for you! In this article, we will talk about the most popular hairstyle for 2021 that you can try out. From how to style it to hair care tips, we got you covered. So, let’s dive in!

Description

The most popular hairstyle for 2021 is the shag haircut. This hairstyle was first introduced in the 1970s and has made a comeback this year. The shag haircut is a versatile style that can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type. It is a layered haircut that is usually styled with messy waves and bangs. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, effortless look.

How to Style

The shag haircut is easy to style and does not require much effort. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your shag haircut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Use a towel to dry your hair and remove any excess water.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product such as mousse or texturizing spray to your hair. This will add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Use the brush to lift the roots of your hair and create volume. Once your hair is dry, use a straightening iron to add waves to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

If you have bangs, style them by blow drying them with a round brush. Use a straightening iron to create a slight curl in your bangs.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your shag haircut:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a straightening iron to add waves to your hair.
  • Style your bangs with a round brush and a straightening iron.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the shag haircut:

Q: What face shape does the shag haircut suit?

A: The shag haircut suits most face shapes, especially those with oval and heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can I style my shag haircut in a different way?

A: Yes, you can style your shag haircut in different ways. You can straighten your hair or create curls using a curling iron.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my shag haircut?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your shag haircut.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your shag haircut looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your shag haircut.

Conclusion

The shag haircut is the most popular hairstyle for 2021. It is a versatile style that can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type. With the right styling products and techniques, you can achieve a low-maintenance, effortless look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep your shag haircut looking its best.
