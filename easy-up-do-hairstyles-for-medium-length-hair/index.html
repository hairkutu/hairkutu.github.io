Easy Up Do Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Petter Jun 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle every day? Do you have medium length hair and don't know what to do with it? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing easy up do hairstyles for medium length hair that will have you looking stylish and feeling confident.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Up do hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, whether it be a formal event or just a casual day out. They can make you look put together and elegant without much effort. Medium length hair is also the perfect length for up do hairstyles, as it provides enough hair to work with while still being easy to manage.

How To

Before we get into the different hairstyles, it's important to know how to create an up do. Firstly, start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is too slippery, you can use some dry shampoo or texture spray to give it some grip. Gather your hair into a high ponytail, making sure it's smooth and secure. Twist the hair around the base of the ponytail and secure it in place with bobby pins. To finish off the look, you can add some hairspray to keep it in place.

Step By Step

Now that you know how to create a basic up do, let's get into some easy hairstyles you can try out:

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for any casual occasion. To achieve this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around the base of the ponytail. Secure it in place with bobby pins and pull out some pieces of hair around your face to frame it.

2. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still show off your locks. Divide your hair into two sections, with the top half being slightly smaller than the bottom half. Gather the top half of your hair and secure it in place with a hair tie or bobby pins.

3. Braided Up Do

The braided up do is a great way to add some texture and dimension to your hair. Start by creating a low ponytail and then braid it all the way down. Wrap the braid around the base of the ponytail and secure it in place with bobby pins.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect up do:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable
  • Don't be afraid to use hair accessories like headbands or clips to add some extra flair to your hairstyle
  • If you have trouble getting your up do to stay in place, try using some hairspray or hair texturizer

FAQ

Q: Can I do an up do with short hair?

A: While it's more difficult to create an up do with short hair, it's not impossible. You can try using hair extensions or hair accessories to add some length and volume to your hair.

Q: How long does it take to create an up do?

A: It depends on the complexity of the hairstyle and your skill level. Some up dos can be created in a matter of minutes, while others may take up to an hour.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you have some easy up do hairstyles to try out, you can switch up your hairstyle and add some variety to your look. With a little practice, you'll be able to create these hairstyles in no time.
