Short Haircuts With Long Bangs On One Side A Trendy And Chic Look

Written by Daniel Jul 27, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and modern haircut, short haircuts with long bangs on one side might be just what you need. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity among women who want to make a bold statement while keeping their hair low-maintenance. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this trendy haircut, including how to style it, tips for maintenance, and answers to common questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Haircuts with Long Bangs on One Side
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Short Haircut with Long Bangs on One Side
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut with Long Bangs on One Side
  • FAQs About Short Haircuts with Long Bangs on One Side
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts with Long Bangs on One Side

Description

Short haircuts with long bangs on one side are just what they sound like: a short haircut with one side of the hair longer than the other, usually with a dramatic sweeping bang. This haircut is versatile and can be customized to fit any face shape or hair type. The asymmetrical look is perfect for those who want a bold and edgy style, while the long bangs on one side add a touch of femininity and elegance.

How to Style Short Haircuts with Long Bangs on One Side

The beauty of this hairstyle is that it can be styled in many ways, depending on your mood or occasion. For a casual look, you can leave your hair naturally wavy or add some texture with a sea salt spray. For a more polished look, you can straighten your hair with a flat iron or blow dry it with a round brush. You can also experiment with different partings, such as a deep side part or a middle part, to create a different look.

Products You'll Need:

  • Sea salt spray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Flat iron
  • Round brush
  • Hairspray

Instructions:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  2. Apply a sea salt spray or texturizing spray to your damp hair and let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a round brush to blow dry it smooth.
  4. Create a deep side part and sweep the longer side of your hair to one side of your face.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Short Haircut with Long Bangs on One Side

If you're ready to take the plunge and get this trendy haircut, follow these steps for a flawless look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair will depend on the shape of your face and the look you're going for. Talk to your stylist about how short you want your hair and how long you want your bangs on one side.

Step 2: Decide on the Bangs

The bangs are the most important part of this haircut. Do you want them wispy or thick? Do you want them to fall over one eye or be pushed back? Your stylist can help you decide on the right bangs for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Your stylist will start by cutting your hair to the desired length. They will then cut the bangs to the length you want on one side and blend them into the rest of your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Your stylist will show you how to style your hair at home, so you can recreate the look on your own. They may recommend using a certain product or tool to achieve the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut with Long Bangs on One Side

Maintaining your short haircut with long bangs on one side is easy if you follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for short haircuts.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh and interesting.

FAQs About Short Haircuts with Long Bangs on One Side

Q: Will this haircut work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this haircut can be customized to fit any face shape. Your stylist will help you choose the right length and bangs for your face shape.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this haircut. Depending on the length of your hair, you may need to use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your hair in place.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts with Long Bangs on One Side

Short haircuts with long bangs on one side require minimal maintenance, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair ties or clips that can cause breakage or damage to your hair.
  • Cover your hair with a scarf or hat when exposed to harsh weather conditions.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short haircut with long bangs on one side and look effortlessly chic and stylish. Whether you're going for a casual or polished look, this hairstyle is versatile and perfect for any occasion. So go ahead and give it a try!
