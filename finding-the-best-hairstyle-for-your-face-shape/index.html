Finding The Best Hairstyle For Your Face Shape

Written by Christine Apr 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Have you ever left the salon feeling disappointed with your new haircut? You're not alone. Many people make the mistake of choosing a hairstyle simply because they like the way it looks on someone else, without considering their own face shape. But finding the best hairstyle for your face shape can be a game-changer. It can accentuate your best features and even make your face look slimmer or more balanced. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to find the best hairstyle for your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. Here are the common face shapes and how to identify them:

Oval

An oval face has balanced proportions with a slightly curved jawline. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.

Round

A round face has soft, curved lines with a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The length and width of the face are roughly the same.

Square

A square face has a strong jawline, a square chin, and a broad forehead. The width and length of the face are roughly the same.

Heart

A heart-shaped face has a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow jawline and chin. The face is wider at the temples and narrows at the jawline.

Diamond

A diamond-shaped face has a narrow forehead and jawline with wide cheekbones. The face is longer than it is wide.

Tips for Finding the Best Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find the best hairstyle for it. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Oval Face

An oval face can pull off almost any hairstyle. If you have an oval face, consider a layered cut, a side part, or bangs. Avoid styles that cover your forehead or make your face look too long.

Round Face

If you have a round face, go for hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown. Long, layered cuts and side-swept bangs can also be flattering. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs that make your face look rounder.

Square Face

If you have a square face, try softening your features with long layers or a side-swept fringe. Avoid blunt cuts and styles that end at your jawline, as they can make your face look boxy.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, consider a chin-length bob or a layered cut that falls below your shoulders. Side-swept bangs can also be flattering. Avoid styles that add volume at the top of your head, as they can make your forehead look wider.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, try a chin-length bob or a pixie cut with volume at the crown. Side-swept bangs can also be flattering. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your cheeks, as they can make your face look wider.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the best hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Choose products that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as it can damage your hair.

FAQ

Can I pull off a certain hairstyle even if it's not recommended for my face shape?

Of course! These tips are just guidelines to help you find the most flattering hairstyle for your face shape. If you love a particular hairstyle, go for it!

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking healthy.

What's the best way to prevent split ends?

Regular trims are the best way to prevent split ends. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Conclusion

Finding the best hairstyle for your face shape can be a game-changer. By following these tips, you can accentuate your best features and look your best. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and the right products to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Happy hair styling!
