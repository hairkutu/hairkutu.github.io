Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Ponytail The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that is easy to maintain? Look no further than the mens haircut short sides long top ponytail. This versatile hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair short and tidy on the sides, while having a longer and more versatile top section that can be styled into a ponytail or other trendy looks. In this guide, we will walk you through how to achieve this haircut, provide tips on styling and hair care, and answer some common questions about this popular hairstyle.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Ponytail

The mens haircut short sides long top ponytail is a relatively easy hairstyle to achieve, but it does require some precision and skill. Here are the steps to get this look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in achieving this haircut is to decide on the length of the top section. Most men opt for a length that falls between 2-4 inches, but it ultimately depends on your personal preference and hair type. You also need to decide on the length of the sides, which should be significantly shorter than the top section.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Using clippers, trim the sides of your hair to the desired length. Most men opt for a fade or undercut, which gradually tapers the hair from short to long as it moves up the head. Make sure to blend the sides seamlessly into the longer top section to create a natural and polished look.

Step 3: Cut and Style the Top Section

Trim the top section of your hair to the desired length, and then style it into a ponytail or other trendy look. The key to this hairstyle is keeping the top section longer and more versatile, so feel free to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

Tips for Styling and Hair Care

Now that you have achieved the mens haircut short sides long top ponytail, it's important to take care of your hair and find the right styling products to maintain your look. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Invest in high-quality styling products that will help you achieve the look you want. Consider using a strong hold gel or wax to keep the top section of your hair in place, and a light pomade or cream to add shine and texture to the ponytail.

Tip 2: Trim Regularly

To maintain your haircut, you should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks. This will help to keep the sides and top section looking polished and tidy, and prevent split ends and other damage.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools. You should also avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about the mens haircut short sides long top ponytail:

Q: Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How long does it take to style this haircut?

A: This depends on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the styling products you use. On average, it takes about 10-15 minutes to style this haircut.

Q: Can I wear my hair down instead of in a ponytail?

A: Absolutely! The beauty of this haircut is that it's versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can wear your hair down, slicked back, or in a half-up, half-down style.

Hair Care

To maintain your mens haircut short sides long top ponytail, it's important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals. This will help to keep your hair and scalp healthy and moisturized.

Tip 2: Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and manageable. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF. This will help to prevent sun damage and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Now that you have all the information you need about the mens haircut short sides long top ponytail, it's time to book your appointment with a skilled hairstylist and try out this trendy and versatile look for yourself!
