How To Style Long Thick Frizzy Wavy Hair

If you have long thick frizzy wavy hair, then you know how difficult it can be to style it. But fear not, with the right techniques and products, you can tame your wild locks and create a beautiful hairstyle that will turn heads. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style long thick frizzy wavy hair in a relaxed English language.

How to Style Long Thick Frizzy Wavy Hair

Styling long thick frizzy wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right approach, you can make it work. Here are some tips to help you style your hair:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step in styling long thick frizzy wavy hair is to use the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as those that are formulated for curly or wavy hair. These products will help to tame frizz and define your waves, making them easier to style.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help soften and detangle your locks. This will make it easier to comb or brush your hair and prevent breakage.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing or brushing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your hair. A wide-tooth comb will gently detangle your hair without causing breakage.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

For a sleek and polished look, blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to smooth out any frizz and add volume to your hair.

5. Apply a Heat Protectant Spray

If you are using heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray first. This will help to protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling.

6. Use a Curling Iron

If you want to add some curls to your hair, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Choose a barrel size that is appropriate for your hair length and thickness.

7. Finish with Hairspray

To set your style in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style long thick frizzy wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly or wavy hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner.
  3. Comb your hair with a wide-tooth comb to detangle it.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to smooth out any frizz and add volume.
  5. Apply a heat protectant spray if you are using heat styling tools.
  6. Use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Thick Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your long thick frizzy wavy hair:

  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and curling iron to make styling easier.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Try different hairstyles, such as braids or updos, to give your hair a break from heat styling.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I wash my long thick frizzy wavy hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

2. How can I prevent frizz in my long thick frizzy wavy hair?

Use a leave-in conditioner and a heat protectant spray before styling your hair. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz.

3. How can I add volume to my long thick frizzy wavy hair?

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray.

Hair Care Tips for Long Thick Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your long thick frizzy wavy hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a high heat setting as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as this can cause breakage.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.

Styling long thick frizzy wavy hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful and sleek hairstyle that will turn heads. Use these tips and tricks to tame your wild locks and show off your natural waves.
