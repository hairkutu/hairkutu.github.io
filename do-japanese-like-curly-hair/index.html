Do Japanese Like Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 25, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're a curly-haired person who's planning to visit Japan or move there permanently, you might be wondering: do Japanese people like curly hair? This question is important as it can affect your self-esteem and confidence. In this guide, we'll explore the topic in-depth and provide you with tips on how to care for your curly hair in Japan.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly hair is not very common in Japan, but it's not unheard of. Japanese people have a preference for straight hair, which is why there are many hair straightening treatments available in salons all over Japan. However, this does not mean that Japanese people don't like curly hair. In fact, many Japanese people find curly hair cute and attractive.

When it comes to fashion and beauty, Japanese people are known for their open-mindedness and willingness to try new things. You'll see people with all sorts of hairstyles in Tokyo, including curly hair. So, if you have curly hair, don't be afraid to show it off!

How To

If you're planning to visit Japan and have curly hair, there are a few things you can do to ensure that your hair looks its best:

1. Bring the Right Hair Products

It's important to bring the right hair products with you to Japan, especially if you have curly hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl defining creams and leave-in conditioners. These products will help keep your curls looking bouncy and defined.

2. Use a Diffuser

If you're staying in a hotel, chances are they'll have a hairdryer in your room. However, most hotel hairdryers in Japan do not come with a diffuser attachment, which is essential for curly hair. A diffuser helps distribute the heat evenly and prevents frizz. Consider bringing your own diffuser attachment or purchasing one in Japan.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible, especially if you're in Japan during the summer months. The humidity can cause your hair to frizz, and heat styling will only make it worse. Instead, embrace your natural curls and opt for low-maintenance hairstyles.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to care for your curly hair in Japan:

1. Shampoo and Condition

Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they will help keep your curls looking bouncy and defined. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

2. Apply Curl Defining Cream

Once you've washed and conditioned your hair, apply a curl defining cream to your damp hair. This will help define your curls and keep them looking bouncy. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hairdryer and set it to low heat. Flip your head upside down and use the diffuser to dry your hair. This will help distribute the heat evenly and prevent frizz.

4. Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, scrunch it to help define your curls. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for your curly hair in Japan:

1. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, especially if you have curly hair. Consider wearing a hat or using a UV protecting spray to protect your hair from the sun's rays.

2. Embrace the Humidity

The humidity in Japan can be a challenge for curly hair, but it can also work in your favor. Embrace the humidity and let your curls do their thing. You might be surprised at how good they look!

3. Get a Haircut

If you're in Japan for an extended period of time, consider getting a haircut. Look for a salon that specializes in curly hair and ask for a stylist who has experience with curly hair. A good haircut can make all the difference!

FAQ

Q: Do Japanese people like curly hair?

A: Japanese people have a preference for straight hair, but many find curly hair cute and attractive.

Q: Can I find curly hair products in Japan?

A: Yes, you can find curly hair products in Japan, but they might be harder to find than in other countries. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Q: Should I straighten my hair if I'm visiting Japan?

A: It's up to you! If you feel more comfortable with straight hair, by all means, go for it. However, if you love your curly hair, embrace it!

Hair Care

Caring for curly hair in Japan can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your curls looking bouncy and defined. Remember to bring the right hair products with you, use a diffuser when drying your hair, and avoid heat styling as much as possible. Embrace your natural curls and don't be afraid to show them off!
