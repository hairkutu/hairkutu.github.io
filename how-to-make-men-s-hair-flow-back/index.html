How To Make Men s Hair Flow Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling flat and lifeless? Do you want to achieve that effortlessly cool, flowy look that seems to be all the rage these days? Look no further! In this article, we'll show you how to make men's hair flow back in just a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair
  • Step 2: Apply a styling product
  • Step 3: Use a hair dryer
  • Step 4: Finish with hairspray
  • Tips for Making Men's Hair Flow Back
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Men's Flowing Hair

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

The first step to achieving that flowy look is to start with clean, damp hair. This will help you avoid any unwanted frizz or flyaways. To achieve this, simply wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner and pat it dry with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. This can be anything from a mousse to a gel or wax, depending on your hair type and desired look. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and tips. This will help add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 3: Use a hair dryer

Once you've applied your styling product, it's time to use a hair dryer. Using a hair dryer will help set your hair in place and add volume. Start by using your fingers to comb your hair back, away from your face. Then, using a round brush, blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to flow.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Finally, finish off your look with a light spritz of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place throughout the day and prevent any unwanted movement. Be sure to choose a hairspray that is lightweight and won't leave your hair feeling crunchy or stiff.

Tips for Making Men's Hair Flow Back

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve that flowy look:

  • Choose a styling product that works for your hair type. If you have fine hair, opt for a lightweight mousse. If you have thick hair, a wax or pomade may work better for you.
  • Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a high-quality hair dryer and round brush for best results.
  • Experiment with different partings and styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

How long does it take to make men's hair flow back?

The entire process should take no more than 10-15 minutes, depending on your hair type and desired look.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you may need to use a straightening iron to achieve the flowy look. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

What if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, you can still achieve the flowy look by using a lightweight styling product and blow drying your hair in the direction you want it to flow.

Hair Care for Men's Flowing Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain that flowy look. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straightening irons and curling wands too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

And there you have it! With these simple steps and tips, you'll be able to make men's hair flow back in no time. So go ahead and give it a try - you might just surprise yourself with how great you look!
