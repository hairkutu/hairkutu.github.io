How To Cut Short Pixie Haircuts A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and want to try something new? Short pixie haircuts are a great way to switch up your look and add some edge to your style. However, cutting your own hair can be intimidating, especially when it comes to a short cut. But fear not, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a chic and stylish pixie cut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut short pixie haircuts and give you tips on how to care for your new 'do.

How to Cut Short Pixie Haircuts

Before we begin, let's gather the tools you'll need for this haircut:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Trimming shears (optional)

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before beginning the haircut. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of your hair, making it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start by sectioning the top layer of hair from the bottom layer. Then, separate the hair at the crown of your head and clip it up. This will be the last section you cut as it will determine the overall length of your pixie cut.

Step 3: Start cutting

Begin cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and begin cutting in a straight line. Continue cutting small sections of hair until the entire bottom layer is complete.

Step 4: Cut the sides

Move on to the side sections of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the side and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut in a diagonal line towards the back of your head. Repeat this process until both sides are complete.

Step 5: Cut the top layer

Unclip the crown section of your hair and begin cutting it to your desired length. This will be the longest section of your pixie cut. Comb the hair straight up and hold it between your fingers. Cut in a straight line and continue until the entire section is complete.

Step 6: Check for balance

Once you've completed the haircut, check for balance by comparing both sides of your hair. Make any necessary adjustments to even out the length.

Tips for Cutting Short Pixie Haircuts

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting short pixie haircuts:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter if you're unsure about the length.
  • Use trimming shears to add texture and movement to your pixie cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it will shrink when it dries.
  • Choose a style that complements your face shape.

FAQs About Cutting Short Pixie Haircuts

Q: Can I cut my own hair if I'm not a professional?

A: Yes, with the right tools and techniques, you can cut your own hair. However, if you're unsure about cutting your hair, it's best to seek out a professional stylist.

Q: How often should I cut my short pixie haircut?

A: Short haircuts require more maintenance than longer styles. It's recommended to trim your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and length.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Haircuts

Short haircuts require less maintenance than longer styles, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short pixie haircuts:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your pixie cut.

Cutting short pixie haircuts can be a fun and daring way to switch up your style. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a chic and stylish look from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and getting regular trims to keep your pixie cut looking its best.
