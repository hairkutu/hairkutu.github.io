Hairstyles For Diamond Face Shape Male Thick Hair The Ultimate Guide

If you have a diamond face shape and thick hair, you may be wondering what hairstyles will complement your features. The good news is, there are plenty of options that will make you look your best. In this guide, we will go over some of the best hairstyles for diamond face shape male thick hair, as well as tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents:

  • What is a Diamond Face Shape?

What is a Diamond Face Shape?

A diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. Men with this face shape typically have a sharp and angular appearance, with a prominent jawline.

When it comes to hairstyles, the goal is to soften the angular features and create balance. This can be achieved with the right cut and styling techniques.

Hairstyles for Diamond Face Shape Male Thick Hair

Here are some of the best hairstyles for diamond face shape male thick hair:

1. Short Sides and Long Top

This is a classic men's haircut that works well for diamond face shapes. The short sides and back help to balance out the wide cheekbones, while the long top adds volume and texture. This style can be worn with a side part or slicked back for a more polished look.

2. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a low-maintenance style that is perfect for thick hair. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer and textured. This style can be worn with a fringe or swept to the side.

3. Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a great option for men who want a more relaxed and casual look. This style involves leaving the hair longer on top and using a styling product to create volume and texture. The sides can be faded or tapered for a more polished look.

4. Side Swept Fringe

The side swept fringe is a versatile style that can be worn with any length of hair. It involves sweeping the hair to one side and creating a fringe that covers the forehead. This style can be worn with a fade or undercut for a more dramatic look.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you have chosen a hairstyle that works for your diamond face shape and thick hair, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.
  • Finish with a hairspray or hair wax to hold your style in place.

Tips for Hair Care and Maintenance

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a diamond face shape male?

The best haircut for a diamond face shape male is one that balances out the wide cheekbones and creates a sense of proportion. Short sides and long top, textured crop, messy quiff, and side swept fringe are all great options.

How do I style my hair for a diamond face shape?

To style your hair for a diamond face shape, choose a hairstyle that works well with your features and use a high-quality styling product to create volume and texture. Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go and use a comb or brush to style it.

How can I take care of my thick hair?

To take care of your thick hair, wash it regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid using hot tools too often, get regular haircuts, and eat a healthy diet.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your diamond face shape and thick hair, and look your best every day!
