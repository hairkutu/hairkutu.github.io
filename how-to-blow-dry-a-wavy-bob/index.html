How To Blow Dry A Wavy Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Blow drying your wavy bob can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure where to start. However, with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a salon-worthy blowout in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of blow drying a wavy bob, step-by-step.

Table of Contents

In this article, we'll cover:

  • How to Blow Dry a Wavy Bob
  • Tips for a Perfect Blowout
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Bobs

How to Blow Dry a Wavy Bob

Before we dive into the steps, make sure you have the following tools and products:

  • Blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle
  • Round brush
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Styling cream or mousse

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray all over your hair. Distribute a small amount of styling cream or mousse evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start with the bottom section and work your way up. Use a round brush to smooth out each section of hair before blow drying.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Attach the concentrator nozzle to your blow dryer and set it to a medium heat setting. Starting with the bottom section, place the round brush at the roots and pull it downwards while following it with the blow dryer. Repeat this process until the section is dry and smooth. Continue with each section of hair until your entire head is dry.

Step 4: Finish

Once your hair is dry, use a cool shot button on your blow dryer to set the style. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for a Perfect Blowout

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect blowout:

  • Invest in a high-quality blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle.
  • Use a round brush to smooth out each section of hair before blow drying.
  • Start with the bottom section and work your way up.
  • Use a cool shot button on your blow dryer to set the style.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

FAQs

How often should I blow dry my hair?

Blow drying your hair every day can cause damage and dryness. It's best to limit blow drying to 2-3 times a week.

Do I need to use a heat protectant spray?

Yes, using a heat protectant spray before blow drying can help protect your hair from heat damage.

Can I blow dry my hair without a round brush?

A round brush can help smooth out your hair and create volume, but it's not essential. You can use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume instead.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Bobs

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your wavy bob:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Use a hydrating hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Blow drying your wavy bob can be a simple and enjoyable process with the right technique and tools. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a salon-worthy blowout in the comfort of your own home.
