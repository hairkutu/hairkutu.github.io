How To Have Long Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 14, 2023 · 6 min read

Long wavy hair is a timeless and beautiful hairstyle that never goes out of fashion. Whether you have naturally curly hair or straight hair, you can achieve long wavy locks with the right techniques and products. In this guide, we will show you how to have long wavy hair in a relaxed and easy-to-follow way. From how to style your hair to how to care for it, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Have Long Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Wavy Hair

How to Have Long Wavy Hair

The first step to having long wavy hair is to grow it out. Depending on your hair type and growth rate, this may take several months or even years. However, there are several things you can do to promote healthy hair growth:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid heat styling and harsh chemicals
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

Once your hair has reached your desired length, you can start styling it into long waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create long wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair
  3. Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips
  4. Use a curling iron to curl each section of hair away from your face
  5. Hold each curl for 10-15 seconds before releasing
  6. Continue curling each section until your entire head is curled
  7. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the waves in place

And voila! You now have long, beautiful waves.

Tips for Maintaining Long Wavy Hair

Maintaining long wavy hair requires a bit of effort. Follow these tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet to prevent breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve long wavy hair without using heat?

A: Yes! You can braid your hair before bed and leave the braids in overnight for natural waves. You can also use a sea salt spray to create beachy waves.

Q: How often should I wash my long wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people can go several days without washing their hair, while others prefer to wash it every other day. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I dye my long wavy hair?

A: Yes, but it’s important to use a gentle, ammonia-free hair dye and to deep condition your hair regularly to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Long Wavy Hair

Follow these hair care tips to keep your long wavy hair looking its best:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and techniques, you can easily achieve long wavy hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Whether you’re going for a casual beachy look or an elegant evening style, long wavy hair is versatile and always in style.
