How To Choose The Best Hair Color For Your Skin Tone

Written by William Apr 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we'll show you how to determine your skin tone and choose the best hair color to complement it. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Best Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones
  • Best Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones
  • Best Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the best hair color for your skin tone is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral.

Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, your skin will have a yellow or golden undertone. You may also have green veins on your wrist.

Some examples of warm skin tones are BeyoncÃ©, Jennifer Lopez, and Jessica Alba.

Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, your skin will have a pink or blue undertone. You may also have blue veins on your wrist.

Some examples of cool skin tones are Rihanna, Lupita Nyong'o, and Emma Stone.

Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, your skin will have a mix of both warm and cool undertones. Your veins may appear blue-green.

Some examples of neutral skin tones are Kerry Washington, Angelina Jolie, and Mila Kunis.

Best Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, you'll want to stick with warm hair colors. Some great options include:

  • Golden blonde
  • Honey blonde
  • Copper
  • Auburn
  • Warm brown

Avoid cool hair colors like platinum blonde, ash blonde, and jet black.

Best Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, you'll want to stick with cool hair colors. Some great options include:

  • Platinum blonde
  • Ash blonde
  • Jet black
  • Cool brown
  • Blue-black

Avoid warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, and auburn.

Best Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you're lucky because you can pull off both warm and cool hair colors. Some great options include:

  • Caramel
  • Chocolate brown
  • Burgundy
  • Warm blonde
  • Ash brown

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you've chosen the perfect hair color for your skin tone, it's important to take care of your colored hair to keep it looking its best.

Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid hot showers and heat styling
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

FAQs

What if I have a tan?

If you have a tan, your skin tone may appear warmer than it actually is. Keep this in mind when choosing a hair color.

What if I want to go bold?

If you want to go for a bold hair color, go for it! Just make sure it complements your skin tone.

Can I dye my hair at home?

You can dye your hair at home, but it's recommended to go to a professional to ensure the best results.

Choosing the best hair color for your skin tone doesn't have to be overwhelming. With our tips, you'll be sure to find the perfect shade for you. Remember to take care of your colored hair to keep it looking its best. Happy coloring!
