How To See What Hairstyle Looks Best On You A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine May 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle but you're not sure what will suit you, it can be a daunting task. With so many styles to choose from, it's hard to know where to start. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to see what hairstyle looks best on you, step-by-step. From determining your face shape to considering your hair type, we'll provide you with all the tips and tricks you need to find your perfect look.

  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding your perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you narrow down the styles that will complement your features. There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Use a measuring tape to measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure the widest part of your forehead, just above your eyebrows.
  • Cheekbones: Measure the widest part of your cheekbones.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the bottom of your earlobe, then double that measurement.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, you can use them to determine your face shape. There are many online tools and resources that can help you with this.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Once you've determined your face shape, you need to consider your hair type. Your hair type will determine which styles will work best for you. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily.

Straight hair is smooth and shiny, while wavy hair has a slight wave to it. Curly hair has tight curls or ringlets, and coily hair has a tight, kinky curl pattern. Knowing your hair type will help you choose a style that will work with your natural texture.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Now that you know your face shape and hair type, it's time to start looking for inspiration. Browse through magazines, social media, and celebrity photos to find styles that you like. Pay attention to the styles that are popular for your face shape and hair type.

When you find a style that you like, save the photo or bookmark the page so that you can show it to your hairstylist later.

Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist

Once you have a few styles in mind, it's time to consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you determine which styles will work best for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Be sure to bring your inspiration photos with you to the salon. Your hairstylist can use these photos to create a customized look that will suit you.

Tips for Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

  • Consider your lifestyle: Choose a style that will work with your daily routine.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment: Try different styles until you find one that you love.
  • Be realistic: Choose a style that you can maintain and that will work with your natural texture.
  • Consider the season: Choose a style that will work for the current season.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and the style you choose. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you're not happy with your new hairstyle, talk to your hairstylist. They may be able to make adjustments or suggest a different style that will work better for you.

What if I can't afford to go to a salon?

There are many affordable options for haircuts, such as beauty schools or salons that offer discounted services. You can also try cutting your hair at home, but be sure to do your research and use caution.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Hairstyle

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your style.

By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to find a hairstyle that will suit you perfectly. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a style, and don't be afraid to try something new. With a little bit of experimentation and the help of a professional hairstylist, you'll be able to find your perfect look.
