How To Do Upstyles For Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Upstyles are a great way to show off your long hair, and they're perfect for any occasion. Whether you're attending a wedding, a prom, or just want to switch up your everyday look, mastering the art of upstyles is a must. In this post, we'll guide you through the steps to achieve some stunning upstyles for your long hair.

How to Do Upstyles for Long Hair

Before we get started, here are some essential tips to make sure your upstyle turns out perfectly:

Tips

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use hair products such as mousse or hairspray to add texture and hold
  • Don't be afraid to use hair accessories such as pins, clips, or headbands
  • Have a mirror nearby so you can check your progress as you go

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by combing your hair thoroughly to eliminate any tangles or knots. Then, apply a small amount of mousse or hairspray to add texture and hold.

Step 2: Choose Your Upstyle

There are countless upstyles to choose from, including buns, braids, and twists. Choose a style that suits the occasion and your personal taste.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable portions, starting from the crown of your head. Use clips to secure each section as you go.

Step 4: Create Your Upstyle

Follow the steps for your chosen upstyle. For example, if you're creating a bun, twist your hair into a tight coil and secure it with pins. If you're doing a braid, separate your hair into three sections and weave them together.

Step 5: Secure Your Upstyle

Once you've created your upstyle, use pins, clips, or elastic bands to hold it in place. Make sure it's secure and won't fall out during the day or night.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

To add extra hold and keep your upstyle in place, finish with a spritz of hairspray.

FAQ

How long does an upstyle take to create?

It depends on the complexity of the upstyle you choose. Simple styles such as buns or ponytails can be done in a matter of minutes, while more intricate styles like braids or twists may take longer.

Can I create an upstyle with short hair?

While some upstyles may be difficult to achieve with shorter hair, there are still plenty of options available. Try a half-up, half-down style or experiment with small braids or twists.

How do I keep my upstyle in place all day or night?

Use plenty of hairpins and clips to secure your upstyle, and finish with a spritz of hairspray. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause it to loosen or fall out.

Hair Care

To keep your long hair healthy and strong, make sure you're using quality hair products and avoiding harsh chemicals. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to create stunning upstyles for your long hair in no time. Whether you're attending a special event or just want to switch up your everyday look, upstyles are a versatile and stylish option.
