Best Products To Slick Back 4C Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Mar 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're someone with 4C hair, you know how challenging it can be to slick it back. Whether you're trying to achieve a sleek ponytail or a low bun, finding the right product to hold your hair in place can be a struggle. In this article, we'll cover the best products to slick back 4C hair and provide tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Slick Back 4C Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Back 4C Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for 4C Hair

Description

Slicking back 4C hair requires a product that can hold the hair in place without causing damage or breakage. The best products for this hair type are those that provide a strong hold while also moisturizing and nourishing the hair. Some of the best products for slicking back 4C hair include gels, pomades, and edge controls.

How to Slick Back 4C Hair

Slicking back 4C hair can be achieved in a few simple steps. First, you'll need to gather the necessary products, which include a comb, brush, and the hair product of your choice. Next, you'll want to section your hair and apply the product to each section, making sure to distribute it evenly. Use the comb or brush to smooth the hair back and create the desired look. Finally, you can use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to slicking back 4C hair:

  1. Start with clean, detangled hair
  2. Section your hair into small sections
  3. Apply your chosen product to each section
  4. Use a comb or brush to smooth the hair back
  5. Secure the style in place with a hair tie or bobby pins

Tips for Slicking Back 4C Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back style:

  • Use a product that provides a strong hold
  • Don't apply too much product, as this can weigh your hair down
  • Use a comb or brush to smooth the hair back, starting from the roots
  • Consider using a silk or satin scarf to wrap your hair at night to maintain the style

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back 4C hair:

What type of product should I use to slick back 4C hair?

The best products for slicking back 4C hair include gels, pomades, and edge controls.

How much product should I use?

Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing your hair down.

Can I slick back my hair every day?

Yes, but make sure to use a product that nourishes and moisturizes your hair to avoid breakage.

Hair Care for 4C Hair

To maintain healthy 4C hair, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, deep conditioning, and using products that nourish and moisturize your hair. Avoid using heat tools and harsh chemicals, and consider protective styles to minimize breakage.

In conclusion, slicking back 4C hair can be achieved with the right product and technique. Consider using gels, pomades, or edge controls to create the perfect look, and follow a proper hair care routine to maintain healthy hair.
