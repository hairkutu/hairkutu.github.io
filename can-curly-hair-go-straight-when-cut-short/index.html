Can Curly Hair Go Straight When Cut Short

Written by Oliver Sep 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have curly hair and are thinking of cutting it short, you may be wondering if your curls will go straight. The short answer is no, cutting your curly hair short will not make it straight. However, there are ways to achieve a straight hairstyle with short curly hair. In this blog post, we will explore how to straighten curly hair when cut short, along with tips and FAQs.

Description

Curly hair is made up of a unique curl pattern that is determined by the shape of the hair follicle. Cutting your curly hair short does not change the shape of the hair follicle, which means your curls will remain intact. However, the weight of long hair can sometimes pull curls down, making them appear looser than they actually are. When you cut your hair short, the weight is no longer there to pull your curls down, which can result in more defined curls.

How to Straighten Curly Hair When Cut Short

If you want to achieve a straight hairstyle with short curly hair, there are several options available:

Blow Drying

Blow drying your hair straight is a quick and easy way to achieve a straight hairstyle with short curly hair. Start by applying a heat protectant to your hair, then use a round brush and blow dryer to smooth out your curls. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to further smooth out any remaining curls.

Flat Ironing

Using a flat iron is another way to straighten short curly hair. Start by applying a heat protectant to your hair, then use a flat iron to straighten small sections of hair at a time. Be sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Keratin Treatment

A keratin treatment is a semi-permanent way to straighten curly hair. It involves applying a keratin solution to your hair and then using heat to seal it in. The result is smooth, frizz-free hair that lasts for several months.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to straighten curly hair when cut short:

  1. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair straight using a round brush and blow dryer.
  3. Use a flat iron to further straighten any remaining curls.
  4. Apply a smoothing serum to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.

Tips for Straightening Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when straightening curly hair:

  • Always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a low heat setting on your flat iron to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it appear greasy.
  • Consider getting a professional keratin treatment for a long-lasting, semi-permanent straight hairstyle.

FAQs

Can I straighten my curly hair without heat?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair without heat by using a straightening serum or cream. Apply the product to your hair and then use a round brush to smooth out your curls. Once your hair is dry, it should be straighter and smoother.

How often should I straighten my curly hair?

Straightening your curly hair too often can cause damage and breakage. It is best to limit heat styling to once or twice a week, and always use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

What is the best way to care for curly hair that has been straightened?

After straightening your curly hair, it is important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. You should also avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to add moisture and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as this can cause damage to your hair.

In conclusion, cutting your curly hair short will not make it straight, but there are ways to achieve a straight hairstyle with short curly hair. Whether you choose to blow dry, flat iron, or get a keratin treatment, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. With the right care and styling techniques, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.
