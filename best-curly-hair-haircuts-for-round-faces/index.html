Best Curly Hair Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Bardi Jun 02, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut for your round face? Well, you’re in luck because we’ve compiled a list of the best curly hair haircuts for round faces. In this article, we’ll cover everything from how to choose the perfect haircut to hair care tips to keep your curls looking their best. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Perfect Curly Haircut for Round Faces
  • Short Curly Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Long Curly Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Layered Curly Haircuts for Round Faces
  • FAQs
  • Curly Hair Care Tips

How to Choose the Perfect Curly Haircut for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing the perfect curly haircut for round faces, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long
  • Opt for haircuts that add height to the crown of your head
  • Choose haircuts that have layers to add movement and dimension
  • Avoid haircuts that have blunt ends

By keeping these tips in mind, you’ll be able to choose a curly haircut that flatters your round face and accentuates your best features.

Short Curly Haircuts for Round Faces

If you’re looking to go short, there are several curly haircuts that are perfect for round faces:

Bob

A curly bob is a great option for a round face. The key is to keep the length just below the chin and add layers to create movement and volume.

Pixie

A curly pixie cut is another great option for a round face. Keep the length short and add layers to create volume and texture.

Long Curly Haircuts for Round Faces

If you prefer longer hair, there are still plenty of curly haircuts that will flatter your round face:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for curly hair. They add movement and volume while still keeping the length of the hair.

Beach Waves

Beach waves are a great option for a round face. They add texture and volume without overwhelming your face.

Layered Curly Haircuts for Round Faces

Layered haircuts are great for curly hair because they add movement and dimension. Here are a few layered haircuts that are perfect for round faces:

Curly Shag

A curly shag is a great option for a round face. It adds layers and texture while still keeping the length of the hair.

Long Layered Bob

A long layered bob is another great option for a round face. It adds movement and volume while still keeping the length of the hair.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face with curly hair?

The best haircut for a round face with curly hair is one that adds height to the crown of your head and has layers to add movement and dimension.

Should I avoid blunt haircuts if I have a round face?

Yes, blunt haircuts can make your face look wider. It’s best to choose haircuts that have layers to add movement and dimension.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Now that you’ve chosen the perfect curly haircut for your round face, it’s important to take care of your curls. Here are a few tips to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat on your hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated

By following these tips, you’ll be able to keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

We hope this article has helped you find the perfect curly haircut for your round face. Remember to keep in mind the tips we’ve shared, and take care of your curls with our hair care tips. With the right haircut and proper care, your curly hair will look amazing!
