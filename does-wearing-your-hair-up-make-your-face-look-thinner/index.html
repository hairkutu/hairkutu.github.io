Does Wearing Your Hair Up Make Your Face Look Thinner

Written by Eveline Jun 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you've ever looked in the mirror and wished your face looked slimmer, you're not alone. Many people want to achieve a more defined jawline and cheekbones. But did you know that the way you wear your hair can also affect the appearance of your face? In this article, we'll answer the question: does wearing your hair up make your face look thinner? We'll also provide tips on how to achieve this look and take care of your hair in the process.

Description

Wearing your hair up can create an illusion of a slimmer face. This is because it pulls the hair away from your face, making your facial features more visible. Additionally, when your hair is up, it can make your neck appear longer and more slender, which can also contribute to a thinner-looking face.

How To

If you're looking to try out this look, here are some tips:

1. Find the right hairstyle

Not all updos are created equal. Some styles can actually make your face look wider or rounder. Look for styles that elongate your face, such as a high ponytail or a top knot.

2. Use hair accessories

Adding hair accessories like headbands or clips can also help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Look for accessories that draw attention to the top of your head, rather than the sides.

3. Experiment with different partings

The way you part your hair can also affect the appearance of your face. A middle parting can create symmetry and make your face appear slimmer. Alternatively, a side parting can help create volume and balance out a round face.

Step by Step

Here's how to achieve a slimmer face with an updo:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. This will help your hair stay in place and prevent it from getting oily throughout the day.

Step 2: Brush your hair back

Using a brush, sweep your hair back and away from your face. Secure it with a hair tie or clip.

Step 3: Create volume

If you want to create more volume, tease the hair at the crown of your head. This will help elongate your face.

Step 4: Add accessories

Finish off your look with some hair accessories, such as a headband or clips. These can help draw attention to the top of your head and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

1. Don't pull your hair too tight

Pulling your hair too tight can actually make your face look wider. Make sure your updo is secure, but not overly tight.

2. Keep your makeup light

Heavy makeup can detract from the slimming effect of an updo. Stick to natural-looking makeup to keep the focus on your hair.

3. Experiment with different styles

Not everyone's face is the same shape, so it's important to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Does wearing your hair up make your face look thinner?

Yes, wearing your hair up can create an illusion of a slimmer face by pulling the hair away from your face and elongating your neck.

What are some updo styles that can make my face look thinner?

Styles that elongate your face, such as a high ponytail or a top knot, can help create a slimmer appearance.

What accessories should I use to create a slimmer face?

Accessories that draw attention to the top of your head, such as headbands or clips, can help create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Hair Care

1. Don't wear your hair up too often

Wearing your hair up too often can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Make sure to give your hair a break and wear it down sometimes.

2. Use a good quality hair tie

A hair tie that's too tight or has metal parts can cause damage to your hair. Look for a hair tie that's gentle on your hair.

3. Don't forget about hair care

Just because your hair is up doesn't mean you can neglect hair care. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly and use heat protectant when styling.

In conclusion, wearing your hair up can create an illusion of a slimmer face. By finding the right hairstyle, using hair accessories, and experimenting with different partings, you can achieve this look. Just remember to take care of your hair in the process and give it a break from being up too often.
