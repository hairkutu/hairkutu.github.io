Will Fringe Suit Round Face

Description

Having a round face can be a challenge when it comes to choosing a hairstyle. Fringe, also known as bangs, is a popular hairstyle that many people love. But will fringe suit round face? The answer is yes, it can. However, it is important to choose the right type of fringe that complements your round face shape.

How to Determine if Fringe Suits Round Face

The first step in determining if fringe suits round face is to identify your face shape. A round face is characterized by having a circular shape with a width and length that are almost equal. The cheeks are usually full, and the jawline is soft and rounded. If you have a round face, you can still rock a fringe as long as you choose the right type.

Opt for a side-swept fringe rather than a straight-across fringe. This will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. You can also try a wispy fringe that is tapered at the edges. This will help to soften your features and create a more balanced look.

Step by Step Guide on How to Get Fringe

If you've decided that fringe is the right hairstyle for you, here's a step by step guide on how to get it:

  1. Decide on the type of fringe you want. Remember to choose a side-swept or wispy fringe for round face.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to ensure that the fringe complements your round face.
  3. Wash and dry your hair before getting the fringe cut.
  4. Section off the hair that you want to be cut into the fringe.
  5. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length and shape.
  6. Style the fringe using a round brush and blow dryer. You can also use a flat iron to straighten the fringe.

Tips on Styling Fringe with Round Face

Here are some tips on how to style your fringe with round face:

  • Use a round brush to blow dry your fringe in the direction that you want it to fall.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to keep your fringe in place.
  • Avoid cutting your fringe too short as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Keep your fringe trimmed regularly to avoid it becoming too long and covering your eyes.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the ones that work best with your fringe and round face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fringe with round face:

Can I get a straight-across fringe with round face?

You can, but it is not recommended as it can make your face appear wider. Opt for a side-swept or wispy fringe instead.

What is the best length for fringe with round face?

The best length for fringe with round face is just below the eyebrows or at cheekbone level. This will help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

Can I style my fringe with round face in different ways?

Yes, you can. Experiment with different hairstyles such as ponytails and buns to find the ones that work best with your fringe and round face.

Hair Care Tips for Fringe with Round Face

Here are some hair care tips for fringe with round face:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as this can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using a flat iron.
  • Trim your fringe regularly to keep it in shape and avoid split ends.
  • Avoid pulling your fringe back tightly as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

So, will fringe suit round face? Absolutely, as long as you choose the right type of fringe and style it correctly. Follow these tips and tricks to rock your fringe and create a more balanced and flattering look for your round face.
