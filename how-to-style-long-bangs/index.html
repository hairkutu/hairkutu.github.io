How To Style Long Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long bangs can be a versatile and stylish addition to any haircut, but styling them can be tricky. Whether you're going for a messy, boho look or a sleek, polished style, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to style long bangs and give you some tips for keeping them looking great.

How to Style Long Bangs

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Before you start styling your long bangs, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be harder to control and style.

Step 2: Prep your bangs with a heat protectant.

If you're using heat tools to style your long bangs, it's important to protect your hair from damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your bangs before using a blow dryer or flat iron.

Step 3: Blow dry your bangs.

Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to lay. If you want a sleek, polished look, use a flat brush instead.

Step 4: Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks.

If your bangs are still a little unruly after blow drying, use a flat iron to smooth them out. Be sure to use a heat protectant before using a flat iron.

Step 5: Add texture with a texturizing spray.

If you want a messy, tousled look, use a texturizing spray to add some texture to your bangs. Use your fingers to work the spray through your bangs and style them as desired.

Tips for Styling Long Bangs

Tip 1: Don't overuse heat tools.

Using heat tools too often can damage your hair and make it harder to style. Try to limit your use of heat tools to once or twice a week, and always use a heat protectant.

Tip 2: Experiment with different styles.

Long bangs can be styled in a variety of ways, so don't be afraid to experiment. Try out different styles and see what works best for you.

Tip 3: Use a high-quality hairbrush.

A high-quality hairbrush can make a big difference when styling your long bangs. Look for a brush with natural bristles that won't damage your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my long bangs without heat tools?

A: Yes! You can use a round brush to blow dry your bangs without using a flat iron or curling iron.

Q: How often should I trim my long bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to look overgrown. Generally, it's a good idea to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential for keeping your long bangs looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.

- Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your long bangs like a pro. Whether you're going for a boho look or a sleek, polished style, your long bangs will look great and stay healthy.
