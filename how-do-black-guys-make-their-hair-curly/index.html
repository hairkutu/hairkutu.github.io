How Do Black Guys Make Their Hair Curly

Written by Oliver Sep 12, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're a black guy looking to switch up your hairstyle, you might be wondering how to make your hair curly. Fortunately, there are several methods you can use to achieve the curly look. In this article, we'll explore how to make black hair curly, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Black Hair Curly
  • FAQs about Making Black Hair Curly

How to Make Black Hair Curly

There are several ways to make black hair curly, including:

  • Using a curling iron or wand
  • Using rollers or flexi rods
  • Using a perm or relaxer
  • Using natural methods, such as twist-outs or braid-outs

Each method has its pros and cons, and the best option for you will depend on your hair type, desired curl pattern, and personal preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

Using a Curling Iron or Wand

To use a curling iron or wand to make your hair curly, follow these steps:

  1. Prep your hair by washing and conditioning it, then towel-drying until it's damp but not soaking wet.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent damage from the heat.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, holding for 10-15 seconds.
  5. Release the hair from the iron or wand and repeat with the remaining sections of hair.
  6. Once all sections are curled, gently comb through your hair with your fingers to blend the curls and create a more natural look.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or styling cream to hold the curls in place.

Using Rollers or Flexi Rods

To use rollers or flexi rods to make your hair curly, follow these steps:

  1. Prep your hair by washing and conditioning it, then towel-drying until it's damp but not soaking wet.
  2. Apply a styling cream or mousse to your hair to help the curls hold.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a roller or flexi rod, securing the ends with a clip or elastic band.
  5. Repeat with the remaining sections of hair.
  6. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until the curls have set.
  7. Remove the rollers or flexi rods and gently comb through your hair with your fingers to blend the curls and create a more natural look.
  8. Finish with a hairspray or styling cream to hold the curls in place.

Using a Perm or Relaxer

A perm or relaxer is a chemical treatment that permanently alters the texture of your hair. To use a perm or relaxer to make your hair curly, you'll need to visit a professional stylist who specializes in these treatments. The process typically involves applying the chemical solution to your hair, then wrapping it in rods or rollers to create the desired curl pattern. After a set amount of time, the solution is rinsed out and a neutralizer is applied to lock in the curls.

Using Natural Methods

There are several natural methods you can use to make black hair curly, including:

  • Twist-outs: Divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a tight coil, then allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until the twists have set. Undo the twists and gently comb through your hair with your fingers to create a more natural look.
  • Braid-outs: Divide your hair into sections and braid each section, then allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until the braids have set. Undo the braids and gently comb through your hair with your fingers to create a more natural look.
  • Finger coils: Starting at the roots, wrap small sections of hair around your finger in a spiral motion, then allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until the curls have set.

Tips for Making Black Hair Curly

To get the best results when making black hair curly, follow these tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum when using a curling iron or wand to prevent damage from the heat.
  • Choose the right size curling iron or wand for your desired curl pattern.
  • Use a styling cream or mousse when using rollers or flexi rods to help the curls hold.
  • Let your hair air dry or sit under a hooded dryer to minimize heat damage.
  • Don't overdo it with styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make the curls less defined.
  • Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for your hair type and desired curl pattern.

FAQs about Making Black Hair Curly

Can black guys make their hair curly without heat?

Yes, there are several natural methods for making black hair curly without heat, including twist-outs, braid-outs, and finger coils.

How long does it take to make black hair curly?

The time it takes to make black hair curly will depend on the method you choose. Using a curling iron or wand typically takes 30-60 minutes, while using rollers or flexi rods can take several hours to overnight. Perms and relaxers require a visit to a professional stylist and can take several hours.

Do I need to use a special shampoo or conditioner for curly hair?

While you don't necessarily need to use a special shampoo or conditioner for curly hair, using products specifically designed for curly hair can help enhance your curls and minimize frizz. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to stay healthy and maintain its shape. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, comb your hair with a wide-tooth comb when it's wet and use your fingers to detangle.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to your hair to enhance your curls and minimize frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make your curls less defined.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips and using the right products and techniques, you can achieve the curly look you desire and keep your hair healthy and strong.
