How To Cut Short Hair Layers Video A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 10, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of your short hair looking flat and lifeless? Adding layers can give your hair the volume and texture it needs to look fabulous. Cutting your own hair can be daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy look from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we’ll show you how to cut short hair layers video in easy-to-follow steps.

Description

This guide will teach you how to cut short hair layers video. It will provide step-by-step instructions on how to create layers in short hair, along with tips and tricks to ensure the best results. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced hairdresser, this guide will help you achieve a professional look.

How to Cut Short Hair Layers Video – Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather your tools and materials

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools and materials. You will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to hold hair in place
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Haircutting cape or towel to protect your clothes

Step 2: Section your hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Divide it into four sections: top, sides, and back. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the back section

Start with the back section. Comb your hair straight down, and cut a straight line across the bottom. This will be your guide for the rest of the haircut.

Next, take a small section of hair from the back, and comb it straight up. Use your fingers or a comb to hold the hair at a 45-degree angle, and trim the ends. Continue to work in small sections until you reach the top of your head.

Step 4: Cut the side sections

Move on to the side sections. Comb your hair straight down, and use your guide from the back section to cut a straight line across the bottom.

Next, take a small section of hair from the side, and comb it straight up. Hold the hair at a 45-degree angle, and trim the ends. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Cut the top section

Finally, move on to the top section. Comb your hair straight up, and hold it at a 45-degree angle. Use your guide from the back section to cut a straight line across the top.

Next, take small sections of hair from the top, and comb them straight up. Hold the hair at a 45-degree angle, and trim the ends. Work in small sections until you achieve your desired layering.

Step 6: Check for balance

Check your hair for balance. Make sure the layers are even on both sides, and adjust any sections that need it.

Step 7: Style your hair

Once you’re satisfied with your haircut, style your hair as desired. Use a hairdryer and styling products to add volume and texture.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair Layers

  • Start with small sections of hair, and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Be patient and take your time. Rushing can lead to uneven layers.
  • Keep your scissors sharp to ensure clean cuts.
  • Use a spray bottle to mist your hair with water as you cut. This will help you see any uneven sections.
  • Don’t be afraid to make mistakes. Hair grows back, and you can always fix any errors.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can. Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with practice, you can achieve great results. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the right steps.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and style. Short hair usually needs to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

What if I make a mistake?

Don’t panic. Hair grows back, and you can always fix any errors. If you’re not confident in your skills, it’s best to seek the help of a professional hairdresser.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its health and shape. Use a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, and try to air-dry your hair whenever possible.

Cutting short hair layers video can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great look. Follow these steps, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles. Who knows, you might discover a new look that you love!
