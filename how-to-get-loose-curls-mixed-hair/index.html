How To Get Loose Curls For Mixed Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have mixed hair, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve gorgeous loose curls that will make heads turn. In this post, we'll show you how to get loose curls mixed hair using easy steps and tips.

Description

Loose curls are a great option for mixed hair, as they add volume and texture to your hair without being too tight or too loose. To achieve this look, you'll need some basic hair tools and products, such as a curling iron, heat protectant, and hairspray.

How to Get Loose Curls for Mixed Hair

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it's essential to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely. Make sure to use a round brush to add volume and smoothness to your hair.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair evenly.

Step 5: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl each section of your hair away from your face. Hold the curling iron for a few seconds to set the curl, then release. Repeat this process until you've curled all of your hair.

Step 6: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-toothed comb to gently comb through your curls. This will help to separate them and create a more natural look.

Step 7: Apply Hairspray

To keep your loose curls in place, apply a light hold hairspray. Be sure to hold the can about 12 inches away from your hair to prevent it from becoming too stiff.

Tips for Getting Loose Curls for Mixed Hair

- Use a curling iron with a barrel size of 1-1.5 inches for loose curls.
- If you have fine hair, use a lower heat setting on your curling iron to prevent damage.
- For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions.
- Use a hair serum or oil to add shine to your hair.

FAQs

Can I get loose curls without using heat?

Yes, you can use heatless methods such as braiding your hair or using flexi rods to get loose curls.

How long will my loose curls last?

Your loose curls should last for about 2-3 days with proper care and maintenance.

How often should I curl my hair?

It's best to curl your hair no more than 2-3 times a week to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Mixed Hair

To keep your mixed hair healthy and beautiful, it's essential to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using moisturizing products, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Additionally, make sure to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve gorgeous loose curls for your mixed hair that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
