Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair Wedding Guest

Written by Frank Jun 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you attending a wedding as a guest and wondering what hairstyle to choose for your shoulder length hair? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of stunning hairstyles that will make you look and feel amazing on the special day. From elegant updos to romantic curls, these hairstyles are perfect for any wedding guest with shoulder length hair.

Description

Shoulder length hair is perfect for experimenting with different hairstyles, whether you prefer soft waves or a sleek updo. When it comes to weddings, you want a hairstyle that is elegant, sophisticated, and complements your outfit. The hairstyles we have chosen are easy to create, and they will ensure that you stand out as a stylish and chic wedding guest.

How to

To create these hairstyles, you will need a few essential hair tools, such as a curling iron, hairpins, a hairbrush, and hairspray. You can also add hair accessories to enhance your look, such as a headband, a flower clip, or a sparkly hairpin. Follow these steps to create these stunning hairstyles:

1. Soft Waves

Soft waves are perfect for a romantic and effortless look. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron, leaving the ends straight for a natural look. Brush through your curls with a hairbrush to create loose waves, then finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Sleek Updo

A sleek updo is perfect for a formal wedding. Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with hairpins. Finish with hairspray to keep the style in place.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for showing off your shoulder length hair while still keeping it out of your face. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron, then section off the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie. You can leave it straight or add some loose waves for a more romantic look.

4. Braided Bun

A braided bun is perfect for a boho-chic wedding. Start by braiding a small section of hair at the front of your head, then gather the rest of your hair into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with hairpins. Finish by wrapping the braided section around the bun and securing it with hairpins.

Step by Step

Follow these step-by-step instructions to create these stunning hairstyles:

1. Soft Waves

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and curl each section with a curling iron. Leave the ends straight for a natural look.
  4. Brush through your curls with a hairbrush to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Sleek Updo

  1. Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with hairpins.
  3. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with a hairbrush.
  4. Finish with hairspray to keep the style in place.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  3. Curl your hair with a curling iron, leaving the ends straight for a natural look.
  4. Section off the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  5. Leave it straight or add some loose waves for a more romantic look.

4. Braided Bun

  1. Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it into a low ponytail.
  2. Braid a small section of hair at the front of your head.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with hairpins.
  4. Wrap the braided section around the bun and secure it with hairpins.
  5. Finish with hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect hairstyle for a wedding:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to ensure that your hairstyle lasts all day.
  • Use a heat protectant when using a curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to add hair accessories to enhance your look.
  • Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.
  • Practice your hairstyle before the wedding to ensure that you are happy with the final result.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for shoulder length hair wedding guest:

What are the best hairstyles for shoulder length hair?

The best hairstyles for shoulder length hair include soft waves, sleek updos, half-up half-down, and braided buns.

How do I create a soft wave hairstyle?

To create a soft wave hairstyle, curl your hair with a curling iron, leaving the ends straight for a natural look. Brush through your curls with a hairbrush to create loose waves, then finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

What hair accessories can I use to enhance my hairstyle?

You can use a headband, a flower clip, or a sparkly hairpin to enhance your hairstyle and add some extra glamour.

Hair Care

To ensure that your hair looks its best on the wedding day, it's important to take care of it in the weeks leading up to the event. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine if you go swimming.

With these stunning hairstyles and hair care tips, you will look and feel amazing as a wedding guest with shoulder length hair.
