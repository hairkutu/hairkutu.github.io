How To Not Look Petite Tips For A Taller Appearance

Written by Aprilia Jul 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of being mistaken for someone younger or smaller than your actual size? Being petite is not a bad thing, but sometimes you just want to look taller and more commanding in a room. This guide will show you how to not look petite by making subtle changes to your style, posture, and grooming habits. Follow these tips and you'll be surprised at how much taller and confident you'll feel.

Posture Matters

The easiest way to look taller is to fix your posture. Stand up straight, pull your shoulders back, and hold your head high. Don't slouch or hunch over, as this will only make you look shorter. You can practice good posture by imagining a string pulling you up from the top of your head. This will elongate your spine and make you appear taller.

Choose the Right Clothing

Wearing the right clothing can make a huge difference in how tall you look. Avoid baggy clothes and opt for well-fitted pieces that elongate your silhouette. Choose high-waisted pants and skirts to create the illusion of longer legs. Avoid cropped pants and capris, as they tend to make you look shorter. Invest in a few tailored blazers and jackets that cinch at the waist to create a flattering hourglass shape.

Wear High Heels

This one is a no-brainer - high heels instantly add a few inches to your height. Choose heels that are comfortable and easy to walk in, as you don't want to be teetering around all day. Nude pumps are a great option as they blend in with your skin tone and create the illusion of longer legs. If you're not a fan of heels, try platform shoes or wedges as they offer height and stability.

Accessorize with Long Necklaces

Long necklaces are a simple accessory that can make a big impact. They draw the eye vertically and create the illusion of a longer torso. Choose necklaces that hit below your chest and avoid chokers or short necklaces that cut off your neck. Layering several long necklaces can also create a dramatic effect.

Opt for Vertical Stripes

Vertical stripes are a classic pattern that elongate your body. They draw the eye up and down, creating the illusion of a longer silhouette. Avoid horizontal stripes as they tend to widen your frame and make you look shorter. If you're not a fan of stripes, try other vertical patterns like pinstripes or chevron.

Go for Monochromatic Outfits

Wearing one color from head to toe creates a seamless line that elongates your body. This doesn't have to be boring - you can create interest by playing with different shades and textures. For example, a white silk blouse paired with white jeans and white heels creates a chic monochromatic look that makes you look taller.

Avoid Baggy Clothes

Baggy clothes tend to swallow up petite frames, making you look smaller than you actually are. Instead, opt for well-fitted pieces that accentuate your curves. This doesn't mean you have to wear skin-tight clothing - just make sure your clothes fit you properly and show off your best features.

Try Hair Extensions

If you want to add a few inches to your height, consider hair extensions. Long hair can create the illusion of a longer neck and torso. You don't have to go for super long extensions - just a few extra inches can make a big difference. If you're not a fan of extensions, try a volumizing hair product that adds height at the crown of your head.

FAQs

Q: Can wearing flats make me look taller?

A: It's a common misconception that you have to wear heels to look taller. Flats can also elongate your body if you choose the right style. Look for pointed-toe flats, which create the illusion of longer legs. Avoid round-toe flats, as they tend to make you look shorter.

Q: How can I create the illusion of a longer neck?

A: You can create the illusion of a longer neck by wearing V-neck tops or dresses. They draw the eye vertically and elongate your neck. Avoid turtlenecks or high necklines, as they tend to shorten your neck.

Final Thoughts

Being petite is nothing to be ashamed of, but sometimes you just want to look taller and more commanding. These tips will help you create the illusion of a longer, leaner physique without resorting to drastic measures. Remember to stand up straight, choose well-fitted clothing, and play with accessories to create a tall, confident look.

By following these tips, you can learn how to not look petite and feel more confident in your own skin. Remember, it's not about changing who you are - it's about enhancing your natural beauty and feeling your best.
