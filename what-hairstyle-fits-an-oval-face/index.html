What Hairstyle Fits An Oval Face

Written by Aprilia Apr 03, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, it’s important to consider your face shape. One face shape that is considered to be the most versatile is the oval face shape. An oval face shape is characterized by a balanced proportion, with the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline being of similar width. In this article, we’ll discuss what hairstyle fits an oval face and provide some tips on how to style your hair to flatter your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose a Hairstyle for an Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for an Oval Face
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair for an Oval Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for an Oval Face

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile because it can pull off a wide range of hairstyles. However, there are still some styles that are more flattering than others. The key to finding the right hairstyle for an oval face is to create balance and proportion. This means avoiding styles that add too much height or length to the face, as well as styles that make the face appear too round or square.

How to Choose a Hairstyle for an Oval Face

When choosing a hairstyle for an oval face, you want to focus on styles that add width to the forehead and temples, while also creating volume at the crown. This helps to balance out the face and draw attention away from the length. Here are some hairstyles that work well for oval faces:

  • Long layers
  • Shoulder-length bob
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves
  • Pixie cut
  • Updo

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for an Oval Face

Now that you know what hairstyles work best for an oval face, let’s go through a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown.
  2. If you have long hair, add some long layers to create movement and texture.
  3. If you have a bob or a shorter cut, consider adding some side-swept bangs to add width to the forehead.
  4. To create soft waves, use a curling iron or a flat iron to curl your hair away from your face.
  5. If you want to try a pixie cut, make sure to keep the length on top to create volume.
  6. For an updo, consider a low bun or a messy ponytail to add volume at the crown.

Tips for Styling Your Hair for an Oval Face

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair to flatter an oval face:

  • Avoid styles that add too much height or length to the face, such as super-straight hair or a high ponytail.
  • Avoid styles that make the face appear too round or square, such as a blunt bang or a bob that hits at the jawline.
  • Consider using a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to see which one flatters your face shape the most.

FAQs

Q: Can oval faces pull off short hair?

A: Yes, oval faces can pull off short hair. However, it’s important to keep the length on top to create volume and balance out the face shape.

Q: Is it better to have straight or curly hair for an oval face?

A: Both straight and curly hair work well for an oval face. The key is to create volume and texture to add balance to the face.

Hair Care Tips for an Oval Face

Finally, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Now that you know what hairstyle fits an oval face, it’s time to experiment and have fun with your hair. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
