How Do I Get Rid Of Orange Brassy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you've recently dyed your hair and ended up with orange, brassy tones, you're not alone. This can happen when your hair is not properly lightened before coloring, or when the color used is not suitable for your hair type. Nevertheless, there are several ways to fix this issue and restore your hair to its desired shade.

Description

In this blog post, we'll be discussing the various methods you can use to get rid of orange brassy hair. We'll also touch on some common questions and provide tips for maintaining healthy, vibrant hair.

How To

Method 1: Toning

One of the most effective ways to get rid of orange brassy hair is by toning it. Toning involves using a hair dye that has blue or purple undertones to neutralize the orange tones in your hair. You can either do this at home or go to a salon.

To tone your hair at home, you'll need to purchase a toning hair dye and follow the instructions on the package. It's important to note that toning can dry out your hair, so be sure to deep condition your hair afterwards.

Method 2: Color Correction

If toning doesn't work, you may need to consider color correction. This involves using a hair color that is opposite to orange on the color wheel, such as blue or green, to neutralize the orange tones.

Color correction is best done by a professional, as it can be a complicated process. However, if you choose to do it yourself, make sure to do a strand test first and follow the instructions on the hair dye package carefully.

Method 3: Wait it Out

If you're not comfortable with the idea of using hair dye, you can simply wait for the orange tones to fade away naturally. This can take a few weeks to a few months, depending on the level of brassiness and the condition of your hair.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for toning your hair at home:

  1. Choose a toning hair dye with blue or purple undertones.
  2. Mix the dye according to the instructions on the package.
  3. Apply the dye to your hair, focusing on the orange areas.
  4. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time.
  5. Rinse out the dye with cool water.
  6. Deep condition your hair to prevent dryness.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get rid of orange brassy hair and maintain healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent further damage.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about getting rid of orange brassy hair:

How long does it take to get rid of orange brassy hair?

This depends on the level of brassiness and the condition of your hair. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months for the orange tones to fade away naturally.

Can I use a purple shampoo to get rid of orange brassy hair?

Yes, you can use a purple shampoo to tone your hair and neutralize orange tones. However, this may not be as effective as using a toning hair dye or getting color correction.

Can I dye my hair again to get rid of orange brassy tones?

It's not recommended to dye your hair again immediately after getting orange brassy tones, as this can further damage your hair. It's best to wait a few weeks and use one of the methods above to get rid of the orange tones before considering dyeing your hair again.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important for maintaining healthy, vibrant hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with lukewarm water, as hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Wear a swim cap when swimming in chlorinated water to prevent damage.

By following these tips and using the methods above, you can get rid of orange brassy hair and maintain healthy, vibrant hair.
