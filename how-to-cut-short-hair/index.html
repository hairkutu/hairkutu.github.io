How To Cut Short Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline May 12, 2023 · 6 min read
How to Cut Short Hair - Step by Step Guide | Hair Care Tips

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a haircut? Cutting your own hair can be nerve-wracking, especially if you have short hair. But with a few tips and tricks, you can give yourself a stylish haircut without breaking the bank. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting short hair, step by step.

How to Cut Short Hair

Before we start, it’s important to have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It’s also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp during the cutting process.

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry completely before you start cutting. This will ensure that your hair is at its natural state and you can see the true length.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using the comb, section your hair into manageable pieces. Start by dividing your hair in half with a part down the middle, then divide each side into two sections. Clip the sections you are not working on to keep them out of the way.

Step 3: Start cutting

Starting at the nape of your neck, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and cut off the desired length. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way up to the top of your head.

Step 4: Check the length

Once you have finished cutting all the sections, check the length. If you notice any uneven areas, go back and trim them until you achieve your desired length.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once you are happy with the length, style your hair as desired. You can use a hair dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect short haircut:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help if you need it.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQs

Can I cut my own short hair?

Yes, you can cut your own short hair with the right tools and techniques.

What is the best way to cut short hair?

The best way to cut short hair is to start with clean, dry hair, section your hair, and cut small sections at a time.

How often should I cut my short hair?

It’s recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short hair.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own short hair and save money on salon visits. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don’t be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With practice, you can achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut at home.
