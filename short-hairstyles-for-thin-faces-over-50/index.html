Short Hairstyles For Thin Faces Over 50

Written by William Apr 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out, and our face shape may also change. It can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident. If you have a thin face and are over 50, you may be wondering what short hairstyles would suit you best. In this article, we will share some of the best short hairstyles for thin faces over 50 and provide you with valuable tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Thin Faces Over 50
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Thin Faces Over 50
  4. Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Thin Faces Over 50
  5. FAQs About Short Hairstyles for Thin Faces Over 50
  6. Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Description

Short hairstyles are a great option for thin faces over 50 as they can make your face appear fuller and more youthful. A well-chosen short hairstyle can also highlight your best features and complement your personal style. However, it's essential to choose a cut that flatters your face shape and hair type.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Thin Faces Over 50

When choosing a short hairstyle for thin faces over 50, consider the following:

  • The shape of your face: If you have a thin face, you want to avoid hairstyles that will make your face look even thinner. Opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to your face, such as a layered bob or a short pixie cut.
  • Your hair type: Thin hair can be challenging to style, so choose a cut that works well with your hair type. If you have fine hair, consider getting a blunt bob or a pixie cut with a side-swept fringe. If you have curly hair, opt for a short layered cut that will enhance your natural texture.
  • Your personal style: Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and personal style. Consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair and choose a cut that fits your lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Thin Faces Over 50

Styling short hair can be quick and easy if you follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting at the roots to add volume
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and shape to your hair
  5. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your style in place

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Thin Faces Over 50

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, consider the following tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage and dry it out
  • Try using a dry shampoo between washes to refresh your hair and add volume

FAQs About Short Hairstyles for Thin Faces Over 50

What is the best short hairstyle for a thin face?

The best short hairstyle for a thin face is one that adds volume and width to your face. Consider getting a layered bob or a short pixie cut with a side-swept fringe.

Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a thin face?

Yes, a pixie cut can be a great option for a thin face, as long as it's styled with volume and texture. Consider getting a layered pixie cut with a side-swept fringe to add dimension to your hair.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Thin hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and full. Consider the following tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage and dry it out
  • Try using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid pulling and breaking your hair

With the right short hairstyle and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age. Choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type, and don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styles until you find the one that works best for you.
