Men s Haircut Long On Top And Back Short On Sides A Relaxed Guide

In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the popular men's haircut long on top and back short on sides. From the description and how-to, to tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care, we've got you covered. So, let's dive in!

Description

The men's haircut long on top and back short on sides is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that has been trending for a while now. It features longer hair on the top and back of the head, while the sides are cut short, creating a sharp contrast between the two lengths. This haircut is perfect for men who want to look stylish and fashionable without sacrificing comfort and ease of maintenance.

How to

The men's haircut long on top and back short on sides is a relatively easy haircut to achieve, but it's important to go to a professional barber or stylist if you're not confident enough to do it yourself. Here's a general guide on how to get the look:

What you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair gel or wax (optional)

Step by step

1. Start by sectioning the hair into three parts: the top, the back, and the sides.

2. Use the clippers to cut the sides short, leaving enough length on top.

3. Blend the sides into the back using scissors or clippers with a longer guard.

4. Cut the hair on top to the desired length, leaving it longer than the sides and back.

5. Style the hair on top using gel or wax, if desired.

6. Clean up any stray hairs and adjust the cut as needed.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your men's haircut long on top and back short on sides:

  • Choose a length that suits your face shape and hair type. Your barber or stylist can help you with this.
  • Use a good quality gel or wax to style your hair on top. This will help you achieve the desired look and hold it in place throughout the day.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut long on top and back short on sides:

Q: How long does it take to get this haircut?

A: It usually takes around 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of the cut and your hair type.

Q: Can I style my hair differently every day?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styling techniques to achieve different looks.

Q: Is this haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: This haircut works best for people with oval or square face shapes, but it can be adapted for other face shapes as well.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your men's haircut long on top and back short on sides:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

That's it for our guide on the men's haircut long on top and back short on sides. We hope you found it helpful and informative. Remember, the key to a great haircut is to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type, and to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish!
