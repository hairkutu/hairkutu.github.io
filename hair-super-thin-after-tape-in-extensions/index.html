Why Is Your Hair Super Thin After Tape In Extensions

Written by Frank Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have recently gotten tape in extensions, and now your hair is super thin, you are not alone. Many women experience this issue, and it can be frustrating and upsetting. In this article, we will discuss the reasons why your hair may be thin after tape in extensions, and what you can do to remedy the situation.

Description

Tape in extensions are a popular way to add length and volume to your hair. They are easy to install and can last for a few months with proper care. However, some women experience hair thinning after getting tape in extensions. This can be due to a variety of reasons, including:

  • Improper installation
  • Too much weight on the hair
  • Pulling or tugging on the extensions
  • Using low-quality extensions
  • Not taking proper care of the extensions

If you are experiencing hair thinning after getting tape in extensions, it is important to figure out the cause so that you can take the necessary steps to fix the problem.

How to

If you are experiencing hair thinning after getting tape in extensions, there are a few things you can do to fix the problem:

  1. Remove the extensions: If the extensions were installed improperly or are causing too much weight on your hair, it may be necessary to remove them.
  2. Use high-quality extensions: Make sure you are using high-quality extensions that are made from real human hair. Synthetic extensions can cause damage to your natural hair and make it thin.
  3. Take proper care of the extensions: Make sure you are following the proper care instructions for your extensions. This includes washing and conditioning them regularly, and avoiding heat styling tools.
  4. Consult with a professional: If you are unsure of what is causing your hair thinning, it may be helpful to consult with a professional stylist or extension specialist.

Step by step

Here are the steps you can take to fix hair thinning after tape in extensions:

Step 1: Determine the cause

The first step is to determine the cause of the hair thinning. This may require a consultation with a professional stylist or extension specialist.

Step 2: Remove the extensions

If the extensions are causing too much weight on your hair or were installed improperly, it may be necessary to remove them.

Step 3: Use high-quality extensions

Make sure you are using high-quality extensions that are made from real human hair. Synthetic extensions can cause damage to your natural hair and make it thin.

Step 4: Take proper care of the extensions

Follow the proper care instructions for your extensions, including washing and conditioning them regularly, and avoiding heat styling tools.

Step 5: Consult with a professional

If you are unsure of what is causing your hair thinning, it may be helpful to consult with a professional stylist or extension specialist.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help prevent hair thinning after tape in extensions:

  • Choose a reputable salon or stylist for your extensions
  • Avoid installing extensions too close to the scalp
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your extensions
  • Do not sleep with wet hair or with your hair tied up tightly

FAQ

Can tape in extensions cause hair thinning?

Yes, tape in extensions can cause hair thinning if they are installed improperly, too heavy, or if they are not taken care of properly.

How long do tape in extensions last?

Tape in extensions can last for a few months with proper care.

Can I dye my tape in extensions?

Yes, you can dye your tape in extensions, but it is recommended to have a professional stylist do it for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to prevent hair thinning after tape in extensions. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Do not brush your hair when it is wet
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair

By following these tips, you can prevent hair thinning after tape in extensions and keep your natural hair healthy and strong.
