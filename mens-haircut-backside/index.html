Mens Haircut Backside Tips How To And Hair Care

If you're looking for a new hairstyle, you might want to consider a mens haircut backside. This hairstyle is all about keeping the back of your head shorter than the top and sides. It's a classic look that's been popular for decades, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this article, we'll be sharing tips, how to, and hair care for the mens haircut backside. So, let's get started!

Description

The mens haircut backside is a classic hairstyle that's been around for decades. It's a simple and clean look that's perfect for any occasion. The back of the head is kept shorter than the top and sides, giving it a tapered look. This hairstyle is easy to maintain and can be styled in many different ways. Whether you're going for a professional look or a casual one, the mens haircut backside is a great choice.

How to

The mens haircut backside is a simple hairstyle that can be done at home or by a professional. To get started, you'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, and a comb. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you see the natural fall of your hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide how short you want the back of your head to be. Use the clippers to trim the hair at the nape of your neck. Gradually move the clippers up the back of your head, using a higher guard as you go.

Step 3: Blend the Sides and Top

Use scissors to blend the sides and top of your hair. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a pomade, gel, or wax to give it a sleek look or a messy one.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting a mens haircut backside:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Decide how short you want the back of your head to be.
  3. Use the clippers to trim the hair at the nape of your neck.
  4. Gradually move the clippers up the back of your head, using a higher guard as you go.
  5. Blend the sides and top of your hair using scissors.
  6. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward.
  7. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting a mens haircut backside:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way up to a shorter one.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Consider getting your hair cut by a professional if you're not comfortable doing it yourself.

FAQ

What's the difference between a mens haircut backside and a taper?

A mens haircut backside is a type of taper. The back of the head is kept shorter than the top and sides, giving it a tapered look.

Can I style my hair differently with a mens haircut backside?

Yes, you can style your hair in many different ways with a mens haircut backside. You can use a pomade, gel, or wax to give it a sleek or messy look.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut backside looking great, here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Visit a professional for regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips, how to, and hair care information, you'll be able to rock a mens haircut backside with confidence. Whether you're going for a classic look or a modern one, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
