Ladies Hairstyles Short Back And Sides Long On Top A Guide To The Trendiest Look Of 2023

Written by Robby Jul 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking to switch things up? Look no further than the latest trend in ladies hairstyles - short back and sides long on top. This edgy and versatile look has taken the fashion world by storm, and we are here to guide you through everything you need to know about it. Read on for a complete how-to, tips, and hair care advice for this trendy hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Style
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

Description

The short back and sides long on top hairstyle, also known as an undercut, is characterized by shaved or closely cropped sides and a longer top. This look can be customized to suit your preferences - you can opt for a dramatic contrast between the top and sides or a more subtle fade. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add an edgy and bold touch to their look.

How to Achieve the Look

The short back and sides long on top hairstyle requires a bit of maintenance, but achieving the look is relatively easy. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Decide on the length of the top of your hair.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist to have the sides and back of your hair shaved or closely cropped.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to fade the hair on the sides and back gradually into the longer hair on top.
  4. Style the hair on top in a way that complements your face shape and personal style.

Note: It's essential to visit a professional hairstylist to achieve the look correctly.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Now that you have achieved the short back and sides long on top hairstyle, it's time to maintain it. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking fresh and stylish:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short back and sides long on top hairstyle:

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, but the length and style of the hair on top may need to be adjusted to suit your face shape.

Q: How often do I need to visit the hairstylist for touch-ups?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows, but most people need touch-ups every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

A: Absolutely! You can experiment with different styles, such as slicking it back, spiking it up, or leaving it messy and tousled.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after you have achieved the short back and sides long on top hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heating tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.

Overall, the short back and sides long on top hairstyle is a trendy and stylish look that is perfect for those who want to add an edgy touch to their appearance. With a bit of maintenance and care, you can rock this look with confidence and style.
