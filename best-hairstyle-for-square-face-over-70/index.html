Description

As we age, our face shape changes, and it becomes essential to choose the right hairstyle that suits our face shape. Women over 70 with a square face shape need to opt for a hairstyle that softens the edges and complements their features. A square face shape has a defined jawline and forehead, and the width of the face is similar to the length. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for square face over 70.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Square Face Over 70

Choosing the right hairstyle for a square face over 70 can seem overwhelming, but with a few tips, you can find the perfect one. Firstly, opt for a hairstyle that adds softness to the edges of your face. Layers, waves, and curls work well to soften the angular features of a square face shape. Secondly, choose a hairstyle that complements your facial features, such as your eyes, nose, and lips. Finally, opt for a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle.

Best Hairstyles for Square Face Over 70

The following are some of the best hairstyles for square face over 70:

  • Short Bob with Side-Swept Bangs
  • Layered Pixie Cut
  • Wavy Lob
  • Curly Bob
  • Long Layered Cut

Step by Step Guide to Get the Best Hairstyle for Square Face Over 70

If you have decided to get a new hairstyle, follow these steps to get the best one:

  1. Research different hairstyles that suit a square face shape
  2. Consult with a hairstylist who has experience in styling hair for women over 70 with a square face shape
  3. Show your hairstylist pictures of the hairstyle you want to get
  4. Ask your hairstylist to suggest modifications to the hairstyle that suit your facial features and hair texture
  5. Take care of your hair by following a hair care routine

Tips to Maintain the Best Hairstyle for Square Face Over 70

Once you have found the perfect hairstyle for your square face over 70, follow these tips to maintain it:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type and texture
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut
  • Use hair products like mousse, hairspray, and serum to style your hair
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can women over 70 opt for long hair?

Yes, women over 70 can opt for long hair. However, it is essential to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture.

2. Is it necessary to consult with a hairstylist before getting a new hairstyle?

It is always advisable to consult with a hairstylist who has experience in styling hair for women over 70 with a square face shape. They can suggest hairstyles that suit your facial features and hair texture.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days using a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Over 70

Following a hair care routine is essential to maintain healthy hair, especially as we age. Here are some hair care tips for women over 70 with a square face shape:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type and texture
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly
  • Use hair products like mousse, hairspray, and serum to style your hair
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water

Choosing the best hairstyle for square face over 70 can be challenging, but with the right tips and guidance, you can find the perfect one. Remember to consult with a hairstylist who has experience in styling hair for women over 70 with a square face shape and follow a hair care routine to maintain healthy hair.
