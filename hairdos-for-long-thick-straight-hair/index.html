Hairdos For Long Thick Straight Hair

Written by Petter May 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for some new hairdos to try out with your long, thick, and straight hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a variety of hairdos that will help you switch up your look and keep your hair feeling fresh. Whether you have a special event coming up or just want to try something new, these hairdos are sure to impress.

Description

Long, thick, and straight hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have voluminous hair, it can be difficult to manage and style. That's why we've put together a list of hairdos that are perfect for this hair type. These hairdos will help you make the most out of your hair while also keeping it looking healthy and beautiful.

How to

Before we dive into the hairdos, it's essential to prepare your hair properly. Make sure that your hair is clean and dry before styling. You can use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage if you plan to use heat tools. It's also a good idea to detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or a brush before styling.

1. Sleek Ponytail

The sleek ponytail is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for long, thick, and straight hair. To achieve this look, start by brushing your hair back and securing it with an elastic band. Use a small amount of hair gel or hairspray to smooth down any flyaways and create a polished finish.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your long, thick, and straight hair while also keeping it out of your face. To achieve this look, start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and securing it with an elastic band. You can leave your hair straight or add some curls to the bottom half for a more textured look.

3. Top Knot

The top knot is a trendy and effortless hairstyle that is perfect for long, thick, and straight hair. To achieve this look, start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail and securing it with an elastic band. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail and secure it with bobby pins. Use a small amount of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for achieving the sleek ponytail, half-up half-down, and top knot hairstyles:

Sleek Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair back and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Smooth down any flyaways using a small amount of hair gel or hairspray.
  3. Add a ribbon or scrunchie for a pop of color.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Section off the top half of your hair and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Optional: add some curls to the bottom half of your hair.
  3. Add a hair accessory, such as a clip or barrette, to the top section of your hair.

Top Knot

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Add a hair accessory, such as a hair scarf or headband, for a boho vibe.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect hairdos for long, thick, and straight hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools.
  • Detangle your hair before styling.
  • Use a small amount of hair gel or hairspray to smooth down any flyaways.
  • Add a hair accessory, such as a ribbon or barrette, to add some extra flair to your hairdo.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairdos for long, thick, and straight hair:

What are some easy hairdos for long, thick, and straight hair?

Some easy hairdos for long, thick, and straight hair include the sleek ponytail, half-up half-down, and top knot hairstyles.

How do I keep my long, thick, and straight hair healthy?

Keeping your long, thick, and straight hair healthy involves using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat tools as much as possible, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

What hair products should I use for long, thick, and straight hair?

You should use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, a heat protectant spray if you plan to use heat tools, and a small amount of hair gel or hairspray to smooth down any flyaways.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your long, thick, and straight hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools as much as possible.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair.

With these hairdos and hair care tips, you'll be sure to keep your long, thick, and straight hair looking fabulous! Try out these hairstyles and let us know which one is your favorite.
