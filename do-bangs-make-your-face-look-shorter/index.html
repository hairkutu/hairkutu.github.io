Do Bangs Make Your Face Look Shorter? | Hair Care Tips 2023

If you're considering getting a new haircut, you might be wondering whether bangs will make your face look shorter. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look, but it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape. In this article, we'll explore the question of whether bangs make your face look shorter, and provide some tips on how to choose the right bangs for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Bangs can make your face look shorter or longer depending on how they are cut. If you have a long face, bangs can help to balance out your features and make your face appear shorter. However, if you have a round or square face, bangs that fall straight across your forehead can make your face appear even shorter.

How To

The key to choosing the right bangs for your face shape is to consider your individual features. If you have a long face, you may want to opt for side-swept bangs that angle down towards your cheekbones. This will help to create the illusion of width across your forehead, which can make your face appear shorter.

If you have a round or square face, you may want to avoid blunt bangs that fall straight across your forehead. Instead, try a layered, textured fringe that falls at an angle. This will help to create the illusion of length in your face, which can help to balance out your features.

Step by Step

If you're considering getting bangs, here are some steps you can follow to ensure you choose the right style for your face shape:

  1. Take a good look at your face shape. You may want to consult with a hairstylist to determine your face shape if you're not sure.
  2. Consider the features you want to emphasize or de-emphasize. For example, if you have a long forehead, you may want to choose bangs that cover it up.
  3. Choose a style that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose side-swept bangs that angle down towards your cheekbones.
  4. Be prepared for regular maintenance. Bangs require regular trimming to keep them looking their best.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing bangs:

  • Consider your hair texture. If you have curly hair, bangs may be more difficult to manage.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you're active or constantly on-the-go, you may want to choose a low-maintenance style.
  • Try out different styles before committing to a cut. You can use hair extensions or clip-in bangs to experiment with different looks.
  • Be prepared for some trial and error. It may take a few tries to find the perfect bangs for your face shape and personal style.

FAQ

Do bangs make your face look shorter or longer?

Bangs can make your face look shorter or longer depending on how they are cut. Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of width across your forehead, which can make your face appear shorter. Blunt bangs that fall straight across your forehead can make your face appear even shorter.

What face shape looks best with bangs?

The best face shape for bangs depends on the style of bangs you choose. Side-swept bangs can be flattering on most face shapes, while blunt bangs may be more difficult to pull off if you have a round or square face.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen the perfect bangs for your face shape, it's important to take good care of them. Here are some hair care tips for keeping your bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup on your scalp and hairline.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Use a small amount of styling product to keep your bangs in place.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your bangs looking fresh.
