Older Men s Hairstyles For Round Face Tips And Ideas

Written by Bardi Aug 03, 2023 · 7 min read

As we age, our hair undergoes multiple changes that ultimately affect our overall appearance. Hairstyles for older men with round faces can be quite challenging to find. However, there are plenty of ways to make the most out of your hair, no matter the thickness, length, or texture. In this blog post, we'll be discussing some of the best hairstyle ideas for older men with round faces.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Round Face

Before we dive into the different hairstyle options, let's discuss how you can choose the right hairstyle for your round face. The first thing you should do is determine your face shape. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that complement your features.

For older men with round faces, the goal is to elongate the face and add some height. This can be achieved by going for hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, as this can make your face appear rounder.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's discuss how you can style your hair to achieve the desired look. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply some styling product to add volume to the top of your head.
  3. Use a comb to style your hair in the desired direction.
  4. Finish by using a hairspray or hair wax to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Get a haircut every four to six weeks to keep your hair in shape.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair regularly.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about older men's hairstyles for round faces:

Q: Can older men with round faces have short hair?
A: Yes, older men with round faces can have short hair. However, it's important to avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head. Q: What are some of the best hairstyles for older men with round faces?
A: Some of the best hairstyles for older men with round faces include the classic crew cut, the side-swept fringe, and the textured crop. Q: Are there any hairstyles that older men with round faces should avoid?
A: Yes, older men with round faces should avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as the bowl cut or the buzz cut.

Hair Care for Older Men with Round Faces

Proper hair care is essential for older men with round faces. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as hair dyes or relaxers, as these can weaken your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.

In conclusion, finding the right hairstyle for older men with round faces can be challenging, but with the right tips and ideas, it's possible to achieve a stylish and age-appropriate look. Remember to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and adds some height, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
