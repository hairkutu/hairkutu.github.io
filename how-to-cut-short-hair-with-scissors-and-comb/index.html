How To Cut Short Hair With Scissors And Comb

Written by Aprilia Sep 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that cutting it yourself is impossible. However, with the right tools and technique, you can cut your short hair with scissors and comb. In this article, we will walk you through the step-by-step process on how to cut short hair with scissors and comb, including tips and FAQs.

How to Cut Short Hair with Scissors and Comb

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first talk about the tools you will need:

  • Scissors - make sure they are sharp and specifically designed for cutting hair
  • Comb - preferably one with fine teeth
  • Hair clips or pins - to section off your hair
  • Spray bottle with water - to dampen your hair

Now that you have your tools ready, let's start cutting your short hair with scissors and comb.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Dampen your hair with water. This will make it easier to cut and will help you achieve a more precise cut.

Step 2: Section off your hair. Use hair clips or pins to separate your hair into manageable sections. Start with the bottom section, and work your way up.

Step 3: Comb your hair. Use a fine-toothed comb to comb your hair straight down. Make sure it is tangle-free.

Step 4: Cut your hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Make sure you are cutting evenly and not cutting too much hair at once.

Step 5: Repeat steps 3 and 4 for each section of your hair. Make sure to blend the sections together so that your hair looks uniform.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair with Scissors and Comb

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your short hair with scissors and comb:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Start small. It's better to cut a little bit of hair at a time than to cut too much and regret it.
  • Keep your hair damp. This will help you achieve a more precise cut.
  • Don't rush. Take your time and make sure you are cutting evenly and not cutting too much hair at once.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my short hair with regular scissors?

A: No, you should use scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts.

Q: How often should I cut my short hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your hair.

Q: Can I cut my short hair when it's dry?

A: It's not recommended to cut your hair when it's dry because it's harder to achieve a precise cut. Dampening your hair with water will make it easier to cut and will help you achieve a more even cut.

Hair Care After Cutting Short Hair with Scissors and Comb

After cutting your short hair with scissors and comb, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

Cutting your short hair with scissors and comb can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking cut. Follow the step-by-step guide and tips we've provided, and don't forget to take care of your hair after cutting it.
