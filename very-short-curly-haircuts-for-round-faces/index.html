Very Short Curly Haircuts For Round Faces A Guide To The Best Styles

Are you tired of having long hair and want to try something new? Or are you someone who has always had shorter hair but wants to switch it up a bit? Whatever your reason may be, very short curly haircuts for round faces can be a great option for you.

Description

Very short curly haircuts for round faces are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. These haircuts are perfect for those who have naturally curly hair, as they can enhance the natural curl pattern.

When it comes to choosing a very short curly haircut for a round face, the key is to choose a style that will elongate the face and add height to the crown of the head. This will help create the illusion of a longer face and make the face appear more oval-shaped.

How To

Before getting a very short curly haircut for a round face, it is important to consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with curly hair. They will be able to help you choose a style that will work best with your hair type and face shape.

When choosing a haircut, opt for styles that are shorter on the sides and longer on the top. This will help elongate the face and add height to the crown of the head. Some popular styles for very short curly haircuts for round faces include a curly pixie cut, a curly bob, and a curly shag.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a very short curly haircut for a round face:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with curly hair.
  2. Choose a style that is shorter on the sides and longer on the top.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to the top of the hair to create volume and height.
  4. Consider adding highlights to the top of the hair to add dimension.
  5. Use a curl-enhancing product to help define the natural curl pattern.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a very short curly haircut for a round face:

  • Choose a style that works with your natural curl pattern.
  • Ask your hairstylist to add layers to the top of the hair to create volume and height.
  • Consider adding highlights to the top of the hair to add dimension.
  • Use a curl-enhancing product to help define the natural curl pattern.
  • Be prepared to style your hair every day to maintain the shape and volume.

FAQ

Q: Will a very short curly haircut make my round face look wider?

A: No, as long as you choose a style that is shorter on the sides and longer on the top, it will help elongate the face and make it appear more oval-shaped.

Q: How often should I get my very short curly haircut trimmed?

A: It is recommended to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and volume of the haircut.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your very short curly haircut for a round face, it is important to use products that will enhance your natural curl pattern and add volume to the hair.

Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair before styling. It is also important to use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance the natural curl pattern.

Overall, very short curly haircuts for round faces can be a great option for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair. Remember to consult with a hairstylist before getting a new haircut, and use products that will enhance your natural curl pattern and add volume to the hair.
