How To Make Short Straight Hair Curly For Guys

Written by Bardi Apr 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having the same old straight hair every day? Do you want to switch things up and add some curls to your short hair? Well, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll show you how to make short straight hair curly for guys in a few easy steps.

Description

Adding some curls to your short hair can be a great way to change up your look and add some volume and texture. However, many guys with short straight hair may feel intimidated by the thought of trying to create curls. But don't worry â€“ with the right tools and techniques, anyone can make their hair curly.

How to

If you want to make your short straight hair curly, here's what you'll need to do:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, you'll need to wash and condition it. This will help to remove any dirt or oil that may be weighing your hair down and make it easier to style. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Curling your hair can be damaging, so it's important to protect your hair from heat damage by applying a heat protectant spray or serum. This will help to prevent breakage and frizz, and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, you'll need to dry it. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely, making sure to lift your roots and add volume. This will make it easier to style your hair and create curls that last.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Now it's time to create your curls! Use a curling iron with a barrel size that's appropriate for your hair length â€“ for short hair, a smaller barrel size is best. Wrap small sections of hair around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat until all sections of your hair have been curled.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your curls in place all day, finish with a light hairspray. This will help to set your curls and prevent them from falling flat. You can also use a texturizing spray to add some definition and volume to your curls.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your short straight hair curly:

  • Use a high-quality curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly â€“ this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques, such as twisting or braiding your hair before curling.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to separate and define your curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much â€“ this can cause your curls to fall out.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making short straight hair curly for guys:

Can all hair types be curled?

Yes, most hair types can be curled with the right tools and techniques. However, if you have very fine or thin hair, you may need to use a smaller barrel size or use a setting spray to help your curls hold.

How long will my curls last?

How long your curls last will depend on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, you can expect your curls to last for a few hours to a full day.

Can I make my hair curly without heat?

Yes, there are several ways to make your hair curly without heat, such as using rollers, braiding your hair, or using a texturizing spray. However, these methods may not work as well for short hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair after you've curled it. This means using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, and avoiding excessive heat styling or touching your hair too much. You can also use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

That's it â€“ now you know how to make short straight hair curly for guys! With these tips and techniques, you can switch up your look and add some texture and volume to your hair.
