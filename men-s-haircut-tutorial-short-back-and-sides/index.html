Men s Haircut Tutorial Short Back And Sides

Written by Frank Sep 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a classic yet stylish haircut? The short back and sides is a popular choice for men of all ages. In this tutorial, we'll show you how to achieve this timeless look step by step. From choosing the right length to maintaining the style, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a classic men's hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides while leaving it longer on top. This style has been popular for decades and is a great choice for men who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look. It works well for both formal and casual occasions and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How To

Here's how to achieve the short back and sides haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to decide how short you want the back and sides to be. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your head. Generally, a number 2 or 3 clipper guard is used for the back and sides, while the top is left longer. However, you can adjust the length to suit your style.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Next, section the hair by dividing it into three parts: the top, the back, and the sides. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Cut the Back and Sides

Starting at the bottom, use the clipper to cut the hair in an upward motion. Move the clipper around the head, following the natural hairline. Use a mirror to check the back and sides for evenness.

Step 4: Blend the Top

Once the back and sides are cut, it's time to blend the top. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, blending it into the shorter back and sides. Be sure to leave enough length on top for styling.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Now that your hair is cut, it's time to style it. Use a hair product of your choice, such as gel or pomade, to create your desired look. This could be a slicked-back style, a messy fringe, or anything in between.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short back and sides haircut:

  • Bring a photo of the style you want to show your barber or stylist.
  • Communicate clearly about the length and style you want.
  • Ask for advice on how to maintain the style at home.
  • Use a high-quality hair product to keep the style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short back and sides haircut:

Is the short back and sides haircut easy to maintain?

Yes, this style is low-maintenance and requires minimal styling. However, you may need to visit the barber or stylist every few weeks to maintain the length.

What face shapes suit the short back and sides haircut?

This style works well for most face shapes, but particularly suits those with square or oval faces.

Can the short back and sides haircut be customized?

Yes, you can customize this style by adjusting the length, texture, and styling. Talk to your barber or stylist about your preferences.

Hair Care

To keep your short back and sides looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Now that you know how to achieve the short back and sides haircut, you can enjoy a classic yet stylish look that's easy to maintain. Whether you're heading to the office or a night out with friends, this versatile style is sure to turn heads. Give it a try and see the difference for yourself!
