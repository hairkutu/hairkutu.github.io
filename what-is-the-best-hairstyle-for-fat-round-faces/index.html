The Best Hairstyles For Fat Round Faces

Written by Petter Jun 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face with a bit of extra weight, you might be wondering what the best hairstyle is for you. The right hairstyle can help to balance out your features and make your face appear slimmer. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best hairstyles for fat round faces.

Description

A round face is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you might feel self-conscious about the extra weight in your cheeks and chin. However, with the right hairstyle, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face shape.

How to

The key to finding the best hairstyle for a fat round face is to create the illusion of length and height. This means avoiding hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous around the cheeks and chin. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head and create a slimming effect around the face.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a hairstyle for a fat round face:

  1. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous around the cheeks and chin.
  2. Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head.
  3. Consider a layered haircut to add texture and movement to your hair.
  4. Try a side-swept fringe to create the illusion of length.
  5. Avoid blunt bangs that end at the cheekbones.
  6. Consider a bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back.
  7. Try a pixie cut with volume on top.
  8. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or stick straight.
  9. Consider adding waves or curls to your hair to add volume and texture.
  10. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing the best hairstyle for a fat round face:

  • Consider your hair texture and volume when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Try out different hairstyles using online virtual hairstyle tools.
  • Experiment with different hair products to achieve the desired look.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for fat round faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a fat round face?

You should avoid hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous around the cheeks and chin. Examples include blunt bangs that end at the cheekbones and hairstyles that are too flat or stick straight.

What hairstyles are best for a fat round face?

The best hairstyles for a fat round face are those that add height to the crown of your head and create a slimming effect around the face. Examples include layered haircuts, side-swept fringes, bobs that are longer in the front and shorter in the back, and pixie cuts with volume on top.

Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a fat round face?

Yes, you can still wear your hair straight if you have a fat round face. However, it's important to avoid hairstyles that are too flat or stick straight. Instead, consider adding volume and texture to your hair with waves or curls.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using heat protectant products when styling with heat tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals and dyes. Additionally, consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance.
