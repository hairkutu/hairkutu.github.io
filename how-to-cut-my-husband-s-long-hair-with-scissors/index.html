How To Cut My Husband s Long Hair With Scissors

Written by Robby Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a way to cut your husband's long hair with scissors? This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to do it. We will also share some tips on how to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions about hair cutting. Let's get started!

Step by Step Guide

Before you start cutting your husband's long hair with scissors, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a spray bottle filled with water. Once you have all the tools, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your husband's hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will make it easier to comb through and cut.
  2. Comb through the hair to remove any tangles or knots. Use the hair clips to separate the hair into sections. Start by dividing the hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back.
  3. Choose the section you want to cut first. Spray it with water to make it damp. This will make it easier to cut and prevent split ends.
  4. Use the comb to comb through the hair again. Hold the scissors in your dominant hand and use your other hand to hold the hair in place. Cut the hair slowly and carefully, making sure to cut it straight across.
  5. Continue cutting the hair in small sections until you have cut all the hair in that section. Then move on to the next section.
  6. Once you have cut all the sections, check the hair to make sure it is even. Use the scissors to trim any uneven ends.

Congratulations! You have successfully cut your husband's long hair with scissors.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair

Cutting long hair can be challenging, especially if you are doing it for the first time. Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Start by cutting a small amount of hair. You can always cut more if needed, but you can't put it back once it's cut.
  • Keep the hair damp while cutting. This will prevent split ends and make it easier to cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Take breaks if needed. Cutting hair can be tiring and time-consuming, so don't rush the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair cutting:

Q: Can I cut my husband's hair with regular scissors?

A: It is recommended to use hair cutting scissors, as regular scissors may not be sharp enough and can damage the hair.

Q: How often should I cut my husband's hair?

A: It depends on how fast his hair grows and how short he wants it. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Q: How do I know which hairstyle to choose?

A: It's best to talk to a professional stylist or do some research online to find a hairstyle that suits your husband's face shape and hair type.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your husband's long hair, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips on how to care for the hair after cutting:

  • Wash the hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish the hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the hairstyle.

Now that you know how to cut your husband's long hair with scissors, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. Remember to follow the steps carefully, use the right tools, and take care of the hair properly after cutting. Good luck!
