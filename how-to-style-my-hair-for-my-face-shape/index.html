How To Style Your Hair For Your Face Shape In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jun 14, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of styling your hair the same way every day? Do you want to switch things up but don't know where to start? It's time to consider styling your hair for your face shape. By doing so, you can highlight your best features and enhance your overall look. In this article, we'll show you how to style your hair based on your face shape, provide step-by-step instructions, share tips, answer frequently asked questions, and give you some hair care advice.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in styling your hair for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart-shaped, diamond-shaped, and long. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Pull your hair back away from your face.
  2. Stand in front of a mirror.
  3. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face onto the mirror.
  4. Step back and look at the shape you've traced.
  5. Compare the shape to the descriptions of the six face shapes.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start styling your hair accordingly.

Styling an Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! You can pull off just about any hairstyle. However, you'll want to avoid styles that add too much height on top of your head or too much width on the sides, as they can make your face appear longer.

Styling a Round Face

If you have a round face, you'll want to create the illusion of length. To do so, consider hairstyles with height on top and long, face-framing layers. Avoid short, blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can emphasize the roundness of your face.

Styling a Square Face

If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your angular features. Consider hairstyles with soft layers and wispy bangs. Avoid blunt cuts and straight, long hairstyles, as they can emphasize the squareness of your face.

Styling a Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to balance out your wider forehead with your narrower chin. Consider hairstyles with side-swept bangs and long, face-framing layers. Avoid short, blunt cuts and heavy, voluminous hairstyles at the top of your head.

Styling a Diamond-Shaped Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you'll want to balance out your narrow forehead and chin with your wider cheekbones. Consider hairstyles with volume at the top of your head and soft layers around your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too wide or too narrow.

Styling a Long Face

If you have a long face, you'll want to create the illusion of width. Consider hairstyles with volume at the sides and soft layers around your face. Avoid long, straight hairstyles, as they can elongate your face even more.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know what hairstyles work best for your face shape, it's time to start styling! Follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or hairspray.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on creating volume at the top and sides.
  4. If necessary, use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or straighten your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for your face shape:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for your face shape.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and switch things up!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair for face shape:

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or bun?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail or bun. Just make sure to position it in a way that flatters your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, consider a high ponytail to create the illusion of length.

Do I have to follow these guidelines exactly?

No, you don't have to follow these guidelines exactly. They're simply suggestions to help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Ultimately, the most important thing is that you feel confident and comfortable in your hairstyle.

Hair Care Advice

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Styling your hair for your face shape is a great way to enhance your natural beauty and switch up your look. By following these guidelines, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful. So go ahead and experiment with different styles, and don't be afraid to try something new!
