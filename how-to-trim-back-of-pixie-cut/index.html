How To Trim Back Of Pixie Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a pixie cut, you know how important it is to keep it trimmed and looking fresh. However, sometimes it can be difficult to trim the back of your hair on your own. In this article, we will show you how to trim the back of your pixie cut at home. We will cover everything from how to section your hair to how to use the right tools. So, let's get started!

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut close to the head. It is a popular choice for women who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish. However, to keep your pixie cut looking its best, it needs to be trimmed regularly. Trimming the back of your hair can be tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it yourself at home.

How to Trim Back of Pixie Cut

Step 1: Section Your Hair

The first step in trimming the back of your pixie cut is to section your hair. Use a comb to create a part down the middle of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, use the comb to create two more sections on each side of your head. Pin each section up with a hair clip so that you can work on one section at a time.

Step 2: Use the Right Tools

To trim the back of your pixie cut, you will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. Make sure that your scissors are sharp and clean, as dull or dirty scissors can damage your hair.

Step 3: Start at the Nape of Your Neck

Begin trimming at the nape of your neck. Use the comb to lift a small section of hair and hold it straight up. Then, use the scissors to cut across the ends of the hair. Make small cuts and work your way up, checking your progress in the handheld mirror as you go. Be sure to cut the hair evenly and avoid cutting too much off at once.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Once you have trimmed the back of your hair, move on to the sides. Use the same technique, lifting small sections of hair with the comb and cutting across the ends with the scissors. Be sure to cut the hair evenly and avoid cutting too much off at once.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you have finished trimming the back and sides of your hair, use the handheld mirror to check your work. Make any necessary touch-ups to ensure that your hair is even and well-shaped.

Tips

  • Trim your hair when it is dry, as wet hair can be difficult to cut evenly.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair before cutting, as this will help you to see the shape of your hair and avoid cutting too much off.
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up, as this will help you to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Be patient and take your time, as rushing can lead to uneven cuts and mistakes.

FAQ

Can I trim the back of my pixie cut myself?

Yes, you can trim the back of your pixie cut yourself. However, it can be tricky, so be sure to use the right tools and techniques.

How often should I trim my pixie cut?

It is recommended that you trim your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking its best.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake while trimming your hair, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to have it fixed.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You can also use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Now that you know how to trim the back of your pixie cut, you can keep your hair looking fresh and stylish all year round. Just remember to use the right tools, take your time, and be patient. Happy trimming!
