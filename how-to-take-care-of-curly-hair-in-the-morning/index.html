How To Take Care Of Curly Hair In The Morning A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 13, 2023 ¬∑ 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks gorgeous when styled properly, it can also be difficult to manage, especially in the morning. If you're struggling with how to take care of curly hair in the morning, this guide is for you. Here, we'll provide step-by-step instructions on how to care for your curls from the moment you wake up until you step out the door. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 2: Refresh Your Curls
  • Step 3: Detangle Your Hair
  • Step 4: Apply Products

  • FAQs About Taking Care of Curly Hair in the Morning

Step 1: Start with a Good Night's Sleep

The first step to taking care of curly hair in the morning actually starts the night before. Make sure you go to bed with dry hair, as sleeping on wet hair can cause breakage and frizz. You should also use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent your curls from getting tangled and frizzy while you sleep.

Step 2: Refresh Your Curls

When you wake up in the morning, your curls may look flat or frizzy. To refresh them, use a spray bottle filled with water and a little bit of leave-in conditioner. Spritz your hair until it's damp, then scrunch it with your hands to help reform the curls.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Next, it's time to detangle your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently remove any knots or tangles. Don't yank or pull at your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.

Step 4: Apply Products

Once your hair is detangled, it's time to apply your products. Start with a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls moisturized throughout the day. Then, apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to help define your curls and reduce frizz. Be sure to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

The final step is to style your hair. There are many different ways to style curly hair, so experiment to find what works best for you. Some popular styling methods include scrunching your hair with your hands, diffusing your hair with a blow dryer, or using a hair pick to add volume.

Tips for Taking Care of Curly Hair in the Morning

Here are some additional tips for taking care of curly hair in the morning:

  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Instead, try washing it every 2-3 days.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your curls.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

FAQs About Taking Care of Curly Hair in the Morning

Q: Should I brush my curly hair in the morning?

A: No, it's best to detangle your hair with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your curls.

Q: How often should I trim my curly hair?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my curly hair?

A: It's best to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they are formulated to provide extra moisture and reduce frizz.

Hair Care Routine for Curly Hair

If you're looking for a comprehensive hair care routine for curly hair, here's a sample to get you started:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner and styling product every morning to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat tools, and instead, let your hair air dry or diffuse it with a blow dryer on a low setting.
  • Get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and incorporating them into your morning routine, you can achieve healthy, beautiful curls that look great every day.
