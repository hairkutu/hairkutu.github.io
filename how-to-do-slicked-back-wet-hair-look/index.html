How To Do Slicked Back Wet Hair Look A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a sleek and stylish hairstyle that will turn heads? Look no further than the slicked back wet hair look! This trendy hairstyle has been spotted on runways, red carpets, and in everyday life. It's a versatile look that works for both men and women and can be adapted to your personal style. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect slicked back wet hair look in just a few simple steps.

Description

The slicked back wet hair look is a hairstyle that involves combing your hair back and applying a styling product to give it a wet, shiny appearance. This hairstyle can be achieved with different hair lengths and textures and works well for both formal and casual occasions. It's a versatile style that can add a touch of sophistication to your overall look.

How to

To achieve the slicked back wet hair look, you will need a few key products and tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb or brush
  • Styling gel, pomade, or wax
  • Hair spray (optional)

Now that you have all the necessary tools, let's get started!

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Before you begin styling, make sure your hair is clean and damp. You can either take a shower or dampen your hair with water. Avoid styling your hair when it's completely wet, as this can make it difficult to control.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Take a small amount of styling gel, pomade, or wax and rub it between your palms. Then, run your hands through your hair, starting from the front and working your way back. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a comb or brush, comb your hair back from your forehead towards the back of your head. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or tangles as you go.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once your hair is combed back, use your fingers to style it into the desired shape. You can slick it straight back for a classic look, or you can add some volume for a more modern look.

Step 5: Finish with hair spray (optional)

If you want your hairstyle to stay in place all day, you can finish it off with a light mist of hair spray. This will help to hold your hair in place and keep it looking sleek and shiny.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back wet hair look:

  • Use a styling product that works well with your hair type and texture.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid damaging your hair.
  • If you have a receding hairline, avoid slicking your hair straight back.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the slicked back wet hair look:

Is the slicked back wet hair look suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle can be adapted to different hair types and textures. Just make sure to use a styling product that works well with your hair type.

Can I achieve this look without using a styling product?

No, a styling product is necessary to achieve the wet, shiny appearance of this hairstyle.

How often should I wash my hair if I want to maintain this hairstyle?

You can wash your hair as often as you normally would, but make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to trim split ends and maintain your hairstyle.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect slicked back wet hair look in no time. Whether you're heading to a formal event or just want to add some style to your everyday look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
