How To Cut Men s Hair Long Length A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair has been a popular trend for men in recent years, and many are opting for longer locks. But with longer hair comes the need for regular maintenance and trimming. Cutting men's hair long length can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, anyone can do it at home. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve a long, flowing style that looks professionally done.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools Needed
  2. Preparation
  3. Sectioning the Hair
  4. Cutting the Hair
  5. Blending the Hair
  6. Texturizing the Hair
  7. Finishing Touches
  8. Hair Care Tips
  9. FAQs

Tools Needed

Before you start cutting hair, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Shears (scissors)
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water sprayer
  • Cape or towel

Preparation

Before you start cutting, make sure your client's hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can stretch and make it difficult to get an accurate cut. It's also important to have a clear workspace and good lighting. You may want to lay down a towel or cape around the person's shoulders to catch any loose hairs.

Sectioning the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into manageable sections. Use hair clips to separate the top from the sides and back. Begin cutting the back section first, working from the bottom up. Use the clippers with a guard to trim the hair to the desired length.

Cutting the Hair

Once the back section is complete, move on to the sides. Use the shears to trim the hair to the desired length, keeping in mind the shape you want to achieve. Work in small sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and cut with the shears in a straight line.

Blending the Hair

Once the sides are complete, it's time to blend the hair. Use the shears to trim any excess hair at the top of the head, blending it into the sides and back. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and cut in a diagonal line to create a natural-looking blend.

Texturizing the Hair

If you want to add texture to the hair, use the shears to create layers. Hold the hair at a 45-degree angle and cut into the ends to create a layered effect. This will also help to remove any bulkiness in the hair.

Finishing Touches

Once the haircut is complete, use the clippers to clean up any stray hairs around the ears and neckline. Use the water sprayer to dampen the hair and style it as desired. You may want to use a styling product to keep the hair in place.

Hair Care Tips

To keep long hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools (such as straighteners and curling irons) too often
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to comb through wet hair to prevent breakage
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair long length?

Yes, you can cut your own hair at home, but it may be more difficult to achieve the desired look. It's always best to have someone else help you with cutting your hair.

How often should I get a trim?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

What if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. You can always trim a little more off, or even it out by cutting the other side to match.

Cutting men's hair long length is a great way to achieve a trendy, modern look. With the right tools and techniques, anyone can do it at home. Just remember to take your time and work in small sections to achieve the best results. Happy cutting!
