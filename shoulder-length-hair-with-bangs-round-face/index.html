Shoulder Length Hair With Bangs Round Face A Perfect Combination

Written by Oliver May 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you someone with a round face and looking for a perfect hairstyle that can complement your face shape? Well, you're in luck because shoulder length hair with bangs is a great option for you. In this blog post, we will discuss how to style shoulder length hair with bangs for a round face, step by step guide, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips. So, let's dive into it.

Description

Shoulder length hair with bangs is a versatile hairstyle that can suit any face shape, including round faces. The length of hair falling on the shoulders can make the face appear slimmer, while the bangs can draw attention to the eyes and forehead, creating a balanced look. The key is to find the right type of bangs that complement your face shape and hair texture.

How To Style Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs for Round Face

The first step to style shoulder length hair with bangs for a round face is to choose the right type of bangs. For round faces, side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are the best options as they create an illusion of a longer face. Avoid straight and blunt bangs as they can make the face appear wider.

Next, decide on the length of your hair. Shoulder length hair is the perfect length for round faces as it falls just below the jawline, creating a slimming effect. You can add layers to your hair to create volume and movement.

Step By Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse to damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  3. Part your hair to the side and section off the bangs.
  4. Blow-dry the bangs using a small round brush, directing them to the side.
  5. Once the bangs are dry, style the rest of your hair using a curling iron or straightener.
  6. Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Experiment with different types of bangs to find the one that suits you best.
  • Accessorize your hair with hair clips or headbands to add a stylish touch to your look.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I wear blunt bangs with a round face?

No, blunt bangs can make your face appear wider, which is not ideal for round faces. Opt for side-swept bangs or curtain bangs instead.

2. Will shoulder length hair make my face appear slimmer?

Yes, shoulder length hair falling just below the jawline can create an illusion of a slimmer face.

3. How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent them from getting too long.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your shoulder length hair with bangs looking healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to nourish and hydrate it.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your bangs.

Now that you know how to style shoulder length hair with bangs for a round face, go ahead and try out this trendy and chic hairstyle. With the right type of bangs and hair length, you can create a flattering and balanced look that complements your face shape.
