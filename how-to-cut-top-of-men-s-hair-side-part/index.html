How To Cut Top Of Men s Hair Side Part A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to cut your hair in a side part style? This classic look never goes out of style, and it's perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut the top of men's hair for a side part style. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced barber, this guide will help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

The side part is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a simple and elegant look that can be worn for any occasion. To achieve this look, you need to cut the hair on top of the head and create a distinct part. This style works best on men with straight or wavy hair, but it can also be adapted for curly hair.

How to Cut Top of Men's Hair Side Part

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

The first step is to wash and dry your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free from any dirt or oils that could make it difficult to cut. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Once you've washed your hair, towel dry it until it's slightly damp.

Step 2: Comb Hair

Using a comb, comb your hair straight back. This will help you to determine where the part should be. The part should be in line with the outer corner of your eye. Once you've found the right spot, comb the hair on either side of the part forward.

Step 3: Cut the Top of Hair

Starting at the front hairline, use scissors to cut the hair on top of the head. Cut the hair to the desired length, making sure to leave some length on top. Work your way back, cutting the hair in small sections. Make sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides of the part.

Step 4: Create the Part

Using the end of the comb, create a part in the hair. The part should be straight and in line with the outer corner of your eye. Use scissors to clean up any stray hairs that are sticking out of the part.

Step 5: Style Hair

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your hair. Use your fingers to style the hair on top of your head. Comb the hair on either side of the part forward, and comb the rest of the hair back. You can also use a hair dryer to style your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect side part:

  • Use sharp scissors to cut the hair.
  • Don't cut the hair too short on top.
  • Make sure the part is straight.
  • Use a small amount of styling product to avoid a greasy look.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

Can I do this myself or should I go to a barber?

You can do this yourself if you have experience cutting hair, but it's recommended to go to a barber for best results.

What if I have curly hair?

The side part style can be adapted for curly hair. It's recommended to use a hair gel or mousse to help tame the curls and keep them in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

Cutting the top of men's hair for a side part style is easy with these step-by-step instructions. With a little practice and the right tools, you can achieve the perfect look. Don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
