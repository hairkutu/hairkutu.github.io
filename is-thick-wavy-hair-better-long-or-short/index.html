Is Thick Wavy Hair Better Long Or Short

Written by Daniel Aug 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's voluminous and full of life, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. One of the biggest debates when it comes to thick wavy hair is whether it's better long or short. In this article, we'll explore both options and help you determine which one is best for you.

  • How to Style Thick Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide: Long vs Short
  • Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair

Description

If you have thick wavy hair, you've probably wondered whether you should keep it long or chop it all off. There are pros and cons to both options, so it really comes down to personal preference and lifestyle. Long hair can be romantic and feminine, but it can also be high-maintenance and heavy. Short hair, on the other hand, is low-maintenance and practical, but it can also be limiting in terms of styling options.

How to Style Thick Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the long vs short debate, let's talk about how to style thick wavy hair in general. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent frizz and dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers instead.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your ends to keep them moisturized.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Experiment with different hair products, such as mousse, gel, and texturizing spray, to find what works best for your hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide: Long vs Short

Long Hair

If you're considering growing out your thick wavy hair, here's what you need to know:

  • Long hair requires regular trims to prevent split ends and keep it healthy.
  • You'll need to invest in some quality hair products, such as a deep conditioner and heat protectant, to prevent damage from styling tools.
  • Long hair can be styled in many different ways, such as braids, updos, and loose waves.
  • Long hair can be heavy and cumbersome, especially in hot weather.

Short Hair

If you're thinking about cutting your thick wavy hair short, here are some things to consider:

  • Short hair is low-maintenance and easy to style.
  • You'll need to get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Short hair can be limiting in terms of styling options, but you can still experiment with different textures and accessories.
  • Short hair can make your face appear more angular, so it's best suited for those with round or heart-shaped faces.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair

Regardless of whether you choose to keep your hair long or short, here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful locks:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Consider getting regular deep conditioning treatments at a salon.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about thick wavy hair:

Q: Is it better to wash thick wavy hair every day?

A: No, it's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping it of natural oils.

Q: Can I still straighten my hair if it's thick and wavy?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant and not straighten it too often to avoid damage.

Q: What's the best haircut for thick wavy hair?

A: It really depends on your face shape and personal style. Consult with a hairstylist to find a cut that flatters you.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some products that are great for maintaining thick wavy hair:

  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Shampoo
  • OGX Renewing + Argan Oil of Morocco Conditioner
  • Moroccanoil Treatment
  • Living Proof No Frizz Nourishing Styling Cream
  • Briogeo Scalp Revival Charcoal + Coconut Oil Micro-Exfoliating Shampoo

Whether you prefer long or short hair, the most important thing is to embrace your natural texture and find a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. With the right products and styling techniques, you can rock your thick wavy hair like a pro.
