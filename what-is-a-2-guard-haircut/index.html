What Is A 2 Guard Haircut

Written by Daniel Jul 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our blog post all about the 2 guard haircut! In this article, we will be discussing what exactly a 2 guard haircut is, how to achieve the look, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. Whether you're a hair enthusiast or someone who is just looking to switch up their hairstyle, this post is for you!

Description

A 2 guard haircut is a type of haircut that is achieved using a hair clipper with a guard size of 2. The guard size refers to the length of the hair that will be left on your head after the cut. In this case, a 2 guard will leave approximately 1/4 inch or 6mm of hair on your head. The 2 guard is a popular choice for those who want a short, low maintenance hairstyle without going completely bald.

How to Achieve a 2 Guard Haircut

Before attempting a 2 guard haircut, it's important to have the right tools. You will need a good quality hair clipper with a 2 guard attached, a comb, and a pair of scissors. It's also important to have a clean, dry head of hair.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Start by attaching the 2 guard to your hair clipper.

2. Begin by trimming the sides and back of your head, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

3. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any longer hairs that the clipper missed.

4. Move on to the top of your head, using the same technique as before.

5. Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs that you want to keep, such as bangs or a fringe.

6. Finally, use the clipper without the guard to trim the hair around your ears and the nape of your neck.

Tips

1. If you're unsure about the length of hair you want to keep, start with a larger guard size and work your way down until you find the perfect length.

2. Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head for any missed spots.

3. If you're cutting someone else's hair, make sure to communicate with them throughout the process to ensure they are happy with the length and style.

FAQs

Q: Is a 2 guard haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a 2 guard haircut can be achieved on all hair types, although it may look different depending on the texture of your hair.

Q: How often should I get a 2 guard haircut?

A: This depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. As a general rule, a 2 guard haircut should be maintained every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After getting a 2 guard haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using products such as hair oil or serum to keep the hair healthy and shiny. It's also important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

In conclusion, a 2 guard haircut is a versatile and low maintenance hairstyle that can be achieved on all hair types. With the right tools, technique, and care, you can achieve the perfect 2 guard haircut that will have you looking and feeling your best!
