How To Cut The Top Of Men s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every few weeks? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair at home? If you're a man looking to cut the top of your hair with clippers, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step, providing tips and tricks along the way. Let's get started!

Description

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. However, with the right tools and a little bit of practice, it's not as difficult as you might think. In this guide, we'll focus specifically on cutting the top of men's hair with clippers. Clippers are a versatile tool that can be used to create a variety of styles, from short and simple to long and layered. By following the steps outlined below, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.

How to Cut the Top of Men's Hair with Clippers

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary supplies on hand. You'll need a pair of clippers with guards, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a towel or cape to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Next, decide on the length you want to cut your hair. This will depend on your personal preference and the style you're going for. Use the clippers' guards to achieve the desired length. It's always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually, as it's easier to adjust the length than to add it back later.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Once you've decided on the length, use the clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut. Be sure to blend the sides and back into the top for a natural-looking finish.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Now it's time to tackle the top of your hair. Use the comb to lift the hair straight up, and use the scissors to cut any excess length. Start with small snips and work your way up gradually, checking your progress in the mirror as you go. Be careful not to cut too much off at once, as it's difficult to add it back.

Step 5: Blend the Top and Sides

Once you've cut the top to your desired length, it's time to blend it into the sides. Use the clippers without a guard or a smaller guard to trim around the edges, creating a seamless transition between the top and sides. Take your time and be careful not to cut too much off.

Step 6: Clean Up

Finally, use the clippers to clean up any stray hairs around the neck and ears. Brush off any hair clippings and take a shower to wash off any loose hairs.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut your hair:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually
  • Take your time and don't rush
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles
  • Be careful not to cut too much off at once
  • Blending is key for a natural-looking finish

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with scissors instead of clippers?

Yes, you can cut your hair with scissors instead of clippers. However, clippers are generally easier to use and provide a more even cut.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your personal preference and the style you're going for. Generally, you should aim to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't panic! If you make a mistake, take a step back and assess the situation. If it's a small mistake, you can usually fix it by trimming a little bit more off. If it's a bigger mistake, you may need to visit a professional stylist to have it corrected.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are a few tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair (e.g. blow dryers, straighteners)
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized

Cutting the top of men's hair with clippers is a simple and cost-effective way to maintain your hairstyle at home. By following the steps outlined in this guide and taking care of your hair afterwards, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut in no time.
