How To Cut Hair At Home Straight Across A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to save money on haircuts or simply prefer to do things yourself, cutting your own hair at home can be a great option. One of the easiest and most popular styles to cut at home is a straight across cut. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve this classic look.

How to:

To begin, gather the necessary supplies:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or ties

Next, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to start with clean and dry hair so that you have a clear idea of how much length you want to take off.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, part your hair down the middle and then divide each half into two sections. Clip or tie each section so that they are out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Starting with one of the bottom sections, comb out any tangles and then cut the hair straight across. Use the length of the hair in the middle as a guide, making sure to cut both sides evenly.

Step 4: Move to the Middle Section

Release the middle section of hair and comb it out. Again, using the length of the hair from the bottom section as a guide, cut the hair straight across.

Step 5: Cut the Top Sections

Lastly, release the two top sections and repeat the process.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you've cut all sections, check to make sure the hair is even. If you notice any uneven spots, simply trim those sections until you achieve your desired look.

Tips:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, be sure to cut it when it's dry to ensure the length is accurate.

FAQ:

Is it easy to cut hair at home?

While it may seem intimidating at first, cutting hair at home can be easy with the right tools and techniques.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Generally, every 6-8 weeks is a good timeframe to trim your hair to maintain its health and shape.

Can I cut my own hair if it's really long?

Yes, you can cut your own hair no matter the length. However, if you're looking to make more drastic changes, it may be best to seek the help of a professional.

Hair Care:

After cutting your hair at home, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure it stays healthy and looks its best. Some tips include:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often and always use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.

Cutting your hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a straight across cut that looks salon-worthy.
