Choose The Best Hairstyle For Your Face Shape

Written by Daniel Jul 13, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle, it's important to consider the shape of your face. Not all hairstyles are created equal, and what looks great on one person may not work for another. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks for choosing the best hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Best Hairstyle
  • Tips for Choosing the Best Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back so your face is fully visible.
  2. Take a look at the widest part of your face. This is usually around your cheekbones.
  3. Measure the length and width of your face, from your hairline to your chin.
  4. Compare your measurements to the chart below to determine your face shape.

Round Face

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The length and width of a round face are usually equal.

Oval Face

An oval face is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and a face that is longer than it is wide.

Square Face

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, a square chin, and a forehead that is the same width as the jawline.

Heart Face

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin.

Diamond Face

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead and chin, and wide cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Best Hairstyle

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose the best hairstyle. Follow these steps to find the perfect look:

  1. Consider your hair type. Is your hair thin or thick, curly or straight?
  2. Think about your lifestyle. Do you have time to style your hair every day, or do you need something low-maintenance?
  3. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines, social media, and online hair galleries to find styles you like.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you choose a style that works for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.
  5. Try it out. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hairstyle for your face shape:

  • For round faces, try a layered cut that adds volume and creates angles.
  • For oval faces, try a long, layered cut or a blunt bob.
  • For square faces, try a soft, layered cut or a side-swept fringe.
  • For heart-shaped faces, try a chin-length bob or long layers that frame the face.
  • For diamond-shaped faces, try a chin-length bob or a layered cut with volume at the crown.

FAQs

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It's common to have a combination of face shapes. Focus on the parts of your face that are most prominent and choose a style that flatters those features.

Can I still wear a certain style if it doesn't match my face shape?

Of course! These guidelines are simply suggestions to help you find the most flattering style. Ultimately, you should wear whatever makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health.

With these tips and tricks, you're well on your way to choosing the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. Remember to have fun and experiment until you find the look that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
