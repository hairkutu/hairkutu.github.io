Can Short Hair Look Good With Round Faces

Written by Daniel Jul 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short haircuts have become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. They are easy to maintain, versatile, and can help you achieve a more youthful, fresh look. However, many people with round faces worry that short haircuts may not be suitable for them. In this article, we will explore whether short hair can look good on round faces and provide tips on how to choose the right haircut for your face shape.

Description

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for your round face, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, you need to determine the shape of your face. Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved lines, and lack of sharp angles. They typically have a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a rounded jawline and chin.

While some people may feel self-conscious about their round face, it is actually a very versatile shape that can look great with a variety of haircuts. With the right styling techniques and cut, short hair can look amazing on round faces and help to accentuate your best features.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it is important to consider the length and shape of the cut. A short bob or pixie cut can work well on a round face, as they help to create the illusion of length and height. A layered or asymmetrical cut can also be flattering, as they add movement and texture to the hair.

When styling your short haircut, it is important to avoid adding volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, focus on adding volume at the crown of your head, which will help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Step by Step

1. Determine the shape of your face.

2. Choose a short haircut that works well with your face shape.

3. Avoid adding volume to the sides of your face.

4. Focus on adding volume at the crown of your head.

Tips

1. Consider the texture of your hair when choosing a short haircut.

2. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the right look for you.

3. Ask your hairstylist for advice on which haircut would suit your face shape best.

FAQ

Can a bob haircut look good on a round face?

Yes, a bob can look great on a round face, especially if it is a longer bob that falls just below the chin.

What is the best way to style short hair on a round face?

The best way to style short hair on a round face is to focus on adding volume at the crown of your head, rather than the sides of your face.

What should I avoid when choosing a short haircut for a round face?

Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it is important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.

Regular trims are also important to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Conclusion

Short hair can look amazing on round faces, as long as you choose the right cut and styling techniques. Remember to focus on adding volume at the crown of your head, and avoid adding volume to the sides of your face. With the right haircut and care, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look that enhances your best features.
