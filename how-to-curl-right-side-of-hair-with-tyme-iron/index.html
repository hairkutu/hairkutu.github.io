How To Curl The Right Side Of Your Hair With Tyme Iron

Written by Eveline Sep 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to curl the right side of your hair, using a Tyme Iron is a great option. In this blog post, we'll go over how to use a Tyme Iron to achieve beautiful curls on the right side of your hair. We'll also provide some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your Tyme Iron and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Description

Tyme Iron is a hair styling tool that can be used to straighten or curl your hair. It's designed to be easy to use and can help you achieve salon-quality results at home. The Tyme Iron has a unique design that allows you to create a variety of different hairstyles, including curls, waves, and straight styles.

How to Curl the Right Side of Your Hair with Tyme Iron

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to curl the right side of your hair with a Tyme Iron:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the Tyme Iron's heat.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is prepped, you'll need to section it so that you can curl it more easily. Use a clip to hold the left side of your hair out of the way, and then divide the right side of your hair into small sections.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Tyme Iron

Next, you'll need to heat up your Tyme Iron. Set the temperature to the desired level for your hair type (lower for fine or damaged hair, higher for thick or coarse hair). Wait for the Tyme Iron to heat up before moving on to the next step.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Starting at the top of your head, place a small section of hair between the Tyme Iron's plates. Twist the Tyme Iron away from your face, wrapping the hair around the barrel as you go. Hold the Tyme Iron in place for a few seconds, and then release the curl. Repeat this process with each section of hair until you've curled the entire right side of your head.

Step 5: Finish Your Style

Once you've curled the right side of your hair, you can finish your style by applying a light-hold hairspray. Gently finger-comb your curls to loosen them up and give them a more natural look.

Tips for Using a Tyme Iron

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your Tyme Iron:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage
  • Section your hair to make it easier to curl
  • Adjust the temperature to suit your hair type
  • Be careful not to burn your fingers with the Tyme Iron's hot plates
  • Practice makes perfect!

FAQ

Can I use a Tyme Iron on wet hair?

No, you should always start with clean, dry hair when using a Tyme Iron. Using it on wet hair can cause damage and make it more difficult to achieve the desired style.

How long does it take to curl my hair with a Tyme Iron?

The amount of time it takes to curl your hair with a Tyme Iron will depend on the length and thickness of your hair. However, most people can curl their hair in about 10-20 minutes.

Can I use a Tyme Iron to straighten my hair?

Yes, the Tyme Iron can be used to straighten hair as well as curl it. Simply use the flat plates of the Tyme Iron to smooth out your hair and create a sleek, straight style.

Hair Care

Using a Tyme Iron can be a great way to achieve beautiful curls, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

By following these tips and using a Tyme Iron properly, you can achieve beautiful, healthy-looking curls on the right side of your hair.
