Will Your Face Shape Change

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 · 5 min read

As we age, our bodies go through a lot of changes, including our faces. One of the most common concerns people have is whether their face shape will change over time. In this article, we’ll explore the answer to this question and provide tips to help you take care of your face and hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Face shape is determined by the bone structure of your face. There are several types of face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. While your face shape is largely determined by genetics, it can also be affected by weight gain or loss, aging, and even certain hairstyles.

How to

There are a few things you can do to help maintain your face shape as you age:

  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Wear sunscreen to protect your skin from sun damage
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Exercise regularly to keep your facial muscles toned

Step by step

Here are some steps you can take to help maintain your face shape:

  1. Start by maintaining a healthy weight through diet and exercise.
  2. Wear sunscreen every day to protect your skin from sun damage, which can cause premature aging and changes to your face shape.
  3. Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, which can damage your skin and cause wrinkles and sagging.
  4. Exercise your facial muscles regularly by doing facial exercises, such as smiling, frowning, and puckering your lips.
  5. Consider getting regular facials or other skin treatments to help keep your skin looking healthy and youthful.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help maintain your face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can cause damage to your hairline and alter the appearance of your face shape.
  • Try to sleep on your back to avoid putting pressure on your face and causing wrinkles.
  • Use a moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.
  • Consider getting a facial massage to help increase blood flow to your face and promote healthy skin.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about face shape:

Can your face shape change over time?

Yes, your face shape can change over time due to aging, weight gain or loss, and other factors.

Is it possible to change your face shape?

While it’s not possible to change the bone structure of your face, there are certain hairstyles and makeup techniques that can help alter the appearance of your face shape.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining your face shape, your hair can play a big role. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and taking care of your face and hair, you can help maintain your face shape and keep your skin looking healthy and youthful.
