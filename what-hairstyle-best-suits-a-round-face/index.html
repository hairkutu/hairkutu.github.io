What Hairstyle Best Suits A Round Face

Written by Oliver Jun 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you someone with a round face and struggling to find a hairstyle that suits you? Look no further because we have got you covered! In this article, we will discuss what hairstyle best suits a round face, how to style it, and tips to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are wider in the cheek area and have a softer jawline. The goal is to create an illusion of a longer face and a more defined jawline. The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds length to the face and creates angles to balance the softness of the features. Below are some hairstyles that work best for a round face.

How to

The first step to finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Once you know you have a round face, it will be easier to choose a hairstyle that suits you. Hairstyles with layers, angles, and height on the crown work best for a round face. Avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides as it will only make your face look rounder.

Step by Step

Here are some hairstyles that work best for a round face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers add length to the face and make it look slimmer. It also adds movement and texture to the hair, making it look more voluminous. To style, blow-dry your hair with a round brush and add some texturizing spray for an effortless look.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs create an angle on the face and draw attention away from the roundness. It is a low maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style. To style, blow-dry your hair and use a flat iron to create a side-swept bang.

3. Bob with Angled Layers

A bob with angled layers creates a flattering shape for a round face. It adds volume on the crown and creates an angle on the face. To style, blow-dry your hair with a round brush and use a flat iron to create an angle.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides.
  • Choose hairstyles with layers and angles.
  • Side-swept bangs create an angle on the face.
  • A bob with angled layers creates a flattering shape for a round face.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush for volume.
  • Use a flat iron to create angles on the hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just make sure to create angles on the hair to balance the softness of the features.

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a round face. Just make sure to keep the top longer and create angles on the sides.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to avoid split ends.

Now that you know what hairstyle best suits a round face, it's time to book that appointment with your stylist and get ready to rock your new look!
