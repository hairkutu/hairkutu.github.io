Is Having Braids Haram

Written by Christine Apr 18, 2023 · 6 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, braids are a popular choice for many people. However, for those who follow the Islamic faith, there may be some confusion about whether having braids is haram or not. In this article, we will explore this topic in detail, including how to style braids, tips for maintaining them, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that having braids is haram because it is seen as imitating the hairstyles of non-Muslims. However, there is no clear consensus on this issue, and it ultimately comes down to personal interpretation and beliefs.

Some scholars argue that as long as the braids are not overly extravagant or immodest, they are permissible. Others believe that any hairstyle that imitates non-Muslims is haram, including braids.

Ultimately, the decision to have braids is a personal one, and it is up to individuals to decide whether they believe it is haram or not.

How to

If you do decide to have braids, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips for styling your braids:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Divide your hair into small sections using a comb.
  • Braid each section tightly, making sure to keep the braid close to your scalp.
  • Secure the end of each braid with a small elastic band.
  • If desired, you can add hair extensions to make your braids longer or thicker.

Step by step

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair or let it air dry.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections using a comb.
  4. Take one section of hair and divide it into three equal parts.
  5. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  6. Cross the left section over the new middle section.
  7. Continue braiding in this pattern until you reach the end of the section.
  8. Secure the end of the braid with a small elastic band.
  9. Repeat steps 4-8 for each section of hair.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your braids:

  • Use a satin or silk scarf to wrap your hair at night to prevent frizz and tangling.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products on your braids, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.
  • Wash your braids every 2-3 weeks to keep them clean and fresh.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your scalp moisturized.

FAQ

Is it haram to have braids?

There is no clear consensus on whether having braids is haram or not. It ultimately comes down to personal interpretation and beliefs.

Can I wear braids if I am Muslim?

Yes, as long as you feel comfortable doing so and it aligns with your personal beliefs.

Are there any specific guidelines for wearing braids as a Muslim?

There are no specific guidelines, but it is generally recommended to keep the braids modest and not overly extravagant.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you choose to have braids or not, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to moisturize your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and nutrients to promote hair growth.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement, such as biotin or vitamin E, to support healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, whether having braids is haram or not is a personal decision that each individual must make for themselves. If you do choose to have braids, make sure to take proper care of them to keep them looking healthy and beautiful.
