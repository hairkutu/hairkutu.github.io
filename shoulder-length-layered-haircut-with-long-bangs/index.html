Shoulder Length Layered Haircut With Long Bangs A Trendy Look For 2023

Written by Frank May 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a chic and versatile hairstyle, the shoulder length layered haircut with long bangs may be the perfect option for you. This trendy look has been popular for several years and is expected to continue to be in style for 2023. In this blog post, we'll show you how to achieve this look, provide tips and answer some frequently asked questions about this haircut, and offer hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Description

The shoulder length layered haircut with long bangs is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. This style typically features shoulder-length hair that is cut into layers to create movement and texture. Long bangs are also incorporated into the style, which can be swept to the side or worn straight down. This haircut works well for both straight and wavy hair and can be styled in a variety of ways, including straight, curly, or wavy.

How to Achieve the Shoulder Length Layered Haircut with Long Bangs

If you're interested in trying this trendy haircut, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Before getting your hair cut, it's important to consult with your hairstylist to determine if this style will work for your hair type and face shape. Your hairstylist will be able to recommend the best length and layering techniques for your hair.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Before your haircut, make sure to shampoo and condition your hair. This will help to remove any product buildup and leave your hair clean and healthy.

Step 3: Get Your Hair Cut

Once you've consulted with your hairstylist and shampooed your hair, it's time to get your hair cut. Your hairstylist will use a variety of cutting techniques to create layers and shape your hair. They will also incorporate long bangs into the style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After your haircut, it's time to style your hair. This style can be worn straight, curly, or wavy. You can use a flat iron, curling iron, or hot rollers to achieve your desired look. Finish off with hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Shoulder Length Layered Haircut with Long Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

When styling your hair with heat tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

To maintain the shape and style of your haircut, it's important to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Tip 3: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Q: Will the Shoulder Length Layered Haircut with Long Bangs work for all face shapes?

A: This style can work for most face shapes, but it's important to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and layering techniques for your face shape.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail with this style?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail with this style. However, the long bangs may need to be pinned back or styled to the side to keep them out of your face.

Q: Can I color my hair with this style?

A: Yes, you can color your hair with this style. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hair color for your skin tone and to ensure that your hair remains healthy and hydrated after coloring.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

Tip 1: Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Using a sulfate-free shampoo will help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

Tip 2: Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling your hair with heat tools can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of heat tools and opt for air-drying your hair when possible.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth and prevent breakage.

Overall, the shoulder length layered haircut with long bangs is a stylish and versatile haircut that can work for many hair types and face shapes. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish all year round.
