How To Cut Curly Hair Straight Across

Written by Petter Jul 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be difficult to maintain and style. One of the most challenging parts of having curly hair is getting a straight across cut that looks good. In this article, we will go over the steps you can take to cut your curly hair straight across.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Curly Hair Straight Across
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Cut Curly Hair Straight Across

The first thing you need to do is make sure that your hair is clean and dry. You don't want to cut your hair while it's wet because it will shrink up as it dries, and you won't get an accurate cut.

Next, you'll need to section your hair. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then bring the hair in front of your shoulders. Separate the front section into two even parts, one on each side of your head.

Now, take one of the sections and bring it forward so that it is parallel to the floor. Comb the hair straight down and then take your scissors and cut straight across. Do this for both sides.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Part your hair down the middle.

3. Bring the front section of your hair forward and divide it into two even parts.

4. Cut one section straight across.

5. Repeat on the other side.

6. Comb your hair straight down to check for any unevenness.

7. Trim any parts that need it.

8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

- Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

- Take your time and don't rush the process.

- Start with a little bit of hair and gradually work your way up to avoid cutting too much off.

- If you have really curly hair, consider cutting it while it's wet and then letting it dry before doing any touch-ups.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my curly hair straight across myself?
A: Yes, you can cut your curly hair straight across yourself. Just make sure you follow the steps outlined in this article.

Q: How often should I cut my curly hair?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it starts to look unkempt. Generally, every 6-8 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
- Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle it.
- Use a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
- Don't over-wash your hair. Curly hair tends to be dry, so washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.
- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and using the steps outlined in this article, you can successfully cut your curly hair straight across and have it looking great in no time!
