How To Install A Frontal Bob Wig Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Looking to switch up your hairstyle without damaging your natural hair? A frontal bob wig is the perfect solution! Installing a wig may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a flawless and natural-looking installation. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of installing a frontal bob wig.

Table of Contents

  • How to Install a Frontal Bob Wig
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips for Installing a Wig
  • FAQs about Wig Installation
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Wig

How to Install a Frontal Bob Wig

The first step to installing a frontal bob wig is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Wig cap
  • Tweezers
  • Scissors
  • Adhesive (either wig glue or tape)
  • Comb

Once you have all the necessary tools, you can move on to the installation process.

Step-By-Step Guide

Follow these steps to install your frontal bob wig:

  1. Prepare your natural hair by braiding it into cornrows or wrapping it flat against your head.
  2. Put on the wig cap to secure your natural hair and create a smooth surface for the wig to sit on.
  3. Place the wig on your head, ensuring that the hairline aligns with your natural hairline.
  4. Use tweezers to pluck the excess hair from the frontal lace to create a more natural hairline.
  5. Cut the lace using scissors, leaving a small amount of lace to attach to your head.
  6. Apply adhesive to the perimeter of the wig cap, either using wig glue or tape.
  7. Press the wig down onto the adhesive, starting from the center and working your way outwards.
  8. Use a comb to blend your natural hair with the wig hair for a seamless look.

Congratulations, you have successfully installed your frontal bob wig! Now it's time to style it to your liking.

Tips for Installing a Wig

Here are some tips to make your wig installation process even smoother:

  • Always make sure your natural hair is flat and smooth before putting on the wig cap.
  • Use a wig cap that matches your skin tone for a more natural look.
  • Apply adhesive sparingly to avoid damaging your natural hair.
  • Use a scarf or headband to secure the wig in place while the adhesive dries.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and parting options to find what suits you best.

FAQs about Wig Installation

Here are some common questions about wig installation:

How long will my wig installation last?

With proper care and maintenance, your wig installation can last for several weeks to a few months.

Can I swim or exercise with my wig on?

It's best to avoid getting your wig wet or sweaty, as this can damage the adhesive and cause the wig to shift or come off.

How do I remove my wig?

To remove your wig, gently lift the edges of the wig and peel it off your head. Use a wig adhesive remover to remove any leftover adhesive.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wig

Proper hair care is essential to prolong the lifespan of your wig. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig every 2-4 weeks, depending on how often you wear it.
  • Use a wig shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for synthetic or human hair wigs.
  • Allow your wig to air dry on a wig stand to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on synthetic wigs, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Store your wig in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a flawless and natural-looking installation for your frontal bob wig. Have fun experimenting with different styles and enjoy your new hairstyle!
