How To Scissor Cut The Top Of Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money on haircuts or just want to try your hand at cutting your own hair, learning how to scissor cut the top of men's hair is a great place to start. With a few tools and some basic techniques, you can achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut right at home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to scissor cut the top of men's hair and provide tips and tricks for achieving the best results.

How to Scissor Cut the Top of Men's Hair

Tools You'll Need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips (optional)

Before you get started, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb for sectioning the hair, and hair clips (if you want to clip away sections of hair as you work).

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Start by washing and towel-drying the hair. This will make it easier to work with and help you achieve a more even cut.

2. Use the comb to section off the top of the hair, starting at the front hairline and working your way back. Use hair clips to clip away any sections you're not working on.

3. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight up. Hold it between your fingers at the length you want to cut it.

4. Use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, following the line of your fingers. Be sure to use sharp, clean scissors and make clean cuts to avoid split ends.

5. Continue working your way through each section of hair, taking small sections at a time and cutting them to the desired length.

6. Once you've cut all the sections, comb the hair straight up and check for any uneven spots. Use the scissors to trim any areas that need it.

7. Style the hair as desired using a comb or your fingers.

Tips for Scissor Cutting Men's Hair:

- Always start by cutting less hair than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more if needed.

- Use your fingers as a guide to ensure an even cut. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across.

- Take breaks if you need to. Cutting hair can be tiring, so give yourself a break if you start to feel fatigued.

- Use a comb to help guide your scissors and ensure a straight cut.

FAQ:

Q: Can I scissor cut the top of men's hair with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can scissor cut curly hair. Just be sure to cut each curl individually and use a comb to help guide your scissors.

Q: Can I scissor cut the top of men's hair with thinning hair?

A: Yes, you can scissor cut thinning hair. Just be sure to take smaller sections of hair and cut less off each time to avoid making the hair look too thin.

Hair Care:

After you've finished cutting your hair, be sure to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized, and consider using styling products to help hold the shape of the haircut. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks will also help keep the hair looking its best.

Learning how to scissor cut the top of men's hair can be a great way to save money and achieve a stylish haircut right at home. With the proper tools and techniques, anyone can master this skill and achieve great results.
