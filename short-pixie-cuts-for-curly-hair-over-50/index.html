Short Pixie Cuts For Curly Hair Over 50 A Guide To Effortlessly Stylish Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, frizzy hair that takes hours to style and manage? Or maybe you're simply looking for a new, fresh look to spice up your appearance. Whatever your reason may be, a short pixie cut for curly hair over 50 is a trendy and low-maintenance option that can instantly transform your look. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about rocking a pixie cut with curly hair over 50, including how to style it, tips for maintenance, and more.

Why Choose a Short Pixie Cut for Curly Hair Over 50?

A short pixie cut is a bold and chic hairstyle that can work wonders for women over 50 with curly hair. Not only is it a low-maintenance option that can save you time and effort, but it can also help you look and feel younger and more confident. A short pixie cut can flatter your facial features and highlight your natural curls, creating a stylish and effortless look that's perfect for any occasion.

How to Choose the Right Pixie Cut for You

Before you decide to go for a short pixie cut, it's important to choose the right style that will complement your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences. Some popular pixie cut styles for curly hair over 50 include:

  • Layered pixie cut
  • Cropped pixie cut
  • Asymmetrical pixie cut
  • Textured pixie cut

Consult with your hairstylist to determine which style will work best for you based on your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle.

How to Style Your Short Pixie Cut

One of the best things about a short pixie cut for curly hair over 50 is its versatility. You can style it in a variety of ways depending on your mood and occasion. Here are some styling tips:

  • Use a curl-defining cream or mousse to enhance your natural curls and add texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to avoid frizz and create volume.
  • Accessorize with headbands, scarves, or hairpins to add a pop of color and personality to your look.

How to Maintain Your Short Pixie Cut

Keeping your short pixie cut looking fresh and stylish requires some maintenance. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

Can I still dye my hair if I have a pixie cut?

Absolutely! You can still experiment with different hair colors and highlightsâeven with a short pixie cut. Just be sure to consult with your hairstylist to choose the right shade and type of dye for your hair type and skin tone.

Will a short pixie cut make me look older?

No, not necessarily. In fact, a short pixie cut can make you look and feel younger and more vibrant, especially when styled properly.

Can I still wear hair extensions with a short pixie cut?

While it's possible to wear hair extensions with a short pixie cut, it may not be necessary since the cut itself can create volume and texture. If you decide to go for extensions, make sure they blend seamlessly with your natural hair and are properly installed to avoid damage.

Hair Care Tips for Short Pixie Cuts for Curly Hair Over 50

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your short pixie cut looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent stripping it of its natural oils. Instead, try washing it every other day or every few days.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can damage your curls and cause frizz. Use lukewarm or cool water instead.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a short pixie cut for curly hair over 50 and enjoy a stylish, low-maintenance hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. So go ahead, embrace your curls, and show off your new look!
