Does A Beard Make Your Face Look Longer

Written by Daniel May 14, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re considering growing a beard but aren’t sure how it will change the look of your face, you may be wondering, “Does a beard make your face look longer?” The answer isn’t straightforward, as it depends on various factors. In this article, we’ll explore how a beard can affect the perceived length of your face and provide tips on how to choose the right beard style for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. How Beard Length Affects Face Shape
  3. Best Beard Styles for Different Face Shapes
  4. Tips for Maintaining a Healthy Beard
  5. FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining whether a beard will make your face look longer is to identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Diamond
  • Heart
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face. Look at the outline of your face and compare it to the six shapes listed above.

How Beard Length Affects Face Shape

The length of your beard can affect how long your face appears. Generally, longer beards can elongate the face, while shorter beards can make the face appear shorter. However, this isn’t always the case, as the shape of your beard can also impact the perceived length of your face.

For example, a beard that is narrow at the chin and wider at the sides can make a round face appear longer. On the other hand, a beard that is wider at the chin and narrower at the sides can make a long face appear shorter.

Best Beard Styles for Different Face Shapes

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can choose a beard style that complements your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round Face: A beard that is narrow at the chin and wider at the sides can help elongate the face. Avoid wide beards that make the face appear rounder.
  • Oval Face: Most beard styles will suit an oval face, so choose a style that you like and that complements your features.
  • Square Face: A beard that is short on the sides and longer at the chin can help soften the angles of a square face.
  • Diamond Face: A beard that is shorter at the chin and longer at the sides can help balance the features of a diamond-shaped face.
  • Heart Face: A beard that is wider at the chin and narrower at the sides can help balance the features of a heart-shaped face.
  • Triangle Face: A beard that is wider at the chin and narrower at the sides can help balance the features of a triangle-shaped face.

Tips for Maintaining a Healthy Beard

Once you’ve chosen a beard style, it’s important to maintain it properly to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to soften and hydrate the hair.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain the desired length and shape.
  • Brush your beard daily to remove tangles and distribute oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers, on your beard as they can damage the hair.

FAQs

Q: Will a beard make my face look longer?

A: The length and shape of your beard can affect the perceived length of your face. Longer beards can elongate the face, while shorter beards can make the face appear shorter. However, the shape of your beard can also impact the perceived length of your face.

Q: How do I choose the right beard style for my face shape?

A: Choose a beard style that complements your features. For example, a beard that is narrow at the chin and wider at the sides can help elongate a round face, while a beard that is shorter at the chin and longer at the sides can help balance the features of a diamond-shaped face.

Q: How do I maintain a healthy beard?

A: Wash your beard regularly with a mild shampoo, use a beard oil or balm to soften and hydrate the hair, trim your beard regularly, brush your beard daily, and avoid using hot tools on your beard.
