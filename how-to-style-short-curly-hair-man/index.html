How To Style Short Curly Hair For Men Tips And Tricks

Short curly hair can be a bit challenging to style for men, but with the right techniques and products, it can look stylish and effortless. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to style short curly hair for men.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Curly Hair for Men
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair for Men
  • FAQs

How to Style Short Curly Hair for Men

The first step to styling short curly hair for men is to find the right haircut. You'll want to choose a cut that complements your natural curls and face shape. Some popular short haircuts for men with curly hair include the high and tight, crew cut, and side part.

Once you have the right haircut, you'll need to invest in some styling products. A good styling cream or pomade can help tame frizz and define your curls. You'll also want to invest in a good quality hair dryer with a diffuser attachment, as this can help enhance your curls.

Products you will need:

  • Styling cream or pomade
  • Hair dryer with diffuser attachment
  • Wide-tooth comb

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to style short curly hair for men:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too vigorously as this can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your hair, focusing on the ends and avoiding the roots.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your curls.
  5. Attach the diffuser attachment to your hair dryer and set it to low heat and low speed.
  6. Start drying your hair using the diffuser attachment, scrunching your hair upwards as you go.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently scrunch your curls to add definition.
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair for Men

Here are some additional tips for styling short curly hair for men:

  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different haircuts and styles to find what works best for your curls.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: How can I tame frizz in my short curly hair?

A: Use a good quality styling cream or pomade to help tame frizz, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Q: Can I blow dry my short curly hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to use a diffuser attachment and low heat to avoid damaging your curls.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your short curly hair:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily style your short curly hair and keep it looking healthy and stylish.
