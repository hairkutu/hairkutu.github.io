How To Know How I Would Look With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you considering chopping off your locks but are unsure if you would look good with short hair? Don't worry; you're not alone. Many women find themselves in the same predicament, but the good news is that there are ways to determine how you would look with short hair before you make the cut. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to know how you would look with short hair. We will also share some tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice to help you make an informed decision.

Table of Content

  • How to Know How You Would Look with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Consider
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Know How You Would Look with Short Hair

There are several ways to determine how you would look with short hair. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Use a Virtual Hairstyle App

Virtual hairstyle apps have become increasingly popular in recent years, and for a good reason. These apps allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles, including short haircuts. You can experiment with different styles and see how they would look on your face shape and skin tone. Some of the most popular virtual hairstyle apps include:

  • Hairstyle Try On
  • Virtual Makeover
  • ModiFace

2. Consult with a Hairstylist

A hairstylist can provide you with expert advice on what short haircut would suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also show you photos of their previous clients with similar features who have had short haircuts. If you're unsure about cutting your hair, booking a consultation with a hairstylist can give you peace of mind.

3. Try on a Wig

If you're not ready to commit to a short haircut, trying on a wig can give you an idea of how you would look with short hair. You can experiment with different styles and colors and see what suits you best. Wigs are available in different lengths, textures, and price points, so you're sure to find one that fits your taste and budget.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know how to determine how you would look with short hair let's go through a step-by-step guide on how to make the cut:

Step 1: Research Short Haircuts

Before you make the cut, it's essential to do your research. Look for short haircuts that suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. You can use social media platforms like Instagram and Pinterest to find inspiration and save photos of hairstyles you like.

Step 2: Book an Appointment with a Hairstylist

Once you've decided on a short haircut, it's time to book an appointment with a hairstylist. Make sure to choose a hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair and can provide you with expert advice.

Step 3: Discuss Your Haircut with Your Hairstylist

During your appointment, discuss your haircut with your hairstylist. Show them photos of the hairstyle you want and let them know if you have any concerns or questions. Your hairstylist will provide you with advice on what will suit you best and how to maintain your new haircut.

Step 4: Make the Cut

Now that you've discussed your haircut with your hairstylist, it's time to make the cut. Sit back, relax, and let your hairstylist work their magic. Once they're done, you can admire your new look in the mirror.

Tips to Consider

Here are some tips to consider when deciding on a short haircut:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair.
  • Think about the length you want and how short you're willing to go.
  • Consider the maintenance and upkeep of your new haircut.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will a short haircut suit me?

A short haircut can suit anyone regardless of their face shape or hair type. It's all about choosing the right style that complements your features and suits your lifestyle.

2. How do I know if I'm ready for a short haircut?

If you're considering a short haircut, ask yourself if you're ready for a change. Short haircuts can be liberating and refreshing, but they require a certain level of confidence and commitment.

3. How do I maintain my short haircut?

Short haircuts require regular maintenance and upkeep to keep them looking their best. Your hairstylist will provide you with advice on how to maintain your new haircut, including how often to get it trimmed and what products to use.

Hair Care Advice

Short hair requires different care than long hair. Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh your hair down.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know how to know how you would look with short hair, you can confidently make the cut. Remember, a short haircut can be a liberating and refreshing change, so embrace it and enjoy your new look!
