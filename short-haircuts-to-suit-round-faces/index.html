Short Haircuts To Suit Round Faces

Description

Short haircuts are a great way to show off your features and add some volume to your hair. If you have a round face, you may be wondering what the best short haircuts are for you. The good news is that there are plenty of options that can help you show off your best features and flatter your face shape.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect short haircut for your round face is to determine your face shape. One easy way to do this is to look in a mirror and see if your face is as wide as it is long. If it is, then you have a round face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can begin to look for short haircuts that will flatter your features. Some great options for round faces include pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts. These styles can help to add some height and volume to your hair, which can help to elongate your face and make it appear less round.

Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts are a great option for round faces because they can help to elongate your face and add some height to your hair. This style is typically cut short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. This can help to create a more angular look, which can help to balance out your round face shape.

Bob Cuts

Bob cuts are another great option for round faces. This style is typically cut to chin-length or just below, with layers that frame the face. This can help to create a more angular look, which can help to balance out your round face shape. You can also add some texture to your bob by adding some loose waves or curls.

Layered Cuts

Layered cuts are a great option for round faces because they can help to add some volume and height to your hair. This style typically features layers that are cut at different lengths, which can help to create a more textured look. This can help to elongate your face and make it appear less round.

Step by Step

If you have decided to go for a short haircut to suit your round face, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Choose a short haircut that flatters your features
  3. Show your hair stylist a picture of the style you want
  4. Ask your hair stylist for advice on how to style your new haircut
  5. Follow your hair stylist's advice on how to care for your new haircut

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for your round face:

  • Avoid cuts that are too short or too blunt, as these can make your face appear rounder
  • Choose a cut that adds some height and volume to your hair
  • Consider adding some texture to your hair with loose waves or curls
  • Be sure to ask your hair stylist for advice on how to style your new haircut
  • Invest in some good quality hair products to help keep your new haircut looking great

FAQ

What short haircuts are best for round faces?

Pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts are all great options for round faces because they can help to elongate your face and add some height to your hair.

What should I avoid when choosing a short haircut for my round face?

Avoid cuts that are too short or too blunt, as these can make your face appear rounder. Instead, look for cuts that add some height and volume to your hair.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen a short haircut for your round face, it is important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking great. Be sure to use good quality hair products, such as shampoo and conditioner, to keep your hair healthy and shiny. You may also want to invest in some styling products, such as mousse or hairspray, to help keep your new haircut looking great.

With the right short haircut and some good hair care, you can show off your best features and flatter your round face shape.
