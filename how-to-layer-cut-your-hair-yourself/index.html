How To Layer Cut Your Hair Yourself Step By Step Guide 2023

Written by Frank May 31, 2023 · 7 min read
How to Layer Cut Your Hair Yourself - Step by Step Guide 2023

Are you tired of shelling out money to a salon every time you need a haircut? Layer cutting your hair yourself can be a great way to save money and time. It may seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it’s totally doable. In this article, we’ll show you how to layer cut your hair yourself in a relaxed, easy-to-understand language.

How to Layer Cut Your Hair Yourself

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let’s go over some of the tools and preparations you’ll need to do.

What You’ll Need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Towel or cape to protect your clothes

Preparation:

Wash your hair and let it dry. It’s best to work with clean, dry hair as it’s easier to see the layers. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these easy steps to layer cut your hair yourself:

Step 1: Part Your Hair

Start by parting your hair down the middle. Use a comb to make a straight part from your forehead to the back of your head, dividing your hair into two equal sections.

Step 2: Tie Up the Top Section

Clip or tie up the top section of your hair so it’s out of the way. This will make it easier to work on the bottom section first.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Take the bottom section of your hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and cut straight across. This will be the base layer of your haircut.

Step 4: Release the Top Section

Unclip or untie the top section of your hair. This section will be the top layer of your haircut.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

Take a small section of hair from the top layer and hold it straight up. Slide your fingers down to the desired length of hair you want to cut. Use the base layer as a guide to cut straight across. Repeat this process for the rest of the top layer.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Once you’ve finished cutting the top layer, blend the layers by combing through your hair. Cut any longer strands that are sticking out.

Step 7: Check the Layers

Check the layers by standing in front of a mirror and looking at the back of your hair. Make any necessary adjustments to even out the layers.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Now that you’ve successfully layer cut your hair, it’s time to style it. You can use a curling iron, straightener or blow dryer to create your desired look.

Tips for Layer Cutting Your Hair Yourself

Here are some tips to keep in mind when layer cutting your hair yourself:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use sharp scissors to get a clean cut.
  • Always cut the hair in a straight line.
  • Don’t rush the process. Take your time and be patient.
  • If you’re unsure, it’s best to cut less hair rather than more.

FAQs

Can I layer cut my hair with any type of scissors?

No, it’s best to use sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Using regular scissors can result in split ends and a jagged cut.

How often should I layer cut my hair?

It depends on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it’s recommended to layer cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

Layer cutting your hair can be a great way to freshen up your look, but it’s important to take care of your hair afterwards. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.

Layer cutting your hair yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your home. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips, and you’ll be on your way to a fabulous new look!
