The Best Haircut For Diamond Shaped Face Female A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 25, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you are in luck! This face shape is considered one of the most versatile, as it can pull off a wide variety of hairstyles. However, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect haircut that flatters your features. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about the best haircut for diamond shaped face female in relaxed English language.

Description

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a pointed chin. The goal of choosing the best haircut for diamond shaped face female is to create balance and enhance your features. The right haircut can soften your jawline and bring attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

How to Determine if You Have a Diamond Shaped Face

If you are unsure about your face shape, there are a few simple steps you can take to determine if you have a diamond shaped face. First, measure your face. The length of your face should be roughly equal to the width of your cheekbones. Next, look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker. You should see a diamond shape with the widest point at your cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Best Haircut for Diamond Shaped Face Female

Now that you know you have a diamond-shaped face, it's time to choose the best haircut for you. Here are some tips:

1. Emphasize Your Cheekbones

Your prominent cheekbones are one of your best features, so choose a haircut that emphasizes them. Long layers that start at your cheekbones and frame your face are a great option. This will draw attention to your cheekbones and soften your jawline.

2. Soften Your Jawline

Since diamond-shaped faces have a pointed chin, you want to soften this area with your haircut. A bob that ends at your chin or just below it is a great option. This will create balance and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

3. Avoid Volume at the Crown

Since diamond-shaped faces are already narrow at the forehead, you want to avoid adding volume to this area. This can make your face look even narrower. Instead, opt for a side part and long layers that start at your cheekbones.

4. Bangs are Your Friend

Bangs are a great way to balance out a diamond-shaped face. Soft, side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a wider forehead and make your face look more oval-shaped. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can emphasize your narrow forehead.

5. Experiment with Different Lengths

While a bob that ends at your chin is a great option, don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths. Shoulder-length hair can also be flattering on a diamond-shaped face. Just make sure the layers start at your cheekbones to create balance.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have chosen the best haircut for your diamond-shaped face, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential to maintaining the shape of your haircut. You should aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. This will help you achieve the desired look and keep your hair healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat and sun damage by using a heat protectant spray and wearing a hat when you are outside for extended periods of time.

FAQs

1. Can I Wear Short Hair with a Diamond Shaped Face?

Yes, you can wear short hair with a diamond-shaped face. However, it's important to choose a style that creates balance and softens your jawline. A pixie cut with longer layers around the face can be a great option.

2. Should I Avoid Updos?

No, you do not need to avoid updos. However, it's important to create balance with your updo. Consider adding volume to the top of your head to balance out your narrow forehead.

Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Face Female

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, follow these tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

2. Use a Heat Protectant Spray

Before using heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

In conclusion, choosing the best haircut for diamond shaped face female is all about creating balance and emphasizing your best features. Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the perfect haircut for you. Remember to maintain your haircut with regular trims and use the right products to keep your hair healthy and looking great.
