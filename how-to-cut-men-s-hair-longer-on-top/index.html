How To Cut Men s Hair Longer On Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting men's hair can be tricky, especially if you're going for a longer style on top. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great look that your client will love. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of how to cut men's hair longer on top, from start to finish. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: How to Prepare Your Client's Hair
  • Section 2: How to Cut Men's Hair Longer on Top
  • Section 3: Tips for Cutting Men's Hair Longer on Top
  • Section 4: Frequently Asked Questions
  • Section 5: Hair Care Tips for Men with Long Hair

Section 1: How to Prepare Your Client's Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to prepare your client's hair. This will help ensure that you get the best results possible.

First, make sure your client's hair is clean and dry. If it's wet, it will be harder to see the length and texture of the hair, which could lead to mistakes.

Next, comb through the hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the final result is even.

Finally, consider using a styling product to help hold the hair in place. This can be especially helpful if your client has thick or unruly hair. A small amount of gel or wax can help keep the hair in place while you work.

Section 2: How to Cut Men's Hair Longer on Top

Now it's time to start cutting! Follow these steps to achieve a longer style on top:

  1. Start by sectioning off the top of the hair. Use a comb to separate the hair at the crown of the head.
  2. Using scissors, trim the hair along the top section to the desired length. It's best to start with small cuts and work your way up to the final length.
  3. Next, blend the sides and back of the hair into the longer top section. Use scissors or clippers to create a gradual fade from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  4. Finally, use thinning shears to add texture to the longer hair on top. This will help create a natural, tousled look.

Remember to take your time and check your work frequently. It's better to make small adjustments as you go than to have to start over from scratch.

Section 3: Tips for Cutting Men's Hair Longer on Top

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting men's hair longer on top:

  • Start with a shorter length and work your way up. It's easier to add length than to take it away.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors and create a straight, even cut.
  • Blend the sides and back of the hair into the longer top section for a natural look.
  • Don't be afraid to use thinning shears to add texture to the hair.
  • Check your work frequently to make sure you're on track.

Section 4: Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have when it comes to cutting men's hair longer on top:

1. How long should the hair be on top?

The length of the hair on top will depend on your client's preferences. Generally, a longer style will be around 3-5 inches in length.

2. Can you cut men's hair longer on top with clippers?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut men's hair longer on top. However, it's important to use guards and take your time to achieve a gradual fade.

3. How often should you trim men's hair that's longer on top?

How often your client needs a trim will depend on their hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, a trim every 4-6 weeks will help maintain the style.

Section 5: Hair Care Tips for Men with Long Hair

If your client is going for a longer style on top, it's important to take care of their hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash hair regularly to remove dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage hair.
  • Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep hair healthy and shiny.

Cutting men's hair longer on top can be a great way to achieve a modern, stylish look. By following these tips and techniques, you can help your clients look and feel their best.
