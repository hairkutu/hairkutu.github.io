Mens Slicked Back Undercut The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel May 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile haircut, the mens slicked back undercut is the perfect choice. This classic hairstyle has been around for decades and continues to be a popular choice for men of all ages. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about the slicked back undercut, from how to get the look to the best products to use for styling.

Description

The mens slicked back undercut is a hairstyle where the sides and back of the head are shaved or cut short, while the top is left longer and slicked back. This creates a sharp contrast between the short sides and the longer top, giving the hairstyle a bold and edgy look. The slicked back style can be achieved with a variety of products, including pomades, gels, and waxes.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in getting a slicked back undercut, the first step is to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look. Make sure to bring in a photo of the style you're looking for to give your stylist a clear idea of what you want.

During the haircut, the sides and back of your head will be shaved or cut short, while the top will be left longer. The length of the top will vary depending on your personal preference, but it's typically around 2-4 inches. Your stylist will then apply a product to the top of your hair, such as a pomade or wax, and comb it back to create the slicked back look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the mens slicked back undercut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting a slicked back undercut is to choose the right length for the top of your hair. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Generally, the top should be around 2-4 inches long, but it can be longer or shorter depending on your desired look.

Step 2: Shave or Cut the Sides and Back

Next, your stylist will shave or cut the sides and back of your head. This will create the sharp contrast between the short sides and the longer top that is characteristic of the slicked back undercut.

Step 3: Apply Product

Once the sides and back are cut, your stylist will apply a styling product to the top of your hair, such as a pomade, wax, or gel. This will help to hold the hair in place and create the slicked back look.

Step 4: Comb Back

After applying the product, your stylist will comb your hair back to create the slicked back look. Make sure to use a fine-toothed comb for best results.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your mens slicked back undercut:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that is designed for your hair type
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed
  • Use a fine-toothed comb to create a smooth, slicked back look
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a side part or a messy, textured look

FAQs

What is a slicked back undercut?

The slicked back undercut is a hairstyle where the sides and back of the head are shaved or cut short, while the top is left longer and slicked back. This creates a sharp contrast between the short sides and the longer top, giving the hairstyle a bold and edgy look.

What products should I use to style a slicked back undercut?

You can use a variety of products to style a slicked back undercut, including pomades, waxes, and gels. Choose a product that is designed for your hair type and provides the hold and shine you're looking for.

How often should I get a haircut if I have a slicked back undercut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your slicked back undercut and keep the sides and back looking clean and neat.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your mens slicked back undercut looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove buildup from styling products
  • Use a high-quality conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stylish and edgy mens slicked back undercut with confidence. Whether you're going for a classic look or a more modern twist, the slicked back undercut is a versatile and timeless hairstyle that is sure to turn heads.
