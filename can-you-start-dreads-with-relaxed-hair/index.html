Can You Start Dreads With Relaxed Hair A Guide To Starting Your Dreadlocks Journey

Written by Eveline Aug 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're someone who has relaxed hair and is considering starting dreadlocks, you may have some questions about whether or not it's possible. The good news is that yes, you can start dreads with relaxed hair! In this guide, we will go over everything you need to know about starting your dreadlocks journey with relaxed hair.

How to Start Dreads with Relaxed Hair

Starting dreadlocks with relaxed hair is a process that requires patience and dedication. Here are the steps you need to follow to start your dreadlocks journey:

Step 1: Stop Relaxing Your Hair

The first step to starting dreadlocks with relaxed hair is to stop relaxing your hair. This is because relaxed hair is chemically treated, which can weaken the hair and make it more prone to breakage. To start your dreadlocks journey, you will need to let your hair grow out and transition to your natural hair texture.

Step 2: Decide on Your Method

There are several methods for starting dreadlocks, including the twist and rip method, the backcombing method, and the neglect method. It's important to research each method and decide which one is best for you based on your hair type and lifestyle.

Step 3: Start Your Dreads

Once you've decided on your method, it's time to start your dreadlocks! This process typically involves sectioning your hair, backcombing or twisting each section, and then palm rolling the sections to help them lock up. It's important to be gentle with your hair during this process to avoid breakage.

Tips for Starting Dreadlocks with Relaxed Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when starting your dreadlocks journey with relaxed hair:

  • Be patient. It can take several months or even years for your dreadlocks to fully form.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates or other harsh chemicals.
  • Consider getting help from a professional loctician to ensure that your dreads are started correctly.

FAQs

Can you start dreads with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can start dreads with relaxed hair. However, you will need to let your hair grow out and transition to your natural hair texture before starting the dreadlocks process.

How long does it take to start dreadlocks with relaxed hair?

It can take several months or even years for your dreadlocks to fully form. The length of time will depend on your hair type and the method you use to start your dreads.

Can you wash your hair when starting dreadlocks with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can wash your hair when starting dreadlocks with relaxed hair. However, it's important to use a gentle shampoo and to be gentle with your hair when washing and drying it.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks with Relaxed Hair

Once you've started your dreadlocks journey with relaxed hair, it's important to take good care of your dreads to keep them healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your dreadlocks with relaxed hair:

  • Wash your dreads regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Moisturize your dreads regularly to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates or other harsh chemicals.
  • Protect your dreads while sleeping by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your dreads.

Starting dreadlocks with relaxed hair is possible, but it requires patience and dedication. By following the steps and tips outlined in this guide, you can start your dreadlocks journey with confidence and achieve the beautiful, unique hairstyle you've always wanted.
