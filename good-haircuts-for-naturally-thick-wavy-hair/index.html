Good Haircuts For Naturally Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Bardi Mar 21, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have naturally thick wavy hair, you know that finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a cut that will complement your natural texture without making it look too bulky or unmanageable. To help you out, we’ve put together a list of some of the best haircuts for naturally thick wavy hair. Read on to find out more!

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Naturally Thick Wavy Hair

When it comes to finding the right haircut for your naturally thick wavy hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape: Different haircuts work better on different face shapes. For example, a layered cut can help balance out a round face, while a long bob can complement an oval face.
  • Think about your lifestyle: If you’re always on the go, you may want a low-maintenance cut that doesn’t require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you love experimenting with different hairstyles, you may want a cut that gives you plenty of styling options.
  • Consult with your stylist: Your stylist can help you choose a haircut that works with your natural texture and complements your features.

Step by Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut for Your Naturally Thick Wavy Hair

Here are the steps to take when getting a haircut for your naturally thick wavy hair:

  • Choose a style that works with your natural texture.
  • Consult with your stylist to determine the best length and layers for your hair.
  • Ask your stylist to use thinning shears to remove bulk without sacrificing length.
  • Consider adding bangs to frame your face.
  • Ask your stylist to show you how to style your new cut at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Naturally Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your naturally thick wavy hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help tame frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: What is the best haircut for naturally thick wavy hair?

A: Some of the best haircuts for naturally thick wavy hair include long layers, a shaggy bob, and a long bob with bangs.

Q: Can I wear my naturally thick wavy hair straight?

A: Yes, you can wear your naturally thick wavy hair straight, but be sure to use a heat protectant and a straightening serum to prevent damage.

Q: How often should I wash my naturally thick wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people with naturally thick wavy hair can go 2-3 days between washes.

Hair Care Products for Naturally Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care products that can help keep your naturally thick wavy hair looking its best:

  • Shampoo and conditioner designed for thick, wavy hair
  • Leave-in conditioner or hair oil
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Straightening serum
  • Hair mask for deep conditioning

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can keep your naturally thick wavy hair looking healthy and gorgeous. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you, and don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice!
