How To Get Rid Of Pcos Hair Loss Naturally

Written by Aprilia May 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder that affects many women. One of the most common symptoms of PCOS is hair loss. This can be a frustrating and distressing experience for many women. However, there are ways to manage and even reverse hair loss caused by PCOS. In this article, we will outline effective natural remedies to help you get rid of PCOS hair loss naturally.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Rid of PCOS Hair Loss Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Remedies
  • Tips for Managing PCOS Hair Loss
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Routine for PCOS Hair Loss

How to Get Rid of PCOS Hair Loss Naturally

PCOS hair loss is caused by an excess of male hormones called androgens. This causes the hair follicles to shrink, resulting in thinner and weaker hair. The following natural remedies can help to balance hormone levels, improve circulation to the scalp, and promote hair growth.

1. Diet

Eating a diet rich in whole foods, fruits, and vegetables can help to balance hormone levels and reduce inflammation. Avoiding processed foods and sugar can also help to manage insulin levels, which can be a factor in PCOS hair loss. Some foods that are particularly beneficial for hair health include:

  • Salmon and other fatty fish
  • Eggs
  • Spinach
  • Avocado
  • Walnuts
  • Blueberries

2. Supplements

Supplements can also be helpful in managing PCOS hair loss. Some supplements that have been shown to improve hair health include:

  • Omega-3 fatty acids
  • Vitamin D
  • Zinc
  • Biotin

3. Exercise

Regular exercise can help to balance hormone levels and reduce stress, which can be beneficial for hair health. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise per day, such as brisk walking or cycling.

4. Scalp Massage

Massaging the scalp can improve circulation to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage the scalp in circular motions for a few minutes each day.

5. Essential Oils

Essential oils can also be beneficial for hair health. Some oils that have been shown to promote hair growth include:

  • Lavender oil
  • Peppermint oil
  • Rosemary oil
  • Cedarwood oil

Step-by-Step Remedies

Here are some step-by-step remedies to help you get rid of PCOS hair loss naturally:

1. DIY Hair Mask

Mix 1 tablespoon of coconut oil, 1 tablespoon of honey, and 1 egg yolk in a bowl. Apply the mixture to your hair and scalp, and leave it on for 30 minutes. Rinse with warm water and shampoo as usual.

2. Herbal Tea Rinse

Brew a strong cup of herbal tea, such as nettle or chamomile. Allow the tea to cool, and then pour it over your hair and scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes, and then rinse with warm water.

3. Scalp Massage with Essential Oils

Mix a few drops of your favorite essential oil with a carrier oil, such as coconut or jojoba oil. Use your fingertips to massage the oil into your scalp in circular motions for a few minutes each day.

Tips for Managing PCOS Hair Loss

Here are some additional tips for managing PCOS hair loss:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments, such as perms and hair dyes
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, which can cause breakage

FAQ

Can PCOS hair loss be reversed?

Yes, PCOS hair loss can be reversed with the right treatment. Natural remedies, such as diet, exercise, and scalp massage, can be effective in promoting hair growth and reducing hair loss.

What are the best supplements for PCOS hair loss?

Omega-3 fatty acids, vitamin D, zinc, and biotin have all been shown to improve hair health and reduce hair loss in women with PCOS.

How long does it take to see results from natural remedies for PCOS hair loss?

Results can vary depending on the individual and the severity of the hair loss. It may take several weeks or even months to see noticeable results from natural remedies for PCOS hair loss.

Hair Care Routine for PCOS Hair Loss

Here is a hair care routine for managing PCOS hair loss:

  • Wash hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Massage scalp with essential oils
  • Eat a diet rich in whole foods and fruits and vegetables
  • Take supplements to promote hair health

By following these natural remedies and tips, you can effectively manage PCOS hair loss and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient and consistent in your efforts, and you will see results in time.
