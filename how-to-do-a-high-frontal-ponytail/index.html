How To Do A High Frontal Ponytail

Written by Eveline Jul 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old ponytail at the back of your head? Why not try something new and trendy by doing a high frontal ponytail? This hairstyle not only looks chic but also helps to highlight your facial features. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to do a high frontal ponytail, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a High Frontal Ponytail
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect High Frontal Ponytail
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for High Frontal Ponytail

How to Do a High Frontal Ponytail

Before we start, let's gather our tools: a comb, hairbrush, hair elastic, and hairspray. Follow these simple steps to achieve a perfect high frontal ponytail:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Take a section of hair at the front of your head, just above your forehead.

Step 3: Use a comb to tease the hair at the roots to create volume.

Step 4: Gently pull the teased hair backward and secure it with a hair elastic.

Step 5: Gather the rest of your hair and tie it into a high ponytail, just above the crown of your head.

Step 6: Use a hairbrush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step 7: Secure the ponytail with another hair elastic, and adjust the height and tightness to your liking.

Step 8: Apply hairspray to set the hairstyle in place.

Tips for a Perfect High Frontal Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high frontal ponytail:

  • Use a teasing comb to create volume at the roots of the hair.
  • Make sure to brush your hair thoroughly to avoid any tangles or knots.
  • Choose a hair elastic that matches the color of your hair to avoid any visible hair ties.
  • Adjust the tightness of the ponytail according to your comfort level.
  • Apply hairspray to set the hairstyle and prevent any flyaways.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I do a high frontal ponytail on short hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a high frontal ponytail on short hair by adding hair extensions or using bobby pins to secure the hair in place.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from getting tangled in the elastic?

A: To avoid any tangles, make sure to brush your hair thoroughly before tying it into a ponytail. You can also use a hair tie made of soft fabric instead of a rubber band to prevent any damage to the hair.

Q: Can I wear a high frontal ponytail for a formal event?

A: Yes, a high frontal ponytail is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for both casual and formal events. You can add accessories such as a hair clip or a hairband to dress up the look.

Hair Care for High Frontal Ponytail

To maintain a healthy and vibrant-looking hair, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair by limiting the use of straighteners or curling irons.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a neat appearance.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can achieve a perfect high frontal ponytail and maintain healthy and beautiful hair. So go ahead, try out this trendy hairstyle and show off your unique style!
