How To Cut A Short Pixie Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a bold and low-maintenance hairstyle, a short pixie cut might be just what you need. This versatile cut works for all hair types and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a short pixie hairstyle at home, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut a Short Pixie Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Pixie Cut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically has layers and texture. It can be cut in a variety of ways, including with bangs, longer layers at the crown, or a tapered back. Pixie cuts are a great option for those who want to embrace a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic and stylish. Plus, they're perfect for hot summer months!

How to Cut a Short Pixie Hairstyle

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair into a short pixie style, here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hairdressing scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Sectioning clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut a short pixie hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb through your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and then from ear to ear, creating four sections.
  3. Starting at the nape of the neck, use scissors or clippers to cut the hair to your desired length. Work your way up to the crown of the head, cutting each section as you go.
  4. Use the comb to lift the hair at the crown of the head and cut it to your desired length.
  5. Create texture by using point-cutting or razor-cutting techniques. This will give your hair a choppy, edgy look.
  6. Move to the sides and use the same technique to create texture and remove any excess length.
  7. For the fringe, use the comb to create a diagonal parting and cut the hair to your desired length. You can choose to have a blunt fringe or a textured fringe.
  8. Blend the fringe into the sides using the same technique as before.
  9. Check for any uneven sections and trim as necessary.
  10. Style your new pixie cut using a styling product, such as a wax or pomade.

Tips for a Perfect Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a short pixie hairstyle:

  • Start with longer hair and gradually work your way shorter to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a spray bottle to keep your hair damp while cutting. Wet hair is easier to work with and will allow you to see any uneven sections.
  • Invest in a good pair of hairdressing scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it more difficult to cut.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and textures. A pixie cut is customizable and can be tailored to your personal style.

FAQ

Is a pixie cut easy to maintain?

Yes, a pixie cut is low-maintenance and easy to style. However, you will need to get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Can anyone pull off a pixie cut?

Yes, a pixie cut can work for any face shape and hair texture. However, it's important to talk to your hairstylist to determine the best cut for your features.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Cutting a short pixie hairstyle might seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done at home. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips for a perfect pixie cut that's easy to maintain and customize to your personal style.
