Men s Mop Top Hairstyle A Cool And Trendy Look

Written by Daniel Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and looks effortlessly cool? Look no further than the men's mop top hairstyle. This classic hairstyle has been popular since the 1960s and has recently made a comeback. In this guide, we will discuss how to achieve the perfect mop top look, tips for styling and maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Achieve the Men's Mop Top Hairstyle

The men's mop top hairstyle is characterized by its long, shaggy layers that cover the forehead and ears. The hair is usually parted down the middle and styled to have a slight wave or curl. Here are the steps to achieve the look:

Step 1: Grow Out Your Hair

In order to achieve the mop top look, you will need to grow out your hair. Aim for hair that is at least shoulder-length, with longer layers on top.

Step 2: Part Your Hair Down the Middle

Using a comb, part your hair down the middle. This will create the signature mop top look.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Using a curling iron or a hair straightener, create a slight wave or curl in your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to create a more tousled look.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining the Men's Mop Top Hairstyle

Now that you have achieved the mop top look, you will need to know how to maintain it. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Texturizing Spray

Using a texturizing spray will help to create a more tousled, messy look. Spray the product onto your hair and scrunch it up with your fingers to create volume and texture.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

While you want to grow out your hair to achieve the mop top look, you will still need to trim it regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Tip 3: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Long hair can easily become dry and brittle, so it is important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs About the Men's Mop Top Hairstyle

Q: What Face Shape Is Best for the Mop Top Hairstyle?

A: The mop top hairstyle looks great on most face shapes. However, it is especially flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can I Still Achieve the Mop Top Look if I Have Curly Hair?

A: Yes! The mop top look can work for those with curly hair, too. Just make sure to keep your curls under control with a curl-defining product.

Q: Can I Wear the Mop Top Hairstyle to Work or Formal Events?

A: While the mop top hairstyle is generally a more casual look, it can still be worn to work or formal events. Just make sure to style it neatly and use a texturizing spray to create a more polished look.

Hair Care for the Men's Mop Top Hairstyle

Achieving the perfect mop top look takes time and effort, so it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it brittle. Try to avoid using them on a regular basis and opt for air-drying your hair instead.

Tip 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner will help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Apply it to damp hair and comb it through for best results.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, so make sure to wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF when you're outside for extended periods of time.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect men's mop top hairstyle and keep it looking great all year round. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, the mop top is a versatile and cool hairstyle that is sure to turn heads.
