How To Style Pixie Bob Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Apr 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Pixie bob hair is one of the trendiest hairstyles of the moment. It combines the best of both worlds - the edginess of a pixie cut and the femininity of a bob. If you've recently gotten a pixie bob haircut and are wondering how to style it, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll walk you through step-by-step instructions on how to style pixie bob hair, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • Step-by-Step Instructions

  • FAQs

How to Style Pixie Bob Hair

Styling pixie bob hair is easier than you might think. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, lifting at the roots for volume
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add waves or curls
  5. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your style in place

Of course, there are many variations on this basic technique, which we'll explore in more detail below.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps involved in styling pixie bob hair:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair tends to be oily or greasy, you may want to wash it daily or every other day. If your hair is dry or prone to breakage, you may want to wash it less frequently.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. This could be a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair type and the look you're going for. Work the product through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections, starting at the back and working your way forward. Lift at the roots to add volume, and use the brush to smooth out any kinks or waves.

Step 4: Use a flat or curling iron to style your hair

Depending on the look you're going for, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add waves or curls. If you're using a flat iron, work in sections, starting at the back and working your way forward. Clamp the iron down on a section of hair near the roots, then glide it down to the ends, twisting the iron slightly as you go to create a slight bend.

If you're using a curling iron, wrap sections of hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing. For a more natural look, vary the direction of the curls, wrapping some sections towards your face and others away from your face.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray or texturizing spray

To hold your style in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray or texturizing spray. If you're going for a more tousled, undone look, opt for a texturizing spray. If you want your hair to stay put all day, go for a hairspray with a strong hold.

Tips for Styling Pixie Bob Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your pixie bob haircut:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type
  • To add volume at the roots, try flipping your hair upside down while blow-drying
  • For a more polished look, tuck one side of your hair behind your ear
  • To add texture, try scrunching your hair with your fingers while blow-drying
  • For a more edgy look, consider adding a side shave or undercut

FAQs

Q: Can I style my pixie bob hair without heat?

A: Yes! You can create waves or curls in your hair using braids or twists, rather than a curling iron. Simply braid or twist sections of hair while damp, then let them dry overnight. When you take out the braids or twists in the morning, you'll have natural-looking waves or curls.

Q: How often should I get my pixie bob haircut trimmed?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain your haircut. As a general rule, you should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Q: Can I wear my pixie bob hair up?

A: Yes! While your hair is still short, you may not be able to pull it all the way up into a ponytail, but you can use bobby pins or hair clips to create a half-up, half-down style. As your hair grows, you'll have more options for styling it up.

Hair Care for Pixie Bob Hair

Now that you know how to style your pixie bob hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-blocking spray
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to style your pixie bob hair with confidence and ease. Whether you're going for a sleek and polished look or a more tousled and undone vibe, your pixie bob hair is sure to turn heads!
