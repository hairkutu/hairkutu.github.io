Oval Head Shape Haircuts For Men

Written by Frank Apr 05, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you a man with an oval head shape and struggling to find the perfect haircut? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about oval head shape haircuts for men, including how to choose the right haircut, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

Men with oval head shapes are lucky because they can pull off various hairstyles. The oval shape is the perfect shape for a man's head because it is symmetrical and proportional. The trick is to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your features.

How To Choose The Right Haircut

Choosing the right haircut is crucial for an oval head shape. The goal is to create balance and symmetry, so avoid haircuts that add height to the top of your head. Instead, opt for haircuts that keep the sides and top of your hair equal in length, such as a buzz cut, crew cut, or a side part.

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic haircut that is perfect for men with oval head shapes. It is low maintenance, easy to style, and accentuates the natural shape of your head. To achieve this look, ask your barber to use clippers to cut your hair evenly all over your head.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is another excellent option for men with oval head shapes. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to your liking. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut the sides and back of your hair short while leaving the top longer. You can style the top with a side part or add texture with hair products.

Side Part

The side part is a classic haircut that works well with an oval head shape. It is a timeless look that is easy to style and perfect for both formal and casual occasions. To achieve this look, ask your barber to leave the top of your hair longer and create a deep side part on one side of your head.

Step By Step Instructions

1. Choose your desired haircut based on your face shape and hair type.

2. Find a reputable barber or hairstylist who can execute your desired haircut.

3. Communicate your preferences and desired outcome to your barber or hairstylist.

4. Sit back, relax, and let your barber or hairstylist work their magic.

5. Maintain your haircut by scheduling regular appointments with your barber or hairstylist.

Tips

- Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect one for you.

- Use high-quality hair products to enhance your hairstyle.

- Schedule regular appointments with your barber or hairstylist to maintain your haircut.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for an oval head shape?

The best haircut for an oval head shape is one that maintains balance and symmetry. Opt for haircuts that keep the sides and top of your hair equal in length, such as a buzz cut, crew cut, or a side part.

Can men with oval head shapes pull off long hair?

Yes, men with oval head shapes can pull off long hair. However, avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head and instead opt for hairstyles that keep your hair balanced and proportional.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is crucial for any hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

- Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

- Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

Now that you know everything you need to know about oval head shape haircuts for men, it's time to book your appointment with your barber or hairstylist. Remember, the key is to choose a haircut that enhances your features and complements your face shape. Experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one for you.
