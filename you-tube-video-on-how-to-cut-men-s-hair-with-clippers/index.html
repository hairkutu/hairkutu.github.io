You Tube Video On How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to save some money and give yourself or someone else a haircut, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing a You Tube video on how to cut men's hair with clippers. We'll be covering everything from the tools you need to the step-by-step process of cutting your hair.

Description

The You Tube video we'll be sharing is a tutorial on how to cut men's hair with clippers. It's a comprehensive guide that covers everything you need to know about cutting your own hair or someone else's. The video is about 10 minutes long and it's easy to follow along with.

How to

To get started, you'll need to gather the following tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel
  • Clip or hair tie (optional)

Once you have all your tools, you can watch the You Tube video and follow along. The video will take you through the process of sectioning your hair, cutting the sides and back, and trimming the top. It's important to take your time and go slowly to avoid any mistakes.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting men's hair with clippers:

  1. Section your hair by parting it down the middle and then from ear to ear. Use clips or a hair tie to keep the sections separate.
  2. Start with the sides and back. Use a longer guard on your clippers and work your way up from the bottom. Repeat until you've reached your desired length.
  3. Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top.
  4. Move on to the top of your head. Use a longer guard to start and go over the entire top of your head. Gradually decrease the guard size until you've achieved your desired length.
  5. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs on the top of your head.
  6. Blend the sides and back with the top using scissors.
  7. Trim your sideburns and the hair around your ears.
  8. Clean up the neckline using a trimmer or razor.
  9. Remove any hair on your face or neck using a razor or trimmer.
  10. Clean up any loose hairs and style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use scissors to blend the sides and back with the top for a natural look.
  • Take your time and go slowly to avoid any mistakes.
  • Invest in a good pair of clippers for the best results.
  • Ask for help if you're unsure about anything.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with clippers:

Q: Can I cut my own hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. It just takes some practice and patience.

Q: What kind of clippers should I use?

A: It's best to invest in a good pair of clippers that come with different guard sizes.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, most men get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.

Now that you've watched the You Tube video on how to cut men's hair with clippers, you're ready to give it a try. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and ask for help if you need it. Good luck!
