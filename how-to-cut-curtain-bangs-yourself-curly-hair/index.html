How To Cut Curtain Bangs Yourself On Curly Hair

Written by Robby May 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curtain bangs have been trending for a while now, and for good reason. They frame the face beautifully and add a touch of glamour to any hairstyle. However, cutting them yourself can seem daunting, especially if you have curly hair. Don't worry, though, because we're here to guide you through the process step by step.

Description

Curtain bangs are soft, wispy bangs that are parted in the middle, framing your face on either side. They're perfect for adding some interest to your look, and they work well on just about any hair type. If you have curly hair, though, you'll want to approach cutting your curtain bangs a little differently.

How to Cut Curtain Bangs Yourself on Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your own curtain bangs on curly hair:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Make sure your hair is clean, dry, and detangled before you start. It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, so you can see how the curls will fall. If you have a specific part, make sure it's in place before you start.

Step 2: Section your hair

Separate the front section of your hair from the rest of your hair. The section should start at the top of your head and end at your temples. Clip the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Decide on the length

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. It's best to start longer and gradually trim them shorter if needed. Use a comb to bring the hair you're cutting in front of your face.

Step 4: Cut the hair

Using hair cutting scissors, cut the hair at an angle. Start at the center of your face and work your way to the sides. Be sure to cut small sections at a time, so you don't accidentally cut too much hair. Cut the hair to the length you want.

Step 5: Blend the hair

Take small sections of hair from each side of your face and blend them together. This will create a seamless transition between the bangs and the rest of your hair.

Step 6: Style your hair

Style your hair as you normally would. You can blow dry your bangs straight or leave them curly. If you want to add some volume, use a round brush to blow dry your bangs.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own curtain bangs:

  • Start with longer bangs and trim as needed.
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Blend your bangs with the rest of your hair for a seamless look.
  • Don't cut your bangs straight across. Cut them at a slight angle to create a softer look.

FAQ

Can I cut my own curtain bangs on curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your own curtain bangs on curly hair. Just make sure you take your time and follow the steps carefully.

How do I decide on the length of my bangs?

It's best to start with longer bangs and gradually trim them shorter if needed. Use a comb to bring the hair you're cutting in front of your face.

Do I need to use hair cutting scissors?

Yes, it's best to use hair cutting scissors when cutting your own bangs. These scissors are designed to cut hair cleanly and evenly.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your curtain bangs, you'll want to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Cutting your own curtain bangs on curly hair can seem intimidating, but with the right tools and some patience, you can achieve a beautiful, trendy look. Follow these steps carefully, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
