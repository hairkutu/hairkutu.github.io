What Hair Parting Suits A Long Face

Written by Frank Mar 27, 2023 · 7 min read

Have you ever considered that the way you part your hair could be elongating or shortening the appearance of your face? If you have a long face shape, finding the right hair parting can make a world of difference. In this article, we’ll go over the best hair parting for a long face, how to achieve it, and some helpful tips to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Find the Right Hair Parting
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Helpful Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Long Faces

How to Find the Right Hair Parting

Before we dive into the specifics of a long face shape, let’s quickly touch on how to find the right hair parting for any face shape.

The general rule of thumb is to part your hair in a way that balances your facial features. If you have a round face, for instance, a middle part can help elongate it. If you have a square face, a side part can help soften the angles.

For a long face shape, you’ll want to create the illusion of width. This means avoiding a middle part, which can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for a side part that falls at the corner of your eyebrow.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how to achieve the perfect hair parting for a long face shape:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair in the direction you want it to fall. This will help train your hair to lay flat.
  2. Using the end of a comb, create a part at the corner of your eyebrow on one side of your head.
  3. Run the comb down the length of your hair to make sure the part is straight.
  4. Style your hair as desired, keeping the part in place.

Helpful Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair parting for a long face shape:

  • If you have bangs, make sure they’re swept to the side opposite your parting. This will help create the illusion of width.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair. Layers can help add volume and shape to a long face.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that suits you best. Everyone’s face shape is slightly different, so what works for one person may not work for another.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair partings for long faces:

Can I part my hair in the middle if I have a long face?

You can, but it’s generally not recommended. A middle part can make your face look even longer, which is not ideal if you’re trying to create balance.

What if my hair doesn’t want to stay in place?

If your hair is particularly stubborn, try using a styling product like hairspray or pomade to help keep it in place.

What if I don’t like the way a side part looks on me?

That’s okay! The beauty of hair is that it’s versatile. If you don’t like the way a side part looks on you, try experimenting with a different parting until you find one that you’re happy with.

Hair Care for Long Faces

Now that you know how to find the perfect hair parting for a long face, it’s important to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for long hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right hair parting for your face shape, you can achieve a beautiful, balanced look that’s sure to turn heads.
