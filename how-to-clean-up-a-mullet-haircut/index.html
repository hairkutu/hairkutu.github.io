How To Clean Up A Mullet Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you experiencing a bad mullet haircut? You're not alone. Mullet haircuts have been popular in the past, and some people still like them. However, if you're not one of them, you may want to know how to clean up a mullet haircut. This guide will walk you through the process of cleaning up your mullet haircut to make it look more presentable.

Description

A mullet haircut is a hairstyle that is shorter on the top and sides and longer in the back. It was popular in the 80s and 90s and has made a comeback in recent years. However, not everyone likes the look of a mullet haircut. If you're one of them, this guide will help you clean up your mullet haircut to make it look more modern.

How to Clean Up a Mullet Haircut

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cleaning up your mullet haircut, you'll need to gather some tools. You'll need a pair of haircutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you have clippers, you can use them to trim the sides and back of your hair.

Step 2: Trim the Top and Sides

Start by trimming the top and sides of your hair. Use your comb to part your hair and trim it with your scissors. If you have clippers, use them to trim the sides and back of your hair. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down until you reach the desired length.

Step 3: Trim the Back

Next, trim the back of your hair. Use your comb to separate the hair at the nape of your neck and trim it with your scissors. If you have clippers, use them to trim the hair at the back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've trimmed all the hair, it's time to blend it. Use your comb to blend the shorter hair on top with the longer hair in the back. This will create a more natural look and make your mullet haircut look more modern.

Tips

  • Don't rush the process. Take your time to ensure that you're getting the right length and shape.
  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • If you're not sure how to trim your hair, consider going to a professional stylist.
  • Wash your hair before you start trimming it. Clean hair is easier to cut and style.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles until you find one that works for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I clean up my mullet haircut by myself?

A: Yes, you can clean up your mullet haircut by yourself. However, if you're not sure how to trim your hair, it's best to go to a professional stylist.

Q: How often should I clean up my mullet haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, you should clean up your mullet haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Hair Care

Once you've cleaned up your mullet haircut, it's important to take care of it. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy. Use a styling product to keep your hair in place and to add shine. And don't forget to trim it regularly to maintain its shape.

With this step-by-step guide, you now know how to clean up a mullet haircut. Follow these tips and take care of your hair, and you'll have a modern and stylish look that you can be proud of.
