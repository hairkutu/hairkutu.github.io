How To Wear A Baseball Cap With Short Natural Hair

Written by Daniel Apr 11, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a fan of baseball caps but struggle with how to wear them with your short natural hair? Look no further! In this article, we’ll provide you with step-by-step instructions and tips on how to rock a baseball cap with ease. So, grab your favorite cap and let’s get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Wear a Baseball Cap with Short Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Baseball Cap with Short Natural Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Choose the Right Cap

The first step to wearing a baseball cap with short natural hair is to choose the right cap. Make sure to select a cap that fits your head comfortably and has a deeper crown to accommodate your hair. Avoid caps with shallow crowns, as they won’t provide enough room for your hair and can cause discomfort.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Before putting on your cap, you’ll want to style your hair in a way that will complement the cap. If you have a TWA (teeny weeny afro), you can leave it as is or add a small amount of product to define your curls. If your hair is longer, you can opt for a wash-and-go or a twist-out. Whatever style you choose, make sure it won’t create bulk under your cap.

Step 3: Wear Your Cap

Now it’s time to put on your cap! Start by tilting the cap forward slightly and placing it on the back of your head. Pull the cap down until it sits comfortably on your head, making sure not to flatten your hair. You can adjust the cap to sit higher or lower on your head depending on your preference.

Step 4: Style the Brim

The final step is to style the brim of your cap. You can wear the brim straight forward, tilted to the side, or even slightly curved. Experiment with different angles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Wearing a Baseball Cap with Short Natural Hair:

  • Choose a cap with a deeper crown to accommodate your hair.
  • Avoid caps with shallow crowns, as they won’t provide enough room for your hair and can cause discomfort.
  • Style your hair in a way that won’t create bulk under your cap.
  • Experiment with different brim angles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ:

Can I wear a baseball cap with a TWA?

Yes! A baseball cap can look great with a TWA. You can leave your hair as is or add a small amount of product to define your curls.

What if my hair is too thick to fit under a baseball cap?

If your hair is too thick to fit comfortably under a baseball cap, you can try wearing a satin-lined cap or a headband to keep your hair in place. You can also try using a styling gel or cream to smooth down any flyaways.

Hair Care Tips:

When wearing a baseball cap with short natural hair, it’s important to take care of your hair to avoid breakage and damage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Avoid wearing caps for long periods of time to prevent friction and breakage.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using products with harsh chemicals that can dry out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know how to wear a baseball cap with short natural hair, you can rock your favorite cap with confidence. Follow these tips and tricks, and you’ll be sure to turn heads!
