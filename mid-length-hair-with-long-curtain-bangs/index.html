Mid Length Hair With Long Curtain Bangs A Stylish Choice In 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both trendy and versatile, mid length hair with long curtain bangs might just be the perfect choice for you. This popular hairstyle combines the practicality of mid length hair with the chicness of long curtain bangs, creating a look that's both effortless and stylish. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look, give you some tips on how to maintain it, and answer some common questions about this hairstyle.

Description

Mid length hair with long curtain bangs is a hairstyle that features hair that falls just below the shoulders, paired with long and wispy bangs that frame the face. The bangs are called "curtain" because they're parted in the middle and swept to the sides, much like a pair of curtains. This hairstyle is great for those who want a low-maintenance look that still has a touch of elegance.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're starting with long hair, you'll need to have it cut to mid length first. This can be done by your hairstylist, or you can do it yourself at home with a pair of sharp scissors. Once you have the right length, you can proceed to cutting your bangs. To achieve the "curtain" effect, part your bangs in the middle and cut them at an angle, longer at the sides and shorter in the middle. This will create a soft and wispy effect that's perfect for this hairstyle.

Styling mid length hair with long curtain bangs is also very easy. You can either let your hair air-dry for a natural look, or blow-dry it with a round brush to add some volume. You can also use a straightener or a curling iron to create different styles, depending on your mood and the occasion.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve mid length hair with long curtain bangs:

  1. Cut your hair to mid length.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it forward. This will be your guide for cutting your bangs.
  4. Cut your bangs at an angle, longer at the sides and shorter in the middle.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for the other side.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mid length hair with long curtain bangs:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools.
  • Experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh and interesting.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about mid length hair with long curtain bangs:

Can I still wear a ponytail with this hairstyle?

Yes, you can still wear a ponytail with mid length hair with long curtain bangs. Just make sure to leave your bangs out for a more stylish look.

Can I curl my bangs?

Yes, you can curl your bangs using a curling iron or a straightener. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

How often do I need to get a trim?

You should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your mid length hair with long curtain bangs looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

Mid length hair with long curtain bangs is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that still looks chic. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can achieve this look and keep it looking its best.
