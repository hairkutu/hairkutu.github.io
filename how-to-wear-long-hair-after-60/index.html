How To Wear Long Hair After 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Mar 29, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Long hair can be beautiful at any age, and just because you've reached your 60s doesn't mean you have to chop it all off. In fact, long hair can be incredibly flattering and youthful-looking when styled correctly. If you're wondering how to wear long hair after 60, we've got you covered with tips and tricks to help you look and feel your best.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Wear Long Hair After 60
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Hair After 60

How to Wear Long Hair After 60

The first step in wearing long hair after 60 is to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type. Some popular options include:

  • Layered cuts
  • Long bobs
  • Beachy waves
  • Straight and sleek
  • Natural curls

Once you've chosen a style, it's important to keep your hair well-groomed and healthy. Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and protective styles can all help to keep your hair looking its best.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to long hair or want some guidance on how to style it, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Add any desired styling products, such as mousse, gel, or hairspray.
  3. Use a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and shape.
  4. If desired, use a curling iron or straightener to add texture.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for wearing long hair after 60:

  • Consider adding layers to add movement and volume.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Try out different styles, such as braids or updos, to switch things up.
  • Accessorize with hair clips, headbands, or scarves to add interest.

FAQs

Can I still wear long hair if I have thinning hair?

Absolutely! In fact, adding layers or texture can actually make thinning hair appear fuller.

Is it okay to wear my hair in a ponytail or bun?

Yes, but be sure to vary your hairstyle regularly to avoid putting too much stress on one area of your scalp.

How often should I wash my long hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with long hair find that washing it every other day or every few days works best, while others prefer to wash it every day.

Hair Care for Long Hair After 60

To keep your long hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear long hair after 60 and feel beautiful and stylish at any age.
