How To Make Curly Hair Grow Down Instead Of Out Male

Written by Frank Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. While it can look great when styled properly, it can also be difficult to manage and can grow outwards instead of downwards, making it hard to achieve the desired look. In this article, we will provide tips on how to make curly hair grow down instead of out for males.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Curly Hair Grow Down Instead of Out Male
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Make Curly Hair Grow Down
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Curly Hair Grow Down Instead of Out Male

The first step to making curly hair grow down instead of out is to start with the right haircut. Ask your hairstylist for a cut that is longer on the top and shorter on the sides. This will help to weigh down the hair on the top of your head and prevent it from growing outwards.

Another important factor to consider is the products that you use on your hair. Avoid using heavy products such as gels and pomades, as these can add weight to your hair and cause it to grow outwards. Instead, opt for lightweight products such as mousse or leave-in conditioner, which can help define your curls without weighing them down.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your curly hair grow down instead of out:

  1. Start with the right haircut
  2. Avoid heavy products
  3. Use lightweight products such as mousse or leave-in conditioner
  4. Apply products to damp hair
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair
  6. Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day
  7. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair
  8. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  9. Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  10. Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth

Tips to Make Curly Hair Grow Down

Here are some additional tips to help make your curly hair grow down instead of out:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while you sleep
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or headbands, which can cause tension and breakage
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment, which can help to smooth and weigh down your hair
  • Invest in a high-quality deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair moisturized and healthy

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making curly hair grow down instead of out for males:

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It is generally recommended to wash curly hair once or twice a week, as washing too often can strip it of its natural oils.

Can I straighten my curly hair to make it grow down?

Straightening your hair too often can cause damage and breakage, which can actually make it harder to achieve the desired look. Instead, focus on using the right products and styling techniques to define your natural curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for making curly hair grow down instead of out. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your curly hair grow down instead of out for a more polished and put-together look.
