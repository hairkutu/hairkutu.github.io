How To Fit A Lace Front Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter May 12, 2023 · 8 min read

Lace front wigs have become increasingly popular over the years, and it’s not hard to see why. They’re versatile, easy to style, and can give you a natural-looking hairline. However, fitting a lace front wig can be a bit daunting if you’ve never done it before. In this blog post, we’ll be giving you a step-by-step guide on how to fit a lace front wig.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fit a Lace Front Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Fitting a Lace Front Wig
  • FAQs About Fitting a Lace Front Wig
  • Hair Care Tips for Lace Front Wigs

How to Fit a Lace Front Wig

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s briefly go over what you’ll need to fit your lace front wig:

  • A wig cap
  • Adhesive (e.g., wig glue, tape, or adhesive spray)
  • Scissors
  • A comb or brush

Once you have all of the necessary materials, you’re ready to start fitting your lace front wig.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you put on your wig cap, you’ll need to make sure your hair is out of the way. You can either braid your hair or put it in a low bun. Make sure that your hair is as flat as possible so that the wig cap fits smoothly over it.

Step 2: Put on the Wig Cap

Put the wig cap over your hair, making sure that it’s snug but not too tight. Tuck in any stray hairs so that they’re under the wig cap.

Step 3: Trim the Lace

Trim the lace on your wig. Be careful not to cut too much, as you don’t want to ruin the shape of the wig. If you’re not sure how much to cut, it’s better to start with a little and work your way up.

Step 4: Apply Adhesive

Apply adhesive to the perimeter of the lace. You can use wig glue, tape, or adhesive spray. Follow the instructions for the specific adhesive you’re using, as each one may have different application methods.

Step 5: Put on the Wig

Place the wig on your head, making sure that the hairline is aligned with your natural hairline. Gently press the wig down onto the adhesive. Use a comb or brush to blend your natural hair with the wig hair.

Step 6: Style Your Wig

You can style your wig just like you would style your natural hair. You can curl it, straighten it, or put it up in a ponytail. Be careful not to damage the wig by using too much heat or pulling too hard.

Tips for Fitting a Lace Front Wig

  • Make sure your wig cap matches your skin tone as closely as possible to avoid any noticeable lines.
  • Use a wig stand or mannequin head to help you style your wig.
  • Don’t pull too hard on the wig when you’re styling it, as this can cause the lace to tear.
  • Be patient when you’re trimming the lace. It’s better to take your time and get it right than to rush and make a mistake.

FAQs About Fitting a Lace Front Wig

Q: How long does a lace front wig last?

A: The lifespan of a lace front wig depends on how well you take care of it. With proper care, a lace front wig can last up to six months to a year.

Q: Can I swim or shower with a lace front wig on?

A: It’s not recommended to swim or shower with a lace front wig on, as it can cause the adhesive to loosen and the wig to slip off.

Q: Do I need to use adhesive to fit my lace front wig?

A: It’s not necessary to use adhesive, but it can help the wig stay in place and look more natural.

Hair Care Tips for Lace Front Wigs

  • Wash your wig every two weeks or as needed.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the wig.
  • Let the wig air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the wig, as this can damage the hair fibers.

Now that you know how to fit a lace front wig, you can experiment with different styles and looks. Whether you want a short bob or long waves, a lace front wig can help you achieve the hair of your dreams.
