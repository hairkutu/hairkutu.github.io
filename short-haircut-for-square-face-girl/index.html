Short Haircut For Square Face Girl A Complete Guide In 2023

If you are a girl with a square face, you might have thought that having a short haircut is not for you. But that's not true! With the right technique and style, short hair can actually complement your square face shape. In this article, we will guide you on how to choose the best short haircut for square face girl, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Square Face Girl

When choosing a short haircut for square face girl, the key is to soften the angles of your face. You can do this by adding layers, texture, and volume to your hair. Here are some short haircut ideas that can flatter your square face shape:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a popular short haircut that can work well with a square face. The key is to add volume to the top of your head to balance out your jawline. You can achieve this by adding layers and texture to your hair. A side-swept fringe can also soften your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is another classic short haircut that can flatter a square face. The key is to add layers and texture to create movement and soften your face. You can also add a side-swept fringe or a long bang to create a more feminine look.

Layered Cut

A layered cut can also work well with a square face. The layers can add volume and texture to your hair, creating a more relaxed and effortless look. You can also add a side-swept fringe or a middle part to create a more balanced look.

How to Style Short Haircut for Square Face Girl

Styling your short haircut can be fun and easy. Here are some styling tips for short haircut for square face girl:

Add Texture

Adding texture to your hair can create a more relaxed and effortless look. You can use a texturizing spray or a dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also use a sea salt spray to create beachy waves.

Create Volume

Creating volume at the top of your head can balance out your jawline and soften your face. You can use a volumizing mousse or a root lifter to add volume to your hair. You can also blow dry your hair upside down to create more volume.

Use Hair Accessories

Using hair accessories can add a pop of color and personality to your short haircut. You can use a headband, a hair clip, or a scarf to create different looks.

Step-by-Step Guide on Getting a Short Haircut for Square Face Girl

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a short haircut for square face girl:

Step 1: Choose Your Short Haircut

Choose a short haircut that flatters your square face shape. You can consult with your hairstylist on which short haircut suits you best.

Step 2: Book an Appointment

Book an appointment with your hairstylist. Make sure to communicate clearly about the type of short haircut that you want.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before your appointment. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut your hair.

Step 4: Get Your Haircut

During your appointment, your hairstylist will cut your hair according to the short haircut that you chose. Make sure to communicate if you want any changes or adjustments.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After your haircut, your hairstylist will style your hair. You can also ask for tips on how to style your hair at home.

Tips for Maintaining a Short Haircut for Square Face Girl

Maintaining a short haircut can be easy and low-maintenance. Here are some tips on how to maintain your short haircut:

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can keep your short haircut looking fresh and healthy. You can schedule a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Use the Right Products

Using the right products can enhance your short haircut and keep it looking great. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. You can also use a styling product such as a hair wax, a hair gel, or a hairspray.

Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. You can also protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or a scarf.

FAQs on Short Haircut for Square Face Girl

Q: Can I still have a short haircut if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can! With the right technique and style, short hair can actually complement your square face shape.

Q: What short haircut is best for a square face?

A: Pixie cut, bob cut, and layered cut are some short haircuts that can flatter a square face.

Q: How do I style my short haircut?

A: You can add texture, create volume, and use hair accessories to style your short haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircut for Square Face Girl

Here are some hair care tips for short haircut for square face girl:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair. If you need to use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray first.

Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet can promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to eat foods that are rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.

In conclusion, having a short haircut for square face girl is not only possible, but it can also be flattering and stylish. With the right technique, style, and care, you can rock a short haircut that suits your square face shape. Remember to communicate with your hairstylist and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
