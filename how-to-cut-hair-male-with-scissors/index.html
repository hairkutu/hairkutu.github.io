How To Cut Hair Male With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 12, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of shelling out money at the barbershop every few weeks? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair at home? With a little practice and the right tools, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without ever leaving your house. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut hair male with scissors, from start to finish.

Description

Cutting hair with scissors is a skill that takes time and practice to master. But with the right techniques and tools, you can give yourself a great haircut at home. This guide will cover everything you need to know about how to cut hair male with scissors, including what tools you’ll need, how to section your hair, and how to create different styles.

How To

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you’ll need to gather the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp hair-cutting scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to invest in a handheld mirror so you can see the back of your head.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and give you a more accurate result. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel-dry your hair until it’s only slightly damp.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into sections. Start by dividing your hair down the middle from the front of your head to the back, and then divide each section into smaller sections. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin by cutting the hair at the back of your head. Take a small section of hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven cut. Work your way around your head, cutting each section of hair to the desired length.

Step 5: Blend Your Hair

Once you’ve cut all the hair to the desired length, it’s time to blend the layers. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, creating a soft, blended edge. Work your way around your head, blending the layers until you achieve the desired result.

Step 6: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Once you’ve finished blending the layers, it’s time to cut the top of your hair. Use your comb to lift a section of hair straight up, and then cut the hair to the desired length. Work your way around your head, cutting the top of your hair to the same length.

Step 7: Trim the Sides and Back

Use your comb to lift the hair on the sides and back of your head, and then trim the hair to the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven cut.

Step 8: Clean Up Your Neckline

Use your comb to lift the hair at the back of your head, and then trim the hair along your neckline. This will give your haircut a clean, polished look.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, it’s time to style it. Use a styling product to create the look you want, whether it’s a slicked-back style or a messy, textured look.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure you’re cutting evenly.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help if you’re unsure about cutting a certain section of hair.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so make sure you’re comfortable and taking breaks as needed.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, it’s best to use hair-cutting scissors, as they are sharper and designed specifically for cutting hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Generally, it’s recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

Don’t panic! If you make a mistake, you can always trim the hair a little shorter or style it to cover up the mistake. If you’re unsure about cutting a certain section of hair, it’s best to leave it alone or ask for help.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it’s important to take care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Be sure to trim your hair regularly to maintain your style, and use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Now that you know how to cut hair male with scissors, you can save money and achieve a great haircut at home. Remember to take your time, start with small cuts, and don’t be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
