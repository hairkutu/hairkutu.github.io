How To Slick Back Thick Hair Black Girl A Step By Step Guide

Are you a black girl with thick hair and wondering how to slick it back? Slicked back hair is a timeless hairstyle that exudes elegance and sophistication. It's perfect for formal occasions, job interviews or just a night out with friends. But if you have thick hair, achieving this look can be a bit challenging. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to slick back thick hair black girl in a relaxed English language. Let's get started!

How to Slick Back Thick Hair Black Girl

Slicking back thick hair can be a bit tricky, but it's not impossible. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before slicking back your hair, make sure it's clean and free from any products. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup that may prevent the slicked back style from holding.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Gel

Apply a styling gel to your hair starting from the roots to the ends. Make sure to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair. The gel will help to hold your hair in place and give it that sleek look.

Step 3: Use a Fine-Tooth Comb

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back starting from the hairline. Make sure to comb the hair in the direction you want it to lay. If you want a middle part, comb your hair straight back. If you want a side part, comb your hair to one side.

Step 4: Secure with a Hair Tie

Once you have your hair combed back, use a hair tie to secure it in place. Make sure to tie the hair tie tightly to prevent any loose hairs from falling out.

Step 5: Add Hairspray

Finish off the look by adding a light mist of hairspray to hold everything in place. Use a light hold hairspray to prevent your hair from becoming stiff or crunchy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to slick back thick hair black girl:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Apply a styling gel to your hair starting from the roots to the ends.
  3. Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back starting from the hairline.
  4. Secure your hair with a hair tie.
  5. Add a light mist of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips for Slicking Back Thick Hair

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Use a styling gel that's specifically designed for thick hair.
  • Don't use too much product as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a fine-tooth comb to ensure your hair is evenly distributed.
  • Secure your hair tie tightly to prevent any loose hairs from falling out.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to prevent your hair from becoming stiff or crunchy.

FAQs

Q: How long will the slicked back style last?

A: The slicked back style can last up to a day or two if you use the right products and secure your hair tightly.

Q: Can I slick back my hair without using gel?

A: Yes, you can use a pomade or a wax to achieve the slicked back look. Just make sure to choose a product that's specifically designed for your hair type.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Thick hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right hair care routine, you can keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it soft and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to slick back thick hair black girl, you can rock this elegant and sophisticated look with confidence. Remember to follow the steps carefully, use the right products, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
