Cut Your Own Hair High And Tight Haircut

Written by Eveline Mar 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of going to the barber for every haircut? Do you want to save time and money by cutting your hair at home? If so, the high and tight haircut is a great option for you. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair in a high and tight style.

Description

The high and tight haircut is a popular military-style haircut that is short on the sides and back and slightly longer on the top. It is a low-maintenance style that is perfect for busy individuals who want to look sharp and stylish. The high and tight cut is also a great option for those who want to hide receding hairlines or bald spots.

How to Cut Your Own Hair High and Tight Haircut

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to gather all the necessary supplies. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, a mirror, and a towel.

Step 2: Set the Length of Your Clippers

Next, you need to set the length of your clippers. The high and tight cut usually requires a #1 or #2 clipper guard for the sides and back and a #3 or #4 guard for the top.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Start by trimming the sides of your hair with the clippers. Use the #1 or #2 guard and work your way up from the bottom, moving the clippers in an upward motion.

Step 4: Trim the Back

Once you have finished the sides, move to the back of your head. Use the same #1 or #2 guard and trim the hair in an upward motion.

Step 5: Work on the Top

Now it's time to work on the top of your hair. Use the #3 or #4 guard and trim the hair in a downward motion, leaving it slightly longer than the sides and back.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair by using the comb to go over the areas where the different lengths meet. Use the clippers without a guard to create a seamless transition between the different lengths.

Step 7: Clean Up

Use the towel to wipe away any loose hair and check for any uneven spots.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if needed
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head
  • Take your time and be patient
  • Ask a friend or family member for help if needed

FAQ

Can I cut my hair high and tight with scissors?

It is possible to cut your hair high and tight with scissors, but it may be more difficult to get the precise length and blending that clippers provide.

Can I use any type of clippers?

It is recommended to use clippers that are specifically designed for hair cutting. Home grooming clippers may not provide the same quality and precision as professional-grade clippers.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and stylish look. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair with blow dryers and flat irons, and use a heat protectant when necessary.

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a high and tight haircut from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and ask for help if needed.
