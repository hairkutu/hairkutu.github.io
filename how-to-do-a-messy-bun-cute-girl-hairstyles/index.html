How To Do A Messy Bun Cute Girl Hairstyles

Written by Robby Aug 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for an easy and cute hairstyle that you can wear for any occasion? Look no further than the messy bun! This versatile hairstyle is perfect for lazy days, formal events, and everything in between. In this article, we'll show you how to do a messy bun cute girl hairstyles step by step, along with tips and tricks to make the process even easier.

Description

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on your mood and the event you are attending. This hairstyle is popular because it is easy to do and can be achieved with minimal effort. A messy bun is also a great way to keep your hair out of your face and off your neck on hot summer days.

How to

Follow these simple steps to create a messy bun:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and twist it into a loose bun. Don't worry about making it perfect - the messier, the better.
  3. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins. If you have long hair, you may need to wrap the bun around itself several times to make it more secure.
  4. Use your fingers to pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy, undone look. You can also use a comb or brush to tease the bun and create more volume.
  5. Spray the bun with hairspray to keep it in place.

Step by Step

If you're still not sure how to do a messy bun cute girl hairstyles, follow these step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any knots or tangles. This will make it easier to create the messy bun.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Gather your hair at the crown of your head and twist it into a loose bun. Don't worry about making it perfect - the messier, the better.

Step 3: Secure the Bun

Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins. If you have long hair, you may need to wrap the bun around itself several times to make it more secure.

Step 4: Create a Messy Look

Use your fingers to pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy, undone look. You can also use a comb or brush to tease the bun and create more volume.

Step 5: Spray with Hairspray

Spray the bun with hairspray to keep it in place. This will ensure that your messy bun stays put throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect messy bun:

  • Use dry shampoo to add texture to your hair and make it easier to style.
  • Experiment with different placements for your messy bun - you can try it high on your head or low at the nape of your neck.
  • Add accessories like headbands or hair clips to dress up your messy bun for formal events.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different styles and techniques until you find the perfect messy bun for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy buns:

Q: Can I do a messy bun with short hair?

A: Yes! While it may be more difficult to create a messy bun with short hair, it is still possible. You can use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your hair in place and create a messy look.

Q: How do I make my messy bun stay in place?

A: Use hairspray to keep your messy bun in place. You can also use bobby pins or hair ties to make it more secure.

Q: Can I wear a messy bun to a formal event?

A: Yes! While messy buns are often associated with casual looks, they can also be dressed up for formal events. Add accessories like headbands or hair clips to make your messy bun more elegant.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often - this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, like hair dyes or bleach.

With these tips and tricks, you can create the perfect messy bun cute girl hairstyles in no time. Whether you're heading to work, school, or a formal event, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
