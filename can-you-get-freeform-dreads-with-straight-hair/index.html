Can You Get Freeform Dreads With Straight Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have straight hair and are interested in getting freeform dreads, you may be wondering if it's possible. The answer is yes, you can get freeform dreads with straight hair! In this article, we will discuss how to achieve freeform dreads with straight hair, the steps to take, tips, and hair care. Read on for more information.

Description

Freeform dreads are a natural, organic style of dreadlocks that form on their own without the use of specific methods or techniques. They are generally smaller in size, and each dreadlock is unique in shape and size. This style is popular among people with various hair textures, including straight hair.

How to Get Freeform Dreads with Straight Hair

To achieve freeform dreads with straight hair, you will need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Stop using hair products

Stop using hair products such as shampoo, conditioner, and hair gel. These products can prevent the hair from locking up and forming dreadlocks. Switch to natural products or stop using them altogether.

Step 2: Stop combing or brushing your hair

Avoid combing or brushing your hair. This will allow the hair to form knots and eventually dreadlocks. You can use your fingers to separate any knots that form to prevent matting.

Step 3: Be patient

Freeform dreads take time to form, and it can take several months or even years for the hair to lock up completely. Be patient and let the hair do its thing.

Tips for Getting Freeform Dreads with Straight Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve freeform dreads with straight hair:

Tip 1: Keep your hair clean

Although you should stop using hair products, it's important to keep your hair clean. Use a natural shampoo or a dreadlock shampoo to cleanse your hair and scalp.

Tip 2: Protect your hair

Protect your hair from damage and breakage by avoiding tight hairstyles and using a satin or silk pillowcase to sleep on.

Tip 3: Use sea salt spray

Sea salt spray can help speed up the locking process and give your hair some texture. You can make your own sea salt spray by mixing water and sea salt in a spray bottle.

FAQ

Can you get freeform dreads with short hair?

Yes, you can get freeform dreads with short hair. However, it may take longer for them to form since there is less hair to lock up.

Can you wash freeform dreads?

Yes, you can wash freeform dreads. It's important to keep them clean to prevent buildup and odor.

Do freeform dreads require maintenance?

Freeform dreads require minimal maintenance. You can separate any large knots that form to prevent matting, but otherwise, you can let them do their thing.

Hair Care for Freeform Dreads with Straight Hair

To care for your freeform dreads with straight hair, you should:

Tip 1: Wash your hair regularly

Use a natural shampoo or a dreadlock shampoo to cleanse your hair and scalp regularly. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals.

Tip 2: Moisturize your hair

Use a natural oil or moisturizer to keep your hair hydrated and prevent dryness.

Tip 3: Protect your hair

Avoid tight hairstyles and protect your hair from damage and breakage by using a satin or silk pillowcase to sleep on.

Overall, freeform dreads are a great choice for people with straight hair who want a natural, organic style of dreadlocks. By following these steps, tips, and hair care advice, you can achieve freeform dreads with straight hair and enjoy this unique hairstyle.
