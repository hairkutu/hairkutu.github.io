Description

Oval shaped face is considered the most versatile face shape among all. It is characterized by a slightly wider forehead and narrow chin, with the length of the face being longer than the width. This face shape is suitable for almost any hairstyle, from short to long, curly to straight, and everything in between. In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for oval shaped face female in 2023.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval shaped face female, let's first determine if you have an oval shaped face. You can do this by following these simple steps:

  • Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  • Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, and your face is longer than it is wide, then you have an oval shaped face.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Shaped Face Female

Now that you have determined that you have an oval shaped face, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles that will complement your face shape:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for oval shaped face female as they add volume and movement to the hair, while also framing the face nicely. You can wear your hair straight or add some loose waves for a more relaxed look.

2. Bob

Bob is another great option for oval shaped face female. You can go for a classic bob or a long bob, depending on your preference. This hairstyle will accentuate your jawline and cheekbones, while also giving you a chic and sophisticated look.

3. Pixie Cut

If you are looking for a bold and edgy look, then a pixie cut is the way to go. This hairstyle will highlight your facial features and give you a youthful appearance. You can add some texture to your hair for a more playful look.

4. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are perfect for oval shaped face female as they add softness to the face and draw attention to the eyes. You can wear your hair in a ponytail or a bun to show off your bangs.

5. Loose Waves

Loose waves are a great way to add some texture and volume to your hair. You can wear your hair down or tie it up in a messy bun or a ponytail. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or a night out with friends.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling your hair to complement your oval shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face look longer.
  • Try to keep your hair away from your face to show off your facial features.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

1. Can oval shaped face female wear bangs?

Yes, oval shaped face female can wear bangs. Side swept bangs are the best option for oval shaped face as they add softness to the face and draw attention to the eyes.

2. Can oval shaped face female wear a short hairstyle?

Yes, oval shaped face female can wear a short hairstyle. Pixie cut is a great option for oval shaped face as it highlights the facial features and gives a youthful appearance.

3. Can oval shaped face female wear a high ponytail?

Yes, oval shaped face female can wear a high ponytail. However, it is recommended to keep the hair away from the face to show off the facial features.

Hair Care for Oval Shaped Face Female

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oval shaped face female, it's time to experiment and find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun with your hairstyle!
