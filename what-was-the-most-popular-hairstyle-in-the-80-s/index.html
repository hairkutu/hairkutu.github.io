The Most Popular Hairstyle In The 80 S A Nostalgic Look Back

Description

The 80's were a time of big hair, bold colors, and daring fashion. The most popular hairstyle of the era was the "mullet", a cut characterized by short hair on the front and sides, and long hair at the back. This style was popularized by celebrities like Billy Ray Cyrus and Patrick Swayze, as well as rock bands like Guns N' Roses and Bon Jovi.

The mullet was a versatile hairstyle that could be worn by both men and women, and it came in many variations. Some mullets were styled with lots of volume and curls, while others were straight and sleek. No matter the variation, the mullet was a statement hairstyle that defined the era.

How to Achieve the 80's Hairstyle

To achieve the 80's mullet hairstyle, you'll need to visit a hair stylist who is familiar with the cut. It's important to find someone who can create the right balance between the short and long sections of hair, so that the style is proportional.

When it comes to styling the mullet, there are a few different options. For a more voluminous look, you can use a hair dryer and a round brush to create curls and add body to the hair. Alternatively, you can use a straightening iron to create a sleek, straight look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the 80's mullet hairstyle:

  1. Visit a hair stylist who is familiar with the mullet cut.
  2. Ask for a cut that features short hair on the front and sides, and long hair at the back.
  3. Once the cut is complete, decide on the style you want to achieve.
  4. If you want a voluminous look, use a hair dryer and a round brush to add curls and body to the hair.
  5. If you want a sleek, straight look, use a straightening iron to smooth out the hair.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Once you have achieved the 80's mullet hairstyle, there are a few tips you can follow to maintain the look:

  • Regularly trim the ends of the long hair at the back to keep it looking neat.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body to the hair.
  • Avoid washing the hair too frequently, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to style.
  • Use a quality hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 80's mullet hairstyle:

Q: Can women wear the mullet?

A: Yes, the mullet was a versatile hairstyle that could be worn by both men and women.

Q: Is the mullet still popular today?

A: While the mullet is no longer as popular as it was in the 80's, it has experienced a resurgence in recent years. Many celebrities and influencers have been spotted sporting the style.

Q: What kind of hair works best for the mullet?

A: The mullet works best on hair that is thick and straight or wavy. If you have curly hair, you may need to straighten it to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care for the 80's Hairstyle

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny while sporting the 80's mullet hairstyle, it's important to follow a few basic hair care tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling the hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using a hair dryer, straightening iron, or curling iron.
  • Get regular trims to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh the hair down and make it harder to style.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the iconic 80's mullet hairstyle and keep it looking great for years to come. Whether you're looking to relive the glory days or simply want to try out a bold new look, the mullet is a style that is sure to turn heads.
