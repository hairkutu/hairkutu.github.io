How Short Is A Number 0 Haircut

Written by Robby Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

A number 0 haircut is a very short buzz cut that is achieved by using a clipper guard with a length of 1/16 of an inch or 1.5mm. This haircut is also known as a bald fade or a skin fade, as the hair is gradually tapered down to the skin.

Description

The number 0 haircut is the shortest possible length you can get with clippers. It is a popular choice for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that requires little to no styling. It is also a great option for men with thinning hair or balding, as it gives the illusion of a fuller head of hair.

How to

If you are considering getting a number 0 haircut, it is important to find a skilled barber or hairdresser who has experience with this type of cut. Here are the steps they will likely follow:

  • Wash and dry your hair thoroughly
  • Trim any long or uneven hairs with scissors
  • Select the appropriate clipper guard (in this case, the number 0 guard)
  • Start buzzing the hair at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown of the head
  • Blend the hair gradually from the skin to the desired length
  • Trim the hairline and neckline with a razor for a clean finish

Step by Step

Here is a more detailed breakdown of the steps involved in getting a number 0 haircut:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier for the barber to work with.
  2. Next, the barber will trim any long or uneven hairs with scissors.
  3. They will then select the appropriate clipper guard (in this case, the number 0 guard).
  4. Starting at the nape of the neck, the barber will begin buzzing the hair with the clippers. They will work their way up to the crown of the head, making sure to blend the hair gradually from the skin to the desired length.
  5. Once the hair has been buzzed to the desired length, the barber will trim the hairline and neckline with a razor for a clean finish.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you are considering getting a number 0 haircut:

  • Make sure you have a good idea of what the haircut will look like before you get it. You can ask your barber for pictures or examples of previous clients with similar haircuts.
  • Be prepared for a very short haircut. It can be a shock if you are used to having longer hair.
  • Consider investing in a good set of clippers if you plan on maintaining the haircut yourself.
  • Be prepared to get regular touch-ups to keep the haircut looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the number 0 haircut:

Is a number 0 haircut the same as a buzz cut?

Yes, a number 0 haircut is a type of buzz cut.

Will a number 0 haircut make me look bald?

A number 0 haircut will give the appearance of a very short buzz cut, but it should not make you look completely bald.

How often should I get a number 0 haircut?

You should plan on getting a touch-up every two to three weeks to keep the haircut looking fresh.

Hair Care

After getting a number 0 haircut, it is important to take care of your scalp and hair to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh styling products that can irritate the scalp.
  • Protect your scalp from sunburn by wearing a hat or applying sunscreen.

A number 0 haircut is a bold and low-maintenance haircut option for men. If you are considering it, make sure to find a skilled barber or hairdresser who can give you the look you want.
