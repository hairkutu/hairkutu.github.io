How To Cut The Top Of A Guy s Hair With Scissors

Written by Petter Jun 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money on haircuts for your guy? Do you want to learn how to cut the top of a guy's hair with scissors without going to a salon? If so, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you step by step how to do it. We'll also provide you with some tips on how to do it right and some frequently asked questions about haircuts.

Description

Cutting the top of a guy's hair with scissors is a simple process that can be done at home. This haircut is perfect for men who want to keep their hair short and neat. You'll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Before you start cutting, make sure your guy's hair is clean and dry.

How to

Step 1: Start by combing your guy's hair forward. Make sure the hair is evenly distributed.

Step 2: Using your scissors, start cutting the hair at the crown of the head. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the natural line of the head.

Step 3: Continue cutting the hair in a straight line towards the front of the head. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 4: Once you've cut the hair at the front of the head, comb the hair back and check for any uneven spots. If you see any, trim them with your scissors.

Step 5: Use your comb to blend the hair at the top of the head with the hair at the sides and back of the head.

Step by Step

Step 1: Comb the Hair Forward

Using a comb, comb the hair forward, making sure it's evenly distributed.

Step 2: Cut the Hair at the Crown of the Head

Using your scissors, start cutting the hair at the crown of the head. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the natural line of the head.

Step 3: Cut the Hair Towards the Front of the Head

Continue cutting the hair in a straight line towards the front of the head. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 4: Trim Any Uneven Spots

Once you've cut the hair at the front of the head, comb the hair back and check for any uneven spots. If you see any, trim them with your scissors.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use your comb to blend the hair at the top of the head with the hair at the sides and back of the head.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and gradually increase the length.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Use a comb to blend the hair at the top of the head with the hair at the sides and back of the head.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

How often should I cut the top of my guy's hair?

You should cut the top of your guy's hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my guy's hair?

No, you should use hair cutting scissors, which are specifically designed for cutting hair. Using regular scissors can damage the hair and make it look uneven.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my guy's hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your guy's hair, don't panic. You can always trim the rest of the hair to match the mistake. If the mistake is really bad, you may need to go to a salon to get it fixed.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your guy's hair, make sure to rinse his hair with cool water to remove any loose hair. You can also use a hairbrush to remove any loose hair. If your guy's hair is dry or damaged, you can use a conditioner to help restore its health. Make sure to use a conditioner that's designed for his hair type.

Cutting the top of a guy's hair with scissors is a simple process that can be done at home. With the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can give your guy a great haircut that looks professional. Just remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll be sure to get great results.
