Simple Hairstyles For Long Hair Wedding

Written by Bardi Aug 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a simple hairstyle to wear on your long hair wedding day? You've come to the right place! In this article, we will discuss a few simple yet elegant hairstyles that you can wear on your special day. Whether you want a classic or modern look, these hairstyles will surely make you look stunning on your big day.

Description

Wedding hairstyles can be intimidating, especially for those with long hair. But worry not, because there are plenty of simple and easy-to-do hairstyles that you can choose from. These hairstyles are perfect for brides who prefer a more natural and effortless look. They are also great for bridesmaids or guests who want to look elegant without spending too much time or money on their hair.

How to

Before choosing your hairstyle, it's important to consider your dress, makeup, and overall wedding theme. You want your hairstyle to complement your overall look and not clash with it. Once you've determined your overall look, you can start choosing your hairstyle.

For a simple and elegant look, you can try a low bun or a classic ponytail. These hairstyles are easy to do and can be dressed up with accessories such as flowers or hairpins. If you want to add some texture to your hair, you can try a loose braid or a messy updo. These hairstyles are perfect for outdoor weddings or beach weddings.

Step by Step

1. Start by brushing your hair and detangling any knots.
2. If you want to add some texture, you can curl your hair using a curling iron or wand.
3. For a low bun, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. Twist the hair and wrap it around the elastic band to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
4. For a ponytail, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band. You can also add a braid or twist to your ponytail for a more elevated look.
5. For a loose braid, start by creating a side part and gathering your hair to one side. Create a loose braid and secure it with an elastic band. You can also pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.
6. For a messy updo, start by creating a loose bun or ponytail. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look. You can also add some accessories such as flowers or hairpins to complete the look.

Tips

1. Use hair products such as hairspray or mousse to help your hairstyle stay in place.
2. If you're adding accessories, make sure they complement your hairstyle and not overwhelm it.
3. Consider the weather and your hair type when choosing your hairstyle. For example, if you have fine hair, you might want to avoid heavy updos that can weigh your hair down.

FAQ

Q: Can I do these hairstyles myself or do I need a hairstylist?
A: These hairstyles are simple enough that you can do them yourself. However, if you're not confident in your hairstyling skills, you can always ask a hairstylist for help.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly and use hair masks or treatments to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and protect it from the sun and other environmental factors. With proper care, your hair will look and feel great on your wedding day.

Conclusion

There you have it, some simple yet elegant hairstyles for long hair wedding. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your overall look and makes you feel confident and beautiful. With these hairstyles, you'll surely look stunning on your special day.
