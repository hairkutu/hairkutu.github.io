Good Haircuts For Round Faces And Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face and wavy hair, you might find it challenging to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. However, with the right style and cut, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a stunning look that turns heads. In this guide, we will explore the best haircuts for round faces and wavy hair, including how to style and maintain them to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Description

Round faces have soft, curved lines with no angles or corners, and they are generally wider than they are long. Wavy hair has a natural texture that adds volume and movement to your hair, making it an excellent choice for those with round faces. The right haircut can help elongate your face, balance your features, and create a flattering look that complements your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Your Face Shape

The key to finding the perfect haircut for your round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. The best haircuts for round faces and wavy hair are those that add height and volume to the crown of your head, while keeping the sides and back of your hair relatively short. Here are some of the best haircuts to consider:

  • Long Layers: Long layers that start at the chin and extend to the ends of your hair help create the illusion of length and volume, making your face appear narrower.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs that fall just below your eyebrows can help elongate your face and add a touch of glamour to your look.
  • Bob: A bob haircut that is chin-length or slightly longer can help create angles and add definition to your face.
  • Pixie Cut: A pixie cut with longer layers on top and short sides can create height and volume, adding definition to your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Styling your hair is an essential part of creating a flattering look that complements your face shape. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the crown of your head.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add loose waves to the ends of your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have found the perfect haircut, it is essential to maintain it to keep your hair looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause breakage.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face and wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face and wavy hair. However, it is essential to add volume and height to the crown of your head to create the illusion of length and angles.

Q: What hair colors look best with round faces and wavy hair?

A: Warm, natural hair colors such as caramel, honey, and chocolate brown look best with round faces and wavy hair. Avoid harsh, contrasting colors that can make your face look wider.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized and prevent split ends.

With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a stunning look that flatters your round face and wavy hair. Use these tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best and make a statement wherever you go.
