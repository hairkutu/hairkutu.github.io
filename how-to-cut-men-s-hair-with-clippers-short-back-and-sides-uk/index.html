How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Short Back And Sides Uk A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Aug 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our complete guide to cutting men's hair with clippers short back and sides in the UK. Whether you're a professional barber or just want to learn how to cut your own hair, this guide will provide you with all the information you need to get the perfect short back and sides haircut.

Description

A short back and sides haircut is one of the most popular hairstyles for men in the UK. It's a classic look that's easy to maintain and looks great on almost everyone. With the right tools and techniques, cutting a short back and sides haircut with clippers is easy and can be done in the comfort of your own home.

How to

To cut a short back and sides haircut with clippers, you'll need the following tools:

  • Clipper with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clipper oil
  • Cape or towel

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you begin cutting. Then, comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Decide on the length you want and choose the right guard for your clippers. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if necessary.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the hair at the back of the head, starting at the nape of the neck. Use the clippers to create a straight line across the back, moving up towards the crown of the head.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Move to the sides of the head and use the clippers to blend the hair from the back to the sides. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim any longer strands with scissors.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Use scissors to trim the top of the hair to the desired length. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut in a straight line across the top.

Step 6: Finish and Style

Blend the hair at the sides and back of the head with scissors if necessary. Style the hair with product if desired.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get the perfect short back and sides haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard
  • Move the clippers in a smooth, steady motion
  • Use scissors to finish and blend the hair
  • Use clipper oil to keep your clippers running smoothly
  • Don't rush and take your time to get the best results

FAQ

What is a short back and sides haircut?

A short back and sides haircut is a classic men's hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short at the back and sides while leaving the top longer.

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps in this guide.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair with clippers?

You'll need a clipper with guards, scissors, a comb, clipper oil, and a cape or towel to protect your clothing.

Hair Care

To keep your short back and sides haircut looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut a perfect short back and sides haircut with clippers in no time. Whether you're a professional barber or just want to learn how to cut your own hair, this guide has everything you need to know to get started.
