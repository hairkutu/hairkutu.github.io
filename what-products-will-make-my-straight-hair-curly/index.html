What Products Will Make My Straight Hair Curly

Written by Luffy Aug 17, 2023 · 9 min read

Do you have naturally straight hair, but dream of having bouncy, curly locks? Thankfully, there are several products available that can help you achieve the curly hair of your dreams. In this article, we’ll discuss the different types of products that can make your straight hair curly, how to use them, and tips for maintaining your curly locks.

Table of Contents:

  • Types of Products
  • How to Use the Products
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine for Curly Hair

Types of Products:

There are several types of products that can make straight hair curly, including:

1. Curling Irons:

Curling irons are the most popular tool for achieving curly hair. They come in different sizes and shapes, allowing you to create a variety of curls. When choosing a curling iron, look for one with ceramic or tourmaline plates, as these materials will help protect your hair from heat damage.

2. Hot Rollers:

Hot rollers are another option for achieving curly hair. They work by heating up the rollers, which you then roll your hair around. Hot rollers come in different sizes, so you can choose the size that will give you the curls you want.

3. Curling Wands:

Curling wands are similar to curling irons, but they don’t have a clamp. Instead, you wrap your hair around the wand to create curls. Curling wands come in different sizes and shapes, so you can create a variety of curls.

4. Perm Kits:

Perm kits are a more permanent option for achieving curly hair. They work by applying a chemical solution to your hair, which breaks down the bonds that give your hair its shape. Once the bonds are broken down, your hair can be molded into the curly shape you desire. Perm kits should only be used by professionals, as they can be damaging if not used correctly.

How to Use the Products:

The method for using the products will vary depending on which product you choose. Here are some general tips:

1. Curling Irons:

Before using a curling iron, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Clamp the curling iron onto a small section of hair, and twist the iron away from your face. Hold for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat until all of your hair is curled.

2. Hot Rollers:

Heat up the rollers according to the instructions on the package. Section your hair into small sections, and roll each section around a roller. Once all of your hair is rolled, wait for the rollers to cool before removing them. Once you’ve removed all of the rollers, run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls.

3. Curling Wands:

Before using a curling wand, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Wrap a small section of hair around the wand, and hold for a few seconds. Release the curl, and repeat until all of your hair is curled.

4. Perm Kits:

Perm kits should only be used by professionals. If you’re interested in getting a perm, make an appointment with a hairstylist who specializes in perms.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Here’s a step-by-step guide for using a curling iron:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Clamp the curling iron onto a small section of hair.
  5. Twist the iron away from your face.
  6. Hold for a few seconds.
  7. Release the curl.
  8. Repeat until all of your hair is curled.
  9. Run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls.
  10. Apply a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair:

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

FAQs:

Q: Can I use these products on my hair if it’s already curly?

A: Yes, you can use these products on curly hair to enhance your curls.

Q: Will using these products damage my hair?

A: Using heat tools on a regular basis can be damaging to your hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools.

Q: How often should I use these products?

A: You can use these products as often as you like, but using heat tools on a daily basis can be damaging to your hair.

Hair Care Routine for Curly Hair:

Here’s a hair care routine for curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  4. Apply a curl-enhancing cream or gel to your hair.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  6. Once your hair is dry, scrunch your hair to enhance your curls.
  7. Apply a hairspray to hold your curls in place.
  8. Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.
  9. Use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  10. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

With the right products and hair care routine, you can achieve the curly hair of your dreams. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and desired style.
