Best Oil For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by William Sep 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know that it can be difficult to manage. It's important to find the right products that can help you tame your locks and keep them looking healthy and shiny. One of the best things you can do for your hair is to use the right oil. In this article, we'll be discussing the best oil for thick wavy hair, as well as how to use it and some tips for taking care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use
  • Step by Step
  • Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care

Description

The best oil for thick wavy hair is argan oil. It's a lightweight oil that won't weigh down your hair, but it will still provide the nourishment and hydration that your hair needs. Argan oil is also rich in antioxidants, which can help protect your hair from damage caused by free radicals.

In addition to argan oil, there are other oils that can be good for thick wavy hair, including coconut oil, jojoba oil, and almond oil. However, these oils may be heavier than argan oil, so they may not be the best choice if you have very thick hair.

How to Use

To use argan oil on your thick wavy hair, start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet. Then, apply a small amount of argan oil to your palms and rub them together to warm up the oil.

Next, apply the oil to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be sure to focus on the ends of your hair, as they tend to be the driest and most damaged. Avoid applying too much oil to your roots, as this can make your hair look greasy.

Once you've applied the oil, comb your hair to distribute it evenly. You can then style your hair as usual. If you have very thick hair, you may need to use a little more oil than someone with thinner hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to using argan oil on your thick wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Apply a small amount of argan oil to your palms and rub them together to warm up the oil.
  4. Apply the oil to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  5. Focus on the ends of your hair, as they tend to be the driest and most damaged.
  6. Avoid applying too much oil to your roots, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  7. Comb your hair to distribute the oil evenly.
  8. Style your hair as usual.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like flat irons and curling irons, too often. These can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about using oil on thick wavy hair:

Can I use oil on my hair every day?

It's best to use oil on your hair every other day, or even just a few times a week. Using too much oil can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down.

Can I use any type of oil on my hair?

Not all oils are created equal when it comes to hair care. Argan oil, coconut oil, jojoba oil, and almond oil are all good choices for thick wavy hair. However, other oils, like olive oil and castor oil, may be too heavy and can make your hair look greasy.

Can oil make my hair grow faster?

While oil can help nourish and hydrate your hair, there's no scientific evidence to suggest that it can make your hair grow faster. However, keeping your hair healthy and well-nourished may help prevent breakage and damage, which can help your hair grow longer over time.

Hair Care

Using the right oil can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your thick wavy hair. Argan oil is a great choice, but there are other oils that can work well too. Be sure to take good care of your hair by using the right products and following a healthy hair care routine. With a little bit of effort, you can keep your locks looking their best!
