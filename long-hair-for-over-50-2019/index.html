Long Hair For Over 50 2019 Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you over 50 and considering growing out your hair? Long hair can be a beautiful and timeless look for women of any age, but it can be especially stunning for those in their golden years. In this guide, we will go over everything you need to know about long hair for over 50 in 2019, including how to achieve it, tips for styling and care, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Achieve Long Hair for Over 50
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Out Your Hair
  3. Tips for Styling Long Hair Over 50
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips for Long Hair Over 50

How to Achieve Long Hair for Over 50

Before you can have long hair, you need to grow it out! This can take anywhere from several months to a couple of years, depending on your starting length and how quickly your hair grows. To achieve long hair for over 50, follow these steps:

Step 1: Commit to Growing Out Your Hair

It can be tempting to give up and cut your hair short again when you hit that awkward in-between stage, but resist the urge! Keep in mind that the end result will be worth it.

Step 2: Get Regular Trims

While you're growing out your hair, it's important to get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends. Aim for a trim every 8-12 weeks.

Step 3: Be Patient

Remember, growing out your hair takes time, so be patient and enjoy the journey. In the meantime, experiment with different hair accessories and styles to keep your hair looking fresh and stylish.

Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Out Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to growing out your hair:

Step 1: Start with a Good Haircut

Before you start growing out your hair, it's important to start with a good haircut. This will help ensure that your hair grows out evenly and looks healthy throughout the process.

Step 2: Be Patient

As mentioned earlier, growing out your hair takes time, so be patient and don't get discouraged if it seems like your hair isn't growing as quickly as you'd like.

Step 3: Experiment with Different Styles

While you're growing out your hair, experiment with different styles to keep your hair looking fresh and stylish. Try braids, buns, and other updos to keep your hair off your face and neck.

Tips for Styling Long Hair Over 50

Here are some tips for styling long hair over 50:

Tip 1: Embrace Your Natural Texture

As you get older, your hair may start to change in texture. Embrace your natural texture and work with it, rather than against it.

Tip 2: Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can add a fun and stylish touch to your long hair. Try adding a headband, scarf, or hair clip to your look.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Partings

Changing up your parting can give your long hair a fresh and new look. Try a middle part, side part, or zigzag part to switch things up.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will long hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Long hair can be youthful and timeless, especially when styled in a modern and trendy way.

Q: Is it okay to keep my hair long if it's thinning?

A: Yes! Long hair can actually make thinning hair look fuller and thicker. Just make sure to avoid heavy layers and opt for a blunt cut instead.

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but generally, washing your hair every 2-3 days is sufficient. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair Over 50

Here are some hair care tips for long hair over 50:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Long hair can be prone to dryness and damage, so it's important to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can cause damage to your hair over time. Try to limit your use of these tools and opt for heatless styling techniques instead.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Just like your skin, your hair can be damaged by the sun's harmful UV rays. Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to keep your hair safe.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving beautiful, long hair over 50 in 2019. Remember to be patient and enjoy the journey!
