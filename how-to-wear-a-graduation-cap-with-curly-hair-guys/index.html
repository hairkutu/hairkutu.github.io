How To Wear A Graduation Cap With Curly Hair Guys

Written by Robby Apr 13, 2023 · 8 min read

Graduation is a momentous occasion, and it's only natural to want to look your best. But if you're a guy with curly hair, you may be wondering how to wear a graduation cap without ruining your hairstyle. Don't worry – we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to wear a graduation cap with curly hair guys and provide you with some useful tips to ensure your hair stays looking great throughout the ceremony.

Description

Graduation is a time when you want to look your best. However, for guys with curly hair, wearing a graduation cap can be a challenge. The cap can flatten the curls, and you may end up with a frizzy mess. But with a few simple tips, you can wear your graduation cap with confidence and keep your curls looking great.

How to Wear a Graduation Cap with Curly Hair Guys

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step to wearing a graduation cap with curly hair is to prepare your hair. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and prevent them from becoming frizzy. After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is clean and moisturized, it's time to style it. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to comb or rake through your curls. This will help to define your curls and create volume. If you want to add more volume, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Avoid using a brush or comb on your curls, as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Position the Graduation Cap

Now that your hair is styled, it's time to position the graduation cap. Place the cap on your head and adjust it until it's level. The cap should sit just above your eyebrows and be centered on your head. If you have long hair, tuck it behind your ears or under the cap to keep it in place.

Step 4: Secure the Graduation Cap

Once the cap is in position, it's time to secure it. Use bobby pins to anchor the cap to your hair. Insert the bobby pins through the openings in the cap and into your hair. Make sure to insert the pins at an angle to prevent them from slipping out. Use as many bobby pins as necessary to keep the cap in place.

Tips for Wearing a Graduation Cap with Curly Hair Guys

Here are some additional tips to help you wear your graduation cap with curly hair:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Consider using a hair gel or hairspray to help hold your curls in place.
  • If you're unsure about how to style your hair, consider visiting a hair stylist who specializes in curly hair.

FAQ

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail under my graduation cap?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail under your graduation cap. Just make sure the ponytail is low enough that it doesn't interfere with the cap.

Can I wear a hat over my graduation cap?

No, you should not wear a hat over your graduation cap. This can damage the cap and make it difficult to see.

Can I wear my graduation cap tilted to one side?

No, you should wear your graduation cap level on your head. Tilted caps can be distracting and may interfere with the ceremony.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Guys

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, which can dry out your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using a brush or comb on your curls, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

With these tips, you can wear your graduation cap with confidence and keep your curls looking great. Congratulations on your graduation!
