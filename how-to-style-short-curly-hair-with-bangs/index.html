How To Style Short Curly Hair With Bangs

Written by Eveline Apr 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have short curly hair with bangs, you might find it challenging to style it in a way that looks good and suits your personality. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can create a gorgeous look that will make heads turn. In this article, we will give you some tips and ideas on how to style your short curly hair with bangs, so keep reading!

Description

Short curly hair with bangs is a trendy and stylish look that many people love. However, it can be challenging to style, especially if you don't know how to work with your curls. The key to styling short curly hair with bangs is to embrace your natural texture and find a look that suits your face shape and personality. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a more relaxed and natural style, there are many ways to style your short curly hair with bangs.

How to Style Short Curly Hair with Bangs

Here are some steps to follow when styling your short curly hair with bangs:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling your short curly hair with bangs, make sure it's clean and dry. You can use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture. Once you've washed your hair, use a towel to blot it dry. Avoid rubbing your hair, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

To make sure your curls look their best, apply a styling product that suits your hair type and texture. You can use a curl-enhancing cream, gel, or mousse. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends and the roots. Make sure you don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands. This will help enhance your curls and create volume. Make sure you don't use too much heat, as this can damage your hair. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define your curls and create shape.

Step 4: Style your bangs

To style your bangs, you can use a small round brush and a blow dryer. Blow dry your bangs while brushing them downwards and to the side. Once they're dry, you can use a flat iron to smooth them out or curl them under. You can also use a hair clip or bobby pin to pin them to the side for a more relaxed look.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To make sure your style lasts all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your curls in place and prevent frizz. Use a hairspray that suits your hair type and texture, and avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short curly hair with bangs:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently
  • Don't brush your curls, as this can cause frizz
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your curls
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Try different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short curly hair with bangs:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you can wash it every other day. However, you should avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Q: Can I straighten my curls?

A: Yes, you can use a flat iron to straighten your curls. However, you should avoid using too much heat, as this can damage your hair. Make sure you use a heat protectant spray and a low-to-medium heat setting.

Q: How can I prevent frizz?

A: You can prevent frizz by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, applying a styling product that suits your hair type, and avoiding brushing your hair. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and a satin or silk pillowcase.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair with bangs looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends. You should also protect your hair from heat and sun damage by using a heat protectant spray and wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside.

In conclusion, styling short curly hair with bangs can be challenging, but with the right techniques and tools, you can create a gorgeous look that suits your personality. Follow these steps, tips, and ideas to achieve the perfect style for your short curly hair with bangs.
