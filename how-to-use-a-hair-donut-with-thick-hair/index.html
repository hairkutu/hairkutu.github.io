How To Use A Hair Donut With Thick Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 27, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick hair, you might think that using a hair donut is impossible. But fear not, because with the right technique and a little patience, you can create a beautiful bun that looks like it was made by a professional stylist. In this blog post, we’ll show you exactly how to use a hair donut with thick hair.

What is a Hair Donut?

A hair donut is a foam or mesh device that helps you create a perfectly shaped bun. It’s perfect for those who want a sleek and polished look without spending hours styling their hair.

How to Choose the Right Hair Donut for Thick Hair

When choosing a hair donut, make sure to select one that matches the color of your hair. For those with thick hair, a larger-sized hair donut will work best.

How to Use a Hair Donut with Thick Hair

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary supplies. You’ll need a hair donut, bobby pins, and a hair tie.

Step 2: Make a Ponytail

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 3: Thread Your Hair Through the Hair Donut

Place the hair donut at the base of your ponytail and thread your hair through the center of the donut.

Step 4: Spread Your Hair Over the Donut

Cover the hair donut with your hair, spreading it evenly over the surface.

Step 5: Roll the Donut Down Your Hair

Roll the hair donut down your hair until it reaches the base of your ponytail.

Step 6: Secure the Bun with Bobby Pins

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place, making sure to tuck in any loose strands.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Apply hairspray to keep your bun in place all day.

Tips for Using a Hair Donut with Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect bun with a hair donut:

  • Use a hair donut that is slightly larger than your ponytail to accommodate your thick hair.
  • Make sure to spread your hair evenly over the donut to avoid any bumps or unevenness.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place, making sure to tuck in any loose strands.
  • Apply hairspray to keep your bun looking sleek and polished all day long.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a hair donut with curly hair?

Yes, you can use a hair donut with curly hair. Make sure to detangle your hair before putting it in a ponytail and use a larger-sized hair donut to accommodate your curls.

Can I use a hair donut with short hair?

It is more difficult to use a hair donut with short hair, but it is possible. Use a smaller-sized hair donut and wrap your hair tightly around it to create a small bun.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant first.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these hair care tips and using a hair donut correctly, you can create a beautiful bun that looks like it was done by a professional stylist.
