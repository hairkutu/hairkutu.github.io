What Frames Look Good On Oval Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oval face shape, count yourself lucky! This face shape is considered the most balanced and versatile, with a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a gently rounded chin. When it comes to eyewear, you have a lot of options to choose from. But with so many different frame styles and shapes out there, it can be tough to know where to start. In this guide, we'll explore what frames look good on oval face shapes and how to choose the perfect pair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose Frames for Oval Face Shapes
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Frames
  3. Tips for Choosing the Perfect Frames
  4. FAQ About Frames for Oval Face Shapes
  5. How to Style Your Hair with Your New Frames

How to Choose Frames for Oval Face Shapes

The first thing to know is that oval face shapes can pull off almost any frame shape or style. The key is to find frames that balance out your features and complement your personal style. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Look for frames that are as wide as or slightly wider than the broadest part of your face, which is typically around your cheekbones.
  • Avoid frames that are too small or too oversized, as they can throw off your face's natural balance.
  • Consider frames with soft angles or curves to complement your face's gentle curves.
  • Experiment with different materials, colors, and patterns to find a pair that suits your personality and style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Frames

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing frames for oval face shapes:

  1. Start by measuring your face using a measuring tape or ruler. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, as well as the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.
  2. Next, think about your personal style and the look you want to achieve. Do you prefer bold, statement-making frames or something more subtle and classic?
  3. Consider the shape of your face and the features you want to highlight or downplay. For example, if you want to emphasize your high cheekbones, choose frames with a slight curve that follows the natural line of your cheekbones.
  4. Try on different frame shapes and styles to see what looks and feels best. Don't be afraid to experiment with different materials, colors, and patterns.
  5. Finally, make sure your frames fit properly. They should sit comfortably on your nose, without slipping down or pinching. The arms should rest comfortably on your ears without putting too much pressure on them.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Frames

Here are a few additional tips to help you choose the perfect frames:

  • Consider the shape of your eyebrows. Frames that follow the natural curve of your brows can help highlight your eyes and create a more harmonious look.
  • Think about your skin tone and hair color. Frames that complement your natural coloring can help bring out your best features.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match different frame styles and materials. For example, you could pair a classic metal frame with a fun, colorful acetate browline.
  • If you're unsure about which frames to choose, ask a friend or family member for their opinion.

FAQ About Frames for Oval Face Shapes

Q: What frame shapes should I avoid if I have an oval face?

A: There are no hard and fast rules when it comes to frames for oval face shapes, but you may want to avoid frames that are too small or too oversized. Additionally, frames with sharp angles or harsh lines may not be as flattering as those with soft curves and rounded edges.

Q: Can oval face shapes wear aviator frames?

A: Yes! Aviator frames are a classic style that looks great on almost any face shape, including oval. Look for frames that are slightly wider than your face and have a slightly curved top bar to complement your face's gentle curves.

Q: Are there any frame materials that work particularly well for oval face shapes?

A: Oval face shapes can pull off almost any frame material, but you may want to consider lightweight materials like titanium or acetate for added comfort and durability.

How to Style Your Hair with Your New Frames

Once you've found the perfect frames for your oval face shape, you may want to update your hairstyle to complement your new look. Here are a few ideas:

  • Consider a shorter haircut to show off your frames and highlight your facial features.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find a look that flatters your face shape.
  • Tuck your hair behind your ears to show off your frames and create a more streamlined look.
  • Try a half-up, half-down hairstyle to balance out your features and create a more romantic look.

Remember, the most important thing is to choose frames that make you feel comfortable and confident. With so many different styles and shapes to choose from, you're sure to find a pair that's perfect for you!
