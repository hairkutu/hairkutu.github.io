Best Medium Length Hairstyles For Long Faces

Written by Frank Apr 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you someone with a long face and medium length hair? Do you often find it difficult to choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will discuss some of the best medium length hairstyles for long faces that will bring out the best in you.

Description

Long faces are characterized by a narrow chin, high forehead, and a long vertical line. The key to choosing a hairstyle for long faces is to create an illusion of width and volume. Medium length hairstyles are perfect for long faces as they add volume and width to your face.

How To

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for long faces, it is important to consider the shape of your face. The following are some of the best medium length hairstyles for long faces:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a perfect hairstyle for long faces. This hairstyle adds volume and movement to your hair, creating an illusion of width. The layers should be cut in such a way that they frame your face and add volume to your crown.

2. Wavy Lob

A wavy lob is a great hairstyle for long faces as it adds volume and texture to your hair. The waves should be loose and relaxed, creating an illusion of width. This hairstyle can be achieved using a curling iron or by braiding your hair and leaving it overnight.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to balance out a long face. These bangs should be long and sweeping, creating an illusion of width. You can pair these bangs with any medium length hairstyle for a flattering look.

Step By Step

Here are the steps to achieve a perfect layered bob:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.

Step 3: Part your hair down the middle and divide it into sections.

Step 4: Cut the layers starting from the top and moving towards the bottom.

Step 5: Style your hair using a straightener or a curling iron.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while choosing a medium length hairstyle for long faces:

1. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short as they can make your face look even longer.

2. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too straight as they can make your face look longer.

3. Choose hairstyles that add volume and width to your face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for long faces:

Q. Can I wear bangs with a long face?

A. Yes, you can wear bangs with a long face. Side-swept bangs are a great choice for long faces.

Q. Can I wear my hair straight with a long face?

A. Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a long face. However, it is important to add volume to your hair to create an illusion of width.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.

2. Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

3. Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

4. Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

In conclusion, medium length hairstyles are a great choice for long faces. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and adds volume and width to your face. Follow the hair care tips to maintain a healthy and shiny look.
