How To Straighten Your Hair And Still Have Volume

Written by Petter May 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having flat and lifeless hair after straightening it? Fear not, because with the right techniques and products, you can achieve straight hair with volume. In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to straighten your hair and still have volume.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The key to achieving straight hair with volume is to use a combination of heat tools and products. Here are the steps:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will add volume to your hair from the very beginning.

2. Apply a Volumizing Mousse

After washing and conditioning, apply a volumizing mousse to your hair. This will help to add volume and hold to your hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. This will help to add volume, especially at the roots. Make sure to blow dry your hair until it is completely dry.

4. Straighten Your Hair

Once your hair is completely dry, use a straightening iron to straighten your hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before straightening to protect your hair from heat damage.

5. Apply a Volumizing Powder

After straightening, apply a volumizing powder to your roots. This will help to add even more volume and texture to your hair.

6. Finish with Hairspray

To finish off your look, apply a light hairspray to hold your hair in place while still allowing it to have movement.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you straighten your hair and still have volume:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair in sections with a round brush.
  4. Straighten your hair with a straightening iron, using a heat protectant spray.
  5. Apply a volumizing powder to your roots.
  6. Finish off with a light hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a high-quality straightening iron.
  • Blow dry your hair in the opposite direction to add more volume.
  • Use a volumizing powder sparingly.
  • Don't apply too much hairspray, as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to straighten your hair and still have volume:

Can I use a volumizing spray instead of mousse?

Yes, you can use a volumizing spray instead of mousse. However, mousse tends to provide more hold and texture to the hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. However, it is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming overly dry.

Can I straighten my hair without a heat protectant spray?

No, it is not recommended to straighten your hair without a heat protectant spray. Heat can cause damage to your hair, and a heat protectant spray helps to minimize this damage.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve straight hair with volume. Remember to use heat protectant spray, volumizing products, and a light hairspray to achieve the best results. Happy styling!
