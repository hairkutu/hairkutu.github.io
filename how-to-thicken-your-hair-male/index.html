How To Thicken Your Hair Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Aug 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a man who wants to add some volume to your hair? Thinning hair is a common problem among men, but there are ways to thicken your hair and give it a fuller appearance. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks to naturally thicken your hair.

How to Thicken Your Hair Male: A Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Use a Thickening Shampoo

Using a thickening shampoo is one of the easiest ways to add volume to your hair. These shampoos contain ingredients that coat the hair shaft and make it appear thicker. Look for shampoos that contain biotin, keratin, and amino acids, as these ingredients can help strengthen the hair and prevent breakage.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

After shampooing, it's important to condition your hair. Use a conditioner specifically designed for thinning hair. Conditioners help to add moisture to the hair, which can make it appear fuller. Be sure to apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and avoid the roots, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can also help to add volume. Use a round brush while blow drying your hair to lift the roots and create volume. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before blow drying to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Hair Thickening Spray

Hair thickening sprays are another great way to add volume to your hair. These sprays contain polymers that coat the hair shaft and add thickness. Spray the product onto your roots and use your fingers to massage it in.

Tips for Thicker Hair

Aside from the steps above, there are other things you can do to naturally thicken your hair.

Tip 1: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote hair growth. Foods that are high in protein, such as eggs and lean meats, can also help to strengthen the hair.

Tip 2: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help to stimulate hair growth. Use your fingers to massage your scalp in a circular motion for a few minutes each day.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it look thinner. Try to avoid using them as much as possible. If you must use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Tip 4: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it appear thinner. Try to wash your hair every other day or every two days.

FAQs

Q: Can hair thickening products damage your hair?

A: Hair thickening products are generally safe to use, but some products can contain harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. Be sure to read the ingredients before using any product.

Q: How long does it take to see results?

A: It can take several weeks to see results from using hair thickening products. Be patient and continue using the product as directed.

Q: Can genetics play a role in thinning hair?

A: Yes, genetics can play a role in thinning hair. If you have a family history of hair loss, you may be more likely to experience it yourself.

Hair Care for Thicker Hair

Once you've achieved thicker hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent further thinning. Use a wide-toothed comb to brush your hair and avoid pulling it too tightly. Be sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thinner. And don't forget to continue eating a healthy diet to keep your hair strong and healthy.

In conclusion, there are many ways to naturally thicken your hair as a male. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the fuller, thicker hair you desire.
