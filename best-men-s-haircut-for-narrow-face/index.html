Best Men s Haircut For Narrow Face

Written by Aprilia Aug 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for the best men's haircut for narrow face? Narrow faces are often characterized by a longer length and a narrower width. This can make it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your facial features. However, with the right haircut, you can enhance your best features and create a more balanced and proportionate look. In this article, we'll explore the best men's haircut for narrow face, how to style it, and tips for hair care.

Description

The best men's haircut for narrow face is one that adds width and volume to the sides of the face while maintaining a balanced look. To achieve this, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short on the sides. Instead, opt for a haircut that has volume and texture on the sides while keeping the top shorter. This will create the illusion of a wider face and balance out your facial features.

How to

The best men's haircut for narrow face is the textured crop. This haircut involves cutting the sides short and leaving the top longer with layers and texture. This style works well for narrow faces as it adds volume and width to the sides while keeping the top shorter and more balanced. To achieve this style, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with your stylist

Before getting the textured crop, consult with your stylist to ensure that it's the best haircut for your facial features. Your stylist will be able to recommend the best length and style for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Cut the sides short

The sides of the hair should be cut short to create volume and texture. This will also make the face appear wider and more proportionate.

Step 3: Leave the top longer

The top of the hair should be left longer with layers and texture. This will add volume and balance out the face.

Step 4: Style the hair

To style the hair, apply a small amount of styling product to the top of the hair and use your fingers to create texture and volume. You can also use a hair dryer to add more volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the best men's haircut for narrow face:

  • Choose a style that complements your facial features
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long on the sides
  • Ask your stylist for recommendations based on your hair type and face shape
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to the hair
  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best men's haircut for narrow face:

What is the best haircut for a narrow face?

The best haircut for a narrow face is the textured crop. This style involves cutting the sides short and leaving the top longer with layers and texture to add volume and width to the face.

Can I still have long hair with a narrow face?

Yes, you can still have long hair with a narrow face. However, it's important to avoid hairstyles that are too flat or make the face appear longer. Opt for a style with layers and volume to add width and balance.

How often should I get my hair cut?

You should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut. Regular trims will also keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using styling products to add volume and texture, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Additionally, it's important to protect your hair from damage by avoiding excessive heat styling and using a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. You should also avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes that can damage the hair and cause breakage.

By following these tips and maintaining a regular hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking healthy, shiny, and stylish.
