How To Cut Women s Hair Short With Clippers At Home

Written by Eveline Aug 25, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of spending too much money at the salon for a simple haircut? Do you want to try cutting your own hair at home but don’t know where to start? Cutting women’s hair short with clippers at home is easier than you think. Follow this step-by-step guide to achieve a professional-looking cut without leaving your house.

Description

Using clippers to cut women’s hair short at home is a great way to save time and money. It’s also a convenient option for those who prefer short haircuts but don’t want to visit a salon. Clippers can be used to create a variety of styles, from a classic pixie cut to a trendy buzz cut. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a flawless cut every time.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the necessary tools. You’ll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It’s also important to choose the right clipper guard for the desired length of your hair.

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Comb it to remove any tangles or knots.

2. Use the hair clips to section your hair into manageable parts. Clip the top layer of your hair up and out of the way.

3. Attach the desired clipper guard to your clippers. Begin at the nape of your neck and work your way up, using the clippers to trim the hair in even sections.

4. Continue using the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Be sure to use a mirror to check for any uneven spots.

5. Once you’re satisfied with the length of the hair, use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots.

6. Unclip the top layer of your hair and use the comb to lift sections of hair straight up. Use the scissors to trim the hair to the desired length.

7. Once you’ve finished trimming the top layer, use the clippers to blend the sides and top of your hair together.

8. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it’s even and to your liking.

Step by Step

1. Wash and dry your hair. Comb it to remove any tangles or knots.

2. Use hair clips to section your hair into manageable parts. Clip the top layer of your hair up and out of the way.

3. Attach the desired clipper guard to your clippers. Begin at the nape of your neck and work your way up, using the clippers to trim the hair in even sections.

4. Continue using the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Be sure to use a mirror to check for any uneven spots.

5. Once you’re satisfied with the length of the hair, use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots.

6. Unclip the top layer of your hair and use the comb to lift sections of hair straight up. Use the scissors to trim the hair to the desired length.

7. Once you’ve finished trimming the top layer, use the clippers to blend the sides and top of your hair together.

8. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it’s even and to your liking.

Tips

1. Use a clipper guard to create an even length throughout your hair.

2. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if necessary.

3. Use scissors to create texture and blend the hair together.

4. Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots.

5. Take your time and be patient. Cutting your hair at home may take longer than a salon visit.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair with clippers if it’s curly?

A: Yes, you can. However, it’s important to use a longer clipper guard to avoid cutting too much hair.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair growth rate and desired length. On average, short haircuts should be maintained every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, it’s important to take care of it properly. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant if using hot tools. Regular trims will also help maintain the shape of your haircut.

Cutting women’s hair short with clippers at home is a great way to save money and time. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking cut in the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and always use a mirror to check for any uneven spots.
