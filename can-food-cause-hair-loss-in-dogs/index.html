Can Food Cause Hair Loss In Dogs

Written by Eveline Mar 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As pet owners, we always want our dogs to look healthy and happy. One of the most noticeable signs of a dog's overall well-being is its coat. A shiny, thick coat is usually an indication of good health, while a dull, thin one can be a sign of underlying health issues. One of the biggest concerns of pet owners is hair loss in their dogs. While there could be many reasons why dogs lose their hair, one that's often overlooked is their diet. So, can food cause hair loss in dogs? Let's find out.

Description

Dogs that lose hair due to a poor diet usually have a dull coat, thinning hair, and bald patches. The texture of the coat may also change, becoming coarse or brittle. The skin underneath may be dry or flaky, and the dog may have excessive itching or biting of the affected areas. In severe cases, the dog may even develop infections or hot spots.

How to Identify if Your Dog is Losing Hair Due to Diet?

If you suspect that your dog is losing hair due to diet, the first thing you should do is take a closer look at their food. Check the label for any fillers or artificial additives that may be causing the problem. If you are not sure, consult your veterinarian for advice. They may suggest switching to a high-quality dog food that is free of fillers and preservatives.

Step by Step Guide to Prevent Hair Loss Due to Diet

Step 1: Choose High-Quality Dog Food

The first step to preventing hair loss due to diet is to choose high-quality dog food. Look for a brand that has real meat as the first ingredient and is free of fillers, preservatives, and artificial flavors or colors. Also, make sure that the food is appropriate for your dog's age, breed, and size.

Step 2: Add Supplements to Their Diet

If your dog is not getting enough nutrients from their food, you can add supplements to their diet. Some of the supplements that are good for their coat and skin include fish oil, Vitamin E, and biotin.

Step 3: Provide Fresh Water

Make sure that your dog has access to fresh water at all times. Dehydration can affect their overall health and the condition of their coat and skin.

Tips to Keep Your Dog's Coat Healthy

Here are some additional tips to keep your dog's coat healthy:

  • Regular grooming
  • Bathe them regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep their coat soft and shiny
  • Regular exercise to improve circulation
  • Regular visits to the vet to catch any underlying health issues

FAQs

Q. Can food cause hair loss in dogs?

A. Yes, a poor diet can cause hair loss in dogs. Dogs that are not getting enough nutrients from their food may have a dull coat, thinning hair, and bald patches.

Q. What should I feed my dog to prevent hair loss?

A. You should feed your dog a high-quality dog food that is free of fillers and preservatives. Look for a brand that has real meat as the first ingredient and is appropriate for your dog's age, breed, and size.

Hair Care for Dogs

Just like humans, dogs need proper nutrition and care to maintain a healthy coat. A poor diet can cause hair loss, but with the right food and supplements, you can prevent this from happening. Regular grooming, bathing, and exercise can also help keep your dog's coat shiny and healthy. If you notice any changes in your dog's coat or skin, consult your veterinarian for advice.
