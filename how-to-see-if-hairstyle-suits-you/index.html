How To See If Hairstyle Suits You A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Look

Written by Bardi May 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but unsure if it will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle? Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, with so many options available. However, with a few simple tricks, you can easily determine which hairstyle will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding your perfect hairstyle, step by step.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyles will suit you best. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Using a tape measure or ruler, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Note down the measurements.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Shape

Compare your measurements with the following guidelines to determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Forehead width is greater than the jawline, and the face has a gently rounded hairline and chin.
  • Round: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width, and the face has a rounded hairline and chin.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width, and the face has a square hairline and jawline.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the jawline, and the face has a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest point on the face, and the face has a narrow forehead and jawline.

Knowing your face shape will help you choose a hairstyle that will balance your features and create a flattering look.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit you best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Oval face: Almost all hairstyles suit this face shape, but avoid hairstyles that cover your face.
  • Round face: Choose hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown and avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides.
  • Square face: Choose hairstyles that soften the jawline and forehead, such as layered and wavy hairstyles.
  • Heart face: Choose hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as chin-length bobs or layered hairstyles.
  • Diamond face: Choose hairstyles that create width at the forehead and jawline, such as side-swept bangs or layered hairstyles.

Consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you have thick and curly hair, you may prefer a hairstyle that is low maintenance and easy to manage. If you lead an active lifestyle, you may prefer a hairstyle that can be tied back and out of the way.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me before I try it?

A: You can use virtual hairstyle apps or try on wigs to see how a hairstyle will look on you before committing to a haircut.

Q: How often should I change my hairstyle?

A: It depends on your preference and lifestyle. Some people prefer to stick to one hairstyle, while others like to experiment and try new looks every few months.

Q: How can I maintain my hairstyle?

A: Follow a hair care routine that suits your hair type and use styling products that protect and nourish your hair. Regular trims and salon visits will also help maintain your hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and scalp.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a balanced diet to nourish your hair from the inside out.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, no matter what hairstyle you choose.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, you can find a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle, and follow a hair care routine that suits your hair type. With these tips, you can find your perfect look and feel confident and beautiful.
