The Best Way To Cut Your Own Hair Straight Across A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to save money and take control of your hair, cutting it yourself can be a great option. However, cutting your hair straight across can be a bit tricky, especially if you're new to the process. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on the best way to cut your own hair straight across, so you can achieve the perfect look in the comfort of your own home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Hair Straight Across
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

How to Cut Your Hair Straight Across

Cutting your hair straight across is easier than you might think. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a beautiful, even cut that looks like it was done by a professional. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools, it's time to get started. Follow these steps to cut your hair straight across:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. It's important to start with clean, dry hair so you can see exactly how it will look when it's cut.
  2. Comb your hair straight down. Make sure your hair is completely detangled and combed straight down from your crown to the nape of your neck.
  3. Divide your hair. Part your hair in the middle and divide it into two even sections, one on each side of your head.
  4. Clip the top sections. Clip the top sections of your hair up and out of the way so you can focus on cutting the bottom sections first.
  5. Cut the bottom sections. Using your comb as a guide, cut the bottom sections of your hair straight across. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to ensure an even cut.
  6. Unclip the top sections. Once you're happy with the bottom sections, unclip the top sections and repeat the process of cutting small sections straight across.
  7. Check for evenness. Once you've finished cutting all sections of your hair, use a mirror to check for evenness. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind while cutting your hair:

  • Start with small sections. Cutting small sections at a time will help you achieve an even cut.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can cause split ends and make it difficult to achieve a clean cut.
  • Don't cut too much. It's better to take your time and cut a little at a time than to cut too much and end up with a style you don't like.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your hair straight across:

Can I cut my hair straight across with any type of scissors?

No, it's important to use sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Using regular scissors can lead to split ends and an uneven cut.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Is it possible to cut my hair too short?

Yes, it's possible to cut your hair too short. That's why it's important to start with small sections and cut a little at a time.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

After you've finished cutting your hair straight across, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools for at least a few days.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your cut and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best way to cut your own hair straight across, you can save money and enjoy a new look without leaving your home. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the steps carefully for the best results.
