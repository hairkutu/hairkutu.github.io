How To Style Hair While Growing Out Bangs

Written by Oliver Apr 18, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're currently in the process of growing out your bangs, you know how frustrating it can be to style your hair during this awkward phase. But fear not, there are plenty of ways to style your hair while growing out bangs that will keep you looking chic and stylish.

Table of Contents

  • How To Style Hair While Growing Out Bangs
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Hair While Growing Out Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Bangs

How To Style Hair While Growing Out Bangs

One of the easiest ways to style your hair while growing out bangs is to simply part your hair in the middle. This will create a sleek and chic look that is perfect for any occasion. However, if you want to switch things up a bit, there are plenty of other options to choose from.

One popular option is to go for a side-swept look. This is achieved by sweeping your bangs to one side of your face and pinning them in place. This look is perfect for when you want to add a touch of glamour to your outfit.

Another great option is to braid your bangs. This is a fun and playful look that is perfect for casual occasions. Simply braid your bangs and pin them back with bobby pins.

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style your hair while growing out bangs:

  1. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair as usual.
  2. Blow dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  3. Part your hair down the middle or to the side, depending on your preference.
  4. If you want to go for a side-swept look, sweep your bangs to one side of your face and pin them in place with bobby pins.
  5. If you want to braid your bangs, simply braid them and pin them back with bobby pins.
  6. Finish off your look with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Styling Hair While Growing Out Bangs

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your hair while growing out bangs:

  • Experiment with different parting styles until you find one that works for you.
  • Invest in some good quality bobby pins to keep your hair in place.
  • Try out different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some extra flair to your look.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add some volume and texture to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out bangs:

Q: How long does it take to grow out bangs?

A: It can take anywhere from 3-6 months to grow out bangs, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Q: Can I still style my hair while growing out bangs?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of ways to style your hair while growing out bangs, as we've outlined in this article.

Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind while growing out your bangs:

  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair mask to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Be patient! Growing out your bangs can be a slow process, but it will be worth it in the end.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your hair with confidence while growing out your bangs. Whether you choose to go for a sleek middle part or a playful braid, there are plenty of options to choose from. So embrace this awkward phase and have fun experimenting with different styles!
