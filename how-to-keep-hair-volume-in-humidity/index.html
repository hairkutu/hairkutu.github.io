How To Keep Hair Volume In Humidity Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Humidity can be a nightmare for anyone who loves to keep their hair voluminous and bouncy. The humidity in the air can make your hair look flat, limp and lifeless, which can be frustrating. However, there are some tips and tricks that you can follow to keep your hair voluminous even in humid conditions. In this article, we will discuss how to keep hair volume in humidity and related keywords throughout the post.

How to Keep Hair Volume in Humidity

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

One of the most important things to keep your hair voluminous in humid conditions is to use the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed to add volume to your hair. These products will contain ingredients that will help to lift your hair and make it look more voluminous. Also, make sure to use a lightweight conditioner that does not weigh down your hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is a great way to add volume to your hair. This technique works by lifting your hair at the roots and creating more volume. To do this, flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair in a circular motion. Make sure to focus the blow dryer on the roots of your hair.

3. Use a Volumizing Mousse

A volumizing mousse is a great product to use if you want to add volume to your hair. Apply the mousse to your hair while it is still damp, and then blow dry your hair as usual. The mousse will help to add volume to your hair and keep it looking bouncy and voluminous.

4. Avoid Heavy Hair Products

Avoid using heavy hair products, such as hair serums and oils, in humid conditions. These products can weigh down your hair and make it look flat and lifeless. Instead, use lightweight products that are designed to add volume to your hair.

5. Use a Root Lifting Spray

A root lifting spray is a great product to use if you want to add volume to your hair. Apply the spray to the roots of your hair and then blow dry your hair as usual. The spray will help to lift your hair at the roots and create more volume.

6. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Using a clarifying shampoo once a week is a great way to remove any product buildup from your hair. Product buildup can weigh down your hair and make it look flat and lifeless. A clarifying shampoo will help to remove any buildup and make your hair look more voluminous.

Step by Step Guide to Keep Hair Volume in Humidity

Follow these steps to keep your hair voluminous in humid conditions:

  1. Use the right shampoo and conditioner
  2. Blow dry your hair upside down
  3. Use a volumizing mousse
  4. Avoid heavy hair products
  5. Use a root lifting spray
  6. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week

Tips to Keep Hair Volume in Humidity

Here are some additional tips to keep your hair voluminous in humid conditions:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase

FAQs

Q. Can I use hairspray to keep my hair voluminous in humid conditions?

A. Yes, you can use hairspray to keep your hair voluminous in humid conditions. Look for a lightweight hairspray that is designed to add volume to your hair.

Q. Can I use dry shampoo to add volume to my hair?

A. Yes, dry shampoo is a great product to use if you want to add volume to your hair. Spray the dry shampoo at the roots of your hair and then massage it in with your fingers. This will help to lift your hair at the roots and create more volume.

Q. How often should I wash my hair in humid conditions?

A. It is recommended to wash your hair every other day in humid conditions. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and lifeless.

Hair Care Tips for Humid Conditions

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and voluminous in humid conditions:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair voluminous and bouncy even in humid conditions. Remember to use the right products and avoid heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair. Also, use a root lifting spray and blow dry your hair upside down to create more volume. With these tips, you can enjoy voluminous hair all year round!
