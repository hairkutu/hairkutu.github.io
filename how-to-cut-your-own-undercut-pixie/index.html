How To Cut Your Own Undercut Pixie

Written by Frank Jul 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy haircut that's easy to style, an undercut pixie might be just what you need. This trendy hairstyle is perfect for those who want to shave the sides of their head while keeping some length on top. While it may seem daunting to cut your own hair, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stunning undercut pixie from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of cutting your own undercut pixie, as well as offer tips for maintaining and styling your new 'do.

Description

The undercut pixie is a popular hairstyle that features shaved sides and longer hair on top. This haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit your unique style. It's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks chic and stylish.

How to Cut Your Own Undercut Pixie

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and hair ties. It's also a good idea to have a handheld mirror or a second mirror so you can see the back of your head.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into two parts: the top section and the bottom section. Use hair ties to keep the top section out of the way while you work on the undercut.

Step 3: Shave the Sides

Using your hair clippers, start shaving the sides of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use a number 2 or 3 guard for a shorter undercut, or a number 4 or 5 guard for a longer undercut.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've shaved the sides, use your scissors to blend the hair between the undercut and the longer hair on top. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Unclip the top section of your hair and use your scissors to trim the length. Start by cutting a little bit at a time and check your progress in the mirror.

Step 6: Add Texture

To add some texture and volume to your hair, use your scissors to point cut the ends. This will create a choppy, textured look that's perfect for an undercut pixie.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to style it. Use a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to add texture and hold. You can also use a hair dryer and a round brush to create volume and shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut pixie:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time
  • Use a handheld mirror or a second mirror to check the back of your head
  • Invest in high-quality tools to make the process easier

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own undercut pixie:

Can I cut my own hair if I have no experience?

While it's always best to seek the help of a professional stylist, cutting your own hair is possible with the right tools and techniques. Take your time and start with small sections to avoid any major mistakes.

How often should I trim my undercut pixie?

It's recommended to trim your undercut pixie every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut pixie looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Cutting your own undercut pixie can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and edgy haircut from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to avoid any major mistakes.
