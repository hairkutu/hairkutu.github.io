Best Haircut For Square Face 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you having trouble finding the perfect haircut for your square face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing the best haircut for square face 2021. We will cover everything from how to determine your face shape to the different hairstyles that will accentuate your features. So, let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best haircut for square face 2021 is to determine your face shape. Here is a quick and easy way to do it:

  1. Take a picture of your face straight on.
  2. Use a ruler or measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. If all three measurements are relatively the same, then you have a square face shape.

Tips:

Make sure you measure the widest part of each area, and write down the measurements so you can refer to them later.

The Best Haircut for Square Face 2021

Now that you know your face shape, let's talk about the best haircut for square face 2021. The key to finding the perfect haircut for a square face is to soften your sharp features. Here are some hairstyles that will do just that:

1. Long Layers

Long layers will help to soften your jawline and add movement to your hair. This is a great option for those with long hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs will help to soften your forehead and draw attention away from your angular jawline. This is a great option for those with medium-length hair.

3. Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob will help to soften your jawline and add volume to your hair. This is a great option for those with short hair.

4. Soft Waves

Soft waves will help to add movement to your hair and soften your sharp features. This is a great option for those with any hair length.

5. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut will help to highlight your cheekbones and draw attention away from your jawline. This is a great option for those with short hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a bob if I have a square face shape?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure it's a shoulder-length bob with soft, rounded edges.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a square face shape?

A: Yes, but make sure to add some volume and movement to your hair to soften your features.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Shape

Now that you have found the perfect haircut for your square face shape, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best haircut for square face 2021, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist! Remember, the key is to soften your sharp features and add movement to your hair. With the right cut and some hair care tips, you'll be looking and feeling your best in no time!
