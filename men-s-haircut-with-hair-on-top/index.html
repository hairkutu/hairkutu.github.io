Men s Haircut With Hair On Top A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will make you stand out? Then, a men's haircut with hair on top might be the perfect choice for you. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to get and maintain a men's haircut with hair on top.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Men's Haircut with Hair on Top
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining a Men's Haircut with Hair on Top
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Men's Haircut with Hair on Top

Description

A men's haircut with hair on top is a versatile hairstyle that features longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides and back. This style is often referred to as the "undercut" or the "disconnected undercut" because of the contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back. This hairstyle can be styled in different ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured, and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Get a Men's Haircut with Hair on Top

If you are interested in getting a men's haircut with hair on top, you will need to find a skilled hairstylist who has experience cutting this style. Make sure to bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to your hairstylist so they can get a clear understanding of the look you are going for.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a men's haircut with hair on top:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Decide on the length you want for the hair on top and communicate it clearly to your hairstylist.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to cut the sides and back of your hair using clippers. The length of the hair on the sides and back can be customized to suit your preference.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to cut the hair on top using scissors. The hair on top can be blended into the sides and back or left disconnected, depending on the look you are going for.
  5. Style your hair using a hair product of your choice. You can choose to slick it back, create a messy look, or add texture to it.

Tips for Maintaining a Men's Haircut with Hair on Top

Here are some tips for maintaining a men's haircut with hair on top:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the hairstyle.
  • Use a quality hair product to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircut with hair on top:

Q: Can I get a men's haircut with hair on top if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit different hair types, including curly hair. Your hairstylist will be able to recommend the best length and style for your curly hair.

Q: How long does it take to style a men's haircut with hair on top?

A: The time it takes to style a men's haircut with hair on top will depend on the length and texture of your hair and the style you are going for. On average, it can take anywhere from 5-15 minutes to style this hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Haircut with Hair on Top

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining a men's haircut with hair on top:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as this can cause build-up and make your hair look greasy.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling or tugging on it.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a men's haircut with hair on top that will make heads turn. Remember to communicate clearly with your hairstylist and maintain your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
