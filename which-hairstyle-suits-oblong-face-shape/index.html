Which Hairstyle Suits Oblong Face Shape: A Comprehensive Guide

If you have an oblong face shape, it can be difficult to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a high forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. In this article, we will guide you on which hairstyle suits oblong face shape and provide you with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Which Hairstyle Suits Oblong Face Shape
  • Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face Shape
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into which hairstyle suits oblong face shape, it is essential to determine your face shape accurately. To determine your face shape, follow these easy steps:

  1. Take a measuring tape and measure the widest part of your forehead.
  2. Measure your cheekbones from one end to another.
  3. Measure the width of your jawline.
  4. Measure the length of your face from the forehead to the chin.
  5. Once you have all the measurements, compare them to determine your face shape.

Some common face shapes include oval, round, square, heart-shaped, and oblong. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oblong face shape.

Which Hairstyle Suits Oblong Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle for an oblong face shape can help balance out the length of the face and create an illusion of width. Here are some hairstyles that suit oblong face shape:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can create an illusion of width and volume to the hair, making it a perfect choice for oblong face shape. The layers should start at the chin level and gradually get longer towards the ends. This hairstyle can be paired with side-swept bangs to further balance out the length of the face.

2. Bob

A bob haircut is another great option for oblong face shape. The bob should end at the jawline, creating an illusion of width to the face. This hairstyle can be paired with side-swept bangs or blunt bangs to balance out the length of the face.

3. Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out the length of the face and soften the forehead. The bangs should be long enough to touch the eyebrows but not too long that they cover the eyes. This hairstyle can be paired with any length of hair, from long to short.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and sassy hairstyle that can complement an oblong face shape. The cut should be shorter on the sides and longer on top, creating an illusion of width to the face. This hairstyle can be paired with short bangs or side-swept bangs to soften the forehead.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face Shape

To keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, here are some hair care tips for oblong face shape:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I have bangs with an oblong face shape?

Yes, you can have bangs with an oblong face shape. Side-swept bangs or blunt bangs can help balance out the length of the face.

2. Should I avoid long hair with an oblong face shape?

No, you can still have long hair with an oblong face shape. Just make sure to add layers or volume to create an illusion of width to the face.

3. How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for an oblong face shape can help balance out the length of the face and create an illusion of width. Long layers, bob, side-swept bangs, and pixie cuts are some hairstyles that suit oblong face shape. With the right hair care routine and styling techniques, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
