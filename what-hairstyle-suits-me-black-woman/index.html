What Hairstyle Suits Me As A Black Woman

Written by Luffy Sep 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you a black woman struggling to find the perfect hairstyle? It can be challenging to know which hairstyles will flatter your face shape and bring out your natural beauty. In this article, we will be discussing how to choose the best hairstyle for you as a black woman.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As a black woman, there are many different hairstyles to choose from, but not all of them will suit your face shape and hair type. It's essential to find a hairstyle that complements your natural features and enhances your beauty. There are various factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle, such as your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

There are several steps you can take to determine which hairstyles will suit you best:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. There are four main face shapes: oval, round, square, and heart-shaped. Each face shape has different features, and certain hairstyles will flatter them better than others.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Black women have various hair textures, ranging from curly to straight. It's essential to consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle, as some styles may not work well with your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy, voluminous styles that can weigh your hair down.

3. Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Do you prefer a more natural look, or do you like to experiment with bold, edgy styles? Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and make you feel confident and comfortable.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Follow these steps to find the perfect hairstyle for you:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Your face shape will be determined by the widest part of your face:

  • Oval: If your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded jawline, you have an oval face shape.
  • Round: If your face is as wide as it is long, with a rounded jawline, you have a round face shape.
  • Square: If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, with a square jawline, you have a square face shape.
  • Heart-shaped: If your forehead is wider than your jawline, with a pointed chin, you have a heart-shaped face.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Take a look at your hair texture and consider what styles will work best with it:

  • Straight hair: Straight hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Consider a blunt bob, a sleek ponytail, or a layered cut.
  • Curly hair: Embrace your natural curls with a voluminous afro or a curly bob. You can also try braids or twists for a low-maintenance, protective style.
  • Coily hair: Coily hair is similar to curly hair but has a tighter, more defined curl pattern. Consider a short, tapered cut or a twist-out style.

3. Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is an essential factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Natural: If you prefer a more natural look, try a wash-and-go style or a twist-out.
  • Edgy: For a bold, edgy look, consider a shaved undercut or a colorful dye job.
  • Classic: If you prefer a classic, timeless look, a sleek bob or a low bun may be the perfect choice.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle: If you're always on the go, you may want to choose a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of upkeep.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try out different styles and see what works best for you.
  • Consult with a stylist: A professional stylist can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

If you have a round face, you may want to consider a long, layered cut or a side-swept bang. These styles can help elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

What is the best hairstyle for a heart-shaped face?

If you have a heart-shaped face, you may want to consider a chin-length bob or a side-parted pixie cut. These styles can help balance out your features and create a more symmetrical look.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips, you can find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
