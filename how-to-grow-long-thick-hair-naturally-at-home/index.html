How To Grow Long Thick Hair Naturally At Home

Written by Petter Mar 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long, thick, and luscious hair is a dream for many people. However, not everyone is blessed with such voluminous hair. If you're looking to grow your hair naturally at home, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to grow long thick hair naturally at home.

  • How to Grow Long Thick Hair Naturally at Home

How to Grow Long Thick Hair Naturally at Home

There are several natural ways to grow long thick hair at home. These methods are safe, budget-friendly, and effective. We've listed some of the most popular ones below:

1. Balanced Diet

Your hair is made up of proteins, so a balanced diet is crucial for hair growth. Include foods that are rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet. Some examples are eggs, fish, nuts, leafy greens, and fruits.

2. Scalp Massage

Massaging your scalp stimulates blood flow, which can promote hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes every day.

3. Essential Oils

Essential oils like lavender, rosemary, and peppermint can promote hair growth. Mix a few drops of your favorite essential oil with a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil and massage it onto your scalp.

4. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera contains enzymes that can promote hair growth. Apply fresh aloe vera gel onto your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it off with water.

5. Onion Juice

Onion juice is rich in sulfur, which can promote hair growth. Blend an onion and extract its juice. Apply the juice onto your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it off with water.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to grow long thick hair naturally at home:

Step 1: Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep your scalp clean and free of dirt and oil.

Step 2: Massage your scalp

Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes every day to stimulate blood flow.

Step 3: Apply natural hair masks

Apply natural hair masks like aloe vera gel, onion juice, or essential oil blends onto your scalp and hair to promote hair growth.

Step 4: Eat a balanced diet

Eat a diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Tips for Growing Long Thick Hair Naturally at Home

Follow these tips to grow long thick hair naturally at home:

1. Avoid heat styling

Avoid heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners as they can damage your hair and hinder hair growth.

2. Use a silk pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and minimize frizz.

3. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Q. How long does it take to grow long thick hair naturally?

It can take anywhere from several months to a year or more to grow long thick hair naturally. It depends on your hair type, genetics, and overall health.

Q. Can I use hair growth supplements to grow my hair naturally?

While hair growth supplements can promote hair growth, it's always best to get the nutrients you need from a balanced diet.

Hair Care Routine for Long Thick Hair

A good hair care routine is essential for maintaining long thick hair. Follow these tips:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that's free of sulfates and parabens to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

2. Condition your hair

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft, shiny, and moisturized.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently and avoid breakage.

4. Protect your hair

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can grow long thick hair naturally at home. Remember to be patient, consistent, and gentle with your hair. Happy growing!
