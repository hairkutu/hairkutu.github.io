How To Sharpen Thinning Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Mar 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Thinning scissors are an essential tool for any hair stylist or barber. They are perfect for creating texture and thinning out thick hair. However, over time, the blades of your thinning scissors will become dull, making it difficult to achieve the desired results. In this blog post, we will show you how to sharpen thinning scissors so that they can be used again and again without losing their effectiveness.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Sharpen Thinning Scissors
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Sharpening Thinning Scissors
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Thinning scissors are designed to remove bulk from hair without changing the overall length. They consist of two blades that are separated by a series of teeth. These teeth are what create the texture in the hair. Over time, the blades of your thinning scissors will become dull, making it difficult to achieve the desired results. Sharpening your scissors will restore their effectiveness and prolong their lifespan.

How to Sharpen Thinning Scissors

Sharpening thinning scissors is a simple process that can be done at home. All you need is a sharpening stone, some oil, and a cloth. Follow these steps to sharpen your thinning scissors:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by cleaning your scissors. Use a cloth to wipe away any hair or debris.
  2. Apply some oil to the blades. This will help the sharpening stone glide smoothly over the surface.
  3. Hold the sharpening stone at a 45-degree angle and place it against the blade of the scissors.
  4. Using light pressure, move the stone along the blade in a circular motion. Make sure to maintain the same angle throughout.
  5. Repeat this process on the other blade.
  6. Once you have finished sharpening, wipe away any excess oil with a cloth.
  7. Test the scissors by cutting a piece of paper. If they are sharp, they should cut through the paper with ease.

Tips for Sharpening Thinning Scissors

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when sharpening your thinning scissors:

  • Use a sharpening stone that is specifically designed for scissors.
  • Make sure to maintain the same angle throughout the sharpening process.
  • Use light pressure to avoid damaging the blades.
  • Sharpen your scissors regularly to maintain their effectiveness.

FAQs

Q: How often should I sharpen my thinning scissors?

A: It is recommended to sharpen your thinning scissors after every 70 haircuts.

Q: Can I sharpen my thinning scissors with a regular sharpening stone?

A: No, it is best to use a sharpening stone that is specifically designed for scissors.

Q: Can I use a honing steel to sharpen my thinning scissors?

A: No, a honing steel is not suitable for sharpening thinning scissors.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to sharpening your thinning scissors, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great and your thinning scissors in top condition. Happy cutting!
