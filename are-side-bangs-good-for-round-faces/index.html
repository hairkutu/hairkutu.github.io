Are Side Bangs Good For Round Faces

Written by Petter Aug 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Side bangs have been a popular hairstyle for years, and they can instantly transform your look. However, if you have a round face, you might be wondering if side bangs are a good choice for you. The answer is yes, side bangs can look great on round faces if styled correctly. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve the perfect side bangs for round faces, and some tips to maintain your new hairstyle.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. Side bangs can help to balance out the width of the face and create an illusion of length. They can also draw attention to your best features and add a touch of glamour to your look.

How to Achieve the Perfect Side Bangs for Round Faces

There are a few things to consider when getting side bangs for a round face:

1. Length

The length of your side bangs is critical when it comes to flattering your face shape. A shorter bang that ends at or above the cheekbone will help to emphasize your cheekbones and draw attention away from the roundness of your face. If you prefer longer bangs, make sure they are layered and thinned out to avoid adding bulk to your face.

2. Angle

The angle of your side bangs can also make a difference in how they flatter your face shape. A diagonal angle that starts at the top of your head and ends at your cheekbone will create the illusion of length and help to slim down your face. Avoid cutting your bangs straight across, as this can make your face look wider.

3. Texture

The texture of your side bangs can also play a role in how they flatter your face shape. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, consider getting side-swept bangs that are more relaxed and tousled. Straight hair can benefit from blunt, choppy bangs that add texture and dimension to your look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect side bangs for round faces:

Step 1: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Before getting your bangs, it is essential to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length, angle, and texture for your face shape. They can also recommend a style that will work with your hair type and lifestyle.

Step 2: Wash and Blow-Dry Your Hair

Wash and blow-dry your hair as you usually would to prepare for your haircut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before cutting your bangs.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by pulling the rest of your hair back with a hair tie or clip, leaving only the front section of your hair that will become your bangs.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Cut your bangs at a diagonal angle, starting from the top of your head and ending at your cheekbone. Keep the length above or at your cheekbone to avoid adding bulk to your face. Cut your bangs in small sections to ensure accuracy and avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Style your bangs by blow-drying them with a round brush or using a flat iron to add texture and volume. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your bangs and make them look greasy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Side Bangs

Here are some tips to help you maintain your side bangs:

1. Trim Your Bangs Regularly

Regular trims will help to keep your bangs looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long and dragging down your face.

2. Use a Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo can help to absorb excess oil and keep your bangs looking clean and fresh between washes.

3. Avoid Touching Your Bangs

Avoid touching your bangs too often, as this can cause them to become greasy and lose their shape.

FAQs

1. Can I still wear side bangs if I have a round face?

Yes, side bangs can look great on round faces if styled correctly. Make sure to consider the length, angle, and texture of your bangs to flatter your face shape.

2. How do I know if side bangs will look good on me?

The best way to know if side bangs will look good on you is to consult with your hairstylist. They can recommend a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair type and lifestyle.

3. How do I style my side bangs?

You can style your side bangs by blow-drying them with a round brush or using a flat iron to add texture and volume. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your bangs and make them look greasy.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and beautiful, it is essential to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner to keep it moisturized, and avoiding heat styling tools too often. You should also protect your hair from harsh environmental factors, such as sun exposure and pollution, by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

In conclusion, side bangs can look great on round faces if styled correctly. Consider the length, angle, and texture of your bangs to flatter your face shape, and follow a proper hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
