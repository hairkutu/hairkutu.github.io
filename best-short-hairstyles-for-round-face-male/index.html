Best Short Hairstyles For Round Face Male

Written by Christine Jun 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy with a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to find a style that will complement your face shape and bring out your best features. With so many options out there, it can be difficult to know where to start. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best short hairstyles for round face male and give you some tips on how to choose the right one for you.

Description

Round face shapes are characterized by soft, curvy lines and a lack of sharp angles. The goal with any hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. Short hairstyles are a great option for guys with round faces because they can help to elongate the face and create a more angular appearance.

How to

The first step in choosing the right short hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are about the same width, you have a round face. Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Look for hairstyles that will elongate your face and create angles.
  3. Consider a short, textured cut with layers to add volume and depth to your hair.
  4. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear even rounder.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion on what will work best for your face shape and hair type.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or flat, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different lengths and textures to find what works best for you.
  • Consider adding some volume to the top of your hair to create the illusion of height.
  • Use a matte hair product to add texture and definition to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round face male:

What are the best short hairstyles for round face male?

Some of the best short hairstyles for round face male include textured crops, messy quiffs, and side-swept fringes.

What hairstyles should guys with round faces avoid?

Guys with round faces should avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as well as styles that are slicked back or flat.

How can I add volume to my hair?

To add volume to your hair, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair upside down, or using a matte hair product with a strong hold.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and some inspiration from the best short hairstyles for round face male, you're sure to find a style that works for you. Remember to consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure about what will look best with your face shape and hair type.
