Hairstyles For Long Curly Hair Over 50 Tips How To And Hair Care

If you have long curly hair over 50, you know that keeping it looking good can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you look and feel your best. In this article, we'll discuss a variety of hairstyles for long curly hair over 50, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.

How to Style Long Curly Hair Over 50

Styling long curly hair can seem daunting, but with a few simple steps, you can create a beautiful look that's both chic and age-appropriate. Here are some tips:

1. Start with clean, conditioned hair.

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and conditioned. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair to keep your locks healthy and moisturized.

2. Apply a styling product.

To tame frizz and define your curls, apply a styling product such as mousse, gel, or curl cream. Start with a small amount and work it through your hair from roots to ends.

3. Use a diffuser.

To dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help keep your curls intact and prevent them from getting frizzy.

4. Finish with hairspray.

To hold your style in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help your curls stay defined and prevent flyaways.

Hairstyles for Long Curly Hair Over 50

Now that you know how to style your hair, let's take a look at some hairstyles that are perfect for long curly hair over 50:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to add movement and dimension to your curls. Ask your stylist for layers that start at your chin and continue down to your ends. This will help create a natural-looking cascade of curls.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help frame your face and give your hair a more polished look. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs so that they start at your cheekbones and sweep to the side. This will help soften your features and create a more youthful appearance.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down style is perfect for long curly hair over 50. To create this look, simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down to showcase your curls.

4. Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your hair out of your face and add some texture to your curls. Try a simple side braid or a more intricate fishtail braid to switch up your look.

5. Updos

For a more formal occasion, an updo can be a great option. Try a messy bun or a braided updo to keep your curls under control while still looking elegant.

Tips for Maintaining Long Curly Hair Over 50

Now that you know some great hairstyles for long curly hair over 50, let's look at some tips for maintaining your hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb.

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This will help prevent breakage and keep your curls intact.

2. Avoid heat styling.

Limit your use of heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons. Too much heat can damage your hair and make your curls look limp and lifeless.

3. Deep condition regularly.

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your curls healthy and moisturized. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQs About Long Curly Hair Over 50

1. Can I dye my hair if it's curly and over 50?

Absolutely! There's no reason you can't dye your hair if it's curly and over 50. Just make sure to use a hair dye that's formulated for curly hair and follow the instructions carefully.

2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

How often you should wash your curly hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. If you're more active or have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

3. What can I do if my curls are frizzy?

To tame frizzy curls, try using a leave-in conditioner or a frizz-fighting serum. You can also try diffusing your hair on a lower heat setting to help prevent frizz.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair Over 50

Finally, let's look at some general hair care tips for long curly hair over 50:

1. Protect your hair from the sun.

UV rays can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

2. Trim your hair regularly.

Regular trims can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Use a silk pillowcase.

Silk pillowcases can help prevent breakage and keep your curls looking their best. Plus, they're gentle on your skin!

With these tips and hairstyles, you can keep your long curly hair over 50 looking beautiful and healthy. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you!
