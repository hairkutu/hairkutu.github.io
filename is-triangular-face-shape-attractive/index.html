Is Triangular Face Shape Attractive

Written by Luffy Apr 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we all know, every face is unique in its own way. Some faces have a round shape, while others have a square or oval shape. But what about triangular face shape? Is it attractive? In this article, we will discuss everything related to triangular face shape and whether it is attractive or not.

Description

The triangular face shape is also known as the heart-shaped face. This face shape has a wide forehead and cheekbones with a narrow chin. It is considered one of the rarest face shapes, and only a few people have this face shape.

How to Determine If You Have a Triangular Face Shape?

If you want to determine whether you have a triangular face shape, follow the below steps:

  1. Stand in front of the mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. If your forehead and cheekbones are wider than your jawline, you have a triangular face shape.

Tips for Triangular Face Shape

If you have a triangular face shape, follow the below tips to enhance your features:

  • Wear hairstyles that add volume to the chin area.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown area.
  • Wear earrings that are wider at the bottom.
  • Avoid wearing earrings that are wider at the top.

FAQs

Q. Is a triangular face shape attractive?

A. Yes, a triangular face shape is attractive.

Q. Can a triangular face shape be changed?

A. No, a triangular face shape cannot be changed, but you can enhance your features by following the tips mentioned above.

Q. What are the famous celebrities with a triangular face shape?

A. Some famous celebrities with a triangular face shape are Reese Witherspoon, Scarlett Johansson, and Jennifer Lawrence.

Hair Care for Triangular Face Shape

If you have a triangular face shape, follow the below hair care tips:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long.
  • Wear hairstyles that add volume to the chin area.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown area.
  • Use hair products that add volume to the chin area.

Conclusion

So, is a triangular face shape attractive? Yes, it is! Every face shape is unique and beautiful in its own way. So, if you have a triangular face shape, embrace it and enhance your features by following the tips mentioned above.
