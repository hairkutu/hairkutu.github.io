How To Do Boy Hair Style At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of taking your son to the salon for a haircut every month? Do you want to learn how to do boy hair style at home? It's easier than you think! In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to cut and style your son's hair at home. We will also provide you with some tips and frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect boy hair style.

  • How to Do Boy Hair Style at Home

How to Do Boy Hair Style at Home

Before we begin, you need to make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clippers with different guard sizes. You can find these tools at your local beauty supply store or online.

The next step is to choose a hairstyle for your son. You can browse online for inspiration or ask your son what he wants. Once you have decided on a hairstyle, it's time to start cutting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wet your son's hair with a spray bottle or by washing it.

Step 2: Use hair clippers with the appropriate guard size to trim the sides and back of your son's head. Start from the bottom and work your way up. Use a comb to guide the clippers and make sure you cut in the same direction as the hair growth.

Step 3: Use hair cutting scissors to trim the top of your son's hair. Start from the front and work your way to the back. Use your fingers to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 4: Blend the sides and top of your son's hair using hair clippers without a guard. Use a comb to guide the clippers and make sure you blend the hair evenly.

Step 5: Use hair cutting scissors to trim the bangs. Start from the middle and work your way to the sides. Cut the bangs to the desired length and style them as you like.

Tips for Perfect Boy Hair Style

  • Make sure your son is sitting still and comfortable during the haircut.
  • Use a towel or cape to protect your son's clothes from hair clippings.
  • Start with a longer guard size and gradually work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use thinning shears to remove bulk and create texture in the hair.
  • Don't forget to clean and oil your hair clippers after each use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I cut my son's hair?

A: It depends on the hairstyle and how fast your son's hair grows. On average, you should cut your son's hair every 4-6 weeks.

Q: What if I make a mistake while cutting my son's hair?

A: Don't panic! Hair grows back. You can always take your son to the salon to fix any mistakes or wait for the hair to grow out and try again.

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my son's hair?

A: No, you should use hair cutting scissors. Regular scissors can damage the hair and create split ends.

Hair Care for Boys

After you have cut and styled your son's hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your son's hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, too often.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use hair products, such as gel or pomade, sparingly to avoid weighing down the hair.

Now that you know how to do boy hair style at home, you can save time and money by cutting and styling your son's hair yourself. With practice, you will become a pro at boy hair styling. Just remember to have fun and be patient!
