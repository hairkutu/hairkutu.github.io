How To Cut A Pixie Haircut Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 06, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a bold and trendy haircut, a pixie cut might be just what you need. But going to a salon can be expensive, and you might not always have the time to schedule an appointment. That's why we're here to teach you how to cut a pixie haircut yourself! With a few tools and some patience, you can achieve the perfect look in the comfort of your own home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut a Pixie Haircut Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Pixie Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Pixie Cut

How to Cut a Pixie Haircut Yourself

Before we dive into the details, let's go over some basic guidelines for cutting a pixie haircut:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors or clippers.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, let's get started with the step-by-step guide:

  1. Section your hair: Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: the front, the sides, and the back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.
  2. Start with the back: Start cutting the back section first. Use your scissors to create a guide by cutting a small section of hair at the desired length. Gradually work your way up, cutting small sections of hair at a time.
  3. Move to the sides: Once you're done with the back, move on to the sides. Use the same technique as before, cutting small sections of hair at a time and using the back as a guide.
  4. Work on the front: Now it's time to work on the front section. This is where you'll need to be extra careful, as it's the most visible part of your haircut. Use the sides as a guide and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  5. Trim the bangs: If you want bangs, now is the time to trim them. Use your scissors to create a guide and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  6. Refine your haircut: Once you've cut all the sections, take a step back and look at your hair. Use your scissors to refine any areas that need it.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Pixie Haircut

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect pixie haircut:

  • Use a mirror: Make sure to use a mirror to check the back of your head as you're cutting.
  • Don't rush: Take your time and be patient. It's better to take longer and get it right than rush and regret it later.
  • Ask for help: If you're not confident in your abilities, ask a friend or family member to help you.
  • Practice makes perfect: Don't expect to get it perfect the first time. Keep practicing until you're happy with the results.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a pixie haircut:

Can I cut my hair wet or does it need to be dry?

We recommend cutting your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can look longer than it actually is.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair?

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors or clippers, hair clips, and a comb.

Can I cut my hair with kitchen scissors?

We don't recommend using kitchen scissors to cut your hair. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors specifically designed for cutting hair.

Hair Care for Your Pixie Cut

Now that you've cut your own pixie haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Visit a salon every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to cut a pixie haircut yourself in no time. Remember to take your time, be patient, and practice until you're happy with the results. Good luck!
