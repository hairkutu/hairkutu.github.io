How To Contour For A Heart Shaped Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you might be wondering how to contour your features to bring out your best features. Contouring is a makeup technique that uses shading and highlighting to create definition and depth on the face. In this article, we'll walk you through how to contour for a heart shaped face, so you can enhance your natural beauty.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Contour for a Heart Shaped Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Contouring a Heart Shaped Face
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal of contouring for a heart shaped face is to balance out the proportions of the face by creating the illusion of a more defined jawline and a narrower forehead. This can be achieved through strategic shading and highlighting, using makeup products that are a few shades darker and lighter than your natural skin tone.

How to Contour for a Heart Shaped Face

The first step in contouring for a heart shaped face is to choose the right products. You'll need a contouring powder or cream that is a few shades darker than your skin tone, as well as a highlighting powder or cream that is a few shades lighter. You'll also need a blending brush and a powder brush.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to contour your heart shaped face:

  1. Apply your regular foundation all over your face, making sure to blend it well.
  2. Take your contouring product and apply it to the hollows of your cheeks, starting at your ears and blending downwards towards your mouth.
  3. Use the same contouring product to shade along the sides of your forehead, blending it towards your temples.
  4. Apply the highlighting product to the center of your forehead, down the bridge of your nose, and on the high points of your cheekbones.
  5. Blend the highlighting product well, making sure there are no harsh lines.
  6. Use the contouring product to shade along your jawline, starting at your ears and blending downwards towards your chin.
  7. Blend the contouring product well, making sure there are no harsh lines.
  8. Apply a light dusting of powder to set your makeup.

Tips for Contouring a Heart Shaped Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when contouring a heart shaped face:

  • Use a light hand when applying your contouring and highlighting products, as a little goes a long way.
  • Blend well to avoid harsh lines and an unnatural look.
  • Choose products that are the right shade for your skin tone, to avoid looking too dark or too light.
  • Consider using a cream contouring product for a more natural look.
  • Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your face shape.

FAQ

What if I don't have a contouring product?

If you don't have a contouring product, you can use a matte bronzer that is a few shades darker than your skin tone. Just make sure to blend it well and use a light hand.

Can I contour my nose as well?

Yes, you can contour your nose using the same shading and highlighting techniques. Use a small brush to apply the contouring product down the sides of your nose, and a highlighting product down the center.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

If you have a heart shaped face, you might also want to consider hairstyles that flatter your features. Here are some tips:

  • A side part can help balance out a wider forehead.
  • Long layers can help soften a sharp chin.
  • A bob or lob can also be flattering, as long as it hits at the right spot on your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, as this can make your forehead look even wider.

With these tips and techniques, you can contour for a heart shaped face and enhance your natural beauty. Remember to choose the right products, blend well, and experiment to find what works best for you.
