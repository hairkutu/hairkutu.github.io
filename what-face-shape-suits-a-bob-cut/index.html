What Face Shape Suits A Bob Cut

Written by Petter Aug 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Bob cut is a classic and versatile hairstyle that has been in fashion for decades. It's a chic and low-maintenance hairstyle that can give you a fresh and youthful look. However, not everyone can pull off a bob cut. It's important to consider your face shape before getting a bob cut. In this article, we will discuss what face shape suits a bob cut and some tips on how to style and maintain it.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A bob cut is a short hairstyle that typically falls between the chin and shoulders. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. The length and style of your bob cut will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences.

How to

Before getting a bob cut, it's important to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. Each face shape has its own unique features and requires a different bob cut style.

Round Face

If you have a round face shape, a bob cut with layers and volume can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer jawline. Avoid blunt or chin-length bobs, as they can make your face look rounder.

Oval Face

For oval face shapes, almost any bob cut style will work. However, a chin-length or asymmetrical bob can accentuate your cheekbones and jawline.

Square Face

If you have a square face shape, a bob cut with soft layers and texture can soften your angular features. Avoid blunt or straight bobs, as they can make your face look boxy.

Heart Face

For heart-shaped faces, a chin-length bob with side-swept bangs can balance out your forehead and chin. Avoid bobs that end at your jawline, as they can make your chin look pointy.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond face shape, a bob cut with blunt ends and volume at the crown can balance out your cheekbones and narrow forehead. Avoid bobs with too much volume at the sides, as they can make your face look wider.

Long Face

For long face shapes, a chin-length or shoulder-length bob with layers can add volume and width to your face. Avoid bobs that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look even longer.

Step by Step

Here are some steps on how to style your bob cut:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, starting from the roots to the ends.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and volume to your hair. You can also use hair accessories like headbands or clips to style your hair.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips on how to maintain your bob cut:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your bob cut looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Can I still get a bob cut if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still get a bob cut if you have curly hair. However, you may need to choose a bob cut style that suits your hair texture and curl pattern. Consult with your hairstylist on which bob cut style will work best for your curly hair.

Do I need to use a specific shampoo and conditioner for my bob cut?

It's recommended to use shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Look for products that are formulated for short hair or bob cuts. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and vibrant bob cut. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your bob cut.

In conclusion, a bob cut can be a flattering and stylish hairstyle for many face shapes. It's important to choose a bob cut style that suits your face shape and hair type. With the right styling and hair care, you can rock a chic and low-maintenance bob cut.
