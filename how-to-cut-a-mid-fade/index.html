How To Cut A Mid Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Mar 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, a mid fade might be just what you need. This haircut is perfect for men of all ages and can be customized to suit your individual style. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a mid fade step-by-step, including tips and tricks for achieving the perfect look.

How to Cut a Mid Fade

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp during the cutting process.

Step 1: Start with a Longer Length

To start cutting your mid fade, begin with a longer length on top. This will allow you to create the gradual fade effect that makes this haircut so popular. Use your scissors to trim the top of your hair to your desired length, making sure to leave enough hair for the fade.

Step 2: Create the Fade

Next, it's time to create the fade. Use your clippers to start at the bottom of your hairline and work your way up, gradually cutting the hair shorter as you go. Make sure to blend the transition between the shorter and longer hair to create a smooth and natural-looking fade.

Step 3: Blend the Sides

Once you've created the fade, it's time to blend the sides. Use your clippers to trim the sides of your hair to the desired length, making sure to blend the transition between the shorter and longer hair. You can also use your scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a more polished look.

Step 4: Clean Up the Neckline

Finally, it's time to clean up the neckline. Use your clippers to trim any stray hairs on your neck, creating a clean and polished look. You can also use a razor to shave any remaining stubble for an even smoother finish.

Tips for Cutting a Mid Fade

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mid fade:

  • Start with a longer length on top to create a gradual fade effect
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut
  • Keep your hair damp with a spray bottle to make it easier to cut
  • Take your time and be patient to ensure a smooth and even fade
  • Use scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a polished look

FAQ

How long does a mid fade last?

A mid fade can last anywhere from three to six weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. It's important to maintain your haircut by trimming the sides and neckline as needed to keep it looking fresh.

Can I cut a mid fade at home?

Yes, you can cut a mid fade at home with the right tools and some practice. It's important to take your time and be patient to ensure a smooth and even fade.

How do I style my mid fade?

There are many ways to style a mid fade, depending on your individual preferences. You can use hair products like pomade or gel to create a slicked-back look, or simply leave your hair natural for a more casual style.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your mid fade looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy and vibrant hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and health

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut a mid fade like a pro and keep your hair looking its best. Whether you're going for a casual or polished look, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads.
