How To Cut Wispy Layers In Short Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair, you might be looking for ways to add some volume and texture to your style. One great way to achieve this is by cutting wispy layers into your hair. In this guide, we will show you how to cut wispy layers in short hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Wispy Layers in Short Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Wispy Layers in Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care after Cutting Wispy Layers in Short Hair

Description

Wispy layers are a great way to add some texture and movement to short hair. They are cut in a way that creates soft, feather-like ends that blend seamlessly into the rest of the hair. The layers are typically cut using scissors, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style.

How to Cut Wispy Layers in Short Hair

To cut wispy layers in short hair, you will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some clips to section your hair. If you are not confident in cutting your own hair, it is always best to seek the help of a professional stylist.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut wispy layers in short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into parts. Create a horizontal parting at the nape of your neck, and clip the top section of hair up and out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the bottom layer, and comb it straight down.
  4. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and point cut the ends using the scissors.
  5. Continue taking small sections of hair and point cutting the ends until you have completed the bottom layer.
  6. Release the top section of hair, and create a parting that aligns with the previous section.
  7. Repeat steps 3-5 on this section of hair, again point cutting the ends to create wispy layers.
  8. Continue working your way up the head in horizontal sections until you have created wispy layers throughout the entire head.
  9. Blend the layers together by combing through the hair, and making any necessary adjustments.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Wispy Layers in Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting wispy layers in short hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair, and work your way up gradually.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Point cut the ends of the hair to create a soft, feathered effect.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven layers.
  • Make sure to blend the layers together to avoid any harsh lines.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut wispy layers in my own hair?

A: While it is possible to cut wispy layers in your own hair, it is always best to seek the help of a professional stylist if you are not confident in your abilities.

Q: Will cutting wispy layers in short hair make it look thinner?

A: No, cutting wispy layers in short hair can actually create the illusion of more volume and thickness.

Hair Care after Cutting Wispy Layers in Short Hair

After cutting wispy layers in short hair, it is important to take good care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape and length of the layers.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these tips, you can keep your wispy layers looking great for weeks to come!
