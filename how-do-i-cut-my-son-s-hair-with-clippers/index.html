How Do I Cut My Son s Hair With Clippers

Written by Robby May 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As a parent, you want your child to look their best. One way to achieve this is by cutting their hair at home. Using clippers to cut your son's hair can be a great way to save money and time, while also ensuring that they have a fresh haircut. In this article, we will show you how to cut your son's hair with clippers in a step-by-step guide.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Your Son's Hair with Clippers
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Your Son's Hair with Clippers
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Cut Your Son's Hair with Clippers

Before you start cutting your son's hair with clippers, you need to make sure you have the right tools. Here is what you will need:

  • Clippers with different guards
  • Scissors
  • Cape or towel
  • Comb
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Clip or hair tie

Once you have all the necessary tools, you can begin cutting your son's hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Son's Hair

Before you start cutting your son's hair, you need to prepare it. Start by washing and drying his hair. Then, use a comb to remove any tangles or knots. If your son has long hair, tie it up with a clip or hair tie.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

Clippers come with different guard sizes, which determine the length of the hair. Choose the right guard size depending on the length of hair you want your son to have. For example, if you want a shorter cut, use a smaller guard size.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Start cutting your son's hair from the sides. Use a longer guard size for the top and a shorter guard size for the sides. Use the clippers to cut the hair from the bottom to the top in an upward motion. Repeat this process until you have gone around the entire head.

Step 4: Move to the Top

Once you have finished the sides, move to the top of your son's head. Use a longer guard size to achieve the desired length. Cut the hair in small sections, moving from the front to the back of the head. Use the clippers to cut the hair in an upward motion.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have finished cutting the top and sides of your son's hair, it's time to blend the hair. Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and the top. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it with scissors to create a smooth transition between the different lengths.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the neckline. Use a shorter guard size to create a clean line around the neck. Make sure the line is straight and even.

Step 7: Clean Up the Ears

Use the clippers to clean up the hair around the ears. Use a shorter guard size to create a clean line around the ears. Make sure the line is straight and even.

Tips for Cutting Your Son's Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut for your son:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to a shorter guard size.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair for a more natural look.
  • Take breaks if your son is getting restless or uncomfortable.
  • Use a cape or towel to prevent hair from getting on your son's clothes.
  • Use a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair and make it easier to cut.

FAQs

How often should I cut my son's hair?

It depends on how fast your son's hair grows. On average, it's recommended to cut hair every 4-8 weeks.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Use scissors to cut the hair shorter or blend it to make it look more natural.

Hair Care Tips

After cutting your son's hair with clippers, it's important to take care of his hair. Here are some tips to keep his hair looking healthy:

  • Wash his hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep his hair soft and manageable.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to remove tangles and knots.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools.
  • Trim his hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Cutting your son's hair with clippers can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut for your son in the comfort of your own home. Follow these steps and tips to give your son a fresh new look.
