How To Blend Sides With Top A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking to give your hair a fresh, modern look, blending the sides with the top is a great way to do it. However, it can be a bit tricky to get the right look. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to blend the sides with the top, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to keep your new look looking fresh and sharp.

How to Blend Sides with Top

Blending the sides with the top involves creating a seamless transition between the two sections of hair. This means that there should be no visible lines or harsh edges between the two sections. To achieve this look, you'll need to use scissors and/or clippers to gradually taper the hair from the sides to the top.

Tools You'll Need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair Gel or Wax

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to blend the sides with the top:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.
  2. Using your clippers, trim the hair on the sides of your head to your desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to create a gradual taper.
  3. Using your scissors, cut the hair on the top of your head to your desired length. Be sure to blend the hair on the top with the hair on the sides by cutting at a slight angle.
  4. Use your comb to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the top. Comb the hair up and outwards, using your scissors to trim any stray hairs.
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 on the other side of your head.
  6. Apply hair gel or wax to your hair to help hold the style in place.

And that's it! With a bit of practice, you'll be able to create a seamless blend between the sides and top of your hair.

Tips for Blending Sides with Top

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect blend:

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down to a shorter guard. This will help you create a gradual taper on the sides.
  • Use your scissors to blend the hair on the top with the hair on the sides by cutting at a slight angle.
  • Take your time and be patient. Blending the sides with the top can be a bit tricky, so don't rush it.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that your hair looks its best.
  • Experiment with different hair gels and waxes to find one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

FAQs

Q: Can I blend the sides with the top if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can! However, it can be a bit more challenging to get a seamless blend with curly hair, so you may want to consider visiting a professional stylist.

Q: How often do I need to blend the sides with the top?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how long you want to maintain the same style. Generally, you should aim to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

Q: Can I blend the sides with the top myself, or do I need to visit a professional?

A: While it is possible to blend the sides with the top yourself, it can be a bit tricky. If you're unsure, it's best to visit a professional stylist who can give you the look you want.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit a stylist regularly to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

By following these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to blend the sides with the top of your hair like a pro. Whether you're going for a modern, edgy look or a classic, timeless style, blending the sides with the top is a great way to give your hair a fresh, new look.
