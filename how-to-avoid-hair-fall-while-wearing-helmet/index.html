How To Avoid Hair Fall While Wearing Helmet A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Wearing a helmet is an essential safety measure while riding a bike, but it can lead to hair fall and damage. If you are a regular bike rider, you might have noticed hair fall and damage due to helmet usage. However, there are ways to avoid hair fall while wearing a helmet. In this guide, we will share some tips on how to prevent hair fall while wearing a helmet.

How to Avoid Hair Fall While Wearing Helmet

1. Choose the Right Helmet

Choosing the right helmet is crucial to avoid hair fall. A tight helmet can lead to hair fall and damage. On the other hand, a loose helmet can slip and lead to accidents. Before buying a helmet, make sure to measure your head size and choose a helmet that fits perfectly.

2. Wear a Hair Net

Wearing a hair net can protect your hair from damage and hair fall due to helmet usage. It can also keep your hair in place and prevent tangles. Make sure to wear a hair net before putting on your helmet.

3. Use a Silk Scarf

Using a silk scarf can protect your hair from friction and damage due to helmet usage. It can also prevent hair fall and keep your hair in place. Wrap a silk scarf around your head before putting on your helmet.

4. Tie Your Hair in a Bun

Tying your hair in a bun can prevent hair fall and damage due to helmet usage. Make sure to tie your hair in a loose bun to avoid tension on the hair roots.

5. Use a Hair Conditioner

Using a hair conditioner can prevent hair fall and damage due to helmet usage. It can also keep your hair soft and shiny. Apply a hair conditioner before washing your hair after helmet usage.

6. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can prevent hair fall and damage due to helmet usage. It can also keep your hair clean and healthy. Make sure to use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid hair damage.

7. Avoid Wet Hair

Avoid wearing a helmet on wet hair as it can lead to hair fall and damage. Make sure to dry your hair completely before putting on your helmet.

8. Take Breaks

Take breaks while riding to avoid prolonged helmet usage. Prolonged helmet usage can lead to hair fall and damage. Take breaks and remove your helmet to give your hair some rest.

9. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can improve blood circulation and prevent hair fall. It can also keep your hair healthy and strong. Massage your scalp before and after washing your hair.

10. Consult a Hair Specialist

If you are experiencing severe hair fall due to helmet usage, consult a hair specialist. They can suggest the best hair care routine and treatment to prevent hair fall and damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to prevent hair fall and damage:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water for hair wash
  • Avoid using hair styling products
  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Get enough sleep

FAQ

Q. Can wearing a helmet lead to hair fall?

Yes, wearing a helmet can lead to hair fall and damage due to friction and tension on the hair roots.

Q. How can I prevent hair fall while wearing a helmet?

You can prevent hair fall while wearing a helmet by choosing the right helmet, wearing a hair net, using a silk scarf, tie your hair in a bun, using a hair conditioner, washing your hair regularly, avoiding wet hair, taking breaks, and massaging your scalp.

Conclusion

Wearing a helmet is crucial for your safety while riding a bike, but it can lead to hair fall and damage. By following the tips mentioned in this guide, you can prevent hair fall while wearing a helmet. Remember to take care of your hair by following a proper hair care routine and consulting a hair specialist if needed.
