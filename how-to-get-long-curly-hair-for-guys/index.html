How To Get Long Curly Hair For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 17, 2023 · 8 min read

Long curly hair is a popular hairstyle for guys that provides a unique look and adds character to your overall appearance. However, growing out your hair and achieving those luscious curls can be quite challenging, especially if you don't know where to start. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps on how to get long curly hair for guys, including tips and hair care to maintain your curly locks.

How to Get Long Curly Hair for Guys

Before we jump into the guide, it's essential to note that genetics play a significant role in determining your hair type and texture. Some guys are blessed with natural curls, while others have to work hard to achieve them. With that being said, here are some ways to get long curly hair:

Grow Your Hair Out

The first step in getting long curly hair for guys is to grow your hair out. Your hair needs to be long enough to form curls. Typically, hair that's at least 6 inches long can start to curl. Depending on how fast your hair grows, it can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to grow out your hair to the desired length.

Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make all the difference in achieving long curly hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. You can also use styling products like mousse or gel to help define your curls and keep them in place.

Get a Perm

If you're looking for a more permanent solution, you can get a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that adds curls or waves to your hair. However, perms can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly, so make sure to go to a professional stylist who has experience doing perms.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get long curly hair for guys:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair Out

As mentioned earlier, your hair needs to be at least 6 inches long to start forming curls. Let your hair grow out naturally without cutting it too much.

Step 2: Use Curl-Enhancing Products

Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to help define your curls. Apply a curl-defining cream or mousse to damp hair, then scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation.

Step 3: Air Dry Your Hair

Avoid blow-drying your hair as it can cause frizz and damage your curls. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. Use a curl-defining gel or spray to help hold your curls in place.

Tips for Long Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve and maintain long curly hair:

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your curls and cause frizz. Avoid using these tools as much as possible, and if you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your curls while sleeping. Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and lead to frizz and tangles.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks, or as needed.

FAQs

Can Guys Get Perms?

Yes, guys can get perms. However, make sure to go to a professional stylist who has experience doing perms and can give you the desired look without damaging your hair.

What's the Best Product for Curly Hair?

There's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as everyone's hair is different. However, look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, a curl-defining cream or mousse, and a curl-holding spray or gel.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for long curly hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.

Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep your curls hydrated and nourished. Look for a deep conditioning treatment that's specifically designed for curly hair.

Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your hair can cause frizz and disrupt your curls. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve long curly hair for guys. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you'll have beautiful curls in no time.
