Which Hair Color Will Suit Medium Skin Tone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have medium skin tone, you may have difficulty choosing the right hair color that complements your skin. No worries, we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through the process of selecting the perfect hair color that will suit your medium skin tone.

Description

Medium skin tone is a versatile skin tone that can carry almost any hair color. However, it is essential to choose a hair color that enhances your natural beauty and works well with your undertone. The right hair color can make or break your overall look, so it is best to choose a shade that matches your skin tone.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color?

Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can be overwhelming. Here are some factors you should consider when selecting a hair color:

Undertone

Undertone is an essential factor when choosing the right hair color for your skin tone. Your undertone can be warm, cool, or neutral. If you have warm undertones, go for hair colors with golden or caramel tones. If you have cool undertones, choose hair colors with ashy or cool tones. If you have a neutral undertone, you can go for both warm and cool hair colors.

Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also influence the hair color you choose. If you have naturally dark hair, go for shades that are a few tones lighter. If you have light hair, opt for darker shades.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also affect the hair color you choose. If you have an active lifestyle and spend a lot of time outdoors, choose a hair color that is low maintenance and can withstand sun exposure.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here is a step by step guide to help you choose the right hair color for your medium skin tone:

Step 1: Determine Your Undertone

Determine your undertone by looking at the veins in your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If you cannot tell, you may have a neutral undertone.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Consider your natural hair color and choose a shade that is a few tones lighter or darker.

Step 3: Look at Your Favorite Celebrities

Look at your favorite celebrities with similar skin tones and see what hair colors they have tried. This can give you an idea of what hair colors work well with medium skin tones.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you are still unsure about which hair color to choose, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and fits your lifestyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

After choosing the perfect hair color, it is essential to maintain it to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair color looking vibrant.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible as they can damage your hair and fade your hair color.

Protect Your Hair from Sun Exposure

Protect your hair from sun exposure by wearing a hat or using hair products with SPF.

FAQs

What Hair Colors Should I Avoid if I Have Medium Skin Tone?

Avoid hair colors with orange or red tones as they can clash with your skin tone.

Can I Dye My Hair at Home?

While it is possible to dye your hair at home, it is best to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you achieve the desired hair color without damaging your hair.

How Often Should I Touch Up My Hair Color?

The frequency of touch-ups depends on the hair color and how fast your hair grows. Typically, touch-ups are required every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Medium Skin Tone

Now that you have chosen the perfect hair color for your medium skin tone, it is essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

Moisturize Your Hair

Moisturize your hair regularly using hair masks or leave-in conditioners. This will keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Avoid Chemical Treatments

Avoid chemical treatments such as perms or relaxers as they can damage your hair and scalp.

Choosing the right hair color for medium skin tone can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance and tips, it can be a breeze. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
