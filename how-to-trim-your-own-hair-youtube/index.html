How To Trim Your Own Hair Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts every few months? Do you want to learn how to trim your own hair at home? Look no further than YouTube! In this post, we'll walk you through the process of trimming your own hair using YouTube tutorials. From gathering the right tools to perfecting your technique, we've got you covered.

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, you'll need to gather a few tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror (preferably one with magnification)
  • Towel or cape to catch hair clippings

Once you have these tools, you're ready to start trimming your own hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. This will make it easier to see where you need to trim.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section off your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair ties or clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Start Trimming

Find a YouTube tutorial that matches the style you're going for. Follow along with the tutorial, using your scissors to trim your hair. If you want to use clippers, make sure you have the right guard attached.

Step 4: Check Your Progress

After you've finished trimming one section, use your mirror to check your progress. Make sure the length is even and adjust as needed. Once you're satisfied with one section, move on to the next.

Step 5: Blend Your Layers

If you have layers, use your scissors to blend them together. This will give your hair a more natural look.

Step 6: Trim Your Bangs

If you have bangs, trim them separately. Use your comb to separate them from the rest of your hair, and follow a YouTube tutorial to trim them to your desired length.

Step 7: Finish Up

Once you've finished trimming all sections of your hair, use your comb to ensure everything is even. Then, style your hair as you normally would.

Tips for Trimming Your Own Hair

  • Start with small snips, and remember that you can always take more off but you can't put it back on.
  • Use hair ties or clips to keep each section of hair separate.
  • Don't forget to blend your layers for a more natural look.
  • Trim your bangs separately.
  • Use a mirror with magnification so you can see what you're doing.

FAQ

Can I trim my own hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can! Just make sure you're using sharp scissors and follow a YouTube tutorial that matches your hair type.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! You can always go to a professional to fix it. Alternatively, you can try to fix it yourself by trimming the other side to match.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair between trims. Use a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat tools too often. Try to limit your trims to every 3-4 months to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to trim your own hair using YouTube tutorials, you can save money and have the satisfaction of doing it yourself. Happy trimming!
