How To Take Care Of Your Hair Straightener

Written by Petter Aug 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you want to keep your hair straightener in excellent condition, there are a few things you need to do regularly. In this guide, we will show you how to take care of your hair straightener properly. From cleaning it to storing it safely, we have got you covered. Read on for our tips and tricks!

  • How to Clean Your Hair Straightener
  • Step by Step Guide to Taking Care of Your Hair Straightener
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Straightener

  • Why Hair Care Should Be a Priority

How to Clean Your Hair Straightener

One of the essential things you need to do to take care of your hair straightener is to clean it regularly. Here is what you need to do:

  1. Turn off and unplug your hair straightener.
  2. Use a soft cloth or cotton ball to wipe down the plates gently.
  3. If there is any buildup or residue on the plates, use a damp cloth with a mild cleanser to clean them. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners as they can damage the plates.
  4. Use a dry cloth to wipe away any moisture and let the straightener air dry completely before using it again.

Remember to clean your hair straightener after every use to prevent buildup and ensure it works correctly.

Step by Step Guide to Taking Care of Your Hair Straightener

Here is a step-by-step guide to taking care of your hair straightener:

  1. Always read the manufacturer's instructions before using your hair straightener.
  2. Never use your straightener on wet hair as it can damage the plates and cause electrocution.
  3. Use a heat protectant spray before straightening your hair to prevent damage.
  4. Use the correct temperature setting for your hair type to avoid overheating and causing burns or damage.
  5. Store your hair straightener in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture.
  6. Never wrap the cord around the straightener as it can cause damage and shorten the lifespan of the cord.
  7. Always unplug your straightener after use and let it cool down before storing it away.
  8. Check the plates regularly for any signs of wear and tear. If they are damaged or cracked, replace your hair straightener immediately.
  9. Keep your hair straightener away from children and pets.
  10. Have your hair straightener serviced regularly by a professional to ensure it is working correctly.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Straightener

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your hair straightener:

  • Use a heat-resistant mat to rest your hair straightener on while it is heating up, in use, and cooling down.
  • Don't use your hair straightener as a makeshift tool for other tasks, such as ironing clothes, as it can damage the plates and cause safety hazards.
  • Don't use your hair straightener on synthetic extensions or wigs, as the heat can melt and damage them.
  • Consider investing in a quality hair straightener with ceramic or tourmaline plates, as they are less damaging to your hair than metal plates.
  • Don't share your hair straightener with others to prevent the spread of bacteria and skin infections.

FAQs About Hair Straightener Care

How often should I clean my hair straightener?

You should clean your hair straightener after every use to prevent buildup and ensure it works correctly.

Can I use any type of cleaner to clean my hair straightener?

No, you should avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners as they can damage the plates. Instead, use a damp cloth with a mild cleanser to clean the plates gently.

What should I do if my hair straightener is damaged or cracked?

If your hair straightener is damaged or cracked, you should replace it immediately to prevent safety hazards and further damage to your hair.

Why Hair Care Should Be a Priority

Maintaining your hair straightener is an essential part of hair care. By taking care of your hair straightener, you can prevent damage to your hair and ensure it looks healthy and sleek. Remember to follow the manufacturer's instructions and use the correct temperature settings for your hair type. With proper care, your hair straightener can last for years and give you fabulous results every time you use it.
