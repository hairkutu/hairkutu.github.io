Best Thickening Mousse For Fine Hair Uk Achieve Fuller And Bouncier Locks

Written by Daniel Aug 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having limp and lifeless hair? Do you want to achieve fuller and bouncier locks? Look no further than the best thickening mousse for fine hair UK. In this article, we’ll discuss the top picks for thickening mousses, how to use them, and some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Top Picks for Thickening Mousses
  • How to Use Thickening Mousse
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Fine Hair

Top Picks for Thickening Mousses

When it comes to finding the best thickening mousse for fine hair UK, there are several options on the market. Here are our top picks:

1. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Full Form Mousse

This mousse is a cult favorite among hairstylists and for good reason. It adds volume and thickness to fine hair without leaving a sticky or crunchy residue. Plus, it contains conditioning agents to keep your hair soft and smooth.

2. Oribe Grandiose Hair Plumping Mousse

If you’re willing to splurge, this mousse is worth it. It provides long-lasting volume and lift while also nourishing your hair with ingredients like hydrolyzed vegetable protein and vitamin E.

3. Living Proof Full Thickening Mousse

This mousse works by depositing tiny thickening dots on your hair strands, creating the illusion of fuller hair. It’s also formulated without harmful chemicals like parabens and sulfates.

How to Use Thickening Mousse

Using thickening mousse is easy and can be incorporated into your daily hair routine. Here’s how:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Shake the mousse can well and dispense a golf ball-sized amount into your palm.
  3. Distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to lift and add volume.
  5. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.

Remember, a little goes a long way with mousse. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Using thickening mousse is just one part of achieving fuller and healthier hair. Here are some additional tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling wands too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as this can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb.

FAQs

Q: Can thickening mousse be used on all hair types?

A: While thickening mousse is designed for fine hair, it can also be used on other hair types to add volume and texture.

Q: How often should I use thickening mousse?

A: It’s safe to use thickening mousse daily, but be mindful of how much product you’re using to avoid buildup.

Q: Can I use thickening mousse on dry hair?

A: While it’s best to use thickening mousse on damp hair, you can also apply a small amount to dry hair to revive flat or limp locks.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

Fine hair requires special care to prevent breakage and maintain volume. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heavy or oily styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair feeling light.
  • Try a volumizing dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids, as they can cause tension and breakage.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add dimension and movement to your hair.

With the help of the best thickening mousse for fine hair UK and these hair care tips, you can achieve fuller, bouncier locks that will turn heads. Remember, healthy hair starts with a healthy scalp, so be sure to give your hair the care it deserves.
