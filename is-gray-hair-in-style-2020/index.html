Is Gray Hair In Style 2020

Written by Frank Jul 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we enter the year 2023, many people are still wondering whether gray hair is in style. In this blog post, we will explore this topic and provide you with tips on how to rock gray hair.

Description

Gray hair has become increasingly trendy in recent years. Many people are embracing their natural gray hair or even dyeing their hair gray. Gray hair can give a sophisticated and chic look, and it can be styled in various ways. However, some people still wonder if gray hair is in style in 2020.

How to Rock Gray Hair

If you are thinking of embracing gray hair, there are several ways to rock this look. Here are some tips:

1. Get a Good Haircut

A good haircut can make all the difference when it comes to rocking gray hair. Consider getting a modern haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. A good haircut can add texture, volume, and movement to your gray hair.

2. Embrace Your Natural Gray Hair

If you are already going gray, consider embracing your natural hair color. Gray hair can be beautiful and unique, and it can make a statement. You can also use hair products that enhance your natural gray hair, such as hair mousse or hair serum.

3. Dye Your Hair Gray

If you want to dye your hair gray, there are several options available. You can dye your hair at home or go to a professional hair salon. Keep in mind that dyeing your hair gray can be a long process and may require multiple sessions. You should also use hair products that are specifically designed for dyed hair to maintain the color and prevent damage.

Step by Step

If you decide to dye your hair gray, here are the steps you should follow:

1. Choose the Right Shade of Gray

There are many shades of gray to choose from, ranging from light silver to dark charcoal. Consider your skin tone and natural hair color when choosing the shade of gray that will suit you best.

2. Prepare Your Hair

Before dyeing your hair, you should prepare it by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. You should also apply a hair mask or conditioner to hydrate your hair and protect it from damage.

3. Apply the Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box carefully. Apply the dye evenly to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends. Use a comb to distribute the dye evenly.

4. Wait and Rinse

Wait for the recommended time before rinsing the dye out. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water and apply a conditioner to lock in the color and hydrate your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for rocking gray hair:

1. Use Hair Products Specifically for Gray Hair

Gray hair can be more fragile and prone to dryness than other hair types. Use hair products that are specifically designed for gray hair, such as shampoos, conditioners, and hair masks. These products can help to hydrate your hair and prevent damage.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, especially if it is already fragile. If you use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Gray hair can be styled in various ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. You can also try adding hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some extra flair to your gray hair.

FAQ

Q: Is gray hair in style in 2020?

A: Yes, gray hair has been in style in recent years and continues to be popular in 2020.

Q: Can I dye my hair gray at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair gray at home. However, it can be a long process and may require multiple sessions. Follow the instructions on the hair dye box carefully and use hair products that are specifically designed for dyed hair.

Q: What hair products should I use for gray hair?

A: Use hair products that are specifically designed for gray hair, such as shampoos, conditioners, and hair masks. These products can help to hydrate your hair and prevent damage.

Hair Care

Gray hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for gray hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Less Often

Gray hair can be more prone to dryness than other hair types. Wash your hair less often to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. Use a dry shampoo or hair powder between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

2. Condition Your Hair Regularly

Use a conditioner or hair mask regularly to hydrate your hair and prevent damage. Leave the conditioner or mask on for a few minutes before rinsing it out to allow it to penetrate your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair, especially if it is already fragile. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's rays.

In conclusion, gray hair is still in style in 2020 and continues to be popular in 2023. Whether you embrace your natural gray hair or dye it gray, there are many ways to rock this look. Follow our tips and steps to achieve beautiful and chic gray hair.
