How To Shave Armpit Hair With Trimmer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you want to know how to shave your armpit hair with a trimmer? If so, you're in the right place! Shaving your armpit hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily and quickly.

Table of Contents

  • Tools Needed
  • Preparation
  • How to Shave Armpit Hair with Trimmer
  • Tips for Shaving Armpit Hair with Trimmer
  • FAQs
  • Armpit Hair Care

Tools Needed

Before you start shaving your armpit hair, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • A trimmer with a guard attachment
  • Scissors
  • Shaving cream or gel
  • A clean towel
  • A moisturizer

Preparation

Before you start shaving your armpit hair, it's important to prepare your skin and hair. Follow these steps to prepare:

  1. Trim your armpit hair with scissors to make shaving easier.
  2. Take a shower or bathe to soften the hair and open up the pores.
  3. Exfoliate your armpits to remove dead skin cells and prevent ingrown hairs.
  4. Dry your armpits with a clean towel.

How to Shave Armpit Hair with Trimmer

Now that you're ready to shave your armpit hair, follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Attach the guard to your trimmer according to the length of hair you want to leave.
  2. Apply shaving cream or gel to your armpit.
  3. Hold the trimmer at a 45-degree angle and start trimming the hair in the direction of hair growth.
  4. Continue trimming until you've removed all the hair you want to.
  5. Rinse your armpits with warm water to remove any remaining shaving cream or gel.
  6. Dry your armpits with a clean towel.
  7. Apply a moisturizer to your armpits to soothe the skin.

Tips for Shaving Armpit Hair with Trimmer

Here are some tips to make shaving your armpit hair with a trimmer easier:

  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to a shorter one to avoid cutting your skin.
  • Use a light touch when trimming to prevent skin irritation and cuts.
  • Trim in different directions to get all the hair.
  • Use a moisturizer after shaving to soothe the skin.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaving armpit hair with a trimmer:

  • Can I use a razor instead of a trimmer? Yes, you can use a razor to shave your armpit hair, but it's more difficult and can cause more irritation and cuts.
  • Is it okay to shave my armpit hair every day? Shaving your armpit hair every day can cause skin irritation and ingrown hairs. It's best to wait a few days in between shaves.
  • How often should I replace my trimmer? It's best to replace your trimmer every 1-2 years or when it starts to lose its cutting power.

Armpit Hair Care

Now that you've shaved your armpit hair, it's important to take care of your skin and hair. Here are some tips:

  • Apply moisturizer to your armpits daily to prevent skin dryness and irritation.
  • Exfoliate your armpits once a week to prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Avoid using antiperspirants with aluminum, as they can clog your pores and cause irritation.

Now that you know how to shave your armpit hair with a trimmer, you can enjoy smooth and hair-free armpits all year round!
