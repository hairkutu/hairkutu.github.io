Short Hair Styles For Over 60S With Glasses A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you over 60 and wear glasses? Do you want to try a new hairstyle that complements your eyeglasses? Look no further! In this article, we will explore various short hair styles that suit women over 60 with glasses. From bobs to pixies, we've got you covered. So, let's dive in!

Description

Short hair styles are perfect for women over 60 as they are low-maintenance and give a youthful look. Furthermore, short hair styles that complement glasses can enhance your overall appearance. With the right cut and styling, you can achieve a chic and elegant look.

How-To

Before getting a haircut, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and glasses. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which short hair style suits you the best. Once you have decided on the cut, follow these steps to style your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  3. Use a hair straightener or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for three short hair styles that suit women over 60 with glasses:

1. Bob Haircut

The bob haircut is a classic and versatile short hair style that suits most face shapes. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Starting from the back, cut your hair to the desired length. Use a razor to create texture and movement.
  3. Move to the sides and cut your hair to the same length as the back.
  4. Finish with the top section, cutting it slightly shorter than the sides and back.
  5. Style your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a daring and edgy short hair style that suits confident women. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Starting from the back, cut your hair short using scissors or clippers.
  3. Move to the sides and cut your hair to the same length as the back.
  4. Finish with the top section, cutting it slightly shorter than the sides and back.
  5. Style your hair with a hair wax to add texture and definition.

3. Layered Haircut

The layered haircut is a trendy and youthful short hair style that suits most face shapes. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Starting from the back, cut your hair to the desired length. Use a razor to create texture and movement.
  3. Move to the sides and cut your hair to the same length as the back.
  4. Finish with the top section, cutting it slightly shorter than the sides and back.
  5. Use a razor to create layers and texture in your hair.
  6. Style your hair with a curling iron or hair straightener to add volume and movement.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hair style:

  • Consider your face shape, hair texture, and glasses when choosing a cut.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine which cut suits you the best.
  • Use volumizing products to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a hair straightener or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair.
  • Finish with a hairspray or hair wax to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair styles for over 60s with glasses:

1. Are short hair styles suitable for women over 60?

Yes, short hair styles are perfect for women over 60 as they are low-maintenance and give a youthful look.

2. What short hair styles suit women over 60 with glasses?

Bobs, pixie cuts, and layered haircuts are short hair styles that suit women over 60 with glasses.

3. How do I style my short hair with glasses?

Use a hair straightener or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair. Finish with a hairspray or hair wax to hold the style in place.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs proper care. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Conclusion

Short hair styles are perfect for women over 60 with glasses as they are low-maintenance and give a youthful look. From bobs to pixies, there are various short hair styles to choose from. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and glasses when choosing a cut. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which cut suits you the best. Follow the styling and hair care tips to maintain your short hair style. With the right cut and styling, you can achieve a chic and elegant look!
