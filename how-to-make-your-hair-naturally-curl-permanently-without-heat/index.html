How To Make Your Hair Naturally Curl Permanently Without Heat

Written by Aprilia May 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of using hot tools to curl your hair every day? Do you want to achieve natural, bouncy curls without damaging your hair? Look no further! In this article, we will show you how to make your hair naturally curl permanently without heat.

Description

Natural curls are beautiful and can add volume and texture to your hair. However, using heat to curl your hair can cause damage and breakage. Permanent curls can be achieved without heat by using different techniques and products.

How To

There are several ways to make your hair naturally curl permanently without heat:

1. Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that can make your hair curly. It involves applying a solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that hold it straight. The hair is then wrapped around rods and another solution is applied to reform the bonds in a curly shape. The curls can last up to six months.

2. Curlformers

Curlformers are a heatless way to curl your hair. They are plastic tubes that can be wrapped around sections of your hair to create curls. Simply thread your hair through the tubes and leave them in overnight. In the morning, remove the tubes to reveal bouncy curls.

3. Twist-Out

A twist-out is a popular natural hair styling method that can create defined curls. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then apply a styling product like curl cream or gel. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly. Leave the twists in overnight and unravel them in the morning for beautiful curls.

Step by Step

1. Perm

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply the perm solution to your hair.
  3. Wrap your hair around the perm rods.
  4. Apply the neutralizing solution to your hair.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

2. Curlformers

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair.
  3. Separate your hair into sections.
  4. Thread each section through the curlformers.
  5. Leave the curlformers in overnight.
  6. Remove the curlformers in the morning.

3. Twist-Out

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair.
  3. Separate your hair into sections.
  4. Twist each section tightly.
  5. Leave the twists in overnight.
  6. Unravel the twists in the morning.

Tips

  • Use a deep conditioner regularly to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Can I still straighten my hair after getting a perm?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair after getting a perm. However, it is important to wait at least two weeks before using any heat on your hair to allow it to recover from the chemical treatment.

How long do curlformers last?

Curlformers can last for several uses if taken care of properly. To extend their lifespan, store them in a cool, dry place and avoid bending or twisting them.

How often should I do a twist-out?

You can do a twist-out as often as you like. However, it is important to give your hair a break between styling to prevent damage and breakage.

Hair Care

After getting a perm or using any of these techniques, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and prevent damage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using hot tools and limit your use of hair products to prevent buildup.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your hair naturally curl permanently without heat. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you and enjoy your beautiful, bouncy curls!
