Best Volumizing Products For Thin Fine Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having flat and lifeless hair? Do you struggle to add volume to your thin and fine locks? If so, you're not alone. Many people with thin and fine hair struggle to create volume and body in their hair. Fortunately, there are many volumizing products on the market that can help. In this article, we'll highlight the best volumizing products for thin and fine hair.

Volumizing Shampoo

One of the best ways to add volume to thin and fine hair is to use a volumizing shampoo. These shampoos are specially formulated to add body and fullness to hair without weighing it down. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen, as these can help strengthen and thicken hair.

Recommended Product:

OGX Biotin and Collagen Shampoo

Volumizing Conditioner

In addition to using a volumizing shampoo, it's important to also use a volumizing conditioner. These conditioners can help detangle hair and add moisture without weighing it down. Look for conditioners that contain ingredients like silk proteins and wheat amino acids, as these can help strengthen and thicken hair.

Recommended Product:

L'Oreal Paris Elvive Fibralogy Thickening Conditioner

Volumizing Mousse

A volumizing mousse is a great styling product for adding body and fullness to hair. These mousses are lightweight and can be applied to damp hair before blow-drying. Look for mousses that contain ingredients like polymers and proteins, as these can help add volume and hold to hair.

Recommended Product:

Herbal Essences Body Envy Volumizing Mousse

Root Lift Spray

If you're looking to add volume specifically to your roots, a root lift spray can help. These sprays are applied directly to the roots of the hair before blow-drying and can help lift hair and add fullness. Look for sprays that contain ingredients like keratin and panthenol, as these can help strengthen and thicken hair.

Recommended Product:

Big Sexy Hair Root Pump Plus

Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a great product for adding volume to hair on non-wash days. These sprays can help absorb excess oil and add texture to hair, making it easier to style and adding volume. Look for dry shampoos that contain ingredients like rice starch and kaolin clay, as these can help absorb oil and add texture.

Recommended Product:

Batiste Dry Shampoo

Volumizing Hair Spray

A volumizing hair spray is a great finishing product for adding hold and fullness to hair. These sprays can be applied to dry hair and can help keep styles in place while adding volume. Look for hair sprays that contain ingredients like hydrolyzed wheat protein and hydrolyzed keratin, as these can help strengthen and thicken hair.

Recommended Product:

TRESemme Beauty-Full Volume Flexible Finish Hairspray

Volumizing Serum

A volumizing serum can be a great product for adding shine, moisture, and fullness to hair. These serums are applied to damp hair before blow-drying and can help protect hair from heat damage while adding volume. Look for serums that contain ingredients like argan oil and vitamin E, as these can help add shine and moisture to hair.

Recommended Product:

John Frieda Luxurious Volume Perfectly Full Mousse

Scalp Treatment

Healthy hair starts at the scalp, so it's important to take care of your scalp as well as your hair. A scalp treatment can help nourish and revitalize the scalp, promoting healthy hair growth and adding volume. Look for scalp treatments that contain ingredients like tea tree oil and peppermint oil, as these can help stimulate the scalp and promote healthy hair growth.

Recommended Product:

The Body Shop Ginger Scalp Care Shampoo

Hair Growth Supplements

If you're looking to add volume to your hair over the long-term, hair growth supplements can be a great option. These supplements are formulated with vitamins and minerals that can help promote healthy hair growth and strengthen hair. Look for supplements that contain ingredients like biotin, vitamin C, and iron, as these can help nourish and strengthen hair.

Recommended Product:

Nature's Bounty Hair, Skin & Nails Gummies

Tips for Adding Volume to Thin Fine Hair

In addition to using volumizing products, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to add volume to thin and fine hair:

  • Blow-dry hair upside down to add lift at the roots.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to add volume and body to hair.
  • Tease hair at the crown to add height and volume.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down hair, like oils and creams.
  • Get regular trims to keep hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

If you have thin and fine hair, don't despair. With the right volumizing products and styling techniques, you can add body and fullness to your locks. Try some of the products and tips we've recommended in this article to achieve the voluminous hair you've always wanted.
