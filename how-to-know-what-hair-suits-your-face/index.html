How To Know What Hair Suits Your Face

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit your face shape. However, with a little bit of guidance, you can determine your face shape and choose the perfect hairstyle to complement your features. In this article, we'll show you how to know what hair suits your face and give you tips on how to care for your new style.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

The easiest way to determine your face shape is to pull your hair back and examine your face in a mirror. Look for the following characteristics:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Prominent cheekbones and a circular shape.
  • Square: Angular jawline and a square-shaped face.
  • Heart: Wider forehead and cheekbones with a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and chin with wider cheekbones.
  • Triangle: Narrow forehead with wider jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you've determined your face shape, follow these steps to choose the right hairstyle:

  1. Research hairstyles that suit your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness.
  3. Think about your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion.
  5. Choose a hairstyle that you feel confident and comfortable with.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your facial features, such as your eyes and nose, when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality and style.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and ask for feedback from friends and family.
  • Accessorize your hair with clips, headbands, and other hair accessories to add interest and variety to your style.

FAQs

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's not uncommon to have a combination of face shapes. Focus on the dominant features of your face to determine which face shape to use as a guide for choosing your hairstyle.

Can I still wear a certain hairstyle if it doesn't suit my face shape?

Of course! At the end of the day, the most important thing is that you feel confident and comfortable with your hairstyle. Use your face shape as a guide, but don't be afraid to experiment with different styles.

Hair Care for Your New Hairstyle

Once you've chosen your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective hair product.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to choose the perfect hairstyle to suit your face shape and keep your hair looking its best.
