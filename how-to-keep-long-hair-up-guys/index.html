How To Keep Long Hair Up For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by William Apr 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Welcome to our blog post about how to keep long hair up for guys. In this post, we’ll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to keep your hair up, tips on how to maintain your hairstyle, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Keep Long Hair Up for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Keep Long Hair Up for Guys

One of the best ways to keep your long hair up is by using hair ties or hairpins. Here are some other methods that you can use:

  • Man bun: This is a classic hairstyle that involves tying your hair into a bun at the back of your head. You can use a hair tie or hairpin to secure the bun in place.
  • Ponytail: Another popular hairstyle for long hair is the ponytail. You can tie your hair at the back of your head or on top of your head.
  • Braids: If you want to add some variety to your hairstyle, you can try braiding your hair. There are different types of braids that you can do, such as French braids, fishtail braids, and Dutch braids.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re new to keeping your long hair up, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do a man bun:

  1. Gather your hair at the back of your head as if you were going to do a ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair until it starts to coil around itself.
  3. Wrap the twisted hair around itself to form a bun.
  4. Secure the bun in place with a hair tie or hairpin.
  5. Adjust the bun to your desired size and shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Keeping your long hair up requires some maintenance to keep it looking good. Here are some tips that you can follow:

  • Use a hair tie or hairpin that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Add some hair product like pomade or wax to help keep your hair in place.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can I keep my long hair up if I have a receding hairline?

A: Yes, you can still keep your long hair up even if you have a receding hairline. You can try a man bun or a ponytail to keep your hair up.

Q: How long does my hair need to be to keep it up?

A: You can keep your hair up even if it’s not that long. You can try a mini bun or a half-up, half-down hairstyle.

Q: Can I sleep with my hair up?

A: It’s not recommended to sleep with your hair up as it can cause breakage and damage to your hair. You can try a loose braid or a low ponytail instead.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your long hair up is just one part of maintaining your hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips that you can follow:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes foods that are good for your hair, such as salmon, nuts, and leafy greens.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

Now that you know how to keep long hair up for guys, you can experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good.
