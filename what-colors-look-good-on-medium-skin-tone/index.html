What Colors Look Good On Medium Skin Tone A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Jun 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for the perfect colors to complement your medium skin tone? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll be sharing our top tips on what colors look good on medium skin tone, as well as some FAQs and hair care tips to ensure that you look your best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium skin tone is a beautiful and versatile skin type that can carry off a wide range of colors. However, not all colors suit every medium skin tone, and it’s important to choose colors that complement your skin tone and bring out your natural beauty. In this guide, we’ll be sharing some of the best colors for medium skin tone, as well as some tips on how to wear them.

How To

The key to finding the perfect colors for medium skin tone is to choose shades that complement your skin’s undertones. Medium skin tones can have warm, cool, or neutral undertones, and it’s essential to determine your undertones before selecting colors.

Step 1: Determine Your Undertones

The easiest way to determine your undertones is to look at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear green, you have warm undertones. If they look blue, you have cool undertones. If you can’t tell whether your veins are green or blue, you have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Choose Your Colors

Once you know your undertones, you can start selecting colors that will complement your skin tone. Here are some of the best colors for medium skin tone:

  • Warm colors: orange, peach, yellow, gold, brown, and olive
  • Cool colors: blue, purple, green, pink, and silver
  • Neutral colors: beige, gray, and taupe

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the best colors for your medium skin tone:

  • Avoid colors that wash you out, such as pastels and pale shades.
  • Wear bold and bright colors to make a statement.
  • Opt for jewel tones, such as emerald, ruby, and sapphire, for a luxurious look.
  • Experiment with prints and patterns, such as leopard print and floral, to add interest to your outfit.

FAQ

What colors should I avoid if I have medium skin tone?

Avoid colors that are too pale or pastel, as they can wash you out. You should also stay away from colors that clash with your undertones, such as cool colors if you have warm undertones.

Can I wear black if I have medium skin tone?

Yes, black looks great on medium skin tone. However, you can also experiment with other dark colors, such as navy and dark green.

Can I wear white if I have medium skin tone?

Absolutely! White is a great color for medium skin tone, especially in the summer months. Just make sure to choose a shade that complements your undertones.

Hair Care

Finally, it’s essential to take care of your hair to ensure that it complements your medium skin tone. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, opt for golden or caramel highlights. If you have cool undertones, opt for ash or platinum highlights.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking healthy and glossy.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair looks great and complements your medium skin tone perfectly.

So there you have it – our guide to what colors look good on medium skin tone. By following these tips, you can create a wardrobe that showcases your natural beauty and makes you feel confident and stylish. Have fun experimenting with different colors and styles until you find the perfect combination for you!
