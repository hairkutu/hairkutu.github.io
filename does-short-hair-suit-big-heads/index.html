Does Short Hair Suit Big Heads

Written by Christine Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short haircuts have been in fashion for quite some time now. However, one of the most frequently asked questions when it comes to going for a short haircut is, does short hair suit big heads? The answer to this question is not straightforward as it depends on various factors like face shape, hair texture, and personal preference. In this article, we will be discussing how to determine whether short hair suits big heads and some tips on how to rock short hair with a big head.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Whether Short Hair Suits Big Heads
  • Tips for Rocking Short Hair with a Big Head
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Determine Whether Short Hair Suits Big Heads

Short haircuts can suit big heads, but it all depends on the shape of your face. If you have a round face, then a short haircut might not be the best option for you. This is because a round face already has a lot of fullness, and a short haircut might add more volume to it, making it look even rounder. Instead, go for a longer bob or a shoulder-length haircut that will balance out the fullness of your face.

On the other hand, if you have an oval face, then short haircuts will suit you perfectly. An oval face has balanced proportions, and a short haircut will highlight your facial features, making you look elegant and sophisticated. However, be careful not to go too short as it might make your head look disproportioned.

If you have a square face, then short haircuts can work wonders for you. A square face has a strong jawline, and a short haircut will soften the angles of your face, giving you a more feminine look. You can go for a pixie cut or a short bob to achieve this look.

Tips for Rocking Short Hair with a Big Head

Now that you know how to determine whether short hair suits big heads, here are some tips to help you rock your short hair:

  1. Accessorize: Adding some accessories like earrings or a headband can help balance out the proportions of your head.
  2. Highlight your facial features: Short haircuts can highlight your facial features like your cheekbones and eyes, so make sure to play them up with makeup.
  3. Experiment with different styles: Short haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many ways. Try different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  4. Take care of your hair: Short hair requires regular maintenance, so make sure to take care of it by using the right products and getting regular trims.

FAQs

Q. Can a big head wear a buzz cut?

A. Yes, a buzz cut can work for a big head, especially if you have a square or oval face shape.

Q. Can a big head wear a bob haircut?

A. Yes, a bob haircut can work for a big head, but make sure to choose the right length and style that complements your face shape.

Q. Can a big head wear a short pixie cut?

A. Yes, a short pixie cut can work for a big head, but make sure to choose the right length and style that complements your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Now that you have decided to go for a short haircut, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

In conclusion, short haircuts can suit big heads, but it all depends on various factors like face shape, hair texture, and personal preference. By following the tips mentioned in this article, you can rock your short hair with confidence and style.
