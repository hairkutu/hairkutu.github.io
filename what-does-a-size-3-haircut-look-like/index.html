What Does A Size 3 Haircut Look Like A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Mar 15, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you planning to get a haircut and wondering what a size 3 haircut looks like? Look no further as this guide will provide you with all the information you need to know.

Description

A size 3 haircut is a clipper guard that is 3/8 inch or 9.5 mm long. It is one of the most popular clipper sizes used by barbers and stylists. The size 3 guard is often used for buzz cuts, crew cuts, and other short hairstyles. It leaves hair long enough to style and short enough to be low maintenance.

How to

To achieve a size 3 haircut, you will need to use clippers with a size 3 guard attached. Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Wash and dry your hair before getting a haircut. This will make it easier for the barber or stylist to cut your hair evenly.

Step 2: Choose your style

Decide on the style you want before getting a haircut. If you are unsure, ask the barber or stylist for recommendations.

Step 3: Use the clippers

Attach the size 3 guard to the clippers and start cutting your hair. Begin at the sides and work your way to the top. Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Trim the top

Use scissors or thinning shears to trim the top of your hair. This will give it a more natural look and prevent it from looking too bulky.

Step 5: Finish with styling

Apply a styling product to your hair and style it as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a size 3 haircut:

  • Communicate clearly with the barber or stylist about the style you want.
  • Be patient, especially if you are getting a buzz cut or crew cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure it is even.
  • Consider getting a touch-up every few weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

FAQ

1. Can I achieve a size 3 haircut with scissors?

No, a size 3 haircut is achieved using clippers with a size 3 guard attached. Scissors are used to trim the top of the hair.

2. Is a size 3 haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a size 3 haircut is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick hair.

3. How long does a size 3 haircut last?

A size 3 haircut can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Hair Care

To maintain your size 3 haircut, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straightening irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what a size 3 haircut looks like and how to achieve it, you can confidently walk into the salon and ask for the style you want. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain your haircut regularly to keep it looking its best.
