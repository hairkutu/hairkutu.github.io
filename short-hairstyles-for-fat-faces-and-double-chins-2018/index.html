Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins 2018

Description

If you have a round face and a double chin, you may feel like it's difficult to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. However, there are plenty of short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins that can make you look and feel your best. In 2018, there were several trends that were particularly popular for women with round faces and double chins, including pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and shaggy styles.

How to Choose a Short Hairstyle for Fat Faces and Double Chins

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle for a round face and double chin is to determine your face shape. Look in the mirror and note the widest part of your face, as well as the length. If your face is widest at the cheeks and tapers down to a narrow chin, you likely have a heart-shaped face. If your face is widest at the jawline and tapers down to a narrow forehead, you likely have a diamond-shaped face. If your face is widest at the cheeks and has a rounded chin, you likely have a round face.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features. For round faces and double chins, short hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown are particularly flattering. Pixie cuts, for example, can be styled with a deep side part or a textured fringe to create the illusion of height. Bob haircuts that are shorter in the back and longer in the front can also be a great choice, as they draw attention away from the chin and towards the hair.

Hairstyles to Avoid

While there are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter round faces and double chins, there are also some styles that should be avoided. In general, hairstyles that are too short or too flat can make the face appear wider and more round. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can draw attention to the chin area, and avoid styles that are too tightly cropped to the head.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Short Hair

If you've decided to go for a short hairstyle, there are a few things you can do to style your hair in a way that flatters your face shape. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting it at the root to add volume.
  4. If you have a pixie cut, use a styling wax or pomade to add texture and definition to your hair.
  5. If you have a bob haircut, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your short haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron to style your hair.

FAQs

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my face look even rounder?

A: No, a short hairstyle can actually make your face appear longer and slimmer. By adding height and volume at the crown, you can create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right style of bangs. Side-swept bangs can be particularly flattering for round faces and double chins, as they draw attention away from the chin area.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles require a slightly different approach to hair care than longer styles. Here are a few tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup.
  • Invest in a good quality leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or cream when using hot tools.

With the right short hairstyle and a few styling tips, you can feel confident and beautiful no matter what your face shape or size.
