How To Cut Short Hair Straight At The Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair, you know how important it is to keep it looking neat and tidy. One of the most challenging things about cutting short hair is getting the back straight. In this guide, we will show you how to cut short hair straight at the back, so you can maintain a sleek, professional look.

Description

Cutting short hair straight at the back can be a challenge, especially if you don't have experience or the right tools. However, with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a straight, polished look. In this guide, we will show you step-by-step how to cut short hair straight at the back.

How to Cut Short Hair Straight at the Back

Cutting short hair straight at the back requires a few essential tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. It's also helpful to have someone to assist you, but it's not necessary.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut short hair straight at the back:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Use a comb to part your hair down the middle, from the crown of your head to the nape of your neck.
  3. Use the handheld mirror to look at the back of your head. Make sure your hair is straight and even.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the middle of your head and comb it straight down. Cut the hair to your desired length, making sure to keep the scissors straight and level.
  5. Repeat this process on both sides of your head, working from the middle to the sides.
  6. Check the back of your head again using the handheld mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your hair is straight and even.
  7. Trim any stray hairs or uneven pieces.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair Straight at the Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting short hair straight at the back:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Start by cutting a little bit of hair at a time. You can always cut more but can't add hair back.
  • Stand in front of a mirror with good lighting to avoid shadows.
  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing can lead to mistakes and uneven cuts.
  • If you're unsure, it's better to err on the side of caution and cut less hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short hair straight at the back:

1. Can I cut my own hair straight at the back?

Yes, you can cut your own hair straight at the back by following the steps outlined in this guide. However, it's always easier to have someone else do it for you.

2. Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

You should cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is, which can result in uneven cuts.

3. How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Cutting short hair straight at the back can be a challenge, but with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a polished, professional look. Whether you're cutting your own hair or someone else's, follow these tips and steps to achieve the best results.
