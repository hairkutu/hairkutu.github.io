How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best hair parting for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. Here are the six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Long

To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and take note of the following:

  • Forehead: Measure from your hairline to the widest point of your forehead.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones at their widest point.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jawbone begins to angle upward.

Once you have these measurements, you can use them to determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Face is almost as wide as it is long, with full cheeks and a rounded chin.
  • Square: Face is roughly as wide as it is long, with a strong, angular jawline and forehead.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheeks and chin, which are tapered.
  • Diamond: Forehead and chin are narrower than the cheekbones, which are the widest point of the face.
  • Long: Face is longer than it is wide, with a high forehead and a long, narrow chin.

Best Hair Parting for Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Oval faces are considered the most versatile of all face shapes, and can pull off almost any hair parting. For a classic, timeless look, try a center parting. For a more modern look, try a deep side parting.

Best Hair Parting for Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a hair parting that helps elongate your face. A deep side parting is a great option, as it creates the illusion of length. Avoid center partings, as they can make your face look even rounder.

Best Hair Parting for Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your strong, angular jawline. A side parting with soft, face-framing layers can help achieve this. Avoid center partings, as they can make your face look even more angular.

Best Hair Parting for Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you'll want to balance out your wider forehead with your narrower chin. A center parting can help achieve this balance. Avoid side partings, as they can make your forehead look even wider.

Best Hair Parting for Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to accentuate your cheekbones, which are the widest point of your face. A deep side parting can help draw attention to your cheekbones. Avoid center partings, as they can make your face look more narrow.

Best Hair Parting for Long Face Shape

If you have a long face shape, you'll want to avoid hair partings that make your face look even longer. A center parting can make your face look even more narrow, so opt for a side parting instead.

Tips for Perfecting Your Hair Parting

Now that you know the best hair parting for your face shape, here are some tips to help you perfect your look:

  • Use a tail comb to create a precise parting.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the right one for your hair type.
  • Try blow-drying your hair in the opposite direction of your parting to create more volume.
  • Consider consulting with a stylist to help you find the perfect hair parting for your face shape.
  • Remember that hair partings can be changed up daily for a fresh look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my hair parting if I don't like how it looks?

Yes! Hair partings can be changed up daily for a fresh look.

Can I still wear a side fringe if I have a round face shape?

Yes, but make sure the fringe is long and side-swept, rather than short and straight across.

Is a center parting suitable for all face shapes?

No, a center parting is not suitable for all face shapes. It can make a heart-shaped face look even wider, and a long face look even more narrow.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Now that you know the best hair parting for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these hair care tips and choosing the best hair parting for your face shape, you're sure to have a fabulous, healthy head of hair!
