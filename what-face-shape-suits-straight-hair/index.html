What Face Shape Suits Straight Hair

Written by Petter Aug 19, 2023 Â· 9 min read

In this year 2023, straight hair is still a popular hairstyle choice for many women. However, not all face shapes are suited for straight hair. If you're considering straightening your locks, it's important to know what face shape suits straight hair best. In this blog post, we'll provide you with a guide on how to choose the best hairstyle based on your face shape, as well as tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Straight Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Straight Hair

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will complement your features.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, for straight hair, a layered cut that falls just below the shoulders is a great option.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face. A long, straight cut with layers that begin at the chin is a great option. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your features with a hairstyle that has soft, wispy layers. A shoulder-length cut with layers that start at the collarbone is a great option. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they will only emphasize the squareness of your face.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that balances out your features. A long, layered cut that falls just below the shoulders is a great option. Avoid short, blunt cuts that will emphasize your forehead.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that will balance out your features. A long, straight cut with layers that start at the chin is a great option. Avoid voluminous hairstyles that will make your face appear wider.

Long Face Shape

If you have a long face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that will add width to your face. A shoulder-length cut with layers that start at the chin is a great option. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that will make your face appear longer.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Straight Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that complements your face shape, it's time to style your straight hair. Follow these steps for a sleek and polished look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools
  3. Use a straightening iron to straighten your hair in small sections
  4. Apply a small amount of hair serum to add shine and tame flyaways
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Maintaining straight hair requires some effort and care. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking sleek and polished:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it's gentler than a brush
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about what face shape suits straight hair:

Q: Can I straighten my hair if I have a curly or wavy texture?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair regardless of its texture. However, it's important to use heat protectant products and to avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Q: How do I determine my face shape?

A: To determine your face shape, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Use these measurements to determine your face shape.

Q: Can I still have bangs with straight hair?

A: Yes, you can still have bangs with straight hair. However, it's important to choose a bang style that complements your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Straight Hair

Straight hair requires some special care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for straight hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair and scalp
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hair ties that can pull and damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can ensure that your straight hair looks its best, no matter what your face shape is.
