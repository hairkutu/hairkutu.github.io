How To Cut The Hair On Top Of Your Head With Clippers

Written by William Jun 06, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking to cut the hair on top of your head with clippers? Look no further, as we have put together a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect cut. Whether you’re looking for a simple trim or a more complex cut, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will be focusing on how to cut the hair on top of your head with clippers. We will cover everything from the tools you will need, to tips on achieving the perfect cut.

How to

Tools You Will Need

The first step in cutting your hair with clippers is to gather all the necessary tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • A set of clippers
  • Clipper guards of different sizes
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • A towel
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • Scissors (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have all the necessary tools, it’s time to get started. Follow these steps for the perfect cut:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it is completely dry before you begin.
  2. Attach the desired clipper guard to your clippers. If you’re unsure which guard to use, start with a bigger size and work your way down.
  3. Comb your hair in the direction it naturally falls.
  4. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Hold the clippers with the blade facing downwards and run it over your head in a smooth motion.
  5. Repeat this process, moving the clippers against the grain of your hair for an even cut.
  6. Switch to a smaller guard for a closer cut around the ears and hairline.
  7. Check the mirror regularly to ensure you are achieving the desired length.
  8. If you want to add texture or blend in the sides, use scissors to trim the hair accordingly.
  9. Once you are satisfied with the length and texture, use the comb to blend any uneven areas.
  10. Finally, use the spray bottle to wet your hair and check for any stray hairs that may need trimming.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Start with a bigger guard and work your way down to avoid taking off too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Take breaks if you need to rest your arms or take a step back to assess the overall look.
  • Experiment with different techniques to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair with clippers:

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

While it is possible to cut your hair with regular scissors, it is not recommended. Hair scissors are specifically designed to achieve a clean, even cut and are less likely to cause split ends or damage to the hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Some people prefer to cut their hair every few weeks, while others may go months without a cut. It’s important to listen to your hair and give it the care it needs.

How do I maintain my hair after cutting it with clippers?

Regular washing and conditioning will help keep your hair healthy and strong. You may also want to invest in a good quality hair oil or treatment to keep your hair looking its best.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair with clippers can be a great way to save time and money on trips to the salon. However, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a heat protectant when using styling tools. A healthy diet and exercise can also help keep your hair looking its best.

So there you have it – a step-by-step guide to cutting the hair on top of your head with clippers. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking cut without ever leaving your home. Happy cutting!
