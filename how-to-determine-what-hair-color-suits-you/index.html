How To Determine What Hair Color Suits You

Written by Frank Jul 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're considering changing your hair color, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect shade that will complement your skin tone and enhance your features. However, with a few tips and tricks, determining what hair color suits you can be a breeze. In this article, we'll guide you through the process step-by-step and answer some frequently asked questions about hair color.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine What Hair Color Suits You
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care After Coloring

How to Determine What Hair Color Suits You

Before choosing a hair color, it's essential to determine your skin's undertone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral, and this will determine which hair color suits you best. The easiest way to determine your skin tone is by looking at your veins. If your veins appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. Neutral skin tones have a mix of both.

Another way to determine your skin tone is by holding up gold and silver jewelry to your face. If gold jewelry complements your skin, you have a warm skin tone, and if silver jewelry looks better on you, you have a cool skin tone. If you look good in both, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you've determined your skin tone, it's time to choose a hair color that suits you best. Here are the steps:

  1. Consider your current hair color. If you want to make a drastic change, it's best to consult a professional stylist.
  2. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone. If you have cool undertones, choose ash, platinum, or cool brown shades. For warm undertones, choose golden, honey, or warm brown shades. For neutral undertones, choose caramel or beige shades.
  3. Consider your eye color. If you have blue or green eyes, consider shades of blonde or brown. If you have brown eyes, consider warm shades of brown or red.
  4. Think about your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance hair color. Balayage or ombre techniques can be low-maintenance options.
  5. Consider your personality. If you're outgoing, you may want to choose a bold hair color, such as purple or pink. If you prefer a more natural look, stick with shades that are close to your natural hair color.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider the season. In the winter, darker shades may be more flattering, while lighter shades work well in the summer.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities with similar skin tones and eye colors.
  • Choose a shade that complements your wardrobe.
  • Consider your hair's condition. If your hair is damaged, avoid drastic changes that require bleach or lightening.
  • Use temporary hair color products, such as hair chalk or semi-permanent hair dye, to test out different shades before committing to a permanent change.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it's best to choose a hair dye that is labeled "hypoallergenic" or "gentle." You can also do a patch test before dyeing your hair to check for any allergic reactions.

How often should I touch up my roots?

This depends on the rate of your hair growth and the color you choose. On average, roots should be touched up every four to six weeks.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's essential to follow the instructions carefully and choose a hair dye that suits your skin tone and hair type. If you're unsure, it's best to consult a professional stylist.

Hair Care After Coloring

After coloring your hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain the color's vibrancy and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect shade that suits your skin tone and enhances your features. Remember to take care of your hair after coloring to maintain its vibrancy and keep it healthy.
