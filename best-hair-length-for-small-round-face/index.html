Best Hair Length For Small Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 21, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a small round face, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. The wrong hairstyle can make your face look even rounder, while the right one can enhance your features and make you look more attractive. In this article, we’ll help you find the best hair length for small round face and share tips on how to style your hair to flatter your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hair Length for Small Round Face
  • Hair Care Tips for Small Round Face
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair length for small round face, let’s first determine your face shape. Follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a close look at the shape of your face. Is it round, square, oval, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped?
  3. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are almost equal, you have a round face shape.

Now that you know your face shape, let’s move on to the best hair length for small round face.

Best Hair Length for Small Round Face

The best hair length for small round face is shoulder-length or longer. Long hair can elongate your face and create a slimming effect. If you prefer short hair, go for a pixie cut or a bob that hits just below your chin. Avoid blunt bangs or a blunt cut that stops at your cheekbones, as they can make your face look wider.

If you want to add some volume to your hair, opt for layers. Layers can add dimension to your hair and create the illusion of a longer face. However, be careful not to add too many layers, as they can make your hair appear thin and flat.

Another great hairstyle for small round face is a side-swept bang. This can help to create angles and balance out your face shape. You can also try a deep side part, which can elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Hairstyles to Avoid

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as a blunt cut that stops at your cheekbones or a full fringe. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face look wider and rounder. If you want to wear your hair up, avoid high ponytails or buns, as they can make your face look shorter and wider.

Hair Care Tips for Small Round Face

Now that you know the best hair length for small round face, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it’s wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hairline and create tension.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a small round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part, but it’s not the most flattering option for small round face. A deep side part can create angles and balance out your face shape.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail if I have a small round face?

A: It’s best to avoid high ponytails if you have a small round face, as they can make your face look shorter and wider. Instead, opt for a low ponytail or a loose bun.

Q: What hair color is best for small round face?

A: Any hair color can look great on small round face, but highlights or balayage can add dimension to your hair and create the illusion of a longer face.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a small round face?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise frequently, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair or a less active lifestyle, you can wash your hair every other day or every three days.

Now that you know the best hair length for small round face, it’s time to book an appointment with your hair stylist and try out some new hairstyles. Remember, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.
