What Hair Length Is Good For Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering what hair length is best for you. The truth is, there are a variety of hair lengths that can flatter a round face, and it all depends on your personal style and preferences. In this guide, we will go over some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hair length for your round face.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Length
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Hair Length
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into what hair length is best for round faces, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Take a photo of yourself facing the camera.
  2. Trace the outline of your face in the photo with a pen or pencil.
  3. Compare the shape of your face to the following descriptions:
  • Round: Full cheeks and a rounded chin with equal width and length.
  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin and the length is about one and a half times the width.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheeks and chin narrows to a point.
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face and the forehead and chin are narrower.

If you have determined that you have a round face, don't worry! There are plenty of hair lengths that can flatter your features.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Length

When choosing the right hair length for a round face, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Longer hair can elongate the face and create the illusion of a slimmer appearance.
  • Layers can add volume and movement to the hair, which can be especially flattering for round faces.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face.
  • Consider a side part, as it can create the appearance of a longer face.
  • Try a bob or lob that hits just below the chin, as it can help to elongate the face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Hair Length

Follow these steps to find the perfect hair length for your round face:

  1. Consider your personal style and preferences. Do you prefer long hair or short hair?
  2. Determine your hair type and texture. This can influence what hair length will work best for you.
  3. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines, Pinterest, or Instagram to find hairstyles that you like.
  4. Consult with your hairstylist. They can help you determine what hair length will work best for your face shape and hair type.
  5. Try it out! If you're not sure about a certain hair length, start with a trim and gradually work your way towards your desired length.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hair length is best for round faces:

  1. Can I pull off short hair with a round face?
    Yes! In fact, a short pixie cut or bob can be very flattering for round faces. Just make sure to choose a cut that is textured and has layers to add dimension.
  2. Is there a specific hair length that I should avoid with a round face?
    Avoid haircuts that end at the chin or cheekbones, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face.
  3. Can bangs work for round faces?
    Yes! Bangs can be very flattering for round faces, as long as they are side-swept or asymmetrical to create the illusion of a longer face.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Once you have found the perfect hair length for your round face, here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hair length for your round face and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
