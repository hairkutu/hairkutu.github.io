What Is The Best Shape Of Face

Written by Daniel Aug 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to beauty, there is no one-size-fits-all answer. However, having the right facial shape can greatly enhance your looks. In this article, we will discuss what is the best shape of face and how to determine if you have it. We will also share some hair care tips that can help you look your best.

Table of Contents

  • What is the Best Shape of Face?
  • How to Determine Your Facial Shape
  • The Best Shape of Face
  • Tips for Hair Care

How to Determine Your Facial Shape

Before we dive into what is the best shape of face, it's important to know how to determine your facial shape. The easiest way to do this is to take a selfie and trace the outline of your face on the photo using a tracing paper or an editing software.

Alternatively, you can stand in front of a mirror and use a washable marker to trace the outline of your face. Make sure to pull back your hair so you can see the full shape of your face.

Once you have the outline of your face, compare it to the four main shapes: oval, round, square, and heart. See which shape fits your outline the most.

The Best Shape of Face

The best shape of face is the oval shape. An oval face has a length that is one and a half times the width. It has soft angles and a gentle curve. This shape is versatile and can pull off any hairstyle, from short to long.

If you have an oval face, count yourself lucky. You can experiment with different haircuts and styles without worrying about whether they will complement your face shape or not.

However, if you have a round, square, or heart-shaped face, don't fret. There are plenty of hairstyles that can complement your features and bring out your best look.

Tips for Hair Care

No matter what facial shape you have, taking care of your hair is key to looking your best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly and use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type and style
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you

FAQ

1. Can I change my facial shape?

No, you can't change your facial shape. However, you can use hairstyles and makeup to create the illusion of a different facial shape.

2. What hairstyles are best for a round face?

Hairstyles that add height and volume on top of the head and elongate the face are best for a round face. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face.

3. What hairstyles are best for a square face?

Hairstyles that soften the angles of the face and add volume to the sides are best for a square face. Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the squareness of the face.

Now that you know what is the best shape of face, you can use this knowledge to choose the right hairstyles and makeup that will enhance your features. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
