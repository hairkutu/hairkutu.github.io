Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair With Bangs

Written by Oliver Mar 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have long hair with bangs, you might feel like you're limited when it comes to hairstyles. However, that couldn't be further from the truth! There are plenty of easy hairstyles you can do with long hair and bangs that will have you looking stylish and put together in no time. In this article, we'll go through some of the best easy hairstyles for long hair with bangs so you can switch up your look whenever you feel like it.

Description

Long hair with bangs can be a beautiful and versatile hairstyle, but it can also be challenging to style. It's essential to have a few quick and easy hairstyles in your repertoire, so you don't have to spend hours getting ready every day. The following hairstyles are perfect for long hair with bangs and will work for any occasion, from casual to formal events.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to prepare your hair correctly. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Once you've dried your hair, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools. If your hair is prone to frizz, use a smoothing serum or cream to tame flyaways.

1. Half Up, Half Down

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is a classic look that works well with long hair and bangs. To achieve this style, start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and securing it with a hair tie or clip. You can leave your bangs down or pin them to the side for a more polished look.

2. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is an effortless and chic hairstyle that is perfect for long hair with bangs. To achieve this style, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. You can leave your bangs down or sweep them to the side for a more sophisticated look.

3. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that looks great with long hair and bangs. To achieve this style, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. You can leave your bangs down or pin them to the side for a more polished look.

4. Side Braid

A side braid is a bohemian-inspired hairstyle that looks great with long hair and bangs. To achieve this style, gather your hair to one side and start braiding from the nape of your neck down to the ends. You can leave your bangs down or pin them to the side for a more polished look.

Step by Step

1. Half Up, Half Down

  1. Section off the top half of your hair.
  2. Secure the top section with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Leave your bangs down or pin them to the side.

2. Low Ponytail

  1. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure your hair with a hair tie.
  3. Leave your bangs down or sweep them to the side.

3. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun.
  3. Leave your bangs down or pin them to the side.

4. Side Braid

  1. Gather your hair to one side.
  2. Start braiding from the nape of your neck down to the ends.
  3. Leave your bangs down or pin them to the side.

Tips

If you want to add some extra oomph to your hairstyles, consider using hair accessories such as headbands or hair clips. You can also experiment with different hair textures by using a curling iron or straightener to create waves or curls. If you're short on time, dry shampoo can be a lifesaver. Just spray it onto your roots, and you'll have fresh-looking hair in no time.

FAQ

What can I do with long hair and bangs?

There are plenty of hairstyles you can do with long hair and bangs. Half-up, half-down, low ponytail, messy bun, and side braids are all easy and stylish options.

How do I keep my bangs looking fresh?

To keep your bangs looking fresh, trim them regularly and use dry shampoo to absorb excess oil. You can also use a straightener to smooth out any flyaways.

What hair products should I use for long hair with bangs?

Use products that are suitable for your hair type, such as shampoo and conditioner. A heat protectant spray is essential if you plan on using styling tools. If your hair is prone to frizz, use a smoothing serum or cream to tame flyaways.

Hair Care

Long hair with bangs requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Be sure to wash your hair regularly and use quality hair care products that are suitable for your hair type. Regular trims will also help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking fresh. If you're using styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
