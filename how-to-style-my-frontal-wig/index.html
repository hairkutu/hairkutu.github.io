How To Style My Frontal Wig

Written by Bardi May 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old wig style every day? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Styling your frontal wig is an easy way to change your look. In this article, we will show you how to style your frontal wig in a few simple steps.

Description

A frontal wig is a type of wig that has a lace closure at the front. This closure allows you to create a natural-looking hairline. Frontal wigs are popular because they can be styled in many different ways. You can wear them in a ponytail, braid, or any other style you desire. Styling your frontal wig can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve any look you want.

How to Style My Frontal Wig

Step 1: Choose Your Style

The first step in styling your frontal wig is to decide on the look you want to achieve. You can choose from a variety of styles, including curls, waves, straight, and updos. Once you have decided on your style, gather the necessary tools.

Step 2: Gather Your Tools

You will need a few tools to style your frontal wig. These include a wig brush, a heat protectant spray, a curling iron or flat iron, and a holding spray. Make sure to use products that are safe for synthetic or human hair wigs.

Step 3: Prep Your Wig

Before styling your wig, make sure it is clean and dry. Use a wig brush to remove any tangles or knots. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your wig from heat damage.

Step 4: Style Your Wig

Now it's time to style your wig. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create the desired style. If you're using a curling iron, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds. If you're using a flat iron, run it over small sections of hair to create a sleek and straight look. Use a holding spray to keep your style in place.

Step 5: Final Touches

Once you have styled your wig, you can add some final touches. You can use a hair accessory, such as a headband or clip, to add some extra flair. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness to your wig.

Tips

  • Use products that are safe for synthetic or human hair wigs.
  • Don't apply too much heat to your wig as it can damage the fibers.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your wig to avoid tangling or shedding.
  • Experiment with different styles and have fun!

FAQ

How often should I wash my frontal wig?

You should wash your frontal wig every 2-3 weeks, depending on how often you wear it. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner to keep your wig clean and soft.

Can I dye my frontal wig?

If your wig is made of human hair, you can dye it. However, if it is made of synthetic fibers, you cannot dye it as it will damage the wig.

How do I store my frontal wig?

You should store your frontal wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape. Keep it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Hair Care

To keep your frontal wig looking its best, you should take proper care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wig brush to remove tangles and knots.
  • Wash your wig every 2-3 weeks with a wig shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water or a hair dryer to dry your wig.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head.

By following these tips, you can keep your frontal wig looking beautiful and stylish for a long time.
