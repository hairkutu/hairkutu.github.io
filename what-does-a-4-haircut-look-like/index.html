What Does A 4 Haircut Look Like A Guide For Men

Written by Christine Apr 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a man who is looking to get a new haircut, you may be wondering what a 4 haircut looks like. A 4 haircut is a specific length of hair that is achieved by using a clipper guard number 4, which leaves the hair about 1/2 inch long. In this article, we'll go over what you can expect from a 4 haircut, how to get it, and how to take care of your hair afterward.

What Does a 4 Haircut Look Like?

A 4 haircut is a medium-length haircut that leaves about 1/2 inch of hair on top of the head. The sides and back of the head are usually tapered or faded down to a shorter length. The cut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Some men choose to wear it slicked back, while others prefer a messy, textured look.

How to Get a 4 Haircut

If you're interested in getting a 4 haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or hair stylist. Here are the steps they will likely take:

  1. Wash your hair to get rid of any dirt or product buildup.
  2. Towel dry your hair or let it air dry.
  3. Sit in the barber's chair and explain that you want a 4 haircut.
  4. The barber will use clippers with a guard number 4 to cut your hair to the desired length.
  5. They will then use scissors to trim any longer hairs and blend the top with the sides and back.
  6. If you want a tapered or faded look, the barber will use different guard sizes to gradually shorten the hair on the sides and back of your head.
  7. Once the haircut is complete, the barber will style your hair to your liking.

Tips for Maintaining Your 4 Haircut

To keep your 4 haircut looking fresh, you'll need to maintain it regularly. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber or hair stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use styling products like gel or pomade to create your desired look.
  • Brush or comb your hair daily to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and strength.

FAQs

Q: How long does a 4 haircut last?

A: A 4 haircut will last about 4-6 weeks before it starts to grow out and lose its shape.

Q: Can I get a 4 haircut if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, a 4 haircut can work well for men with thinning hair. It can create the illusion of thickness and fullness, especially when styled with a little bit of volume.

Q: How much does a 4 haircut cost?

A: The cost of a 4 haircut will vary depending on where you live and the salon or barber you visit. On average, you can expect to pay between $20 and $40.

Hair Care for Men

Getting a 4 haircut is a great way to refresh your look and try something new. By following these tips for maintenance and care, you can keep your hair looking its best and enjoy your new style for weeks to come.
