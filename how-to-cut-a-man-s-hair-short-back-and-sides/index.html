How To Cut A Man s Hair Short Back And Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to give a man a fresh new look, one of the best ways to do it is by cutting their hair short back and sides. This classic style is easy to maintain and looks great on almost everyone. In this article, we'll show you how to cut a man's hair short back and sides in a few simple steps.

Description

Short back and sides is a hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short at the back and sides of the head while leaving the hair longer on top. The style has been popular for decades and continues to be a classic look for men. It's a versatile style that can be worn in a variety of ways, from clean and professional to edgy and modern.

How to

To cut a man's hair short back and sides, you'll need a few tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an accurate length.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Use the comb to section the hair into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Clip the top section out of the way so you can focus on the sides and back.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Using the clippers, start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up to the top. Use a shorter guard for a closer cut or a longer guard for a more gradual fade. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Use the clippers to cut the back of the head, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use the same guard length as you did for the sides.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Back

Use the clippers to blend the sides and back together. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a longer guard as you move toward the top of the head.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Unclip the top section of hair and comb it straight up. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Start at the front and work your way to the back, cutting in a straight line.

Step 7: Blend the Top

Use the scissors to blend the top section of hair into the sides and back. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut into it at an angle, creating a seamless transition between the top and sides.

Step 8: Clean Up

Use the clippers and scissors to clean up any stray hairs or uneven spots.

Tips

Cutting hair can be tricky, especially if you're new to it. Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles.
  • Cut the hair in small sections to ensure accuracy.
  • Take your time and don't rush.

FAQ

Q: How long should the hair be on top?

A: The length of the hair on top can vary depending on personal preference. Generally, it should be long enough to style but not so long that it looks unkempt.

Q: How often should I cut my hair short back and sides?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is important, even if you have a short back and sides haircut. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut a man's hair short back and sides with confidence. It's an easy style to maintain and looks great on almost anyone. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro, you can achieve a clean and professional look with a little bit of practice.
