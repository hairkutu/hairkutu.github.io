Best Hair Style For Round Face 2021

Written by Frank May 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of trying different hair styles but none of them seem to suit your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will guide you through the best hair styles for round face 2021.

Description

Round face is characterized by having a wider forehead, chubby cheeks and a round chin. The goal of choosing a hair style for round face is to create an illusion of length and slimness. Choosing the right hair style can make a huge difference in enhancing your facial features and highlighting your best features.

How to

The first step in choosing the best hair style for round face is to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. If your face is widest at the cheeks and ears and your jawline is soft and rounded, then you have a round face.

Once you have determined your face shape, the next step is to choose the right hair style. The key is to create an illusion of length and slimness. This can be achieved by adding layers, volume and height to the crown of your head.

Here are some of the best hair styles for round face 2021:

Step by Step

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round face as they create an illusion of length and slimness. The layers should start from the chin and go all the way down to the ends. This will make your face appear longer and slimmer.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are another great option for round face as they create an illusion of length and slimness. The bangs should be swept to the side and should not be too thick. This will make your face appear longer and slimmer.

3. Pixie Cut

Pixie cut is a great option for round face as it creates an illusion of length and slimness. The hair should be cut short on the sides and back and longer on the top. This will make your face appear longer and slimmer.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair style for round face:

1. Avoid blunt cuts

Blunt cuts can make your face appear rounder. Avoid hair styles that are cut straight across.

2. Add layers

Adding layers can create an illusion of length and slimness. The layers should start from the chin and go all the way down to the ends.

3. Add volume

Adding volume to the crown of your head can create an illusion of length and slimness. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.

FAQ

1. What hair style should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hair styles that are cut straight across. This can make your face appear rounder.

2. What hair style should I choose if I have a round face?

Choose hair styles that create an illusion of length and slimness. Long layers, side swept bangs and pixie cuts are great options.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Here are some tips for hair care:

1. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. This will keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Avoid using hot tools

Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners as they can damage your hair.

3. Get regular haircuts

Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your hair style. This will also prevent split ends and breakage.

Choosing the best hair style for round face can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, you can find a hair style that suits your face shape and enhances your features. Use the tips and tricks mentioned in this article to achieve a hair style that makes you look and feel confident.
