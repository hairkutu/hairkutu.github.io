Best Short Beard Styles For Round Face

Written by Petter May 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for the best short beard styles for round face to achieve a perfect look? We understand how challenging it can be to choose the best beard style that complements your round face. In this article, we will walk you through the best short beard styles for round face, how to style them, and some hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, soft jawline, and rounded chin. The best short beard styles for a round face should create an illusion of length and sharpness to the face. The following are some of the best short beard styles for round face:

1. The Stubble

The stubble is one of the most popular short beard styles for a round face. It is effortless to maintain and gives a rugged look. Keep the hair length between 2 to 5 mm, depending on the thickness of your hair.

2. The Goatee

The goatee is another excellent short beard style for a round face. It adds length to the chin and creates a sharp jawline. You can either keep it short or let it grow slightly longer.

3. The Short Boxed Beard

The short boxed beard is a classic beard style that suits a round face. Keep the hair length short and trimmed around the cheeks and neck. It adds length to the face and creates a sharp jawline.

4. The Van Dyke

The Van Dyke is a unique short beard style that combines a goatee and a mustache. It adds length to the chin and creates a sharp jawline. Keep the hair length short and trimmed.

5. The Chin Strap

The chin strap is a thin, short beard that runs along the jawline. It creates a sharp jawline and adds length to the chin. Keep the hair length short and trimmed.

How to Style

The following are the steps to style your short beard:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your face and beard with warm water and mild soap.
  2. Trim your beard to the desired length using a beard trimmer.
  3. Define your beard shape by trimming around the cheeks and neck.
  4. Use a beard oil or balm to moisturize your beard and skin.
  5. Comb your beard to remove any tangles and style it to your preference.

Tips

The following are some tips to maintain your short beard:

  • Regularly trim your beard to maintain the desired length.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to moisturize your beard and skin.
  • Comb your beard regularly to remove any tangles.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hot water on your beard as it can damage the hair.

FAQ

1. Can I grow a beard if I have a round face?

Yes, you can grow a beard if you have a round face. The key is to choose a beard style that complements your face shape.

2. How often should I trim my short beard?

You should trim your short beard every 2 to 3 weeks to maintain the desired length.

Hair Care

A healthy diet and lifestyle can help promote healthy hair growth. It is essential to eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals. Regular exercise and stress management can also help promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the best short beard styles for a round face should create an illusion of length and sharpness to the face. The stubble, goatee, short boxed beard, Van Dyke, and chin strap are some of the best short beard styles for round face. Maintain your short beard by regularly trimming, moisturizing, and combing it. A healthy diet and lifestyle can also help promote healthy hair growth.
