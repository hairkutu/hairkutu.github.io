How Long Is A Number 3 Clipper Guard

Written by Bardi Aug 26, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you wondering how long a number 3 clipper guard is? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll discuss what a clipper guard is and how long a number 3 clipper guard is. We’ll also provide some tips and frequently asked questions about clipper guards and hair care.

What is a Clipper Guard?

A clipper guard is an attachment that fits onto a hair clipper to determine the length of hair that gets cut. The length of the hair is determined by the number on the guard. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be.

Clipper guards come in different sizes, ranging from number 0 to number 8. The number 0 guard leaves the hair very short, while the number 8 guard leaves the hair quite long. The number 3 guard is a popular choice for men who want a short but not too short haircut.

How Long is a Number 3 Clipper Guard?

A number 3 clipper guard leaves the hair 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm long. This is a medium-length haircut that is shorter than a number 4 guard but longer than a number 2 guard. It’s a great option for men who want a short but still slightly longer haircut.

It’s important to note that the length of the hair will vary depending on the type of clipper and the manufacturer of the guard. However, most clipper guards are standardized and will give a similar length of hair.

How to Use a Clipper Guard

Using a clipper guard is easy and straightforward. Here’s how:

Step 1: Choose the Right Guard

Choose the clipper guard that will give you the desired length of hair. Remember that the higher the number, the longer the hair will be.

Step 2: Attach the Guard to the Clippers

Attach the clipper guard to the clippers. Make sure it’s securely in place before you start cutting.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Turn on the clippers and start cutting the hair. Move the clippers in the direction of the hair growth, making sure to go slowly and steadily. Be careful around the ears and the neckline.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to use a clipper guard:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will give you a better result.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Choose the clipper guard that will give you the desired length of hair. Remember that the higher the number, the longer the hair will be.

Step 3: Attach the Guard to the Clippers

Attach the clipper guard to the clippers. Make sure it’s securely in place before you start cutting.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Turn on the clippers and start cutting the hair. Move the clippers in the direction of the hair growth, making sure to go slowly and steadily. Be careful around the ears and the neckline.

Step 5: Clean Up

Once you’ve finished cutting, clean up any loose hair around the neck and ears with a brush or towel. Take a shower to wash away any remaining hair.

Tips for Using Clipper Guards

Here are some tips for using clipper guards:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter haircut.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Don’t rush the process. Take your time and go slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Clean the clippers after each use to prolong their lifespan.

FAQ about Clipper Guards

What is the difference between a clipper guard and a clipper blade?

A clipper guard is an attachment that determines the length of the hair that gets cut, while a clipper blade is the part of the clipper that actually cuts the hair.

Can I use a clipper guard on any type of hair?

Clipper guards are designed to work on all types of hair, but they may not work as well on very thick or curly hair. In these cases, it’s best to use a lower number guard or no guard at all.

How often should I replace my clipper guards?

Clipper guards can last for several years with proper care, but they may need to be replaced if they become cracked or damaged. It’s also a good idea to replace them if they start to feel loose or don’t fit securely on the clippers.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage it.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how long a number 3 clipper guard is and how to use it, you can confidently give yourself or someone else a great-looking haircut. Remember to take your time, use the right guard, and follow the tips for the best results. Happy clipping!
