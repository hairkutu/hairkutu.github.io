2020 Hairstyles for Medium Length Thick Hair - Tips, How to, and FAQ

Are you looking for new hairstyles to try on your medium length thick hair? Look no further! This article will give you tips, step-by-step guides, and frequently asked questions about 2020 hairstyles for medium length thick hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

First, it's important to know what type of medium length thick hair you have. Is it straight, wavy, curly, or coily? Each hair type has different styling techniques and products that work best for it.

Once you know your hair type, you can start thinking about different hairstyles that will work for you. Some popular 2020 hairstyles for medium length thick hair include:

  • Long Bob
  • Shoulder-Length Layers
  • Blunt Lob
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Messy Bun
  • Braided Updo

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the Long Bob hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  3. Part your hair in the middle.
  4. Take a section of hair from the back and twist it away from your face.
  5. Pin the twisted section at the back of your head.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. Finish with hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your medium length thick hair:

  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools.
  • Invest in quality hair products that work for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about 2020 hairstyles for medium length thick hair:

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with medium length thick hair?

A: Yes, you can! A high ponytail or a low ponytail with side-swept bangs can be a chic and easy hairstyle.

Q: What products should I use for my curly medium length thick hair?

A: Look for products that define curls, such as curl creams or gels. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your curls.

Q: Can I still get a bob cut with medium length thick hair?

A: Yes, you can! A bob cut can work for medium length thick hair, but talk to your stylist about what length and style would work best for you.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your medium length thick hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips, step-by-step guides, and frequently asked questions, you're ready to rock 2020 hairstyles for medium length thick hair! Remember to experiment, have fun, and take good care of your hair.
