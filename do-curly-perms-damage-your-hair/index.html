Do Curly Perms Damage Your Hair The Truth About Perming

Written by Luffy Jul 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly perms have been around for decades and have been a popular way to add texture and volume to the hair. But, many people wonder if curly perms damage your hair. In this article, we will explore the truth about perming and whether it can cause damage to your hair.

Description

A curly perm is a chemical treatment that is used to add curls or waves to the hair. The process involves applying a solution to the hair that breaks down the hair's protein structure, allowing it to be rearranged into a curly or wavy pattern. After the solution is applied, the hair is wrapped in rods or rollers to set the curls.

How to Get a Curly Perm

If you are interested in getting a curly perm, it is important to find a professional stylist who has experience with perming. The process can take several hours, and it is important to follow the stylist's instructions for aftercare to ensure that your hair stays healthy.

Step by Step Process

1. Consultation with a stylist to discuss the desired outcome and the health of your hair.

2. Shampoo and condition the hair to prepare it for the perm solution.

3. Apply the perm solution to the hair and wrap it around rods or rollers.

4. Process the hair for the recommended time, depending on the hair's texture and the desired outcome.

5. Rinse the hair thoroughly to remove the perm solution.

6. Apply a neutralizer to the hair to re-form the protein structure and set the curls.

7. Rinse the hair again and style as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair After a Curly Perm

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to the hair.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can cause damage to the hair.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.

4. Trim the ends of the hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Can a curly perm cause damage to my hair?

Yes, a curly perm can cause damage to the hair if it is not done properly or if the aftercare instructions are not followed. It is important to find a professional stylist who has experience with perming to ensure that your hair stays healthy.

How long will a curly perm last?

A curly perm can last anywhere from 3-6 months, depending on the hair's texture and the aftercare routine. It is important to avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools during this time to prevent damage to the hair.

Can I color my hair after getting a curly perm?

It is recommended to wait at least two weeks after getting a curly perm before coloring the hair. This will give the hair time to recover from the chemical treatment.

Hair Care Tips for Permed Hair

1. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair gently.

2. Avoid brushing the hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair to prevent frizz.

4. Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage.

Conclusion

In conclusion, a curly perm can cause damage to your hair if it is not done properly or if the aftercare instructions are not followed. It is important to find a professional stylist who has experience with perming to ensure that your hair stays healthy. Follow the tips and hair care advice given in this article to keep your permed hair looking and feeling its best.
