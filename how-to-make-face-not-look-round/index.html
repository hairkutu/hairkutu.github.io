How To Make Your Face Not Look Round Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Mar 21, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round face, you may feel self-conscious about it. But there are ways to make your face look less round and more defined. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the look you want.

Hair Care

Your hair can play a big role in how your face looks. To make your face look less round, try a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head. A pixie cut, a bob with layers, or a long layered cut with waves can all help to elongate your face.

Another tip is to avoid adding too much volume to the sides of your hair, as this can make your face look wider. Instead, focus on adding volume to the top of your head.

Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades of makeup to create the illusion of shadows and depth on your face. By using contouring, you can make your face look slimmer and more defined.

To contour your face, start by applying a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to the areas of your face that you want to slim down, such as your cheeks, jawline, and temples. Then, blend the makeup well to create a natural-looking shadow effect.

Hairstyle

As we mentioned earlier, your hairstyle can play a big role in how your face looks. In addition to adding height to your hair, you can also try parting your hair to the side. This can help to create the illusion of a longer face.

You can also try a side-swept fringe, which can help to frame your face and draw attention away from the roundness.

Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help to tone the muscles in your face and make your face look more defined. One exercise you can try is the cheek puff exercise. To do this, puff out your cheeks and hold for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this exercise several times a day.

You can also try the jawline exercise, which involves clenching your jaw and holding for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this exercise several times a day.

Makeup

In addition to contouring, there are other makeup techniques you can use to make your face look less round. One technique is to use a lighter shade of foundation or concealer on the center of your face, such as your forehead, nose, and chin. This can help to create the illusion of a longer face.

You can also use highlighter on the top of your cheekbones to draw attention upwards and create the illusion of higher cheekbones.

Diet

Your diet can also play a role in how your face looks. To help reduce facial bloating and puffiness, try to avoid foods that are high in sodium and drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

You should also try to eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits and vegetables. These foods are rich in antioxidants, which can help to reduce inflammation and promote healthy skin.

Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for your overall health, but it can also help to reduce facial puffiness and bloating. Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep each night to help keep your skin looking refreshed and healthy.

Hydration

Staying hydrated is important for healthy skin and can also help to reduce facial puffiness. Try to drink at least 8 glasses of water each day, and avoid sugary drinks and caffeine, which can dehydrate you.

Stress Management

Stress can cause a variety of health issues, including skin problems and facial bloating. To help manage stress, try relaxation techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises.

FAQs

Q: Can facial exercises really help to slim down my face?

A: While facial exercises can help to tone the muscles in your face, they are unlikely to make a significant difference in the overall size or shape of your face.

Q: Will contouring make my face look unnatural?

A: With the right technique and blending, contouring can create a natural-looking shadow effect that helps to slim down your face.

Q: How can I tell if a certain hairstyle will flatter my face shape?

A: Generally, hairstyles that add height to the top of your head or elongate your face will flatter a round face shape. You can also consult with a hairstylist for personalized advice.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help to make your face look less round and more defined. Remember, beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, so embrace your unique features and love the skin you’re in!
