How To Cut Long Hair To Shoulder Length At Home

Written by Bardi Mar 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and ready for a change? Cutting your hair from long to shoulder length can be a refreshing and exciting way to switch up your look. And the best part is, you can do it right at home! Follow these simple steps and tips to achieve a gorgeous shoulder-length haircut.

  • How to Cut Long Hair to Shoulder Length at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Hair at Home
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Clips

Now, you're ready to get started:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you have a smooth, even cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, part your hair down the middle and then again from ear to ear. This will create four sections of hair. Use hair ties or clips to secure each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut straight across. Continue doing this with small sections until you have cut all of the back hair to the desired length.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Now, move on to the side sections of your hair. Unclip one side and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across, following the length of the back hair. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

After you have finished cutting all of your hair, check for evenness. Take small sections of hair from each side and compare them. If they are uneven, make small adjustments until they are even.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair at Home

Cutting your own hair can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful shoulder-length haircut. Here are some tips to help you:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Use sharp, precise cuts. This will help you achieve a cleaner, more even haircut.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to take small sections and cut a little at a time.
  • Take breaks if you need them. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take breaks and stretch your arms and hands.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can still cut your hair if it's curly. However, keep in mind that curly hair can shrink up when it dries, so you may want to cut it a bit longer than your desired length.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! It's just hair and it will grow back. You can always make small adjustments to even out any mistakes.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

After you have cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your shoulder-length haircut.
  • Consider using hair masks or oils to nourish your hair and prevent split ends.

Cutting your long hair to shoulder length at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a gorgeous haircut that will have you feeling confident and beautiful.
