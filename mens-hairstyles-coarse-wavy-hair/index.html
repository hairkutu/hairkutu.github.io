Mens Hairstyles For Coarse Wavy Hair

Written by Oliver Apr 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, coarse wavy hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men struggle with finding the right hairstyle for their hair type. In this article, we will explore the best mens hairstyles for coarse wavy hair, as well as provide tips on how to care for and maintain your hair.

Description

Coarse wavy hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a look that is both stylish and manageable. This hair type is characterized by thick, wiry strands that tend to curl or wave, making it prone to frizz and tangling. It requires a bit more maintenance than other hair types, but with the right care, it can be transformed into a stylish and enviable look.

How to

The key to managing coarse wavy hair is finding the right haircut. A short, layered cut is ideal for this hair type, as it helps to tame the curls and waves and create a more manageable look. A longer cut can also work, but it will require more maintenance and styling.

When it comes to styling, it's important to use the right products. A good quality styling cream or pomade can help to define your waves and keep them in place. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your natural waves.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for coarse hair.

2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.

3. Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your hair, working it through from roots to ends.

4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.

5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

- Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it even more unruly.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet, as brushes can cause breakage.

- Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner designed for coarse hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair long if I have coarse wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair long, but it will require more maintenance and styling. A layered cut can help to tame your waves and create a more manageable look.

Q: What products should I use to style my coarse wavy hair?

A: A good quality styling cream or pomade can help to define your waves and keep them in place.

Hair Care

Caring for coarse wavy hair requires a bit more effort than other hair types. It's important to use products designed for coarse hair, such as shampoos and conditioners that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients.

Regular trims are also important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Finally, avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it even more unruly. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every two days.

With these tips and tricks, you can transform your coarse wavy hair into a stylish and manageable look that you'll love.
