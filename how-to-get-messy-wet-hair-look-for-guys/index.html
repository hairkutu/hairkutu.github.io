How To Get Messy Wet Hair Look For Guys

Written by Daniel Jul 11, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that’s low maintenance and easy to style? The messy wet hair look for guys is the perfect choice for those who want a relaxed and effortless look. This hairstyle has been trending for a while now, and it’s not hard to see why. It’s easy to achieve, and it doesn’t require a lot of products or styling tools. In this blog post, we’re going to show you how to get the messy wet hair look for guys in simple steps.

How to Get the Messy Wet Hair Look for Guys

Getting the messy wet hair look is quite easy, and you don’t need to be a hairstylist to do it. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to achieving the messy wet hair look is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair. You can use a gel, pomade, or wax, depending on your hair type and the level of hold you want. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and the ends.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers to style your hair. You can tousle your hair to create a messy look or use a comb to create a more structured look. You can also use a hairdryer to set your hair in place.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place. Don’t use too much hairspray as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Getting the Messy Wet Hair Look for Guys

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect messy wet hair look:

  • Use a lightweight styling product to avoid weighing down your hair
  • Don’t apply too much product as it can make your hair look greasy
  • Use your fingers to style your hair instead of a comb or brush
  • Don’t use hot water to wash your hair as it can dry out your scalp
  • Don’t overdo it with the hairspray

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair to get the messy wet hair look?

You should wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Can I use any styling product to get the messy wet hair look?

You can use any styling product that’s suitable for your hair type, but we recommend using a gel or pomade for a stronger hold.

Can I achieve the messy wet hair look with short hair?

Yes, you can achieve the messy wet hair look with short hair, but you may need to use a stronger hold styling product.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, it’s important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair in good condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can dry out your scalp
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy

With these tips and steps, you can now achieve the perfect messy wet hair look for guys. It’s a low maintenance and effortless hairstyle that’s perfect for any occasion. Try it out and see how it works for you.
