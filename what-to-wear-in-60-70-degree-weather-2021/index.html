What To Wear In 60 70 Degree Weather 2021 Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Aug 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering what to wear in 60-70 degree weather 2021? The temperature range of 60-70 degrees can be quite tricky to dress for, especially if you're someone who wants to look stylish while staying comfortable. Whether you're going for a casual walk, running errands or heading to work, we've got you covered with our tips and tricks on what to wear in 60-70 degree weather.

Description

The temperature range of 60-70 degrees is considered to be mild and comfortable weather. However, dressing for this range can be challenging as the weather can vary throughout the day. You may experience chilly mornings and evenings, while the afternoons can be warm. Therefore, it is essential to choose clothes that are versatile enough to keep you comfortable throughout the day.

How to

When dressing for 60-70 degree weather, it is important to focus on layering. Layering allows you to add or remove clothes depending on the temperature. Here's how you can dress for 60-70 degree weather:

Step 1: Choose a base layer

Choose a comfortable and breathable base layer that can keep you warm in the morning and evening. You can opt for a long-sleeved shirt, a lightweight sweater, or a cardigan.

Step 2: Add a mid-layer

Add a mid-layer such as a vest, a sweatshirt or a light jacket. This layer can be easily removed during the warmer parts of the day.

Step 3: Choose the right pants

Choose pants that are comfortable and lightweight such as jeans, khakis, or leggings. Avoid wearing shorts as they may not provide enough warmth during the cooler parts of the day.

Step 4: Pick the right shoes

Choose shoes that are comfortable and suitable for the weather. Sneakers, loafers or ankle boots are a great option. Avoid wearing sandals or flip flops as they may not provide enough warmth.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when dressing for 60-70 degree weather:

  • Opt for breathable fabrics such as cotton or linen to keep yourself comfortable
  • Accessorize with a scarf, hat or sunglasses to add a touch of style to your outfit
  • Carry a lightweight jacket or a cardigan in case the temperature drops
  • Avoid wearing heavy jackets or coats as they may be too warm for the weather

FAQ

Q. Can I wear shorts in 60-70 degree weather?

A. While you can wear shorts in 60-70 degree weather, it is not recommended as they may not provide enough warmth during the cooler parts of the day.

Q. What type of shoes should I wear in 60-70 degree weather?

A. You can wear sneakers, loafers or ankle boots in 60-70 degree weather. Avoid wearing sandals or flip flops as they may not provide enough warmth.

Q. How can I add a touch of style to my outfit?

A. You can add a scarf, hat or sunglasses to your outfit to add a touch of style.

Hair Care

When dressing for 60-70 degree weather, it is important to keep your hair in mind. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Avoid using too many hair styling products as they can weigh your hair down
  • Use a lightweight hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Opt for a messy bun or a braid to keep your hair off your face

Dressing for 60-70 degree weather can be fun and easy if you keep these tips and tricks in mind. Remember to focus on layering, choose breathable fabrics and accessorize with a scarf, hat or sunglasses to add a touch of style to your outfit. And don't forget to take care of your hair by using lightweight hair products and opting for a messy bun or a braid.
