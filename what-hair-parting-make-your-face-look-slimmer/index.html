What Hair Parting Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are looking for a way to make your face look slimmer without going under the knife, you might want to try changing your hair parting. The right hair parting can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones, making your face look slimmer and more sculpted. In this article, we will discuss what hair parting make your face look slimmer and how to achieve it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Part Your Hair to Make Your Face Look Slimmer
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Parting
  • FAQ

How to Part Your Hair to Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Before we dive into the different hair parting options, it's important to understand that the right parting for you will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are a few general tips to keep in mind:

  • A deep side parting can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones.
  • A center parting can elongate your face and make it look slimmer.
  • If you have a round face, avoid parting your hair in the middle as it can make your face look wider.
  • If you have a square face, avoid a sharp middle parting as it can make your face look more angular.

Now that you have a better idea of what to look for, let's explore the different hair parting options.

Deep Side Parting

A deep side parting can instantly make your face look slimmer and more defined. To create a deep side parting, use a comb to create a straight line from the arch of your eyebrow to the back of your head. Then, use your fingers to comb your hair over to one side, creating a deep parting.

Off-Center Parting

An off-center parting can also help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. To create an off-center parting, use a comb to create a diagonal line from the arch of your eyebrow to the crown of your head. Then, use your fingers to comb your hair over to one side, creating an off-center parting.

Center Parting

If you have a long face, a center parting can help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer. To create a center parting, use a comb to create a straight line from the center of your forehead to the crown of your head. Then, use your fingers to comb your hair over to either side of the parting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you create the perfect hair parting:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  3. Decide on the type of parting you want to create.
  4. Use a comb to create the parting.
  5. Use your fingers to comb your hair over to either side of the parting.
  6. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a sleek finish.
  7. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the parting in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Parting

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your hair parting:

  • Use a tail comb to touch up your parting if it starts to shift throughout the day.
  • Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your parting to keep it in place.
  • If you have fine hair, avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down and cause your parting to collapse.
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls and maintain your parting.

FAQ

What is the best hair parting for a round face?

If you have a round face, a deep side parting can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Avoid parting your hair in the middle as it can make your face look wider.

What is the best hair parting for a long face?

If you have a long face, a center parting can help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

Can changing my hair parting really make my face look slimmer?

Yes, changing your hair parting can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones, making your face look slimmer and more sculpted.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to help protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and experimenting with different hair parting options, you can create a look that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
