Would Short Hair Suit Me Male

Written by Petter May 13, 2023 · 8 min read

Short hair is a great choice for men who want a low-maintenance look that is stylish and modern. However, not all men can pull off the short hair look. If you're wondering, "Would short hair suit me male?" the answer depends on several factors, including your face shape, hair type, and personal style preferences. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and advice on determining whether short hair is right for you.

How to Determine if Short Hair is Right for You

Before making the decision to cut your hair short, it's important to consider whether it will suit your face shape and hair type. Here are some steps you can take to determine whether short hair is right for you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining whether short hair will suit you is to determine your face shape. There are several different face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has different haircuts that suit it best.

If you're not sure what your face shape is, try this simple test: stand in front of a mirror and use a washable marker to trace the outline of your face onto the mirror. Step back and look at the shape you've traced. This will give you an idea of your face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step is to consider your hair type. Short hair works best for men with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly or kinky hair, it may be more difficult to style short hair. However, there are still options for men with curly or kinky hair, such as a curly undercut or textured crop.

Step 3: Look at Short Hair Styles

Once you've determined your face shape and hair type, it's time to look at short hair styles. There are many different short hair styles for men, including the buzz cut, crew cut, textured crop, and undercut. Look at pictures of men with similar face shapes and hair types to see what short hair styles would suit you.

Tips for Pulling Off Short Hair

If you've determined that short hair is right for you, here are some tips for pulling off the look:

  • Choose a short hair style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Visit a reputable barber who specializes in short haircuts.
  • Maintain your short hair with regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the right one for your hair type.
  • Consider adding some texture to your short hair with a matte finish product.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair for men:

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: Short hair can make a face look rounder, but it depends on the cut. If you have a round face, avoid getting a buzz cut or a crew cut, as these styles can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for a textured crop or an undercut.

Q: How short should I go?

A: The length of your short hair will depend on your personal style and face shape. Generally, short hair is defined as hair that is less than 2 inches long. However, you may want to go shorter or longer depending on the style you choose.

Q: Can I still style my short hair?

A: Yes, you can still style your short hair. Use a styling product that is suitable for your hair type and follow the instructions on the label.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair is low-maintenance, but it still requires some care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a gentle shampoo.
  • Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great choice for men who want a low-maintenance and stylish look. To determine whether short hair is right for you, consider your face shape and hair type, and look at different short hair styles. If you decide to go for a short haircut, follow our tips for pulling off the look and taking care of your hair.
