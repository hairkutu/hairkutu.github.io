How To Know What Type Of Face Shape You Have

Written by Luffy Apr 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Knowing what type of face shape you have is essential when it comes to choosing hairstyles, makeup, and glasses that complement your features. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to determine your face shape. We will also share some tips and answer frequently asked questions, so keep reading to find out more.

  • Step 2: Identify Your Face Shape

Step 1: Measure Your Face

The first step in determining your face shape is to measure your face using a soft measuring tape or ruler. You will need to take the following measurements:

  • Forehead: Measure across your forehead at the widest point, which is usually just below your hairline.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones at the widest point, just below the outer corner of your eyes.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw starts to angle up.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have taken these measurements, write them down so you can refer to them later.

Step 2: Identify Your Face Shape

Now that you have your measurements, it's time to identify your face shape. There are six basic face shapes:

Oval Face Shape

If your face length is longer than your cheekbones, and your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have an oval face shape. This is considered the most versatile face shape, as most hairstyles and makeup looks will suit you.

Round Face Shape

If your face length and cheekbone measurements are similar, and your forehead and jawline are also similar in width, you have a round face shape. To elongate your face, choose hairstyles with height at the crown and avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin.

Square Face Shape

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width, you have a square face shape. To soften your features, choose hairstyles with soft layers and avoid blunt cuts.

Heart Face Shape

If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, and your chin is pointed, you have a heart face shape. To balance your features, choose hairstyles that add volume at the jawline and avoid heavy bangs.

Diamond Face Shape

If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower, you have a diamond face shape. To soften your features, choose hairstyles with soft layers and avoid high volume at the crown.

Pear Face Shape

If your jawline is wider than your forehead and your cheekbones, you have a pear face shape. To balance your features, choose hairstyles that add volume at the crown and avoid styles that accentuate the width of your jawline.

Tips on Choosing Hairstyles and Makeup

Now that you know your face shape, here are some tips on choosing hairstyles and makeup:

  • Oval: Most hairstyles and makeup looks will suit you, so experiment with different styles.
  • Round: Choose hairstyles with height at the crown and avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin. Use contouring techniques to add definition to your face.
  • Square: Soften your features with hairstyles that have soft layers. Use blush to add softness to your cheeks.
  • Heart: Add volume at the jawline with hairstyles, and avoid heavy bangs that accentuate your forehead. Use highlighter to accentuate your cheekbones.
  • Diamond: Soften your features with soft layers, and avoid high volume at the crown. Use bronzer to add definition to your cheekbones.
  • Pear: Add volume at the crown with hairstyles, and avoid styles that accentuate the width of your jawline. Use highlighter to accentuate your cheekbones.

FAQs about Face Shapes

Q: Can face shape change over time?

A: No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure, which does not change over time.

Q: Can hairstyles change the appearance of my face shape?

A: Yes, hairstyles can create the illusion of a different face shape. For example, adding volume at the crown can elongate a round face shape.

Q: Is there a perfect face shape?

A: No, all face shapes are beautiful and unique in their own way.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Here are some general hair care tips for all face shapes:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use heat protectant before styling with hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and identifying your face shape, you can choose hairstyles and makeup that enhance your natural beauty. Remember, every face shape is unique and beautiful, so embrace yours and rock it with confidence!
