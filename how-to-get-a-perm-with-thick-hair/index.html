How To Get A Perm With Thick Hair A Step By Step Guide

If you have thick hair and have always wanted to try a perm, you may be wondering if it's possible. The good news is that it is! With the right technique and products, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that last for months. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of getting a perm with thick hair, including tips and tricks for best results.

How to Get a Perm with Thick Hair

Getting a perm with thick hair requires a bit of extra care and attention, as the hair is more resistant to curling. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Salon

Not all salons are created equal when it comes to perms. Look for a salon that has experience working with thick hair and that uses high-quality products. You may need to pay a bit more for a good perm, but it's worth it to get the results you want.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

Before getting a perm, consult with your stylist about what you want and whether it's possible. They can help you choose the right size of rods for your hair type and length, and they can also give you advice on how to care for your perm after it's done.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

On the day of your appointment, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Don't use any styling products, as these can interfere with the perm process. Your stylist may also apply a protective cream to your scalp to prevent irritation.

Step 4: Wrap Your Hair

Your stylist will section your hair and wrap it around perm rods. The size of the rods will depend on the type of curl you want. For tight curls, smaller rods are used, while larger rods create looser waves.

Step 5: Apply the Perm Solution

Your stylist will apply a perm solution to your hair, which will break down the bonds of your hair and allow it to take on the shape of the rods. This process can take up to 30 minutes, depending on the thickness of your hair.

Step 6: Rinse and Neutralize

After the perm solution has been applied, your hair will be rinsed thoroughly to remove any excess chemicals. Then, a neutralizing solution will be applied to lock in the new shape of your hair.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Your stylist will blow-dry your hair and style it as desired. Keep in mind that your perm may look a bit frizzy at first, but this will settle down in a few days.

Tips for Best Results

Here are some tips to help you get the best possible results from your perm:

  • Choose a salon that has experience working with thick hair
  • Be clear with your stylist about what you want
  • Don't use any styling products on the day of your appointment
  • Consider a protein treatment before your perm to strengthen your hair
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after your perm
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your curls
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as these can damage your perm

FAQs

Is it possible to get a perm with thick hair?

Yes, it is possible to get a perm with thick hair. However, it may require a bit of extra care and attention to get the best results.

What size of perm rods should I use for thick hair?

The size of perm rods you use will depend on the type of curl you want. For tight curls, smaller rods are used, while larger rods create looser waves. Your stylist can help you choose the right size of rods for your hair type and length.

How long will my perm last?

A perm can last for several months, but the exact length of time will depend on your hair type and how well you care for your perm. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, and consider getting regular protein treatments to help maintain your curls.

Hair Care After a Perm

After getting a perm, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain your curls. Here are some tips:

  • Don't wash your hair for at least 24 hours after your perm
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as these can damage your perm
  • Consider getting a regular protein treatment to strengthen your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause frizz

With these tips and tricks, you can get a beautiful perm with thick hair that lasts for months. Remember to choose the right salon, consult with your stylist, and take good care of your hair after your perm to maintain your curls.
