Short Hairstyles For Diamond Face Male How To Find The Perfect Look

Written by Luffy Mar 28, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're a man with a diamond-shaped face, you're in luck. Your face shape is considered one of the most versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, with so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect look for you. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the best short hairstyles for diamond face male.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Short Hairstyles for Diamond Face Male
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyles

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best short hairstyles for diamond face male, it's important to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is to stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to take the following measurements:

  • Forehead: Measure from the peak of one eyebrow arch to the peak of the opposite arch.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to below your ear at the point where your jaw angles upwards. Multiply that number by two to get your jawline measurement.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, use the following chart to determine your face shape:

If you have a diamond-shaped face, your cheekbones will be the widest part of your face and your chin and forehead will be narrow.

Short Hairstyles for Diamond Face Male

As mentioned earlier, diamond face male can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, short hairstyles tend to work best as they help balance out the wider cheekbones.

1. Short Textured Crop

This hairstyle features short, textured hair on top with a fade on the sides. The textured hair on top adds volume and height to the crown, while the fade helps balance out the wider cheekbones.

2. Side-Parted Short Hair

This classic hairstyle features short hair with a side part. The side part adds dimension to the hair and helps balance out the wider cheekbones. This style also works well with a fade on the sides.

3. Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a great option for diamond face male. The short hair all around helps balance out the wider cheekbones and creates a more angular look.

4. Short Quiff

A short quiff features short hair on the sides and back with a longer, textured top. The longer top adds height and volume to the crown, which helps balance out the wider cheekbones. This style also works well with a fade on the sides.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a short hairstyle for diamond face male, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as full fringes or wide sideburns.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head, such as textured crops or quiffs.
  • Consider a fade on the sides to balance out wider cheekbones.

FAQ

What other face shapes can pull off short hairstyles?

Short hairstyles tend to work well for oval, square, and heart-shaped faces as well.

Can I still wear longer hairstyles with a diamond-shaped face?

Absolutely! While short hairstyles tend to work best for diamond-shaped faces, longer hairstyles can still look great. Just be sure to avoid styles that add width to the sides of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyles

Once you've found the perfect short hairstyle for your diamond face male, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

With these tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect short hairstyle for your diamond face male. Remember to choose a style that works best for your personal taste and lifestyle.
