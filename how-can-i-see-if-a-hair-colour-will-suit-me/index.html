How Can I See If A Hair Colour Will Suit Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing a new hair colour can be a daunting task, especially when you're not sure if it will suit you. With so many colours and shades to choose from, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. However, with a few simple tips and tricks, you can easily find the perfect hair colour for you. In this article, we'll explore how you can see if a hair colour will suit you and provide you with some helpful tips along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How To See If A Hair Colour Will Suit Me
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips For Choosing The Right Hair Colour
  • FAQs About Choosing A Hair Colour

How To See If A Hair Colour Will Suit Me

Before choosing a new hair colour, it's important to determine your skin tone and undertones. Skin tone can be classified into three categories: warm, cool, and neutral. To find your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If you can't tell, you probably have a neutral undertone.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start selecting hair colours that complement it. If you have a cool undertone, look for hair colours with ash or cool undertones. If you have a warm undertone, look for hair colours with golden or warm undertones. If you have a neutral undertone, you can choose pretty much any hair colour.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to see if a hair colour will suit you:

  1. Determine your skin tone and undertones.
  2. Look for hair colours that complement your skin tone and undertones.
  3. Consider your natural hair colour and eye colour.
  4. Choose a shade that's no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair colour.
  5. Do a strand test to see how the colour will look on your hair.
  6. Consult a professional stylist if you're unsure about which colour to choose.

Tips For Choosing The Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle and whether you're willing to maintain your new hair colour.
  • Choose a colour that flatters your eye colour.
  • Take into account your skin's undertones and whether you want to enhance or counteract them.
  • Try temporary hair colour before committing to a permanent colour.
  • Consult a professional stylist for advice on which colours will suit you best.

FAQs About Choosing A Hair Colour

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair colour:

What's the best hair colour for my skin tone?

The best hair colour for your skin tone depends on whether you have a warm, cool, or neutral undertone. If you have a cool undertone, look for hair colours with ash or cool undertones. If you have a warm undertone, look for hair colours with golden or warm undertones. If you have a neutral undertone, you can choose pretty much any hair colour.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it's important to choose a hair dye that's free of harsh chemicals and irritants. Look for hair dyes that are labeled "sensitive" or "gentle" and always do a patch test before dyeing your hair.

How often should I touch up my roots?

The frequency of root touch-ups depends on the colour of your hair and how fast it grows. For natural hair colours, touch-ups are usually needed every 4-6 weeks. For brighter or more dramatic colours, touch-ups may be needed every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care Tips After Colouring Your Hair

Once you've coloured your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure the colour lasts as long as possible. Here are some hair care tips for after colouring your hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily find a hair colour that suits you and keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Remember, choosing a hair colour is a personal decision, so don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
