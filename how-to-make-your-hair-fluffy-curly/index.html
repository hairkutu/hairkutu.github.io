How To Make Your Hair Fluffy Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to get your curls to look bouncy, fluffy and defined. But with the right products and techniques, you can achieve those gorgeous, fluffy curls that you've always wanted. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair fluffy curly in a few easy steps.

How to Make Your Hair Fluffy Curly

Fluffy curls are all about creating volume and bounce in your hair. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

The first step to achieving fluffy curls is to start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, and then towel dry it gently. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause frizz.


Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product

Next, apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This can be a mousse, a gel, or a cream, depending on your hair type and the look you're going for. Work the product through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.


Step 3: Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer

Using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help to enhance your natural curls and add volume. Flip your head upside down and dry your hair on a low heat setting, using the diffuser to gently scrunch your hair as you go.


Step 4: Finish with a hairspray

To help your curls hold their shape, finish with a light hairspray. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your head and mist your hair lightly. Avoid spraying too much, as this can weigh your hair down.


Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a detailed step-by-step guide to help you make your hair fluffy curly:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any buildup and prepare your hair for styling.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove any excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse, a gel, or a cream. Work the product through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser Attachment on Your Hair Dryer

Using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help to enhance your natural curls and add volume. Flip your head upside down and dry your hair on a low heat setting, using the diffuser to gently scrunch your hair as you go.

Step 5: Finish with a Hairspray

To help your curls hold their shape, finish with a light hairspray. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your head and mist your hair lightly. Avoid spraying too much, as this can weigh your hair down.


Tips for Fluffy Curls

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a towel, as this can help to reduce frizz.
  • Don't touch your hair too much while it's drying, as this can disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Experiment with different curl-enhancing products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing spray or powder to add extra lift.

FAQs

How can I make my curls last longer?

To make your curls last longer, try sleeping with your hair in a pineapple or a loose bun. This can help to preserve the curl pattern and reduce frizz.

Can I use a diffuser on straight hair?

Yes, you can use a diffuser on straight hair to add volume and texture.

Can I use a hair dryer without a diffuser?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer without a diffuser, but this may cause your curls to become frizzy.


Hair Care for Fluffy Curls

To keep your fluffy curls looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.

Now that you know how to make your hair fluffy curly, it's time to give it a try. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve those gorgeous, bouncy curls that you've always wanted.
