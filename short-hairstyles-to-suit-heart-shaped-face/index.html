Short Hairstyles To Suit Heart Shaped Face

Written by Christine Jun 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you are lucky because it is one of the most versatile face shapes that can carry a variety of hairstyles. However, if you prefer short hair, then you need to be careful when selecting a hairstyle that will complement your face shape. Short hairstyles for heart shaped face are plenty, and in this article, we will discuss some of the best ones that you can try out.

Description

A heart shaped face has a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The aim of any hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the forehead and the chin. Short hairstyles are perfect for this face shape because they draw attention to the eyes and create an illusion of width around the jawline.

How to

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle for a heart shaped face is to determine the length. A short bob or a pixie cut are both excellent options for this face shape. The next step is to decide on the style.

Step by step

Step 1: Determine the length of your hair.

Step 2: Choose a style that complements your face shape.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to ensure that the style you have chosen suits you.

Step 4: Maintain the hairstyle by trimming it regularly.

Tips

When selecting a short hairstyle for a heart shaped face, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that adds volume to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid styles that are too close to the head, as they can make your face look longer.
  • Layering is ideal for this face shape as it adds volume to the hair.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle as they can soften the forehead and balance out the face.

FAQ

1. Which short hairstyles are best for heart shaped faces?

A pixie cut or a short bob are both excellent options for this face shape.

2. Should I add bangs to my short hairstyle?

Yes, adding bangs can help soften the forehead and balance out the face.

3. Can I have a short hairstyle if I have a heart shaped face?

Absolutely! Short hairstyles are perfect for this face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your short hairstyle, you need to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

In conclusion, short hairstyles are perfect for heart shaped faces. When selecting a hairstyle, choose a style that complements your face shape and adds volume to the crown of your head. Consult with a hairstylist to ensure that the style you have chosen suits you, and take good care of your hair to maintain your hairstyle.
