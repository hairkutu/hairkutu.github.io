Mens Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby May 26, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you’re in luck! Your hair is one of the most versatile types out there, giving you plenty of styling options. In this guide, we’ll go over some of the best mens hairstyles for thick wavy hair, as well as some tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking healthy and full.

Table of Contents:

  • How To: Styling Men’s Hair with Thick Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step: How to Achieve the Perfect Mens Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care: Essential Products for Thick Wavy Hair

How To: Styling Men’s Hair with Thick Wavy Hair

When it comes to styling your hair, it’s important to find a style that suits your hair type. With thick wavy hair, you have plenty of options, from short and messy to long and flowing. Here are some of the most popular men’s hairstyles for thick wavy hair:

  • The Messy Quiff
  • The Classic Side Part
  • The Textured Crop
  • The Man Bun
  • The Wavy Pompadour
  • The Brushed Back

Each of these styles has its own unique look and requires different techniques for styling. Experiment with different techniques to find a style that suits you.

Step-by-Step: How to Achieve the Perfect Mens Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

Here’s a step-by-step guide for achieving two of the most popular mens hairstyles for thick wavy hair:

The Messy Quiff:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Using a hair dryer, blow-dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and tousle your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to create a deep side part.
  5. Using your fingers, tousle the top of your hair to create a messy, textured look.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

The Textured Crop:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Using a hair dryer, blow-dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and tousle your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to create a deep side part.
  5. Using a pair of scissors, trim the sides and back of your hair to a short length.
  6. Leave the top of your hair longer, and use a texturizing product to create a messy, textured look.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair

Maintaining thick wavy hair requires a bit of extra effort, but it’s worth it to keep your hair looking healthy and full. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for thick, wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and make your hair frizzy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush, as it can break your hair and cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for thick wavy hair?

The best haircut for thick wavy hair depends on your personal style and face shape. Some popular haircuts for thick wavy hair include the messy quiff, the classic side part, and the textured crop.

How do I style my thick wavy hair?

To style thick wavy hair, start by washing and towel-drying your hair. Apply a styling product to your hair, and use a hair dryer to blow-dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and tousle your hair. Experiment with different styles to find one that suits you.

Hair Care: Essential Products for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some essential hair care products for maintaining thick wavy hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner for thick wavy hair
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Hair oil
  • Texturizing spray or mousse
  • Hairspray

Investing in these products will help keep your hair looking healthy and full.

In conclusion, with the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care products, men with thick wavy hair can achieve a variety of stylish looks. Experiment with different styles to find one that suits you, and don’t forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and full.
