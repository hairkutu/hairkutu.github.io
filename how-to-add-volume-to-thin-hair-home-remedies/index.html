How To Add Volume To Thin Hair Home Remedies

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thin hair, you may feel like it's impossible to get the full and voluminous look you desire. But there are actually several home remedies you can try to add volume to your hair without having to spend a lot of money on expensive products or treatments. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to add volume to thin hair using natural ingredients and techniques.

Description

Thin hair can be caused by a number of factors, including genetics, age, and hormonal changes. While there is no cure for thinning hair, there are ways to make your hair appear fuller and more voluminous. Many people turn to hair products or styling techniques to achieve this look, but these can often be damaging to your hair over time. By using natural home remedies, you can add volume to your hair without causing any harm.

How to Add Volume to Thin Hair Home Remedies

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

The first step in adding volume to your hair is to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are specially formulated to plump up your hair strands and create the illusion of thicker hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, or collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair over time.

2. Try a hair mask

A hair mask is a deep conditioning treatment that can help nourish your hair and add volume. You can make your own hair mask at home using ingredients like avocado, coconut oil, or egg whites. Apply the mask to your hair once a week and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Use a volumizing mousse or spray

A volumizing mousse or spray can help give your hair a lift and make it appear fuller. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair using your fingers or a comb. Be sure to choose a product that won't weigh your hair down or make it look greasy.

4. Blow dry your hair upside down

Blow drying your hair upside down can help create volume at the roots. Start by flipping your head upside down and blow drying your hair until it's about 80% dry. Then flip your head back up and use a round brush to finish drying your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Start by wetting your hair and applying a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Massage the products into your hair and scalp, then rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Apply a hair mask.

If you're using a hair mask, apply it to your hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 3: Apply a volumizing mousse or spray.

Once your hair is clean and conditioned, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and work it through your hair using your fingers or a comb.

Step 4: Blow dry your hair upside down.

Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair until it's about 80% dry. Then flip your head back up and use a round brush to finish drying your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 5: Style as desired.

Once your hair is dry, style it as desired using a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tools.

Tips

  • Avoid using heavy products like oils or serums, which can weigh your hair down.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too often, as this can cause breakage and make your hair appear thinner.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, which can help promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Q: Can I use these remedies if I have color-treated hair?

A: Yes, these remedies are safe to use on color-treated hair. However, be sure to choose products that are specifically formulated for color-treated hair.

Q: How often should I use a hair mask?

A: You can use a hair mask once a week or as often as needed, depending on the condition of your hair.

Q: Will these remedies work for all hair types?

A: These remedies can work for all hair types, but the results may vary depending on the thickness and texture of your hair.

Hair Care

In addition to these home remedies, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and voluminous. Be sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools or harsh chemicals on your hair. Eat a healthy diet and get plenty of rest to promote healthy hair growth, and consider taking supplements like biotin or collagen to strengthen your hair from the inside out.
