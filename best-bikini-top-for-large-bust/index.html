The Best Bikini Top For Large Bust A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Jun 29, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a large bust, you know how hard it can be to find a bikini top that fits comfortably and looks great. Luckily, there are plenty of options out there that are designed specifically for women with larger chests. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the best bikini tops for large busts, as well as offer tips for finding the perfect fit and taking care of your swimsuit.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Bikini Top for Large Busts
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Finding the Right Fit
  • Tips for Taking Care of Your Bikini Top
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for the Beach

How to Choose the Best Bikini Top for Large Busts

When shopping for a bikini top, there are a few things to keep in mind if you have a larger bust:

  • Look for tops with underwire or molded cups for added support.
  • Avoid tops with thin straps, as they may dig into your shoulders and cause discomfort.
  • Bralette-style tops may not offer enough support, so opt for a more structured style.
  • Consider tops with adjustable straps, as they can be customized for the perfect fit.
  • Choose a top with a thicker band around the back and sides for added support.

Step-by-Step Guide for Finding the Right Fit

Follow these steps to find the best bikini top for your body:

  1. Measure your bust size to determine your cup size. Use a measuring tape and measure around the fullest part of your bust.
  2. Take note of your band size as well. This is the measurement around your ribcage, just under your bust.
  3. When shopping for a bikini top, look for one that corresponds with your cup and band size.
  4. Try on the top and adjust the straps and band as needed for the perfect fit.
  5. Make sure the top feels secure and supportive, without any gaps or spillage.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Bikini Top

Once you've found the perfect bikini top, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure it lasts for many beach trips to come:

  • Rinse your top thoroughly with cold water after each use to remove any sand, salt, or chlorine.
  • Avoid using harsh detergents or fabric softeners when washing your bikini top, as they can damage the fabric and elasticity.
  • Hang your top to dry in a shaded area, as direct sunlight can fade the color and weaken the fabric.
  • Avoid wringing out your top, as this can damage the elastic and shape of the cups.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about finding the best bikini top for large busts:

  • Q: Can I wear a bandeau-style top if I have a large bust?
    A: It's possible, but it may not offer enough support. Look for a bandeau-style top with underwire or molded cups for added support.
  • Q: Do I need to spend a lot of money to find a good bikini top for a large bust?
    A: Not necessarily. There are plenty of affordable options out there that offer great support and fit.
  • Q: Can I wear a halter-style top if I have broad shoulders?
    A: Yes, but make sure the straps are wide enough to distribute the weight of your bust evenly.

Hair Care Tips for the Beach

Don't forget about your hair when hitting the beach! Here are a few tips for keeping your locks looking great:

  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to protect your hair from the sun and salt water.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers, straighteners, or curling irons on damp hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Opt for a chic and practical hairstyle, like a messy bun or braid, to keep your hair out of your face and off your neck.
  • Bring a wide-brimmed hat or scarf to protect your scalp from the sun.

With these tips and recommendations, you're sure to find the best bikini top for your large bust and look and feel great at the beach.
