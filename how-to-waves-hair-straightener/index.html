How To Create Waves With A Hair Straightener

Written by Robby Aug 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to add some waves to your hair, using a hair straightener is a great option. With a little practice, you can create beautiful beachy waves or defined curls using a straightener. In this article, we'll guide you through the process step-by-step so you can learn how to waves hair straightener.

How to:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. This will help to prevent damage to your hair from the heat of the straightener. Blow-dry your hair or let it air dry until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. This will make it easier to manage and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Straightener

Turn on your hair straightener and set it to the temperature that's appropriate for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a lower temperature, and if you have thick hair, use a higher temperature.

Step 4: Start Waving Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp your straightener down on it near the root. Twist the straightener away from your face while sliding it down to the end of the hair. Repeat this process on all of the sections of hair until you've waved all of them.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've finished waving your hair, use a light hold hairspray to set the waves in place. Spray from a distance to avoid making your hair too stiff.

Tips:

  • For beachy waves, alternate the direction in which you twist the straightener for each section of hair.
  • For defined curls, twist the straightener in the same direction for each section of hair.
  • Use a comb to separate the waves and create a more natural look.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ:

Can you use any type of straightener to create waves?

Yes, you can use any type of straightener to create waves. However, a straightener with a rounded edge will be easier to use and will create more natural-looking waves.

How long do the waves last?

The waves should last for several hours, or until you wash your hair. If you want them to last longer, use a stronger hold hairspray or a texturizing spray.

Hair Care:

While using a straightener to create waves is a quick and easy way to style your hair, it's important to take care of your hair as well. Make sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use them frequently, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Learning how to waves hair straightener is a great way to add some variety to your hair styling routine. With a little practice, you can create beautiful waves that will last for hours. Remember to take care of your hair and use a heat protectant to prevent damage.
