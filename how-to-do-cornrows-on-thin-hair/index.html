How To Do Cornrows On Thin Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cornrows are a popular hairstyle that have been around for centuries. They are not only stylish, but they also help to protect your hair from damage. However, if you have thin hair, you may be wondering if cornrows are even possible. The good news is that cornrows can be done on thin hair, but there are a few things you need to keep in mind. In this article, we will show you how to do cornrows on thin hair, step by step.

How to:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start braiding, it's important to make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is dirty or greasy, it will be harder to braid and the style will not last as long. To prep your hair, wash it with a clarifying shampoo and condition it. Once your hair is clean, detangle it with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you will need to section your hair. Use a rat-tail comb to create a part down the middle of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, create two more parts, one on each side of the middle part. You should now have three sections of hair.

Step 3: Begin Braiding

Now it's time to start braiding. Take a small section of hair from the front of the middle section and divide it into three equal parts. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. Repeat this process, adding more hair to each section as you go. Keep the braids tight to prevent them from slipping out of place.

Step 4: Continue Braiding

Continue braiding the middle section of your hair until you reach the nape of your neck. Then, secure the braid with a small elastic band. Repeat this process on the other two sections of hair, making sure to keep the braids tight and neat.

Step 5: Finish the Style

Once you have braided all three sections of hair, it's time to finish the style. You can leave the braids as they are or you can create a more intricate style by weaving the braids together. Use bobby pins to secure the braids in place and spray them with hairspray to keep them in place.

Tips:

  • Use a detangling spray to make it easier to comb through your hair.
  • If you have very thin hair, consider adding hair extensions to add volume and length to your braids.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if your first attempt at cornrows doesn't turn out the way you want it to. Keep practicing and you'll get better.

FAQ:

Can I do cornrows on very thin hair?

Yes, you can do cornrows on very thin hair. However, you may need to use extensions to add volume and length to your braids.

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last up to six weeks if properly cared for.

Is it okay to sleep with cornrows?

Yes, it is okay to sleep with cornrows. Just make sure to tie your hair up with a satin scarf to prevent frizz and breakage.

Hair Care:

To keep your cornrows looking fresh and neat, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly with a light oil, such as coconut oil or jojoba oil.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • When it's time to take out your cornrows, be gentle to avoid pulling and breaking your hair.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve beautiful cornrows on thin hair. Remember to take your time and be patient, and soon you'll be rocking a stylish and protective hairstyle!
