How Can I Fix My Brassy Hair At Home

Written by Aprilia Aug 30, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’ve recently dyed your hair blonde or lightened your natural hair color, you may find that it’s turned brassy over time. Brassiness can occur when the color oxidizes and starts to turn yellow or orange. This can be frustrating, especially if you’ve spent a lot of money at the salon. Luckily, you can fix brassy hair at home without having to go back to the salon. Here’s how.

Description

Brassy hair is a common problem that can occur when you dye your hair blonde or lighten your natural hair color. It happens when the color oxidizes and starts to turn yellow or orange. This can be caused by a number of factors, including exposure to the sun, hard water, and the type of hair products you use. Fortunately, there are several ways to fix brassy hair at home.

How to Fix Brassy Hair at Home

There are several ways to fix brassy hair at home. Here are some of the most effective methods:

Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a type of shampoo that contains purple pigments. These pigments help to neutralize the yellow and orange tones in your hair, leaving it looking more cool-toned. To use a purple shampoo, wet your hair and massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair. Leave it on for 3-5 minutes, then rinse it out. Use it once or twice a week to maintain the results.

Try a Toning Treatment

A toning treatment is a type of hair mask that contains purple or blue pigments. These pigments help to neutralize the yellow and orange tones in your hair, leaving it looking more cool-toned. To use a toning treatment, apply it to your hair and leave it on for the recommended amount of time. Rinse it out and style your hair as usual. Use it once a week to maintain the results.

Use Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is a natural remedy that can help to remove brassiness from your hair. Mix equal parts apple cider vinegar and water in a spray bottle. Spray it onto your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes. Rinse it out and style your hair as usual. Use it once a week to maintain the results.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to fix brassy hair at home:

Step 1: Choose a Method

Choose a method that works best for you. You can use a purple shampoo, a toning treatment, or apple cider vinegar.

Step 2: Gather Your Supplies

Gather the supplies you’ll need, including the product you’ve chosen, a towel, and a comb or brush.

Step 3: Wet Your Hair

Wet your hair in the shower or sink. Make sure it’s completely saturated.

Step 4: Apply the Product

Apply the product to your hair, making sure to cover all areas. Massage it into your scalp and hair.

Step 5: Wait

Wait for the recommended amount of time. This can vary depending on the product you’re using.

Step 6: Rinse

Rinse the product out of your hair with warm water. Make sure to get all of it out.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as usual. You should notice a difference in the brassiness of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you fix brassy hair at home:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent further damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause further damage.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you’re outside to prevent your hair from getting too much sun exposure.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

What causes brassy hair?

Brassy hair can be caused by a number of factors, including exposure to the sun, hard water, and the type of hair products you use.

Can I fix brassy hair at home?

Yes, you can fix brassy hair at home using methods like a purple shampoo, a toning treatment, or apple cider vinegar.

How often should I use a toning treatment?

You should use a toning treatment once a week to maintain the results.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to prevent brassiness and other issues. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and using the methods outlined above, you can fix brassy hair at home and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
