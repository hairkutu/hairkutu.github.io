Best Short Hairstyles For Over 40

Written by Daniel Aug 31, 2023 · 10 min read

As we age, our hair changes, and we need to adjust our hairstyles accordingly. For women over 40, short hairstyles can be a great option as they are easy to maintain, look stylish, and can make you look younger. In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for over 40 and provide tips on how to style them.

  • The Pixie

The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages, including those over 40. It is a versatile cut that can be worn straight or wavy, and it can be styled with or without bangs. A bob that hits just above the shoulders is a great option as it frames the face and can make you look younger.

To style a bob, use a round brush to blow dry it straight or use a curling iron to add some waves. You can also add some texture with a texturizing spray or pomade.

The Pixie

The pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance hairstyle that can be a great option for women over 40. It is a bold and edgy look that can make you look younger and more modern. The pixie cut can be styled with or without bangs, and it can be worn straight or with some texture.

To style a pixie cut, use a styling cream or gel to add some texture and definition. You can also use a flat iron to straighten it or a curling iron to add some waves.

The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a great option for women over 40 who want a short hairstyle that is not too short. It is a versatile cut that can be worn straight or wavy, and it can be styled with or without bangs. The lob is a great option for women who want to keep some length but still have a short hairstyle.

To style a lob, use a round brush to blow dry it straight or use a curling iron to add some waves. You can also add some texture with a texturizing spray or pomade.

The Shag

The shag is a layered hairstyle that can be a great option for women over 40. It is a versatile cut that can be worn straight or wavy, and it can be styled with or without bangs. The shag is a great option for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish.

To style a shag, use a texturizing spray or pomade to add some texture and definition. You can also use a flat iron to straighten it or a curling iron to add some waves.

The Fauxhawk

The fauxhawk is a short, edgy hairstyle that can be a great option for women over 40 who want to make a bold statement. It is a modern take on the classic mohawk, and it can be worn with or without bangs. The fauxhawk is a great option for women who want to keep some length but still have a short, edgy hairstyle.

To style a fauxhawk, use a styling cream or gel to add some texture and definition. You can also use a flat iron to straighten it or a curling iron to add some waves.

Curls

If you have naturally curly hair, a short hairstyle can be a great option for you. Curly hair looks great in a bob or a pixie cut, and it can be styled with or without bangs. Short curly hair can be a great option for women over 40 who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish.

To style curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product and let your hair air dry. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair with minimal frizz.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great addition to any short hairstyle. They can help to frame the face and can make you look younger. Bangs can be worn with any short hairstyle, including the bob, the pixie, and the shag.

To style bangs, use a round brush to blow dry them straight or use a flat iron to straighten them. You can also use a texturizing spray or pomade to add some texture.

Color

Adding some color to your short hairstyle can be a great way to update your look. Highlights or lowlights can help to add dimension and texture to your hair. You can also consider adding some fun colors, such as pink or blue, for a bold statement.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and styling tools.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add some texture and definition to your hair.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and control frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your short hairstyle looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

A: No, a short hairstyle can actually make you look younger and more modern. It is all about finding the right style for you.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes, there are many ways to style a short hairstyle, including wearing it up. You can try a messy bun or a sleek ponytail.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

In conclusion, there are many great short hairstyles for women over 40. The bob, the pixie, the lob, and the shag are all great options. Adding some color or bangs can also help to update your look. With the right styling tools and products, you can easily maintain a stylish short hairstyle.
