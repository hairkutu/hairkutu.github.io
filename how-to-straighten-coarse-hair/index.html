How To Straighten Coarse Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy, unruly hair? Do you struggle to get your hair to lay flat and smooth? If you have coarse hair, it can be difficult to get the sleek, straight look you desire. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve straight, shiny hair that looks healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips and tricks for straightening coarse hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Straighten Coarse Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Straightening Coarse Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Coarse Hair

How to Straighten Coarse Hair

When it comes to straightening coarse hair, your first step should be to invest in a good flat iron. Look for one that has ceramic or tourmaline plates, as these materials will help to distribute heat evenly and reduce the risk of damage to your hair. You'll also want to use a heat protectant spray or serum to shield your hair from the high temperatures of the flat iron.

Before straightening your hair, it's important to make sure it's clean and dry. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and then blow dry it until it's completely dry. If you have very thick hair, you may want to divide it into sections to make the process easier.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to straightening coarse hair:

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections, if necessary.
  3. Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron around it, as close to the scalp as possible.
  4. Gently glide the flat iron down the length of the hair, pulling it taut as you go.
  5. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair until it's completely straight.
  6. Finish with a smoothing serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Straightening Coarse Hair

Here are some additional tips for straightening coarse hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before straightening it.
  • Avoid using a flat iron on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Adjust the temperature of your flat iron based on your hair type and thickness.
  • Don't apply too much pressure when using the flat iron, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about straightening coarse hair:

Can you straighten coarse hair without a flat iron?

Yes, you can straighten coarse hair without a flat iron. You can use a blow dryer and a round brush to smooth out your hair, or you can try a keratin treatment or chemical straightening process.

Is it bad to straighten coarse hair?

Straightening coarse hair can cause damage if it's not done properly. However, if you use the right techniques and products, you can straighten your hair without causing harm.

How often should you straighten coarse hair?

You should avoid straightening coarse hair every day, as this can lead to damage and breakage. Instead, try to limit straightening to once or twice a week.

Hair Care for Coarse Hair

In addition to straightening your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for coarse hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can lead to breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can lead to damage and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve straight, shiny hair that looks healthy and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products and techniques to keep it looking its best.
