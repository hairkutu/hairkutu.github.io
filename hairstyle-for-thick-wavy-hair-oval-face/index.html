Hairstyle For Thick Wavy Hair Oval Face

Written by Robby Sep 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick wavy hair and an oval face shape, you are in luck! There are many hairstyles that can flatter your features and enhance your natural texture. In this blog, we will discuss how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your thick wavy hair and oval face shape, along with some tips and tricks to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

Description

Having thick wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it can look voluminous and luscious, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you have an oval face shape, you have a versatile canvas to work with when it comes to hairstyles. Oval faces are considered the most balanced face shape, with balanced proportions and a slightly rounded jawline. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural features and create a flattering look.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for your thick wavy hair and oval face shape, there are a few key factors to consider:

Length:

The length of your hair can make a big difference in how it complements your oval face shape. For example, long layers can add movement and dimension, while a blunt bob can frame your face and highlight your jawline. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair when choosing a length.

Texture:

Your natural texture can also play a role in which hairstyle is best for you. If you have tight, defined waves, you may want to consider a layered bob or a shoulder-length cut with long layers. If your waves are looser and more relaxed, a long layered cut with face-framing layers can be flattering.

Face Shape:

While oval face shapes are versatile, there are still some guidelines to follow when it comes to choosing a hairstyle. For example, if you have a high forehead, you may want to consider bangs to balance out your features. On the other hand, if you have a square jawline, you may want to avoid chin-length cuts that can make your face appear wider.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to creating a flattering hairstyle for your thick wavy hair and oval face shape:

Step 1:

Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2:

Using a curling iron or wand, curl small sections of hair away from your face. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 3:

Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a natural, tousled look.

Step 4:

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place without weighing down your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep your hairstyle looking great:

Tip 1:

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Tip 2:

Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and dryness. Use a heat protectant spray and try to air-dry your hair whenever possible.

Tip 3:

Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

What are the best haircuts for thick wavy hair oval face?

The best haircuts for thick wavy hair oval face include long layers, a blunt bob, and a shoulder-length cut with long layers.

How do I style thick wavy hair?

To style thick wavy hair, use a curling iron or wand to create loose, natural-looking waves. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural texture.

How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. If your hair is dry or damaged, you may want to wash it less frequently to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your thick wavy hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Tip 1:

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

Tip 2:

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can cause breakage and frizz.

Tip 3:

Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as it can cause damage and dryness. Try to air-dry your hair whenever possible.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and rock your thick wavy hair and oval face shape with confidence!
