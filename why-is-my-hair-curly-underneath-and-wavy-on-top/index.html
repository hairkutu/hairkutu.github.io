Why Is My Hair Curly Underneath And Wavy On Top

Written by Daniel Sep 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Have you ever wondered why your hair is curly underneath and wavy on top? This is a common hair type that many people have, and it can be frustrating to style and manage. In this blog post, we will discuss the reasons why your hair may be curly underneath and wavy on top, and provide tips on how to care for and style your hair.

Description

Hair that is curly underneath and wavy on top is typically called "mixed hair." This hair type is a combination of two different textures, and it can be challenging to manage. The hair underneath is usually tighter and coarser, while the hair on top is looser and more manageable. This type of hair can be found in many ethnicities and is common in people with multi-ethnic backgrounds.

How to Determine Your Hair Type

Determining your hair type is essential for proper hair care. To determine if your hair is curly underneath and wavy on top, you can do a simple hair analysis. Take a few strands of hair from different parts of your head and examine them closely. Pay attention to the curl pattern, texture, and thickness of the hair. If you notice that the hair underneath is tighter and coarser than the hair on top, you have mixed hair.

Step by Step Guide to Manage Mixed Hair

Managing mixed hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, it can be done. Here are some steps to help you manage your hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfate-free products are gentle on the hair and scalp and do not strip the natural oils from your hair. Use lukewarm water to prevent hair breakage and damage.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots. This will prevent hair breakage and damage. You can also use a detangling spray or leave-in conditioner to make the process easier.

Step 3: Moisturize Your Hair

Mixed hair needs moisture to prevent breakage and damage. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to lock in moisture and keep your hair healthy. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it greasy.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

You can style your mixed hair in different ways, depending on your preference. You can wear it in a curly or wavy style, or you can straighten it using a flat iron. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage and breakage.

Tips for Managing Mixed Hair

Here are some tips to help you manage your mixed hair:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for mixed hair. Look for products that are moisturizing and gentle on the hair and scalp. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair at Night

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep. Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and damage your hair. You can also wear a silk or satin bonnet or scarf to protect your hair while you sleep.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. Split ends can cause your hair to look frizzy and unhealthy. A regular trim can help your hair look healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my mixed hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your mixed hair using a flat iron. However, you should use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage and breakage.

Q: How often should I wash my mixed hair?

A: You should wash your mixed hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Q: Can I dye my mixed hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your mixed hair. However, you should use hair dye that is specifically designed for your hair type and texture. You should also use a deep conditioning treatment after dyeing your hair to keep it healthy and moisturized.

Hair Care for Mixed Hair

Managing mixed hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, it can be done. It is essential to use products that are gentle on your hair and scalp and to protect your hair from heat and friction. By following the tips and steps outlined in this blog post, you can manage your mixed hair and keep it healthy and beautiful.
