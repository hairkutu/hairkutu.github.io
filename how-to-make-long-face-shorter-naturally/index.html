How To Make Long Face Shorter Naturally

Written by Aprilia Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a long face, you may feel self-conscious about its length. Fortunately, there are a number of natural ways to make your face appear shorter. In this article, we'll explore some of the most effective ways to naturally shorten a long face.

Description

Having a long face can be a source of insecurity for many people. It can make you feel self-conscious and affect your confidence in social situations. However, there are ways to make your face appear shorter without resorting to surgery or other invasive procedures. By making some simple changes to your hairstyle, makeup, and posture, you can achieve a more balanced and proportionate facial appearance.

How to

One of the most effective ways to make a long face appear shorter is to focus on your hairstyle. By choosing the right cut and style, you can create the illusion of a shorter face. Here are some tips:

Step 1:

Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face. This can help to balance out the length of your face and make it appear shorter. A layered style with side-swept bangs can be particularly effective.

Step 2:

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for a style that is sleek and close to your head.

Step 3:

Consider getting a fringe or bangs. This can help to shorten the appearance of your forehead and make your face appear more proportional.

Step 4:

Avoid middle parts. Instead, opt for a side part, which can help to create the illusion of a shorter face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making a long face appear shorter:

Tip 1:

Use makeup to contour your face. By applying bronzer or blush to the sides of your face, you can create the illusion of a more rounded face shape.

Tip 2:

Pay attention to your posture. Standing up straight and pulling your shoulders back can help to elongate your neck and create the illusion of a shorter face.

Tip 3:

Wear statement earrings. Large earrings can help to draw attention away from the length of your face and towards your ears.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my face appear shorter without changing my hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can use makeup, posture, and accessories to make your face appear shorter. Applying bronzer or blush to the sides of your face, standing up straight, and wearing statement earrings can all help to create the illusion of a more balanced facial appearance.

Q: Can I permanently shorten my face?

A: No, there is no permanent way to shorten your face. However, by using the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can create the illusion of a shorter face.

Q: Will losing weight make my face appear shorter?

A: It depends on where you carry your weight. If you carry weight in your face, losing weight may help to create a more balanced facial appearance. However, if you carry weight in other areas of your body, losing weight may not have a significant impact on the length of your face.

Hair Care

When trying to make your long face appear shorter, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1:

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type. This can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.

Tip 2:

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

Tip 3:

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your long face appear shorter naturally. Whether you choose to focus on your hairstyle, makeup, or posture, there are plenty of ways to create the illusion of a more balanced facial appearance. So don't let your long face hold you back - with a little effort and creativity, you can achieve the look you want!
