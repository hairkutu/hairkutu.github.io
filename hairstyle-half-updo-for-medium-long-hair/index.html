Hairstyle Half Updo For Medium Long Hair A Guide To A Chic And Easy Look

Written by Christine Jun 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is both chic and easy to do? Look no further than the half updo for medium long hair! This versatile hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, from casual outings to formal events. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to achieve this hairstyle, provide tips and answer frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to do a Half Updo for Medium Long Hair
  • Tips for Styling a Half Updo
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Half Updo

How to do a Half Updo for Medium Long Hair

The half updo for medium long hair is quick and easy to do. Follow these steps:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to get rid of any tangles or knots.
  2. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it to add volume.
  3. Smooth the teased section of hair back and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Take a section of hair from each side of your head and bring them to the back, securing them with bobby pins.
  5. If you have bangs, you can leave them down or sweep them to the side and secure with bobby pins.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Congratulations, you have now achieved a half updo for medium long hair!

Optional Variations:

There are many variations you can try to customize your half updo:

  • Add braids or twists to the sections of hair brought to the back for extra texture.
  • Add accessories such as hair clips or headbands for an extra touch of style.
  • Instead of teasing the crown section of hair, you can create a small bun or twist for a different look.

Tips for Styling a Half Updo

To achieve the perfect half updo, keep these tips in mind:

  • Start with clean, dry hair for best results.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to keep them hidden.
  • Experiment with different section sizes to find what works best for your hair.
  • Use hairspray to hold the style in place and prevent flyaways.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories for a personal touch.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can you do a half updo for medium long hair with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can! Follow the same steps as outlined above, but leave some of your curls down for a more relaxed look.

Q: How long does a half updo for medium long hair take to do?

A: The half updo for medium long hair is a quick and easy style to achieve. It should take no more than 10-15 minutes.

Q: What face shapes does a half updo for medium long hair suit?

A: A half updo for medium long hair suits most face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for those with round or heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care for Half Updo

To keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a chic and easy half updo for medium long hair that is perfect for any occasion. Try it out for yourself and see how you can rock this versatile hairstyle!
