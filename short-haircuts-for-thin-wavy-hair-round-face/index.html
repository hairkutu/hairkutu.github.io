Short Haircuts For Thin Wavy Hair Round Face A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Oliver Apr 14, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have thin wavy hair and a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a cut that will flatter your face shape, enhance your waves, and add body and volume to your hair. In this guide, we’ll share some of the best short haircuts for thin wavy hair round face, along with tips on styling and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Thin Wavy Hair Round Face
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut
  • FAQs: Short Haircuts for Thin Wavy Hair Round Face
  • Hair Care Tips for Thin Wavy Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Face Shape

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and height. You want to avoid cuts that will make your face look wider or rounder. Here are some short haircuts that can work well for a round face:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, layered cut that can work well for thin wavy hair. The layers can add volume and movement, and the short length can help elongate a round face. Style with a side part and add texture with a texturizing spray.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic and versatile cut that can work well for thin wavy hair. A chin-length bob can help elongate a round face, while a layered bob can add volume and movement. Style with a center or side part and add texture with a salt spray.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a trendy and edgy cut that can work well for thin wavy hair. The longer layers on one side can help create the illusion of length, while the shorter layers on the other side can add volume and movement. Style with a side part and add texture with a styling cream.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Thin Wavy Hair Round Face

Styling short haircuts for thin wavy hair requires a bit of effort and technique. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you style your hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add body and volume to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair, focusing on the roots. This will help add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down, lifting the hair as you go. This will help add volume and body to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a texturizing spray or salt spray. This will help enhance your waves and add texture to your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, beachy look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Maintaining a short haircut for thin wavy hair requires a bit of effort and upkeep. Here are some tips to help you maintain your haircut:

Tip 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape and style. Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim.

Tip 2: Use Heat Protectant

When styling your hair with heat tools, use a heat protectant to prevent damage and breakage.

Tip 3: Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

FAQs: Short Haircuts for Thin Wavy Hair Round Face

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short haircut for a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a short haircut for a round face. Opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that can help elongate your face.

Q: Will a short haircut make my thin wavy hair look even thinner?

A: It depends on the cut and styling. A layered cut can add volume and movement to thin wavy hair, while a blunt cut can make it look thinner. Use styling products and techniques to enhance your waves and add volume to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Wavy Hair

Thin wavy hair requires special care and attention to maintain its health and vitality. Here are some hair care tips for thin wavy hair:

Tip 1: Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and volume to your hair.

Tip 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

Tip 3: Avoid Over-Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your hair too often can lead to breakage and damage. Instead, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, beachy look.

By following these tips and using the right short haircut for your face shape, you can create a flattering and stylish look for your thin wavy hair round face. Experiment with different cuts and styles to find the one that works best for you, and don’t forget to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
