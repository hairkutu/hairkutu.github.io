What Styles Do Guys Find Attractive

Written by Luffy Jul 20, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to fashion, women often think about what styles and trends to follow that will make them look attractive to guys. In this article, we will discuss the styles that guys find attractive and why they are drawn to them. Keep reading to find out how you can incorporate these styles into your wardrobe and turn heads wherever you go.

Table of Content

  • Casual Styles
  • Sporty Styles
  • Feminine Styles
  • Edgy Styles

Casual Styles

Guys find girls who can rock a casual look to be very attractive. The effortless vibe of a casual outfit can be captivating to guys. Some of the most popular casual styles that guys find attractive include ripped jeans, oversized t-shirts, and sneakers. These pieces are comfortable and easy to wear which makes them perfect for a relaxed day out or a casual date.

When putting together a casual outfit, make sure to keep it simple and avoid over-accessorizing. A pair of sunglasses or a simple necklace can add the perfect touch to your casual look.

Sporty Styles

Guys love girls who are active and enjoy sports. Sporty styles can be very attractive to guys because they show that you are confident and comfortable in your own skin. Some popular sporty styles that guys find attractive include yoga pants, sneakers, and sports bras. These pieces can be worn both for working out and for casual outings.

When putting together a sporty outfit, make sure to choose clothes that fit well and are comfortable to move in. Avoid wearing clothes that are too tight or too loose as they can be distracting and unflattering.

Feminine Styles

Guys find girls who embrace their femininity to be very attractive. Feminine styles can be fun and flirty, and they can make you feel confident and beautiful. Some popular feminine styles that guys find attractive include dresses, skirts, and high heels. These pieces can be worn for formal events or for a night out on the town.

When putting together a feminine outfit, make sure to choose colors and patterns that complement your skin tone and body shape. Accessorize with jewelry and a clutch purse to complete your feminine look.

Edgy Styles

Guys find girls who have an edgy sense of fashion to be very attractive. Edgy styles can be bold and daring, and they can make you stand out in a crowd. Some popular edgy styles that guys find attractive include leather jackets, combat boots, and ripped jeans. These pieces can be worn for concerts, parties, or casual outings.

When putting together an edgy outfit, make sure to choose clothes that fit well and accentuate your best features. Accessorize with studded jewelry and a statement bag to complete your edgy look.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what style you choose to wear, taking care of your hair is essential. Make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip away natural oils and make your hair dry and brittle.

Invest in a good hairbrush and comb to detangle your hair gently. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools such as blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons. This will protect your hair from heat damage and keep it looking healthy and shiny.

FAQ

What styles do guys find unattractive?

Guys usually find styles that are too revealing or too conservative to be unattractive. Clothes that are too tight or too loose can also be unflattering.

What accessories should I wear to complement my outfit?

You can accessorize your outfit with jewelry, sunglasses, hats, and bags. Make sure to choose accessories that complement your outfit and add to its overall look.

Can I mix and match different styles?

Yes, you can mix and match different styles to create a unique look that reflects your personality. Just make sure to balance the different elements of your outfit and avoid going overboard with accessories.

Now that you know what styles guys find attractive, it's time to experiment with your wardrobe and find the style that suits you best. Remember to have fun and be confident in your own skin, and you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.
