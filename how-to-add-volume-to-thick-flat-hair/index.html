How To Add Volume To Thick Flat Hair

Written by William Jun 05, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick hair, you might think that volume is not something you need to worry about. However, even thick hair can look flat and lifeless without some extra body. Fortunately, there are many ways to add volume to your hair without damaging it. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for adding volume to thick flat hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Add Volume to Thick Flat Hair
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Add Volume to Thick Flat Hair

Here are some tips and tricks for adding volume to your thick, flat hair:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are specifically designed to add body and lift to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin, biotin, and collagen, which can help strengthen your hair and add volume.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

When you blow dry your hair, flip your head upside down and blow dry from the roots to the tips. This will help to lift your roots and create volume. Use a large round brush to add even more body to your hair.

3. Use a Root Lifter

Apply a root lifter to your hair before blow drying. This will help to lift your roots and add volume. Use a small amount and apply it directly to your roots.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an easy way to add volume. Take a small section of hair and backcomb it with a fine-toothed comb. Repeat this process throughout your hair, paying particular attention to the crown of your head.

5. Use Hot Rollers

Hot rollers are a great way to add volume to your hair without damaging it. Roll your hair in sections, starting at the crown of your head. Leave the rollers in for at least 20 minutes, then remove them and gently brush out your hair.

6. Use a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair before blow drying. This will help to add body and volume to your hair. Apply a small amount to your roots and work it through your hair.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide for adding volume to your thick, flat hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a root lifter to your hair and blow dry upside down.
  3. Tease your hair at the crown to add volume.
  4. Use hot rollers to add even more body to your hair.
  5. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to your hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup from your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners, as they can weigh your hair down.
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and add volume.
  • Experiment with different haircuts and styles to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Can I add volume to my hair without using heat?

Yes, you can add volume to your hair without using heat. Try using a volumizing mousse and letting your hair air dry. You can also braid your hair before bed and let it out in the morning for natural-looking waves and volume.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less frequently. Aim to wash your hair at least once a week to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools on your hair.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal clasps, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Adding volume to thick, flat hair is easy with the right techniques and products. Use these tips and tricks to create a full, voluminous hairstyle that will turn heads.
