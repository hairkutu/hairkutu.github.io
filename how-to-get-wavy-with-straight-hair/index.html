How To Get Wavy With Straight Hair

Are you tired of having straight hair but don't want to use heat tools to achieve waves? Well, you're in luck because in this article, we will be discussing how to get wavy with straight hair without using any heat tools. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve beautiful waves.

How to Get Wavy with Straight Hair

The key to getting wavy hair with straight hair is to braid your hair when it's wet and leave it in overnight. Here's what you'll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair tie
  • Hair spray

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair as you normally would. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's wet to prevent any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing and conditioning your hair, towel dry it so that it's damp but not dripping wet. You want your hair to be damp enough to braid but not too wet that it won't dry overnight.

Step 3: Part Your Hair

Part your hair in the middle or on the side, whichever you prefer.

Step 4: Braid Your Hair

Separate your hair into two sections and braid each section. You can either do a regular braid or a French braid. Secure the end of each braid with a hair tie.

Step 5: Wait Overnight

Leave your hair braids in overnight to ensure that your hair has enough time to dry and set in the waves.

Step 6: Take Out the Braids

In the morning, take out the braids and use your fingers to gently separate the waves. Spray with hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips

  • The tighter the braids, the tighter the waves will be. If you want looser waves, braid your hair more loosely.
  • If you have trouble braiding your own hair, ask a friend or family member to help.
  • For added texture, you can use a sea salt spray before braiding your hair.

FAQ

Can I do this on dry hair?

No, this method works best on damp hair. If your hair is dry, wet it with a spray bottle before braiding.

How long will the waves last?

The waves can last up to two days depending on your hair type and how well you take care of them.

Can I use this method on short hair?

Yes, you can use this method on short hair. However, the waves may not be as prominent as they would be on longer hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy waves. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant when using heat tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know how to get wavy with straight hair, you can achieve beautiful waves without damaging your hair with heat tools. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to maintain healthy and gorgeous waves.
