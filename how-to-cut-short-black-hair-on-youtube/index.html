How To Cut Short Black Hair On Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a way to cut your short black hair at home? Do you want to learn how to do it yourself instead of going to a salon? Look no further than Youtube! In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting short black hair on Youtube with easy-to-follow steps, tips, and FAQs. Plus, we'll provide you with some essential hair care tips to maintain your new look. Let's get started!

How to Cut Short Black Hair on Youtube

Cutting short black hair can seem intimidating, but with the right tools and instructions, it can be done with ease. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers or scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Mirror
  • Protective cape or towel

Once you have all the necessary tools, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an accurate cut. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair, then towel dry or blow dry it until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Decide on Your Haircut

Before you start cutting, decide on the style and length you want for your hair. Look for haircut inspiration on Youtube or other social media platforms to find a style that suits you. Once you've decided on the style, make sure you have a clear idea of how you want to cut your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use a clip or hair tie to section your hair into manageable parts. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and accurately. Start with the back of your head and work your way to the front.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using your clippers or scissors, start cutting your hair. If you're using clippers, use a guard to achieve the desired length. If you're using scissors, start by cutting small sections of hair and gradually work your way up to the desired length. Remember to cut slowly and carefully to avoid any mistakes.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you've finished cutting each section, use a mirror to check your progress. Make sure your hair is even and that you've achieved the desired length. If you need to make any adjustments, go back and trim any uneven sections.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it as you normally would. Use a good quality hair product to add texture and definition to your hair.

Tips for Cutting Short Black Hair on Youtube

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way down to the desired length.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to make small cuts and check your progress than to cut off too much hair and have to start over.
  • Use a comb to guide your cutting and ensure that your hair is even.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with scissors instead of clippers?

Yes, you can cut your hair with scissors instead of clippers. Just make sure you start with small sections of hair and cut slowly and carefully.

Do I need to use a protective cape or towel?

Yes, using a protective cape or towel will help keep your clothes clean and protect your skin from any hair clippings.

How often should I cut my short black hair?

It depends on the style and length of your hair. Generally, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain your new look. Here are some essential hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles and breakage.

With these tips and instructions, you can confidently cut your short black hair at home using Youtube. Remember to take your time, be patient, and enjoy the process. Happy cutting!
