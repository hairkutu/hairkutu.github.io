How To Style My Short Hair With Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 21, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have recently cut your hair into a short style with bangs, you may be wondering how to style it in different ways. Short hair with bangs is a versatile look that can be styled in many ways to create various looks, from edgy to chic. In this blog post, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to style your short hair with bangs, along with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Style Short Hair with Bangs

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about some of the different ways you can style short hair with bangs:

  • Sleek and straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Messy and textured

Each of these styles can be achieved using different techniques and tools, and can be adapted to suit your personal style and the occasion.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair with Bangs

Now let's get into the nitty-gritty of how to style your short hair with bangs:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and then blow-dry it using a round brush to create volume and smoothness.

2. Prep your hair with heat protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it is important to protect your hair from damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair, focusing on the ends and bangs.

3. Use a straightener for a sleek look

If you want to create a sleek and straight look, use a flat iron to smooth out your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using a comb to guide the hair as you go. Repeat this process for your bangs, making sure to keep the iron moving to avoid burning your hair.

4. Create waves with a curling iron

If you want to create waves in your hair, use a curling iron to add texture and volume. Start by sectioning your hair and curling small sections at a time, holding the iron for a few seconds before releasing. For your bangs, curl them away from your face to create a flattering frame.

5. Add curls with a wand

If you prefer loose and bouncy curls, use a curling wand to create this look. Wrap small sections of hair around the wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing. For your bangs, curl them inwards towards your face to create a softer look.

6. Texturize with a texturizing spray

If you want to create a messy and textured look, use a texturizing spray to add grit and volume to your hair. Spray the product onto your hair, and then scrunch and tousle your hair with your fingers to create a lived-in look.

Tips for Styling Short Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect style for your short hair with bangs:

  • Invest in good quality styling tools, such as a flat iron, curling iron, and wand.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try out different hair accessories, such as hair clips and headbands, to add interest to your style.

FAQs About Styling Short Hair with Bangs

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with bangs. Try a half-up, half-down style, or a top knot with your bangs swept to the side.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair with bangs?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but it is generally recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: What products should I use to style my short hair with bangs?

A: It depends on the style you want to achieve, but some useful products include heat protectant spray, texturizing spray, volumizing mousse, and hairspray.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair with Bangs

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your short hair with bangs looking healthy and stylish:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray or serum.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your style.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of stylish looks with your short hair and bangs. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
