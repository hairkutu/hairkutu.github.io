Look Better With Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Apr 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering chopping off your locks and going for a shorter hairstyle, you're not alone. Short hair has been a popular trend for years now, and for good reason. Not only is it low-maintenance, but it can also make you look and feel more confident. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to look better with short hair.

Description

Short hair can be both liberating and intimidating. While it can open up a whole new world of styling options, it can also leave you feeling self-conscious and unsure of how to style it. But don't worry, we've got you covered. This article will provide you with everything you need to know to rock a short hairstyle with confidence.

How to

Before you take the plunge and chop off your locks, it's important to do some research. Look up different styles and find one that suits your face shape and personal style. Once you've decided on a style, make an appointment with a reputable hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts. They will be able to give you personalized advice and create a cut that flatters your face shape and features.

When it comes to styling short hair, less is more. Use a small amount of styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Step by Step

If you're unsure of how to style your short hair, here's a simple step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your fingertips.
  3. Rub the product between your fingertips to warm it up.
  4. Apply the product to your hair, focusing on the ends and working your way up.
  5. Use your fingers to create texture and shape your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you look better with short hair:

  • Experiment with different partings to change up your look.
  • Add some volume to your hair by blow-drying it upside down.
  • Accessorize your short hair with headbands, clips, and other hair accessories.
  • Don't be afraid to try bold colors or highlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Maintain your short hair by getting regular trims to keep it in shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair:

Will short hair make my face look rounder?

Short hair can actually make your face look slimmer, as long as you choose a style that flatters your face shape. Consult with your hairstylist to find a style that works for you.

Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

Absolutely! While it may take some creativity, there are plenty of ways to style short hair in an updo. Experiment with braids, twists, and buns to find a style that works for you.

Do I need to use different hair products for short hair?

Not necessarily. While you may need to use less product than you did with longer hair, you can still use the same products. Just be sure to use them sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than longer hair, but it still needs some TLC. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short hairstyle with confidence and style. Remember, the key to looking good with short hair is choosing a style that works for your face shape and personal style. So go ahead, take the plunge, and enjoy your new low-maintenance look!
