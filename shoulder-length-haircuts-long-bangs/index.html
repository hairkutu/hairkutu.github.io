Shoulder Length Haircuts Long Bangs A Perfect Style For 2023

Written by Oliver Aug 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is both trendy and versatile? Look no further than shoulder length haircuts with long bangs. This style is perfect for those who want a change without sacrificing too much length. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care.

Table of Contents

How to Get Shoulder Length Haircuts Long Bangs

If you have long hair, getting a shoulder length haircut with long bangs is a great way to change up your look. To get this style, start by finding a hairstylist who has experience with layered haircuts. Communicate your desired length and style, and ask for long bangs that reach your eyebrows.

For those with shorter hair, it may take some time to grow your hair out to shoulder length. In the meantime, you can experiment with clip-in bangs to see if you like the look.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Section off the top layer of hair and clip it up.

3. Cut the bottom layer of hair to shoulder length.

4. Release the top layer of hair and cut it to the same length as the bottom layer.

5. Cut long bangs that reach your eyebrows.

6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Haircuts Long Bangs

One of the best things about shoulder length haircuts with long bangs is their versatility. Here are some tips for styling this look:

- Use a round brush to blow dry your hair for extra volume.

- Curl your hair away from your face for a romantic look.

- Straighten your hair for a sleek, modern look.

- Use a hair oil or serum to add shine.

- Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQs

Q: Will shoulder length haircuts with long bangs suit my face shape?

A: This style can be flattering on many different face shapes. Talk to your hairstylist about which length and style will work best for you.

Q: How often should I get my haircut to maintain this style?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but typically every 6-8 weeks should suffice.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Haircuts Long Bangs

Maintaining shoulder length haircuts with long bangs requires regular hair care. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

- Use a heat protectant before using hot tools.

- Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking sharp.

- Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

By following these tips, you can keep your shoulder length haircuts with long bangs looking great all year round.
