Easy Bun Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Apr 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have medium length hair and are looking for a quick and easy hairstyle, a bun is the way to go. Not only is it practical, but it can also be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. In this article, we will go over some easy bun hairstyles for medium length hair that you can try out.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

To create an easy bun hairstyle for medium length hair, start by gathering your hair into a ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie.

Another option is to create a low bun by gathering your hair at the nape of your neck and twisting it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins or a hair tie.

Accessorize

You can also accessorize your bun with hair clips, headbands, or scarves to add a pop of color or style to your hair.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to create an easy bun hairstyle for medium length hair:

  1. Gather your hair into a ponytail
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie
  4. Accessorize with hair clips, headbands, or scarves

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable
  • Spray your hair with hairspray before creating the bun to help it stay in place
  • Tease your hair before creating the bun to add volume

FAQ

How do I make my bun look fuller?

You can make your bun look fuller by teasing your hair before creating the bun or by using a hair donut to add volume.

Can I create a bun if I have layered hair?

Yes, you can still create a bun if you have layered hair. You may need to use more bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to take good care of it. This includes washing it regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.

Now that you know how to create some easy bun hairstyles for medium length hair, try them out for yourself and see which one you like best. Whether you're running errands or going out for a night on the town, a bun is a versatile hairstyle that can work for any occasion.
