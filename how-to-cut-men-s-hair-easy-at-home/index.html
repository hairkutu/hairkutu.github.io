How To Cut Men s Hair Easy At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're tired of spending money at the barbershop or salon, cutting your own hair at home is a great way to save money and time. With the right tools and techniques, cutting men's hair can be a simple and easy process. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about how to cut men's hair at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Men's Hair at Home
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Cut Men's Hair at Home

Cutting men's hair at home requires some basic tools and techniques. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clipper set with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel to keep hair off of clothes
  • Chair or stool with a backrest
  • Mirror

Before you begin cutting, it's important to have a plan in place for the style you want to achieve. Look up pictures of the style you want and have them on hand for reference.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps for cutting men's hair at home:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can result in uneven length.
  2. Use the clipper with the longest guard to trim the hair on the top of the head. Start at the front and work your way back, following the natural direction of the hair.
  3. Switch to a shorter guard to trim the sides and back of the head. Use the comb to lift sections of hair and trim with the clippers.
  4. Blend the hair on the sides and back into the hair on top by using different guards and trimming in an upward motion.
  5. Use scissors to trim any longer pieces or to add texture to the hair. Start at the front and work your way back, cutting small sections at a time.
  6. Trim the hairline around the ears and neck with the clippers or scissors.
  7. Check for any uneven spots and make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Style as desired.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair at Home

Here are some additional tips to make cutting men's hair at home even easier:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use the comb to lift sections of hair and trim with the clippers to ensure an even cut.
  • Trim small sections of hair at a time with the scissors to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Take breaks if your arm gets tired or if you need to step back and assess the haircut.
  • If you're unsure about cutting certain areas, start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about cutting men's hair at home:

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair can be a simple process.

What tools do I need to cut men's hair at home?

You'll need clippers with guards, scissors, a comb, a cape or towel, a chair or stool, and a mirror.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always make adjustments and try again. Take your time and be patient.

Hair Care after Cutting Men's Hair at Home

After cutting men's hair at home, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and styled. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any leftover hair.
  • Style as desired using a hair product that works for your hair type.
  • Visit a professional stylist every few months to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Cutting men's hair at home can be a simple and easy process with the right tools and techniques. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a great haircut every time.
