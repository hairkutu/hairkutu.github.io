Mens Skin Fade Hairstyles 2021 A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Frank Sep 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle in 2023? Look no further than the mens skin fade hairstyle. This popular hairstyle has been trending for years and it is not going away anytime soon. In this guide, we will break down how to achieve the perfect mens skin fade hairstyle and provide tips for hair care.

Description

A skin fade hairstyle is a short haircut style that fades from the skin to longer hair on top. The hair on the top is usually styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

How to Achieve the Perfect Mens Skin Fade Hairstyle

1. Choose the right barber or hairstylist. Make sure to bring in a picture of the style you want and discuss with your barber or hairstylist any concerns or questions you may have.

2. Decide on the length of the fade. A skin fade can be a high, mid, or low fade. A high fade starts at the top of the head, a mid fade starts in the middle, and a low fade starts near the ears.

3. Choose the length and style of the hair on top. The hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. Decide on the length and style that best suits your face shape and personal style.

4. Maintain the hairstyle by getting regular trims. The mens skin fade hairstyle requires regular trims to keep it looking fresh and clean.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a comb to part the hair on top in the desired direction.

2. Use clippers to create the fade. Start with a higher guard on the clippers and gradually work your way down to a lower guard as you get closer to the skin.

3. Blend the fade. Use a comb and scissors to blend the hair on top with the faded hair.

4. Style the hair on top. Use your preferred styling product to create the desired look.

Tips

- Experiment with different lengths and styles on top to find what works best for you.

- Use a high-quality styling product to keep the hair on top in place throughout the day.

- Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Is the mens skin fade hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the mens skin fade hairstyle can be adapted to suit all face shapes.

Q: How often should I get a trim to maintain the mens skin fade hairstyle?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the hairstyle.

Hair Care

To keep the mens skin fade hairstyle looking fresh, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and nourished. Avoid using too much heat when styling the hair, as this can damage the hair and cause split ends. Finally, make sure to get regular trims to maintain the shape of the hairstyle.

In conclusion, the mens skin fade hairstyle is a stylish and low-maintenance option for men in 2023. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can achieve the perfect look and keep it looking fresh and clean.
