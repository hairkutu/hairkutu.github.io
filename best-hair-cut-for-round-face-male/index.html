Best Hair Cut For Round Face Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a guy with a round face and struggling to find the perfect haircut that accentuates your features? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best hair cut for round face male, along with tips and tricks to maintain and care for your hair.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. To balance out the roundness, it's essential to choose a haircut that adds volume and structure to the upper part of the head while keeping the sides and back short.

The key to finding the best hair cut for round face male is to create an illusion of length and angles. The right haircut can help elongate your face and draw attention to your best features while keeping your hair looking stylish and trendy.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, keep in mind the following:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head and keep the sides short.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides or create a round shape.
  • Consider incorporating layers into your haircut to add texture and dimension.
  • Choose a haircut that complements your hair type and face shape.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Step by step

Here are some of the best hair cut for round face male:

1. The Side Part

This classic hairstyle works wonders for round faces. Sweep your hair to one side and create a deep part on the opposite side to add volume and structure to the top of the head.

2. The Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that adds height to the top of the head and creates an illusion of length. The sides are kept short, while the top is styled into a point or a spike.

3. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a low-maintenance and versatile hairstyle that works well for round faces. The sides and back are shaved, while the top is kept short and styled slightly forward.

4. The Undercut

The undercut is a modern and stylish hairstyle that creates a sharp contrast between the short sides and the longer top. The longer hair on top can be styled in various ways, such as a slick back or a messy fringe.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling and maintaining your hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and create a greasy look.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to style your hair effectively.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face male?

The best haircut for a round face male is one that adds volume and structure to the top of the head while keeping the sides and back short. Some of the best hair cut for round face male include the side part, faux hawk, crew cut, and undercut.

What hairstyles should round face males avoid?

Round face males should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides or create a round shape, such as the bowl cut or the mushroom cut. These hairstyles can make the face appear even rounder.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, the best hair cut for round face male is one that adds volume and structure to the top of the head while keeping the sides and back short. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best, and don't forget to take proper care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
