What Hair Parting Suits A Big Forehead

Written by Petter May 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a big forehead, it can be challenging to find the right hair parting that flatters your features. However, with the right hair parting, you can balance your facial proportions and enhance your overall look. In this blog post, we'll discuss what hair parting suits a big forehead, how to do it, and some essential tips and tricks to make the most of your hairstyle.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A big forehead can make your face look longer or wider than it is, depending on the shape of your face. If you have a heart-shaped or oval face, a big forehead can be an asset. But if you have a round or square face, a big forehead can make you look unbalanced. The solution is to find a hair parting that works with your facial features, hair texture, and personal style.

How to

The first step in finding the right hair parting for your big forehead is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking in the mirror. Your face shape will be either heart-shaped, round, square, or oval. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hair parting that flatters your features.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to find the right hair parting for your big forehead:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Decide on a hair parting that suits your features.
  3. Experiment with different hair partings to see what works best for you.
  4. Use hair styling products to enhance your hair parting and create volume or texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you find the right hair parting for your big forehead:

  • Avoid straight bangs, which can make your forehead look even bigger.
  • Try a side-swept fringe or a deep side parting to draw attention away from your forehead.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands, clips, or scarves to add interest to your hair parting.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hair parting suits a big forehead:

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle parting with a big forehead?

A: It depends on your face shape and hair texture. If you have an oval or heart-shaped face, a middle parting can work well. But if you have a round or square face, a middle parting can make your forehead look even bigger.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid with a big forehead?

A: Avoid hairstyles that emphasize your forehead, such as straight bangs or slicked-back hair. Instead, try hairstyles that add volume or texture to your hair, like layered cuts or beachy waves.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, shiny hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat, which can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, finding the right hair parting for a big forehead is all about balance and proportion. By choosing a hair parting that flatters your features, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your hairstyle.
