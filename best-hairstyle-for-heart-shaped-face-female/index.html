Best Hairstyle For Heart Shaped Face Female A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel May 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you are one of the lucky ones! This face shape is considered to be the most coveted and attractive because of its balanced proportions. However, finding the best hairstyle for heart shaped face female can be a daunting task. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for heart-shaped face females, how to style them, and tips on how to take care of your hair to maintain your look.

Description of Heart-Shaped Face

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead and cheekbones with a narrow chin. This face shape is also known as an inverted triangle face shape. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for heart shaped face female is to balance out the proportions of your face.

How to Determine If You Have a Heart-Shaped Face

If you are unsure if you have a heart-shaped face, here's how you can find out:

  • Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline
  • If your forehead and cheekbones are wider than your jawline, you have a heart-shaped face

Best Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Face Female

Now that you know you have a heart-shaped face, it's time to find the best hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some hairstyles that work well for heart-shaped face females:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for heart-shaped face females as they add volume and movement to the hair. This hairstyle also softens the jawline and balances out the face shape. You can style your long layers with loose waves or straighten them for a sleek look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for heart-shaped face females as they draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones while softening the forehead. This hairstyle also balances out the proportions of the face. You can style your side-swept bangs with loose waves or straighten them for a chic look.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is another great option for heart-shaped face females. This hairstyle adds volume to the hair, balances out the proportions of the face, and accentuates the cheekbones. You can style your bob haircut with loose waves or straighten it for a sleek look.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy option for heart-shaped face females. This hairstyle accentuates the cheekbones and draws attention to the eyes. You can style your pixie cut with a side-swept bang or add volume to the crown for a textured look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you've found the perfect hairstyle for your heart-shaped face, it's time to style your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume
  4. Style your hair with a curling iron or straightener
  5. Apply a hair spray to hold your hairstyle in place

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking great, here are some tips:

  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Avoid over-styling your hair to prevent damage
  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat with a hat or scarf

FAQs

1. Can heart-shaped face females pull off short hair?

Yes, heart-shaped face females can definitely pull off short hair. A pixie cut or a bob haircut is a great option for heart-shaped face females.

2. Should heart-shaped face females avoid bangs?

No, heart-shaped face females can definitely rock bangs. Side-swept bangs are a great option for heart-shaped face females as they draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones while softening the forehead.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain its natural oils and prevent it from drying out.

Hair Care Tips for Heart-Shaped Face Females

Here are some hair care tips for heart-shaped face females:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair
  • Protect your hair from chlorine and saltwater by wearing a swim cap or applying a leave-in conditioner
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy and shiny

In conclusion, finding the best hairstyle for heart-shaped face female can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and tricks, you can rock any hairstyle with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain your look and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
