How To Decide Blonde Or Brunette A Guide To Choosing The Right Hair Color

Written by Eveline May 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're torn between blonde and brunette. Both colors have their unique advantages and disadvantages, and it's important to consider your skin tone, lifestyle, and personal style before making a decision. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to help you decide whether you're better suited for blonde or brunette hair.

How to Decide Blonde or Brunette: Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in deciding whether you're better suited for blonde or brunette hair is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral, and this can affect how well certain hair colors complement your features. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones, and if they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you probably have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in deciding whether you're better suited for blonde or brunette hair. If you have an active lifestyle and spend a lot of time outside, you may want to consider a darker hair color. Darker hair colors are less likely to fade in the sun and require less maintenance than lighter hair colors. On the other hand, if you have a more sedentary lifestyle and prefer a low-maintenance hair color, blonde hair may be a better option.

Step 3: Determine Your Personal Style

Your personal style can also play a role in deciding whether you're better suited for blonde or brunette hair. If you have a classic or elegant style, brunette hair may be a better fit. Brunette hair is timeless and sophisticated and can complement a wide range of clothing styles. If you have a more playful or trendy style, blonde hair may be a better option. Blonde hair is fun and flirty and can add a touch of whimsy to your look.

Step 4: Try on Wigs or Hair Extensions

If you're still unsure about whether you're better suited for blonde or brunette hair, try on wigs or hair extensions in both colors. This can help you visualize how each color will look on you and make a more informed decision. You can also consult with a hairstylist or colorist to get their professional opinion.

Tips for Maintaining Blonde or Brunette Hair

Once you've decided on your hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining blonde or brunette hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use heat protectant when styling with heat tools
  • Avoid over-processing your hair with chemical treatments
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

FAQs

Is blonde or brunette hair more popular?

This can vary depending on the current trends, but both blonde and brunette hair are generally popular hair colors.

Can I dye my hair blonde or brunette if it's naturally a different color?

Yes, you can dye your hair blonde or brunette even if it's naturally a different color. However, it's important to consult with a professional colorist to ensure that the color is applied correctly and doesn't damage your hair.

How often do I need to touch up my hair color?

This can vary depending on the individual and the hair color, but generally, you'll need to touch up your hair color every 4-6 weeks.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process, but it's important to consider your skin tone, lifestyle, and personal style before making a decision. By following these steps and tips, you can confidently decide whether you're better suited for blonde or brunette hair and maintain your new hair color with ease.
