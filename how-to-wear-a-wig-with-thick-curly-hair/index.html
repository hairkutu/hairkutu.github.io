How To Wear A Wig With Thick Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Apr 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to manage. It can be even more challenging when you want to wear a wig. But don't worry! With the right techniques and tips, you can wear a wig confidently and comfortably. In this article, we'll go over how to wear a wig with thick curly hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Wear a Wig with Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Wear a Wig with Thick Curly Hair

The first step in wearing a wig with thick curly hair is to find a wig that is suitable for your hair type. Look for a wig that is made of high-quality synthetic or human hair that can be styled to match your natural hair texture.

When choosing a wig, consider the length and style. If you have long hair, a long wig may be a good option. If you have shorter hair, look for a wig that is similar in length and style.

Types of Wigs for Thick Curly Hair

There are different types of wigs that are suitable for thick curly hair:

  • Lace front wigs: These wigs have a lace front that mimics the natural hairline, making them look more realistic.
  • Full lace wigs: These wigs have a lace cap that covers the entire head, allowing for more styling options.
  • U-part wigs: These wigs have a U-shaped opening that allows you to blend your natural hair with the wig, giving a more natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear a wig with thick curly hair:

  1. Start by brushing your natural hair and securing it in a low ponytail or braid.
  2. Put on a wig cap that matches your skin tone.
  3. Place the wig on your head and adjust it to fit comfortably.
  4. Secure the wig with combs or clips.
  5. Style the wig to match your natural hair texture using a curling iron or other styling tools.
  6. Blend your natural hair with the wig by pulling out some strands from the wig and tucking them under the wig cap.
  7. Finish by adding some hair accessories, such as a headband or scarf, to complete the look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you wear a wig with thick curly hair:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place and prevent the wig from slipping.
  • Choose a wig that matches your natural hair color and texture.
  • Trim the wig to fit your head if necessary.
  • Use styling products, such as hairspray or mousse, to help the wig stay in place.
  • Wear a scarf or headband to cover the wig cap and create a more natural look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a wig with thick curly hair:

Q: Can I wear a wig with thick curly hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a wig with thick curly hair. Just make sure to choose a wig that matches your hair type and texture.

Q: How do I choose the right wig for my hair type?

A: Look for a wig that is made of high-quality synthetic or human hair that can be styled to match your natural hair texture. Consider the length and style of the wig as well.

Q: Can I style the wig to match my natural hair texture?

A: Yes, you can style the wig using a curling iron or other styling tools to match your natural hair texture.

Hair Care for Wigs and Natural Hair

It's important to take care of both your wig and natural hair to ensure they stay healthy and looking their best.

Wig Care Tips

  • Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape.
  • Wash the wig regularly using a wig shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on synthetic wigs, as they can melt or damage the fibers.
  • Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to detangle the wig.

Natural Hair Care Tips

  • Moisturize your hair regularly using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear a wig with thick curly hair confidently and comfortably. Remember to take care of both your wig and natural hair to ensure they stay healthy and looking their best.
