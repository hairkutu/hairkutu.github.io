How To Cut Layers In Short Wavy Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Cutting your own hair can be daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done successfully. If you have short, wavy hair, adding layers can give your hair movement and texture. This guide will show you how to cut layers in short wavy hair yourself, without having to visit a salon.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparation
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Tips for a Successful Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A mirror

Preparation

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to prepare your hair and workspace.

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Section your hair into layers using clips.
  4. Set up your workspace in a well-lit area with a mirror.

Cutting Your Hair

Now that you're prepared, it's time to start cutting your hair.

  1. Start with the bottom layer of your hair.
  2. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down.
  3. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and cut the hair at an angle.
  4. Continue cutting small sections of hair at an angle until you've finished the bottom layer.
  5. Release the next layer of hair and repeat the process until you've cut all the layers.

Tips for a Successful Cut

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid split ends.
  • Cut your hair at an angle for a natural, blended look.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will appear shorter when it dries.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can cut curly hair using the same technique. Just make sure to cut your hair when it's dry, as curly hair shrinks when it dries.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care Tips

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and maintain your new style.

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Cutting your own hair can be a rewarding experience, especially when you achieve the desired look. By following this guide on how to cut layers in short wavy hair yourself, you'll be able to add movement and texture to your hair without having to visit a salon.
