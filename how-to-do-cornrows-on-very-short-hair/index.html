How To Do Cornrows On Very Short Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 07, 2023 · 7 min read

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Cornrows are a popular hairstyle for many people because of their versatility and low maintenance. However, if you have very short hair, you may think that cornrows are not possible. The truth is that cornrows can be done on very short hair, but it requires a slightly different technique than if you had longer hair.

In this article, we will show you how to do cornrows on very short hair. We will cover everything from the materials you need to the step-by-step process and tips to make your cornrows look their best. By the end of this article, you’ll be able to confidently create beautiful cornrows on your very short hair.

How to

The first step in doing cornrows on very short hair is to gather all the materials you need. You will need:

  • Rat-tail comb
  • Hair clips
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hair ties

Once you have gathered all your materials, you can start the cornrowing process. Follow the steps below:

Step-by-Step

  1. Start by parting your hair into sections with a rat-tail comb. The sections should be small, about 1/4 inch in width.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to the section of hair you want to cornrow. This will help to hold the hair in place and prevent frizz.
  3. Use the rat-tail comb to divide the section of hair into three equal parts, just like you would for a regular braid.
  4. Start braiding the hair by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section.
  5. As you braid, keep adding more hair to each section from the scalp to create a cornrow. Make sure to keep the sections small and tight.
  6. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the hair. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  7. Repeat steps 1-6 for the rest of the sections of hair until you have completed all the cornrows.
  8. Once you have completed all the cornrows, you can style them as desired. You can leave them as is, or you can create a more intricate design by weaving the cornrows together.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing cornrows on very short hair:

  • Use a rat-tail comb to make sure your parts are straight and even.
  • Apply hair gel or pomade to each section of hair before braiding to prevent frizz and flyaways.
  • Make sure to keep the cornrows tight, especially at the base of the braid, to prevent them from coming loose.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cornrowing can be time-consuming, but the end result is worth it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cornrows on very short hair:

Q: How long does my hair need to be to do cornrows?
A: You can do cornrows on any length of hair, but the shorter your hair, the smaller the sections should be. Q: How long do cornrows last?
A: Cornrows can last anywhere from 2-6 weeks depending on how well they are maintained. Q: Can I wash my hair with cornrows?
A: Yes, you can wash your hair with cornrows, but be gentle and avoid using too much shampoo to prevent frizz.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair while wearing cornrows:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly with a light oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf at night to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid pulling on your cornrows or wearing tight hats or headbands that can damage your hair.
  • When it’s time to take out your cornrows, use a detangling spray or conditioner to make the process easier and prevent breakage.

Now that you know how to do cornrows on very short hair, you can experiment with different styles and designs. Whether you wear your cornrows for a few days or a few weeks, they are a great way to protect your hair and express your personal style.
