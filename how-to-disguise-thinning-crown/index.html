How To Disguise Thinning Crown Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jul 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you experiencing thinning hair on the crown of your head? It can be a frustrating and embarrassing issue, but there are ways to disguise it. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks to help you hide your thinning crown and boost your confidence.

What Causes Thinning Crown?

There are various reasons why people experience thinning hair on their crown, including:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Nutrient deficiencies
  • Stress
  • Aging

If you're concerned about your hair loss, it's important to consult a dermatologist or trichologist to diagnose the underlying cause and determine the best treatment for you.

How to Disguise Thinning Crown

While there's no cure for hair loss, there are ways to hide your thinning crown and boost your confidence.

1. Change Your Hairstyle

Changing your hairstyle can be an easy and effective way to disguise your thinning crown. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair tightly, such as braids or ponytails, as they can cause further hair loss.
  • Try a side part, which can help to cover up the thinning area.
  • Consider a shorter haircut, as shorter hair tends to look fuller.

2. Use Hair Fiber Products

Hair fiber products, such as Toppik or Caboki, can be a great option for disguising your thinning crown. These products are made of tiny fibers that cling to your existing hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

3. Use Hair Extensions

Hair extensions can also help to hide your thinning crown. Clip-in extensions are easy to use and can add volume and length to your hair. Just make sure to choose high-quality extensions that blend well with your natural hair.

4. Use Hair Concealers

Hair concealers, such as DermMatch or Nanogen, can be applied directly to your scalp to cover up the thinning area. These products are waterproof and sweatproof, so they'll stay in place all day.

Step by Step: How to Disguise Thinning Crown

Here's a step-by-step guide to disguising your thinning crown:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply hair fiber products or hair concealers to the thinning area.
  3. Style your hair in a way that covers up the thinning area.
  4. If you're using hair extensions, clip them in and style as desired.
  5. Finish with hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

While these tips can help to disguise your thinning crown, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron and biotin.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Massage your scalp to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.

FAQs

1. Are hair fiber products safe to use?

Yes, hair fiber products are safe to use and won't cause any damage to your hair or scalp.

2. Can I use hair extensions if I have thinning hair?

Yes, hair extensions can be a great option for hiding thinning hair. Just make sure to choose high-quality extensions that won't damage your hair.

3. Can stress cause hair loss?

Yes, stress can cause hair loss. If you're experiencing hair loss due to stress, it's important to manage your stress levels through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.

Conclusion

Thinning crown can be a frustrating issue to deal with, but with these tips and tricks, you can disguise it and boost your confidence. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss, and consult a professional if you're concerned about your hair loss.
