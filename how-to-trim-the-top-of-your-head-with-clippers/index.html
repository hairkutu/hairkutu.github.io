How To Trim The Top Of Your Head With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a simple haircut? With the right tools and technique, you can give yourself a professional-looking trim at home. In this guide, we will show you how to trim the top of your head with clippers, including tips and tricks for a perfect cut every time.

How to Trim the Top of Your Head with Clippers

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You will need a pair of clippers with adjustable guards, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to have a cape or towel to protect your clothing from hair clippings.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it is damp. This will make it easier to see the natural growth patterns of your hair and ensure a more even cut. You can also apply a small amount of styling product to help your hair stay in place.

Step 2: Choose Your Guard Size

Attach the appropriate guard size to your clippers. The guard size will depend on how short you want your hair to be. For a shorter cut, use a smaller guard size, and for a longer cut, use a larger guard size.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Starting at the sides of your head, run the clippers in an upward motion towards the top of your head. Keep the clippers parallel to your head to ensure an even cut. Work your way around the sides of your head, using the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it.

Step 4: Move to the Top

Once you have trimmed the sides, move to the top of your head. Use the same technique, running the clippers in an upward motion towards the front of your head. Keep the clippers parallel to your head, and use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it evenly.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Top

Now that you have trimmed the sides and top, it's time to blend them together. Use a shorter guard size to trim the hair around the crown of your head, gradually working your way towards the longer hair at the top. This will create a more natural-looking transition between the shorter sides and the longer top.

Step 6: Check for Uneven Spots

Once you have finished trimming, take a close look at your hair in the mirror. Check for any uneven spots or missed areas, and make any necessary touch-ups with the clippers.

Tips for a Perfect Cut

- Start with a longer guard size and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
- Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it evenly.
- Take breaks to check your progress in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.
- Clean your clippers after each use to ensure a longer lifespan.

FAQs

Q: Can I use scissors instead of clippers?
A: While scissors can be used to trim hair, clippers are specifically designed for this purpose and will give you a more even cut.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?
A: This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. As a general rule, it's a good idea to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain a clean and polished look.

Hair Care

After trimming your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. You may also want to use a styling product to keep your hair in place and protect it from heat damage. And finally, don't forget to schedule regular haircuts to keep your hair looking its best.
