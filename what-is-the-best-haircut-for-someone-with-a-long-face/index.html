What Is The Best Haircut For Someone With A Long Face

Written by Petter Sep 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you wondering what the best haircut is for someone with a long face? Look no further, as we have the answers for you! In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for those with long faces, as well as provide tips and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow features. The best haircut for someone with a long face is one that adds width and volume to the sides of the face, while also balancing the length of the chin. A good haircut can help to create the illusion of a shorter face and make your features appear more balanced and proportionate.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a long face, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. For those with straight hair, a long bob or blunt cut can work well to add volume and width to the sides of the face. Those with curly or wavy hair may want to opt for layers or a shaggy cut to add texture and movement.

Consider the Length

The length of your hair is also an important consideration. If you have long hair, you may want to consider a layered cut that adds volume and movement. If you prefer shorter hair, a chin-length bob or pixie cut can help to balance out the length of your face.

Choose the Right Bangs

Bangs can be a great addition to a long face, but it's important to choose the right style. Side-swept bangs can help to balance out a high forehead, while blunt bangs can make a long face appear even longer.

Step by Step

If you're unsure about what haircut will work best for your long face, it's a good idea to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture. Here are some steps to follow when getting a haircut for a long face:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Choose a length and style that adds width and volume to the sides of the face.
  3. Consider adding layers or texture to add movement and volume.
  4. Choose the right bangs to balance out your features.
  5. Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape and style.

Tips

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, as this can make a long face appear even longer.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and movement.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can accentuate the length of your face.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, as this can make a long face appear even longer. Also, avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the face appear longer and more narrow.

Is a bob a good haircut for a long face?

Yes, a bob can be a great haircut for those with long faces. A chin-length bob can help to balance out the length of the face and add width to the sides.

Should I get layers if I have a long face?

Yes, layers can be a great addition to a long face, as they can add movement and volume. However, it's important to choose the right type of layers for your hair texture and face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your locks looking their best. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to remove split ends and maintain the shape and style.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as this can damage the hair and scalp.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can enhance your features and create a balanced, proportionate look. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture. With the right care and maintenance, you can keep your hair looking beautiful and healthy.
