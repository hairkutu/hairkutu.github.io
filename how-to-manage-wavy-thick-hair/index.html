How To Manage Wavy Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter May 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have wavy thick hair, you know how difficult it can be to manage. The combination of waves and thickness can make it hard to style, prone to frizz, and easily tangled. But with a few tips and tricks, you can learn how to manage your hair and make it look its best. In this blog post, we will cover everything you need to know about how to manage wavy thick hair, from the best products to use to the right styling techniques.

Description

Wavy thick hair is a type of hair that has a natural wave pattern and is also thick in texture. This hair type can be difficult to manage, but with the right techniques and products, you can make it look amazing. Some common challenges of wavy thick hair include frizz, tangles, and difficulty styling.

How to

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step to managing wavy thick hair is to use the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type, such as a moisturizing formula for thick hair. Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.

Step 2: Towel Dry

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it by wrapping your hair in a towel and squeezing out the excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage your hair.

Step 3: Apply Products

Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair to help tame frizz and define your waves. You can also use a heat protectant spray if you plan to use heat styling tools.

Step 4: Air Dry

When possible, let your hair air dry to avoid using heat styling tools. If you do need to use a blow dryer or diffuser, use a low heat setting and avoid touching your hair with the nozzle.

Step 5: Style

Once your hair is dry, you can style it using a curling iron or flat iron. For a more natural look, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your waves. You can also try braiding your hair while it's damp and letting it air dry for a beachy wave look.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

FAQ

What are the best products for wavy thick hair?

Look for products that are designed for wavy thick hair, such as a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a leave-in conditioner, styling cream, or heat protectant spray to help tame frizz and define your waves.

How often should I wash my wavy thick hair?

Wavy thick hair can be prone to dryness, so you may not need to wash it every day. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every few days, depending on how oily your scalp gets.

How can I prevent frizz in my wavy thick hair?

Use a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to help tame frizz, and avoid using hot tools on your hair every day. You can also try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.

Hair Care

Managing wavy thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can make it look amazing. Remember to shampoo and condition your hair regularly, and use a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to help tame frizz. When possible, let your hair air dry to avoid using heat styling tools, and be sure to get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. With these tips and tricks, you can learn how to manage your wavy thick hair and make it look its best.
