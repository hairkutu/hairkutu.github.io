How To Fix Baby Hair On Lace Front Wigs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone who loves wearing lace front wigs, then you know how important it is to make them look as natural as possible. One of the ways to achieve this is by fixing baby hair on your lace front wig. In this blog post, we'll guide you on how to fix baby hair on lace front wigs in a step-by-step manner.

How to Fix Baby Hair on Lace Front Wigs

The following steps will guide you on how to fix baby hair on your lace front wig:

Step 1: Wash Your Wig

The first step is to wash your wig. This will help to remove any excess oils or products that may be on the wig. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to wash the wig. Rinse it thoroughly with water and let it air dry.

Step 2: Create Baby Hair

Take a small section of hair from the front of the wig and use scissors to cut it to the desired length. Use a razor to create a more natural look. Repeat this process until you have created baby hair all around the front of the wig.

Step 3: Apply Gel

Apply a small amount of gel to the baby hair. This will help to hold it in place and make it look more natural. Use a toothbrush or a small brush to comb the hair into place.

Step 4: Use a Blow Dryer

Use a blow dryer on low heat to dry the gel. This will help to set the baby hair in place. Make sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging the wig.

Step 5: Style the Wig

Style the wig as desired. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to create different styles. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging the wig.

Tips for Fixing Baby Hair on Lace Front Wigs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when fixing baby hair on lace front wigs:

  • Use a toothbrush or small brush to comb the baby hair into place.
  • Apply a small amount of gel to the baby hair to hold it in place.
  • Use a blow dryer on low heat to dry the gel and set the baby hair in place.
  • Style the wig as desired using a flat iron or curling iron. Use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging the wig.

FAQs

Can I use hair products on my lace front wig?

Yes, you can use hair products on your lace front wig. However, make sure to use a mild shampoo and conditioner to wash the wig. Avoid using products that contain alcohol or other harsh chemicals that can damage the wig.

How often should I wash my lace front wig?

You should wash your lace front wig every 6-8 wears. This will help to keep the wig clean and maintain its quality.

Hair Care for Lace Front Wigs

To maintain the quality of your lace front wig, you should take proper care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the wig regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water on the wig as it can damage the lace.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the wig as it can damage the hair.
  • Store the wig properly in a cool and dry place to avoid damage.

By following these tips, you can keep your lace front wig looking beautiful and natural for a long time.

Conclusion

Fixing baby hair on your lace front wig can help to make it look more natural. By following the steps and tips in this blog post, you can achieve a beautiful and natural look for your wig. Remember to take proper care of your wig to maintain its quality and keep it looking beautiful for a long time.
