White Male Braids Hairstyles 2019 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In 2019, white male braids hairstyles were all the rage. From celebrities to everyday men, everyone seemed to be sporting some kind of braided hairstyle. Fast forward to 2023, and these hairstyles are still going strong. If you're looking to join the trend, this guide has got you covered. We'll cover everything from how to style your hair to hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Braids
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Your Hair

Before you start braiding your hair, you need to decide on the type of braid you want. There are many different styles to choose from, including:

  • Box Braids
  • Cornrows
  • Fishtail Braids
  • Dutch Braids
  • French Braids

Once you've decided on the style, you'll need to make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is too oily, the braids won't hold as well. You'll also need to gather all the necessary tools, such as a comb, bobby pins, and hair ties.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have everything you need, it's time to start braiding. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by parting your hair into sections using a comb. The size of the sections will depend on the style of braid you're doing.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the top of one of the sections and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Begin braiding the three sections together, starting at the top of your head.
  4. As you braid, keep adding small sections of hair to each of the three sections.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section.
  6. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie or bobby pin.
  7. Repeat the process for the remaining sections of hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Braids

Once you've braided your hair, you'll want to make sure it stays looking great for as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your braids:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your braids.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid pulling on your braids, as this can cause them to come loose.
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your braids looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about white male braids hairstyles:

Can white men wear braids?

Yes, absolutely! Anyone can wear braids, regardless of their race or gender.

How long do braids last?

Braids can last anywhere from a few days to a few months, depending on the style and how well they're maintained.

Can I wash my hair with braids?

Yes, you can wash your hair with braids. Just be gentle and use a mild shampoo.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to the tips mentioned earlier, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause damage.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock any white male braids hairstyle with confidence. Whether you're going for a classic look or something more modern, there's a braided hairstyle out there for everyone.
