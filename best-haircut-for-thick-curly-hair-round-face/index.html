Best Haircut For Thick Curly Hair Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of feeling like your hair is taking over your face? Do you struggle to find the perfect haircut that flatters your round face and thick, curly hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips, tricks, and recommendations for the best haircut for thick curly hair round face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it is undoubtedly beautiful, it can also be challenging to manage and style. When you add a round face into the mix, finding the perfect haircut can seem impossible. However, with the right guidance, you can achieve a look that flatters your face shape and showcases your beautiful curls.

How to

The first step in finding the best haircut for thick curly hair round face is to identify your face shape. If you are unsure, take a picture of yourself facing forward and trace the outline of your face. Once you have determined that you have a round face, you can start exploring different hairstyles that will complement your features.

Consider Layers

Layers are an excellent option for those with thick curly hair and a round face. They add volume and movement, which can help to balance out the roundness of your face. Opt for long layers that start just below your chin or shorter layers that frame your face.

Try a Bob

Bobs are a classic haircut that can work well for those with thick curly hair and a round face. Choose a bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back, as this will help to elongate your face. Avoid bobs that are too short or hit at the chin, as they can make your face appear wider.

Consider Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to draw attention away from the roundness of your face. Opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help to create a diagonal line across your face, which can help to elongate your features.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to achieve the best haircut for thick curly hair round face:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Explore different hairstyles that work well for round faces
  3. Consider layers, bobs, and bangs
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best option for you
  5. Maintain your haircut with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments

Tips

Follow these tips to ensure that your haircut looks its best:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are specifically designed for curly hair
  • Consider getting regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair healthy and hydrated

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face with thick curly hair?

The best haircut for a round face with thick curly hair is one that incorporates layers, such as long layers that start just below the chin or shorter layers that frame the face. Bobs that are longer in the front and shorter in the back can also work well, as can side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Follow these hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo that is specifically designed for curly hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz

In conclusion, finding the best haircut for thick curly hair round face can be challenging, but with the right guidance, it is possible. Consider layers, bobs, and bangs, and consult with a hairstylist to determine the best option for you. Follow these tips and hair care recommendations to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
