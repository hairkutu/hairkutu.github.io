How To Use Thinning Shears On Side Bangs A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Side bangs can be a great way to add some flair to your hairstyle. However, maintaining them can be a bit of a challenge. Using thinning shears is a great way to keep them looking fresh and neat. In this article, we’ll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to use thinning shears on side bangs.

Description

Thinning shears are a type of scissors that have teeth on one of the blades. These teeth are designed to remove some of the hair while leaving some of it behind. Thinning shears are commonly used to add texture to hair or to thin out hair that is too thick. When used on side bangs, thinning shears can help to blend them seamlessly with the rest of your hair.

How to Use Thinning Shears on Side Bangs

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Before you start using thinning shears, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to see where your side bangs start and end.

Step 2: Comb your side bangs

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your side bangs straight down. This will help you see how long they are and where they need to be trimmed.

Step 3: Use the thinning shears

Hold the thinning shears parallel to your side bangs and make small snips, starting at the ends of your hair. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven cut. Keep making small snips until you’ve thinned out your bangs to your desired thickness.

Step 4: Blend your side bangs

Once you’ve thinned out your side bangs, use a regular pair of scissors to blend them with the rest of your hair. This will help to create a seamless transition between your bangs and the rest of your hair.

Tips

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Be patient – it’s better to take your time and get it right than to rush and make a mistake.
  • If you’re unsure about using thinning shears, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQ

Can I use thinning shears on curly hair?

Yes, you can use thinning shears on curly hair. However, it’s important to be careful not to thin out your hair too much, as this can result in frizz.

How often should I thin out my side bangs?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how thick your side bangs are. As a general rule, you should aim to trim them every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Maintaining your hair is important to keep it healthy and looking its best. Make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Using thinning shears on your side bangs can be a great way to keep them looking fresh and stylish. By following these tips and steps, you’ll be able to create a seamless transition between your side bangs and the rest of your hair.
