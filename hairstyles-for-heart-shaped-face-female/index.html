Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face Female Tips How To And Care

Written by Luffy Mar 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Female celebrities like Reese Witherspoon, Jennifer Lopez, and Halle Berry have heart-shaped faces. If you have a heart-shaped face, choosing the right hairstyle can enhance your features and balance your face shape. In this article, we will provide you with tips, how-to, and care for hairstyles that suit a heart-shaped face female.

Table of Content:

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles for Heart-Shaped Face Female
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing the right hairstyle for your heart-shaped face, you need to determine your face shape. Here are the steps:

  1. Pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Take a lipstick or an eyeliner and draw the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you have drawn.
  4. Determine which of the following shapes your face most closely resembles: oval, round, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped.

If your face shape is heart-shaped, congratulations! Now, let's move on to the next section.

Hairstyles for Heart-Shaped Face Female

The key to choosing the right hairstyle for heart-shaped face female is to balance its features. You want to draw attention away from the forehead and enhance the chin area. Here are some hairstyles that suit heart-shaped face female:

Long Layers

Long layers can soften the angles of a heart-shaped face and draw attention to the chin area. You can wear your hair straight or curled to add volume to the ends. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the cheekbones.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can balance the width of a heart-shaped face and highlight the eyes. You can wear them long or short, depending on your preference. Avoid blunt bangs that end at the eyebrows.

Messy Updo

A messy updo can add volume to the crown area and elongate the neck. You can leave some strands loose to frame your face or tuck them behind your ears. Avoid tight and sleek updos that accentuate the forehead.

Bob Cut

A bob cut can create an illusion of width at the chin area and balance the forehead. You can wear it straight or wavy, depending on your mood. Avoid bobs that end at the cheekbones.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling your hair if you have a heart-shaped face:

  • Avoid center parts that accentuate the forehead.
  • Try asymmetrical styles that balance the face shape.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands and clips to add volume to the crown area.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head.
  • Experiment with different lengths and textures to find what suits you best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can heart-shaped face female wear short hair?

A: Yes, heart-shaped face female can wear short hair as long as it balances the features of the face. A pixie cut or a short bob can work well.

Q: Can heart-shaped face female wear a middle part?

A: Heart-shaped face female should avoid a middle part as it accentuates the forehead. Side parts or zigzag parts can work better.

Hair Care for Heart-Shaped Face Female

Here are some tips for hair care if you have a heart-shaped face:

  • Avoid heavy products that weigh down the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hairline.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid breakage.

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle, style your hair, and take care of it, you can confidently rock any look that suits your heart-shaped face. Remember to embrace your unique features and have fun with your hair!
