How To Stop Hair Fall Naturally In Rainy Season

Written by Daniel May 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Rainy season brings a lot of joy and happiness, but it also comes with its share of hair problems. Hair fall is one such issue that we face during this season. The reason behind hair fall in rainy season can be attributed to the high humidity levels in the air, which can make the scalp oily and prone to infections. However, the good news is that you can prevent hair fall naturally by following some simple tips and tricks. In this article, we will discuss how to stop hair fall naturally in rainy season.

  • How to Stop Hair Fall Naturally in Rainy Season
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Stop Hair Fall Naturally in Rainy Season

How to Stop Hair Fall Naturally in Rainy Season

To stop hair fall naturally in rainy season, you need to follow some simple steps. They are as follows:

1. Keep Your Scalp Clean and Dry

The first and foremost step to prevent hair fall in rainy season is to keep your scalp clean and dry. Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and dry it with a soft towel. Avoid using a hair dryer, as it can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Also, avoid tying your hair when it's wet, as it can lead to hair fall.

2. Massage Your Scalp with Essential Oils

Massaging your scalp with essential oils can help stimulate hair growth and prevent hair fall. You can use coconut oil, olive oil, or any other essential oil of your choice. Massage your scalp gently for 10 to 15 minutes and leave it on for a few hours before washing it off.

3. Eat a Healthy and Balanced Diet

Eating a healthy and balanced diet can go a long way in preventing hair fall. Include foods that are rich in vitamins, minerals, and proteins, such as green leafy vegetables, fruits, nuts, and lean meats. Also, drink plenty of water to keep your body hydrated.

4. Avoid Chemical Treatments

Avoid chemical treatments like hair coloring, perming, and straightening during the rainy season, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Instead, opt for natural hair treatments like henna, amla, and shikakai.

5. Protect Your Hair from Rain

Protect your hair from rain by wearing a hat, scarf, or umbrella. This will prevent your hair from getting wet and reduce the risk of hair fall. Also, avoid going out in the rain when it's windy, as the wind can cause your hair to tangle and break.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to stop hair fall naturally in rainy season:

  1. Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  2. Dry your hair with a soft towel.
  3. Avoid using a hair dryer.
  4. Massage your scalp with essential oils.
  5. Eat a healthy and balanced diet.
  6. Avoid chemical treatments.
  7. Protect your hair from rain.

Tips to Stop Hair Fall Naturally in Rainy Season

Here are some additional tips to stop hair fall naturally in rainy season:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hair styling products like gels and sprays.
  • Get enough sleep to reduce stress levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I use hair oil during rainy season?

Yes, you can use hair oil during rainy season. However, make sure to use a light oil like coconut oil or olive oil, as heavy oils can make your hair greasy and attract dirt.

2. How often should I wash my hair during rainy season?

You should wash your hair at least twice a week during rainy season to keep it clean and healthy.

3. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can cause hair fall. Try to reduce your stress levels by practicing yoga, meditation, or any other relaxation techniques.

Hair Care Tips for Rainy Season

Here are some additional hair care tips to follow during rainy season:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair serum to protect your hair from humidity.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons.
  • Avoid touching your hair too often, as it can transfer dirt and oil from your hands to your hair.

By following these simple tips and tricks, you can prevent hair fall naturally in rainy season and keep your hair healthy and strong.
