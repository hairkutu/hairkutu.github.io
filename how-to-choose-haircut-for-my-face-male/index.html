How To Choose The Perfect Haircut For Your Face Shape A Guide For Men

Written by Bardi Jul 11, 2023 · 9 min read

Choosing the right haircut is essential for men to look their best. However, with so many different hairstyles available, it can be challenging to find one that suits your face shape. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to choose the perfect haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose a Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Before we get into the specifics of choosing a haircut for your face shape, it's essential to understand why it's so important. The right haircut can enhance your facial features and balance out any asymmetry, making you look more attractive and put together. On the other hand, the wrong haircut can highlight any flaws and make you look unflattering.

How to Choose a Haircut for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: round, square, oval, rectangle, diamond, and heart. Once you have identified your face shape, you can then choose a haircut that complements your face and enhances your features.

Round Face

If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length and definition. A haircut that is shorter on the sides and longer on top, such as a pompadour or quiff, can elongate your face. Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face look wider.

Square Face

A square face has a strong jawline and angular features. To soften the angles, you want a haircut that is shorter on the sides and longer on top, such as a textured crop or a side-swept fringe. Avoid haircuts that are too boxy or angular, as they can make your face look harsh.

Oval Face

An oval face is considered the most versatile face shape and can pull off a variety of haircuts. However, you want to avoid haircuts that are too long, as they can make your face look droopy. Opt for a classic side part or a textured quiff to enhance your features.

Rectangle Face

A rectangle face is similar to a square face, but with a longer forehead and chin. To balance out the proportions, you want a haircut that is shorter on the sides and longer on top, such as a messy fringe or a textured crop. Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face look longer.

Diamond Face

A diamond face has a narrow forehead and chin, with wider cheekbones. To enhance your cheekbones, you want a haircut that is shorter on the sides and longer on top, such as a slicked-back undercut or a messy fringe. Avoid haircuts that are too voluminous, as they can make your face look wider.

Heart Face

A heart face has a wider forehead and narrower chin. To balance out your proportions, you want a haircut that is shorter on the sides and longer on top, such as a side-swept fringe or a messy quiff. Avoid haircuts that are too voluminous, as they can make your forehead look even wider.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape and which haircuts suit it best, here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the perfect haircut:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Research haircuts that suit your face shape.
  3. Show your hairstylist a picture of the haircut you want.
  4. Ask your hairstylist if the haircut will suit your face shape.
  5. Discuss any modifications to the haircut to suit your preferences.
  6. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using the right products.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect haircut:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair.
  • Bring a picture of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist.
  • Ask for recommendations from your hairstylist.
  • Experiment with different haircuts to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Q: What products should I use for my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Ask your hairstylist for recommendations on the best products for your hair.

Q: How can I style my hair at home?

A: Again, it depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Ask your hairstylist for tips on how to style your hair at home, and practice until you get the hang of it.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to choose the perfect haircut for your face shape and keep your hair looking and feeling healthy. Remember, a good haircut can make all the difference in your appearance, so take the time to find the right one for you.
