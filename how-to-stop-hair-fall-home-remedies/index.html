How To Stop Hair Fall Home Remedies

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women. It can be caused by various factors such as genetics, hormonal changes, poor diet, stress, and environmental factors. If you are experiencing hair fall, there are many home remedies that you can try to stop it. In this article, we will discuss some effective home remedies that can help you stop hair fall.

How to Stop Hair Fall: Home Remedies

1. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a natural ingredient that can help reduce hair fall. It contains enzymes that can promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. To use aloe vera for hair fall, take a fresh aloe vera leaf and extract the gel. Apply the gel on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water. Repeat this once a week for best results.

2. Onion Juice

Onion juice is another effective home remedy for hair fall. It contains sulfur, which can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. To use onion juice for hair fall, extract the juice from an onion and apply it on your scalp. Leave it for 30 minutes and then rinse it off with water. Repeat this twice a week for best results.

3. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a natural oil that can help nourish your hair and prevent hair fall. It contains fatty acids that can penetrate the hair shaft and strengthen the hair. To use coconut oil for hair fall, warm up some coconut oil and apply it on your scalp. Massage it for a few minutes and leave it for an hour. Rinse it off with shampoo. Repeat this twice a week for best results.

4. Indian Gooseberry

Indian gooseberry, also known as amla, is a natural ingredient that can help prevent hair fall. It is rich in vitamin C, which can promote hair growth and strengthen the hair. To use Indian gooseberry for hair fall, mix some amla powder with water to make a paste. Apply the paste on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water. Repeat this once a week for best results.

5. Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds are another effective home remedy for hair fall. They contain proteins and nicotinic acid that can help strengthen the hair and promote hair growth. To use fenugreek seeds for hair fall, soak some seeds in water overnight. In the morning, grind the seeds to make a paste. Apply the paste on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water. Repeat this once a week for best results.

Steps to Follow

To use these home remedies for hair fall, follow these steps:

  • Choose the home remedy that you want to use.
  • Gather all the ingredients that you need.
  • Prepare the home remedy according to the instructions given.
  • Apply the home remedy on your scalp and hair.
  • Leave it on for the recommended time.
  • Rinse it off with water.
  • Repeat the process according to the recommended frequency.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you stop hair fall:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair.
  • Manage your stress levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you are stressed, your body releases hormones that can affect your hair growth cycle, leading to hair fall.

Q. Can I use these home remedies if I have sensitive skin?

A. Yes, you can use these home remedies even if you have sensitive skin. However, if you experience any irritation or discomfort, stop using the remedy and consult a doctor.

Q. How long will it take to see results?

A. It may take a few weeks or months to see results. Consistency is key when it comes to using these home remedies.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important to prevent hair fall. Here are some hair care tips that you should follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after every wash.
  • Use a hair serum to protect your hair from damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

In conclusion, hair fall is a common problem that can be treated with home remedies. Try these remedies and follow the tips mentioned to prevent hair fall and promote hair growth.
