Is It Common To Have Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Jun 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone who has wavy hair, you might be wondering whether it's common or not. Wavy hair is a hair type that falls somewhere between straight and curly hair. In this article, we'll be discussing whether it's common to have wavy hair, how to embrace your wavy locks, how to style it, and how to care for it.

Table of Content

  • Is it Common to Have Wavy Hair?
  • How to Embrace Your Wavy Hair
  • How to Style Your Wavy Hair
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair
  • Wavy Hair FAQ

Is it Common to Have Wavy Hair?

Yes, it is very common to have wavy hair. In fact, it's one of the most common hair types out there. It's estimated that about 60% of the world's population has wavy hair.

Wavy hair can come in many different forms, from loose and barely-there waves to more defined and voluminous waves. It's important to embrace your natural hair type and work with it, rather than against it.

How to Embrace Your Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, the best thing you can do is to embrace it. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your natural waves
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Embrace your natural texture and avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use products that are specifically designed for wavy hair, such as mousse or curl-enhancing creams
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

How to Style Your Wavy Hair

There are many different ways to style wavy hair, depending on the look you're going for. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

  • Embrace your natural texture and wear your hair down, allowing your waves to shine
  • Create loose, beachy waves using a curling iron or wand
  • Braid your hair before bed to create natural-looking waves in the morning
  • Experiment with different updos, such as a messy bun or a braided crown

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness and frizz, so it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your wavy locks looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves

Wavy Hair FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair if you want to. However, it's important to use heat protectant products and not straighten your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.

Q: Can I wear my wavy hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear your wavy hair in a ponytail. Just be sure to use a hair tie that won't cause damage or breakage, and avoid pulling your hair too tight.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my wavy hair?

A: To prevent frizz in your wavy hair, avoid brushing it when it's dry, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle it when it's wet, and use a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum.

Q: Can I dye my wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your wavy hair if you want to. However, it's important to use hair dye that is specifically designed for your hair type and to follow the instructions carefully.

Q: How often should I get trims if I have wavy hair?

A: It's a good idea to get a trim every 6-8 weeks if you have wavy hair. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

In conclusion, having wavy hair is very common, and it's important to embrace your natural texture and work with it, rather than against it. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can keep your wavy locks looking healthy and beautiful.
