How To Sew A Pixie Cut Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and want to switch things up? A pixie cut wig might be just what you need! Pixie cut wigs are versatile, low-maintenance, and a great way to experiment with different hair colors and styles without committing to a permanent cut. In this step-by-step guide, we’ll show you how to sew a pixie cut wig from scratch.

How to Sew a Pixie Cut Wig

What You’ll Need

Before you get started, make sure you have all the necessary materials:

  • Wig cap
  • Curved needle
  • Thread
  • Scissors
  • Human hair or synthetic hair
  • Wig stand or mannequin head

Step 1: Prepare the Wig Cap

Begin by placing the wig cap on your wig stand or mannequin head. Make sure the cap is stretched tight and secure. You can use T-pins to keep it in place.

Step 2: Cut the Hair

Next, cut the hair into small sections using your scissors. The sections should be about 2 inches wide and 2 inches long. This will make it easier to sew the hair onto the wig cap.

Step 3: Start Sewing

Thread your curved needle with a length of thread and knot the end. Begin sewing the hair onto the wig cap, starting at the nape of the neck and working your way up to the crown. Be sure to sew each section of hair tightly and securely to the cap.

Step 4: Add Layers

Once you’ve sewn the hair onto the cap, use your scissors to trim and shape the wig into a pixie cut style. You can add layers and texture as desired.

Step 5: Style and Care for Your Wig

Now that your wig is complete, it’s time to style and care for it. You can use heat tools to straighten or curl your wig, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. To keep your wig looking fresh and clean, wash it regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner. And don’t forget to store it properly when you’re not wearing it – a wig stand or mannequin head is the best option.

Tips for Sewing a Pixie Cut Wig

  • Use a wig cap that matches your skin tone for a more natural look.
  • Choose high-quality hair for your wig – human hair is the most natural-looking option, but synthetic hair can be a more affordable alternative.
  • Take your time when sewing the hair onto the cap to ensure a neat and secure finish.
  • Experiment with different pixie cut styles and lengths to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

How long does it take to sew a pixie cut wig?

The time it takes to sew a pixie cut wig depends on your skill level and the complexity of the style. It can take anywhere from a few hours to a few days to complete a wig.

Do I need any special tools to sew a pixie cut wig?

You will need a curved needle and thread specifically designed for sewing hair onto a wig cap. You may also want to invest in a wig stand or mannequin head to make the process easier.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the quality and longevity of your wig. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on synthetic hair wigs.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to prevent tangling and damage.
  • Protect your wig from sunlight, heat, and humidity.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can sew your own pixie cut wig and enjoy a new look whenever you want. Have fun experimenting with different styles and colors!
