How Should A 60 Year Old Woman Wear Her Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Mar 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair inevitably changes too. It can become thinner, drier, and lose its natural shine. However, this doesn't mean that you can't have a fabulous hairstyle that suits your age and personality. If you're wondering how should a 60 year old woman wear her hair, keep reading for some tips and tricks.

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

The first thing you need to think about when deciding on a hairstyle is your face shape. The right hairstyle can enhance your features and make you look younger and more vibrant. Consider visiting a hairstylist who can give you advice on what suits your face shape best.

You should also consider your hair texture and length. As we age, our hair often becomes thinner, and shorter hairstyles can make it look fuller. However, if you've always had long hair and feel comfortable with it, there's no reason why you can't keep it that way.

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles are a popular choice for women over 60. They're easy to maintain and can make you look more youthful. A pixie cut or a bob can work well, especially if you have fine hair that needs a little volume. You can also add layers to your hair to create more texture and movement.

Medium-Length Hairstyles

Medium-length hairstyles are also a great option if you want to keep your hair longer. A shoulder-length cut with layers and side-swept bangs can frame your face and give you a more youthful look. You can also add highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.

Long Hairstyles

If you've always had long hair and feel comfortable with it, there's no reason why you can't keep it that way. However, you may need to adjust your hairstyle to suit your age. Consider adding layers to your hair to create more volume and texture. You can also add waves or curls to create a more relaxed, casual look.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a gorgeous hairstyle that suits a 60 year old woman:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  3. If you have long hair, consider adding layers to create more texture and movement.
  4. If you have fine hair, consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.
  5. If you have short hair, use a styling product such as a wax or pomade to create definition and texture.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or setting spray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a gorgeous hairstyle at 60:

  • Use products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Consider visiting a hairstylist who can give you advice on what suits your face shape best.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find one that suits your personality and lifestyle.
  • Add layers or highlights to create more texture and dimension.
  • Consider using styling products to create definition and texture.

FAQ

Q: Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. A short hairstyle can make you look more youthful and vibrant, especially if you add layers or texture.

Q: Can I still have long hair at 60?

A: Absolutely. If you feel comfortable with long hair, there's no reason why you can't keep it that way. Just make sure you adjust your hairstyle to suit your age.

Hair Care

As we age, our hair can become thinner, drier, and lose its natural shine. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how should a 60 year old woman wear her hair, you can experiment with different hairstyles and find one that suits your personality and lifestyle. Remember, the key is to find a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
