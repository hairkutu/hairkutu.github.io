What Purple Hair Says About You A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter May 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Changing your hair color is a big decision, and it's not just about choosing a shade that complements your skin tone. Your hair color can say a lot about your personality, and if you're considering dyeing your hair purple, you may be wondering what it says about you. In this guide, we'll explore the meaning behind purple hair and what it says about your personality.

Description

Purple hair is a bold and daring choice that's sure to attract attention. It's a vibrant color that's associated with creativity, individuality, and a sense of adventure. Purple hair can be achieved through a variety of techniques, from temporary hair chalks to semi-permanent dyes that last for several weeks.

How to Dye Your Hair Purple

If you're considering dyeing your hair purple, here's how to do it:

  1. Choose your shade of purple. There are many different shades of purple to choose from, including lavender, lilac, and deep violet.
  2. Lighten your hair. If you have dark hair, you'll need to lighten it before you can achieve a vibrant shade of purple.
  3. Apply the dye. Follow the instructions on the box of dye, and be sure to protect your skin and clothing from staining.
  4. Rinse and condition. Rinse the dye out of your hair, and apply a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Purple Hair

If you're ready to take the plunge and dye your hair purple, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose Your Shade of Purple

There are many different shades of purple to choose from, ranging from pastel hues to deep, dark purples. Consider your skin tone and personal style when choosing your shade.

Step 2: Lighten Your Hair

If you have dark hair, you'll need to lighten it before you can achieve a vibrant shade of purple. This can be done with a bleach kit or by visiting a professional stylist.

Step 3: Apply the Dye

Follow the instructions on the box of dye, and be sure to protect your skin and clothing from staining. Apply the dye evenly throughout your hair, and leave it on for the recommended amount of time.

Step 4: Rinse and Condition

Rinse the dye out of your hair, and apply a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Repeat this process every few weeks to maintain your purple hair color.

Tips for Maintaining Purple Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your purple hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can also cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine, as these can also cause the color to fade.

FAQs

What does purple hair say about your personality?

Purple hair is associated with creativity, individuality, and a sense of adventure. It suggests that you're willing to take risks and stand out from the crowd.

Is it hard to maintain purple hair?

Like any hair color, maintaining purple hair requires some effort. You'll need to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid washing your hair too often, and protect your hair from the sun and chlorine to prevent fading.

Can anyone pull off purple hair?

Anyone can pull off purple hair, as long as they choose the right shade for their skin tone and personal style. It's a bold and daring choice, but it can be a lot of fun!

Hair Care for Purple Hair

Caring for purple hair requires a little extra effort to keep the color looking vibrant and fresh. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color from your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid exposure to the sun and chlorine, as these can also cause the color to fade.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know what purple hair says about you and how to achieve and maintain this bold color, you can confidently rock this daring look and show off your adventurous personality!
