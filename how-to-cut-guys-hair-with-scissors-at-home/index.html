How To Cut Guys Hair With Scissors At Home

Written by Frank Apr 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our step-by-step guide on how to cut guys hair with scissors at home. In this guide, we will teach you how to give a simple haircut to your male family members or friends. Cutting hair at home can save you a lot of money and time, and with the right tools and techniques, it can be a fun experience. Follow our easy-to-follow instructions and get ready to be your own hairstylist!

Description

With the current situation, visiting a hair salon may not be the best option for everyone. Cutting hair at home has become an alternative for many people who want to save money and stay safe. This guide will teach you how to cut guys hair with scissors at home without compromising the quality of the haircut. Cutting men's hair can be a lot easier than you think, and with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in no time.

How to Cut Guys Hair with Scissors at Home

Step 1: Preparation

The first step in cutting hair at home is to prepare the tools and the area. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, a spray bottle with water, a mirror, and a towel. Make sure that the area is clean and well-lit, and that you have enough space to move around.

Step 2: Wet the Hair

Before you start cutting, wet the hair with the spray bottle. Wet hair is easier to cut and style, and it will make it easier to see the hair's natural fall.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Use the comb to section the hair into manageable parts. Start with the sides and back, and then move to the top. Secure each section with a clip or a hair tie.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting from the bottom of the hair, and work your way up. Use the comb to hold the hair, and cut the hair with the scissors. Use the comb to guide the hair, and make sure that you cut the hair evenly.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

After you have cut the hair, use the scissors to blend the hair. Blend the sides and the top by cutting the hair at an angle. This will create a natural look and avoid any harsh lines.

Step 6: Trim the Hairline

Use the scissors to trim the hairline around the ears and the neck. Be careful not to cut too much, and make sure that the hairline is even.

Step 7: Check the Hair

After you have finished cutting, check the hair in the mirror. Make sure that the hair is even, and that there are no uneven sections.

Tips

- Start with a longer length and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
- Use the comb to hold the hair and cut the hair at an angle for a natural look.
- Take breaks and step back to check the haircut.
- Use hair clippers for more complex haircuts.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, which are sharper and more precise.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair's length and style. Generally, hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, but it may be difficult to cut the back of your hair. It's always best to have someone help you or go to a salon if you're unsure.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting your hair, it's essential to take care of it. Use a hairbrush to remove any loose hair, and wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. You can also apply a hair mask to nourish and strengthen your hair.

That's it! You've learned how to cut guys hair with scissors at home. With the right tools and techniques, cutting hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. Remember to take your time, and don't be afraid to make mistakes. Happy cutting!
