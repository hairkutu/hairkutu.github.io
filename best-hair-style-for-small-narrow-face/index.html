Best Hair Style For Small Narrow Face

Written by Robby Mar 28, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a small narrow face, you might find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. However, with the right style and cut, you can enhance your facial features and look stunning. In this article, we’ll discuss the best hair style for small narrow face and offer tips on how to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Hair Style for Small Narrow Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Get the Best Hair Style for Small Narrow Face
  4. Tips to Maintain Your Hair Style
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Small Narrow Face

Description

A small narrow face is characterized by a slim face shape with a narrow forehead and jawline. The best hair style for small narrow face should add width and volume to the hair to balance the face. A layered cut or a style with soft waves can help create an illusion of width and volume.

How to Choose the Best Hair Style for Small Narrow Face

When choosing the best hair style for small narrow face, consider the following:

  • Length: Short hairstyles are ideal for small narrow faces as they add volume to the hair. A bob cut or a pixie cut can work wonders for your face shape.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume to the hair and create an illusion of width. Opt for a layered cut that frames your face and adds volume to the crown.
  • Bangs: Bangs can work wonders for small narrow faces. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs can add width to your forehead and balance your face shape.
  • Curls: Soft waves or curls can add volume and width to your hair. Avoid tight curls that can make your face appear smaller.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get the Best Hair Style for Small Narrow Face

Here’s a step-by-step guide to get the best hair style for small narrow face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect style for your face shape.
  2. Choose a style that adds volume and width to your hair.
  3. Opt for a layered cut that frames your face and adds volume to the crown.
  4. Consider adding bangs to add width to your forehead.
  5. Style your hair with soft waves or curls to add volume to your hair.

Tips to Maintain Your Hair Style

Here are some tips to maintain your hair style:

  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the style.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Style your hair with a round brush to add volume and bounce.

FAQs

1. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a small narrow face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a small narrow face. However, make sure to add volume to your hair and avoid tight ponytails that can make your face appear smaller.

2. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a small narrow face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a small narrow face. However, make sure to add volume to your hair and avoid flat hairstyles that can make your face appear smaller.

Hair Care Tips for Small Narrow Face

Here are some hair care tips for small narrow face:

  • Avoid using heating tools on your hair too often as they can damage your hair and make it appear thinner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair as they can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

With the right hair style and care, you can enhance your small narrow face and look stunning. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect style for your face shape and use the tips mentioned in this article to maintain your hair.
