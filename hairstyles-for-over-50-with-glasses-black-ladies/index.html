Hairstyles For Over 50 With Glasses Black Ladies

Written by Daniel Apr 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair and skin change, and we have to adjust our style accordingly. This is especially true for black women over 50 who wear glasses. In this article, we will provide you with a list of hairstyles that you can rock at any age while wearing glasses. We will also give you some tips on how to care for your hair and maintain your glasses. So, let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you wear glasses. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape. The right hairstyle can enhance your facial features and make you look younger. If you have a round face, opt for a hairstyle that adds height to your crown. If you have a square face, choose a hairstyle that softens your jawline.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your glasses. Your glasses should enhance your hairstyle, not clash with it. If you have bold glasses, go for a simple hairstyle. If you have delicate glasses, you can experiment with bolder hairstyles.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you are a busy person, you might want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. If you have more time, you can opt for a more elaborate hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair with Glasses

Now that you have chosen the right hairstyle, here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair with glasses:

  1. Wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Apply a styling product. This could be a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair type and the hairstyle you want to achieve.
  3. Blow-dry your hair. Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.
  4. Style your hair. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls, or use a hairbrush to create a sleek look.
  5. Put on your glasses. Adjust them so that they sit comfortably on your nose.
  6. Add a finishing touch. This could be a hair accessory, such as a headband or a hairpin.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair and Glasses

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair and glasses:

  • Get regular haircuts. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week. This will help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Clean your glasses regularly. Use a microfiber cloth to clean your glasses and avoid scratching the lenses.
  • Store your glasses in a case. This will help protect them from scratches and damage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for black women over 50 with glasses:

What hairstyles are best for black women over 50 with glasses?

The best hairstyles for black women over 50 with glasses are those that complement their face shape, glasses, and lifestyle. Some popular hairstyles include short pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and layered hairstyles.

Should I wear my glasses on top of my hair or under my hair?

It's a personal preference. Some women prefer to wear their glasses on top of their hair, while others prefer to tuck their hair behind their ears and wear their glasses under their hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you might need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips for Black Women Over 50

Here are some hair care tips for black women over 50:

  • Moisturize your hair. Black hair tends to be dry, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling. Heat styling can damage your hair and make it brittle. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Protect your hair at night. Use a silk or satin pillowcase, or wear a silk or satin scarf to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Eat a healthy diet. A healthy diet can promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. Eat foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals, such as fruits, vegetables, and lean protein.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock any look with your glasses and feel confident at any age. Remember to take care of your hair and glasses so that they last longer and look their best.
