Women s Hair Long On Top Short On Sides And Back The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel May 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle, then the long on top short on sides and back is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been a popular choice among women for its versatility and low maintenance. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this hairstyle, tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions. So, let's dive in!

Description

The long on top short on sides and back hairstyle is a combination of long, voluminous hair on the top, and short hair on the sides and back. This hairstyle can be customized to suit individual preferences, such as the length of the top hair, the fade or taper of the sides and back, and the overall shape of the hairstyle. This hairstyle is popular among women of all ages and hair types, and it can be styled in various ways, such as sleek and straight, curly, or messy.

How To

Step 1: Consultation

The first step to achieving the long on top short on sides and back hairstyle is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They will assess your hair type, face shape, and personal preferences to determine the best way to customize the hairstyle to suit you.

Step 2: Cutting

The hairstylist will then start by cutting the sides and back of your hair short, usually with clippers. They will gradually blend the length into the longer hair on the top to create a seamless transition. The length of the top can vary, but it is usually left long enough to be styled in various ways.

Step 3: Styling

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair according to your preference. You can use a hair product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to the top hair. You can also use a hairdryer and a round brush to create volume and shape to the top hair.

Tips

Here are some tips on how to maintain and style your long on top short on sides and back hairstyle:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back of your hair to maintain the shape and fade.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and styling preferences.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as slicked back, messy, or curly.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot styling tools.
  • Wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Is the long on top short on sides and back hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit all face shapes. The hairstylist will adjust the length and shape of the hairstyle to complement your features.

Can I style the top hair in different ways?

Yes, the beauty of this hairstyle is its versatility. You can style the top hair in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, curly, or straight.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim the sides and back of your hair every few weeks to maintain the shape and fade. You can trim the top hair less often, depending on how fast it grows and how long you want to keep it.

Hair Care

To keep your long on top short on sides and back hairstyle looking its best, you should follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot styling tools.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to rock your long on top short on sides and back hairstyle with confidence!
