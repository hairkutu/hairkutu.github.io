How Do I Cover A Bald Spot On My Crown

Written by Frank May 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are experiencing hair loss, you are not alone. Millions of people around the world are facing the same issue. Bald spots on the crown can be a result of several factors such as genetics, age, hormonal changes, and stress. While there are some treatments available to regrow hair, covering the bald spot is a quick and easy solution. Here's how to do it:

Description

A bald spot on the crown can be noticeable and can affect your self-esteem. Covering it up can help boost your confidence and improve your appearance. There are several ways to cover a bald spot, including hair fibers, wigs, hairpieces, and hair tattoos.

How to Cover a Bald Spot on My Crown

1. Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are tiny, colored fibers that you sprinkle onto your hair to make it look thicker and fuller. They are made from keratin, the same protein that makes up your hair. Hair fibers cling to your hair strands, creating the appearance of thicker hair. To use hair fibers, follow these steps:

  • Clean and dry your hair.
  • Shake the hair fibers onto the bald spot.
  • Gently pat the fibers into place.
  • Style your hair as usual.

2. Wigs and Hairpieces

Wigs and hairpieces are a popular solution for covering bald spots. They come in a variety of styles and colors, so you can find one that matches your natural hair. To wear a wig or hairpiece, follow these steps:

  • Select a wig or hairpiece that matches your natural hair.
  • Place the wig or hairpiece on your head.
  • Adjust the wig or hairpiece to fit comfortably.
  • Style your hair as usual.

3. Hair Tattoos

Hair tattoos, also known as scalp micropigmentation, involve tattooing tiny dots on the scalp to create the appearance of hair follicles. This technique can create the illusion of a full head of hair, even if you have a bald spot. To get a hair tattoo, follow these steps:

  • Find a reputable hair tattoo artist.
  • Discuss your desired results with the artist.
  • Schedule an appointment for the procedure.
  • Follow the artist's aftercare instructions.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions to cover a bald spot on your crown using hair fibers:

  1. Clean and dry your hair.
  2. Shake the hair fibers onto the bald spot.
  3. Gently pat the fibers into place.
  4. Style your hair as usual.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cover a bald spot on your crown:

  • Choose a solution that matches your natural hair color.
  • Experiment with different products until you find one that works for you.
  • Consider consulting a hair loss specialist for more permanent solutions.

FAQ

Q. How long do hair fibers last?

A. Hair fibers can last up to 24 hours, but they may need to be reapplied throughout the day.

Q. Can I swim or shower with a wig or hairpiece?

A. Yes, but you should avoid getting the wig or hairpiece wet for extended periods.

Q. Is scalp micropigmentation painful?

A. The procedure may cause some discomfort, but most people find it tolerable.

Hair Care

While covering a bald spot can help improve your appearance, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.
  • Use gentle hair products that don't contain harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can cover your bald spot and maintain healthy hair.
