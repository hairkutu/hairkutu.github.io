Best Hair Color For Green Eyes 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Apr 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hair color but unsure of what would complement your green eyes? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best hair color for green eyes in 2020. We'll cover everything from how to choose the perfect shade to tips on maintaining your new hair color. Let's get started!

Description

Green eyes are rare and beautiful, and finding the perfect hair color to complement them can be a challenge. The right hair color can make your eyes pop and enhance your overall look. In this article, we'll explore the various hair colors that work best with green eyes and how to choose the perfect shade for you.

How to Choose the Best Hair Color for Green Eyes

When selecting the best hair color for green eyes, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining the best hair color for you. If you have fair skin, consider going for lighter hair colors like platinum blonde or light brown. If you have a warmer skin tone, opt for warmer hair colors like honey blonde or caramel brown.

Think About Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also influence the best hair color for you. If you have naturally light hair, consider staying within a few shades of your natural color. If you have darker hair, you might want to consider lightening your hair to make your eyes pop.

Consider Your Eye Color

While green eyes are the focus of this article, it's essential to consider the different shades of green. If you have light green eyes, consider going for lighter hair colors. If you have darker green eyes, opt for darker hair colors like chocolate brown or black.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Best Hair Color for Green Eyes

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the best hair color for green eyes:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Identify your skin tone. If you have fair skin, you have a cool skin tone. If you have a warmer skin tone, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 2: Identify Your Natural Hair Color

Determine your natural hair color. If you have light hair, you have a cool skin tone. If you have darker hair, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 3: Choose a Shade

Based on your skin tone and natural hair color, choose a hair color that complements your green eyes. If you have fair skin and light hair, consider going for platinum blonde or light brown. If you have warmer skin and darker hair, opt for caramel brown or chocolate brown.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you've found the perfect hair color for your green eyes, it's essential to maintain it. Here are a few tips:

Use Color-Safe Products

Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to extend the life of your hair color.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause your color to fade faster. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can cause your hair color to fade. Wear a hat or use a UV protection spray when spending time in the sun.

FAQ

What hair colors complement green eyes?

Hair colors like platinum blonde, light brown, caramel brown, and chocolate brown complement green eyes.

What hair colors should I avoid if I have green eyes?

Stay away from hair colors that clash with green, such as red or orange.

How often should I touch up my hair color?

It depends on the type of hair color you choose. Consult with your stylist for the best touch-up schedule.

Hair Care for Colored Hair

When you color your hair, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Colored hair can become dry and damaged. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Avoid Hot Water

Hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause color fading. Use lukewarm water when washing your hair.

Protect Your Hair at Night

Friction from your pillowcase can cause damage to your hair. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep.

Choosing the best hair color for green eyes can be a challenge, but it's worth it when you find the perfect shade. Use this guide to help you choose the right hair color for you and maintain its vibrancy for as long as possible. Happy coloring!
