How To Determine Your Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 16, 2023 · 9 min read

Determining the right hairstyle for you can be a daunting task. There are so many options out there, from pixie cuts to long layers, and it can be overwhelming to try to figure out what will work best for your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this guide, we’ll take you through the steps to determine your perfect hairstyle, so you can feel confident and beautiful every day.

Table of Contents:

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type
  • Step 3: Find Inspiration
  • Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs About Choosing a Hairstyle
  • Hair Care Tips for Your New Hairstyle

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining your hairstyle is to figure out your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and study your face in the mirror. Look at the width and length of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline to see which shape best fits your features.

Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a style with longer layers to elongate your face. If you have a square face, you may want to choose a style with soft, rounded edges to soften your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step in determining your hairstyle is to consider your hair type. Do you have thin or thick hair? Is it curly or straight? Do you have any problem areas, such as frizz or breakage? Knowing your hair type will help you choose a style that works with your hair’s natural texture and needs.

For example, if you have thick, curly hair, you may want to choose a style with layers to add movement and reduce bulk. If you have fine, straight hair, you may want to choose a style with blunt cuts to add volume and texture.

Step 3: Find Inspiration

Once you know your face shape and hair type, it’s time to start looking for inspiration. Browse magazines, websites, and social media to find styles that catch your eye. Save photos of your favorite hairstyles to show your hairstylist later on.

Keep in mind that the style you choose should not only look good on you, but also fit your lifestyle and personality. If you’re an active person who spends a lot of time outdoors, you may want to choose a low-maintenance style that can withstand wind and sweat. If you’re a creative type who loves to experiment with new looks, you may want to choose a bold, edgy style that showcases your personality.

Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist

Once you have a few styles in mind, it’s time to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you determine which styles will work best for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and can also offer suggestions for tweaks or modifications to make a style your own.

Be sure to bring photos of your favorite styles to your appointment, and be open to your stylist’s suggestions. They are the experts, and can help you choose a style that will make you look and feel your best.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a style.
  • Find inspiration from magazines, websites, and social media.
  • Choose a style that fits your lifestyle and personality.
  • Consult with a hairstylist for their expert opinion.
  • Be open to suggestions and modifications to make a style your own.

FAQs About Choosing a Hairstyle

What if I don’t know my face shape?

If you’re not sure of your face shape, try taking a selfie and tracing the outline of your face with a marker or lipstick. Then, compare it to the six basic face shapes to see which one matches best.

What if I have multiple hair types?

If you have multiple hair types, such as curly and fine, be sure to talk to your stylist about how to best work with your hair’s unique characteristics. They may recommend products or styling techniques to help you achieve your desired look.

What if I don’t like the style my stylist recommends?

It’s important to be open to your stylist’s suggestions, but ultimately, you should choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. If you don’t like the style they recommend, speak up and let them know what you’re looking for.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Hairstyle

Once you’ve chosen your perfect hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type, and avoid over-styling or using too much heat on your hair. Be sure to schedule regular trims to keep your style looking fresh, and enjoy your new look!
