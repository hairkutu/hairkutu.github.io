How To Do A Chignon Yourself Short Hair

Written by William Mar 28, 2023 · 6 min read

In this year 2023, chignon hairstyles are still a popular choice for special events and even everyday wear. If you have short hair, you may think that a chignon is out of reach for you. However, with the right technique and a little practice, you can create a beautiful chignon yourself at home. Follow these steps to learn how to do a chignon yourself short hair.

Description

A chignon is a classic French twist hairstyle that is created by twisting the hair into a knot at the back of the head. It is an elegant and sophisticated look that works well for formal occasions or even just to add a touch of glamour to your everyday style. If you have short hair, you can still achieve a chignon by using bobby pins and hair ties to secure the hair in place.

How to

To create a chignon on short hair, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before you begin. A chignon works best on hair that is slightly dirty, so if your hair is too clean, add a bit of dry shampoo or hair spray to give it some texture and hold.

Step 2: Create a low ponytail

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and create a low ponytail. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 3: Twist the ponytail

Twist the ponytail until it starts to coil around itself. Keep twisting until the entire ponytail is twisted into a tight spiral.

Step 4: Coil the twisted ponytail

Coil the twisted ponytail around itself to create a small bun. Tuck the end of the ponytail under the bun and secure it in place with bobby pins.

Step 5: Secure the bun

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Make sure to hide the bobby pins so that they are not visible.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the chignon in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect chignon:

  • Use a hair donut or sock bun to create a fuller chignon.
  • Add accessories like flowers or jeweled pins to dress up your chignon.
  • Practice the technique a few times before wearing it to a special event.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about chignon hairstyles:

Can I do a chignon on curly hair?

Yes, you can do a chignon on curly hair. Follow the same steps but use bobby pins to secure the hair in place instead of a hair tie.

How long does a chignon last?

A chignon can last all day if it is secured properly with bobby pins and hairspray. It is a great hairstyle for special events because it stays in place and looks elegant all night long.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to do a chignon yourself short hair, you can create this elegant hairstyle anytime you want to add a touch of glamour to your look. With a little practice, you will be able to create a perfect chignon that is sure to turn heads.
