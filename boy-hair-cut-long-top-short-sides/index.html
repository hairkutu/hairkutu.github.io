Boy Hair Cut Long Top Short Sides: A Trendy Look for Boys

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Boy hair cut long top short sides is a trendy hairstyle for boys that has been gaining popularity in recent years. This hairstyle features a longer top that is styled in different ways, such as a pompadour, slick back, or messy look, while the sides and back are kept short. This haircut is perfect for boys who want a stylish and modern look that is easy to maintain.


How to

If you want to achieve a boy hair cut long top short sides, the first step is to find a good hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. You can bring in pictures of the hairstyle you like to show your stylist what you want.


Step by Step

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it so that it's damp.
  2. Next, your stylist will use clippers to trim the sides and back of your head to the desired length, typically between 1/8 to 1/2 inch, depending on your preference.
  3. The top of your hair will be left longer, usually around 2 to 4 inches, and your stylist will cut it to the length and style you want.
  4. You can choose to style the top in various ways, such as a pompadour, slick back, or messy look, using hair products like pomade, gel or wax.
  5. Your stylist may also use scissors to blend the top and sides, making the transition between the two lengths smooth.
  6. Once your hair is styled, your stylist will use a blow dryer to set the style and finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Make sure to find a stylist who has experience with this type of haircut to ensure the best results.
  • Bring in pictures of the hairstyle you want to show your stylist.
  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Use high-quality hair products to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

What face shapes suit this hairstyle?

This hairstyle works well for boys with oval, square, or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I get this haircut?

You should get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the haircut.

What hair products should I use?

You can use pomade, gel, or wax to style the top of your hair and a finishing product like hairspray to hold the style in place.


Hair Care

To maintain your boy hair cut long top short sides, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
