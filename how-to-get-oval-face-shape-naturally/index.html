How To Get Oval Face Shape Naturally A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 07, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of your round or square face shape and want to achieve an oval face shape naturally? You're in the right place! In this article, we'll guide you through step-by-step methods to get an oval face shape naturally. We'll also answer some frequently asked questions about face shapes and hair care tips to maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • What Is Face Shape?
  • How to Get Oval Face Shape Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Methods to Get Oval Face Shape Naturally
  • Tips to Maintain Oval Face Shape
  • FAQs

What Is Face Shape?

Face shape refers to the natural shape of your face. There are several face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, oblong, and diamond. The oval face shape is considered the most balanced and desirable face shape as it has a well-proportioned forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

How to Get Oval Face Shape Naturally

While there are several cosmetic procedures and makeup techniques to achieve an oval face shape, we'll focus on natural methods that don't involve any surgery or makeup. Here are some ways to get an oval face shape naturally:

1. Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and strengthen the muscles in your face, giving it a more defined and sculpted look. Some facial exercises that can help achieve an oval face shape include:

  • Jaw Release Exercise
  • Smiling Fish Exercise
  • Chin Lifts
  • Cheek Puff Exercise

2. Diet and Hydration

Eating a balanced diet rich in nutrients and drinking plenty of water can help reduce facial bloating and promote overall skin health. Avoid consuming excess salt and alcohol, which can cause water retention and puffiness in the face.

3. Sleep and Stress Management

Getting enough sleep and managing stress levels can also contribute to a more defined and oval face shape. Lack of sleep and high stress levels can cause facial swelling and puffiness.

Step-by-Step Methods to Get Oval Face Shape Naturally

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving an oval face shape naturally:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start working towards an oval face shape, you need to determine your current face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and examine your face to identify if it's round, square, heart, oblong, or diamond-shaped.

Step 2: Identify Your Facial Features

Once you've determined your face shape, identify your facial features, including your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. This will help you understand which areas you need to work on to achieve an oval face shape.

Step 3: Start Facial Exercises

Start incorporating facial exercises into your daily routine to tone and strengthen the muscles in your face. Begin with simple exercises and gradually increase the intensity and duration as you progress.

Step 4: Maintain a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet rich in nutrients and avoid consuming excess salt and alcohol. Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and promote overall skin health.

Step 5: Get Enough Sleep and Manage Stress Levels

Make sure you're getting enough sleep and managing stress levels to reduce facial swelling and puffiness.

Tips to Maintain Oval Face Shape

Here are some tips to maintain your new oval face shape:

  • Continue doing facial exercises regularly
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water
  • Get enough sleep and manage stress levels
  • Use sunscreen to protect your skin from sun damage
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption

FAQs

1. Can I achieve an oval face shape naturally?

Yes, you can achieve an oval face shape naturally by incorporating facial exercises, maintaining a balanced diet, and managing stress levels.

2. How long does it take to get an oval face shape naturally?

The time it takes to get an oval face shape naturally varies from person to person. It depends on factors such as your current face shape, facial features, and consistency in following the methods.

3. Can facial exercises help reduce wrinkles?

Yes, facial exercises can help reduce wrinkles and fine lines by toning and strengthening the muscles in your face.

4. How often should I do facial exercises?

You should do facial exercises at least 3-4 times a week to see noticeable results.

5. What haircuts suit oval face shapes?

Haircuts that suit oval face shapes include long layers, shoulder-length cuts, and pixie cuts with side-swept bangs.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shapes

Here are some hair care tips for oval face shapes:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head
  • Opt for hairstyles that frame your face and highlight your cheekbones
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find what suits you best
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage

By following these natural methods and tips, you can achieve an oval face shape that compliments your facial features and enhances your overall appearance. Remember, consistency is key, and it may take time to see noticeable results. Stay patient and committed to your routine, and you'll soon achieve the desired results.
