How To Get Fluffy Hair Guys The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 09, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a guy who wants to achieve fluffy hair, you've come to the right place. Fluffy hair is all the rage and can add a playful, youthful look to your overall appearance. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get fluffy hair guys. From how to style your hair to the best products to use, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Fluffy Hair Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Fluffy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Fluffy Hair

How to Get Fluffy Hair Guys

There are several ways to achieve fluffy hair. The first step is to ensure that your hair is healthy and well-maintained. This includes regular washing, conditioning, and using a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Next, you'll want to invest in some styling products that can help add volume and texture to your hair. Some of the best products for fluffy hair include mousse, volumizing spray, and texturizing powder.

Another important factor to consider when trying to get fluffy hair is your hairstyle. Some hairstyles work better than others when it comes to achieving a fluffy, voluminous look. For example, a layered haircut or a shaggy style can help add volume and texture to your hair.

Product Recommendations:

  • Redken Guts 10 Volumizing Spray
  • Bed Head by TIGI Small Talk Thickifier
  • Living Proof Full Thickening Mousse

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get fluffy hair guys:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing spray or mousse to your roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing powder to add even more volume and texture.
  5. Style your hair as desired, using your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

Tips for Maintaining Fluffy Hair

Once you've achieved the perfect fluffy hairstyle, you'll want to make sure you maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking fluffy and voluminous:

  • Don't over-wash your hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to flat, lifeless hair.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down, such as pomades or waxes.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can flatten your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look flat.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to get fluffy hair guys:

Q: Can I achieve fluffy hair without using styling products?

A: While styling products can definitely help add volume and texture to your hair, you can still achieve a fluffy look without them. Try blow drying your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift.

Q: Can I achieve fluffy hair with short hair?

A: Yes! While longer hair can be easier to style into a fluffy look, you can still achieve a voluminous style with short hair. Consider getting a layered haircut or using a texturizing product to add volume to your hair.

Hair Care Products for Fluffy Hair

Here are some additional hair care products that can help you achieve and maintain fluffy hair:

  • Redken High Rise Volume Lifting Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Bumble and Bumble Thickening Spray
  • Not Your Mother's Beach Babe Texturizing Sea Salt Spray

With a little bit of effort and the right products, anyone can achieve fluffy hair guys. Follow these tips and tricks, and you'll be rocking a voluminous, playful hairstyle in no time!
