Updos For Short Thin Straight Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have short, thin, and straight hair, you might think that updos are not for you. But with the right techniques and styling tips, you can create stunning updos that will make you look and feel amazing. In this article, we will share some of the best updos for short thin straight hair and give you step-by-step instructions on how to achieve them. We will also answer some frequently asked questions and provide you with some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and beautiful. So, let's get started!

How to Create an Updo for Short Thin Straight Hair

Creating an updo for short thin straight hair requires some creativity and patience. Here are the steps you can follow:

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your locks. Then, blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.

Step 2: Choose your updo style

There are many updo styles you can choose from, such as a messy bun, a braided updo, a twisted updo, or a sleek updo. Choose the one that suits your hair length, face shape, and personal style.

Step 3: Prep your hair

Apply a texturizing spray or a styling mousse to your hair to create grip and hold. This will help your updo stay in place for longer. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 4: Create your updo

Follow the instructions for your chosen updo style. You can use bobby pins, hair ties, or clips to secure your hair in place. Don't worry if it's not perfect â messy updos are trendy and effortless.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Spray some hairspray on your updo to add shine and hold. You can also use a shine spray or a serum to add extra gloss to your locks.

Tips for Creating Updos for Short Thin Straight Hair

Here are some tips and tricks to help you create updos for short thin straight hair:

Tip 1: Use hair extensions

If you have very short hair, you can use clip-in hair extensions to add length and volume to your locks. This will make it easier to create updos and give you more styling options.

Tip 2: Tease your hair

Teasing your hair at the roots can add volume and texture to your locks. Use a teasing brush to lightly backcomb your hair, then smooth the surface with a comb or a brush.

Tip 3: Experiment with accessories

You can add a headband, a ribbon, a flower, or a hairpin to your updo to make it more interesting and stylish. Choose an accessory that complements your outfit and your personal style.

FAQ: Updos for Short Thin Straight Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about updos for short thin straight hair:

Q: Can I create an updo with a bob haircut?

A: Yes, you can create an updo with a bob haircut. You can try a messy bun, a half-up half-down style, or a braided updo. Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place.

Q: How can I add volume to my thin and straight hair?

A: You can add volume to your thin and straight hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using a texturizing spray or a dry shampoo. You can also tease your hair at the roots.

Q: How can I make my updo last longer?

A: You can make your updo last longer by using a strong-hold hairspray, avoiding touching your hair too much, and securing your hair with bobby pins or clips. You can also use a hairnet to keep your updo in place.

Hair Care Tips for Short Thin Straight Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short thin straight hair healthy and beautiful:

Tip 1: Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that does not contain harsh chemicals or sulfates. This will prevent your hair from getting dry and brittle.

Tip 2: Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons, curling irons, or hot rollers. These can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

Tip 3: Get regular trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will also help your hair look thicker and fuller.

Creating updos for short thin straight hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and styling tips, you can achieve stunning results. Follow our step-by-step instructions, use our tips and tricks, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Have fun experimenting with different updo styles and accessories, and don't be afraid to try something new!
