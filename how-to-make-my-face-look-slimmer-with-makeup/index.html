How To Make My Face Look Slimmer With Makeup A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Apr 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to create a slimmer, more defined face shape, makeup can be your best friend. By strategically highlighting and contouring your features, you can sculpt your face and create the illusion of a more chiseled jawline, higher cheekbones, and a more streamlined profile.

In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a slimmer face shape using makeup, as well as some tips and tricks to make the most of your look. Let's get started!

How to Make My Face Look Slimmer with Makeup: Step by Step

Step 1: Prep your skin

Before you start applying makeup, it's important to prep your skin for the best possible results. Start by cleansing and moisturizing your face, then apply a primer to help your makeup last longer and go on more smoothly. Be sure to choose a primer that's appropriate for your skin type, whether that's oily, dry, or combination.

Step 2: Apply foundation

Next, apply your foundation evenly over your face, using a brush, sponge, or your fingers. Be sure to blend the foundation well around your hairline, jawline, and neck to avoid any telltale lines.

Step 3: Highlight your features

To create a slimmer face shape, you'll want to highlight the areas that you want to bring forward. This typically includes the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, the tops of your cheekbones, and your cupid's bow (the V-shaped area above your upper lip).

Use a light-colored concealer or highlighter to apply a small amount of product to each of these areas, then blend well with a brush or sponge to create a subtle, natural-looking effect.

Step 4: Contour your features

Now it's time to contour! This step involves applying a darker shade of makeup to the areas of your face that you want to recede. Typically, this includes your temples, the hollows of your cheeks, the sides of your nose, and your jawline.

Choose a contour product that complements your skin tone and apply it sparingly, building up the intensity gradually until you achieve the desired effect. Be sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines or streaks.

Step 5: Add blush

Finally, add a touch of blush to the apples of your cheeks to give your face a natural, healthy glow. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and apply it lightly, blending well for a seamless finish.

Tips for Making Your Face Look Slimmer with Makeup

Now that you know the basic steps to creating a slimmer face shape with makeup, here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your look:

  • Choose a matte finish foundation to avoid adding shine or bulk to your face.
  • Use a small, fluffy brush to apply your contour product, blending well as you go.
  • Avoid applying too much product at once to avoid a heavy, cakey look.
  • When applying blush, smile to find the apples of your cheeks and apply the product there, blending upward and outward.
  • Experiment with different shades and intensities of contour and highlight products until you find the perfect combination for your skin tone and face shape.

FAQs about Making Your Face Look Slimmer with Makeup

Q: Can makeup really make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes! By strategically highlighting and contouring your features, you can create the illusion of a more defined, streamlined face shape.

Q: Do I need to use expensive makeup products to achieve a slimmer face shape?

A: Not necessarily! While high-end makeup products can certainly be effective, there are also plenty of affordable options available that can give you great results.

Q: How do I choose the right shades of contour and highlight products?

A: It's important to choose shades that complement your skin tone and natural features. Start with a product that's only slightly darker or lighter than your skin tone, then build up the intensity gradually until you achieve the desired effect.

Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Slimmer Face Shape

Creating a slimmer face shape with makeup is just one piece of the puzzle. Here are some additional hair care tips to help you make the most of your new look:

  • Consider getting a haircut that complements your face shape, such as a bob or long layers.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume at the crown of your head, like a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, like a heavy, blunt fringe or a center part.
  • Experiment with different hair colors and highlights to find a shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your features.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to creating a slimmer, more defined face shape with makeup. Have fun experimenting and finding the perfect look for you!
