What Blush Suits My Skin Tone

Written by Robby Jul 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some color to your cheeks, blush is a great option. However, not all blush shades are created equal. The right shade of blush can enhance your natural beauty, while the wrong shade can make you look washed out or overdone. In this post, we'll explore what blush suits your skin tone and how to choose the perfect shade.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Blush Shades for Cool Skin Tones
  • Blush Shades for Warm Skin Tones
  • Blush Shades for Neutral Skin Tones
  • How to Apply Blush
  • Blush Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the perfect blush shade is determining your skin tone. Your skin tone can be categorized as cool, warm, or neutral.

Cool skin tones have pink, red, or blue undertones, while warm skin tones have yellow, peach, or golden undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear both blue and green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Blush Shades for Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, opt for blush shades with pink or berry undertones. Shades like rose, mauve, and cherry are great options. Avoid blush shades with orange or brown undertones, as they can make your skin look sallow.

Some great blush options for cool skin tones include:

  • Maybelline Fit Me Blush in Deep Rose
  • NARS Blush in Orgasm
  • Tarte Amazonian Clay 12-Hour Blush in Dollface

Blush Shades for Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, opt for blush shades with peach, coral, or bronze undertones. Shades like apricot, tangerine, and terracotta are great options. Avoid blush shades with pink or purple undertones, as they can clash with your skin tone.

Some great blush options for warm skin tones include:

  • Milani Baked Blush in Luminoso
  • NYX Professional Makeup High Definition Blush in Amber
  • Physicians Formula Butter Blush in Natural Glow

Blush Shades for Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you're lucky! You can pretty much wear any blush shade you like. However, if you're unsure, opt for blush shades with a mix of warm and cool undertones, like dusty rose or soft peach.

Some great blush options for neutral skin tones include:

  • Benefit Cosmetics Dandelion Blush
  • MAC Cosmetics Powder Blush in Melba
  • Nudestix Nudies Bloom All Over Dewy Color in Sweet Peach Peony

How to Apply Blush

Now that you've chosen the perfect blush shade for your skin tone, it's time to learn how to apply it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with a clean, moisturized face. Apply your foundation and concealer as you normally would.
  2. Dip a blush brush into your blush, tapping off any excess powder.
  3. Smile and apply the blush to the apples of your cheeks, blending upwards and outwards towards your temples. Make sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines or streaks.
  4. If you've applied too much blush, use a clean makeup sponge or brush to blend it out.
  5. Finish with a light dusting of setting powder to lock in your look.

Blush Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for applying blush:

  • Choose a blush brush that's the right size for your face. A smaller brush will give you more control, while a larger brush will give you a more diffused look.
  • Don't be afraid to mix blush shades to create your perfect color.
  • If you're using a cream or liquid blush, apply it with your fingers or a damp beauty sponge for a natural-looking finish.
  • For a more sculpted look, apply a darker shade of blush just below your cheekbones and blend well.

FAQs

Can I wear the same blush shade all year round?

While you can certainly wear the same blush shade all year round, it's a good idea to switch things up with the seasons. In the winter, opt for deeper, more dramatic shades, while in the summer, go for lighter, brighter shades.

What if I have acne-prone skin?

If you have acne-prone skin, look for blushes that are non-comedogenic and oil-free. Avoid blushes with shimmer or glitter, as they can accentuate texture on the skin.

Can I wear blush with a bold lip?

Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a blush shade that complements your lip color. For example, if you're wearing a bold red lip, opt for a soft pink or coral blush.

Choosing the perfect blush shade can seem intimidating, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the right shade for your skin tone in no time. Remember to have fun and experiment with different shades and application techniques to find your perfect look!
