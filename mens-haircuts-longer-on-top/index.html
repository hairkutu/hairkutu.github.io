Mens Haircuts Longer On Top A Trending Style In 2023

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, you might want to consider the mens haircuts longer on top. This haircut is perfect for men who want to look fashionable and modern. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look, step by step, and provide you with tips and FAQs to help you maintain your haircut.

How to Achieve Mens Haircuts Longer on Top

The mens haircuts longer on top is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized for different hair types and face shapes. To achieve this look, you need to have longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. You can also add layers or texture to make it more interesting.

It's important to choose the right length and style that suits your personality and lifestyle. Whether you want a messy or a slicked-back look, you need to communicate your preferences with your hairstylist to ensure that you get the haircut that you want.

Step by Step Guide

To achieve the mens haircuts longer on top, follow these steps:

  1. Choose the right length and style for your hair.
  2. Wash and dry your hair.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair to add texture or hold.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair on the top.
  5. Trim the sides and back with scissors or clippers.
  6. Blend the top and sides to create a seamless transition.
  7. Use a mirror to check for any gaps or unevenness.
  8. Adjust the length and style as needed.
  9. Apply a finishing product to add shine or hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To keep your mens haircuts longer on top looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that works best for you.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.

FAQs about Mens Haircuts Longer on Top

Q: What face shapes suit mens haircuts longer on top?

A: Mens haircuts longer on top can suit different face shapes, including oval, square, and diamond. It's important to choose the right length and style that balances your features and enhances your facial structure.

Q: Can I achieve mens haircuts longer on top with thin hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve mens haircuts longer on top with thin hair. You can add layers or texture to create volume and movement. You can also use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to boost your hair's thickness.

Q: How do I style my mens haircuts longer on top for a formal occasion?

A: To style your mens haircuts longer on top for a formal occasion, you can slick back your hair with a pomade or gel. You can also use a hair straightener to create a sleek and polished look.

Hair Care for Mens Haircuts Longer on Top

To maintain your mens haircuts longer on top, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every other day.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to nourish and protect your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or a scarf.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect mens haircuts longer on top that suits your style and personality. Whether you want a messy, textured look or a slicked-back, polished look, you can customize this haircut to fit your preferences. So, go ahead and try this trending hairstyle in 2023!
