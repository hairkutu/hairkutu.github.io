Can You Get A Digital Perm If You Have Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Mar 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you may be wondering if you can get a digital perm. The answer is yes! Digital perms can be done on any hair type, including curly hair. In this blog post, we will explore the process of getting a digital perm for curly hair, the steps involved, and tips for hair care afterwards.

Description

A digital perm is a modern version of the traditional perm, which uses digital technology to create more natural-looking waves or curls. The process involves wrapping the hair around special rods and then heating them with a machine that uses infrared rays to set the curl.

How to

If you're interested in getting a digital perm for your curly hair, here's what you should do:

Step 1: Find a Professional Stylist

The first step is to find a professional stylist who has experience with digital perms. Look for someone who has a portfolio of work that includes curly hair, and read reviews from past clients to ensure that they are reputable.

Step 2: Consultation

During the consultation, your stylist will assess your hair type, texture, and condition. They will also discuss your desired outcome and advise you on what to expect from the process. This is the perfect time to ask any questions and voice any concerns you may have.

Step 3: Prep Your Hair

Before the digital perm process begins, your stylist will wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. They may also trim your hair to ensure that it is healthy and ready for the perm.

Step 4: Perm Solution

Next, your stylist will apply a perm solution to your hair, which will break down the protein bonds that hold the hair's shape. This process will allow the hair to take on a new shape when wrapped around the rods.

Step 5: Rods and Heating

After the perm solution is applied, your stylist will wrap your hair around the rods and then use the digital perm machine to heat the rods. The heat will activate the perm solution and set the curl into place.

Step 6: Rinse and Neutralize

Once the curl has set, your stylist will rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any excess perm solution. They will then apply a neutralizer to your hair to rebalance the pH levels and stop the perming process.

Step 7: Blow-dry and Style

Finally, your stylist will blow-dry your hair and style it to your desired look. They may also recommend products to help maintain and care for your newly permed hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a digital perm for your curly hair:

  • Be patient - the process may take several hours
  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils
  • Avoid heat styling tools for the first few days after the perm
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying to help enhance the curls
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to hydrate and protect the hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about digital perms:

1. Will a digital perm damage my hair?

The perm solution and heat can cause some damage to the hair, but a professional stylist can minimize this by using the correct products and techniques.

2. How long will my digital perm last?

A digital perm can last up to six months, depending on how well you care for your hair afterwards.

3. Can I color my hair after getting a digital perm?

It is not recommended to color your hair immediately after getting a digital perm. It's best to wait at least two weeks to allow your hair to recover and restore its natural oils.

Hair Care

After getting a digital perm, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the curls and keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Don't over-wash your hair - once or twice a week is enough
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

In conclusion, if you have curly hair, you can definitely get a digital perm. Just make sure to find a professional stylist, follow the proper steps, and take care of your hair afterwards. With the right care and maintenance, you can enjoy beautiful, natural-looking curls for months to come!
