Mens Fade Haircuts Short On Top A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut and looking for something new and trendy? Look no further! Mens fade haircuts short on top are the latest craze in the hair world. This style features a gradual fade from short to long, leaving the hair on top of the head short and the sides and back gradually longer. This style is low maintenance, easy to style and looks great on all ages and hair types. Here's everything you need to know about this stylish haircut.

Description

Mens fade haircuts short on top feature a gradual fade from short to long, leaving the hair on top of the head short and the sides and back gradually longer. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type. It is a modern and trendy look that is suitable for any occasion, from casual to formal events. The short top allows for easy styling, while the gradual fade adds dimension and texture to the hair.

How to

The mens fade haircuts short on top can be achieved by a professional hairstylist or barber. Here's how to get the look:

Step 1: Consultation

Discuss with your hairstylist or barber about the desired length and style. Bring in a photo of the look you want to achieve for reference.

Step 2: Clippers

The sides and back of the head are usually cut using clippers. The length can vary depending on the desired fade.

Step 3: Scissors

The hair on top of the head is usually cut using scissors. The length can vary depending on the desired style.

Step 4: Blending

The hair is blended between the short top and the longer sides and back using clippers and scissors. This creates a gradual fade.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

The hair is styled using hair products, such as gel or pomade, to create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling your mens fade haircuts short on top:

  • Visit your hairstylist or barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look
  • Use hair products sparingly to avoid a greasy or oily look
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style
  • Use a comb or brush to style the hair into place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens fade haircuts short on top:

Is this style suitable for all ages?

Yes, this style looks great on all ages, from young boys to older men.

What face shape is best suited for this style?

This style is versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape.

Is this style low maintenance?

Yes, this style is low maintenance and easy to style.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors, such as wind and pollution

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to maintain your mens fade haircuts short on top and achieve the trendy and stylish look you desire. So, what are you waiting for? Book your appointment with your hairstylist or barber today and get ready to turn heads with your new look!
