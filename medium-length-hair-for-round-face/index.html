Medium Length Hair For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline May 11, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a bit tricky. You want a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. Medium length hair is a great option for round faces as it can add length and volume to your face. In this blog post, we will discuss how to choose the best medium length hair for your round face, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Choose the Best Medium Length Hair for Round Face
  2. How to Style Medium Length Hair for Round Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Medium Length Hair for Round Face
  4. Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair for Round Face
  5. FAQs on Medium Length Hair for Round Face
  6. Hair Care for Medium Length Hair for Round Face

How to Choose the Best Medium Length Hair for Round Face

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for your round face, there are a few key things to consider:

Length:

Medium length hair is defined as hair that falls between the chin and the shoulders. This length can be very flattering for round faces as it can add length and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Avoid hair that is too short as it can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Layers:

Layered hairstyles can be very flattering for round faces as they can add volume and movement to the hair. However, be careful not to add too many layers as this can make your face appear wider. Opt for long, face-framing layers that start below the chin.

Texture:

Medium length hair can look great with a variety of textures, from straight and sleek to curly and voluminous. Choose a texture that flatters your face shape and works with your natural hair type.

How to Style Medium Length Hair for Round Face

There are many ways to style medium length hair for round faces. Here are a few ideas:

Straight and Sleek:

Straight, sleek hair can be very flattering for round faces as it can create the illusion of length. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and finish with a shine serum for a polished look.

Beachy Waves:

Beachy waves can add volume and movement to medium length hair. Use a curling wand to create loose waves and finish with a texturizing spray for a beachy, tousled look.

Half-Up, Half-Down:

A half-up, half-down hairstyle can be a great option for medium length hair. Pull the top half of your hair back and secure with a clip or elastic. Leave the bottom half down for a casual, effortless look.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Medium Length Hair for Round Face

Here is a step-by-step guide for styling your medium length hair:

Step 1:

Start with clean, dry hair.

Step 2:

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.

Step 3:

Choose your desired style and use the appropriate styling tool to achieve it.

Step 4:

Finish with a hairspray, texturizing spray or other styling product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair for Round Face

Here are a few tips for maintaining your medium length hair:

Get Regular Trims:

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Use Heat Protectant:

Using a heat protectant spray before styling your hair can help prevent damage from hot styling tools.

Avoid Over-styling:

Over-styling your hair can lead to damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of hot styling tools and opt for air-drying your hair whenever possible.

FAQs on Medium Length Hair for Round Face

Q: Is medium length hair suitable for round faces?

A: Yes, medium length hair can be very flattering for round faces as it can add length and volume to your face.

Q: What kind of layers are best for round faces?

A: Long, face-framing layers that start below the chin can be very flattering for round faces.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair for Round Face

Here are a few tips for taking care of your medium length hair:

Wash your hair regularly:

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Use a deep conditioning treatment:

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Avoid over-brushing:

Brush your hair gently to avoid causing damage and breakage.

With these tips, you can rock your medium length hair and flatter your round face with confidence!
