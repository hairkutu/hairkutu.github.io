How To Hide A Big Chin With Makeup Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a big chin, you may feel self-conscious about it. But don't worry, there are ways to minimize the appearance of your chin with makeup. In this article, we will show you how to hide a big chin with makeup. We will cover everything from the right products to use to the techniques to apply them. So, let's get started.

How to Hide a Big Chin with Makeup: Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to hiding a big chin with makeup:

Step 1: Start with a Clean Face

Before applying any makeup, make sure your face is clean and moisturized. This will help the makeup go on smoothly and last longer.

Step 2: Apply a Primer

Apply a primer to your face to create a smooth canvas for your makeup. This will also help your makeup last longer.

Step 3: Apply Foundation

Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone and apply it all over your face. Make sure to blend it well into your neck and hairline.

Step 4: Apply Concealer

Apply a concealer that is one or two shades lighter than your skin tone under your eyes, on the bridge of your nose, and on your chin. Blend it well with a makeup sponge or brush.

Step 5: Contour your Face

Use a contour powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to contour your face. Apply it under your cheekbones, on the sides of your nose, and under your chin. This will help to minimize the appearance of your chin.

Step 6: Apply Blush

Apply a blush to the apples of your cheeks to add color to your face and distract from your chin. Choose a color that complements your skin tone.

Step 7: Highlight your Face

Apply a highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, nose, and brow bone. This will help to draw attention away from your chin.

Step 8: Apply Lipstick

Choose a lipstick color that complements your skin tone and apply it to your lips. This will help to balance out your face and draw attention away from your chin.

Tips for Hiding a Big Chin with Makeup

Here are some tips for hiding a big chin with makeup:

  • Use a matte foundation to avoid highlighting your chin.
  • Avoid shimmer or glitter on your chin as it will only draw attention to it.
  • Use a small amount of contour powder or cream under your chin to minimize its appearance.
  • Use a light hand when applying blush to avoid making your face look too round.

FAQs About Hiding a Big Chin with Makeup

Q: Can makeup really hide a big chin?

A: Yes, makeup can help to minimize the appearance of a big chin by contouring and highlighting your face.

Q: What are the best makeup products to use for hiding a big chin?

A: You will need a primer, foundation, concealer, contour powder or cream, blush, highlighter, and lipstick.

Q: Should I use a matte or dewy foundation?

A: Use a matte foundation to avoid highlighting your chin.

Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Makeup Look

Here are some hair care tips to complement your makeup look:

  • Style your hair in a way that draws attention away from your chin, such as a side part or loose waves.
  • Consider getting bangs to cover your forehead and draw attention away from your chin.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or sleek as they will only emphasize your chin.

By following these tips and tricks, you can learn how to hide a big chin with makeup and feel confident in your appearance.
