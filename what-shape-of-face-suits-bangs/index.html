What Shape Of Face Suits Bangs

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure if they will suit your face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss what shape of face suits bangs and give you some tips on how to style them.

Description

Bangs are a great way to change up your look, but not all face shapes suit them. The key is to find a style that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. Bangs can help to balance out your facial proportions and draw attention to your eyes, but they can also highlight any flaws or imperfections.

How to

The first step in determining what shape of face suits bangs is to identify your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, square, oblong, heart, diamond, and oval. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a bang style that will flatter your features.

Round Face

If you have a round face, you want to avoid blunt bangs that will make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that will elongate your face and draw attention to your cheekbones.

Square Face

Square faces have strong jawlines and angular features. Soft, wispy bangs or side-swept bangs can help to soften your features and make your face appear more oval-shaped.

Oblong Face

If you have an oblong face, you want to avoid long, straight bangs that will make your face appear even longer. Opt for a wispy fringe or side-swept bangs that will add width to your face.

Heart Face

Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrower chin. Side-swept bangs that hit at the cheekbones can help to balance out your features and make your forehead appear less pronounced.

Diamond Face

Diamond-shaped faces have wide cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. Soft, wispy bangs that hit at the brow bone can help to balance out your features and make your face appear more oval-shaped.

Oval Face

Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, as they can pull off almost any bang style. Blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, or a wispy fringe can all look great on an oval face.

Step by step

Once you have chosen a bang style that suits your face shape, it's important to know how to style them. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip the rest of your hair back.
  3. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush, directing the hair down and away from your face.
  4. If you want a sleeker look, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting bangs:

  • Communicate clearly with your hairstylist about the type of bangs you want.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your bangs.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Be prepared for regular maintenance, as bangs grow out quickly.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs:

How often should you trim your bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

Can you pull off bangs if you have curly hair?

Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a bang style that works with your curls and consider getting them cut while dry to ensure they don't shrink up too much.

Do bangs work with glasses?

Yes, bangs can look great with glasses! Just make sure to choose a style that doesn't interfere with your frames and keeps your eyes visible.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your bangs to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Blow-dry your bangs immediately after washing to prevent them from drying in an awkward shape.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can cause them to become oily or greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to freshen up your bangs.
  • Consider using a lightweight oil or serum to keep your bangs looking shiny and healthy.

Now that you know what shape of face suits bangs, you can confidently choose a style that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. Remember to communicate clearly with your hairstylist and experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.
