How To Find Hair Color That Suits You

Written by Daniel Jul 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Finding the perfect hair color can be a daunting task. With so many shades and options available, it can be difficult to know where to start. In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect hair color that suits you. We will cover everything from determining your skin tone to choosing the right shade.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair color is your skin tone. Your skin tone will determine which shades will look best on you. Here are some tips on how to determine your skin tone:

Step 1: Check Your Veins

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.

Step 2: Look at Your Skin

Look at your skin in natural light. If your skin has a pink or rosy undertone, you have cool undertones. If your skin has a yellow or golden undertone, you have warm undertones.

Step 3: Try the White Test

Hold a piece of white paper up to your face. If your skin appears yellowish or sallow, you have warm undertones. If your skin appears pinkish or rosy, you have cool undertones.

Choosing the Right Shade

Once you have determined your skin tone, it's time to choose the right shade. Here are some tips on how to choose the right shade:

Tip 1: Stick to Your Natural Color

If you're not sure which shade to choose, it's always a safe bet to stick to your natural color. This will ensure that the color looks natural and complements your skin tone.

Tip 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hair color, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you have a low-maintenance lifestyle, choose a color that requires minimal upkeep. If you have an active lifestyle, choose a color that won't fade easily.

Tip 3: Don't Go Too Dark or Too Light

Going too dark or too light can be a drastic change and may not suit your skin tone. Stick to shades that are within a few shades of your natural color.

FAQ

What if I have a neutral skin tone?

If you have a neutral skin tone, you can choose from a wide range of shades. Try warm and cool shades to see which looks best on you.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and choose a high-quality dye.

How often should I touch up my roots?

It depends on the color and how fast your hair grows. Generally, it's recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect shade, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips on how to care for colored hair:

Tip 1: Use a Color-Safe Shampoo

Use a shampoo that is specifically designed for colored hair. This will help to prevent fading and keep your color looking fresh.

Tip 2: Avoid Hot Tools

Hot tools can damage colored hair and cause the color to fade faster. Try to avoid using hot tools as much as possible or use a heat protectant spray.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can cause colored hair to fade and become dull. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Conclusion

Finding the perfect hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By determining your skin tone and choosing the right shade, you can find a color that suits you and complements your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
