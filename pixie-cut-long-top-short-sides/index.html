Pixie Cut Long Top Short Sides A Trendy And Edgy Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Aprilia May 15, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that’s both stylish and low-maintenance? Look no further than the pixie cut long top short sides. This edgy and trendy hairstyle has been popular for a few years now and is still going strong in 2023. In this blog post, we’ll cover everything you need to know about the pixie cut long top short sides, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

In this article, we’ll cover:

  • What is a pixie cut long top short sides?
  • How to get a pixie cut long top short sides
  • Styling tips for a pixie cut long top short sides
  • FAQ about pixie cut long top short sides
  • Hair care tips for a pixie cut long top short sides

What is a Pixie Cut Long Top Short Sides?

A pixie cut long top short sides is a hairstyle that features a longer top section and shorter sides and back. The longer top section can be styled in different ways, such as swept to the side or tousled for a messy look. The shorter sides and back can be tapered or buzzed, depending on your preference. This style is popular because it’s both stylish and low-maintenance.

How to Get a Pixie Cut Long Top Short Sides

If you’re interested in getting a pixie cut long top short sides, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, find a stylist who is experienced in cutting short hair. It’s important to work with someone who understands the nuances of this style and can create a look that complements your face shape and hair type.

When you’re ready to get your haircut, bring in pictures of the style you’re interested in. This will give your stylist a clear idea of what you’re looking for and help them create the perfect cut. Be prepared to spend some time discussing your hair goals with your stylist, as they may have suggestions for tweaks or modifications to make the style work for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Pixie Cut Long Top Short Sides

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a pixie cut long top short sides:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Sit down with your stylist and discuss the style you’re interested in.
  3. Your stylist will section your hair and begin cutting the back and sides.
  4. They’ll then move on to the top section, cutting it to the desired length.
  5. If you’d like, they can add layers or texture to the top section for more volume.
  6. Your stylist will then dry and style your hair to complete the look.

Styling Tips for a Pixie Cut Long Top Short Sides

Once you’ve got your pixie cut long top short sides, it’s time to start styling it. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to the top section.
  • If you’re going for a messy look, use your fingers to tousle your hair instead of a brush or comb.
  • To create a sleeker look, use a round brush and blow dryer to smooth out the top section.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ about Pixie Cut Long Top Short Sides

1. Who can wear a pixie cut long top short sides?

A pixie cut long top short sides can work on a variety of face shapes and hair types. However, it’s best suited for those with a strong jawline and delicate facial features. If you have a round or square face, you may want to consider a different style.

2. How often do I need to get my hair trimmed?

Since a pixie cut long top short sides is a short style, it needs to be trimmed more often than longer styles. Plan on getting a trim every four to six weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

3. Can I still style my hair for special occasions?

Yes! A pixie cut long top short sides can be styled in a variety of ways for special occasions. Try adding accessories like headbands or hair clips for a more festive look.

Hair Care Tips for a Pixie Cut Long Top Short Sides

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips for a pixie cut long top short sides:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock a pixie cut long top short sides in style. Whether you’re looking for a low-maintenance everyday look or something edgy and trendy, this style is sure to turn heads.
