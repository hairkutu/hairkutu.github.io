How To Wear Skinny Jeans Over 60 Tips Tricks And Faqs

Are you over 60 and wondering how to wear skinny jeans? You're not alone! Skinny jeans are a popular style, but they can be intimidating for older women. In this article, we'll share tips, tricks, and FAQs about how to wear skinny jeans over 60.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Wear Skinny Jeans Over 60

Skinny jeans are a versatile style that can be dressed up or down. Here are a few tips for wearing skinny jeans over 60:

Choose the Right Fit

The key to wearing skinny jeans over 60 is to choose the right fit. Look for jeans with a high waist and a bit of stretch. This will help smooth out any bumps and give you a more flattering silhouette.

Pair with a Tunic Top

A tunic top is a great way to balance out the skinny jeans. Look for a top that covers your hips and bottom, and has a loose, flowy fit. This will create a more relaxed and comfortable outfit.

Add Layers

If you're not comfortable wearing skinny jeans on their own, add layers to your outfit. A long cardigan or blazer can add some extra coverage and create a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing skinny jeans over 60:

  1. Choose a pair of high-waisted skinny jeans with a bit of stretch.
  2. Pair with a tunic top that covers your hips and bottom.
  3. Add a long cardigan or blazer for extra coverage.
  4. Accessorize with a scarf or statement necklace.
  5. Finish with comfortable shoes, such as loafers or ankle boots.

Tips for Wearing Skinny Jeans

Here are a few more tips for wearing skinny jeans:

Choose Dark Wash Jeans

Dark wash jeans are more slimming and versatile than light wash jeans. They can be dressed up or down and look great with a variety of tops and shoes.

Try a Monochromatic Look

A monochromatic look, where you wear all one color, can be a great way to elongate your silhouette and create a slimming effect. Try pairing black skinny jeans with a black tunic top and black ankle boots for a chic look.

Experiment with Different Shoes

Skinny jeans can be worn with a variety of shoes, from ankle boots to sneakers to sandals. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Can I Wear Skinny Jeans if I'm Plus Size?

Yes, absolutely! Skinny jeans can be worn by women of all sizes. Look for jeans with a bit of stretch and a high waist for a more comfortable and flattering fit.

What Tops Should I Wear with Skinny Jeans?

Tunic tops, blouses, and sweaters are all great options for wearing with skinny jeans. Look for tops that cover your hips and bottom and have a loose or flowy fit.

What Shoes Should I Wear with Skinny Jeans?

Skinny jeans can be worn with a variety of shoes, from ankle boots to sneakers to sandals. Choose shoes that are comfortable and complement your outfit.

Hair Care Tips

Your hair can also play a role in how you feel about wearing skinny jeans. Here are a few hair care tips:

Try a New Haircut

A new haircut can make you feel more confident and stylish. Consider trying a shorter, more modern haircut that complements your face shape.

Experiment with Hair Accessories

Headbands, hair clips, and scarves can add some extra flair to your hair and outfit. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Take Care of Your Hair

Healthy hair is beautiful hair. Make sure to take care of your hair by washing it regularly, using a deep conditioning treatment, and protecting it from heat damage.


With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear skinny jeans over 60. Remember to choose the right fit, pair with a tunic top, add layers if needed, and experiment with different shoes and accessories. And don't forget to take care of your hair, too!
