Best Natural Shampoo For Postpartum Hair Loss A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jun 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Postpartum hair loss can be a frustrating experience for new moms. The hormonal changes that occur after giving birth can cause hair to fall out in clumps. While this is a normal part of the postpartum process, it can be distressing for many women. Using the right shampoo can help to minimize the effects of hair loss and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we will explore the best natural shampoo for postpartum hair loss.

What Causes Postpartum Hair Loss?

During pregnancy, the increased levels of estrogen in the body prevent hair from falling out as it normally would. However, after giving birth, hormone levels drop rapidly, causing hair to shed at a faster rate. This shedding can continue for several months before hair growth returns to normal levels.

How to Choose the Best Natural Shampoo for Postpartum Hair Loss

When choosing a shampoo for postpartum hair loss, it is important to look for natural ingredients that promote hair growth and strengthen the hair shaft. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as these can further damage hair that is already weak and fragile.

Here are some of the best natural ingredients to look for:

  • Biotin: A water-soluble B vitamin that promotes healthy hair growth.
  • Keratin: A protein that strengthens hair and prevents breakage.
  • Jojoba Oil: A natural oil that moisturizes and nourishes hair.
  • Argan Oil: A natural oil that helps to prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.

Top 5 Best Natural Shampoo for Postpartum Hair Loss

1. Maple Holistics Biotin Shampoo

This shampoo contains biotin, keratin, and jojoba oil to promote healthy hair growth and strengthen the hair shaft. It is sulfate-free and gentle enough for daily use.

2. ArtNaturals Argan Oil Shampoo

This shampoo contains argan oil, which helps to prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair growth. It is also sulfate-free and gentle on the hair.

3. PURA D'OR Original Gold Label Anti-Thinning Shampoo

This shampoo contains a blend of natural ingredients, including biotin, keratin, and argan oil, to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. It is also free from harsh chemicals and sulfates.

4. Shea Moisture Jamaican Black Castor Oil Strengthen & Restore Shampoo

This shampoo contains Jamaican black castor oil, which strengthens hair and prevents breakage. It is also free from sulfates and other harsh chemicals.

5. Avalon Organics Biotin B-Complex Thickening Shampoo

This shampoo contains biotin, quinoa protein, and vitamin E to promote healthy hair growth and strengthen the hair shaft. It is also free from sulfates and other harsh chemicals.

Tips for Promoting Healthy Hair Growth

In addition to using a natural shampoo, there are several other things you can do to promote healthy hair growth:

  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments, such as coloring or straightening.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair, such as braids or ponytails.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow.
  • Get enough sleep and manage stress levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can postpartum hair loss be prevented?

Postpartum hair loss is a normal part of the postpartum process and cannot be prevented. However, using a natural shampoo and following a healthy hair care routine can help to minimize the effects of hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.

When will my hair stop falling out?

Postpartum hair loss can last for several months before hair growth returns to normal levels. However, every woman's experience is different.

Can I use regular shampoo on postpartum hair?

Regular shampoo can be too harsh for postpartum hair, which is already weak and fragile. It is best to use a natural shampoo that is free from harsh chemicals and sulfates.

Conclusion

Postpartum hair loss can be a frustrating experience, but it is a normal part of the postpartum process. Using a natural shampoo that contains ingredients like biotin, keratin, and jojoba oil can help to promote healthy hair growth and minimize the effects of hair loss. By following a healthy hair care routine and taking care of your body, you can help to promote healthy hair growth and feel confident in your postpartum journey.
