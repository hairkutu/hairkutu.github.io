Guys Haircut Long On Top Short On Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Jul 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile haircut that's perfect for any occasion, then the guys haircut long on top short on sides is the way to go. This haircut is a classic look that's been around for decades, but it's still as popular as ever. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The guys haircut long on top short on sides is a versatile hairstyle that's perfect for any hair type. It involves having longer hair on the top of the head, while the sides and back are kept short. This haircut can be styled in many different ways, making it an ideal choice for guys who want a low-maintenance look that still looks great.

How to

Step 1: Choose your hair length

The first step in getting a guys haircut long on top short on sides is to choose the length of your hair. You'll want to leave the hair on the top of your head longer than the hair on the sides and back. This can be anywhere from a few inches to several inches, depending on your personal preference.

Step 2: Find a good barber

Once you've decided on the length, you'll need to find a good barber who knows how to cut this type of hairstyle. Look for someone who has experience with this type of cut, and bring in a photo of the style you want to make sure you and your barber are on the same page.

Step 3: Get the cut

When you're ready for the cut, sit back and relax while your barber works their magic. They'll use scissors and clippers to create the long on top short on sides look. Be sure to communicate with them throughout the process to make sure you're happy with the length and shape of your new haircut.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once you've got your new haircut, it's time to style it. There are many different ways to style a guys haircut long on top short on sides. You can slick it back with gel, comb it to the side for a more classic look, or leave it messy for a more casual vibe. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your guys haircut long on top short on sides:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as gel or pomade, to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh and clean.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and experiment with different looks.

FAQ

Is the guys haircut long on top short on sides suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I get my guys haircut long on top short on sides trimmed?

It's generally recommended that you get your haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and clean.

Can I still wear a hat with a guys haircut long on top short on sides?

Yes, you can still wear a hat with this type of haircut. Just be sure to choose a hat that fits well and doesn't mess up your hairstyle too much.

Hair Care

To keep your guys haircut long on top short on sides looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh and clean.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stylish and versatile guys haircut long on top short on sides that's sure to turn heads. So go ahead, book that appointment with your barber, and get ready to look your best!
