Description

Hair volumizing sprays are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. They work by lifting the roots of your hair, making it appear fuller and thicker. In India, there are many hair volumizing sprays available, and it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. We've compiled a list of the best hair volumizing sprays in India that are worth trying.

1. L'Oreal Paris Studio Line Boost & Gloss Volume Spray

This hair spray from L'Oreal Paris is perfect for adding volume and shine to your hair. It has a lightweight formula that doesn't weigh down your hair and is easy to apply. It also provides a long-lasting hold, so you don't have to worry about your hair falling flat throughout the day.

2. BBLUNT Blown Away Volumizing Leave-In Spray

The BBLUNT Blown Away Volumizing Leave-In Spray is a great option for those with fine or limp hair. It contains hydrolyzed rice protein that helps to add volume and thickness to your hair. It also has a heat protectant formula, which makes it perfect for using before blow-drying or styling your hair with heat tools.

3. Toni & Guy Volume Plumping Mousse

If you're looking for a hair volumizing product that is easy to apply and provides great results, then the Toni & Guy Volume Plumping Mousse is worth trying. It has a lightweight formula that doesn't leave any residue or stickiness in your hair. It also provides a long-lasting hold, making it perfect for those with fine or limp hair.

How to Use Hair Volumizing Spray

Using a hair volumizing spray is easy, and it can be done in just a few simple steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Towel-Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel-dry it to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Apply the Volumizing Spray

Spray the volumizing spray onto your roots, focusing on the crown area. Avoid spraying too much, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to blow-dry your hair. This will help to lift your roots and create volume. Make sure to direct the nozzle of the blow dryer towards your roots for maximum volume.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. You can use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or straighten your hair, or you can leave it as it is for a natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use hair volumizing spray:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply the Volumizing Spray

Hold the volumizing spray bottle about 6 inches away from your hair and spray it onto your roots. Make sure to focus on the crown area, as this is where you want to create the most volume. Avoid spraying too much, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to blow-dry your hair. This will help to lift your roots and create volume. Make sure to direct the nozzle of the blow dryer towards your roots for maximum volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. You can use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or straighten your hair, or you can leave it as it is for a natural look.

Tips for Using Hair Volumizing Spray

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your hair volumizing spray:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to prepare your hair before using the spray.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create maximum volume.
  • Avoid spraying too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're going to blow-dry or style your hair with heat tools.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes and add volume.

FAQs

1. Can hair volumizing spray damage my hair?

No, hair volumizing spray doesn't damage your hair if used correctly. However, using too much product or applying it too close to your scalp can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

2. Can I use hair volumizing spray on colored or chemically treated hair?

Yes, you can use hair volumizing spray on colored or chemically treated hair. However, make sure to choose a spray that is specifically formulated for your hair type.

3. Can I use hair volumizing spray every day?

Yes, you can use hair volumizing spray every day. However, make sure to wash your hair regularly to avoid buildup and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Voluminous Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain voluminous and healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this is gentler on your hair than a brush.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know about the best hair volumizing spray in India and how to use it, you can achieve that perfect voluminous look that you've always wanted. Remember to choose a spray that is specifically formulated for your hair type and follow the tips and guidelines we've provided for best results.
