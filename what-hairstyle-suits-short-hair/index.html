What Hairstyle Suits Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver May 11, 2023 · 10 min read

Short hair can be incredibly chic and stylish, but finding the right hairstyle to suit your face shape and hair type can be a challenge. Whether you have a pixie cut, a bob or a lob, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we'll explore the best hairstyles for short hair, including how to style them and some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair.

Description

Short hair is a popular choice for women who want a low-maintenance, modern look. It's versatile, easy to style and can be tailored to suit any face shape or hair type. However, finding the right hairstyle for your short hair can be tricky. The key is to choose a style that complements your features and highlights your best assets.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Short Hair

When choosing a hairstyle for short hair, there are several factors to consider:

Face Shape

Your face shape will play a big role in determining which hairstyle will suit you best. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to opt for a style that adds height and length to your face, such as a pixie cut with long, side-swept bangs. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any style, but a sleek bob or a messy, textured lob will look particularly flattering.

Hair Type

Your hair type will also influence which hairstyle will work for you. If you have fine, straight hair, a blunt bob or a layered pixie cut can add volume and texture. If you have thick, curly hair, a short, layered cut can help to reduce bulk and make your curls more manageable.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle will also play a role in choosing the right hairstyle for short hair. If you're a busy mom who doesn't have a lot of time to spend on styling, a low-maintenance bob or lob may be the way to go. If you're a fashion-forward city girl, a stylish pixie cut with bold, choppy layers may be more your style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your short hair, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before styling. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to fine hair, and a smoothing shampoo and conditioner to tame frizz and flyaways.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to protect it from damage caused by heat styling tools.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. If you have curly hair, you can use a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 4: Style

Once your hair is dry, style it using a flat iron, curling iron or hot rollers, depending on the look you're going for. Use a styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to add definition and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy, Beautiful Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

Get Regular Trims

Short hair tends to grow out quickly, so it's important to get regular trims to maintain your style and keep your ends from splitting.

Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in high-quality hair products, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products, to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and give your hair a break from styling products every now and then.

FAQ

Q: What is the best hairstyle for short hair?

A: The best hairstyle for short hair depends on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Some popular options include a pixie cut, a bob, and a lob.

Q: How do I style short hair?

A: To style short hair, start with clean, dry hair, apply heat protectant, blow dry, and then style using a flat iron, curling iron or hot rollers. Use a styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to add definition and hold.

Q: How often should I get my short hair trimmed?

A: Short hair tends to grow out quickly, so it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your ends from splitting.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and free from dirt and oil. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray or cream to protect your hair.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to keep your hair healthy and strong. Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean protein in your diet.

In conclusion, short hair can be incredibly stylish and versatile, but finding the right hairstyle to suit your face shape and hair type can be a challenge. By considering factors such as face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your features and highlights your best assets. With regular maintenance and proper hair care, you can keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful.
