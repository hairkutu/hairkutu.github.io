Which Hairstyle Suits Me Male Online A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you are doing it online. With so many options available, it can be challenging to determine which hairstyle suits you best. In this post, we will guide you on how to choose the perfect hairstyle for yourself, even when you are sitting at home. So, let's get started!

How To Determine Your Face Shape?

Before you start searching for hairstyles, it is essential to determine your face shape. Your face shape plays a vital role in choosing the right hairstyle. The four common face shapes are:

  • Oval
  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart

To determine your face shape, take a picture of yourself facing the camera. Then, draw lines on the picture to identify the shape of your face. Once you know your face shape, you can start searching for hairstyles that suit you.

How To Choose The Right Hairstyle?

Choosing the right hairstyle is all about finding the right balance between your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, your face shape plays a crucial role in determining the right hairstyle for you. Here are some hairstyle recommendations for different face shapes:

  • Oval Face: Almost all hairstyles suit this face shape.
  • Square Face: Short hairstyles with texture on top work best for this face shape.
  • Round Face: Angular and structured hairstyles that add height on top work best for this face shape.
  • Heart Face: Short to medium length hairstyles with volume on top work best for this face shape.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a significant role in determining the right hairstyle for you. Here are some hairstyle recommendations for different hair textures:

  • Straight Hair: Buzz cuts, slicked-back hairstyles, and pompadours work best for straight hair.
  • Wavy Hair: Textured hairstyles, messy crops, and slicked-back hairstyles work best for wavy hair.
  • Curly Hair: Short curly hairstyles, afros, and curly quiffs work best for curly hair.

Consider Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also be considered while choosing the right hairstyle. If you have a more traditional style, you might want to opt for a classic hairstyle. However, if you have a more modern style, you might want to opt for a more edgy hairstyle.

Step By Step Guide To Choosing The Right Hairstyle Online

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle let's discuss how to do it online. Follow these simple steps to choose the perfect hairstyle for yourself online:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

As discussed earlier, determining your face shape is the first step in choosing the right hairstyle. Take a picture of yourself facing the camera and draw lines on the picture to identify your face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Once you know your face shape, start researching hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair texture. You can use Google, Pinterest, or Instagram to find inspiration.

Step 3: Use Virtual Hairstyle Apps

There are many virtual hairstyle apps available online that allow you to upload a picture of yourself and try different hairstyles virtually. Try different hairstyles and see which one suits you best.

Step 4: Consult With A Hairstylist

If you are still unsure about which hairstyle suits you best, consult with a hairstylist. They can guide you on which hairstyle will suit you best based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Tips For Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you have chosen the perfect hairstyle, it is essential to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair in shape.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type and hairstyle.

FAQ

Q. How often should I get a haircut?

A. It depends on your hairstyle and how fast your hair grows. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q. Can I dye my hair?

A. Yes, you can dye your hair, but it is essential to choose a color that suits your skin tone and personal style.

Q. How can I make my hair look thicker?

A. You can use volumizing hair products, such as mousse or volumizing spray, to make your hair look thicker.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a challenging task, but with the right guidance, it can be done easily. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this post to choose the perfect hairstyle for yourself, even when you are doing it online.
