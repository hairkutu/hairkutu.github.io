How To Avoid Falling Hair After Giving Birth

Written by Daniel Aug 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss after giving birth? It’s a common issue that many new moms face. Don’t worry, there are ways to prevent it. In this blog post, we’ll provide you with tips on how to avoid falling hair after giving birth.

Table of Content

  • How to Avoid Falling Hair After Giving Birth
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Hair Loss After Giving Birth
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Giving Birth

How to Avoid Falling Hair After Giving Birth

When you’re pregnant, your body goes through a lot of changes. One of these changes is the increase in estrogen levels, which helps keep your hair healthy and strong. But after giving birth, your estrogen levels drop, and this can cause hair loss. Here’s how to avoid it:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet is crucial for preventing hair loss after giving birth. Make sure to include plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals in your meals. Foods like eggs, fish, nuts, fruits, and vegetables are all excellent sources of nutrients that your body needs to keep your hair healthy.

2. Take Vitamins

In addition to eating a healthy diet, taking vitamins can also help prevent hair loss after giving birth. Biotin, vitamin D, and iron are all essential for healthy hair growth. You can take these vitamins in supplement form or get them from your diet.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoiding tight hairstyles is another way to prevent hair loss after giving birth. Tight hairstyles like braids, ponytails, and buns can cause hair breakage and damage. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that don’t put too much tension on your hair.

4. Be Gentle with Your Hair

After giving birth, your hair may be more fragile than usual. Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, and avoid using hot styling tools like curling irons and straighteners. These tools can damage your hair and cause further hair loss.

5. Manage Stress

Stress can also contribute to hair loss after giving birth. Make sure to take time for yourself and manage your stress levels. You can do this by practicing relaxation techniques like yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to avoid falling hair after giving birth:

  1. Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  2. Take vitamins like biotin, vitamin D, and iron.
  3. Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause hair breakage.
  4. Be gentle with your hair when brushing or combing.
  5. Manage your stress levels with relaxation techniques like yoga or meditation.

Tips to Prevent Hair Loss After Giving Birth

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair loss after giving birth:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s specifically designed for postpartum hair loss.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid chemical treatments like perms and relaxers.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

FAQs

Q: How long does postpartum hair loss last?

A: Postpartum hair loss usually lasts for a few months, but it can take up to a year for your hair to return to its pre-pregnancy state.

Q: Can breastfeeding cause hair loss?

A: Yes, breastfeeding can cause hair loss due to the hormonal changes that occur in your body.

Q: Will my hair grow back after postpartum hair loss?

A: Yes, your hair will grow back after postpartum hair loss. It may take some time, but with proper hair care and nutrition, your hair will eventually return to its pre-pregnancy state.

Hair Care After Giving Birth

After giving birth, it’s important to take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s specifically designed for postpartum hair loss.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid chemical treatments like perms and relaxers.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

By following these tips, you can prevent hair loss after giving birth and keep your hair healthy and strong. Remember to take care of yourself and your body, and your hair will thank you for it!
