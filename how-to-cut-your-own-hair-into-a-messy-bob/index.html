How To Cut Your Own Hair Into A Messy Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and want to switch it up with a messy bob? Going to the salon can be expensive and time-consuming, but with the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut your own hair into a trendy messy bob. In this article, we will guide you through the process to achieve the perfect messy bob without breaking the bank.

How to Cut Your Own Hair into a Messy Bob

What You Will Need

Before we begin, here are the tools you will need to cut your own hair into a messy bob:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers or trimmers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Hair spray

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect messy bob:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back.
  3. Take one section and comb it straight down.
  4. Hold the ends of your hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle to create a messy look.
  5. Repeat this step on the other sections of your hair, making sure to cut at the same angle for consistency.
  6. Use clippers or trimmers to trim the back of your hair to the desired length.
  7. Use scissors to add texture and layers to your hair.
  8. Once you have achieved your desired length and style, spray your hair with hairspray to set it in place.
  9. Repeat these steps on the other side of your hair.
  10. Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it with your fingers to create a messy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair into a messy bob:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to your desired length to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair while cutting.
  • Use hair ties or clips to hold sections of hair in place while cutting.
  • Be patient and take your time to achieve the perfect messy bob.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair into a messy bob:

Is it easy to cut your own hair into a messy bob?

With the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair into a messy bob can be easy and fun.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always trim it later or visit a professional stylist to fix it.

How often should I cut my hair to maintain a messy bob?

You should trim your hair every eight to twelve weeks to maintain a messy bob.

Hair Care

Now that you have successfully cut your own hair into a messy bob, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use hair masks and oils to nourish your hair and promote growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.

Cutting your own hair into a messy bob can be a fun and easy way to switch up your look. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect messy bob without breaking the bank. Remember to be patient, take your time, and have fun while cutting your own hair.
