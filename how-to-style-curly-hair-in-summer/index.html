How To Style Curly Hair In Summer

Written by Daniel Sep 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Summer is a great time to show off your beautiful curly hair. However, the heat and humidity can make it challenging to maintain your hairstyle. Don't worry! In this article, we will share some tips on how to style curly hair in summer and keep it looking great all season long.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How to Style Curly Hair in Summer
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling Curly Hair in Summer
  5. FAQs

Hair Care

Before we get into how to style curly hair in summer, let's talk about hair care. The key to having beautiful curly hair is to keep it healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping natural oils from your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a scarf.

By following these tips, your curly hair will be healthy and ready for any summer hairstyle.

How to Style Curly Hair in Summer

When it comes to styling curly hair in summer, the key is to keep it simple. Here are some easy and stylish ways to wear your curly hair:

Bun

A bun is a great hairstyle to keep your hair off your neck and look chic at the same time. You can do a high or low bun depending on your preference. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair and tie it into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands to frame your face.

Pineapple

The pineapple is a popular hairstyle among curly girls. It's easy to do and keeps your curls intact. Here's how to do it:

  1. Flip your hair over and gather it at the top of your head.
  2. Tie it with a scrunchie or hair tie.
  3. Fluff out your curls to create volume.

Braid

A braid is a great way to keep your hair off your face and look stylish at the same time. Here's how to do it:

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front and start braiding it.
  3. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair.
  4. Repeat on the other side.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let's go through a step-by-step guide on how to style curly hair in summer.

  1. Start with clean, moisturized hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  3. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.
  4. Choose a hairstyle that works for you.
  5. Follow the steps for the hairstyle you chose.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair in Summer

Here are some additional tips to keep your curly hair looking great in the summer:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Refresh your curls with a spray bottle filled with water and a leave-in conditioner.
  • Protect your hair from chlorine and saltwater by wearing a swim cap or applying a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my curly hair in the summer?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you sweat a lot or swim frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. However, if you have dry hair, you may want to wash it less frequently. Aim to wash your hair once or twice a week.

2. How do I prevent frizz in the summer?

To prevent frizz, avoid touching your hair too much, use a microfiber towel to dry your hair, and use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

3. Can I still straighten my curly hair in the summer?

You can, but it's not recommended as heat styling can damage your hair. If you want to wear your hair straight, consider using a heat protectant and a lower heat setting on your styling tool.

4. How do I protect my hair from the sun?

You can protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a scarf. You can also use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from UV rays.

5. How do I refresh my curls in the summer?

You can refresh your curls by using a spray bottle filled with water and a leave-in conditioner. Spritz your hair with the mixture and scrunch your curls to revive them.

Conclusion

Styling curly hair in summer can be challenging, but with the right tips and techniques, you can keep your hair looking great all season long. Remember to take care of your hair by following a good hair care routine and protect it from the sun and chlorine. With these tips, you'll be able to show off your beautiful curls all summer long.
