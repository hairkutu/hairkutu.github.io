How To Make A Pixie Cut Wig With A Frontal A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle without committing to a chop? A pixie cut wig with a frontal can be the perfect solution! In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create your own pixie cut wig with a frontal. Let's get started!

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut close to the scalp. A frontal is a type of wig that has lace at the front, which allows for a natural-looking hairline. Combining these two elements creates a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle that can be customized to your liking.

How to Make a Pixie Cut Wig with a Frontal

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary materials. You will need:

  • A frontal wig cap
  • Human hair extensions (at least 8-10 inches in length)
  • A wig stand or mannequin head
  • Scissors
  • Thread and needle
  • Wig clips
  • Wig glue (optional)

Step 2: Measure and Cut the Hair Extensions

Measure and cut the hair extensions to the desired length for your pixie cut. Keep in mind that the hair should be cut shorter in the back and longer in the front for a classic pixie cut look.

Step 3: Sew the Hair Extensions onto the Frontal Wig Cap

Using a needle and thread, sew the hair extensions onto the frontal wig cap. Start at the back of the cap and work your way forward, making sure to sew the hair as close to the edge of the cap as possible. Leave a small amount of space at the front of the cap for the lace to be attached.

Step 4: Attach the Lace Frontal

Attach the lace frontal to the front of the wig cap using either wig clips or wig glue. Make sure the frontal is centered and aligned with your natural hairline.

Step 5: Cut and Style the Wig

Using scissors, cut the hair extensions to your desired length and style. For a classic pixie cut, the back should be short and tapered, while the front should be longer and styled to frame your face.

Tips

  • When sewing the hair extensions onto the wig cap, make sure to sew through both the cap and the weft of the hair to ensure a secure hold.
  • If you're using wig glue to attach the frontal, be sure to follow the manufacturer's instructions carefully.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the perfect pixie cut for you!

FAQ

How long does a pixie cut wig with a frontal typically last?

A well-made wig can last for several months with proper care and maintenance.

Can I wash my pixie cut wig with a frontal?

Yes, you can wash your wig just like you would your natural hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and allow the wig to air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head.

Can I style my pixie cut wig with a frontal with heat?

Yes, you can use heat tools to style your wig, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings for extended periods of time.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut wig with a frontal looking fresh and stylish, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle the hair extensions.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down the hair.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Protect the wig from heat and excessive sunlight to prevent damage.

Conclusion

Creating a pixie cut wig with a frontal is a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle. With the right materials and a little bit of patience, you can create a stylish and low-maintenance wig that will last for months. Give it a try and see how you like your new look!
