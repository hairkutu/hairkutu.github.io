How To Make Your Hair Grow Curly For Black Guys

Written by Luffy Aug 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of straight, flat hair and longing for bouncy, curly locks? As a black guy, it may seem like a challenge to achieve naturally curly hair, especially if you have relaxed hair. But fear not, with the right techniques and care, you can make your hair grow curly. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to make your hair grow curly for black guys.

How to Make Your Hair Grow Curly

There are several methods you can try to make your hair grow curly.

1. Natural Hair Care

The first step to growing curly hair is to take care of your hair naturally. This means avoiding harsh chemicals such as relaxers, which can weaken hair strands and inhibit hair growth. Instead, use natural hair products that nourish and strengthen hair, such as coconut oil, shea butter, and aloe vera.

2. Heatless Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and blow dryers, which can dry out your hair and cause damage. Instead, embrace heatless styling methods such as twist-outs, braid-outs, and bantu knots to create natural curls without heat. These styling methods also help to stretch your hair, making it easier to manage and style.

3. Proper Nutrition

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to include foods that are high in protein, such as lean meats, fish, and beans, as well as fruits and vegetables that are rich in vitamins A, C, and E.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair grow curly for black guys.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to remove dirt and buildup. Follow up with a moisturizing conditioner to hydrate your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to provide extra moisture and prevent breakage. Look for a leave-in conditioner that contains natural oils such as coconut oil, jojoba oil, or argan oil.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small, manageable sections using hair clips or elastics. This will make it easier to apply products and style your hair.

Step 4: Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product such as a curl defining cream, gel, or mousse to each section of your hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly, using your fingers or a wide-toothed comb.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use heatless styling methods such as twist-outs, braid-outs, or bantu knots to create natural curls without heat. Let your hair air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair gently.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve and maintain healthy, curly hair.

1. Protect Your Hair at Night

Use a satin or silk pillowcase to avoid friction and breakage while you sleep. You can also wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet to protect your curls.

2. Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too often can strip your hair of natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

3. Trim Your Ends Regularly

Regular trims are essential for healthy hair growth. Trim your ends every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

Can I grow curly hair if I have relaxed hair?

Yes, you can still grow curly hair if you have relaxed hair. However, it may take longer to achieve curly hair as relaxed hair tends to be weaker and more susceptible to damage.

How long does it take to grow curly hair?

The length of time it takes to grow curly hair varies depending on your hair type and genetics. It can take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years to grow curly hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, curly hair.

1. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning is essential for hydrating and nourishing your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls healthy and moisturized.

2. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet. This will prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can dry out your hair and cause damage. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your hair grow curly for black guys. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and before you know it, you'll have bouncy, curly locks to be proud of.
