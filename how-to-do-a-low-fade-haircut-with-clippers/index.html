How To Do A Low Fade Haircut With Clippers A Beginner s Guide

Written by Aprilia May 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money on haircuts? Learning how to do a low fade haircut with clippers can help you save time and money. This haircut is versatile and easy to maintain. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to do a low fade haircut with clippers. We will also share tips and answer frequently asked questions about hair care.

Description

A low fade haircut is a type of haircut that gradually fades from short hair on the sides and back of the head to longer hair on the top. This haircut is also known as a drop fade or a temple fade. This haircut can be done with different lengths, depending on your preference.

How to Do a Low Fade Haircut with Clippers

Before starting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a set of clippers, guards, scissors, comb, and a mirror. Follow these steps to do a low fade haircut with clippers:

Step 1: Wash Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or oils from the hair and make it easier to cut. Dry your hair with a towel.

Step 2: Choose Guard Size

Choose the guard size you want to use for the sides and back of the head. Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller size. For a low fade haircut, we recommend starting with a number 3 or 4 guard.

Step 3: Cut Sides and Back

Start at the bottom of the head and work your way up. Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head, using the guard size you chose. Use an upward motion to blend the hair between the different guard sizes. Repeat this process with smaller guard sizes until you reach the desired length.

Step 4: Create a Fade

Use a smaller guard size to create a fade between the sides and back of the head and the longer hair on the top. Use a clipping motion to blend the hair between the different guard sizes. Use the mirror to check the back of the head and make sure the fade is even.

Step 5: Cut Top of the Hair

Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of the head to the desired length. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly. Trim the hair around the edges to create a clean look.

Step 6: Style Hair

Style the hair on the top of the head using your preferred hair product. Use your fingers or a comb to create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing a low fade haircut with clippers:

  • Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller size to create a gradual fade.
  • Use the mirror to check the back of the head and make sure the cut is even.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair requires precision and attention to detail.
  • Practice makes perfect. Don't be afraid to experiment with different guard sizes and lengths.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on the length and style of your hair. For a low fade haircut, we recommend getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

Q: Can I do a low fade haircut on my own?

A: Yes, you can. However, we recommend getting a friend or family member to help you with the back of the head.

Q: What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

A: Don't panic. Use a larger guard size to fix the mistake and work your way down to a smaller size to blend the hair.

Hair Care

After getting a low fade haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the look.

Learning how to do a low fade haircut with clippers can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
