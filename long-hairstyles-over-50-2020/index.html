Long Hairstyles Over 50 2020 A Guide For Women

Written by Aprilia May 30, 2023 · 8 min read

Description

As women age, they tend to shy away from long hairstyles, thinking that shorter hair is more appropriate for their age. But the truth is, long hair can be just as flattering for women over 50 as it is for younger women. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best long hairstyles for women over 50 in 2020.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle

When choosing a long hairstyle, it’s important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the right long hairstyle for you:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Your face shape will determine which long hairstyles will be most flattering for you. Common face shapes include oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Step 2: Consider your hair texture

Your hair texture will also play a role in which long hairstyles will work best for you. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a long hairstyle with layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a long hairstyle with blunt ends to keep your hair from looking too bulky.

Step 3: Think about your lifestyle

If you’re a busy woman who doesn’t have a lot of time to spend on your hair, you may want to choose a long hairstyle that’s easy to maintain. If you’re someone who loves to experiment with different hairstyles, you may want to choose a long hairstyle with lots of layers and texture.

Step by Step Guide to Long Hairstyles Over 50 2020

Now that you know how to choose the right long hairstyle, let’s take a look at some of the best long hairstyles for women over 50 in 2020:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great way to add volume and movement to your hair. They’re also easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways. To get the look, ask your stylist to cut long layers throughout your hair. You can wear your hair straight, wavy, or curly – the layers will add texture and movement to your hair.

2. Blunt Ends

If you have thick hair, blunt ends are a great way to keep your hair from looking too bulky. This hairstyle is also very easy to maintain. To get the look, ask your stylist to cut your hair to one length. You can wear your hair straight or add some texture with a curling iron or flat iron.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften your face and add some dimension to your hair. They’re also very low-maintenance. To get the look, ask your stylist to cut your bangs so that they’re longer on one side than the other. You can wear your bangs straight or sweep them to the side for a more relaxed look.

Tips for Long Hairstyles Over 50 2020

Here are some tips to help you keep your long hairstyle looking great: - Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet. - Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or flat iron. - Visit your stylist regularly to get your hair trimmed and to keep it looking healthy.

FAQ

Q: Are long hairstyles appropriate for women over 50?

A: Yes! Long hairstyles can be just as flattering for women over 50 as they are for younger women.

Q: What should I consider when choosing a long hairstyle?

A: When choosing a long hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Hair Care for Long Hairstyles Over 50 2020

To keep your long hairstyle looking great, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair healthy: - Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet. - Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or flat iron. - Visit your stylist regularly to get your hair trimmed and to keep it looking healthy.

Conclusion

Long hairstyles can be just as flattering for women over 50 as they are for younger women. When choosing a long hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. And don’t forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
