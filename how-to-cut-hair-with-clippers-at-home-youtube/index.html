How To Cut Hair With Clippers At Home Youtube

Written by Robby Aug 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon or barber and spending a lot of money on haircuts? With the help of YouTube, you can learn how to cut hair with clippers at home. In this article, we will guide you on how to do it step-by-step, with some tips and FAQ to make it easier for you. Let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you start cutting your hair, here are some things you need:

  • A good pair of clippers
  • Hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Cape or towel to cover your clothes
  • A mirror to see what you're doing

Once you have all the tools, you can start cutting your hair with clippers at home by following these steps:

1. Determine the Length of Hair You Want

You need to decide how long you want your hair to be. Use the guards that come with your clippers to adjust the length. You can start with a longer guard and gradually go shorter until you reach your desired length.

2. Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start with the back of your head and work your way to the sides and top. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

3. Start Cutting

Begin cutting your hair with the clippers. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use a comb to lift the hair and clip it off with the clippers. Make sure to use even strokes to avoid uneven cuts.

4. Blend the Sides and Top

After cutting the back of your head, move on to the sides and top. Blend the hair using the same technique as before. Use a comb to lift the hair and clip it off with the clippers. Make sure to blend the sides and top for a seamless look.

5. Cut the Bangs

Finally, cut the bangs if you have them. Use your hair cutting scissors to trim them to your desired length. Make sure to cut them evenly to avoid any mistakes.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers at Home

Here are some tips to make cutting your hair with clippers at home easier:

  • Make sure your clippers are clean and oiled before using them.
  • Use a cape or towel to cover your clothes and keep the hair off of you.
  • Start with a longer guard and gradually go shorter until you reach your desired length.
  • Use even strokes to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Blend the sides and top for a seamless look.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with clippers if I have long hair?

Yes, you can cut your hair with clippers if you have long hair. Use a longer guard to start and gradually go shorter until you reach your desired length.

Do I need to wet my hair before cutting it with clippers?

No, you don't need to wet your hair before cutting it with clippers. However, wet hair is easier to cut and makes it easier to see what you're doing.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want it to be. On average, it's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair with Clippers at Home

After cutting your hair with clippers at home, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Now that you know how to cut hair with clippers at home YouTube, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. With these tips and FAQ, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your home. Happy cutting!
