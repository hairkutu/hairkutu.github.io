What Kind Of Bangs Go With A Round Face

Written by Eveline Jul 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs but you're not sure what style would suit your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll go through the different types of bangs that work best with a round face shape, and give you some tips on how to style and care for them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces typically have fuller cheeks, a rounded chin, and a softer jawline. The goal when choosing bangs for a round face is to create the illusion of length and balance out the proportions of the face. Bangs that are too short or blunt can make the face appear even rounder, while longer, layered bangs can help elongate the face.

How To

When deciding on what kind of bangs to get, it's important to consider your hair texture and your personal style. Here are some popular bang styles that work well with a round face:

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces because they create the illusion of length and draw attention away from the width of the face. This style works well for both thick and thin hair textures, and can be cut at different lengths depending on your preference.

Long, Layered Bangs

Long, layered bangs are another great option for round faces because they help to elongate the face. This style works well for those with thick hair, as it can help to reduce bulk around the face. However, if you have thin hair, you may want to opt for a shorter version of this style to avoid weighing down your hair too much.

Curly Bangs

If you have curly hair and a round face, curly bangs can be a great option. This style works well because it adds volume to the top of the head, creating the illusion of length. Curly bangs can be cut at different lengths depending on your preference, but it's important to work with a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair.

Step By Step

Once you've decided on the type of bangs you want, it's important to consider how to style them to complement your face shape. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your bangs, such as a texturizing spray or a light-hold styling cream.
  3. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  4. Once your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray or a shine spray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to ensure your bangs look their best:

  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing your bang style.
  • Work with a stylist who is experienced in cutting bangs.
  • Keep your bangs trimmed regularly to avoid them getting too long or becoming uneven.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
  • If you're not sure about getting bangs, try out clip-in bangs first to see how they look on you.

FAQ

What kind of bangs should I avoid with a round face?

Avoid short, blunt bangs that end at or above the eyebrows, as they can make the face appear even rounder. Also avoid heavy, thick bangs that sit straight across the forehead, as they can emphasize the width of the face.

How do I know if bangs will suit me?

If you're unsure whether bangs will suit you, try out clip-in bangs first to see how they look on you. You can also consult with a stylist who can recommend a bang style that will complement your face shape and hair texture.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to avoid them becoming greasy or oily.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as they can weigh them down and make them look greasy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much throughout the day, as this can cause them to become greasy or lose their shape.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray when blow-drying or styling your bangs to prevent damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect bangs for your round face shape and keep them looking their best. Whether you opt for side-swept bangs, long, layered bangs, or curly bangs, you'll be sure to turn heads with your new look!
