How to Cut the Sides of a Man's Hair with Scissors

Cutting the sides of a man's hair with scissors is a simple process that can be done at home. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a more even result.

Step 2: Comb the Hair

Using a comb, comb the hair straight down so that it's even on both sides. Make sure there are no tangles or knots in the hair.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Take a small section of hair from the side of the head and hold it between your fingers. Use the comb to comb the hair straight down, and then cut the hair with the scissors at the desired length. Repeat this process for the rest of the hair on the sides of the head.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After cutting the hair on both sides, use the comb to check for evenness. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure both sides are the same length.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are more detailed step-by-step instructions on how to cut the sides of a man's hair with scissors:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair if necessary.

Step 2: Prepare the Hair

Wash the hair and towel-dry it until it's slightly damp. Comb the hair straight down so that it's even on both sides.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Starting at the temple, take a small section of hair between your fingers and comb it straight down. Use the scissors to cut the hair at the desired length. Repeat this process until you have cut all the hair on both sides of the head.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Use the comb to check for evenness on both sides. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure both sides are the same length.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve a professional-looking haircut:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use a comb to comb the hair straight down before cutting.
  • Hold the hair between your fingers to ensure an even cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Trim a little bit of hair at a time to avoid cutting off too much.
  • Check for evenness frequently to ensure both sides are the same length.
  • Take breaks if you get tired to avoid making mistakes.

FAQs

Can I cut men's hair with regular scissors?

It's best to use hair-cutting scissors, as they are designed to cut hair evenly and without causing split ends. Regular scissors may not give you the desired result.

How often should I cut men's hair?

This depends on the individual's hair growth rate and desired hairstyle. Generally, men's hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and shape.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting the hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep the hair nourished.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and shape.

That's it! With these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut for the sides of a man's hair with scissors. With practice, you can even experiment with different styles and techniques. Happy cutting!
