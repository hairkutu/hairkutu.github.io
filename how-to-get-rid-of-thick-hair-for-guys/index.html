How To Get Rid Of Thick Hair For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time, especially for guys. While it gives a voluminous and healthy look to your hair, it also makes it difficult to manage, style, and groom. If you are a guy with thick hair and want to get rid of it, this article is for you. In this guide, we will discuss various ways to get rid of thick hair, including how to, step by step, tips, FAQs, and hair care. So, let's get started.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Rid of Thick Hair for Guys
  • Step by Step Process
  • Tips for Managing Thick Hair
  • FAQs

How to Get Rid of Thick Hair for Guys

There are several ways to get rid of thick hair for guys. You can choose the one that suits your preferences and needs.

Cutting your Hair Short

One of the easiest ways to get rid of thick hair is to cut your hair short. Short hair will not only make your hair look thinner but will also make it easier to manage and style. You can go for a buzz cut, crew cut, or any other short hairstyle that suits your face shape and personality.

Using Hair Products

You can also use hair products, such as hair gels, mousses, and creams, to make your hair look thinner. These products will not only add volume to your hair but also provide a sleek and shiny finish. However, make sure to use the products in moderation, or else your hair might look greasy and heavy.

Chemical Treatments

If you want a more permanent solution, you can opt for chemical treatments, such as hair relaxing or hair rebonding. Hair relaxing will loosen the curls and waves in your hair, making it look straighter and thinner, while hair rebonding will create a sleek and straight look by breaking the bonds in your hair. However, these treatments can be expensive and time-consuming, and you need to take proper care of your hair after the treatment.

Step by Step Process

If you want to cut your hair short to get rid of thick hair, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a short hairstyle that suits your face shape and personality.
  2. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  3. Dry your hair with a towel and blow-dryer.
  4. Apply a hair gel or cream to add volume and texture to your hair.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair.
  6. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the short length.

If you want to opt for chemical treatments, consult a professional hairstylist and follow their instructions carefully.

Tips for Managing Thick Hair

Here are some tips for managing thick hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with natural bristles to detangle your hair.
  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners and curling irons, frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use hair products, such as hair oils and serums, to nourish and protect your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Q. Can I use hair wax to make my hair look thinner?

A. Yes, you can use hair wax to make your hair look thinner, but make sure to use it in moderation, or else your hair might look greasy and heavy.

Q. Can I get rid of thick hair permanently?

A. Yes, you can opt for chemical treatments, such as hair relaxing or hair rebonding, to get rid of thick hair permanently.

Q. How often should I trim my hair?

A. You should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the short length and avoid split ends.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, you need to take proper care of it to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some hair care tips for thick hair:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid dryness and damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners and curling irons, frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to protect your hair from environmental damage and prevent frizz.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Thick hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right techniques and products, you can make it work for you. Whether you choose to cut your hair short or opt for chemical treatments, make sure to take proper care of your hair to keep it healthy and manageable. Follow the tips and suggestions mentioned in this guide to get rid of thick hair for guys.
