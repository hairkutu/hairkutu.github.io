How To Tell If Short Hair Suits You A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Mar 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're thinking about switching up your hairstyle, going for a shorter look can be a great option. However, it can be difficult to know if short hair will suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this guide, we'll take you through some key factors to consider before taking the plunge and chopping off your locks.

Table of Contents:

  • Face Shape
  • Hair Texture
  • Personal Style
  • How to Try it Out
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQs

Face Shape

The first thing to consider when deciding if short hair suits you is your face shape. Some face shapes look better with shorter hair, while others are more suited to longer styles. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have a round face, a short hairstyle with layers can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.
  • If you have a square face, a short bob or pixie cut can soften your features and create a more feminine look.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, a short pixie cut can accentuate your cheekbones and draw attention to your eyes.
  • If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle, including short hair.

Of course, these are just general guidelines, and there are exceptions to every rule. The best way to know if short hair suits you is to try it out yourself (more on that later).

Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair suits you is your hair texture. Some hair textures are better suited to shorter styles, while others may require more length to look their best. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • If you have straight hair, a short bob or pixie cut can look sleek and sophisticated.
  • If you have curly hair, a shorter cut can help define your curls and make them easier to manage.
  • If you have fine hair, a short hairstyle can add volume and texture.
  • If you have thick hair, a shorter cut can help lighten the load and make styling easier.

Again, these are just general guidelines, and there are exceptions to every rule. The best way to know if short hair suits you is to try it out yourself (more on that later).

Personal Style

One of the most important factors to consider when deciding if short hair suits you is your personal style. Your hairstyle should match your personality and make you feel confident and comfortable. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • If you have a bold and edgy style, a short pixie cut or shaved head can be a great statement look.
  • If you have a classic and timeless style, a short bob or sleek pixie cut can be chic and sophisticated.
  • If you have a bohemian or natural style, a shorter cut with tousled waves can be effortless and breezy.
  • If you have a sporty or active style, a shorter cut can be easier to manage and keep out of your way.

Ultimately, the best hairstyle is one that makes you feel like the best version of yourself. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things!

How to Try it Out

The best way to know if short hair suits you is to try it out yourself. Here are some tips for making the switch:

  • Start with a longer short style, like a lob or a long bob, before going for a super short cut.
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your stylist and ask for their opinion on what would suit you best.
  • Consider getting a wig or trying out virtual try-on tools to see what you would look like with short hair before committing to the cut.

Remember, hair grows back! If you're not happy with your short hair, you can always grow it out again.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair can be easy to manage, but it still requires some maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your ends looking fresh.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products, like pomade or texturizing spray, to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Consider changing up your hair color to add dimension and interest to your shorter style.

With a little bit of effort, you can rock your short hair like a pro.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair suit me if I have a double chin?

A: A shorter hairstyle can actually help draw attention away from a double chin and create a more defined jawline. Consider a bob or a pixie cut with longer layers at the front to help frame your face.

Q: Can I still style short hair in different ways?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and wavy. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Q: Will short hair be easier to manage than long hair?

A: Short hair can be easier to manage in some ways, but it still requires some maintenance and styling. However, it can save you time in the morning and be more comfortable during hot weather.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short cut?

A: Yes! While some updos may be more challenging with short hair, there are still plenty of styles you can try, from messy buns to sleek ponytails.

Now that you know how to tell if short hair suits you, it's time to make the switch! Whether you opt for a bob, a pixie cut, or something in between, remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
