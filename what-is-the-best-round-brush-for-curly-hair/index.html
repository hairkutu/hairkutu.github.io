What Is The Best Round Brush For Curly Hair

Written by Petter May 13, 2023 · 8 min read

Curly hair can be both beautiful and frustrating. While it’s a great way to stand out from the crowd, it can also be challenging to manage. One of the best ways to achieve a great hairstyle is by using the right tools, such as a round brush. But with so many options on the market, it can be hard to know which one is the best for your curly hair. In this article, we’ll explore the best round brush for curly hair, how to use it, and some tips for maintaining your hair.

Why Use a Round Brush?

A round brush is a versatile tool that can be used to create different hairstyles. Its shape makes it easy to create volume, curls, and waves. When it comes to curly hair, a round brush can be used to smooth out the curls and give them a more polished look. It can also be used to create volume at the roots and add bounce to the hair.

How to Choose the Best Round Brush for Curly Hair

When choosing a round brush for curly hair, there are a few things to consider:

Size:

The size of the brush will depend on the length of your hair. For short hair, a smaller brush is recommended, while a larger brush is best for longer hair.

Bristles:

The bristles of the brush should be gentle enough to not damage your hair but firm enough to create tension. Natural bristles are a great option as they provide a gentle grip on the hair and distribute oils throughout the hair.

Shape:

A round brush with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel is recommended, as it helps to distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz.

How to Use a Round Brush on Curly Hair

Using a round brush on curly hair can help to smooth out the curls and give them a more polished look. Here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Make sure to use products that are suitable for curly hair to help define your curls.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it’s important to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair until it’s about 80% dry. Use a diffuser attachment to help define your curls.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting from the bottom. Clip the remaining hair out of the way.

Step 5: Use the Round Brush

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the round brush. Use a blow dryer to heat the hair while you gently pull the brush through the hair. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

Step 6: Finish with a Styling Product

Once you’ve finished styling your hair, apply a styling product to help hold the style in place. Use a light hold product for a natural look, or a stronger hold product for a more defined look.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

Tip 3: Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 4: Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and keep your curls looking great.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

A: It’s best to use a round brush on hair that’s about 80% dry. Wet hair is more fragile and can be easily damaged.

Q: How do I clean my round brush?

A: To clean your round brush, use a comb to remove any hair that’s stuck in the bristles. Then, wash the brush with warm water and a mild shampoo. Allow it to air dry before using it again.

Q: Can I use a round brush on short hair?

A: Yes, a smaller round brush is recommended for short hair.

Conclusion

A round brush is a great tool for creating different hairstyles on curly hair. When choosing a round brush, consider the size, bristles, and shape. Use the brush to smooth out your curls and add volume to your hair. Remember to use a heat protectant and styling product to keep your hair healthy and in place. With the right tools and care, your curly hair can look its best.
