Best Haircut For Square Face Over 60

Written by William Sep 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our face shape changes and so does our hairstyle. Finding the perfect haircut can be tricky, especially when you have a square face. A square face has a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for square face over 60, how to achieve it, and some tips to maintain it.

Description

A square face is a beautiful and unique feature. However, it can be challenging to find a suitable haircut that flatters your face shape. A good hairstyle can enhance your natural features and make you look youthful and vibrant.

How to

The best haircut for a square face over 60 is a layered bob. A layered bob is a versatile and flattering hairstyle that suits most face shapes. It softens the jawline and adds volume to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker. Here are the steps to achieve a layered bob:

Step 1: Consultation

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape. Show them some pictures of the hairstyle you want to achieve and discuss any concerns or preferences.

Step 2: Shampoo and Conditioner

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Use a towel to dry your hair before proceeding to the next step.

Step 3: Sectioning

Section your hair into small sections using clips or elastics. This will make it easier to cut and style your hair.

Step 4: Cutting

Cut your hair in layers, starting from the back and moving towards the front. Use scissors or a razor to create texture and movement in your hair. The layers should be longer in the front and shorter in the back.

Step 5: Styling

Style your hair using a round brush and a blow dryer. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. Finish off with a hairspray or a styling product of your choice.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your layered bob:

  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and length.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for a square face over 60:

What other hairstyles suit a square face?

Other hairstyles that suit a square face include a pixie cut, a long layered cut, and a side-swept bangs.

Can I dye my hair if I have a square face?

Yes, you can dye your hair any color you like. However, make sure to choose a color that suits your skin tone and complements your face shape.

Can I style my layered bob differently?

Yes, you can style your layered bob differently by adding curls or waves, straightening it, or wearing it in a messy bun or ponytail.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair, especially when you have a square face. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, finding the best haircut for a square face over 60 can be challenging, but a layered bob is a versatile and flattering hairstyle that suits most face shapes. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape. Follow the steps to achieve a layered bob and use the tips to maintain your hair.
