How To Cut Wavy Hair So It Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Wavy hair can be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to getting those perfect curls. But with the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that will last all day. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps of how to cut wavy hair so it curls.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Wavy Hair So It Curls
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Wavy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Description

Wavy hair is characterized by its natural wave pattern, which can range from loose waves to tight curls. This type of hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right cut and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural waves and create beautiful, bouncy curls.

How to Cut Wavy Hair So It Curls

The key to cutting wavy hair so it curls is to create layers that enhance the natural wave pattern. This will allow the hair to move freely and create beautiful curls that last all day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut wavy hair so it curls:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning the hair to remove any buildup or product residue.
  2. Towel-dry the hair, then apply a styling product that is designed for wavy hair, such as a curl enhancer or mousse.
  3. Section the hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  4. Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair and twist it gently. Cut the hair at an angle, starting at the tip and working your way up to create layers. Repeat this process throughout the back section of the hair.
  5. Move on to the side sections, taking small sections of hair and cutting them at an angle to create layers that enhance the natural wave pattern.
  6. Finally, work on the top section of the hair, taking small sections and cutting them at an angle to create layers that will enhance the natural wave pattern and add volume to the hair.
  7. Once you have finished cutting the hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry the hair. This will help to enhance the natural wave pattern and create bouncy curls.
  8. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep the curls in place.

Tips for Cutting Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for cutting wavy hair so it curls:

  • Work with small sections of hair to create more defined layers.
  • Use scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair, as they will create cleaner, more precise cuts.
  • Don’t cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven layers.
  • Be patient and take your time, as cutting wavy hair requires precision and attention to detail.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting wavy hair:

  • Q: Will cutting my wavy hair make it more curly?
  • A: Cutting wavy hair can enhance the natural wave pattern and make it more curly, especially if you create layers that allow the hair to move freely.
  • Q: How often should I cut my wavy hair?
  • A: It is recommended to cut wavy hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy, bouncy curls.
  • Q: Can I cut my wavy hair at home?
  • A: While it is possible to cut wavy hair at home, it is recommended to visit a professional stylist who has experience working with wavy hair.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair and cause frizz.
  • Air dry the hair whenever possible, or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance the natural wave pattern.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to the ends of the hair to keep them moisturized and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can cut wavy hair so it curls and achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that will last all day.
