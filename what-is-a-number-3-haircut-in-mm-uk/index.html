What Is A Number 3 Haircut In Mm Uk

Written by Bardi May 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In the UK, haircuts are often distinguished by their clipper guard sizes. One of the most popular haircuts is the Number 3 haircut, which refers to the length of the hair that is left after the barber or hairdresser uses a clipper guard size of 3. In millimeters (mm), a Number 3 haircut is equivalent to 9 mm. If you're planning to get a Number 3 haircut, read on to learn more about how to achieve this look.

How to Get a Number 3 Haircut

If you're looking to get a Number 3 haircut, the first step is to find a good barber or hairdresser who is experienced in using clippers. Once you've found someone you trust, you can follow these simple steps to achieve the Number 3 look:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before your haircut, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier for the barber to cut your hair evenly and accurately.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

Ask your barber to use a clipper guard size of 3. This will ensure that your hair is cut to a uniform length all over your head.

Step 3: Start at the Sides and Back

The barber will typically start cutting at the sides and back of your head, using the clippers to create an even length all around. They may also use scissors to trim any longer hairs that are sticking out.

Step 4: Trim the Top

Once the sides and back are done, the barber will move on to the top of your head. They may use scissors or clippers to create a layered look or to blend the top with the sides and back.

Step 5: Finish with Detailing

Finally, the barber will use a razor or trimmer to clean up any stray hairs around your hairline and ears. This will give your haircut a polished and professional finish.

Tips for Maintaining Your Number 3 Haircut

Once you've got your Number 3 haircut, it's important to keep it looking sharp. Here are a few tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair at the right length.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Style your hair with a light pomade or wax to add texture and hold.

FAQ

What other clipper guard sizes are available?

Clipper guard sizes range from 0 (no guard) to 8 (1 inch). Each guard size corresponds to a specific length of hair that is left after cutting.

Will a Number 3 haircut suit me?

A Number 3 haircut is a versatile and classic style that suits most face shapes and hair types. However, it's always a good idea to consult with your barber or hairdresser before making any drastic changes to your hairstyle.

Can I achieve a Number 3 haircut at home?

If you have some experience using clippers, you can try cutting your own hair at home. However, it's always best to leave it to the professionals to avoid any mistakes or uneven cuts.

Hair Care

It is important to maintain healthy hair. Hair health can be affected by stress, diet, and environmental factors. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle can help to improve hair health. Keeping the hair clean and moisturized is also important. This can be achieved by using a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Additionally, avoiding heat styling tools and using protective hair products can help to prevent damage.
