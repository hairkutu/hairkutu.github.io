How To Style Medium Length Thick Layered Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Medium length thick layered hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it gives you plenty of volume and versatility when it comes to styling, it can also be overwhelming to figure out how to tame it. In this article, we'll show you how to style your medium length thick layered hair in a way that looks effortlessly chic and put-together.

  2. How to Style Medium Length Thick Layered Hair

Description

Medium length thick layered hair is typically defined as hair that falls between the shoulders and the mid-back and has multiple layers to create volume and movement. This type of hair can be difficult to style, especially if you're not sure how to work with its thickness and texture. However, with the right styling tools and techniques, you can achieve a variety of looks that are both flattering and on-trend.

How to Style Medium Length Thick Layered Hair

When it comes to styling medium length thick layered hair, there are a few key tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in high-quality styling tools, such as a curling iron, straightener, and hair dryer.
  • Use products that are designed for your hair type, such as volumizing mousse or smoothing serum.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the ones that work best for your face shape and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your medium length thick layered hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots to create lift.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to smooth out any frizz and add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, divide it into sections and curl each section with a 1-inch curling iron, alternating the direction of the curls for a natural look.
  5. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to separate the curls and create loose waves.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place without weighing it down.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Thick Layered Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you style your medium length thick layered hair:

  • Try a half-up half-down style to showcase your layers and add volume to the crown of your head.
  • Experiment with different partings to create different looks, such as a deep side part or a center part.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add definition to your layers and create a more tousled, beachy look.
  • Consider getting a balayage or ombre hair color to add dimension to your layers and create a more dynamic look.

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about styling medium length thick layered hair:

Q: What hairstyles are best for medium length thick layered hair?

A: Some of the best hairstyles for medium length thick layered hair include beachy waves, messy buns, and braids.

Q: How often should I wash my medium length thick layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people with medium length thick layered hair can go 2-3 days between washes.

Q: How can I prevent my medium length thick layered hair from getting frizzy?

A: Use a smoothing serum or oil to tame frizz, and avoid using a hairbrush on wet hair, which can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care for Medium Length Thick Layered Hair

When it comes to caring for your medium length thick layered hair, there are a few key tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your medium length thick layered hair with confidence and ease. Whether you're going for a casual everyday look or a more formal style, your hair is sure to look gorgeous and on-trend.
