How To Get Big Fluffy Curls On Natural Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your natural hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to achieve big, fluffy curls that will make you stand out in a crowd? Look no further! In this article, we'll show you how to get big fluffy curls on natural hair using simple techniques that you can do at home. Read on for step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Big Fluffy Curls
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Perfect Curls
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Advice

How to Get Big Fluffy Curls

Getting big fluffy curls on natural hair is easier than you might think. All it takes is a few simple techniques and the right products. Here are the steps:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy, which is essential for achieving big fluffy curls. Rinse your hair thoroughly and blot it dry with a towel.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to smooth out any tangles and prepare your hair for styling. Be sure to apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair, from roots to ends.

3. Use a Curl Enhancing Cream

Apply a curl enhancing cream to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help to define your curls and give them more bounce. Be sure to use a cream that is specifically designed for curly hair, as this will give you the best results.

4. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

5. Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to low heat. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and prevent your hair from becoming too dry. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, scrunching your hair as you go.

6. Finish with a Hair Spray

Once you've finished diffusing your hair, finish with a hair spray to set your curls in place. Be sure to use a spray that is designed for curly hair, as this will help to hold your curls without making them feel stiff or crunchy.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you get big fluffy curls on natural hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner
  3. Use a curl enhancing cream
  4. Section your hair
  5. Use a diffuser
  6. Finish with a hair spray

Tips for Perfect Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect, big fluffy curls:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair before styling
  • Avoid using too much heat, as this can damage your hair and make your curls look frizzy
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled while you sleep
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting big fluffy curls on natural hair:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and how oily it gets. Too much washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and damage.

Q: Can I use regular hair spray on my curls?

A: It's best to use a hair spray that is specifically designed for curly hair, as this will help to hold your curls without making them feel stiff or crunchy.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some general tips for keeping your natural hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve big fluffy curls on natural hair that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair by using gentle products and avoiding harsh styling methods. With a little patience and practice, you can achieve the curls of your dreams!
