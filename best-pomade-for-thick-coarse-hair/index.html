Best Pomade For Thick Coarse Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 12, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have thick, coarse hair, you know that finding the right styling product can be a challenge. Pomade is a great option for taming unruly locks, but not all pomades are created equal. In this guide, we’ll take a look at the best pomades for thick coarse hair, how to use them, and some tips for keeping your hair looking great.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Pomade for Thick Coarse Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Applying Pomade
  • Tips for Using Pomade on Thick Coarse Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Coarse Hair

Description

When it comes to finding the best pomade for thick coarse hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you’ll want to look for a pomade that is specifically formulated for thick hair. These products will typically be heavier and provide more hold than pomades designed for fine or thin hair.

Second, you’ll want to look for a pomade that is moisturizing. Thick, coarse hair can be prone to dryness and breakage, so a pomade that provides hydration and nourishment can help keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Finally, you’ll want to consider the hold and finish you’re looking for. Some pomades provide a strong, matte finish, while others offer a more natural, flexible hold. It’s important to choose a pomade that meets your specific styling needs.

How to Use Pomade for Thick Coarse Hair

Using pomade on thick coarse hair is a fairly simple process, but it’s important to use the right technique to avoid weighing down your hair or creating a greasy, oily look. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair.

Before applying pomade, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a gentle shampoo designed for thick hair, and avoid using too much conditioner, which can weigh down your locks.

Step 2: Warm up the pomade in your hands.

Scoop a small amount of pomade onto your fingertips and rub your hands together to warm up the product. This will make it easier to apply evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply the pomade to your hair.

Starting at the back of your head, work the pomade through your hair using your fingertips. Be sure to distribute the product evenly, paying special attention to the ends of your hair.

Step 4: Style your hair.

Once the pomade is evenly distributed, use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired. You can create a sleek, polished look or opt for a more natural, tousled style.

Step-by-Step Guide for Applying Pomade

Here’s a more detailed guide for applying pomade to thick coarse hair:

Step 1: Choose the right pomade.

As we mentioned earlier, it’s important to choose a pomade that is specifically designed for thick, coarse hair. Look for a product that provides a strong hold and is moisturizing.

Step 2: Prep your hair.

Before applying pomade, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can use a blow dryer to help speed up the drying process, but be sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 3: Warm up the pomade.

Scoop a small amount of pomade onto your fingertips and rub your hands together to warm up the product.

Step 4: Apply the pomade to your hair.

Starting at the back of your head, work the pomade through your hair using your fingertips. Be sure to distribute the product evenly, focusing on the ends of your hair where it tends to be driest.

Step 5: Style your hair.

Once the pomade is evenly distributed, use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired. You can create a sleek, polished look or opt for a more natural, tousled style.

Tips for Using Pomade on Thick Coarse Hair

Here are some additional tips for using pomade on thick coarse hair:

  • Start with a small amount of pomade, and add more as needed.
  • Focus on the ends of your hair, where it tends to be driest.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create your desired style.
  • Avoid using too much pomade, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different types of pomade to find the one that works best for your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best pomade for thick coarse hair?

There are many great pomades on the market that are specifically designed for thick, coarse hair. Some popular options include Baxter of California Clay Pomade, American Crew Fiber, and Suavecito Pomade.

How often should I use pomade on my hair?

It’s best to use pomade sparingly, as too much can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Depending on your styling needs, you may only need to use pomade once or twice a week.

Can I use pomade on wet hair?

Pomade is best used on dry hair. Applying it to wet hair can dilute the product and make it less effective.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Coarse Hair

While using the right pomade can help keep your hair looking great, it’s important to take care of your hair from the inside out. Here are some tips for keeping your thick, coarse hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo designed for thick hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or relaxers, on your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to support healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and using the right pomade for your hair type, you can keep your thick, coarse locks looking great year-round.
