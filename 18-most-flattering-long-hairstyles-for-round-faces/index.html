18 Most Flattering Long Hairstyles For Round Faces

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging. You want to create an illusion of length and slimness, and long hair is just the trick. In this article, we'll be sharing the 18 most flattering long hairstyles for round faces that will make you look and feel fabulous.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Round Faces

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's talk about how to choose the right one for your face shape. The goal is to elongate your face and create a more oval shape. Here are some tips:

  • Go for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.
  • Choose hairstyles with layers that start below the chin. This will create an illusion of length.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs.
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs or layers around your face to create angles and break up the roundness.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Round Faces

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's take a look at the 18 most flattering long hairstyles for round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers starting at the chin or below will add movement and dimension to your hair. This will create the illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer.

2. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great way to add volume to the crown of your head and create texture. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

3. Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers that start at the cheekbones or jawline will create angles and break up the roundness of your face. This will make your face appear slimmer and more defined.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs that start at the cheekbones or jawline will add angles to your face and create a more oval shape. This will make your face appear slimmer and more defined.

5. Layered Bangs

Layered bangs that start below the chin will add dimension and movement to your hair. This will create an illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer.

6. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle will add height to the crown of your head and create volume. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

7. Loose Updo

A loose updo that sits high on the crown of your head will add height and create a more oval shape. This will make your face appear slimmer and more defined.

8. Braided Crown

A braided crown will add height to the crown of your head and create texture. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

9. Side Braid

A side braid will create angles and break up the roundness of your face. This will make your face appear slimmer and more defined.

10. High Ponytail

A high ponytail will add height to the crown of your head and create a more oval shape. This will make your face appear slimmer and more defined.

11. Sleek and Straight

Sleek and straight hair will elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Avoid adding width or volume to the sides of your face.

12. Voluminous Curls

Voluminous curls will add height to the crown of your head and create texture. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

13. Side Part

A side part will create angles and break up the roundness of your face. This will make your face appear slimmer and more defined.

14. Long Bob

A long bob that starts below the chin will create an illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer.

15. Layered Lob

A layered lob will add movement and dimension to your hair. This will create an illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer.

16. Side-Swept Updo

A side-swept updo will create angles and break up the roundness of your face. This will make your face appear slimmer and more defined.

17. Loose Waves

Loose waves will add volume to the crown of your head and create texture. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

18. Layered Pixie

A layered pixie cut will add dimension and movement to your hair. This will create an illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer.

Tips to Maintain Your Long Hairstyle for Round Faces

Now that you've chosen the perfect long hairstyle for your round face, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and let your hair air dry as much as possible.
  • Use hair serum or oil to keep your hair shiny and frizz-free.

FAQs on Long Hairstyles for Round Faces

1. Can I pull off a blunt bob with a round face?

A blunt bob can add width to the sides of your face, which is not ideal for a round face. Instead, go for a long bob that starts below the chin.

2. Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

No, you can still have bangs if you have a round face. Just make sure they start below the cheekbones or jawline to create angles.

3. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just make sure to avoid adding width or volume to the sides of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it is more prone to breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid tying your hair up tightly, as it can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.

With these 18 most flattering long hairstyles for round faces, you'll be sure to find the perfect one for you. Remember to choose a hairstyle that adds height and dimension to your hair, creates angles, and avoids adding width or volume to the sides of your face. With proper care and maintenance, you'll have gorgeous, healthy, and flattering long hair.
