How To Know Which Hairstyle Suits Me App A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of always wearing the same hairstyle? Do you want to try a new look but don't know which hairstyle suits you? Well, you're in luck because there's an app for that! With the "how to know which hairstyle suits me app," you can easily discover which hairstyle suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll show you how to use the app, step-by-step, and provide you with tips for maintaining your new hairstyle.

How to Use the App

The "how to know which hairstyle suits me app" is a user-friendly app that you can download for free on your smartphone. Here's how to use the app:

Step 1: Take a Selfie

The first step is to take a selfie. Make sure you take a photo of your face from the front and in good lighting.

Step 2: Upload Your Selfie

Once you have your selfie, upload it to the app. The app will then analyze your face shape and hair type to determine which hairstyles would suit you best.

Step 3: Browse Hairstyles

After analyzing your selfie, the app will provide you with a variety of hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair type. Browse through the hairstyles and choose the one that you like the most.

Step 4: Try on the Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, you can try it on virtually. The app will superimpose the hairstyle onto your selfie so you can see how it would look on you in real life.

Step 5: Save and Share

If you like the hairstyle, you can save it to your phone and show it to your hairdresser. You can also share it with your friends and family to get their opinion.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Now that you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it so that it looks great all the time. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Different hairstyles require different products. Make sure you use the right products for your hair type and style. For example, if you have curly hair, use products that are designed for curls.

Tip 2: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help keep your hairstyle looking fresh and prevent split ends. Make sure you get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using heat protectant products and limiting your use of hot styling tools.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "how to know which hairstyle suits me app":

What if I don't like any of the hairstyles suggested by the app?

If you don't like any of the hairstyles suggested by the app, you can try taking another selfie with different lighting or angles. You can also browse through other hairstyle apps to find more options.

Is the app accurate?

The app uses advanced facial recognition technology to analyze your face shape and hair type, so it's generally accurate. However, it's important to keep in mind that the app is just a tool and your personal preferences also matter.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is essential to maintaining a good hairstyle. Regular washing and conditioning of hair is important for healthy hair. If you have colored hair, use sulfate-free shampoos to help keep the color vibrant.

In conclusion, the "how to know which hairstyle suits me app" is a great tool for anyone who wants to try a new hairstyle but doesn't know where to start. By following the steps outlined in this article and taking care of your hair, you'll be able to rock your new hairstyle with confidence!
