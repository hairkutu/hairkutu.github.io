Male Haircut Short Back And Sides A Timeless Style

Written by Luffy Jun 08, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking for a classic, timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style, then a male haircut short back and sides might be just what you need. This style has been around for decades and has remained popular among men of all ages. In this article, we'll take a closer look at this haircut, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get a Male Haircut Short Back and Sides
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Short Back and Sides Haircut
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Short Back and Sides Haircut

Description

The male haircut short back and sides is a classic, timeless hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top. The hair is usually tapered or faded, meaning it gradually gets shorter towards the bottom. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it perfect for any occasion.

How to Get a Male Haircut Short Back and Sides

Getting a male haircut short back and sides is easy and can be done at any barbershop or hair salon. Simply ask your hairstylist for a short back and sides haircut, and they will know exactly what to do. You can also bring in a picture of the style you want to make sure you get the exact look you're going for.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for getting a male haircut short back and sides:

  1. Wash your hair before your appointment.
  2. Sit down in the barber's chair and explain the style you want.
  3. The hairstylist will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving it shorter than the hair on top.
  4. The hair on top of your head will be cut to the desired length and styled as desired.
  5. The hairstylist will use scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  6. The hairstylist will style your hair using products like pomade or wax.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Back and Sides Haircut

To keep your male haircut short back and sides looking sharp, here are a few tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair using a small amount of pomade or wax for hold.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can make your hair look greasy.

FAQs

Can anyone get a male haircut short back and sides?

Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for most hair types and face shapes.

How short should the sides and back be?

The length of the sides and back can vary depending on personal preference, but typically they are cut to between 1/4 inch and 2 inches in length.

How long should the hair on top be?

The length of the hair on top can vary depending on personal preference, but typically it is between 1 and 3 inches long.

Hair Care Tips for Short Back and Sides Haircut

To keep your male haircut short back and sides looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can make your hair look greasy.

Overall, the male haircut short back and sides is a classic, timeless hairstyle that is easy to maintain and looks great on anyone. Follow these tips to keep your hair looking its best and enjoy your new, stylish haircut!
