Short Hairstyles For Square Face Girl The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Apr 04, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you a girl with a square face shape and looking for a chic and trendy short hairstyle that suits your face shape? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll take you through some of the best short hairstyles for square face girls. From pixie cuts to bob hairstyles, we’ve got you covered with style inspiration, how-to’s, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose a Short Hairstyle for Square Face Girl
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hairstyles for Square Face Girl
  • Tips to Maintain and Style Short Hairstyles for Square Face Girl
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Square Face Girls with Short Hair

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square-shaped hairline. If you have a square face shape, you’re lucky because you can pull off many different hairstyles. However, it’s essential to choose a hairstyle that softens your facial features and complements your face shape.

Short hairstyles are perfect for square face girls because they highlight the jawline, neck, and cheekbones, creating a balanced and proportional look. Whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated bob or a fun and edgy pixie cut, there’s a short hairstyle for every square face girl.

How to Choose a Short Hairstyle for Square Face Girl

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle for square face girl is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they’re all about the same width, you have a square face shape.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Short hairstyles come in a variety of lengths, textures, and styles, so it’s essential to choose a hairstyle that works for your hair type and complements your personal style.

Here are some short hairstyles that work well for square face girls:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that looks great on square face girls. This style is short on the sides and back and slightly longer on top, creating a soft and feminine look. You can add side-swept bangs to soften your forehead and create a more balanced look.

Bob Hairstyle

The bob hairstyle is another classic short hairstyle that works well for square face girls. This style is typically chin-length or shorter and can be styled straight or with waves to add texture and movement. You can also add layers to soften your facial features and create a more flattering look.

Short Shag

The short shag is a trendy and edgy short hairstyle that looks great on square face girls with thick hair. This style features choppy layers and textured ends, creating a messy and tousled look. You can also add bangs to frame your face and soften your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hairstyles for Square Face Girl

Styling short hairstyles for square face girls is easy and straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your short hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to add volume and hold.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement to your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips to Maintain and Style Short Hairstyles for Square Face Girl

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your short hairstyles for square face girl:

  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your hair looking fresh and stylish.

FAQs

What short hairstyles are best for square face girls?

Short hairstyles that work well for square face girls include the pixie cut, bob hairstyle, and short shag.

How do I determine my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

How do I style short hairstyles for square face girls?

To style short hairstyles for square face girls, start by washing your hair, applying a styling product, blow-drying your hair, and then using heat styling tools to add texture and movement.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Girls with Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips for square face girls with short hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

In conclusion, short hairstyles are perfect for square face girls. Whether you prefer a classic bob or an edgy pixie cut, there’s a short hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to maintain and style your short hair with ease!
