How To Bald Fade Step By Step

Written by William Aug 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you are looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, a bald fade might be the perfect choice for you. A bald fade is a type of haircut where the hair gradually fades from longer to shorter until it reaches the skin, leaving a bald area. This haircut is popular among men of all ages and can be styled in many different ways. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of how to bald fade, including tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Bald Fade
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Bald Fade

Bald fade is a type of haircut that requires some skill and technique. It is recommended that you visit a professional barber or hairdresser to get the perfect bald fade. However, if you like to do it yourself, you can follow these simple steps.

Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

The first step in bald fading is to choose the right clippers. You will need a pair of clippers with different guard lengths. It would help if you also had a pair of scissors and a comb to even out the hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start with the cutting process, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you see the lines and the shape of the haircut better.

Step 3: Start with the Longest Guard

Start with the longest guard on your clippers and cut the hair around the sides and back of your head. Make sure to keep the clipper parallel to your head and move it in an upward motion.

Step 4: Change the Guard Length

Change the guard length and repeat the same process, moving the clipper up the sides and back of your head. Make sure to blend the different lengths to create a smooth transition.

Step 5: Use Scissors and Comb

Once you have reached the desired length, use scissors and a comb to even out the hair. This will help you create a more natural look and blend the different lengths.

Step 6: Trim the Top

Now that you have finished the sides and back, it is time to trim the top. You can choose the length you prefer and use scissors and a comb to cut the hair evenly.

Step 7: Create a Fade

To create a fade, you need to blend the hair from the sides and back to the top. You can use different guard lengths and scissors to achieve a smooth transition.

Step 8: Define the Hairline

The last step is to define the hairline around your ears and neck. You can use a trimmer to create clean lines and give your haircut a polished look.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard length and work your way down to create a natural fade.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure everything is even.
  • Take your time and be patient. Bald fading requires some skill and technique, so do not rush it.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQs

Can I bald fade my hair at home?

Yes, you can bald fade your hair at home, but it is recommended that you visit a professional barber or hairdresser to get the perfect bald fade.

What is the difference between a bald fade and a skin fade?

A bald fade leaves a bald area on the sides and back of the head, while a skin fade does not leave a bald area. The hair gradually fades from shorter to longer until it reaches the skin.

How often should I get a bald fade?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you prefer to maintain your haircut. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and in shape.

Hair Care

After getting a bald fade, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

Now that you know how to bald fade step by step, you can try it yourself or visit a professional barber or hairdresser for the perfect look. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style.
