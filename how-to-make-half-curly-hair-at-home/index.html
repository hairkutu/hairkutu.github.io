How To Make Half Curly Hair At Home

Written by Robby Aug 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having the same straight hair look every day? If you're looking to switch things up and add some volume and texture to your hair, making it half curly is a great option. In this article, we'll take you through the process of how to make half curly hair at home.

Description

Half curly hair is a hairstyle that combines both curly and straight hair in one look. It's a great way to add some texture to your hair and create a more voluminous look. The good news is that you don't need to spend a lot of money at the salon to achieve this look. You can easily do it at home with the right tools and techniques.

How to

Here are the steps to follow to make half curly hair at home:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or buildup on your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it's important to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Next, use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely. Make sure to use a round brush while blow drying to help add some volume to your hair.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, section it into two parts - the top half and the bottom half. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 5: Curl the Bottom Half

Take the bottom half of your hair and use a curling iron to curl it. Make sure to use a large barrel curling iron to create loose, beachy waves. Curl each section of hair away from your face.

Step 6: Straighten the Top Half

Once you've curled the bottom half of your hair, use a flat iron to straighten the top half. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before straightening to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 7: Comb and Style

After straightening the top half and curling the bottom half, comb through your hair to create a seamless transition between the two styles. You can also add some hair spray or other hair products to help hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making half curly hair at home:

  • Use a good quality curling iron and flat iron to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't overdo it with hair products - a little goes a long way.
  • Experiment with different barrel sizes on your curling iron to create different types of curls.
  • Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making half curly hair at home:

Can I make half curly hair with short hair?

Yes, you can make half curly hair with short hair. However, you may need to use smaller barrel curling irons to create tighter curls.

How long will half curly hair last?

Half curly hair can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on how well you maintain it and the products you use.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after styling it. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know how to make half curly hair at home, it's time to give it a try. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a beautiful, voluminous look that's sure to turn heads.
