What Bangs For My Face Shape Tips And Tricks To Find The Perfect Bangs Style

Written by Luffy Apr 07, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Adding bangs to your hair can be a great way to switch things up and frame your face in a new and exciting way. However, not all bangs styles are created equal. In this article, we'll go over how to determine what bangs for your face shape and give you some tips and tricks to make sure you get the perfect look.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Bangs Styles for Different Face Shapes
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Own Bangs
  • Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Bangs
  • FAQs About Bangs for Face Shapes
  • Hair Care Tips for Bangs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can determine what bangs style will look best on you, you need to know your face shape. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Here's how to figure out which one you have:

  • Oval: your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead is slightly wider than your jaw.
  • Round: your face is nearly as wide as it is long, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Square: your face is as wide as it is long, and your jaw is the most prominent feature.
  • Heart: your forehead is wider than your jaw, and your chin comes to a point.
  • Diamond: your forehead and jaw are both narrow, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Long: your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jaw are all about the same width.

Bangs Styles for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to figure out what bangs will look best on you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval: You're lucky - just about any bangs style will look great on you! Consider a blunt cut or long, side-swept bangs.
  • Round: Go for long, side-swept bangs that angle down towards the sides of your face. Avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across.
  • Square: Soften your angular features with long, wispy bangs that angle slightly to the side.
  • Heart: Balance out your face shape with long, side-swept bangs that angle towards the outer corners of your eyes.
  • Diamond: Similar to heart-shaped faces, go for long, side-swept bangs that angle towards the outer corners of your eyes.
  • Long: Add some width to your face with wispy, curtain bangs that start at your temples and angle towards the outer corners of your eyes.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Own Bangs

If you're feeling brave and want to cut your own bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with dry, styled hair that's free of tangles.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape, with the point of the triangle at the center of your forehead.
  3. Use hair cutting shears to cut straight across the triangle, starting at the center and working outwards. Cut less than you think you need - you can always go back and trim more later.
  4. Use a comb to blend the bangs into the rest of your hair, and make any additional adjustments as needed.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Bangs

Once you have your bangs, there are a few things you can do to keep them looking great:

  • Wash your bangs more frequently than the rest of your hair, as they can get greasy faster.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to give them volume and shape.
  • Use dry shampoo to extend the time between washes and keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting shears if you plan on trimming your own bangs regularly.

FAQs About Bangs for Face Shapes

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs for different face shapes:

Can round faces pull off blunt bangs?

While it's not impossible, blunt bangs can make round faces look even rounder. Long, side-swept bangs that angle down towards the sides of your face are a better option.

What bangs style looks best on heart-shaped faces?

Long, side-swept bangs that angle towards the outer corners of your eyes can help balance out a heart-shaped face.

Should square faces avoid choppy bangs?

Yes - soft, wispy bangs that angle to the side are a better option for square faces.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Lastly, here are some general hair care tips for keeping your bangs looking their best:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools on your bangs.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much throughout the day, as the oils from your fingers can make them greasy.
  • If you're struggling to style your bangs, consider using a hair straightener or curling iron to add some texture.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from getting too long and weighing down your face.

With these tips and tricks, you're well on your way to finding the perfect bangs for your face shape. Whether you go for long, side-swept bangs or wispy curtain bangs, remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect look for you!
