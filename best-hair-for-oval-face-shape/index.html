Best Hair For Oval Face Shape How To Choose The Perfect Style In 2023

Written by Frank May 29, 2023 · 10 min read

The oval face shape is considered one of the most versatile face shapes out there, as it can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, choosing the best hair for oval face shape can still be a daunting task, especially if you want to flatter your features and bring out your natural beauty. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape, from the best cuts and styles to the essential hair care tips you need to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Hair for Oval Face Shape
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair's Health and Beauty
  5. FAQs About Best Hair for Oval Face Shape
  6. Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shapes

Description

The oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline all roughly the same width. This face shape is considered the ideal for many, as it can pull off almost any hairstyle with ease. However, not all hairstyles are created equal, and some may flatter your features more than others.

When choosing the best hair for oval face shape, it's important to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Some hairstyles may require more maintenance than others, or may not suit your face shape if you have particularly fine or thick hair. Similarly, some styles may be more suitable for casual wear, while others may be better suited for formal occasions.

How to Choose the Best Hair for Oval Face Shape

The best hair for oval face shape is one that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect style:

  • Consider your hair texture: Fine hair may look best with shorter styles, while thicker hair may benefit from longer cuts.
  • Think about your lifestyle: If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance style may be more suitable for you.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities with oval face shapes: Famous oval-faced beauties include Beyoncé, Jessica Alba, and Emma Watson.
  • Consult with a hairstylist: A professional hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Follow these steps to find the best hair for oval face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape: Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are roughly equal, you have an oval face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture: Fine hair may look best with shorter styles, while thicker hair may benefit from longer cuts.
  3. Think about your lifestyle: If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance style may be more suitable for you.
  4. Take inspiration from celebrities with oval face shapes: Famous oval-faced beauties include Beyoncé, Jessica Alba, and Emma Watson.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist: A professional hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your personal style.
  6. Try on different styles: Use virtual try-on tools or try out different wigs to see how different styles look on you.
  7. Experiment with color: If you're feeling daring, try out a new hair color to enhance your features.
  8. Consider hair extensions: If you have short hair or want to add volume to your locks, hair extensions can be a great option.
  9. Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
  10. Take care of your hair: Use high-quality hair care products and follow a regular hair care routine to keep your locks looking their best.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair's Health and Beauty

If you want to keep your locks looking their best, here are some tips to follow:

  • Use high-quality hair care products: Invest in shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat can damage your hair and lead to split ends and breakage. Try to air dry your hair whenever possible, or use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can damage your hair and fade your color. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to shield your locks from the sun's harmful rays.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Stay hydrated: Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs About Best Hair for Oval Face Shape

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the best hair for oval face shape:

What hairstyles flatter oval face shapes?

Almost any hairstyle can flatter an oval face shape, but some popular styles include layered cuts, long bobs, and blunt bangs.

Should I choose a short or long hairstyle?

It depends on your hair texture and personal style. Fine hair may look best with shorter styles, while thicker hair may benefit from longer cuts. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best length for you.

Can I wear my hair in a center part with an oval face shape?

Yes, a center part can be a great option for oval face shapes, as it can help balance out your features and draw attention to your eyes.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shapes

Here are some essential hair care tips for oval face shapes:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

Choosing the best hair for oval face shape can be a fun and rewarding experience, as you explore different styles and find the perfect look for you. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep your locks looking their best and enhance your natural beauty.
