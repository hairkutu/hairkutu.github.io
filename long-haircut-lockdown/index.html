Long Haircut Lockdown How To Get The Perfect Long Haircut At Home

Written by Eveline Aug 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long hair during lockdown but can't go to the salon because of the pandemic? Don't worry because we have you covered! In this blog post, we will teach you how to get the perfect long haircut at home. From how to prepare your hair to the step-by-step process, we will give you all the tips and tricks you need to know to get the perfect haircut during lockdown. So, let's get started!

Description

Long haircut lockdown is a term used to describe the situation where people can't visit a hair salon due to lockdown restrictions. This means that people have to cut their hair at home, which can be a daunting task, especially if you don't have any experience. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself a perfect long haircut at home.

How to Get the Perfect Long Haircut at Home

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step in getting the perfect long haircut at home is to prepare your hair. This means washing your hair thoroughly and combing it out to remove any tangles. This will ensure that your hair is clean and easy to work with.

Step 2: Gather Your Tools

Once your hair is clean and tangle-free, it's time to gather your tools. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Make sure that your scissors are sharp and clean to ensure a clean cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

The next step is to section your hair. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then divide it into two sections. Clip each section to keep it in place.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting! Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up. Take small sections of hair and cut them with your scissors. Make sure to cut evenly and follow the shape of your hair. Take your time and don't rush.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Once you have finished cutting your hair, it's time to check your progress. Look in the mirror and make sure that your hair is even and that there are no uneven spots. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them until your hair is even.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect long haircut at home:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Use hair clips to keep your hair in place while you're cutting. This will make it easier to work with.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. This can result in uneven cuts.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so it's okay to take a break if you need one.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long haircut lockdown:

1. How often should I cut my hair during lockdown?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. If you want to maintain your current length, you should cut your hair every two to three months.

2. Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, you should always use hair cutting scissors. Regular scissors can damage your hair and result in uneven cuts.

3. Should I cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

You should always cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can stretch and result in uneven cuts.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently give yourself the perfect long haircut during lockdown. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the steps carefully. Happy cutting!
