Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair Indian

Written by Bardi Jul 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long hair is a crowning glory, especially for Indian women. However, managing long hair can be a daunting task, especially when you're looking for quick and easy hairstyles. In this article, we'll explore some easy hairstyles for long hair Indian that you can try out yourself.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to easy hairstyles for long hair Indian, there are numerous options. From simple braids to elegant updos, the possibilities are endless.

Whether you're looking for a hairstyle for everyday wear or a special occasion, we've got you covered. These hairstyles are easy to do and require minimal effort and time.

How To

The first step to achieving any hairstyle is to ensure that your hair is clean and well-conditioned. It's also essential to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Next, gather the necessary tools such as a comb, hair ties, bobby pins, and hair spray. You may also need a curling iron or straightener, depending on the hairstyle you choose.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for easy hairstyles for long hair Indian:

Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair in the middle and braid two sections of hair on either side of your head.
  2. Wrap the braids around your head, securing them with bobby pins.
  3. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it into a ponytail.
  2. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to the loose hair.
  3. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and tie it with a hair tie.
  2. Twist the hair around the hair tie to create a bun.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying out easy hairstyles for long hair Indian:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and ribbons to add some flair to your hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member when styling your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for long hair Indian:

Q: Can I do these hairstyles on my own?

A: Yes, these hairstyles are easy to do and require minimal effort and time.

Q: What tools do I need to achieve these hairstyles?

A: You will need a comb, hair ties, bobby pins, and hair spray. You may also need a curling iron or straightener, depending on the hairstyle you choose.

Q: How do I keep my hair healthy and shiny?

A: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy and shiny long hair:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip the scalp of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these easy hairstyles for long hair Indian, you can switch up your look without spending hours in front of the mirror. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to add some flair with hair accessories.
