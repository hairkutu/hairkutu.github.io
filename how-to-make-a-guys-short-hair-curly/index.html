How To Make A Guy s Short Hair Curly Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight and boring short hair? Do you want to spice things up and add some texture to your look? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to make a guy's short hair curly in a few easy steps. From using the right products to styling techniques, we've got you covered. So, let's dive right in!

How to Make a Guy's Short Hair Curly

Before we get started, it's important to note that there are different ways to make short hair curly, depending on your hair type and texture. However, the following steps are suitable for most hair types and will help you achieve a curly look.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to achieving curly hair is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup that can weigh down your curls.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

After washing your hair, apply a curl enhancing product to damp hair. This product will help define your curls and keep them in place. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your preference. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. A diffuser will help create volume and enhance your curls. Start by drying your hair at a low heat and speed setting. Once your hair is about 80% dry, switch to a cool setting to set your curls in place.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your hair and define your curls. You can also use a curling iron or wand to add more curls and texture to your hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant before using any hot tools.

Tips for Making a Guy's Short Hair Curly

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect curly look:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair once it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, as this can also cause frizz.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a guy's short hair curly:

Can I make my short hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can use a variety of methods to make your hair curly without using heat, such as using rollers, braiding your hair, or using a twist out method.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls can last up to a few days, depending on how well you maintain them. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and preserve your curls overnight.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your guy's short hair curly and rock a new look. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. Have fun and embrace your curls!
