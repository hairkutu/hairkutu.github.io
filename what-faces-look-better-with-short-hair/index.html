What Faces Look Better With Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're thinking about getting a short haircut, you might be wondering if it will suit your face shape. Short hair can be a great choice for many people, but not all face shapes are created equal. In this article, we'll go over what faces look better with short hair, how to choose the right haircut for your face shape, and some tips for taking care of your new 'do.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut
  • Tips for Taking Care of Your Short Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

Description

Short hair can be a great choice for many people. It's low-maintenance, versatile, and can be styled in many different ways. However, not all face shapes look good with short hair. The key is to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and brings out your best features.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right short haircut is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a haircut that flatters your features and balances out your proportions.

Oval face: Lucky you! Oval faces look good with almost any haircut, including short styles like pixie cuts, bobs, and lobs.

Round face: Round faces look best with haircuts that add height and length to the face, such as layered bobs and long pixie cuts.

Square face: Square faces look great with haircuts that soften the jawline and add volume to the crown, such as layered bobs and A-line cuts.

Heart face: Heart-shaped faces look good with haircuts that balance out the forehead and the chin, such as short bobs and pixie cuts with side-swept bangs.

Diamond face: Diamond faces look good with haircuts that add fullness to the forehead and chin, such as textured bobs and layered pixie cuts.

Long face: Long faces look best with haircuts that add width to the face, such as blunt bobs and layered pixie cuts.

Key Takeaway:

When choosing a short haircut, consider your face shape to find a style that flatters your features and balances out your proportions.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut

If you've decided to take the plunge and get a short haircut, here's a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

  1. Research different haircuts and styles that you like.
  2. Find a reputable hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts.
  3. Bring pictures of the haircut you want to your appointment.
  4. Discuss your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle with your stylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  5. Ask your stylist to show you how to style your new haircut at home.
  6. Take care of your new haircut by washing and conditioning it regularly, using a heat protectant before styling, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape.

Key Takeaway:

Getting a short haircut can be a big change, but with the right stylist and a little bit of care, you can rock your new 'do with confidence.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts are low-maintenance, but that doesn't mean you can neglect your hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your short haircut:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

Key Takeaway:

Short haircuts are easy to care for, but it's important to use the right products and tools to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

FAQs

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. The key is to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and brings out your best features. A layered bob or long pixie cut can add height and length to a round face, making it appear more oval.

Q: Can I still style my short haircut in different ways?

A: Absolutely! Short haircuts can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous. Experiment with different products and tools to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short haircuts are easy to care for, but it's important to use the right products and tools to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for short hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

Key Takeaway:

Short haircuts can be a great choice for many people, but it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape and suits your lifestyle. With the right care, you can enjoy your new 'do and feel confident and stylish every day.
