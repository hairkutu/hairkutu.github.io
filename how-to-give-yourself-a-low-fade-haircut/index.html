How To Give Yourself A Low Fade Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new look? A low fade haircut might just be what you need. This haircut is becoming increasingly popular, and it’s easy to see why. It’s a stylish, modern look that’s perfect for any occasion. The best part? You can easily do it yourself at home. In this article, we’ll show you how to give yourself a low fade haircut.

What is a Low Fade Haircut?

Before we get into the steps, let’s talk about what a low fade haircut is. A fade is a hairstyle that gradually blends from short to long hair. A low fade means that the fade starts just above the ears and the neckline. This creates a clean, polished look that’s perfect for any occasion.

How to Give Yourself a Low Fade Haircut

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step is to gather all the tools you’ll need. You’ll need clippers, scissors, a comb, a mirror, and a towel. Make sure your clippers have guards of different sizes so you can get the length you want.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. Use the clippers with the appropriate guard to trim the top of your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you get the desired length.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back

Now it’s time to trim the sides and back. Start with a longer guard and work your way down. Remember, you want the fade to start just above the ears and the neckline. Use a comb to help guide the clippers.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

Once the sides and back are trimmed, it’s time to blend the top and sides. Use the scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. Use the comb to blend the top with the sides. This will create a smooth transition between the top and sides.

Step 5: Define the Hairline

Use the clippers to define the hairline around the ears and neckline. Be careful not to cut too much. You can always go back and trim more if needed.

Step 6: Clean Up

After you’ve finished trimming, it’s time to clean up. Use the towel to wipe away any loose hair. Take a shower to wash away any remaining hair and to refresh your scalp.

Tips for Giving Yourself a Low Fade Haircut

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down. You can always trim more, but you can’t add hair back.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers.
  • Take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Use the mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Ask for help if you need it.

FAQ

Can I give myself a low fade haircut?

Yes, you can. It takes a bit of practice, but it’s definitely doable.

What if I make a mistake?

Don’t worry. Hair grows back. You can always go to a barber to fix any mistakes.

How often should I trim my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have a fresh new haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. This can damage it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth.

Now you know how to give yourself a low fade haircut. With a little practice, you’ll be able to create a stylish new look whenever you want.
