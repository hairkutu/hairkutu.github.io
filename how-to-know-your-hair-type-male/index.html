How To Know Your Hair Type Male A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 05, 2023 · 8 min read

Knowing your hair type is crucial for proper hair care. However, it can be tricky for men, especially those who have recently started taking care of their hair. In this guide, we will help you identify your hair type and provide tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Hair Type
  • Different Hair Types for Men
  • Tips for Hair Care Based on Your Hair Type
  • FAQs About Men's Hair Types

How to Determine Your Hair Type

Before we dive into identifying your hair type, you need to make sure that your hair is clean and dry. It is also best to avoid using any hair products before determining your hair type.

Once your hair is clean and dry, follow these steps to identify your hair type:

Step 1: Look at Your Hair Texture

Take a strand of your hair and feel it between your fingers. Is it fine, medium, or thick? Fine hair is usually delicate and feels soft, medium hair is thicker and feels rougher, while thick hair is the thickest and feels coarse.

Step 2: Check Your Hair Density

Next, check your hair density, which refers to the number of hair strands on your scalp. Use a mirror to check if you can see your scalp easily. If you can see it, you have low hair density. If you can barely see it, you have medium hair density. If you cannot see it at all, you have high hair density.

Step 3: Observe Your Hair Pattern

Finally, observe your hair pattern, which refers to the shape of your hair strands. Is your hair straight, wavy, curly, or coily? Straight hair is usually smooth and lacks any visible curls or waves, wavy hair has a slight curl and is usually easy to manage, curly hair has visible curls and can be prone to frizz, while coily hair has tight curls and can be prone to dryness.

Different Hair Types for Men

Based on your hair texture, density, and pattern, your hair can fall into one of the following categories:

Straight Hair

Straight hair is usually soft and shiny, but can be prone to becoming oily quickly. It is also susceptible to damage from heat and chemicals.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair has a slight curl and is usually easy to manage. It can be prone to frizz and can benefit from regular conditioning.

Curly Hair

Curly hair has visible curls and can be prone to dryness. It requires regular moisturizing and gentle handling to prevent breakage.

Coily Hair

Coily hair has tight curls and can be prone to dryness and breakage. It requires ample moisture and gentle handling to maintain its shape and prevent damage.

Tips for Hair Care Based on Your Hair Type

Once you have identified your hair type, you can tailor your hair care routine accordingly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair based on your hair type:

Straight Hair

  • Use a mild shampoo that does not strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently to prevent damage.
  • Use a conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.

Wavy Hair

  • Use a shampoo that is designed for wavy hair to enhance its natural texture.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

Curly Hair

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to prevent breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.

Coily Hair

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner and seal in the moisture with oil or butter.

FAQs About Men's Hair Types

Q: Can hair type change over time?

A: Yes, hair type can change due to factors such as hormones, medication, and aging.

Q: How often should I wash my hair based on my hair type?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Straight hair can be washed daily, while curly and coily hair can be washed once a week or less frequently.

Q: Can I style my hair differently if I know my hair type?

A: Yes, knowing your hair type can help you choose the best styling products and techniques for your hair.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy and great-looking hair based on your hair type. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
