How To Give Curls Volume Tips And Tricks In 2023

Written by William May 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone with curly hair, you know the struggle of keeping your curls looking bouncy and voluminous. Sometimes, curls can fall flat and lose their shape, leaving you feeling frustrated and defeated. But fear not, because there are some simple tips and tricks you can use to give your curls the volume they deserve. In this article, we'll explore how to give curls volume in relaxed English language so that you can achieve a full, voluminous look with ease.

Table of Contents

  • How to Give Curls Volume
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Voluminous Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Voluminous Curls

How to Give Curls Volume

The key to giving curls volume is to start with the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they will help to enhance your curls and prevent frizz. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your curls.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing mousse or gel to wet hair, scrunching it in to encourage curl formation.
  • Dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, using a low heat setting to avoid frizz.
  • Once your hair is dry, gently fluff your curls with your fingers to add volume.

These simple steps will help to give your curls the volume they need to look their best.

Step-by-Step Guide

To give your curls volume, follow these simple steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing mousse or gel to wet hair, scrunching it in to encourage curl formation.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting to avoid frizz.
  4. Gently fluff your curls with your fingers to add volume.
  5. For extra volume, use a hair pick to lift your roots.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

By following these steps, you can achieve full, voluminous curls that will last all day.

Tips for Voluminous Curls

Here are some additional tips for giving your curls volume:

  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your curls.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can disrupt curl formation and cause frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your curls healthy and prevent split ends.

By incorporating these tips into your hair care routine, you can keep your curls looking voluminous and healthy.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and texture, but most people with curly hair can benefit from washing their hair every 2-3 days. This helps to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz.

Can I use a hair dryer without a diffuser attachment?

You can, but using a diffuser attachment helps to distribute heat more evenly and prevent frizz. If you don't have a diffuser attachment, try using a low heat setting and holding the hair dryer at a distance from your head.

What should I do if my curls are still falling flat?

You may need to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type. Consider consulting with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair for personalized advice.

Hair Care for Voluminous Curls

To keep your curls looking their best, be sure to take good care of your hair. This includes:

  • Using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can disrupt curl formation.
  • Avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Deep conditioning your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and frizz while you sleep.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your curls looking voluminous and healthy.

Conclusion

Giving your curls volume doesn't have to be a challenge. By using the right products, techniques, and hair care practices, you can achieve full, bouncy curls that will turn heads. Whether you're getting ready for a special occasion or just want to feel confident in your daily life, these tips and tricks will help you achieve the voluminous curls of your dreams.
