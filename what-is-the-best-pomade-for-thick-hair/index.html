The Best Pomade For Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Apr 27, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks great, it can be tough to manage. Finding the right pomade for your thick hair can make all the difference in achieving the perfect hairstyle. In this guide, we'll take a look at what pomade is, how to choose the best pomade for your thick hair, and some tips for using it effectively.

What is Pomade?

Pomade is a hairstyling product that is typically used to give hair a high shine, slick look. It is made from a combination of waxes, oils, and other ingredients that provide hold and texture. Pomade can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, from classic slicked-back looks to messy, textured styles.

How to Choose the Best Pomade for Thick Hair

Choosing the right pomade for your hair type can be a challenge. When it comes to thick hair, you'll want to look for a pomade that provides strong hold and control. Here are some key factors to consider:

Hold

The most important factor to consider when choosing a pomade for thick hair is the hold. Look for a pomade that provides a strong hold that can withstand the weight and thickness of your hair. A pomade with a strong hold will also help your hairstyle last longer throughout the day.

Texture

Thick hair can be difficult to style, so you'll want to look for a pomade that provides texture and separation. A pomade with a matte finish can help to create a more natural, textured look.

Ingredients

When choosing a pomade, it's important to consider the ingredients. Look for a pomade that is made with natural ingredients, such as beeswax, coconut oil, or shea butter. These ingredients can help to nourish your hair while providing hold and texture.

Step by Step: How to Use Pomade on Thick Hair

Now that you've chosen the best pomade for your hair type, it's time to learn how to use it effectively. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any product buildup and prepare your hair for styling.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Pomade

Scoop a small amount of pomade onto your fingertips and rub your hands together to warm it up. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips. Be sure to distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired. For a slicked-back look, comb your hair straight back. For a messy, textured look, use your fingers to create separation and texture.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

For extra hold, finish your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Using Pomade on Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your pomade:

Tip 1: Use a Small Amount

A little goes a long way when it comes to pomade. Start with a small amount and add more as needed. Using too much pomade can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

Tip 2: Experiment with Different Styles

Pomade can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, so don't be afraid to experiment. Try out different styles until you find the one that works best for you.

Tip 3: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Try to limit washing your hair to every other day or every few days to keep it healthy and moisturized.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Pomade for Thick Hair

Here are some common questions people have about using pomade on thick hair:

Q: Can pomade damage my hair?

A: Pomade is generally safe to use on hair, but using too much or using it too frequently can cause buildup and make your hair look greasy. Be sure to wash your hair regularly and use pomade in moderation.

Q: Can pomade be used on all hair types?

A: Pomade can be used on all hair types, but the type of pomade you use may vary depending on your hair type. For thick hair, choose a pomade with a strong hold and texture.

Q: How do I remove pomade from my hair?

A: Pomade can be removed from hair with shampoo and warm water. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all traces of pomade.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Using the right pomade can help you achieve the perfect hairstyle, but it's also important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for taking care of thick hair:

Tip 1: Use a High-Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for thick hair. Look for products that contain nourishing ingredients, such as keratin or argan oil, to help strengthen and protect your hair.

Tip 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Thick hair can be prone to tangling, so use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair. Avoid using a brush, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage. Try to limit your use of hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, and use a heat protectant spray when you do style your hair.

With the right pomade and proper hair care, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle for your thick hair. Use these tips and tricks to get the most out of your pomade and keep your hair looking healthy and strong.
