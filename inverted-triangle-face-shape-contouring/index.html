Inverted Triangle Face Shape Contouring How To Enhance Your Features

Written by Aprilia Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to contour your inverted triangle face shape? Don't worry! In this article, we will guide you on how to enhance your features with the right contouring techniques. Whether you have a prominent forehead or a broad jawline, we have got you covered.

What is an Inverted Triangle Face Shape?

An inverted triangle face shape is characterized by a wide forehead and cheekbones with a narrow jawline. This face shape is also known as a heart-shaped face. If you have this face shape, your forehead and cheekbones are the most prominent features of your face.

How to Contour an Inverted Triangle Face Shape?

Contouring an inverted triangle face shape involves creating an illusion of a balanced face by minimizing the forehead and cheekbones and enhancing the jawline. Here are the steps to achieve the perfect contour:

Step 1: Prep Your Face

Before you start contouring, it's essential to prep your face. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser, and then apply a moisturizer to hydrate your skin. Apply a primer to create a smooth base for your makeup.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply a foundation that matches your skin tone. Use a beauty blender or a brush to blend the foundation evenly on your face.

Step 3: Highlight

Use a highlighter to bring attention to the center of your face. Apply the highlighter on your forehead, under your eyes, and on your nose bridge. Blend it well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Contour

Now it's time to contour your face. Use a contour stick or powder that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it on your temples, along your hairline, and under your cheekbones. Blend it well to create a natural-looking shadow.

Step 5: Enhance Your Jawline

To create a defined jawline, apply a contour shade under your jawline and blend it well. You can also apply a highlighter on your chin to make it pop.

Step 6: Finish with Blush and Setting Powder

Apply a blush on the apples of your cheeks to add a natural flush to your face. Finally, set your makeup with a setting powder to make it last longer.

Tips for Inverted Triangle Face Shape Contouring

Here are some tips to keep in mind while contouring your inverted triangle face shape:

  • Blend your makeup well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Use a light hand while applying the contour shade.
  • Choose a contour shade that's only one or two shades darker than your skin tone.
  • Apply the contour shade in a diagonal line to create a natural-looking shadow.
  • Use a highlighter that's lighter than your skin tone to create a contrast.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best contouring product for an inverted triangle face shape?

You can use a cream contour stick or a powder contour kit to contour your face. Choose a shade that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone.

Can I contour my face without foundation?

While it's not necessary to apply foundation before contouring, it's recommended to create a smooth base for your makeup.

How often should I contour my face?

You can contour your face as often as you like. However, it's essential to give your skin a break from heavy makeup products to avoid clogged pores and breakouts.

Hair Care for Inverted Triangle Face Shape

If you have an inverted triangle face shape, you can balance your features by opting for hairstyles that add volume to your jawline. Side-swept bangs, layered haircuts, and beachy waves can help soften your face shape.

Now that you know how to contour your inverted triangle face shape, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your skin. Remember to choose the right products and blend your makeup well for a natural-looking finish!
