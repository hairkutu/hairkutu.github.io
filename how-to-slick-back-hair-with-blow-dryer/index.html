How To Slick Back Hair With Blow Dryer A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a sleek and stylish way to wear your hair, slicking it back with a blow dryer is a great option. This technique is perfect for men and women with medium to long hair who want a polished and sophisticated look. In this article, we'll show you how to slick back your hair with a blow dryer in just a few easy steps.

How to Slick Back Hair with Blow Dryer

Slicking back your hair with a blow dryer is a simple process that can be done in just a few minutes. Here are the basic steps:

What You'll Need:

  • Blow dryer
  • Brush
  • Hair gel or pomade

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a clean towel to gently pat your hair dry. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizziness.

Step 3: Apply Hair Gel or Pomade

Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair Back

Using a brush, gently brush your hair back away from your face. Start at the hairline and work your way down to the nape of your neck.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer on the low or medium setting, blow dry your hair in the direction of the brush. Hold the dryer about 6 inches away from your head and move it around to evenly distribute the heat.

Step 6: Apply More Hair Gel or Pomade (Optional)

If you want a more polished look, you can apply another small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, focusing on the ends.

Tips for Slicking Back Hair with Blow Dryer

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair gel or pomade is crucial for achieving a sleek and polished look. Look for products that provide a strong hold and a shiny finish.

Tip 2: Don't Overdo It

Applying too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Tip 3: Use a Quality Brush

A good brush can make all the difference when it comes to slicking back your hair. Look for a brush with firm bristles that can easily detangle your hair.

FAQs

Q: How often should I slick back my hair with a blow dryer?

A: It's best to limit the use of heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair. Slicking back your hair with a blow dryer once or twice a week should be sufficient.

Q: Can I slick back my hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still slick back your hair if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use more product and spend more time blow drying your hair to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle and free from harsh chemicals. This will help to prevent damage and breakage.

Tip 2: Condition Regularly

Conditioning your hair regularly can help to keep it soft and manageable. Look for a conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Using heat styling tools can cause damage to your hair over time. To minimize the risk, use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using other heat styling tools.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look by slicking back your hair with a blow dryer. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
