How To Do A Man Bun With Short Hair

Written by Luffy May 11, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have short hair and are looking to rock a man bun, you might be wondering how to make it work. While it may seem impossible at first, there are actually a few tricks and tips that can help you achieve the look you want. In this article, we’ll show you how to do a man bun with short hair step by step, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice to keep your bun looking great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a Man Bun with Short Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Do a Man Bun with Short Hair

The first thing you need to do is grow your hair out to a length that can be tied up into a bun. This can take some time, but be patient and avoid cutting your hair in the meantime. You’ll also need a few tools to help you create the perfect bun:

  • Hair elastics
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair gel or wax

Once your hair is long enough, follow the steps below to create your man bun:

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by combing your hair back and gathering it into a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a hair elastic to secure it in place.

Step 2: Twist the ponytail tightly to create a rope-like formation.

Step 3: Wrap the twisted ponytail around the base of the hair elastic to form a bun shape.

Step 4: Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place, making sure to hide them within the hair so they aren’t visible.

Step 5: Apply hair gel or wax to any loose strands of hair to keep them in place and create a more polished look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect man bun:

  • Use a hair elastic that matches your hair colour to avoid it being visible.
  • Try different variations of the man bun, such as a half-up bun or a messy bun.
  • Don’t tie your hair too tightly, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQ

Q: How long does my hair need to be to do a man bun?

A: Your hair should be at least 6 inches in length to be able to tie it up into a bun.

Q: Can I still do a man bun if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still do a man bun with thin hair. Use hair gel or wax to create volume and texture, and consider adding clip-in extensions if you want more fullness.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair healthy and looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a man bun with short hair and look great doing it. Remember to be patient and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong.
