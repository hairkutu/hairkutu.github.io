How To Do A Classy High Ponytail

Written by Petter Apr 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

A high ponytail is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that never goes out of style. It can be worn on any occasion, be it a casual day out or a formal event. A high ponytail can also make you look taller and accentuate your cheekbones. In this post, we will guide you on how to do a classy high ponytail step by step.

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to achieve a classy high ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it is smooth.
  2. Gather all your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. To hide the hair tie, take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie. Secure it with a bobby pin.
  4. You can also add some volume to your ponytail by teasing it with a comb or brush. This will make your ponytail look fuller and more voluminous.
  5. Finish off the look by spraying some hairspray to keep your ponytail in place.

And voila! You have a classy high ponytail that is perfect for any occasion.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • If you have thin hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray before creating your ponytail to add more volume.
  • If you have a round face, try pulling some hair out around your temples to create a more flattering look.
  • You can also add some accessories such as a scrunchie or a bow to your ponytail to make it look more stylish.

FAQ

How can I make my high ponytail look more polished?

You can use a flat iron to straighten your hair and make it look more polished. You can also use a hair serum to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

Can I wear a high ponytail if I have short hair?

Yes, you can still wear a high ponytail even if you have short hair. You can use hair extensions or a hairpiece to add length and volume to your ponytail.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too many heat styling tools as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy hair and achieve a perfect high ponytail every time.

So there you have it, a step-by-step guide on how to do a classy high ponytail. Remember to follow these tips and take care of your hair to achieve the best results. Whether youâre going on a date or attending a wedding, a high ponytail is a versatile hairstyle that will never go out of style.
