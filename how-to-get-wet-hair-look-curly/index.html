How To Get Wet Hair Look Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 22, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of always having straight hair? Do you want to switch things up and try out a curly look? Getting the wet hair look curly is a popular trend that can add volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we’ll show you how to achieve this look in just a few simple steps. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Wash your hair
  • Step 2: Apply curl-enhancing products
  • Step 3: Scrunch your hair
  • Step 4: Let your hair air-dry
  • Tips for getting the wet hair look curly
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Wash your hair

The first step to getting the wet hair look curly is to wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. These products are specially formulated to add moisture and definition to your curls. After washing, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply curl-enhancing products

Next, apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to your damp hair. These products will help define your curls and keep them in place. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch your hair

Once your hair is coated in curl-enhancing product, use your hands to scrunch your hair. This will help enhance your curls and create that wet hair look. Continue to scrunch your hair until you have achieved the desired level of curliness.

Step 4: Let your hair air-dry

Finally, let your hair air-dry. Avoid using a hairdryer, as this can disrupt your curls and make them frizzy. Instead, let your hair dry naturally, and don’t touch it until it’s completely dry. Once your hair is dry, you can use your hands to fluff up your curls and create more volume.

Tips for getting the wet hair look curly

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before washing.
  • Don’t use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to help enhance your curls.
  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, so it’s important to find a balance that works for you.

Q: Can I get the wet hair look curly without using product?

A: It’s possible, but using product can help enhance your curls and keep them in place. If you have naturally curly hair, you may be able to achieve the wet hair look without product. However, using a curl-enhancing product can help define your curls and make them more voluminous.

Hair Care Tips

Getting the wet hair look curly can be a fun way to switch up your hairstyle. However, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach and relaxers, on your hair.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you can achieve the wet hair look curly and keep your hair healthy and strong. Happy styling!
