Description

Shoulder length hair is an incredibly versatile length. It can be styled in a variety of ways, from braids to updos to loose waves. However, if you have a short neck, you may be wondering if shoulder length hair is right for you. The good news is that there are ways to style shoulder length hair that can complement a shorter neck. In this blog post, we will give you tips and tricks for styling shoulder length hair with a short neck.

How to Determine if Shoulder Length Hair is Right for a Short Neck

If you have a short neck, you may be hesitant to try shoulder length hair. However, there are a few things you can consider to determine if this length is right for you.

Consider Your Face Shape

First, consider your face shape. If you have a round or square face shape, shoulder length hair may actually help elongate your face and draw attention away from your neck. If you have a heart-shaped face, you may want to opt for a shorter cut to avoid adding too much volume around your chin.

Consult with a Stylist

If you're still unsure, it's always a good idea to consult with a stylist. They can help you determine the best length and style for your face shape and neck length.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair with a Short Neck

Now that you've determined that shoulder length hair is right for you, it's time to start styling! Here is a step-by-step guide to styling shoulder length hair with a short neck:

Step 1: Start with a Good Haircut

The first step to styling shoulder length hair with a short neck is to start with a good haircut. Ask your stylist for layers that will add volume and movement to your hair.

Step 2: Avoid Heavy Bangs

If you have a short neck, heavy bangs can make your neck look even shorter. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or no bangs at all.

Step 3: Add Volume to the Crown

Adding volume to the crown of your head can help elongate your neck. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your roots.

Step 4: Style with Loose Waves

Loose waves can help soften the look of your hair and draw attention away from your neck. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair with a Short Neck

Now that you've styled your hair, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining shoulder length hair with a short neck:

Tip 1: Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that will nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have a short neck?

A: Yes! Just be sure to keep your hair loose and avoid tight updos that can emphasize your neck.

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a short neck?

A: Yes, but opt for side-swept bangs or no bangs at all to avoid adding volume around your chin.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair with a Short Neck

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for shoulder length hair with a short neck:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb will help prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days instead.

Tip 3: Use a Hair Mask Once a Week

A weekly hair mask will keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock shoulder length hair with a short neck. Remember to consult with a stylist if you're unsure, and always take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
