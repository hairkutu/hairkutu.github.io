Mens Short Spiky Hair A Guide For Relaxed Easy To Understand Styling

Written by Aprilia Jul 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a man looking for a low-maintenance, stylish haircut, look no further than mens short spiky hair. This look is versatile, easy to style, and perfect for those who want a bit of edge in their hair without spending a lot of time on it.

Description

Mens short spiky hair is a haircut where the hair is cut short on the sides and back, and left longer on top. The longer hair on top is then styled into short, spiky pieces using hair gel, wax, or pomade. This style is popular among men of all ages and is perfect for those who want a modern, edgy look.

How To

To achieve mens short spiky hair, you'll need to start with the right cut. Ask your barber to cut the sides and back short with clippers, leaving the hair on top longer. The length on top will depend on your personal preference, but it's typically around 2-3 inches.

Once you have the right cut, you'll need to style your hair. Start by applying a small amount of hair product to your fingertips. Gel, wax, and pomade all work well for this style. Rub the product between your fingers to warm it up, then apply it to your hair, starting at the roots.

Use your fingertips to spike the hair up and outwards, creating short, spiky pieces. You can style your hair straight up, or push it to one side for a more relaxed look.

Step by Step

1. Start with the right cut - short on the sides and back, longer on top.

2. Apply a small amount of hair product to your fingertips.

3. Rub the product between your fingers to warm it up.

4. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the roots.

5. Use your fingertips to spike the hair up and outwards.

6. Style your hair straight up or to one side.

Tips

- Use a small amount of product to start with, you can always add more if needed.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

- Keep your hair trimmed regularly to maintain the look.

FAQ

Q: Is mens short spiky hair easy to style?

A: Yes, it's one of the easiest hairstyles to style. All you need is a bit of product and your fingertips.

Q: What length should the hair be on top?

A: It depends on your personal preference, but typically around 2-3 inches.

Q: Can I style mens short spiky hair in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your hair straight up or to one side for a more relaxed look.

Hair Care

To keep your mens short spiky hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy. And, make sure to visit your barber regularly to keep your hair trimmed and looking sharp.

With this guide, you're ready to rock mens short spiky hair with ease. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance, stylish haircut or just want to switch up your look, this style is perfect for any man.
