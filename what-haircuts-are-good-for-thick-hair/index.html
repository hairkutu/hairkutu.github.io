What Haircuts Are Good For Thick Hair

Written by Robby Jun 22, 2023 · 8 min read

Thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it provides volume and fullness, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Choosing the right haircut can make all the difference, and in this article, we’ll explore what haircuts are good for thick hair.

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick hair is characterized by a high volume of hair per square inch. It can be coarse or fine, and it can be straight or curly. When choosing a haircut for thick hair, it’s important to consider the texture and volume of the hair, as well as the shape of the face.

How to

Choosing the right haircut for thick hair can be a daunting task. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

1. Consider your face shape

The shape of your face plays a big role in determining the best haircut for you. If you have a round face, for example, a long, layered haircut can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a choppy, layered bob can soften your features.

2. Think about the texture of your hair

The texture of your hair can also influence the type of haircut that will be most flattering. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob can add fullness and volume. If you have coarse hair, a layered cut can help to thin it out and make it more manageable.

3. Consider your lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in choosing the right haircut. If you have a busy schedule and don’t have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance cut like a pixie or a bob can be a good option. If you have more time to spend on your hair, a longer, layered cut can be a great way to show off your thick hair.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a haircut for thick hair:

1. Find inspiration

Look through magazines or online to find hairstyles that you like. Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing styles to try.

2. Consult with your stylist

Bring pictures of the hairstyles you like to your stylist and ask for their advice. They can help you choose a style that will work well with your hair and face shape.

3. Consider the length

Decide how long you want your hair to be. A shorter cut can be low-maintenance and easy to style, while a longer cut can be more versatile.

4. Choose the right layers

Layers can help to thin out thick hair and make it more manageable. Ask your stylist to help you choose the right type of layers for your hair texture and face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for thick hair:

1. Use the right products

Using the right products can help to keep thick hair looking its best. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners.

2. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help to keep thick hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

3. Experiment with styling

Thick hair can be styled in many different ways, so don’t be afraid to experiment. Try different updos, braids, and curls to find a style that works for you.

FAQ

What are some good haircuts for thick, curly hair?

Layered cuts can work well for thick, curly hair. A long, layered cut can help to weigh down curls and prevent frizz, while a shorter cut can add volume and bounce.

What are some good haircuts for thick, straight hair?

A blunt bob can work well for thick, straight hair. It can help to add fullness and volume, while also providing a sleek and stylish look.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping thick hair looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Condition regularly

Thick hair can be prone to dryness, so it’s important to condition regularly. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep hair soft and nourished.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage thick hair, so it’s important to avoid excessive use of flat irons and curling irons. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray first.

3. Protect from the sun

The sun’s rays can damage hair, so it’s important to protect your hair from the sun. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to prevent damage.

Choosing the right haircut for thick hair can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you can find a style that works for you. Remember to consult with your stylist and consider your face shape and hair texture when making your decision. With the right cut and proper hair care, you can keep your thick hair looking healthy and beautiful.
