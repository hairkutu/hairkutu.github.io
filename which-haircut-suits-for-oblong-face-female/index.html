Description

Oblong face is characterized by a longer face length and a narrow width. If you have an oblong face, choosing the right haircut is essential to balance out the length and create an illusion of a wider face. In this article, we will give you tips and tricks on which haircut suits for oblong face female and how to style it to enhance your natural features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the right haircut for your oblong face, you need to determine your face shape first. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face.
  3. Use the measurements to determine your face shape. Oblong face has a longer face length than width and a prominent chin.

Step by Step Guide on Choosing the Best Haircut for Oblong Face Female

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right haircut for your oblong face. Here's a step by step guide:

Step 1: Consider a Layered Haircut

A layered haircut can add volume to your hair and create an illusion of a wider face. Opt for soft layers that start from your chin downwards.

Step 2: Choose a Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help shorten the length of your face and create balance. Avoid blunt bangs that can make your face look longer.

Step 3: Opt for Soft Waves or Curls

Soft waves or curls can add width to your face and soften your facial features. Avoid straight and flat hairstyles that can elongate your face.

Step 4: Avoid Center Parting

Avoid center parting that can make your face look longer. Instead, opt for a side part that can create an asymmetrical balance.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling your hair if you have an oblong face:

  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly as it can elongate your face. Opt for loose updos instead.
  • Use volumizing products to add volume to your hair.
  • Accessorize with hair clips or headbands to create volume and add width to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short as they can elongate your face. Opt for medium to long hairstyles instead.

FAQs

What is the Best Haircut for Oblong Face Female?

The best haircut for oblong face female is a layered haircut with soft layers that start from your chin downwards. Side-swept bangs and soft waves or curls can also help balance out the length of your face.

Can I Wear My Hair in a Ponytail if I Have an Oblong Face?

Avoid pulling your hair back tightly as it can elongate your face. Opt for loose updos instead.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face Female

Here are some hair care tips for oblong face female:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. Opt for air-drying your hair or using a diffuser instead.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing the right haircut for your oblong face can enhance your natural features and create balance. Follow our tips and tricks and experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.
