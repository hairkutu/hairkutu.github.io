How To Dry Your Hair Curly With A Blow Dryer

Written by Petter May 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it. Blow drying your hair curly can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve beautiful curls that last all day. In this article, we will discuss how to dry your hair curly with a blow dryer, step by step.

Description

Drying your curly hair with a blow dryer can help you achieve defined curls and volume. However, it is important to use the right techniques to avoid damaging your hair. Using a diffuser attachment and low heat settings can help you achieve the best results.

How to

Follow these steps to dry your hair curly with a blow dryer:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Begin by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair soft and hydrated during the drying process.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel. This will help to define your curls and make them easier to style.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and make it easier to style your curls.

Step 4: Attach the Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer. A diffuser helps to distribute the heat evenly and prevent frizz.

Step 5: Dry Your Hair

Begin drying your hair on a low heat setting. Hold the diffuser close to your roots and gently scrunch your hair upwards. Continue drying your hair in sections until it is completely dry.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results:

Tip 1: Use Low Heat

Using high heat can damage your hair and cause frizz. Use a low heat setting and take your time to dry your hair.

Tip 2: Use a Microfiber Towel

Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair before blow drying. This will help to prevent frizz and breakage.

Tip 3: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft. This will help to prevent frizz and make it easier to style your curls.

FAQ

Can I Blow Dry My Hair Every Day?

It is not recommended to blow dry your hair every day, as this can cause damage and dryness. Try to limit your blow drying to 2-3 times per week.

Can I Use a Regular Blow Dryer on Curly Hair?

Yes, you can use a regular blow dryer on curly hair. However, using a diffuser attachment can help to achieve better results.

How Do I Prevent Frizz When Blow Drying My Hair Curly?

To prevent frizz, use a diffuser attachment, low heat settings, and avoid touching your hair too much while drying it.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair healthy and hydrated, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and deep condition your hair regularly. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant when you do.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls with a blow dryer. Remember to take your time and be gentle with your hair to avoid damage and breakage.
