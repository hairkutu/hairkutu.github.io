How To Keep Thick Hair Healthy Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 05, 2023 · 7 min read

Thick hair is a blessing and a curse all at once. While it looks gorgeous and voluminous, it can be hard to manage and maintain. Many people struggle with keeping their thick hair healthy, as it tends to be more prone to breakage and damage. However, with a few simple tips and tricks, you can keep your thick hair looking and feeling healthy. Here’s how:

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Keep Thick Hair Healthy
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Thick Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Products for Thick Hair

How to Keep Thick Hair Healthy

The first step to keeping your thick hair healthy is to make sure you are taking care of it properly. This means using the right products, avoiding heat damage, and keeping it moisturized. Here are some tips on how to keep your hair healthy:

1. Use the Right Products

When it comes to thick hair, using the right products is key. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for thick hair, as they will help to keep your hair moisturized and nourished. You should also consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

2. Avoid Heat Damage

Heat damage is one of the biggest culprits when it comes to damaged hair, so it’s important to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum to help minimize damage. You should also try to air dry your hair whenever possible.

3. Keep it Moisturized

Keeping your hair moisturized is crucial for maintaining its health and preventing breakage. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized, and consider using a hair mask or oil treatment for extra hydration.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to keeping your thick hair healthy:

1. Wash your hair with a shampoo designed for thick hair

Look for a shampoo that is designed specifically for thick hair, as it will be formulated to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

2. Condition your hair

Use a conditioner designed for thick hair, and leave it on for a few minutes to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

3. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil

Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

4. Air dry your hair whenever possible

Avoid using heat styling tools whenever possible, and try to air dry your hair instead.

5. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your thick hair:

1. Brush your hair gently

When brushing your hair, be gentle to avoid breaking or damaging it. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush designed for thick hair.

2. Avoid tight hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, as they can cause breakage and damage.

3. Get regular trims

Get regular trims to help prevent split ends and breakage.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to help protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my thick hair?

A: You should wash your hair every two to three days, as washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.

Q: What kind of brush should I use for my thick hair?

A: Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush designed for thick hair to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Can I use hair oil on my thick hair?

A: Yes, using hair oil can help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Hair Care Products for Thick Hair

Here are some hair care products that are great for thick hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair
  • Leave-in conditioner or hair oil
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • UV protectant spray

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your thick hair healthy and looking beautiful.
