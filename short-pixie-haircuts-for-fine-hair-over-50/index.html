Short Pixie Haircuts For Fine Hair Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Apr 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a woman over 50 with fine hair, you may be wondering how to update your hairstyle. The good news is that short pixie haircuts are a great option for fine hair because they add volume and texture. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the right pixie cut for fine hair, how to style it, and how to take care of your hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Pixie Haircut for Fine Hair Over 50

When choosing a short pixie haircut for fine hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to opt for a longer pixie cut with side-swept bangs to elongate your face. If you have a square face, a short, textured pixie cut can soften your features.

For fine hair, it's best to choose a pixie cut that adds volume and texture. This can be achieved with layers, choppy ends, and asymmetrical cuts. A pixie cut that is too blunt can make fine hair look limp and lifeless.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Pixie Haircuts for Fine Hair Over 50

Styling a short pixie haircut for fine hair is relatively easy. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, washed hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture.
  5. Use a small amount of styling wax or pomade to define the ends and add texture.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling products for fine hair. Too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Pixie Haircut for Fine Hair Over 50

Here are some tips for maintaining your short pixie haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Frequently Asked Questions about Short Pixie Haircuts for Fine Hair Over 50

Here are some frequently asked questions about short pixie haircuts for fine hair over 50:

Q: Will a short pixie haircut make me look older?

A: No, a short pixie haircut can actually make you look younger and more modern. It's all about choosing the right cut for your face shape and hair texture.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a short pixie haircut?

A: Depending on the length of your pixie cut, you may be able to wear your hair in a small ponytail or a half-up style. Talk to your hairstylist about the best styling options for your haircut.

Q: How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your pixie cut looking fresh.

Final Thoughts

Short pixie haircuts are a great option for women over 50 with fine hair. By choosing the right cut for your face shape and hair texture, and by following these styling and maintenance tips, you can have a chic and modern hairstyle that makes you look and feel your best.
