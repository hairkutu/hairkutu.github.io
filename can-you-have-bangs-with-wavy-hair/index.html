Can You Have Bangs With Wavy Hair Tips Tricks And Care

Written by Eveline Aug 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have wavy hair, you might be wondering if you can pull off bangs. The answer is yes, absolutely! Bangs can look great with wavy hair, and there are many different styles to choose from. In this post, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling bangs with wavy hair, as well as how to care for your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Bangs with Wavy Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Bangs with Wavy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair with Bangs

How to Style Bangs with Wavy Hair

When it comes to styling bangs with wavy hair, there are a few different approaches you can take. Here are some tips:

1. Embrace Your Natural Texture

One of the best things about wavy hair is its natural texture. Instead of fighting it, embrace it! Your wavy hair will give your bangs a beautiful, tousled look that works great for a casual, beachy vibe.

2. Use a Curling Iron

If you want to add a bit more definition to your waves, you can use a small curling iron or wand to curl your bangs. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray first, and don't curl them too tightly.

3. Experiment with Different Lengths

There's no one-size-fits-all when it comes to bangs, so feel free to experiment with different lengths. Some people with wavy hair prefer shorter bangs that sit just above the eyebrows, while others like longer, side-swept bangs that blend in with the rest of their hair.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs with Wavy Hair

Once you have your bangs styled just the way you want them, you'll want to keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

1. Trim Them Regularly

Bangs can grow out quickly, so it's important to trim them regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy. You can either do this yourself with a pair of hair cutting scissors, or you can visit a salon for a professional trim.

2. Use a Dry Shampoo

Wavy hair can be prone to oiliness, which can make your bangs look greasy. To avoid this, use a dry shampoo between washes to absorb excess oil.

3. Invest in a Good Hairspray

A good hairspray can help keep your bangs in place, even in windy or humid weather. Look for a lightweight, flexible hold hairspray that won't weigh down your waves.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs with wavy hair:

Can you have bangs with curly hair?

Yes, you can! Bangs can add a playful, youthful look to curly hair. However, keep in mind that curly hair can be more difficult to style, so you may need to spend more time on your hair each day to keep your bangs looking great.

What kind of bangs look best with wavy hair?

There are many different types of bangs that look great with wavy hair, including side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and choppy, textured bangs.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair with Bangs

Finally, here are some tips for keeping your wavy hair healthy and looking its best:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Wavy hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day, and use a dry shampoo in between washes if needed.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When you're combing out your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This will help minimize breakage and frizz, and will help your waves keep their shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can definitely pull off bangs with wavy hair. Just remember to take good care of your hair, and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you!
