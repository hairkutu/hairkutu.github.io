Updos For Shoulder Length Hair With Fringe Tips Tricks And Ideas

Written by Aprilia Apr 28, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have shoulder-length hair with fringe, you may think that your styling options are limited. However, that’s not the case at all! With a little creativity, you can create some stunning updos that are perfect for any occasion. From braids to buns, there are plenty of styles to choose from. In this article, we’ll share some tips, tricks, and ideas for updos for shoulder-length hair with fringe.

Description

A shoulder-length haircut is a stylish and versatile option that works well with all hair types. You can add a fringe or bangs to your shoulder-length hair to give it a new look. However, styling your hair in an updo can be a bit challenging. You may think that your hair is too short to create an updo, but that’s not true. There are many updo options that are perfect for shoulder-length hair with fringe.

How to Style Updos for Shoulder Length Hair with Fringe

Styling an updo for shoulder-length hair with fringe requires some creativity and patience. Here’s how you can create an updo:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before styling your hair, it’s important to wash and dry it. Apply a styling product that will help hold your hair in place. You can use a texturizing spray, mousse, or hairspray.

Step 2: Create the updo

There are many updo options that are perfect for shoulder-length hair with fringe. Here are some of our favorites:

Braided Updo

To create a braided updo, start by dividing your hair into three sections. Create a braid with each section and secure it with an elastic band. Wrap each braid around the back of your head and secure it with bobby pins.

Messy Bun

To create a messy bun, gather your hair into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

Twisted Updo

To create a twisted updo, divide your hair into two sections. Twist each section and wrap it around the back of your head. Secure each section with bobby pins.

Tips for Updos for Shoulder Length Hair with Fringe

Here are some tips that will help you create the perfect updo for your shoulder-length hair with fringe:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to keep your updo in place without being noticeable.
  • Use a hairspray or texturizing spray to add volume and hold to your updo.
  • Experiment with different updo styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I create an updo with shoulder-length hair and a side fringe?

A: Yes, you can create an updo with shoulder-length hair and a side fringe. Try a braided updo or a twisted updo.

Q: How can I make my updo last all day?

A: Use a hairspray or texturizing spray to add hold to your updo. You can also use bobby pins to keep your updo in place.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair with Fringe

To keep your shoulder-length hair with fringe healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a stunning updo for your shoulder-length hair with fringe. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
