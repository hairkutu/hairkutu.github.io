How To Tell If You Would Look Good With A Pixie Cut A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy May 31, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're considering a pixie cut, you're not alone. This short, chic hairstyle has been popular for decades, and it's still going strong in 2023. But how do you know if a pixie cut is right for you? Here's a guide to help you decide.

Table of Contents:

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Texture
  • Assessing Your Personal Style
  • Highlighting Your Facial Features
  • Taking Your Facial Proportions into Account
  • Feeling Confident and Ready for a Change
  • Pixie Cut Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs About Pixie Cuts

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding if a pixie cut is right for you is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a pixie cut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

There are several face shapes, including:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

Each face shape has its own unique characteristics, and certain pixie cuts may look better on some shapes than others.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, use the following guidelines:

  • Round face: equal width and length, soft features
  • Oval face: slightly longer than wide, balanced features
  • Square face: equal width and length, sharp features
  • Heart face: wider forehead, narrow chin, pointed chin
  • Diamond face: narrow forehead and chin, wide cheekbones

Once you know your face shape, you can start exploring pixie cuts that are best suited for you.

Considering Your Hair Texture

Another factor to consider when deciding if a pixie cut is right for you is your hair texture. Pixie cuts work best on hair that is fine or medium in texture, and that has some natural wave or curl. If your hair is very thick or curly, a pixie cut may be difficult to style and maintain.

However, if you have fine or medium hair with some natural wave or curl, a pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair, and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Assessing Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when deciding if a pixie cut is right for you. Pixie cuts are bold, edgy, and attention-grabbing, so they're best suited for women who are confident and comfortable standing out.

If you prefer a more understated or classic look, a pixie cut may not be the best choice for you. However, if you love experimenting with your style and trying new things, a pixie cut can be a fun and exciting change.

Highlighting Your Facial Features

One of the benefits of a pixie cut is that it can highlight your facial features and draw attention to your eyes, cheekbones, and lips. If you have strong, defined features that you want to show off, a pixie cut can be a great choice.

However, if you have features that you prefer to downplay, such as a prominent nose or chin, a longer hairstyle may be more flattering.

Taking Your Facial Proportions into Account

In addition to your face shape and features, you'll want to take your facial proportions into account when deciding if a pixie cut is right for you. This means considering the size and shape of your forehead, nose, chin, and jawline, and choosing a pixie cut that balances these features.

If you have a small forehead or chin, for example, a pixie cut with short bangs may not be the best choice, as it can make these features appear even smaller.

Feeling Confident and Ready for a Change

Ultimately, the most important factor in deciding if a pixie cut is right for you is how you feel about it. If you're excited about the idea of a short, stylish haircut and feel confident and ready for a change, a pixie cut can be a great choice.

On the other hand, if you're hesitant or unsure about the idea of a pixie cut, it may be best to stick with a longer hairstyle for now.

Pixie Cut Hair Care Tips

If you do decide to go for a pixie cut, there are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as pomade or wax, to create different looks
  • Consider investing in a good quality flat iron or curling iron to style your hair

FAQs About Pixie Cuts

Q: Will a pixie cut work with curly hair?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can work with curly hair, but it's important to choose a cut that works with your natural curl pattern and texture. Ask your stylist for recommendations.

Q: Will a pixie cut work with glasses?

A: Absolutely! A pixie cut can be a great way to show off your glasses and draw attention to your eyes.

Q: How often will I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: You'll typically need to get your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your cut.

Q: How do I style a pixie cut?

A: There are many ways to style a pixie cut, from slicking it back with pomade to adding volume with a volumizing mousse. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Now that you know how to tell if you would look good with a pixie cut, it's time to make your decision. Remember, a pixie cut can be a bold and exciting change, but it's important to choose a cut that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Good luck!
