How To Cut Men s Wavy Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 09, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're looking to save some money and time by cutting your own hair at home, you're not alone. Many men have taken matters into their own hands and started cutting their own hair, especially during the pandemic. However, cutting wavy hair can be tricky, as it requires a different approach than straight or curly hair. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting men's wavy hair at home, from start to finish.

How to Cut Men's Wavy Hair at Home

Before you start cutting your hair at home, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you have longer hair, you may also need hair clippers. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any products. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Start with the sides. Comb the hair straight down and cut it to your desired length. Work your way up to the top, cutting small sections at a time. Make sure both sides are even.

Step 5: Cut the Back

Next, move on to the back. Comb the hair straight down and cut it to your desired length. Use a hand mirror to check the back of your head to ensure it's even.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top. Comb the hair straight up and cut it to your desired length. Work your way from the front to the back, cutting small sections at a time. Make sure the top is even with the sides and the back.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to cutting men's wavy hair at home:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Make sure you have a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you have longer hair, you may also need hair clippers.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Step 6: Cut the Back

Step 7: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top. Comb the hair straight up and cut it to your desired length. Work your way from the front to the back, cutting small sections at a time. Make sure the top is even with the sides and the back.

Step 8: Blend the Layers

Use the scissors to blend the layers together. Cut the hair at an angle to create a natural, layered look.

Step 9: Trim the Ends

Use the scissors to trim the ends of your hair. This will help give your hair a neater, more polished look.

Step 10: Clean Up

Brush your hair and clean up any stray hairs. Rinse your hair with cold water to seal the cuticles, and style it as desired.

Tips for Cutting Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you cut men's wavy hair at home:

  • Start with dry hair, as wet hair can stretch and look longer than it actually is.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean cuts.
  • Go slow and cut small sections at a time.
  • Use a comb to pull the hair taut before cutting.
  • Cut the hair at an angle to create a natural, textured look.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're having trouble.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your own hair at home. However, it's important to have the right tools and follow proper techniques to avoid any mishaps.

Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

It's best to cut your hair dry, as wet hair can stretch and look longer than it actually is. This can result in uneven cuts.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. However, most people should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their desired length and style.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Now that you know how to cut men's wavy hair at home, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your wavy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length and style.

By following these tips, you can keep your wavy hair looking healthy, shiny, and stylish.
