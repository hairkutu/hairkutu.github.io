How To Wave Short Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Waving short natural hair is not only stylish but also a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. It can be done using various methods, from using heat tools to setting your hair overnight. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to wave short natural hair, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect waves.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wave Short Natural Hair
  2. Tips for Waving Short Natural Hair
  3. FAQs about Waving Short Natural Hair
  4. Hair Care Tips for Waved Short Natural Hair

How to Wave Short Natural Hair

Before we dive into the steps, here are some things you will need:

  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron or flat iron
  • Clips or hair ties
  • Hairspray

Now, let's get started:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier for you to work on each section.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or flat iron. Hold it for a few seconds, then release.
  4. Repeat the process on the rest of your hair, alternating the direction of the waves for a more natural look.
  5. Once you've waved all your hair, use your fingers to loosen the waves and create volume.
  6. Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Congratulations, you've successfully waved your short natural hair!

How to Wave Short Natural Hair Overnight

If you prefer not to use heat tools, you can wave your short natural hair overnight using the following steps:

  1. Start with clean and damp hair. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or curl enhancing cream, to your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.
  3. Take one section and twist it tightly, then wrap it around itself to create a small bun. Secure the bun with a hair tie or clip.
  4. Repeat the process on the rest of your hair.
  5. Leave your hair wrapped overnight.
  6. In the morning, remove the clips or hair ties and gently loosen the waves with your fingers.
  7. Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Waving Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect waves:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage to your hair.
  • Don't hold the curling iron or flat iron on your hair for too long as this can cause damage.
  • Alternate the direction of the waves for a more natural look.
  • Loosen the waves with your fingers for a more voluminous look.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to hold the waves in place without making your hair stiff.

FAQs about Waving Short Natural Hair

Can I wave my short natural hair without heat?

Yes, you can wave your short natural hair without heat by setting your hair overnight using the bun method.

How long will the waves last?

The longevity of the waves depends on the method used and how well you maintain them. Heat-styled waves can last up to 2-3 days, while overnight waves can last up to a week with proper maintenance.

Can I wave my short natural hair if it's relaxed?

Yes, you can still wave your short natural hair if it's relaxed, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

Hair Care Tips for Waved Short Natural Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your waved short natural hair healthy:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using products with harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

With these tips and steps, you can easily wave your short natural hair and keep it healthy and beautiful. Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for your hair type and texture. Have fun with your new waves!
