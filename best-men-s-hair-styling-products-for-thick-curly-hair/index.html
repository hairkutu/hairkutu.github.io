Best Men s Hair Styling Products For Thick Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a lot of volume and texture to work with. On the other hand, it can be difficult to find the right hair styling products that work for your hair type. In this blog post, we will be discussing the best men's hair styling products for thick curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Thick Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Thick Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Description

Thick curly hair requires hair styling products that can provide hold, definition, and moisture. Some of the best men's hair styling products for thick curly hair are:

1. Curl Creams

Curl creams are great for defining curls and reducing frizz. They provide hold without making your hair feel crunchy or stiff. Look for curl creams that are lightweight and contain natural ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil.

2. Hair Gels

Hair gels are perfect for creating sleek and defined hairstyles. They provide strong hold and help to tame frizz. Look for hair gels that are alcohol-free and contain natural ingredients like aloe vera and jojoba oil.

3. Pomades

Pomades are great for creating classic and sophisticated hairstyles. They provide medium hold and a natural shine. Look for pomades that are water-based and contain natural ingredients like beeswax and castor oil.

How to Style Thick Curly Hair

Styling thick curly hair can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips on how to style your hair:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

2. Apply Hair Styling Product

Apply your chosen hair styling product to damp hair. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to reduce frizz and define your curls.

4. Finish with Hair Spray

Finish your style with a light hold hair spray to keep your curls in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style your thick curly hair:

1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp.

3. Apply your chosen hair styling product to your hair.

4. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

5. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

6. Finish your style with a light hold hair spray.

Tips for Styling Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your thick curly hair:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Aim to wash your hair no more than twice a week.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent damage and breakage.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. These can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

FAQs

1. Can I use hair styling products on dry hair?

Yes, you can use hair styling products on dry hair. However, it is recommended to use them on damp hair for better distribution and hold.

2. How do I prevent frizz in my hair?

To prevent frizz, use hair styling products that contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter and coconut oil. Also, avoid touching your hair too much and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick curly hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

2. Deep condition your hair once a week.

3. Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

4. Avoid using heat styling tools.

5. Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Overall, styling thick curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right hair styling products and hair care routine, you can achieve the perfect style for your hair type.
