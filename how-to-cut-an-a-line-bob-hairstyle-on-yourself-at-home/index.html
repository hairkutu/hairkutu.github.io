How To Cut An A Line Bob Hairstyle On Yourself At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of long hair and looking for a fresh and modern change? The A-line bob haircut is a classic style that can be easily achieved at home. Not only is it trendy and chic, but it's also low maintenance and perfect for any face shape. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut an A-line bob hairstyle on yourself at home.

Table of Contents:

  • Materials Needed
  • Preparation
  • Sectioning Your Hair
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Blow Drying and Styling
  • Tips for a Perfect A-Line Bob
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your A-Line Bob

Materials Needed:

Before starting, gather the following materials:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Hairdryer

Preparation:

Start by washing and conditioning your hair, and let it air dry. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting. Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.

Sectioning Your Hair:

Section your hair by creating a middle part from the forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, divide your hair into two sections, left and right. Clip the left section to keep it out of the way. Take the right section and divide it again into three parts: the crown, middle, and nape. Clip the crown and middle sections up, leaving the nape section loose.

Cutting Your Hair:

Starting with the nape section, comb the hair and hold it straight down with your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the natural hairline. Repeat this step for the middle and crown sections, making sure to keep the hair straight and level.

Next, it's time to create the A-line shape. Take the middle section and comb it straight down. Use your fingers to create a diagonal line from the middle of your head to the bottom of your ear. Cut along this line, keeping the hair straight and level. Repeat the same process for the crown section, making sure to match the angle of the middle section.

Blow Drying and Styling:

Once you've finished cutting, blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Style your A-line bob as desired, and you're ready to show off your new look!

Tips for a Perfect A-Line Bob:

  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid split ends.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, ask a friend or family member for help.

FAQs:

Q: Can I cut an A-line bob on curly or wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, keep in mind that the A-line shape will be more noticeable on straight hair.

Q: How often should I trim my A-line bob?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Your A-Line Bob:

To keep your A-line bob looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Congratulations, you've successfully cut your own A-line bob hairstyle at home! With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking cut without leaving your house.
