Does Layer Cut Suit Oval Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Sep 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face, you are lucky because almost any hairstyle suits you. But when it comes to layer cut, it's important to know whether it would be the best fit for your face shape or not. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about whether layer cut suits an oval face or not. So, let's get started!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A layer cut is a hairstyle that involves cutting the hair into different lengths, creating layers. The purpose of layering is to add volume, texture, and movement to the hair. When it comes to an oval face, layer cut can be a great fit as it accentuates the cheekbones and jawline, making the face look more defined. Moreover, layer cut can also provide more volume and bounce to the hair, making it look fuller and healthier.

How to

If you are considering getting a layer cut and have an oval face, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Consult a professional stylist who can guide you on the best layer cut for your face shape.
  • Consider the length of the layers as it can make a significant difference in how the hair falls on your face.
  • Choose the right type of layer cut, such as long layers or choppy layers, depending on your hair type and texture.

Step by step

If you have already decided to get a layer cut, here are the steps you can follow:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry or use a blow dryer to dry it completely.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  3. Start cutting the hair at the back, starting from the nape of the neck.
  4. Gradually move towards the front, cutting the hair in layers.
  5. Once you have finished cutting the layers, style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you make the most of your layer cut:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add more volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as beachy waves or a messy bun, to see what suits you best.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of the layers.

FAQ

Q: Can layer cut make my face look longer?

A: No, layer cut can actually make your face look more defined and balanced. It can add volume to the hair, making it look fuller and healthier.

Q: Can I get a layer cut if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can get a layer cut even if you have thin hair. In fact, layer cut can add more texture and volume to thin hair, making it look fuller.

Q: How often should I get a layer cut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the layers. Generally, it is recommended to get a layer cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the layers.

Hair Care

To keep your layer cut looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.

So, there you have it! Layer cut can be a great fit for an oval face, adding volume and definition to the hair. By following the tips and getting regular trims, you can maintain the shape and length of the layers and keep your hair looking its best. Consult a professional stylist to find the best layer cut for your face shape and hair type, and experiment with different hairstyles to see what suits you best.
