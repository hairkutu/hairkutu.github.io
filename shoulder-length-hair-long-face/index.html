Shoulder Length Hair Long Face - A Guide for 2023

If you have a long face and you're looking for the perfect hairstyle, shoulder length hair might be the perfect fit for you. In this article, we will guide you through how to choose and style shoulder length hair for a long face, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile hairstyle that suits many face shapes, including long faces. It falls just above or below the shoulders and can be styled in various ways to suit your personal style.

If you have a long face, shoulder length hair can help balance out your facial features and create a more proportionate look. It also adds volume and texture to your hair, making it appear fuller and healthier.

How to Choose Shoulder Length Hair for a Long Face

When choosing shoulder length hair for a long face, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, you want to avoid hairstyles that make your face appear longer. This means avoiding styles that are too long or too short.

Instead, opt for a shoulder length hairstyle that falls just above or below your shoulders. This will help create a more balanced look and draw attention away from your face's length.

Another thing to consider is the shape of your face. If you have a long face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face. This can be achieved through layers, waves, or curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair for a Long Face

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling shoulder length hair for a long face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.
  6. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair for a Long Face

To keep your shoulder length hair looking its best, here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

What face shape suits shoulder length hair?

Shoulder length hair suits many face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces. It's especially suitable for long faces, as it helps balance out facial features and creates a more proportionate look.

How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry or curly hair, you can wash it every 2-3 days. If you exercise or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Can I style shoulder length hair without heat?

Absolutely! You can style shoulder length hair without heat by using braids, twists, or buns. These styles create waves or curls without the need for heat styling tools. You can also use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair for a Long Face

When it comes to hair care, there are a few things to keep in mind for shoulder length hair for a long face. Firstly, use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and scalp. If you have oily hair, choose a clarifying shampoo to remove excess oil. If you have dry hair, choose a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to hydrate your hair.

Secondly, avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you need to use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Thirdly, get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Lastly, protect your hair from the sun, chlorine, and pollution. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from the sun's UV rays. Rinse your hair with clean water after swimming in a pool or the ocean to remove chlorine and salt. And wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and pollution.

Shoulder length hair is a versatile and flattering hairstyle for a long face. By choosing the right length and style, and following the right hair care tips, you can create a beautiful and healthy hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style.
