How To Know Your Face Shape A Guide To Picking The Perfect Hairstyle

Written by Oliver Jun 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Knowing your face shape can help you choose the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. In this article, we’ll show you how to identify your face shape and give you tips on how to pick a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Know Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

How to Know Your Face Shape

There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, you’ll need to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Here’s how to do it:

Oval Face Shape

If your face is longer than it is wide and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have an oval face shape.

Round Face Shape

If your face is round and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a round face shape.

Square Face Shape

If your face is square and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a square face shape.

Heart Face Shape

If your face is wider at the temples and tapers down to a narrow chin, you have a heart face shape.

Diamond Face Shape

If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline, you have a diamond face shape.

Oblong Face Shape

If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have an oblong face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, it’s time to pick a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here’s how:

  1. Identify your face shape using the guide above.
  2. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair type and texture.
  4. Talk to your hairstylist about the best hairstyle for your face shape.
  5. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle that flatters your face shape:

  • Choose a hairstyle that balances out your features.
  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your problem areas.
  • Consider your hair type and texture.
  • Experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Can I have more than one face shape?

It’s possible to have a combination of two face shapes. For example, you might have a heart-shaped face with a square jawline.

Can I wear any hairstyle I want?

While you can wear any hairstyle you want, certain styles will flatter your face shape more than others.

Do haircuts have to be symmetrical?

No, haircuts don’t have to be symmetrical. Your hairstylist can create a cut that flatters your face shape, even if it’s asymmetrical.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips for your face shape:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hair care products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, no matter what your face shape.
