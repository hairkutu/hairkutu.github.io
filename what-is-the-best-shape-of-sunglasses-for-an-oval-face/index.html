What Is The Best Shape Of Sunglasses For An Oval Face

Written by Bardi Jun 27, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you wondering what the best shape of sunglasses is for an oval face? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we will discuss the various shapes of sunglasses that compliment oval faces, how to determine your face shape, and some tips and tricks for taking care of your sunglasses.

Table of Contents

Determining Your Face Shape

Before we get into the best shapes of sunglasses for oval faces, it’s important to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Pay attention to the widest part of your face and the length of your face. If your face is longer than it is wide and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have an oval face shape.

Best Shapes of Sunglasses for Oval Faces

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck! This face shape can pull off almost any shape of sunglasses. However, there are a few shapes that particularly compliment oval faces:

  • Round sunglasses
  • Aviator sunglasses
  • Wayfarer sunglasses
  • Cat-eye sunglasses

These shapes of sunglasses all have a balance of round and sharp features, which compliment the oval face shape. Additionally, oversized sunglasses can look great on oval faces, as they add a touch of glamour to any outfit.

How to Choose the Right Sunglasses for Your Face

When choosing sunglasses, it’s important to take into consideration not only your face shape, but also your skin tone and hair color. Neutral skin tones look best with warm colors like gold and tortoise, while cool skin tones look best with silver and black. Additionally, if you have light hair, go for darker frames, and if you have dark hair, go for lighter frames.

Another tip for choosing the right sunglasses for your face is to try on different shapes and sizes to see what looks best on you. Don’t be afraid to step out of your comfort zone and try a new style!

Tips for Taking Care of Your Sunglasses

To make your sunglasses last longer, it’s important to take care of them properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Store your sunglasses in a case when not in use
  • Clean your sunglasses regularly with a microfiber cloth
  • Avoid leaving your sunglasses in hot cars or direct sunlight

By following these tips, you can ensure that your sunglasses will last for many summers to come.

FAQ About Sunglasses for Oval Faces

1. Can oval faces wear square sunglasses?

Yes, oval faces can wear square sunglasses. However, it’s important to make sure that the square frames have rounded edges to compliment the oval face shape.

2. Do oval faces look good in oversized sunglasses?

Yes, oversized sunglasses can look great on oval faces, as they add a touch of glamour to any outfit.

3. Can oval faces wear cat-eye sunglasses?

Yes, oval faces can wear cat-eye sunglasses. The sharp features of the cat-eye frame compliment the round features of the oval face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Sunglasses Wearers

If you wear sunglasses regularly, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid wearing tight headbands or hair ties that can cause breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or hair products that contain SPF
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great while wearing sunglasses.

Now that you know what the best shape of sunglasses is for an oval face, it’s time to go out and find the perfect pair for you. Remember to take into consideration your face shape, skin tone, and hair color, and to take care of your sunglasses properly to make them last. Happy shopping!
