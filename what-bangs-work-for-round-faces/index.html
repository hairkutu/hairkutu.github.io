What Bangs Work For Round Faces A Guide In 2023

Written by Daniel Apr 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what bangs work best for your face shape. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some personality to your hairstyle. In this guide, we'll go over what types of bangs work best for round faces and some tips for maintaining them.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose Bangs for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Getting Bangs
  • Tips for Maintaining Bangs
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Bangs

How to Choose Bangs for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing bangs for round faces, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid bangs that are too short or too straight across. These can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for bangs that are longer and angled, which will help elongate your face.

Another important consideration is the thickness of your bangs. If you have thick hair, you may be able to pull off thicker bangs. If your hair is thin, however, you'll want to opt for thinner bangs that won't overpower your face.

Lastly, consider your styling preferences. Do you prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle or are you willing to spend time styling your hair each day? If you're looking for a low-maintenance option, consider side-swept bangs that can be easily styled with a little bit of product. If you don't mind spending time on your hair, you can opt for more intricate bang styles like curtain bangs or choppy bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide for Getting Bangs

Ready to take the plunge and get bangs? Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose the type of bangs you want based on your face shape and styling preferences.
  2. Find a reputable hair stylist who has experience cutting bangs.
  3. Bring in pictures of the type of bangs you want to show your stylist.
  4. Discuss the length and thickness of your bangs with your stylist.
  5. Let your stylist know if you prefer a blunt or textured look.
  6. Sit back and relax while your stylist cuts your bangs.
  7. After your bangs are cut, style them using the products and tools recommended by your stylist.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs

Now that you have your new bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to keep them clean and free of oil.
  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a styling product like hairspray or pomade to keep your bangs in place.
  • If your bangs start to look greasy, use dry shampoo to refresh them.
  • If you're growing out your bangs, use bobby pins or hair clips to keep them out of your face.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I get bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still get bangs if you have curly hair. However, you'll want to choose a type of bang that works well with your natural curl pattern. Side-swept bangs or longer, layered bangs can be a good option.

Q: Will bangs make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the type of bangs you choose. Bangs that are too short or straight across can make your face look rounder. However, longer, angled bangs can help elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Q: How often should I get my bangs trimmed?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. Generally, you'll want to get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care for Bangs

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep your bangs looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, especially on your bangs.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors that can damage it.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to choose the perfect bangs for your round face and keep them looking great. Whether you opt for side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, or choppy bangs, you can enjoy a fresh new look that flatters your face shape.
