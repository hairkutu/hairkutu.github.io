What Does A 60 Year Old Bride Wear Tips And Ideas

Written by Petter Aug 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a 60-year-old bride-to-be wondering what to wear on your big day? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll provide you with tips and ideas on what to wear as a mature bride. From dresses to accessories, we'll cover it all. Let's get started!

Description

Getting married at any age is a special occasion, and as a 60-year-old bride, you want to look and feel your best. You might be wondering what type of dress to wear or what accessories to choose. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming. But don't worry, we'll help you find the perfect outfit for your big day.

How to

When it comes to choosing your wedding dress, there are a few things to keep in mind as a mature bride. First, consider the style and silhouette that flatters your body. You want to choose a dress that accentuates your best features while still being comfortable to wear all day long. A-line dresses and empire waist gowns are both great options for mature brides.

Next, consider the color of your dress. While traditional white or ivory is always a classic choice, don't be afraid to step outside the box and choose a color that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Champagne, blush, and light blue are all popular choices for mature brides.

Accessorizing your outfit is also important. A simple pair of earrings and a necklace can add a touch of elegance to any dress. Consider wearing comfortable shoes, such as low-heel pumps or sandals, that match your dress and won't cause discomfort throughout the day.

Step by Step

1. Choose a dress that flatters your body type and is comfortable to wear all day long.

2. Consider the color of your dress and choose a shade that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

3. Accessorize your outfit with a simple pair of earrings and a necklace.

4. Choose comfortable shoes that match your dress and won't cause discomfort throughout the day.

Tips

  • Don't be afraid to step outside the box and choose a non-traditional color for your dress.
  • Choose comfortable shoes that you can wear all day long.
  • Consider adding a wrap or shawl to your outfit for added warmth and elegance.
  • Choose accessories that complement your dress without overpowering it.

FAQ

Q. Can I wear a traditional white or ivory wedding dress as a mature bride?

A. Yes, you can absolutely wear a traditional white or ivory wedding dress if that's what you feel most comfortable in. However, don't be afraid to explore other color options that might be more flattering for your skin tone.

Q. What type of shoes should I wear as a mature bride?

A. It's important to choose comfortable shoes that you can wear all day long. Low-heel pumps or sandals are both great options for mature brides.

Q. Can I wear a veil as a mature bride?

A. Yes, you can absolutely wear a veil if that's what you want. However, you might want to consider wearing a fascinator or a hair accessory instead for a more modern look.

Hair Care

On your big day, you want your hair to look its best. Consider getting a trim or a fresh cut a few weeks before the wedding to ensure your locks are healthy and shiny. If you're planning on wearing your hair up, consider getting a trial run with your hairstylist to ensure it stays in place throughout the day. Don't forget to add a touch of hairspray to keep your style in place!

As a 60-year-old bride, you want to feel confident and beautiful on your big day. By following these tips and ideas, you'll be sure to find the perfect outfit that makes you feel your best. Congratulations on your upcoming wedding!
