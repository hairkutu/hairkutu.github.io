Best Haircuts For Coarse Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Oliver Mar 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with your thick, coarse, and wavy hair? If so, you're not alone. Many people with this hair type struggle to find a haircut that works for them. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircuts for coarse thick wavy hair. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Coarse, thick, and wavy hair can be challenging to manage, but the right haircut can make all the difference. The best haircuts for this hair type are those that help manage the volume and texture while enhancing the natural waves. From long layers to shoulder-length bobs, there are many haircuts to choose from. In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircuts for coarse thick wavy hair that will leave you with a look you'll love.

How to

The first step in finding the best haircut for your coarse thick wavy hair is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and complements your hair type. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for inspiration from magazines, social media, and your favorite celebrities. Consider the length, layers, and texture of the haircut before making a final decision.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best haircuts for coarse thick wavy hair:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for those with coarse thick wavy hair. This haircut helps to manage the volume and texture while still enhancing the natural waves. Ask your stylist to add layers that start at your chin and gradually get longer towards the ends. This will give your hair movement and dimension.

Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is a classic haircut that works well for those with coarse thick wavy hair. This style helps to reduce the volume and add definition to the waves. Ask your stylist to cut your hair to shoulder length and add layers that start at your jawline. This will give your hair a soft, natural look.

Choppy Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a shorter style, a choppy pixie cut is a great option for those with coarse thick wavy hair. This style adds texture and movement to your hair while still managing the volume. Ask your stylist to create choppy layers throughout your hair and leave the top layers longer for a more textured look.

Tips

When it comes to managing your coarse thick wavy hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Air dry your hair whenever possible to enhance the natural waves.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for coarse thick wavy hair?

The best haircuts for this hair type are those that help manage the volume and texture while enhancing the natural waves. From long layers to shoulder-length bobs, there are many haircuts to choose from.

How do I manage my coarse thick wavy hair?

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, air dry your hair whenever possible, and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for managing coarse thick wavy hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, air dry your hair whenever possible, and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny. Additionally, get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. With the right hair care routine and a great haircut, you can manage your coarse thick wavy hair with ease.
