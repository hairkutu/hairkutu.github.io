How To Do Your Hair 40S Style

Written by Frank Jun 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to add a touch of vintage glamour to your look, why not try a 40s hairstyle? This era was all about soft curls, waves, and victory rolls, and it's a great way to add some elegance to any outfit. In this article, we'll show you how to do your hair 40s style in relaxed English language.

How to:

To start, you'll need some basic hair tools, including a curling iron, bobby pins, and hairspray. Here's how to get the look:

Step 1: Wash and Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help give your hair some lift and body, which is key for creating 40s-style curls. Once you're done washing, towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.

Step 2: Create Soft Curls

Use your curling iron to create soft, loose curls throughout your hair. Start by sectioning off your hair and curling each section away from your face. Once you've curled all of your hair, gently brush through the curls with a soft-bristled brush to create a more natural, relaxed look.

Step 3: Style Your Victory Rolls

Victory rolls were a popular hairstyle in the 40s, and they're a great way to add some vintage flair to your look. To create them, start by sectioning off a small section of hair at the front of your head. Roll this section of hair upwards and towards the back of your head, securing it in place with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side, and use hairspray to keep everything in place.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your 40s hairstyle in place, be sure to finish with a generous spritz of hairspray. This will help keep your curls and victory rolls intact throughout the day.

Tips:

- If you're short on time, try using hot rollers instead of a curling iron.

- For an authentic 40s look, try adding a hair accessory like a flower or a scarf.

- If you have bangs, try styling them to the side to create a more vintage look.

FAQ:

Q: Can I do this hairstyle on short hair?

A: Yes, you can still create a 40s-inspired look on short hair. Try adding curls or waves throughout your hair, and use bobby pins to create a faux victory roll at the front of your head.

Q: Will this hairstyle work on all hair types?

A: Yes, this look can work on all hair types. However, if you have particularly thick or coarse hair, you may need to use more product to keep your curls and victory rolls in place.

Hair Care:

- To keep your hair healthy and strong, be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair over time.

- If you're looking for a more permanent 40s-inspired look, consider getting a vintage-inspired haircut like a bob or a pixie cut.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a stunning 40s-inspired hairstyle in no time. Whether you're heading to a party or just want to add some vintage glamour to your everyday look, this style is sure to turn heads.
