What Length Of Hair Suits A Round Face

Description

If you have a round face, you might be wondering what length of hair suits your face shape best. A round face is characterized by having equal width and length, with a rounded jawline and cheekbones. While all hair lengths can look great on a round face, some styles will flatter your features better than others.

In this blog post, we'll cover how to determine the best hair length for a round face, a step-by-step guide on choosing the right hair length, tips on styling hair for a round face, FAQs, and hair care tips for a healthy mane.

How to Determine the Best Hair Length for a Round Face

The best hair length for a round face is one that elongates your face shape and creates the illusion of angles. If you have a round face, avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, like blunt bobs or curly hair. Instead, opt for haircuts that add height and length to your face, like long layers or a lob.

When choosing a hair length, consider your hair texture and lifestyle. If you have thick hair, a shorter length might be easier to manage. If you have fine hair, a longer length can add volume and body. Also, consider your daily routine and how much time you have to style your hair. Shorter haircuts might require more maintenance, while longer hair can be styled in a variety of ways.

Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers that start just below the cheekbones can elongate a round face shape. These layers create angles and draw attention away from the width of the face. Adding layers to long hair can also add movement and texture.

Long Bob

A long bob, or lob, is a great choice for a round face. This length hits just below the chin and can be styled straight or wavy. The length of a lob elongates the face shape, while the layers add movement and texture.

Shoulder-Length Hair

Shoulder-length hair that is cut in long layers can be a great choice for a round face. This length adds height to the face, while the layers create angles and movement. Shoulder-length hair can be styled straight or wavy, making it a versatile option.

Step by Step Guide on Choosing the Right Hair Length for a Round Face

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hair length for a round face is to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width and length of your face. If your face is equal in width and length, with a rounded jawline and cheekbones, you have a round face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Consider your hair texture when choosing a hair length. If you have thick hair, a shorter length might be easier to manage. If you have fine hair, a longer length can add volume and body.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Think about your daily routine and how much time you have to style your hair. Shorter haircuts might require more maintenance, while longer hair can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hair length for your face shape and hair texture. Your hairstylist can recommend styles that will flatter your features and fit your lifestyle.

Tips on Styling Hair for a Round Face

Tip 1: Add Volume at the Crown

Adding volume at the crown of your head can elongate a round face shape. Use a volumizing mousse or root lifter to add height to your hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Blunt Bangs

Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make a round face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides.

Tip 3: Embrace Texture

Embrace texture in your hair, as it can create the illusion of angles. Use a texturizing spray or salt spray to add movement and dimension to your hair.

Tip 4: Experiment with Different Styles

Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Try wearing your hair in a side part or playing with different hair accessories.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a pixie cut with a round face. However, it's important to add height and volume to the top of your head to elongate your face shape.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face?

A: No, you don't have to avoid short haircuts if you have a round face. A short bob or pixie cut can be a great choice if you add height and volume to your hair.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just make sure to add volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for a Healthy Mane

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

Use a heat protectant before using hot tools on your hair. This will help prevent damage and breakage.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Tip 3: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tip 4: Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Tip 5: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.


In conclusion, the best hair length for a round face is one that elongates your face shape and creates the illusion of angles. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a hair length. Experiment with different styles and use these hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
