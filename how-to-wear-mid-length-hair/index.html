How to Wear Mid Length Hair

If you're looking for a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle, mid length hair might be the perfect fit for you. Mid length hair, which falls just below the shoulders or right above the collarbone, can be worn in a variety of styles and is suitable for most hair types.

To wear mid length hair in a relaxed, effortless style, follow these tips:

1. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, don't fight it! Mid length hair looks great when it's allowed to air dry and show off its natural texture. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls or waves, and scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the texture.

2. Add Layers for Movement

To add movement and dimension to your mid length hair, consider adding layers. Layers can help to frame your face and create a more dynamic look. Talk to your stylist about the best type of layers for your hair type and face shape.

3. Experiment with Parting

Changing up your part can completely transform your mid length hairstyle. Try a deep side part for a glamorous look, or a middle part for a more bohemian vibe. If you're feeling daring, try a zigzag part for a playful twist.

4. Play with Accessories

Accessorizing your mid length hair can be a fun way to add some personality to your look. Try a headband, hair scarf, or hair clip to add some interest and hold your hair back.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide for styling your mid length hair:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair gently, being careful not to rough up the cuticle.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

If you plan to use heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant product to your hair to prevent damage.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to smooth out your hair and add some volume. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

4. Style Your Hair

Depending on the look you're going for, you can style your mid length hair in a variety of ways. Try a messy bun, a half-up half-down style, or loose waves.

5. Finish with Hairspray

To hold your style in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Tips for Styling Mid Length Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your mid length hair:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb to keep your hair tangle-free.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Experiment with different hair products, such as texturizing spray or serum, to find what works best for your hair type.
  • If you're in a rush, try a messy bun or a low ponytail for an easy, chic look.

FAQs about Mid Length Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about mid length hair:

1. Is mid length hair easy to style?

Yes! Mid length hair is a great option for anyone who wants a low-maintenance hairstyle. It can be styled in a variety of ways, and doesn't require as much upkeep as longer hair.

2. Can I still wear my hair up with mid length hair?

Absolutely! Mid length hair can be worn in a variety of updos, including messy buns, top knots, and braids.

3. Is mid length hair suitable for all hair types?

Yes, mid length hair can work for all hair types, from thin and fine to thick and curly.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

To keep your mid length hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear your mid length hair in a relaxed, effortless style that looks great and feels comfortable.
