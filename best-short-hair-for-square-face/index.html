Best Short Hair for Square Face - Tips, FAQ and Hair Care

Square faces have a strong, angular jawline, and a forehead and cheekbones of the same width. The key to finding the best short hair for square faces is to soften the angles and balance the proportions. In this article, we'll guide you through some of the best short haircuts for square faces, how to style them, tips, FAQ, and hair care. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Best Short Hairstyles for Square Face
  • How to Style Short Hair for Square Face
  • Tips for Short Hair for Square Face
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Short Hair for Square Face

Best Short Hairstyles for Square Face

The best short haircuts for square faces are those that soften the angles and create a balance. Here are some of the most flattering short hairstyles for square faces:

1. Bob Haircut

A classic bob haircut is perfect for square faces. Keep the length just below the chin or above the shoulders, and add some layers to soften the angles. You can also go for an asymmetrical bob, where one side is longer than the other, to create a more balanced look.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a daring option for square faces, but it can be very flattering if done right. Keep the length short on the sides and back, and add some volume on top to balance the proportions. You can also add some bangs to soften the forehead.

3. Shag Haircut

A shag haircut is a great option for square faces as it adds volume and movement to the hair. Keep the length short to medium, and add some choppy layers to create texture. You can also add some bangs to soften the forehead.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften the forehead and balance the proportions of a square face. Keep the length just below the eyebrows, and sweep them to the side. You can also add some layers to the rest of the hair to create texture.

How to Style Short Hair for Square Face

The key to styling short hair for square faces is to create volume and movement. Here are some tips to help you style your short hair:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

A volumizing shampoo can help add volume and texture to your hair. Use it regularly to create a full-bodied look.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can help create volume and movement. Use a round brush to lift the roots and create a bouncy look.

3. Use a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray can help create texture and movement in your hair. Spray it on your hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create a beachy, tousled look.

Tips for Short Hair for Square Face

Here are some additional tips to help you find the best short hair for your square face:

1. Accentuate Your Best Features

If you have beautiful eyes, go for a short haircut that draws attention to them. If you have a great jawline, opt for a style that accentuates it.

2. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can make a square face look even more angular. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that soften the forehead.

3. Experiment with Layers

Layers can help soften the angles of a square face. Experiment with choppy layers, feathered layers, or textured layers to find the right look for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What short haircut is best for a square face?

Bob haircut, pixie cut, shag haircut, and side-swept bangs are some of the best short haircuts for square faces.

Can I wear a bob with a square face?

Yes, a bob haircut is a great option for square faces. Keep the length just below the chin or above the shoulders, and add some layers to soften the angles.

What should I avoid with a square face?

Avoid heavy bangs, blunt cuts, and hairstyles that add volume on the sides of the face.

Hair Care for Short Hair for Square Face

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape and style. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best short hair for square faces and keep it looking great. Remember to choose a style that accentuates your best features and softens the angles of your face. Happy hairstyling!
