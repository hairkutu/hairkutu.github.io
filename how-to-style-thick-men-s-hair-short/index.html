How To Style Thick Men s Hair Short

Written by William Jul 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. While it offers plenty of volume and texture, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Short haircuts are a great option for men with thick hair, as they can help to reduce the weight and make styling easier. In this article, we'll take you through the steps of how to style thick men's hair short.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Thick Men's Hair Short
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Style Thick Men's Hair Short

Short haircuts can work well for men with thick hair, as they can make it easier to manage and style. Here are the steps to follow:

1. Choose Your Haircut

The first step is to choose your haircut. There are plenty of options for short haircuts for men, including the buzz cut, crew cut, and short textured crop. When choosing a haircut, consider your face shape and hair texture, as well as your lifestyle and personal style.

2. Wash Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub it too hard and cause damage.

3. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold the style in place. The type of product you use will depend on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Pomade, wax, and clay are all good options for short haircuts for men with thick hair.

4. Style Your Hair

Once you've applied your styling product, it's time to style your hair. Use a comb or your fingers to create your desired hairstyle. If you're going for a textured look, use your fingers to work the product through your hair and create definition.

5. Finish with Hairspray

To help hold your style in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair looking great all day.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for men with thick hair:

1. Choose your haircut.

2. Wash your hair.

3. Apply styling product.

4. Style your hair.

5. Finish with hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling short hair for men with thick hair:

  • Choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Dry your hair gently with a towel to avoid damage.
  • Apply styling product to damp hair for best results.
  • Use your fingers to work the product through your hair for a textured look.
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair for men with thick hair:

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

The best haircut for thick hair will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some good options for short haircuts for men with thick hair include the buzz cut, crew cut, and short textured crop.

What styling product should I use for short hair?

The styling product you use will depend on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Pomade, wax, and clay are all good options for short haircuts for men with thick hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for men with thick hair, especially if you're styling it regularly. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and flat irons too often, as they can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair health.
  • Stay hydrated and get plenty of rest to keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to style your thick men's hair short with ease. Remember to choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture, and use the right styling product for your hair type. With a little practice, you'll be able to create a stylish and easy-to-manage look that will keep you looking great all day long.
