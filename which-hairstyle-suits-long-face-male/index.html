Which Hairstyle Suits Long Face Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Apr 19, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have a long face, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits you. Your face shape affects how your hair looks, and it’s essential to choose a style that complements your features. In this article, we’ll discuss which hairstyle suits long face male, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to make your hair look great.

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, an elongated chin, and a narrow jawline. Men with long faces can have different hair types, including straight, wavy, or curly hair. The right hairstyle can make your face look more proportional and enhance your overall appearance.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

The key to finding the right hairstyle for a long face is to add width and volume to the sides of your face. This will balance out your features and make your face look more proportional. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

Consider Your Hair Type

Choose a hairstyle that works with your hair type. If you have straight hair, a layered cut can add volume and texture to your hair. For wavy or curly hair, a longer cut can help control frizz and add definition to your curls.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair can also affect how your face looks. If you have a long face, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. A medium-length hairstyle that falls just above or below the shoulders can be a good choice.

Add Layers

Adding layers to your hair can create movement and volume, making your face look more proportional. A layered cut can also help soften your features and add texture to your hair.

Choose the Right Parting

The right parting can make a big difference in how your hair looks. For a long face, consider a side parting or a diagonal parting to add width to your face.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to consider when choosing the right hairstyle, here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect look:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing your hairstyle, determine your face shape. This will help you understand which hairstyles will suit you best.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length and Style

Based on your face shape, choose the right length and style for your hair. Consider adding layers or a side parting to add volume to your face.

Step 3: Visit a Professional Hairstylist

Visit a professional hairstylist to achieve the perfect look. They will be able to recommend the best hairstyle for you and provide tips on how to maintain it.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when styling your hair:

Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling can damage your hair and make it look unhealthy. Keep your styling to a minimum and use products that are gentle on your hair.

Use a Good Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. This will keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Experiment with Different Styles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one. Try different lengths, styles, and partings to find what works best for you.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you have an active lifestyle, choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and won’t get in the way.

FAQ

What Hairstyle Suits a Long Face?

A hairstyle that adds volume and width to the sides of your face, such as a layered cut or a side parting, can suit a long face.

What Hair Products Should I Use?

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and gentle on your hair. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohol.

How Often Should I Get My Hair Cut?

How often you get your hair cut depends on your hair type and the style you choose. Generally, it’s recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

Hair Care

Keep your hair healthy and looking great with these hair care tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling can damage your hair and make it look unhealthy. Keep your styling to a minimum and use products that are gentle on your hair.

Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals that are essential for healthy hair, such as protein, iron, and biotin.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often. Excessive heat can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a long face can be challenging, but with these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect look. Remember to experiment with different styles, use good quality hair products, and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
