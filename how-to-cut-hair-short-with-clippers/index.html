How To Cut Hair Short With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the hair salon just to maintain a short hairstyle? Well, with the right tools and technique, you can easily cut your hair short with clippers at home. In this guide, we will show you how to cut hair short with clippers in a relaxed English language, so you can achieve the perfect style without breaking the bank.

Description

Cutting your hair short with clippers is a simple and easy process that can save you money and time. Clippers are designed to cut hair to a precise length, and they come with different guard sizes to achieve different lengths. This means that you can customize your haircut to suit your desired length and style.

How to Cut Hair Short with Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, there are a few things that you need to do:

  • Wash your hair and dry it completely
  • Choose the right guard size for your desired length
  • Set up your clippers and make sure they are fully charged
  • Find a well-lit area with a mirror

Once you've prepared your tools and space, you're ready to start cutting your hair:

  1. Start by using a longer guard size (e.g. 4 or 5) to trim the top of your hair. Use the clippers to trim from the front to the back of your head, moving against the direction of your hair growth.
  2. Next, switch to a shorter guard size (e.g. 2 or 3) to trim the sides and back of your head. Use the clippers to trim from the bottom of your hairline to the top, moving with the direction of your hair growth.
  3. Use a comb to blend the top and sides of your hair, making sure there are no harsh lines or uneven patches.
  4. If you want a shorter haircut, you can remove the guard and use the clippers without it. Start at the bottom of your hairline and move upwards, using short strokes to trim your hair to the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much at once, as this can result in an uneven haircut.
  5. Once you've finished trimming your hair, use a mirror to check for any missed spots or uneven areas. You can use scissors to make any small corrections if necessary.

Tips for Cutting Hair Short with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short haircut with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much at once
  • Use a comb to keep your hair straight and even while trimming
  • Trim your hair in a well-lit area with a mirror to ensure you can see what you're doing
  • Take your time and be patient - rushing can lead to mistakes
  • Don't forget to clean your clippers after use to ensure they last longer

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair short with clippers if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your hair short with clippers regardless of your hair type. However, you may need to use a longer guard size to ensure that your hair is not cut too short and that your curls are not damaged.

Q: How often should I cut my hair short with clippers?

A: This depends on your desired length and style. If you want a shorter haircut, you may need to trim your hair every 2-4 weeks to maintain the length. If you prefer a longer style, you may only need to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care after Cutting Hair Short with Clippers

After cutting your hair short with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length

Cutting your hair short with clippers is a simple and easy process that can save you time and money. By following this step-by-step guide, you can achieve the perfect short haircut at home. Remember to take your time, use the right guard sizes, and take care of your hair after cutting it to maintain its style and health.
