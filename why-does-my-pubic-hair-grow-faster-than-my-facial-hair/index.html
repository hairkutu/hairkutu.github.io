Why Does My Pubic Hair Grow Faster Than My Facial Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 · 6 min read

As men, we often wonder why our pubic hair seems to grow faster than our facial hair. It’s a common question that many people have asked themselves at some point in their lives. In this article, we will explore the reasons why pubic hair grows faster than facial hair.

Description

Pubic hair is a type of hair that grows in the pubic area, which is the area between the genitals and the anus. Facial hair, on the other hand, grows on the face, including the chin, cheeks, and upper lip. Both types of hair are made up of the same protein called keratin, but they grow at different rates.

How to

The rate at which hair grows is determined by genetics, hormones, and age. The hair follicles in the pubic area are more sensitive to androgens, which are male hormones, than the hair follicles on the face. Androgens stimulate hair growth, which is why pubic hair grows faster than facial hair.

Another reason why pubic hair grows faster than facial hair is because the hair follicles in the pubic area are larger than the hair follicles on the face. Larger hair follicles produce thicker and coarser hair, which grows faster than finer hair.

Step by Step

Pubic hair growth also tends to be more consistent than facial hair growth. Pubic hair grows at a steady rate throughout a man’s life, while facial hair growth can be sporadic and may not start until puberty. This is because facial hair growth is also influenced by the amount of androgens in the body, which varies throughout a man’s life.

Finally, pubic hair is also less likely to fall out than facial hair. The hair follicles in the pubic area are anchored more deeply in the skin than the hair follicles on the face, which means that pubic hair is less likely to be affected by external factors like shaving or rubbing against clothing.

Tips

If you’re looking to speed up facial hair growth, there are a few things you can do. First, make sure you’re eating a healthy diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals. Vitamins like Biotin and Zinc can help promote hair growth. Secondly, try using a beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair healthy and moisturized. Finally, consider using a derma roller or taking supplements like Minoxidil to promote hair growth.

FAQ

Q: Does shaving make facial hair grow faster?

A: No, shaving does not make facial hair grow faster. When you shave, you’re simply cutting off the hair that’s already there. The hair will grow back at the same rate as before.

Q: Will plucking or waxing my facial hair make it grow back thicker?

A: No, plucking or waxing your facial hair will not make it grow back thicker. When you pluck or wax, you’re simply removing the hair from the follicle. The hair will grow back at the same rate and thickness as before.

Hair Care

In conclusion, pubic hair grows faster than facial hair due to genetics, hormones, and age. While there’s no way to speed up facial hair growth, you can take steps to keep your facial hair healthy and moisturized. And remember, there’s nothing wrong with having less facial hair than pubic hair – it’s just a natural part of being a man.
