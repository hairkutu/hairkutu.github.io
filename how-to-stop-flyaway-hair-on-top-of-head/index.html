How To Stop Flyaway Hair On Top Of Head Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver May 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with flyaway hair on top of your head? It can be frustrating to have hair that sticks up in all directions, especially when you're trying to look your best. Luckily, there are some simple tips and tricks you can use to tame those pesky flyaways and keep your hair looking sleek and smooth. In this article, we'll explore some of the most effective ways to stop flyaway hair on top of your head.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Stop Flyaway Hair on Top of Head
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Stop Flyaway Hair on Top of Head

There are several ways to stop flyaway hair on top of your head, including using hair products, changing your hair care routine, and taking steps to protect your hair from damage. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Use a Smoothing Serum

One of the easiest ways to tame flyaway hair is to use a smoothing serum. Look for a product that is designed to combat frizz and flyaways, and apply a small amount to your hair before styling. Be sure to concentrate the serum on the areas where you have the most flyaways, such as the top of your head.

2. Use Hair Spray

Hair spray is another great option for taming flyaway hair. Look for a hairspray that is specifically designed for flyaways, and spray it lightly over your hair after you've styled it. Be sure to hold the can several inches away from your head to avoid making your hair look greasy or weighed down.

3. Change Your Hair Care Routine

If you're dealing with persistent flyaway hair, it may be time to change up your hair care routine. Try using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed to nourish and strengthen your hair. You may also want to consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to stopping flyaway hair on top of your head:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub it too hard.
  3. Apply a smoothing serum or hair oil to your hair, concentrating on the areas where you have the most flyaways.
  4. Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting, using a round brush to smooth out any flyaways.
  5. Once your hair is dry, apply a light mist of hairspray to keep flyaways in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for stopping flyaway hair on top of your head:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent flyaways while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and make flyaways worse.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause static and make flyaways worse.
  • Try using a boar bristle brush to help smooth out flyaways.

FAQ

Q: Why do I have flyaway hair on top of my head?

A: Flyaway hair is typically caused by dryness, static, or damage to the hair. It can also be caused by using hot tools or over-brushing your hair.

Q: Can I use hair gel to tame flyaway hair?

A: Hair gel can be effective for taming flyaway hair, but it can also make your hair look greasy or weighed down if you use too much. Try using a light hold gel and apply it sparingly to avoid this problem.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help prevent flyaway hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness and flyaways.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or braids, as they can cause breakage and flyaways.

By following these tips and tricks, you can stop flyaway hair on top of your head and keep your hair looking sleek and smooth. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
