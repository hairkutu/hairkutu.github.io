Short Hairstyles For Oval Faces And Thin Hair The Perfect Match

Written by Daniel Apr 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face and thin hair, you might feel like your hairstyle options are limited. However, there are plenty of short hairstyles that complement your face shape and make your thin hair look fuller and more voluminous. In this article, we'll share some of the best short hairstyles for oval faces and thin hair, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thin Hair

Description

An oval face is considered to be the most versatile face shape, as it can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, if you have thin hair, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and dimension to your hair. Short hairstyles are a great option for thin hair, as they create the illusion of thickness and add texture to your hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

When choosing a short hairstyle for oval faces and thin hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Look for hairstyles that add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your hair look even thinner.
  • Consider layers, which can add depth and movement to your hair.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and personal style.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy. Here's a step by step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for thin hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to add lift and volume.
  4. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls to your hair.
  5. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use products that are specifically designed for thin hair, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for oval faces and thin hair:

What is the best short hairstyle for oval faces and thin hair?

The best short hairstyle for oval faces and thin hair is a layered bob or pixie cut. These hairstyles add volume and texture to your hair, while also complementing your face shape.

Can I dye my thin hair?

Yes, you can dye your thin hair. However, it's important to choose a hair dye that is gentle and won't damage your hair. You can also consider using a hair gloss or toner to add shine and dimension to your hair.

How often should I wash my thin hair?

You should wash your thin hair every other day or every two days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it look even thinner.

Hair Care for Thin Hair

Thin hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and full. Here are some tips to help you care for your thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in nutrients, such as protein and vitamins, to promote hair growth.
  • Consider using a hair growth serum or supplement to help your hair grow thicker and stronger.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can rock your short hair with confidence and style. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and personal style, and take care of your hair with the right products and care.
