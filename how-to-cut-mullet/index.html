How To Cut Mullet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to rock a trendy and edgy hairstyle, the mullet might be just the cut for you. This iconic hairstyle, popularized in the '80s, has made a comeback in recent years. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut mullet in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. From the tools you'll need to the step-by-step process, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Tools You'll Need

Before we dive into the cutting process, let's first make sure you have all the necessary tools to get the job done:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and towel-drying your hair. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Then, section your hair by clipping the top section up and away from the bottom section.

Step 2: Cut the Top Section

Using your scissors, cut the top section of your hair to your desired length. Make sure to leave enough length on top to create the classic mullet shape. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle towards the back of your head. This will create layers and add volume to your hair.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Next, use your clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair. You can choose to leave the sides longer or shorter, depending on your preference. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the clipper guard to create an even length. Blend the sides and back into the top section, making sure to create a seamless transition.

Step 4: Create the Mullet Shape

To create the classic mullet shape, you'll need to add some length to the back of your hair. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle towards the center of your neck. Leave the hair at the nape of your neck longer than the rest of your hair. This will create the iconic mullet shape.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Once you've created the mullet shape, go back and check for any uneven spots or sections. Trim any stray hairs or sections that need it. Style your hair as desired, using a hair product to add texture and hold.

Tips for Cutting Mullet

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member.
  • Remember to take breaks and step away from the mirror to avoid over-cutting or overthinking the process.

FAQ

How long does a mullet haircut last?

A mullet haircut can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Can I cut my own mullet?

Yes, you can cut your own mullet at home. Just make sure you have the necessary tools and follow the step-by-step guide carefully.

Is the mullet hairstyle still in fashion?

Yes, the mullet hairstyle has made a comeback in recent years and is considered a trendy and edgy hairstyle.

Hair Care for Mullet

To keep your mullet looking its best, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a hair product to add texture and hold, and avoid over-styling or over-brushing your hair. Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Congratulations, you now know how to cut mullet like a pro. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve the iconic mullet shape and rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence.
