How To Cut Long Layers In Your Own Hair Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle without breaking the bank, cutting your own long layers can be a great option. By following a few simple steps and using the right tools, you can create a fresh and trendy look from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut long layers in your own hair YouTube style, so you can achieve salon-quality results without leaving your house.

How to Cut Long Layers in Your Own Hair YouTube: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp, high-quality hair scissors, a comb or hairbrush, and hair ties or clips to section off your hair. It's also a good idea to have a mirror on hand so you can see what you're doing from all angles.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. Brush out any tangles or knots, and make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin cutting.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb or hairbrush, divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair down the middle, then create two additional sections on either side of your head. Use hair ties or clips to secure each section.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Take a small section of hair (about 1 inch thick) from the bottom section, and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair straight across, making sure to keep the scissors at a slight angle to create a subtle taper.

Continue cutting small sections of hair from the bottom layer, gradually working your way up to the top layer. As you move up, make sure to blend the layers by cutting at a slight angle.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you've finished cutting, take a look in the mirror to see how your layers are shaping up. If you notice any unevenness or areas that need more attention, don't be afraid to go back and make adjustments.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you're happy with your new layers, it's time to style your hair. You can use a hair straightener or curling iron to create loose waves or sleek, straight locks.

Tips for Cutting Long Layers in Your Own Hair YouTube

Here are a few helpful tips to keep in mind as you cut your own long layers:

  • Use sharp hair scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid making big mistakes
  • Take breaks if you need to rest your arms or hands
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it

FAQ: Cutting Long Layers in Your Own Hair YouTube

Can I cut my own long layers if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to cut your layers when your hair is dry and styled in its natural state, so you can see how the layers will look when you wear your hair curly.

How often should I cut my long layers?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your layers. As a general rule, you should plan to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep your layers looking fresh.

Hair Care: Maintaining Your Long Layers

To keep your long layers looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cut long layers in your own hair YouTube style!
