Does A Fringe Make Your Face Look Fatter

Written by Oliver Jun 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Having a fringe is a popular hairstyle option for many people. However, there is a common belief that having a fringe can make your face look fatter. In this blog post, we will explore whether or not this is true and provide some tips for those considering getting a fringe.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

There is no straightforward answer to whether or not a fringe makes your face look fatter. It largely depends on the shape of your face and the type of fringe you choose. While a fringe can help to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes, it can also make your face appear wider if not styled correctly.

How to

If you are considering getting a fringe, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape: If you have a round face, a full fringe may not be the best option. Instead, opt for a side-swept fringe that will help to elongate your face.
  • Think about your hair texture: If you have thick hair, a heavy fringe may make your face look wider. On the other hand, if you have thinner hair, a fringe can add volume and create the illusion of a fuller face.
  • Consult with a hairstylist: A professional hairstylist can help you determine the best type of fringe for your face shape and hair texture.

Step by step

If you do decide to get a fringe, here are some steps to follow to help avoid making your face look fatter:

  • Choose a flattering length: A fringe that hits just below your eyebrows can help to balance out the width of your face.
  • Style your fringe: Blow-drying your fringe with a round brush can help to add volume and create a flattering shape.
  • Avoid heavy bangs: A thick, heavy fringe can make your face look wider. Opt for a lighter, wispy fringe instead.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for those with a fringe:

  • Use a volumizing spray: Adding volume to your fringe can help to create a flattering shape.
  • Trim your fringe regularly: A fringe that is too long can overwhelm your face and make it look wider.
  • Experiment with different styles: There are many different types of fringes to choose from, so don't be afraid to try something new.

FAQ

Does a full fringe make your face look fatter?

It can, especially if you have a round face. However, a side-swept fringe can help to elongate your face and create a more flattering look.

What is the best type of fringe for a round face?

A side-swept fringe that is longer on one side than the other can help to create the illusion of a longer face.

How often should I trim my fringe?

It's best to trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and prevent it from getting too long.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair and fringe to keep it looking its best. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage. Additionally, be sure to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a protective hair product.

Overall, whether or not a fringe makes your face look fatter largely depends on your face shape and the type of fringe you choose. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can create a flattering and stylish look that works for you.
