How To Wear Curly Hair To Bed A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 21, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse, especially when it comes to bedtime. On one hand, your curls can look amazing when you wake up in the morning. On the other hand, tossing and turning all night can lead to frizz, tangles, and flattened curls. But fear not, because there are ways to wear your curly hair to bed that will help you wake up with gorgeous, defined curls. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to wear curly hair to bed, including tips, tricks, and a step-by-step guide.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Wear Curly Hair to Bed

When it comes to wearing curly hair to bed, there are a few different methods you can try. The key is to find a method that works for your hair type and curl pattern. Here are some of the most popular ways to wear curly hair to bed:

Braid or Twist

One of the easiest ways to wear curly hair to bed is to braid or twist it. This method works well for most hair types and curl patterns, and it helps to prevent tangles and frizz. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Braid or twist each section, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Secure the ends with a hair tie or clip.
  6. Wrap a silk or satin scarf around your head to protect your hair while you sleep.

This method is great because it not only protects your hair while you sleep, but it also creates beautiful, defined curls when you take the braids or twists out in the morning.

Pineapple

The pineapple method is another popular way to wear curly hair to bed. This method is especially great for those with longer hair or tighter curls. Here's how to do it:

  1. Flip your head over and gather all of your hair at the top of your head.
  2. Secure your hair with a gentle hair tie or scrunchie.
  3. Wrap a silk or satin scarf around your head to protect your hair while you sleep.

This method helps to prevent flattening of your curls while you sleep, and it also keeps your hair out of your face.

Plopping

Plopping is a method that involves using a t-shirt or microfiber towel to dry your hair and create defined curls. This method can be done before bed or after washing your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Flip your head over and place a t-shirt or microfiber towel on a flat surface.
  3. Lay your hair gently on top of the t-shirt or towel.
  4. Wrap the t-shirt or towel around your head, creating a "plop" on top of your head.
  5. Secure the ends of the t-shirt or towel with a hair tie or clip.
  6. Leave the plop in place for 10-20 minutes.

This method helps to absorb excess water from your hair and create defined curls without causing frizz or tangles.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different methods for wearing curly hair to bed, let's dive into a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. If desired, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair.
  3. Select a method for wearing your hair to bed (braid or twist, pineapple, or plopping).
  4. Follow the steps for your chosen method.
  5. Wrap a silk or satin scarf around your head to protect your hair while you sleep.
  6. In the morning, remove the scarf and take out the braids, twists, or pineapple.
  7. Gently finger comb your hair to loosen the curls and create volume.
  8. Apply a light styling product, such as a curl refresher or hair oil, to add shine and definition to your curls.

Tips for Wearing Curly Hair to Bed

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing curly hair to bed:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to minimize frizz and tangles.
  • Avoid using cotton pillowcases, as they can absorb moisture from your hair and cause frizz.
  • Avoid tight hair ties or clips, as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair type and curl pattern.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by avoiding hot tools and using a heat protectant when necessary.

FAQ

Can I wear my curly hair down to bed?

It's not recommended to wear your curly hair down to bed, as it can cause tangles, frizz, and flattened curls. Instead, try one of the methods outlined in this post to protect your hair while you sleep.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

How often you wash your curly hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair can go several days or even a week without washing, while others prefer to wash their hair every other day. It's important to listen to your hair and adjust your routine as needed.

How do I refresh my curls in the morning?

If your curls need a little extra oomph in the morning, try using a curl refresher or hair oil to add definition and shine. You can also gently mist your hair with water and scrunch to revive your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to knowing how to wear curly hair to bed, it's important to take good care of your curls to keep them healthy and defined. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to keep it moisturized and defined.
  • Avoid using hot tools, or use a heat protectant when necessary.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and damage.

By following these tips and using the methods outlined in this post, you can wear your curly hair to bed with confidence and wake up with beautiful, defined curls.
