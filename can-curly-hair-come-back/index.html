Can Curly Hair Come Back Tips And Tricks For Restoring Your Curls

If you've been relaxing your hair for years, you may be wondering if it's possible to get your natural curls back. The good news is that with the right care and attention, it is possible to restore your curly hair. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks for bringing your curls back to life.

How to Restore Your Curly Hair

Before you start trying to restore your curly hair, it's important to understand what caused it to become straight in the first place. Relaxers contain chemicals that break down the protein bonds in your hair, making it easier to straighten. While this can be a convenient way to style your hair, it also damages your hair over time.

If you want to bring your curls back, you'll need to stop using relaxers and start taking care of your hair. This means using gentle products that won't strip your hair of its natural oils, and avoiding heat styling as much as possible. It also means being patient, as it can take several months or even a year for your curls to fully return.

Step-by-Step Guide to Restoring Curls

Here are the steps you can take to restore your curly hair:

  1. Stop using relaxers and other harsh chemicals on your hair.
  2. Start using gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  3. Avoid heat styling as much as possible, and use a diffuser when blow-drying.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls.
  5. Try plopping your hair with a t-shirt or microfiber towel to encourage curl formation.
  6. Consider getting a trim to remove any damaged or straight ends.
  7. Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.

Tips for Caring for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase or use a satin cap to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol or silicone, as these can dry out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.

FAQ about Restoring Curly Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about restoring curly hair:

Can curly hair come back after years of relaxing?

Yes, it is possible to restore your natural curls after years of relaxing. However, it can take time and patience.

How long does it take for curly hair to come back after relaxing?

It can take several months or even a year for your curls to fully return after relaxing. It depends on the length of your hair, the severity of the damage, and how well you care for your hair.

What products should I use to restore my curly hair?

You should use gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair, as well as a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls. Deep conditioning treatments can also help restore moisture and health to your hair.
