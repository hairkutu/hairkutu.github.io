How To Style Older Mens Hair A Guide For Relaxed Gents

Written by Bardi May 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As men age, their hair may start to thin or change texture. But that doesn't mean they have to sacrifice style. With the right technique and products, you can keep your locks looking sharp and sophisticated. In this guide, we'll show you how to style older mens hair in a relaxed, effortless way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Older Mens Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Look
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Older Men

How to Style Older Mens Hair

When it comes to styling older mens hair, the key is to keep it simple and natural. Gone are the days of bold, dramatic cuts and colors. Instead, opt for a classic, understated look that complements your features and personality.

Some popular styles for older men include:

  • The Side Part
  • The Slick Back
  • The Crew Cut
  • The Buzz Cut
  • The Caesar Cut
  • The Ivy League Cut

Regardless of which style you choose, make sure to keep it neat and tidy. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized, and invest in a high-quality styling product to help you achieve your desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling older mens hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. If your hair is thinning, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give it more body.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hands and rub them together to distribute it evenly.
  4. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into your desired shape.
  6. Finish with a light spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling older mens hair. Don't overdo it with too much product or too many layers.

Tips for Maintaining Your Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your stylish look:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • If you have gray hair, embrace it! Gray hair can be very attractive and distinguished-looking.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling older mens hair:

Q: Is it okay for older men to have long hair?

A: Absolutely! As long as your hair is healthy and well-maintained, you can rock any length you like.

Q: What's the best way to cover up gray hair?

A: There are many hair dyes and coloring products on the market that can help cover up gray hair. However, if you prefer a more natural look, you can embrace your natural gray and make it work for you.

Q: Can I still use hair gel in my 50s?

A: Yes, you can! Just be sure to use a high-quality product that won't leave your hair feeling greasy or weighed down.

Hair Care Tips for Older Men

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and treatments, such as perms or relaxers.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin, which can help promote hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, which can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.

By following these tips and using the right styling products, you can keep your hair looking stylish and sophisticated, no matter your age.
