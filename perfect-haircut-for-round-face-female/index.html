Perfect Haircut For Round Face Female A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel May 31, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Round face shape is one of the most common face shapes among women. It is characterized by a wide forehead, prominent cheeks, and a rounded chin. Finding the perfect haircut that flatters a round face shape can be a daunting task. But worry not, we've got you covered! In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect haircut for round face female.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by a width that is almost equal to its height. Women with round faces tend to have full cheeks, soft features, and a curved jawline. The aim of finding the perfect haircut for round face female is to create an illusion of length and narrowness, making the face appear more oval-shaped.

How to Find the Perfect Haircut for Round Face Female

Before we dive into the perfect haircuts for round face female, it's essential to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine your face shape in the mirror. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of equal width, you likely have a round face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Tip #1: Opt for Layers

Layered haircuts are perfect for round face shape as they create an illusion of length and add volume to the hair. The layers should be longer around the face, with shorter layers at the back. This will make the face appear more oval-shaped and add texture to the hair.

Tip #2: Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can shorten the face and make it appear rounder. Instead, opt for soft, side-swept bangs that will create an illusion of length and add dimension to the hair.

Tip #3: Embrace the Bob

A lob or a long bob is perfect for round face shape as it lengthens the face and creates a slimming effect. The length should hit just below the chin, with layers around the face to add volume and texture.

Tip #4: Add Volume on Top

Adding volume on top of the head can create an illusion of length and balance out the face shape. A voluminous top paired with a sleek bottom will make the face appear more oval-shaped.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Perfect Haircut for Round Face Female

Now that you know what to look for in the perfect haircut for round face female, here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the look:

Step #1: Determine your Face Shape

Pull your hair back and examine your face shape in the mirror. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of equal width, you likely have a round face shape.

Step #2: Consult with your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist and show them pictures of haircuts that you like. They will be able to recommend a haircut that flatters your features and works with your hair texture.

Step #3: Opt for Layers

Ask your hairstylist for long layers around the face, with shorter layers at the back. This will create an illusion of length and add volume to the hair.

Step #4: Embrace the Bob

Ask your hairstylist for a lob or a long bob that hits just below the chin. The length should be longer around the face, with layers to add volume and texture.

Step #5: Add Volume on Top

Ask your hairstylist to add volume on top of the head to create an illusion of length and balance out the face shape. A voluminous top paired with a sleek bottom will make the face appear more oval-shaped.

Tips for Maintaining the Perfect Haircut for Round Face Female

Now that you have found the perfect haircut for round face female, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips:

Tip #1: Trim your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Tip #2: Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Avoid using products that weigh down the hair or make it appear greasy.

Tip #3: Protect your Hair from Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage the hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

FAQs

What Haircut is Best for a Round Face Female?

The best haircut for a round face female is a lob or a long bob that hits just below the chin. The length should be longer around the face, with layers to add volume and texture.

Can a Bob Flatter a Round Face?

Yes, a bob can flatter a round face if it is the right length and has layers around the face to add volume and texture.

What Hair Products Should I Use for a Round Face Female Haircut?

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Avoid using products that weigh down the hair or make it appear greasy. Consult with your hairstylist for recommendations.

Hair Care for Round Face Female Haircut

Now that you have found the perfect haircut for round face female, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Tip #1: Wash your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy.

Tip #2: Use a Conditioner

Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair soft and manageable.

Tip #3: Protect your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with UV protection to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

With these tips, you can now confidently rock the perfect haircut for round face female. Remember, the key is to find a haircut that flatters your features and works with your hair texture. Consult with your hairstylist for recommendations and maintain your hair with the right products and care.
