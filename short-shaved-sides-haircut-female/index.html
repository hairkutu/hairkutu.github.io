Short Shaved Sides Haircut Female A Bold And Edgy Look

Written by Petter Apr 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle? Look no further than the short shaved sides haircut for females. This hairstyle has been around for a while, but it's still a popular choice for those who want to make a statement. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this look, provide step-by-step instructions, offer tips, answer frequently asked questions, and provide hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Short Shaved Sides Haircut Female
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Short Shaved Sides Haircut Female
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for the Short Shaved Sides Haircut Female

Description

The short shaved sides haircut for females is a bold and edgy look that involves shaving the sides of your head while keeping the top longer. This style can be customized to suit your personal taste, with various lengths and designs available. The shaved sides can be blended into the rest of your hair or left as a distinct and dramatic contrast.

How to Achieve the Short Shaved Sides Haircut Female

The first step to achieving the short shaved sides haircut female is to find a reputable hairdresser who has experience with this style. They will be able to advise you on the best length and design to suit your face shape, hair type, and personal preference.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the short shaved sides haircut female:

  1. Wash and dry your hair, ensuring it is free from tangles and knots.
  2. Section off the hair on the top of your head using clips or hair ties.
  3. Decide on the length of your shaved sides and the design you want.
  4. Use clippers or scissors to shave the sides of your head, starting with the longest length and gradually working your way shorter.
  5. Blend the shaved sides into the rest of your hair if desired.
  6. Style the top of your hair as desired, using products such as wax, pomade, or hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining the Short Shaved Sides Haircut Female

Maintaining the short shaved sides haircut female requires regular upkeep to keep the shaved areas looking neat and tidy. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your hairdresser every 4-6 weeks for a trim and touch-up.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as straighteners or curling irons on the shaved areas, as this can damage the hair.
  • Incorporate hair oils or serums into your hair care routine to keep the shaved areas smooth and shiny.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short shaved sides haircut female:

Is the short shaved sides haircut female suitable for all hair types?

The short shaved sides haircut female can be adapted to suit a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick hair.

Will the shaved hair grow back at the same rate as the rest of my hair?

Yes, the hair on the shaved sides will grow back at the same rate as the rest of your hair. However, it may appear to grow back more quickly due to the contrast between the shaved and longer areas.

Can I style my hair in different ways with a short shaved sides haircut female?

Yes, the top of your hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or curly. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for the Short Shaved Sides Haircut Female

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the short shaved sides haircut female. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on the shaved areas, as this can damage the hair.
  • Apply hair oil or serum to the shaved areas to keep them smooth and shiny.
  • Visit your hairdresser regularly for trims and touch-ups.

The short shaved sides haircut female is a bold and edgy look that can be customized to suit your personal style. Whether you opt for a subtle or dramatic shaved design, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads. Follow our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice to achieve and maintain the perfect short shaved sides haircut female.
