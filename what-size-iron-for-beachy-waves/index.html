What Size Iron For Beachy Waves The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking to achieve those effortless, beachy waves that everyone seems to be rocking these days? Well, you're in luck because we've got the ultimate guide on what size iron for beachy waves. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to choose the right size iron to tips for achieving the perfect wave. So, let's dive in!

Description

Beachy waves are the perfect combination of tousled and textured hair. They're effortless, relaxed, and perfect for any occasion. However, achieving this look can be a bit tricky, especially if you don't have the right tools. That's where the size of your curling iron comes in. Choosing the right size iron can make all the difference in achieving those perfect beachy waves.

How to Choose the Right Size Iron for Beachy Waves

When it comes to choosing the right size iron for beachy waves, there are a few things to consider:

1. Hair Length

The length of your hair plays a big role in determining the size of the iron you should use. If you have short hair, a smaller barrel size will work best. For longer hair, you can go for a larger barrel size.

2. Curl Size

The size of the curl you want to achieve will also determine the size of the iron you should use. If you want tighter curls, go for a smaller barrel size. For looser, more relaxed waves, a larger barrel size will work better.

3. Heat Setting

Make sure to choose an iron with adjustable heat settings. Fine hair requires less heat than thicker hair, so it's important to adjust the heat accordingly to prevent damage.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Beachy Waves

Now that you've chosen the right size iron, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving those beachy waves:

1. Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a heat protectant to prevent damage from the heat of the iron. Blow-dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting at the back of your head. Pin up the top section of your hair to keep it out of the way.

3. Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair, wrap it around the iron, and hold for a few seconds. Release the curl and move on to the next section. Alternate the direction of the curls to achieve a more natural look.

4. Finish Your Look

Once you've curled all of your hair, use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture. Rub your fingers through your hair to loosen up the curls and create a more relaxed look.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Beachy Wave

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying to achieve the perfect beachy wave:

1. Use a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray can help add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to achieve those beachy waves.

2. Alternate Curl Directions

To achieve a more natural look, alternate the direction of your curls. Curl some sections towards your face and others away from your face.

3. Don't Over-Curl

Over-curling your hair can lead to a more formal, polished look. To achieve those relaxed, beachy waves, don't hold the iron on your hair for too long.

FAQ

What Size Iron Should I Use for Beachy Waves?

The size of the iron you should use depends on your hair length and the size of the curl you want to achieve. For shorter hair or tighter curls, go for a smaller barrel size. For longer hair or looser waves, a larger barrel size will work better.

Can I Achieve Beachy Waves with a Straightener?

Yes, you can achieve beachy waves with a straightener. Simply twist the straightener as you pass it through your hair to create waves.

How Long Will Beachy Waves Last?

Beachy waves can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days depending on your hair type and the products you use. To make them last longer, use a hairspray or texturizing spray.

Hair Care for Beachy Waves

When it comes to maintaining your beachy waves, here are a few tips:

1. Use a Deep Conditioner

Beachy waves can be drying on your hair. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days to maintain your beachy waves.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, especially if you have color-treated hair. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

And there you have it, everything you need to know about what size iron for beachy waves. With the right tools and a little practice, you'll be rocking those relaxed, effortless waves in no time!
