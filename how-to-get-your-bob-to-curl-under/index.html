How To Get Your Bob To Curl Under A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your bob just looking blah? Want to add some volume and movement to your hairstyle? One easy way to achieve this is by curling your bob under. In this guide, we'll show you how to get your bob to curl under and achieve a chic, polished look.

Why Curl Your Bob Under?

There are many reasons why you might want to curl your bob under. For starters, it can add volume to flat hair and give the illusion of thicker locks. It can also help frame your face and accentuate your features. Plus, a curled-under bob is a classic look that will never go out of style.

How to Get Your Bob to Curl Under: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help your curls hold their shape better. If your hair is naturally oily, consider using a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add some grip and volume.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your locks healthy. Simply spritz the product evenly over your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use a Round Brush

To curl your bob under, you'll need a round brush. Start by sectioning off your hair and wrapping it around the brush, using tension to create a smooth, sleek finish. Then, use a blow dryer to heat the hair while it's still wrapped around the brush. This will help create the curl.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

If you want more defined curls, you can also use a curling iron. Clamp the iron onto a small section of hair near the ends and roll it up towards your head. Hold it in place for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've curled your bob under, finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep everything in place. Choose a flexible, medium hold hairspray that won't weigh down your hair or make it feel crunchy.

Tips for Getting Your Bob to Curl Under

Here are some additional tips to help you get the perfect curled-under bob:

  • Choose the right brush size. A smaller brush will give you tighter curls, while a larger brush will give you looser, more natural waves.
  • Be patient. Curling your bob under can take some practice, so don't get discouraged if your first attempt doesn't turn out perfectly.
  • Experiment with different techniques. You can use a round brush, curling iron, or even a straightener to curl your bob under. Try different methods to see what works best for you.
  • Don't overdo it. Too much heat styling can damage your hair, so be sure to give your locks a break every once in a while.

FAQs

Can I curl my bob under without heat?

Yes! One way to curl your bob without heat is by using foam rollers. Simply roll small sections of your hair onto the rollers, then leave them in for a few hours (or overnight). When you take them out, you'll have bouncy, curled-under locks.

How do I keep my curls from falling flat?

To keep your curls from falling flat, make sure you're using the right products. A volumizing mousse or texturizing spray can help add grip and hold. You can also try teasing your roots or using bobby pins to anchor your curls in place.

Hair Care Tips for a Healthy Bob

To keep your bob looking its best, be sure to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling too frequently, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims (every 6-8 weeks) to prevent split ends and maintain your bob's shape.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to keep your look fresh and fun!

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to get your bob to curl under in no time. Whether you're heading to the office or out for a night on the town, a curled-under bob is a stylish and versatile look that's perfect for any occasion.
