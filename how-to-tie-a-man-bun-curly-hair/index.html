How To Tie A Man Bun Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by William Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a curly-haired man who wants to rock a man bun? Look no further! In this guide, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to tie a man bun with curly hair. We will also provide tips on maintaining your curly hair and answer frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Tie a Man Bun
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Curly Hair Maintenance
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Tie a Man Bun

Before we dive into the steps, it's important to note that there are different ways to tie a man bun depending on your hair length, texture, and personal preference. However, the following steps are a general guide that can be adapted to fit your needs.

Step 1: Gather Your Hair

Start by gathering all of your hair at the crown of your head. Use a comb or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair

Take a hair tie and wrap it around your hair once. On the second wrap, only pull your hair halfway through the hair tie. This will create a loop with your hair.

Step 3: Secure Your Bun

Take the remaining hair tie and wrap it around the looped hair. Secure the hair tie in place by tucking the end of the hair tie under the bun.

Step 4: Adjust Your Bun

Use your fingers to adjust the bun to your desired shape and size. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know the general steps, let's break it down into a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair to keep your curls healthy and defined.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair to help define your curls and keep them in place. You can use a leave-in conditioner, curl cream, or mousse.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to gently dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Gather Your Hair

Follow step 1 from the general steps above to gather your hair at the crown of your head.

Step 5: Tie Your Hair

Follow step 2 from the general steps above to tie your hair in a looped bun.

Step 6: Secure Your Bun

Follow step 3 from the general steps above to secure your bun in place.

Step 7: Adjust Your Bun

Follow step 4 from the general steps above to adjust your bun to your desired shape and size.

Tips for Curly Hair Maintenance

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I tie a man bun with short curly hair?

Yes, you can still tie a man bun with short curly hair. Follow the general steps above, but use a smaller hair tie and adjust the size of your bun accordingly.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's recommended to wash curly hair once or twice a week. This will prevent dryness and maintain the natural oils in your hair.

Can I use hair gel instead of styling product?

While you can use hair gel as a styling product, it's not recommended for curly hair as it can cause crunchiness and stiffness. Opt for a curl cream or mousse instead.

How do I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

To prevent frizz, avoid rubbing your hair with a towel and instead use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to gently dry your hair. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum to your hair after washing.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to tie a man bun with curly hair, it's time to rock that new hairstyle! Remember to follow the tips for maintaining your curly hair and experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
