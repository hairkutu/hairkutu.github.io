Youtube How To Cut Short Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Do you want to try cutting your short hair yourself? Thanks to Youtube, you can learn how to do it from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting short hair yourself with the help of Youtube videos. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hair-cutter, this guide will help you achieve a great haircut every time.

Description

Cutting short hair yourself can be intimidating, but it's also liberating. You get to save money, experiment with new styles, and have complete control over your hair. However, it's important to have the right tools and techniques to ensure a successful cut. Youtube is a great resource for learning how to cut short hair yourself, as there are countless videos available that cater to different hair types and styles.

How to Cut Short Hair Yourself

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. This includes a pair of sharp scissors, hair clips, a comb, and a mirror. Once you have your tools, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it completely. This will help you see the natural texture of your hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into manageable pieces. Start by dividing your hair in half, from ear to ear. Then, divide each section into smaller pieces using the comb.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the back, working your way towards the front. Use your comb to hold your hair in place and cut small sections at a time. Cut your hair at an angle to create layers, or straight across for a blunt cut. Be sure to cut your hair evenly on both sides.

Step 4: Trim the Sides and Back

Once you've cut the top of your hair, you can trim the sides and back. Use your comb to hold your hair in place and cut small sections at a time. Be sure to cut your hair evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments and trim any stray hairs.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once
  • Take your time and be patient, especially if you're new to cutting hair
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you
  • Watch Youtube videos before you start cutting to get a better understanding of the process

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, regular scissors are not sharp enough and can damage your hair. It's important to use sharp, professional hair cutting scissors.

How often should I cut my short hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. Generally, it's recommended to cut short hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

What if I mess up my haircut?

Don't worry, hair grows back! If you're not happy with your haircut, you can always go to a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain a healthy, shiny appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh
  • Avoid over brushing your hair to prevent breakage

With the help of Youtube, cutting short hair yourself has never been easier. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve a great haircut every time. Happy cutting!
