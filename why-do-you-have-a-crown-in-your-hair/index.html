Why Do You Have A Crown In Your Hair

Written by Daniel Apr 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our blog post about why do you have a crown in your hair. In this article, we will discuss the meaning behind having a crown in your hair, how to achieve the perfect crown, and how to take care of your hair once you have a crown. So, let's get started!

Description: What is a Crown?

A crown is a small braid or section of hair that is twisted and positioned at the top of your head. It is typically done in a circular pattern, resembling a crown or halo. A crown can be incorporated into various hairstyles, including updos, braids, and ponytails. It is a popular hairstyle choice for many women and has been worn by celebrities such as Beyonce and Rihanna.

How to: Achieving the Perfect Crown

Creating a crown can be done in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash and condition your hair before starting the crown. This will help your hair to be smooth and easy to manage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Take a section of hair from the top of your head and divide it into three equal parts.

Step 3: Begin Braiding

Start braiding the section of hair by crossing the right strand over the middle strand, then the left strand over the middle strand. As you braid, continue adding hair to each section until you reach the back of your head.

Step 4: Secure the Braid

Once you have reached the back of your head, secure the braid with a hair elastic.

Step 5: Create the Crown

Take the braid and wrap it around your head in a circular pattern. Secure the ends of the braid with bobby pins.

Tips: Taking Care of Your Hair with a Crown

Now that you have a crown, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are a few tips:

Tip 1: Moisturize Your Hair

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to nourish your hair.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair at Night

Wear a silk or satin bonnet or sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage to your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does a crown last?

A: A crown can last for a few days to a week, depending on how well you take care of it.

Q: Can I create a crown on short hair?

A: Yes, you can create a crown on short hair. You may need to use hair extensions or braids to achieve the desired look.

Q: Can I wear a crown every day?

A: Yes, you can wear a crown every day if you choose to. However, it is important to give your hair a break from styling occasionally to prevent damage.

Hair Care: The Importance of Taking Care of Your Hair

Taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining its health and appearance. Regularly washing and conditioning your hair, using protective styles, and avoiding heat styling can help prevent damage and promote hair growth. A healthy diet and lifestyle can also contribute to the overall health of your hair.
