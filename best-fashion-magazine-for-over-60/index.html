Best Fashion Magazine For Over 60 Your Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Robby Mar 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for fashion inspiration, you might be wondering which magazines are worth your time and money. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to figure out which ones are the best fit for you. That's why we've put together this ultimate guide to the best fashion magazine for over 60. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to choose the right magazine to the top picks for this year.

Why Read Fashion Magazines?

Before we dive into the specifics of which fashion magazines to read, let's talk about why they're worth your time. Fashion magazines are a great source of inspiration and ideas, whether you're looking to update your wardrobe or just want to stay up-to-date on the latest trends. They offer a wealth of information on everything from the latest styles to beauty tips and tricks.

How to Choose the Right Magazine

With so many fashion magazines out there, it can be tough to figure out which ones are best suited for your needs. Here are some factors to keep in mind when choosing a magazine:

Your Personal Style

Think about your personal style and what kind of fashion you're interested in. Are you into high-end designer looks or more casual, everyday wear? Do you prefer classic styles or more trendy pieces? Choosing a magazine that reflects your personal taste will make it more enjoyable to read.

Your Age

Some fashion magazines are geared specifically toward younger women, while others cater to a more mature audience. Make sure to choose a magazine that's appropriate for your age group.

Your Budget

Some magazines feature high-end designer looks that may be out of your price range. If you're on a budget, look for magazines that offer more affordable options.

Your Interests

Do you have a particular interest in beauty, travel, or home decor? Some fashion magazines cover these topics as well, so look for one that aligns with your interests.

Top Picks for 2023

1. AARP The Magazine

AARP The Magazine is a great option for those over 60 who are interested in fashion and lifestyle topics. In addition to fashion and beauty advice, the magazine covers health, travel, and finances. With a circulation of over 22 million, it's one of the most widely read magazines in the country.

2. More Magazine

More Magazine is another great choice for women over 60. It features fashion and beauty advice, as well as articles on health, travel, and finance. The magazine also focuses on empowering women and promoting positive aging.

3. Vogue

While Vogue is often associated with younger readers, the magazine also features fashion and beauty advice that can be useful for older women. In particular, the magazine's "Age Issue" features articles on fashion and beauty for women of all ages.

4. Good Housekeeping

Good Housekeeping is a classic magazine that covers a wide variety of topics, including fashion and beauty. The magazine is known for its practical advice and tips, making it a great choice for those looking for actionable advice.

Tips for Reading Fashion Magazines

Now that you've chosen your magazine, here are some tips for getting the most out of your reading experience:

Take Notes

When you come across an outfit or beauty tip that you like, jot it down in a notebook or on your phone. This will help you remember the ideas you want to try out.

Try New Things

Don't be afraid to step outside your comfort zone and try new styles or beauty techniques. You might be surprised at what you end up loving!

Share with Friends

If you have friends who are also interested in fashion and beauty, share your magazines with them or discuss your favorite articles together. It can be a fun way to bond and get new ideas.

FAQs

What if I'm not interested in high-fashion?

Not all fashion magazines focus on high-end designer looks. Look for magazines that cater to more casual or everyday styles.

What if I'm on a budget?

Some magazines offer more affordable options, or you can focus on the articles that offer practical advice for saving money.

What if I don't have access to a physical magazine?

Many fashion magazines offer digital editions that you can read on your computer or tablet. You can also check out fashion blogs or social media accounts for inspiration.

Hair Care for Women Over 60

While fashion magazines can be a great source of inspiration for women over 60, it's also important to take care of your hair. As we age, our hair can become thinner and more brittle, making it more vulnerable to damage. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

Use Gentle Products

Choose shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are designed for mature hair. Look for products that are free of harsh chemicals and sulfates.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can damage hair, especially if it's already thin or brittle. Try to limit your use of these tools, and when you do use them, use a heat protectant spray.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep hair looking healthy. Aim for a trim every six to eight weeks.

Protect from the Sun

Just like our skin, our hair can be damaged by the sun's UV rays. If you're going to be spending time outside, wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF protection.

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to start enjoying your fashion magazines and taking care of your hair. Happy reading and styling!
