What Is Spiky Hair A Complete Guide In 2023

Written by Robby Aug 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Spiky hair is a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by both men and women. Spiky hair is characterized by its short, textured strands that are styled to stand up and away from the scalp. This look can be achieved with the use of styling products such as gel, wax, or pomade. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about spiky hair, including how to achieve the look, tips and tricks, and hair care advice.

What is Spiky Hair?

Spiky hair is a hairstyle that involves styling the hair to stand up and away from the scalp. This look is achieved by cutting the hair short and using styling products to add texture and hold. The spikes can be short and subtle or long and dramatic, depending on the desired look.

How to Achieve the Spiky Hair Look

To achieve the spiky hair look, you will need to follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product such as gel, wax, or pomade to your hair.
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes by pulling small sections of hair up and away from the scalp.
  4. Continue styling until you achieve the desired look.

It is important to note that the amount of product used will depend on the length and thickness of your hair. It is best to start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Tips and Tricks for Styling Spiky Hair

Here are some tips and tricks for styling spiky hair:

  • Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type.
  • Use a hair dryer to set the spikes in place.
  • Experiment with different lengths and textures to find the look that works best for you.
  • Try adding color or highlights to your hair to make the spikes stand out even more.

FAQs About Spiky Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about spiky hair:

Is spiky hair only for men?

No, spiky hair can be worn by both men and women.

What types of products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a variety of products to style your hair, including gel, wax, and pomade.

How long does it take to style spiky hair?

The amount of time it takes to style spiky hair will depend on the length and thickness of your hair and the desired look. On average, it can take anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes.

How often should I wash my hair when styling it spiky?

It is best to wash your hair every other day to prevent the styling product from building up and to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Advice for Spiky Hair

Here are some hair care tips for spiky hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray when using a hair dryer or other styling tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect spiky hair look and keep your hair healthy and looking great.
