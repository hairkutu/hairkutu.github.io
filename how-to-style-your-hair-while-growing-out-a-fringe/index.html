How To Style Your Hair While Growing Out A Fringe

Written by Robby Aug 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you growing out your fringe and struggling with how to style your hair? It can be a frustrating process, but don't worry! We've got you covered with some tips and tricks to help you rock your hair while it grows out. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to style your hair to hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and strong. Keep reading to learn more!

How To

One of the easiest ways to style your hair while growing out a fringe is to pull it back into a ponytail or bun. This will keep your bangs out of your face and allow them to grow out more easily. Another option is to try a side-swept style, where you part your hair to the side and let your bangs blend in with the rest of your hair.

If you're feeling more adventurous, you can try braiding your bangs or twisting them back and securing them with bobby pins. This will add some texture and interest to your hairstyle while keeping your bangs out of the way.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Decide on the style you want to try (ponytail, bun, side-swept, braided, twisted, etc.).

3. Use a comb or brush to smooth your hair back or to the side, depending on the style you've chosen.

4. Secure your hair with a hair tie or bobby pins.

5. Use hairspray or other styling products to keep your hair in place.

Tips

1. Invest in some cute hair accessories, like headbands or clips, to help keep your hair in place and add some flair to your look.

2. Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.

3. Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on how to style your growing out fringe.

FAQ

Q: How often should I trim my bangs while growing them out?

A: It's best to avoid trimming your bangs while growing them out, as this will slow down the process. Instead, try styling them in a way that blends them in with the rest of your hair.

Q: How can I make my hair grow faster?

A: While there's no magic solution for growing your hair faster, there are some things you can do to promote healthy hair growth. Eating a healthy diet, getting enough sleep, and using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner can all help.

Hair Care

While you're growing out your fringe, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it strong and healthy. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

- Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

- Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

Growing out your fringe can be a challenging process, but with the right styling techniques and hair care tips, you can rock your hair while it grows out. Whether you're trying a ponytail, side-swept style, or braided look, experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you. Don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Happy styling!
