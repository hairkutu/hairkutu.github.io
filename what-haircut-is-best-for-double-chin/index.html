What Haircut Is Best For Double Chin Find Out Here

Written by Eveline Sep 13, 2023 Â· 12 min read

Double chin is a common problem faced by many people, especially as they age. It can be caused due to various factors such as genetics, weight gain, and loss of skin elasticity. While there are various ways to address this issue, one of the easiest and most effective ways is to get a haircut that suits your face shape and minimizes the appearance of a double chin. In this article, we will discuss what haircut is best for double chin and provide tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description: What is a Double Chin?

A double chin is a layer of fat that accumulates under the chin, creating the appearance of a second chin. It can be caused by various factors, including genetics, weight gain, and loss of skin elasticity. While there are various ways to address this issue, one of the easiest and most effective ways is to get a haircut that suits your face shape and minimizes the appearance of a double chin.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

Before we discuss the best haircut for a double chin, it's important to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes, including oval, round, heart, square, diamond, and oblong. You can determine your face shape by pulling your hair back and examining the shape of your face in the mirror.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off most hairstyles, including short, medium, and long haircuts. However, it's best to avoid heavy bangs as they can make your face appear shorter.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, it's best to go for a haircut that adds height to your face, such as a long bob or a layered cut. Avoid blunt cuts that stop at your chin as they can make your face appear wider.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, it's best to go for a haircut that adds volume to your chin area, such as a chin-length bob or a layered cut. Avoid short haircuts, as they can make your forehead appear wider.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, it's best to go for a haircut that softens your jawline, such as a layered cut or a bob with soft waves. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline, as they can make your face appear more angular.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, it's best to go for a haircut that adds width to your forehead and chin, such as a layered cut or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheekbones, as they can make your face appear wider.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, it's best to go for a haircut that adds width to your face, such as a layered cut or a shoulder-length bob. Avoid long haircuts that make your face appear longer.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Best Haircut for Double Chin

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the best haircut for your double chin. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you choose a haircut, determine your face shape by pulling your hair back and examining the shape of your face in the mirror.

Step 2: Choose a haircut that suits your face shape

Based on your face shape, choose a haircut that suits you the best. For example, if you have a round face shape, go for a long bob or a layered cut that adds height to your face.

Step 3: Consider your hair texture and thickness

When choosing a haircut, consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have thick hair, avoid short haircuts as they can make your hair appear bulky. If you have fine hair, go for a layered cut that adds volume to your hair.

Step 4: Consult with your hairstylist

Before getting a haircut, consult with your hairstylist. They can provide valuable advice and recommend a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Tips and Tricks to Minimize the Appearance of Double Chin

In addition to getting the right haircut, there are various tips and tricks you can follow to minimize the appearance of a double chin. Here are some of them:

Tip 1: Maintain good posture

Maintain good posture to avoid slouching, which can make your double chin appear more prominent.

Tip 2: Choose the right neckline

Avoid turtlenecks and high-necked tops as they can make your double chin appear more prominent. Instead, opt for V-neck or scoop-neck tops that draw attention away from your chin.

Tip 3: Use makeup to contour your face

Use makeup to contour your face and minimize the appearance of a double chin. Apply a darker shade of foundation or bronzer under your chin and along your jawline to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Tip 4: Exercise regularly

Exercise regularly to maintain a healthy weight and tone your facial muscles. This can help reduce the appearance of a double chin.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can a haircut really minimize the appearance of a double chin?

A: Yes, a haircut can help minimize the appearance of a double chin by drawing attention away from your chin and creating the illusion of a slimmer face.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a double chin?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your chin area, such as blunt cuts that end at your chin. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to your face, such as layered cuts and long bobs.

Q: Can makeup really minimize the appearance of a double chin?

A: Yes, makeup can help contour your face and minimize the appearance of a double chin. Apply a darker shade of foundation or bronzer under your chin and along your jawline to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you've chosen the perfect haircut for your double chin, it's important to maintain it. Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your haircut looking great:

Tip 1: Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

Tip 2: Avoid using heat styling tools too often

Avoid using heat styling tools such as blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.

Tip 3: Get regular trims

Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

Tip 4: Use hair products sparingly

Use hair products such as hairspray, gel, and mousse sparingly as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut can help minimize the appearance of a double chin and make you look and feel your best. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can choose the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair type and maintain it for a flawless look.
