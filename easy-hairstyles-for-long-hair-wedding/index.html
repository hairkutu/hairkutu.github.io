Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair Wedding

Written by Christine Apr 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Weddings are a big occasion where you want to look your best. One of the most important aspects of your look is your hair. If you have long hair, you have a lot of options for hairstyles that can work for your big day. Here are some easy hairstyles for long hair wedding that you can try.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

These easy hairstyles for long hair wedding are perfect for brides who want to look chic and elegant without spending hours in front of the mirror. Whether you prefer a classic updo or a more relaxed, bohemian look, there are plenty of options to choose from. These hairstyles are also great for bridesmaids or guests who want to look their best on the big day.

How to

To achieve these easy hairstyles for long hair wedding, you will need some basic styling tools and products. A curling iron, straightener, bobby pins, and hair elastics are all essential tools. You will also need hairspray, hair serum, and hair oil to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the most popular easy hairstyles for long hair wedding:

1. Classic Updo

  1. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron.
  2. Separate your hair into three sections: top, middle, and bottom.
  3. Tease the top section of your hair to create volume.
  4. Pull the middle section of your hair back and secure it with bobby pins.
  5. Take the bottom section of your hair and twist it into a loose bun, securing it with bobby pins.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to set the style.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Curl your hair with a curling iron.
  2. Take the top section of your hair and tease it to create volume.
  3. Pull the top section of your hair back and secure it with a hair elastic.
  4. Take a small section of hair from underneath the ponytail and wrap it around the hair elastic to hide it.
  5. Spray your hair with hairspray to set the style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect easy hairstyle for long hair wedding:

  • Start with clean, dry hair that has been brushed through to remove any tangles.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles before the big day to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories, like flowers or hairpins, to your hairstyle to make it more special.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for long hair wedding:

What is the best hairstyle for a long hair wedding?

The best hairstyle for a long hair wedding depends on your personal style and the theme of the wedding. Classic updos and half-up half-down styles are both popular choices.

How can I make my long hair look thicker?

You can make your long hair look thicker by using volumizing products, like mousse or dry shampoo, and teasing your hair at the roots.

Hair Care

Good hair care is essential for achieving the perfect easy hairstyle for long hair wedding. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like curling irons and straighteners, too often to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

These easy hairstyles for long hair wedding are perfect for creating a chic and elegant look on your big day. Whether you prefer a classic updo or a more relaxed, bohemian style, there's a hairstyle for every bride. Just remember to start with clean, dry hair and use the right styling tools and products to achieve the perfect look.
