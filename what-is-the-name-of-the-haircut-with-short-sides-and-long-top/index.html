What Is The Name Of The Haircut With Short Sides And Long Top

Written by Christine Jun 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, you might be considering a style with short sides and a long top. This type of haircut has become incredibly popular in recent years, and for good reason. Not only is it stylish and modern, but it can also be customized to suit a variety of different face shapes, hair types, and personal preferences. But what exactly is this haircut called? In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and provide you with all the information you need to know about this popular hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for this Haircut

Description

The haircut with short sides and a long top is a versatile and trendy style that involves cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head very short, while leaving the hair on the top of the head longer. This style can be customized in a variety of ways, with some people opting for a fade or undercut on the sides, while others prefer a more gradual taper. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, spiked up, or left messy for a more casual look.

How to Get the Haircut

If you're interested in getting this popular haircut, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Bring in some pictures of the style you're interested in, and be sure to communicate your preferences clearly to your stylist. They'll be able to recommend the best approach based on your hair type, face shape, and personal preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the haircut with short sides and a long top:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it until it's damp but not soaking wet.
  2. Section off the hair on the top of your head and clip it out of the way.
  3. Using clippers, your stylist will start cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head very short, gradually blending it into the longer hair on top.
  4. Once the sides and back are cut to your desired length, your stylist will move on to the hair on top of your head. They may use scissors or clippers to trim and shape the longer hair, depending on your preferences.
  5. Your stylist will then style your hair according to your preferences, using products like gel, pomade, or wax to create the desired look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to keep in mind if you're considering the haircut with short sides and a long top:

  • Be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style you want. Bring in pictures if necessary.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing the length and style of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims to keep the shape and length looking fresh.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the haircut with short sides and a long top:

Q: What is the name of this haircut?

A: This style is often referred to as an undercut or fade, depending on the length and style of the hair on the sides.

Q: Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style can be adapted to suit a variety of different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim to maintain this haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every four to six weeks to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

Hair Care for this Haircut

To keep your haircut with short sides and a long top looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause it to become brittle.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair product.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the haircut with short sides and a long top with confidence and style.
