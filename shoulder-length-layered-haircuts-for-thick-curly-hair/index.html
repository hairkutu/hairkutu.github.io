Shoulder Length Layered Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair A Guide

Written by Petter Apr 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick curly hair, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that will enhance your curls without overwhelming them or making your hair look too voluminous. That's where shoulder length layered haircuts come in. These styles are perfect for thick curly hair because they add texture and dimension while still maintaining a manageable length. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about shoulder length layered haircuts for thick curly hair.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length layered haircuts are a popular choice for those with thick curly hair because they provide the perfect balance between length and volume. The layers help to add texture and movement to your curls, while the shoulder length keeps your hair from becoming too heavy or unmanageable. These haircuts can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style, making them a versatile option for any curly-haired individual.

How to

The first step in getting a shoulder length layered haircut is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. It's important to work with someone who understands the unique needs of curly hair, as they will be able to create a style that complements your curls and enhances your natural texture. Once you've found a stylist, schedule a consultation to discuss your desired look and any concerns you may have.

Step by step

The process of getting a shoulder length layered haircut will vary depending on your hair type and the specific style you're going for. However, here are the general steps that your stylist will likely follow:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Blow dry your hair or let it air dry
  3. Section your hair into layers
  4. Cut each layer individually, starting with the bottom layer
  5. Blend the layers together for a seamless look
  6. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a shoulder length layered haircut for thick curly hair:

  • Ask your stylist to cut your hair dry, as wet hair can shrink when it dries and result in a shorter cut than you intended
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and enhance your curls
  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to enhance your curls and reduce frizz
  • Invest in a good quality hair serum or oil to keep your curls hydrated and defined

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length layered haircuts for thick curly hair:

What face shape is best suited for this style?

Shoulder length layered haircuts can work for any face shape, but they are especially flattering for those with round or oval faces.

Will this style work for all types of curly hair?

Yes, shoulder length layered haircuts can work for all types of curly hair. However, the specific cut and styling techniques used may vary depending on your hair type and texture.

How often should I get my hair cut to maintain this style?

You should get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and integrity of your shoulder length layered haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length layered haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

With the right haircut and proper care, shoulder length layered haircuts can be a beautiful and manageable option for those with thick curly hair. Talk to your stylist to determine the best cut and styling techniques for your unique hair type and face shape, and enjoy your new look!
