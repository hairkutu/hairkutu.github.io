How To Do 40 S Waves Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a fan of vintage hairstyles? Do you want to try a 40's waves hairstyle but don't know where to start? Look no further, we have got you covered. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this classic look.

Description

The 40's wave hairstyle is a classic hairstyle that was popular during the 1940s. It is a glamorous, elegant, and sophisticated hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion, whether it is a formal event or a casual day out. This hairstyle is characterized by its soft, loose waves that frame the face, creating a feminine and romantic look.

How to

Here are the steps to achieve the 40's waves hairstyle:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add texture and volume to your hair, which is crucial for this hairstyle. Blow-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots. This will help create more volume and hold for your waves.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting from the nape of your neck. Use hair clips to secure each section in place. This will make it easier for you to curl your hair and ensure that all sections are evenly curled.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl each section of your hair away from your face. Make sure to hold the curling iron vertically and wrap the hair around the barrel, starting from the mid-lengths of your hair. Hold the curl for a few seconds before releasing it. Repeat this for all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Brush Out Your Curls

Once you have curled all sections of your hair, use a paddle brush to gently brush out your curls. This will create soft, loose waves and give your hair a more natural look. Make sure to brush your hair gently to avoid frizz or damage.

Step 5: Set Your Hair

Finally, set your hair with a hairspray or a setting spray. This will help hold your waves in place and prevent them from falling flat. Make sure to spray your hair evenly and hold the spray bottle about 12 inches away from your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 40's waves hairstyle:

  • Use a curling iron with a 1-inch barrel to create soft, loose waves.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a paddle brush to gently brush out your curls and create soft waves.
  • Set your hair with a hairspray or a setting spray to hold your waves in place.

FAQ

How long does the 40's waves hairstyle last?

The 40's waves hairstyle can last up to 3 days with proper care and maintenance. Make sure to sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent your waves from getting flattened.

Can I achieve the 40's waves hairstyle on short hair?

Yes, you can achieve the 40's waves hairstyle on short hair. Use a curling iron with a smaller barrel size and focus on curling the ends of your hair to create soft waves.

Can I achieve the 40's waves hairstyle without a curling iron?

Yes, you can achieve the 40's waves hairstyle without a curling iron. Use a flat iron or a hair straightener to create soft waves. Simply twist your hair around the flat iron and pull it downwards to create waves.

Hair Care

To maintain your 40's waves hairstyle, make sure to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and let it air dry as much as possible. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

Now that you know how to achieve the 40's waves hairstyle, go ahead and try it out for yourself. With a little practice, you can master this classic and timeless hairstyle.
