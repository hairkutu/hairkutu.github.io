How To Cut Your Own Short Hair With Clippers

Written by William May 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every few weeks? Or maybe you prefer the convenience of cutting your hair at home. Whatever the reason, cutting your own short hair with clippers is easier than you think. In this article, we’ll provide step-by-step instructions on how to do it, along with tips and FAQs to ensure you get the best results. Let’s get started!

How To

The first step in cutting your own hair with clippers is to gather the necessary tools. You’ll need a set of clippers with different blade guards, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. Once you have everything, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Guard

The guard you choose will determine the length of your hair. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length. For example, if you want your hair to be 1 inch long, start with a 4 guard and then switch to a 3 guard.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section your hair. Start from the back and work your way forward. Use clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start with the longest guard and work your way down. Begin at the back of your head and move the clippers up towards your crown. Use even strokes and go over each section several times to ensure a clean cut.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Back

Once you’ve finished the top, switch to a shorter guard and blend the sides and back. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Step 5: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers or scissors to clean up the neckline. Make sure it’s straight and even.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually.
  • Use even strokes and go over each section several times.
  • Don’t rush – take your time and be patient.
  • Use the mirror to check your progress from different angles.
  • Trim any stray hairs with scissors.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about cutting your own short hair with clippers:

Can I Cut My Hair Wet or Dry?

You can cut your hair wet or dry, but it’s easier to see the length and texture when it’s dry. Wet hair can also clog the clippers, so be sure to dry it thoroughly.

How Often Should I Cut My Hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length and style.

What If I Make a Mistake?

If you make a mistake, don’t panic. Wait a few days for your hair to grow out a bit and try again. You can also visit a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

After you’ve finished cutting your hair, be sure to clean your clippers and scissors. Store them in a safe place until your next haircut. It’s also important to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using conditioner to keep it soft and healthy.

Cutting your own short hair with clippers is a great way to save money and time. With these tips and instructions, you’ll be able to achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
