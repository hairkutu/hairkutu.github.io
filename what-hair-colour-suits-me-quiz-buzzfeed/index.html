What Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz Buzzfeed A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Hair Colour

Written by Robby Mar 24, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and want to switch things up, but not sure which hair colour will suit you best? Look no further than the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz Buzzfeed" quiz! In this article, we'll guide you through how to take the quiz, provide some tips on choosing the right hair colour, and give you some advice on how to take care of your newly coloured locks.

How to Take the Quiz

The "What Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz Buzzfeed" is a fun and easy way to find out which hair colour will suit you best. To take the quiz, simply head to the BuzzFeed website and search for "What Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz." Click on the quiz and answer the questions that follow. The quiz will ask you about your skin tone, eye colour, and other features to determine which hair colour will complement your natural features.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Once you've taken the quiz and have your results, it's time to choose the right hair colour for you. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your skin tone - people with warm undertones look best with warm hair colours like golden blondes and rich browns, while people with cool undertones look best with cool hair colours like ash blondes and cool browns.
  • Think about your eye colour - people with blue or green eyes often look great with cooler hair colours, while people with brown eyes can pull off warmer hair colours.
  • Consider your lifestyle - if you're someone who spends a lot of time in the sun or in chlorine, you may want to choose a hair colour that won't fade quickly.

Step-by-Step Guide to Colouring Your Hair

If you've decided to go ahead and colour your hair, here's a step-by-step guide to follow:

  1. Choose your hair colour - whether you're going to a salon or doing it yourself at home, make sure you have the right hair colour and tools.
  2. Protect your skin - apply a barrier cream or Vaseline around your hairline to prevent the hair dye from staining your skin.
  3. Apply the dye - follow the instructions on the hair dye package and apply the dye evenly to your hair.
  4. Wait - leave the dye on your hair for the recommended amount of time.
  5. Rinse - rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water until the water runs clear.
  6. Condition - apply conditioner to your hair to help restore moisture.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've coloured your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair colour from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often - washing your hair every other day can help preserve your hair colour.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz Buzzfeed" quiz:

  • Is the quiz accurate? While the quiz is a fun way to get some ideas for which hair colours might look good on you, it's important to remember that everyone's unique and there's no one-size-fits-all answer.
  • Can I take the quiz more than once? Yes, you can take the quiz as many times as you like to get different results.
  • Can I share my results on social media? Yes, you can share your results on social media by clicking the share button.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, but with the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz Buzzfeed" quiz, it's easy and fun. Remember to consider your skin tone, eye colour, and lifestyle when choosing a hair colour, and to take care of your new locks properly with colour-safe products and gentle styling tools. Happy colouring!
