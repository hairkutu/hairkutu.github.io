Different Ways To Clip Your Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Apr 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of wearing your hair down all the time? Do you want to try something new but aren't sure where to start? Clipping your hair is a great way to change up your look without committing to a new haircut. In this article, we will explore different ways to clip your hair, from simple styles to more complex ones.

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: Description
  • Section 2: How to
  • Section 3: Step by Step
  • Section 4: Tips
  • Section 5: FAQ
  • Section 6: Hair Care

Description

Clipping your hair can be a fun and easy way to change up your look. Whether you have long, medium, or short hair, there are endless possibilities when it comes to clipping your hair. From simple bobby pins to intricate braids, there is a style for everyone. Not only can clipping your hair add some variety to your daily routine, but it can also keep your hair out of your face on hot summer days or during workouts.

How to

Before you start clipping your hair, it's important to have the right tools. You will need bobby pins, hair ties, a comb, and possibly hairspray or other styling products depending on the style you choose. Once you have your tools, you can start experimenting with different styles.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for a few different hair clipping styles:

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front on one side and twist it back towards the middle of your head.
  3. Secure the twisted section of hair with a bobby pin.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Take the remaining hair and tie it into a ponytail or bun.

Fishtail Braid

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the inside of the right section.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Continue crossing small sections of hair over until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie the end of the braid with a small hair tie.

Top Knot

  1. Gather all of your hair on top of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun shape and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Use hairspray or other styling products to keep the bun in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for clipping your hair:

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color.
  • Don't be afraid to add some volume to your hair before clipping it.
  • Use hairspray or other styling products to keep your hair in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair clipping:

Is clipping your hair damaging?

No, clipping your hair is not damaging as long as you are using the right tools and not pulling your hair too tight.

Can I clip my hair if I have short hair?

Yes, there are many styles that work well with short hair, such as a half-up half-down style or a top knot.

How do I keep my hair from getting tangled when I clip it?

Make sure to brush your hair before clipping it, and avoid using too many bobby pins in one area.


Hair Care

While clipping your hair can be a fun way to change up your look, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

Overall, clipping your hair is a great way to add some variety to your look. With so many different styles to choose from, you're sure to find one that works for you. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful!
