How To Change Colors In Animal Crossing

Written by Eveline Mar 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Animal Crossing is a popular video game where you can create your own character, build a village, and interact with other players. One of the fun things about this game is that you can customize your character's appearance, including changing their hair color, eye color, and skin tone. In this article, we'll focus on how to change colors in Animal Crossing.

How to Change Hair Color in Animal Crossing

Changing your hair color in Animal Crossing is simple. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Go to the Mirror

In your character's house, find the mirror and interact with it.

Step 2: Select "Change Look"

When you interact with the mirror, you'll see a few options. Select "Change Look."

Step 3: Choose Hair Color

From there, you'll be able to choose a new hair color. You can choose from a variety of colors and shades.

Once you've chosen your new hair color, you're all set!

How to Change Eye Color in Animal Crossing

Changing your eye color in Animal Crossing is just as easy as changing your hair color. Here's how:

Step 1: Go to the Mirror

Like before, find the mirror in your character's house and interact with it.

Step 2: Select "Change Look"

Again, select "Change Look" from the options that appear.

Step 3: Choose Eye Color

From there, you'll be able to select a new eye color. There are several colors to choose from, so take your time and find the perfect one for you.

Once you've chosen your new eye color, you're done!

How to Change Skin Tone in Animal Crossing

If you want to change your character's skin tone in Animal Crossing, here's how:

Step 1: Go to the Mirror

As before, find the mirror in your character's house and interact with it.

Step 2: Select "Change Look"

Select "Change Look" from the options that appear.

Step 3: Choose Skin Tone

From there, you'll be able to select a new skin tone. There are several options to choose from, so find the one that suits you best.

Once you've selected your new skin tone, you're finished!

Tips for Changing Colors in Animal Crossing

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your character's colors:

  • Take your time and choose colors you really like.
  • Experiment with different combinations of hair, eye, and skin colors.
  • Remember that you can always change your colors later.
  • Have fun with it!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about changing colors in Animal Crossing:

Can I change my colors anytime?

Yes! You can change your colors as often as you like.

Do I need any special items to change my colors?

No, all you need is a mirror in your character's house.

Can I change my colors in someone else's village?

No, you can only change your colors in your own village.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Finally, it's important to take care of your character's hair in Animal Crossing. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit Harriet's salon to get a haircut or new style.
  • Use a mirror to check your hair and make sure it looks good.
  • Wear a hat to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Try different hair accessories to add some flair to your style.

With these tips, you'll be able to change colors in Animal Crossing and take care of your character's hair like a pro. Have fun and enjoy customizing your character!
