Which Hair Colour Suits Me Test Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Frank Sep 10, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking for a change, but not sure which shade will suit you best? The good news is that there is a simple solution to your dilemma – the "Which Hair Colour Suits Me Test". This test is designed to help you find the perfect hair colour based on your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. In this article, we will explain how to take the test and give you some tips on choosing and maintaining your new hair colour.

Description

The "Which Hair Colour Suits Me Test" is an online quiz that asks you a series of questions about your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour to determine which hair colour will suit you best. The test takes only a few minutes to complete and is usually free. The quiz will give you a range of hair colour options that are best suited for your complexion and features.

How to Take the Test

There are many online sites that offer the "Which Hair Colour Suits Me Test". Simply search for "hair colour quiz" or "Which Hair Colour Suits Me Test" on your preferred search engine and choose a reputable website. Once you have found a website, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Answer the Questions

The quiz will ask you a series of questions about your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. Be sure to answer these questions honestly and to the best of your ability. The more accurate your answers, the more accurate your results will be.

Step 2: Receive Your Results

Once you have completed the quiz, you will receive your results. The quiz will give you a range of hair colour options that are best suited for your complexion and features. Take note of these options and consider them when choosing your new hair colour.

Step 3: Choose Your New Hair Colour

Now that you have your results, it's time to choose your new hair colour. Consider the options that were recommended by the quiz and choose the colour that you feel most comfortable with. If you are unsure, you can always consult a professional hairstylist for advice.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing your new hair colour:

  • Consider your skin tone – warm skin tones look best with warm hair colours, while cool skin tones look best with cool hair colours.
  • Take your natural hair colour into consideration – if you have dark hair, it may take several sessions to achieve a lighter colour.
  • Choose a colour that complements your eye colour – green eyes look great with warm hair colours, while blue eyes look great with cool hair colours.
  • Remember to take into account the upkeep and maintenance of your new hair colour – some colours require more maintenance than others.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "Which Hair Colour Suits Me Test":

Q: Is the quiz accurate?

A: The quiz is designed to give you a range of hair colour options that are best suited for your complexion and features. While the results may not be 100% accurate, they can be a helpful guide when choosing your new hair colour.

Q: Can I take the quiz more than once?

A: Yes, you can take the quiz as many times as you like. If you are unsure about your results, try taking the quiz again and answering the questions differently.

Q: Can I trust the results of an online quiz?

A: While online quizzes can be a helpful guide, it's always best to consult a professional hairstylist for advice before making any drastic changes to your hair.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips for caring for coloured hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your colour from fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hairdryers, straighteners, and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause your colour to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair product.

Now that you know how to take the "Which Hair Colour Suits Me Test", you can confidently choose a new hair colour that will suit you best. Remember to take care of your hair to ensure that your new colour stays vibrant and healthy. Happy colouring!
