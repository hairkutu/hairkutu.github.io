The Most Common Face Shape For Men Everything You Need To Know

Written by Aprilia Jun 26, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're curious about the most common face shape for men, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about the most common face shape for men, including what it is, how to determine your face shape, and tips for taking care of your hair based on your face shape. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • What is the Most Common Face Shape for Men?
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Tips for Hair Care Based on Your Face Shape
  • FAQ

What is the Most Common Face Shape for Men?

The most common face shape for men is the oval face shape. This face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the jaw, with the length of the face being about one and a half times the width. Oval faces also have a rounded jawline and high cheekbones.

Other common face shapes for men include:

  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is easy. Simply stand in front of a mirror and use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The length of your face is measured from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin. Once you have these measurements, compare them to the characteristics of each face shape to determine which one matches your measurements.

If you're still having trouble determining your face shape, there are online tools and apps that can analyze a photo of your face and give you an accurate assessment.

Tips for Hair Care Based on Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, you can use that information to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some tips:

Oval Face Shape

Men with oval face shapes can pull off just about any hairstyle. However, if you want to accentuate your features, try a short back and sides with a longer top. This will draw attention to your high cheekbones and give your face more definition.

Square Face Shape

Men with square faces should avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. Instead, try a style with a bit of length on top and some texture. This will soften the angles of your jawline and give your face a more balanced look.

Round Face Shape

Men with round faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides, as this will make your face appear wider. Instead, try a style with more height on top and some texture. This will elongate your face and give it a more angular look.

Heart-Shaped Face

Men with heart-shaped faces should avoid hairstyles that are too full on top, as this will make your forehead appear even wider. Instead, try a shorter style with some texture on top. This will balance out your features and give your face a more proportional look.

Diamond-Shaped Face

Men with diamond-shaped faces should avoid hairstyles that add width to the forehead or jawline. Instead, try a style with some length on top and shorter sides. This will balance out your features and give your face a more proportional look.

FAQ

Q: Can I have a combination of face shapes?

A: Yes, it is possible to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, choose a hairstyle that flatters the most prominent features of your face.

Q: Can I change the shape of my face with a different hairstyle?

A: No, you cannot change the shape of your face with a different hairstyle. However, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your facial structure.

Q: Do face shapes change over time?

A: No, face shapes do not change over time. However, as you age, your facial features may become more pronounced or less pronounced, which can affect the appearance of your face shape.

Conclusion

Now that you know the most common face shape for men and how to determine your own face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your facial structure. Remember to take care of your hair based on your face shape, and you'll be looking your best in no time!
