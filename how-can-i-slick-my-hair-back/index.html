How Can I Slick My Hair Back

Written by Eveline Aug 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a sleek and sophisticated hairstyle that will turn heads? Look no further than a slicked-back hairdo. This classic style has been around for decades and is still popular today. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this look, step-by-step instructions, tips and tricks, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Your Hair Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Slick Your Hair Back

Slicking your hair back can seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, anyone can do it. The first step is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first for a smoother look.

Tools You'll Need:

  • Comb
  • Hair Gel or Pomade
  • Hair Spray
  • Blow Dryer (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve a sleek, slicked-back hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your palms and rub them together to warm up the product.
  3. Starting at the front of your hairline, use a comb to evenly distribute the product through your hair, working your way back to the crown of your head.
  4. Use the comb to smooth your hair back, making sure to eliminate any bumps or lumps.
  5. If you have cowlicks or unruly hair, use a blow dryer on low heat to help smooth them down.
  6. Once your hair is slicked back, use a small amount of hairspray to hold the style in place.
  7. Finish with a final pass of the comb to make sure everything is in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • For a more natural look, use a light-hold gel or pomade. For a more dramatic look, use a stronger hold product.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you're in a hurry, use a blow dryer on low heat to speed up the drying process.
  • If you have long hair, tie it back into a low ponytail before slicking it back to ensure a smooth finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about slicked-back hairstyles:

How long does a slicked-back hairstyle last?

A slicked-back hairstyle can last all day with the right hair products and techniques. However, it may need a touch-up throughout the day to maintain its sleek appearance.

Can I slick my hair back if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can slick back curly hair. However, you may need to straighten it first for a smoother finish.

What's the difference between hair gel and pomade?

Hair gel is a water-based product that provides a strong hold and a shiny finish. Pomade, on the other hand, is an oil-based product that provides a more natural, matte finish and is easier to restyle throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair from the inside out.

Slicking your hair back is a simple and stylish way to change up your look. With the right tools, techniques, and hair care routine, you can achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle every time.
