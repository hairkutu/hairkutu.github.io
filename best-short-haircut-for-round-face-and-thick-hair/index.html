Best Short Haircut For Round Face And Thick Hair

Written by Petter Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face and thick hair, finding the perfect short haircut can be a challenge. You want to choose a style that flatters your face shape and makes your hair more manageable. In this article, we'll explore the best short haircuts for round faces and thick hair. We'll cover everything from how to choose the right cut to how to care for your hair after the cut.

  2. The Best Short Haircut for Round Face and Thick Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Best Short Haircut for Round Face and Thick Hair

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to choosing the right short haircut for round face and thick hair, there are a few things to keep in mind.

First, you want to choose a cut that flatters your face shape. Round faces look best with cuts that add height to the crown of the head and slim the face.

Second, you want to choose a cut that works with your hair type. Thick hair can be difficult to manage, so you want to choose a cut that makes styling easier.

The Best Short Haircut for Round Face and Thick Hair

The best short haircut for round face and thick hair is the pixie cut. This cut is perfect for round faces because it adds height to the crown of the head and slims the face. It's also a great choice for thick hair because it's easy to style and maintain.

The pixie cut is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. The layers on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and smooth to tousled and messy. This versatility makes the pixie cut a great choice for any occasion.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Best Short Haircut for Round Face and Thick Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the best short haircut for round face and thick hair:

  1. Choose a reputable stylist who has experience cutting thick hair.
  2. Bring pictures of the pixie cut you want to show your stylist.
  3. Discuss your hair type and face shape with your stylist to ensure the cut will flatter your features.
  4. Sit back and relax while your stylist cuts your hair.
  5. Style your hair at home using a volumizing mousse or spray to add height to the crown of your head.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut for Round Face and Thick Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut for round face and thick hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height to the crown of your head.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and definition to your layers.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

What other short haircuts work well for round faces and thick hair?

In addition to the pixie cut, the bob and the lob (long bob) are also great options for round faces and thick hair.

Can I still wear my hair curly with a pixie cut?

Yes! The layers on top of the pixie cut can be styled in a variety of ways, including curly.

Will a pixie cut make my face look too round?

No. The layers on top of the pixie cut add height to the crown of your head, which slims the face.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face and Thick Hair

Here are some hair care tips for round face and thick hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With the right cut and proper care, your round face and thick hair can look amazing in a short haircut. Try the pixie cut for a bold and stylish look that's easy to maintain.
