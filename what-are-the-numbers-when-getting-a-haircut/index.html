What Are The Numbers When Getting A Haircut

Written by Eveline May 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Getting a haircut is one of the most common things people do to take care of their appearance. However, not everyone knows the terminology used by hairstylists. One of the most common questions people ask is, "What are the numbers when getting a haircut?"

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When getting a haircut, the numbers refer to the guard size on the clipper. Clippers are used to cut hair to a specific length. The guard size determines how much hair will be cut. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be. For example, a #1 guard will leave 1/8 inch of hair, while a #8 guard will leave 1 inch of hair.

How To

When getting a haircut, you can tell your stylist what guard size you want. If you're unsure, your stylist can recommend a guard size based on the length you want your hair to be. Some people prefer to have different guard sizes on different parts of their head. For example, they might want a shorter guard on the sides and back and a longer guard on top.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to communicate with your stylist when getting a haircut:

  1. Decide on the length you want your hair to be
  2. Tell your stylist which guard size you want or ask for their recommendation
  3. If you want different guard sizes on different parts of your head, communicate that to your stylist
  4. Relax and let your stylist do their job

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to help your stylist understand your vision
  • Be open to your stylist's recommendations
  • Communicate any concerns or issues you have with your hair
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a haircut:

Q: What's the difference between a clipper and a trimmer?

A: Clippers are used to cut hair to a specific length, while trimmers are used to trim hair around the edges and create clean lines.

Q: Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

A: Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers, but it's recommended to have someone assist you to ensure an even cut.

Q: How do I clean my clippers?

A: Use a brush to remove any hair from the blades, then wipe them down with a disinfectant spray or wipe.

Hair Care

After getting a haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your new style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends

Now that you know what the numbers mean when getting a haircut, you can communicate effectively with your stylist and achieve the hairstyle you want. Remember to take care of your hair after your haircut to maintain your new look.
