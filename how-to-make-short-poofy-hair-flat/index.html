How To Make Short Poofy Hair Flat A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have short poofy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to style it. While some people love the volume and texture that comes with poofy hair, others prefer a sleeker, more polished look. If you fall into the latter category, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll walk you through how to make short poofy hair flat, step-by-step. We'll also provide some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the best results possible.

How to Make Short Poofy Hair Flat

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to note that there are a few different ways to make short poofy hair flat. The method that works best for you may depend on your hair type, the products you have on hand, and your personal preferences. With that said, here are some common techniques for making short poofy hair flat:

  • Blow-drying with a flat brush
  • Using a straightening iron or flat iron
  • Applying a smoothing or straightening cream

In this article, we'll focus on the blow-drying method, as it tends to be the easiest and most accessible for most people.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make short poofy hair flat:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. If your hair is particularly thick or coarse, you may want to apply a smoothing or straightening cream to help tame it.
  2. Section your hair. Divide your hair into sections, using hair clips or ties to keep each section separate.
  3. Blow-dry each section. Using a flat brush, blow-dry each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to keep the brush as close to your scalp as possible to create tension and smooth out any bumps or kinks.
  4. Repeat as needed. Depending on the thickness of your hair, you may need to repeat this process several times to get all of your hair completely dry and flat.
  5. Apply a finishing product. Once your hair is dry and flat, apply a small amount of finishing product, such as hairspray or serum, to help keep it in place.

That's it! With a little patience and practice, you should be able to make short poofy hair flat using this method.

Tips for Making Short Poofy Hair Flat

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results possible:

  • Use a high-quality flat brush. A good flat brush will help you create tension and get your hair as smooth and flat as possible.
  • Invest in a good blow-dryer. A high-quality blow-dryer can make a big difference in how easily you're able to get your hair flat.
  • Be patient. It may take some trial and error to get the hang of this technique, so don't get discouraged if it doesn't work perfectly the first time.
  • Experiment with different products. Depending on your hair type and texture, different smoothing and straightening products may work better for you than others.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making short poofy hair flat:

Can I make short poofy hair flat without heat?

While heat is typically the most effective way to get your hair flat, you can try using a wet brush or detangling brush to smooth out your hair without using heat.

How often should I wash my hair if I want to keep it flat?

Washing your hair too frequently can actually make it poofier and harder to manage. Try washing your hair every 2-3 days, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep it looking fresh.

What kind of finishing product should I use?

This will depend on your personal preferences and hair type. Some people prefer to use a hairspray to hold their hair in place, while others prefer a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Hair Care for Short Poofy Hair

If you have short poofy hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short poofy hair:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy

By following these tips and using the techniques outlined in this article, you should be able to make short poofy hair flat and enjoy a sleek, polished look.
