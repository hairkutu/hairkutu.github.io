Best Haircut For Small Face

Written by William Aug 30, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your small face? Look no further! In this article, we will go through the best haircuts for small faces, how to choose the right haircut, step-by-step guide, tips, FAQ, and hair care for your small face. Let’s get started!

Table of Content:

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Your Small Face
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Small Face

How to Choose the Right Haircut:

Choosing the right haircut for your small face can be tricky. You want to find a haircut that will flatter your face shape and enhance your best features. Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Consider your face shape - If you have a small face, you want to avoid haircuts that will make your face look even smaller. Instead, opt for haircuts that add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Think about your hair texture - If you have thin hair, you want to avoid haircuts that will make your hair look even thinner. Instead, choose haircuts that add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider your lifestyle - If you have a busy lifestyle, you want to choose a haircut that is easy to maintain and style.
  • Consult with your hairstylist - Your hairstylist can help you choose the right haircut for your face shape and hair texture.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut, let’s take a look at the best haircuts for small faces:

1. Blunt Bob

The blunt bob is a classic haircut that looks great on small faces. This haircut is short and chic, adding volume and texture to your hair. To achieve the blunt bob, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to chin length.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and sassy haircut that looks great on small faces. This haircut is easy to maintain and style, making it perfect for busy lifestyles. To achieve the pixie cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving it longer on top.

3. Layered Lob

The layered lob is a longer version of the bob. This haircut is perfect for small faces because it adds volume and texture to your hair. To achieve the layered lob, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to shoulder length and add layers throughout.

Tips for Your Small Face:

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight - Tight hairstyles can make your face look even smaller.
  • Use volumizing products - Volumizing products can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller.
  • Experiment with different styles - Don’t be afraid to try new hairstyles and experiment with different looks.

FAQ:

What is the best haircut for a small face?

The best haircut for a small face is a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. The blunt bob, pixie cut, and layered lob are all great haircuts for small faces.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a small face?

You should avoid hairstyles that are too tight or hairstyles that make your face look even smaller.

Hair Care for Small Face:

Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly - Washing your hair regularly will keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good conditioner - Using a good conditioner will keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat tools - Heat tools can damage your hair, so it’s best to avoid them if possible.
  • Get regular trims - Getting regular trims will keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best haircut for small face, it’s time to book your appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut. With the right haircut and hair care, you can look and feel your best!
