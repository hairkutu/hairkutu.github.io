Which Haircut Is Best For Thin Hair With Round Face

Written by Aprilia May 23, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of dealing with thin hair and a round face? Finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge, but there are plenty of options that can flatter your features and make your hair appear fuller. In this article, we’ll explore the best haircuts for thin hair with a round face and provide tips on how to care for and style your new ‘do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step: Getting Your Haircut
  • Tips for Styling Thin Hair with a Round Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thin Hair with a Round Face

How to Choose the Right Haircut

The key to choosing the right haircut for thin hair and a round face is to create the illusion of volume and length. Opt for styles that add height at the crown of your head and provide movement and texture throughout your hair. Some of the best haircuts for thin hair with a round face include:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that can work wonders for thin hair. With its tapered sides and voluminous top, it can provide the illusion of height and fullness. Plus, it’s low maintenance and easy to style.

2. Bob

A classic bob can be a great option for those with thin hair and a round face. Opt for a chin-length cut with layers that provide movement and texture. This can help create the illusion of volume and make your hair appear thicker.

3. Long Layers

If you have longer hair, consider adding layers throughout the length of your hair. This can provide movement and texture, while also creating the illusion of length. Make sure to avoid blunt cuts, as these can make your hair appear thinner.

Step-by-Step: Getting Your Haircut

Once you’ve chosen the perfect haircut for your thin hair and round face, it’s time to head to the salon. Follow these steps to ensure you get the cut you want:

1. Do Your Research

Before heading to the salon, do your research and find inspiration photos of the cut you want. This can help you communicate your desired look to your stylist.

2. Communicate with Your Stylist

When you arrive at the salon, make sure to communicate with your stylist about your desired cut. Be clear about the length and style you want, and ask for their professional opinion on what will work best for your hair type and face shape.

3. Follow Up with Styling Tips

After your cut, ask your stylist for tips on how to style your hair at home. They can recommend products and techniques that can help you achieve your desired look.

Tips for Styling Thin Hair with a Round Face

Styling thin hair with a round face can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can make your hair appear fuller and more voluminous. Here are some tips to help you style your new ‘do:

1. Blow Dry with a Round Brush

Blow drying your hair with a round brush can help create volume and movement. Focus on the roots and crown of your head, and use a volumizing mousse or spray for added texture.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make all the difference when it comes to styling thin hair. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners, as well as lightweight styling products that won’t weigh your hair down.

3. Avoid Over-Brushing

While brushing your hair can help distribute oils and create shine, over-brushing can actually make your hair appear thinner. Instead, focus on using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a round face and thin hair?

A: Yes! Wispy, side-swept bangs can be a great addition to a haircut for those with thin hair and a round face. Just make sure to ask your stylist for a cut that adds texture and movement to your bangs.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. A well-chosen short haircut can actually create the illusion of length and balance out a round face shape.

Hair Care for Thin Hair with a Round Face

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, voluminous hair. Here are some tips for caring for thin hair with a round face:

1. Wash Your Hair Less Often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it appear thinner. Aim to wash your hair no more than every other day.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

When using heated styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and breakage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make thin hair appear even thinner. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can make thin hair with a round face look voluminous and full. Follow these tips and tricks to get the most out of your new ‘do!
