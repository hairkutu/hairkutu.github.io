Short Hairstyles For Fine Hair Over 40 With Glasses

Written by Frank Jul 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your fine hair and complements your glasses? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with some of the best short hairstyles for fine hair over 40 with glasses. We will also provide you with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

As we age, our hair starts to lose its thickness and volume. Fine hair can be a challenge to style, especially when you wear glasses. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can still rock a short hairstyle with your glasses.

How-to

The first step in achieving the perfect short hairstyle for fine hair over 40 with glasses is to find a stylist who specializes in this type of hair. They will be able to recommend the best style that suits your face shape and glasses.

Once you have found the right stylist, you can start by considering some of the best short hairstyles for fine hair:

1. Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that suits all face shapes. It is a short haircut that falls just below the chin and can be styled in different ways, such as straight or wavy. It is also easy to maintain and style with glasses.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and edgy haircut that requires minimal styling. It is a low-maintenance style that can be customized to suit your face shape and glasses. It is also great for highlighting your facial features and giving you a youthful look.

3. Layered Cut

The layered cut is a versatile and easy-to-style haircut that works well with fine hair. It adds volume and texture to your hair and can be styled in different ways. It is also a great option for those who want a short haircut that is not too drastic.

Step-by-Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you style your short hair with glasses:

1. Shampoo and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Fine hair needs a lightweight conditioner that does not weigh it down.

2. Towel Dry

Pat your hair dry with a towel, but do not rub it as this can damage your hair. Leave it slightly damp.

3. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to add volume and texture. Use a small amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

4. Blow Dry

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Use a low heat setting and do not over-dry your hair as this can damage it.

5. Style with Glasses

Finally, style your hair with your glasses on to ensure that it complements your face shape and glasses. You can use a styling cream or hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle:

- Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it in shape. - Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools. - Avoid using too much product as this can weigh down your hair. - Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid using a brush that can break your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for fine hair over 40 with glasses:

1. Can I still wear bangs with glasses?

Yes, you can still wear bangs with glasses. However, it is important to consider your face shape and glasses when choosing the right type of bangs. Consult with your stylist to find the best style for you.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Fine hair can be washed every other day, but if you have an active lifestyle or sweat a lot, you may need to wash it more frequently.

3. Can I still wear my glasses with a pixie cut?

Yes, you can still wear your glasses with a pixie cut. In fact, a pixie cut can complement your glasses and make them stand out.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. - Avoid using hot styling tools too often. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. - Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or scarf. - Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals.

By following these tips and using the right haircut and styling techniques, you can rock a short hairstyle with your glasses and look fabulous!
