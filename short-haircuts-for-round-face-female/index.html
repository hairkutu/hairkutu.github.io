Short Haircuts For Round Face Female A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Apr 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a round-faced female looking for a new haircut, you have come to the right place! Short haircuts are a great option for round faces as they can help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. In this guide, we will go over some of the best short haircuts for round face females, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair after the cut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Short Haircut for Round Face Female
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Round Face Females
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Round Face Females

  • Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Round Face Females

How to Choose a Short Haircut for Round Face Female

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it is important to consider the shape of your face and the features you want to highlight or downplay. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for haircuts that add height and volume to the crown of your head, such as pixie cuts or layered bobs.
  • Avoid haircuts that end at chin level or shorter, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs or layers to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes.
  • Avoid blunt cuts or harsh angles, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Short Haircut Ideas for Round Face Females

Here are some short haircut ideas that are perfect for round face females:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Layered bob with side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical bob with side-swept bangs
  • Long pixie cut with textured layers
  • Short shag haircut with choppy layers

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Round Face Females

Styling short haircuts for round face females is easy with the right tools and products. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  4. If you have bangs, blow-dry them to the side using a small round brush.
  5. Use a straightener or curling iron to add texture to your hair.
  6. Finish with a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Round Face Females

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle between washes.

FAQs about Short Haircuts for Round Face Females

1. Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

No, if you choose the right short haircut for your face shape, it can actually help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

2. Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

Yes, there are many ways to style short hair up, such as with bobby pins, hair clips, or hair ties. You can also experiment with braids or twists to add some texture to your updo.

3. How often should I wash my short hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Some people can go a few days between washes, while others may need to wash their hair every day. Use your best judgment and listen to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Round Face Females

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your short haircut looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid pulling or breaking it.

With these tips and ideas, you can confidently choose the perfect short haircut for your round face and keep it looking fabulous all year round!
