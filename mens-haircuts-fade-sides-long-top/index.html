The Ultimate Guide To Men s Haircuts Fade Sides Long Top

Written by Daniel Apr 05, 2023 · 9 min read

If you’re looking for a versatile and trendy haircut, then you should consider getting a men’s haircut fade sides long top. This style features a short or shaved fade on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. It’s a popular hairstyle that has been around for a while and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this guide, we’ll show you everything you need to know about this style, from how to get it to how to maintain it.

Description

The men’s haircut fade sides long top is a modern hairstyle that combines short sides and a longer top. The hair on the sides and back is cut short, usually with a clipper, and gradually fades into longer hair on top. The top can be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked-back, messy, or textured. This hairstyle is versatile and can be tailored to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Get the Men’s Haircut Fade Sides Long Top

If you want to get the men’s haircut fade sides long top, the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. Once you’ve found someone you trust, here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to decide how short you want the sides and back to be. The fade can be high, medium, or low, depending on your preference. The top can be left long, or you can ask your barber to trim it to a specific length.

Step 2: Consult with Your Barber

Before your barber starts cutting your hair, make sure to consult with them about the style you want. Show them pictures of the men’s haircut fade sides long top that you like and ask for their opinion on what would work best for your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Get the Cut

Once you’ve agreed on the style, your barber will start cutting your hair. They will use clippers to create the fade on the sides and back, gradually blending the shorter hair into the longer hair on top. They may also use scissors to trim the top to your desired length and style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After the cut, your barber will style your hair using products such as pomade, wax, or gel. They may also blow-dry your hair to create volume or texture. Once you’re happy with the style, you can take a few selfies and show off your new look.

Tips for Maintaining the Men’s Haircut Fade Sides Long Top

Now that you have your new haircut, it’s essential to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you:

Tip 1: Schedule Regular Haircuts

To keep your haircut looking fresh, make sure to schedule regular haircuts every four to six weeks. This will help to maintain the shape of the fade and prevent the top from getting too long.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential to maintain the men’s haircut fade sides long top. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Use styling products such as pomade, wax, or gel to create your desired style.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

The men’s haircut fade sides long top is a versatile style that can be styled in many ways. Experiment with different styles, such as slicked-back, messy, or textured, to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

What face shape is best for the men’s haircut fade sides long top?

The men’s haircut fade sides long top can work for different face shapes, but it’s best for oval or square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid this style as it can make your face look wider.

Can I style my hair differently with the men’s haircut fade sides long top?

Yes, you can style your hair in many ways with the men’s haircut fade sides long top. Experiment with different styles, such as slicked-back, messy, or textured, to find the one that suits you best.

Can I maintain the men’s haircut fade sides long top at home?

While it’s best to have a professional cut and style your hair, you can maintain the men’s haircut fade sides long top at home. Use the right products and follow the tips we’ve provided to keep your haircut looking its best.

Hair Care for Men’s Haircut Fade Sides Long Top

Keeping your hair healthy and moisturized is essential to maintain the men’s haircut fade sides long top. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished. Use styling products such as pomade, wax, or gel to create your desired style. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or straightening, as this can damage your hair.

In conclusion, the men’s haircut fade sides long top is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can be tailored to suit different face shapes and hair types. Follow the tips and advice we’ve provided to maintain your haircut and keep it looking its best. With the right care and styling, you’ll be rocking this style for years to come.
