How Can You Shape Your Face A Guide To Facial Contouring In 2023

Written by Daniel Apr 13, 2023 · 5 min read

Facial contouring has been a popular trend for years now, and it doesn't seem to be going away anytime soon. If you're curious about how you can shape your face to achieve a more defined look, you've come to the right place. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to contour your face, as well as some tips and tricks to keep in mind. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Contour Your Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care and Facial Contouring

How to Contour Your Face

Facial contouring is the process of using makeup to create shadows and highlights on your face to enhance your natural bone structure. By doing so, you can create the illusion of a more defined jawline, cheekbones, and even a slimmer face. However, before you start contouring, you need to determine your face shape.

Face Shapes

There are six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Long

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start contouring.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to shape your face:

  1. Start by applying your foundation.
  2. Use a bronzer to create shadows where you want to define your face. Typically, this is along the hairline, under the cheekbones, and along the jawline.
  3. Next, use a highlighter to bring out the high points of your face. This is typically on the top of the cheekbones, the bridge of the nose, and the cupid's bow.
  4. Blend everything together using a blending brush or sponge.
  5. Finish with a setting powder to keep everything in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Less is more. It's easier to add more product than to take it away.
  • Use the right tools. A blending brush or sponge is essential for achieving a seamless, natural look.
  • Choose the right shades. Your bronzer and highlighter should be about two shades darker and lighter than your natural skin tone, respectively.
  • Blend, blend, blend. Blending is key to achieving a natural, seamless look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about facial contouring:

Is facial contouring only for women?

No, anyone can contour their face regardless of gender.

Do I need expensive products to contour my face?

No, you can achieve a great contour using drugstore products. It's all about technique.

Can facial contouring damage my skin?

No, as long as you're using makeup products that are safe for your skin type and removing them properly, facial contouring should not damage your skin.

Hair Care and Facial Contouring

Your hairstyle can also play a role in shaping your face. For example, a long bob can help elongate a round face, while a side-swept fringe can soften a square jawline. Consider consulting with a hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape.

In conclusion, facial contouring is a great way to enhance your natural bone structure and achieve a more defined look. By following these tips and tricks, you can contour your face like a pro. Happy contouring!
