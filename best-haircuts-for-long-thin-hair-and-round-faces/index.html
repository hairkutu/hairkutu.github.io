Best Haircuts For Long Thin Hair And Round Faces

Written by Eveline Jul 05, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect haircut for your long thin hair and round face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best haircuts for long thin hair and round faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Long thin hair and round faces can be a challenging combination to work with when it comes to choosing a haircut. However, with the right cut, you can create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face. It’s important to choose a haircut that compliments your face shape while also adding volume and texture to your hair.

How To

Before choosing a haircut, it’s important to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, you want to avoid haircuts that add width to your face. Instead, opt for haircuts that create the illusion of length.

Best Haircuts for Long Thin Hair and Round Faces:

  • Long Layers:

    • Long layers are a great option for adding volume and texture to long thin hair. They also help to elongate the face while adding movement and dimension to your hair.

  • Side Swept Bangs:

    • Side swept bangs are a great option for round faces as they help to create the illusion of a longer face. They also add volume and texture to your hair.

  • Bob:

    • A long bob is a great option for long thin hair and round faces. It helps to elongate the face while adding volume and texture to your hair.

  • Layers with Bangs:

    • Layers with bangs are a great option for long thin hair and round faces. They help to frame the face and add volume and texture to your hair.

Step By Step

When getting a haircut, it’s important to communicate with your stylist to ensure you get the desired cut. Here are some steps to follow:

  • Research different haircuts and styles that would work for your face shape and hair type.
  • Bring pictures to your stylist to show them what you’re looking for.
  • Discuss with your stylist what will work best for your face shape and hair type.
  • Ask your stylist for tips on how to style your hair at home.

Tips

  • Use volumizing products to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt cuts as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Try adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to add volume and texture.

FAQs

What haircuts should I avoid with a round face?

Avoid haircuts that add width to your face such as blunt cuts, short layers, and straight bangs.

What are some haircuts that work well for long thin hair and round faces?

Long layers, side swept bangs, bobs, and layers with bangs are all great options for long thin hair and round faces.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and haircut options, you can finally find the perfect haircut for your long thin hair and round face. Remember to communicate with your stylist to ensure you get the desired cut and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and voluminous.
