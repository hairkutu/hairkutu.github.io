Long Up Top Short On Sides Haircut

Written by Aprilia May 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, then the long up top short on sides haircut might just be the perfect option for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and it's not hard to see why. It allows you to have a lot of length on the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short and sleek. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Description

The long up top short on sides haircut is a timeless and stylish look that has been around for decades. It involves leaving the hair on the top of the head long, while keeping the sides and back short. This creates a striking contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides. The length on top can vary depending on personal preference, but typically it's around 3-6 inches.

How to

The long up top short on sides haircut can be achieved by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose your desired length on top

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal style and how much length you feel comfortable with. You can experiment with different lengths to see what works best for you.

Step 2: Determine the length of the sides and back

The next step is to determine how short you want the sides and back to be. This can also vary depending on your personal preference. The sides and back can be shaved, buzzed, or cut with scissors.

Step 3: Get the cut

Once you have decided on the length of the top, sides, and back, it's time to get the cut. It's best to go to a professional stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your long up top short on sides haircut:

- Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished. - Style your hair with a pomade or wax to create a textured and sleek look. - Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy. - Experiment with different styles, such as a slicked-back look or a messy, textured look.

FAQ

Is the long up top short on sides haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How long does it take to style this haircut?

The styling time will vary depending on the length and texture of your hair. On average, it takes about 5-10 minutes to style this haircut.

Can I still wear a hat with this haircut?

Yes, you can still wear a hat with this haircut. However, it's recommended to use a hat that doesn't flatten your hair too much.

Hair Care

To keep your long up top short on sides haircut looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. - Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too frequently as they can damage your hair. - Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and shiny. - Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

Overall, the long up top short on sides haircut is a classic and stylish look that can work for anyone. With the right styling and hair care, this haircut can look great for years to come.
