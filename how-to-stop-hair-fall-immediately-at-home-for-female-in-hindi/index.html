How To Stop Hair Fall Immediately At Home For Female In Hindi Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Apr 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Hair fall is a common problem faced by both men and women, but it can be especially distressing for women. If you're experiencing hair fall and want to know how to stop it immediately at home, then you're in the right place. In this article, we'll discuss some effective tips and tricks to prevent hair fall in women, all in Hindi.

How to Stop Hair Fall Immediately at Home for Female in Hindi

Here are some effective ways to stop hair fall at home:

1. Onion Juice

Onion juice is a natural remedy that can help in preventing hair fall. It contains sulfur, which helps in promoting hair growth.

To use onion juice, take a few slices of onion and extract its juice. Apply the juice to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is another natural remedy that can help in preventing hair fall. It contains enzymes that can help in promoting hair growth.

To use aloe vera, extract its gel and apply it on your scalp. Leave it on for 45 minutes and then rinse your hair with a mild shampoo.

3. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a popular remedy for preventing hair fall. It contains lauric acid, which helps in nourishing the hair follicles and promoting hair growth.

To use coconut oil, warm it up and apply it on your scalp. Massage your scalp for a few minutes and leave it on overnight. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo in the morning.

4. Egg Mask

Egg mask is a great remedy for preventing hair fall. It contains protein, which helps in promoting hair growth.

To make an egg mask, mix one egg with a tablespoon of olive oil and honey. Apply the mixture on your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to stop hair fall immediately at home for female:

  1. Choose a natural remedy that works for you, such as onion juice, aloe vera, coconut oil, or egg mask.
  2. Prepare the remedy according to the instructions.
  3. Apply the remedy on your scalp and hair.
  4. Leave it on for the recommended time.
  5. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and hair sprays.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Reduce stress levels by practicing yoga or meditation.
  • Get enough sleep to allow your body to repair itself.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair.

FAQs

Q. How much hair fall is normal?

A. It is normal to lose between 50 and 100 hairs per day.

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. It can disrupt the hair growth cycle and lead to hair fall.

Q. How long does it take for hair to grow back?

A. It can take between 3 and 6 months for hair to grow back.

Hair Care Routine

Having a regular hair care routine can help in preventing hair fall. Here's a simple routine you can follow:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo every other day.
  • Apply a conditioner to the ends of your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as hair straighteners and curling irons.
  • Brush your hair gently with a wide-toothed comb.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can stop hair fall immediately at home for female in Hindi. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you'll soon see results.
