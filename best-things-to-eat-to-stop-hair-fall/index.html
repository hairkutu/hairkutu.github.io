Best Things To Eat To Stop Hair Fall

Written by Eveline May 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you experience hair fall? It can be frustrating to see your hair strands scattered everywhere, and it can be even more frustrating to find the right solution. While there are various hair fall treatments available in the market, did you know that certain foods can help prevent hair fall? Yes, you heard it right! In this article, we will discuss the best things to eat to stop hair fall.

Table of Contents

  • How to Stop Hair Fall
  • Best Things to Eat to Stop Hair Fall
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Stop Hair Fall

Before we dive into the best things to eat to stop hair fall, let's understand some basic tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair too tight
  • Do not wash your hair too frequently
  • Massage your scalp regularly with oil
  • Eat a balanced diet

Best Things to Eat to Stop Hair Fall

Now that we know some basic tips to prevent hair fall, let's look at some of the best things to eat to stop hair fall:

Eggs

Eggs are an excellent source of protein, which is essential for healthy hair growth. Hair is made up of protein, and a lack of protein can cause hair fall. Eggs also contain biotin, which is essential for healthy hair growth. So, make sure to include eggs in your diet to prevent hair fall.

Spinach

Spinach is an excellent source of iron, which is essential for healthy hair growth. Iron helps carry oxygen to the hair follicles, which is necessary for hair growth. A lack of iron can cause hair fall, so make sure to include spinach in your diet.

Salmon

Salmon is an excellent source of omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for healthy hair growth. Omega-3 fatty acids help keep the hair hydrated, which prevents hair fall. So, make sure to include salmon in your diet to prevent hair fall.

Walnuts

Walnuts are an excellent source of vitamin E, which is essential for healthy hair growth. Vitamin E helps improve blood circulation to the scalp, which is necessary for hair growth. So, make sure to include walnuts in your diet to prevent hair fall.

Yogurt

Yogurt is an excellent source of probiotics, which help improve gut health. A healthy gut is necessary for healthy hair growth. Yogurt also contains vitamin B5, which helps improve blood flow to the scalp, which is necessary for hair growth. So, make sure to include yogurt in your diet to prevent hair fall.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair fall:

1. What causes hair fall?

Hair fall can be caused by various factors, including poor nutrition, stress, hormonal changes, genetics, and certain medical conditions.

2. How can I prevent hair fall?

You can prevent hair fall by eating a balanced diet, avoiding harsh chemicals on your hair, and taking care of your scalp.

3. How long does it take for hair fall to stop?

The time it takes for hair fall to stop can vary depending on the cause of hair fall. However, with proper care and a healthy diet, you can see results in a few months.

Hair Care

Along with eating the right foods, taking care of your hair is essential for preventing hair fall. Here are some tips to take care of your hair:

  • Use a mild shampoo to wash your hair
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Use a good quality conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after washing
  • Avoid using hair dryers and let your hair air dry

So, these were some of the best things to eat to stop hair fall. Remember, a balanced diet and proper hair care can go a long way in preventing hair fall. If you are experiencing severe hair fall, it is always best to consult a dermatologist.
