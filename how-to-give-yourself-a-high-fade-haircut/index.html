How To Give Yourself A High Fade Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a haircut every few weeks? Why not save some money and give yourself a high fade haircut at home? With the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can achieve the perfect fade in your own bathroom. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through the process of giving yourself a high fade haircut, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You’ll Need
  • Preparation
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Blending Your Hair
  • Cleaning Up
  • Tips for a Perfect High Fade Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Tools You’ll Need

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Clippers with various guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Barber cape or towel
  • Mirror

Preparation

Start by washing and towel-drying your hair. Make sure it’s completely dry before you start cutting. Put on your barber cape or towel to protect your clothes from hair clippings. If you have longer hair, use hair clips to section off the top portion of your hair, leaving the sides and back exposed.

Cutting Your Hair

Begin by selecting the guard size you want to start with. The higher the guard number, the longer the hair will be. We recommend starting with a larger guard and working your way down to achieve the perfect fade. Use the clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair, moving in an upward motion. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Once you’ve finished cutting with the first guard, switch to a smaller guard and repeat the process, starting slightly lower than where you left off with the larger guard. Continue to switch to smaller guards and work your way down until you reach the desired length for the fade.

Blending Your Hair

Now it’s time to blend your hair. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs at the top of your head, being careful not to cut too much off. Then, use the clippers without a guard to blend the sides and back into the top portion of your hair. Use a sweeping motion with the clippers, moving from the bottom up to create a seamless blend.

Cleaning Up

Once you’re finished cutting and blending, it’s time to clean up. Use the clippers to trim any stray hairs around your ears and neckline. Then, take a shower to rinse off any hair clippings and shampoo your hair to remove any remaining loose hair.

Tips for a Perfect High Fade Haircut

  • Take your time – rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down to achieve the perfect fade.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.
  • Be patient when blending – it takes time to achieve a seamless fade.

FAQ

Q: How often should I give myself a high fade haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the fade grows out. Generally, you should aim to give yourself a high fade haircut every 2-3 weeks.

Q: Can I give myself a high fade haircut if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, but it may be more difficult to achieve a seamless blend. We recommend using a smaller guard size to start and taking your time when blending.

Hair Care Tips

After giving yourself a high fade haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your high fade haircut.

Now that you know how to give yourself a high fade haircut, you can save time and money by doing it at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to achieve the perfect fade. Happy cutting!
