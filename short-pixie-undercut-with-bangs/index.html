Short Pixie Undercut With Bangs A Bold And Stylish Look

Written by Bardi Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle, a short pixie undercut with bangs might be just what you need. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd and make a statement with their hair. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve this look, some tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

A short pixie undercut with bangs is a hairstyle where the sides and back of the hair are shaved or cut very short, while the top is left longer. The bangs are usually cut straight across the forehead and can be styled in a variety of ways. This look is often associated with a punk rock or edgy aesthetic, but it can also be styled in a more classic or sophisticated way.

How to Achieve a Short Pixie Undercut with Bangs

To achieve this look, you'll need to visit a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting short hair. Here are the steps they will likely take:

Step 1: Cut the Sides and Back

The stylist will use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back of your hair very short or shave it completely. This will create the undercut effect.

Step 2: Cut the Top

The stylist will leave the top of your hair longer, usually around 2-3 inches. They may use scissors or a razor to create texture and movement.

Step 3: Cut the Bangs

The stylist will cut your bangs straight across your forehead. You can choose to have them cut bluntly or with texture.

Step 4: Style the Hair

You can style your hair in a variety of ways, depending on the look you want to achieve. You can use pomade or wax to create texture and hold, or you can blow-dry your hair for a sleeker look.

Tips for Maintaining a Short Pixie Undercut with Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and stylish:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray if you frequently use hot tools to style your hair.

FAQ

What face shapes are best suited for a short pixie undercut with bangs?

A short pixie undercut with bangs can work for a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Can I still wear accessories like headbands or barrettes with this hairstyle?

Absolutely! In fact, a bold headband or statement barrette can be a great way to add some extra style to your look.

Is this hairstyle low-maintenance?

While the short length of the hair may make it seem like a low-maintenance option, keep in mind that you will need frequent trims and touch-ups to maintain the undercut and keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important, especially if you've recently undergone a dramatic hair transformation like a short pixie undercut with bangs. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too frequently.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

A short pixie undercut with bangs is a bold and stylish look that can work for anyone who wants to make a statement with their hair. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your hairstyle looking fresh and chic.
