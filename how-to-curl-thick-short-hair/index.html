How To Curl Thick Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 04, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have thick short hair, you may think that curling it is impossible or difficult. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that will last all day. In this guide, we’ll show you how to curl your thick short hair in a few easy steps.

Description

Thick short hair can be challenging to style, but curling it can add volume and texture. However, curling thick hair requires more heat and time than other hair types. It’s essential to use the right tools and products to protect your hair from damage and ensure a long-lasting curl.

How to Curl Thick Short Hair

Here are the steps to curl your thick short hair:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant product. Towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, divide your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl. Use hair clips to secure the sections you’re not working on.

Step 3: Heat Your Curling Iron

Heat your curling iron to a temperature that’s suitable for your hair type. For thick hair, use a high heat setting.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the hair on the iron for 10-15 seconds, then release it. Repeat this process with each section of hair until your entire head is curled.

Step 5: Finish Your Style

Once your hair is curled, use a hairbrush or your fingers to loosen the curls and create volume. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Curling Thick Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when curling your thick short hair:

  • Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel, as they distribute heat evenly and reduce the risk of damage.
  • Apply a heat protectant product before curling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Start curling at the back of your head and work your way to the front to avoid burning your face or neck.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions.
  • Use a hairspray with a flexible hold to keep your curls in place without making them stiff or crunchy.

FAQ

How long will my curls last?

Your curls should last all day if you use a hairspray with a flexible hold and avoid touching your hair too much.

Can I curl my hair if it’s wet?

No, you should always curl your hair when it’s dry to avoid damage and ensure a long-lasting curl.

What should I do if my curls fall out quickly?

You may need to use a stronger hold hairspray or curl smaller sections of hair to achieve a longer-lasting curl.

Hair Care for Thick Short Hair

Thick short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Curling your thick short hair may seem intimidating, but with these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that will last all day. Remember to use the right tools and products, and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
