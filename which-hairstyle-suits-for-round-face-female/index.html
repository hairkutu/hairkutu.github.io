Which Hairstyle Suits For Round Face Female

Are you a round face female and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits you? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you on which hairstyle will complement your round face shape and enhance your natural beauty. Here is a table of content for easy navigation:

Description

A round face shape typically means that your face is equal in width and height, with soft and rounded features. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that add height and angle to your face, creating the illusion of length.

When considering hairstyles for round face females, it is important to keep in mind that bangs and layers are your friend. These features can help elongate your face and draw attention to your best features.

How to

Before deciding on a specific hairstyle, it is important to determine your face shape correctly. You can do this by using a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all relatively equal, then you have a round face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, consider these hairstyles that suit round face females:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add dimension and texture to your hair, creating the illusion of length. Make sure to ask your stylist to add face-framing layers to accentuate your best features.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can also elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes. They can be paired with any length of hair, from short to long.

3. Bob with Angled Layers

A bob with angled layers can add height and angles to your face, creating a more oval appearance. This hairstyle is also easy to maintain and style.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect hairstyle for round face females:

1. Long Layers

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair
  2. Part your hair down the middle
  3. Ask your stylist to add long layers, starting at your collarbone
  4. Ask your stylist to add face-framing layers, starting at your cheekbones
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style

2. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair
  2. Part your hair on one side
  3. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the middle of your forehead and ending at your cheekbones
  4. Blow-dry your bangs to the side using a round brush
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style

3. Bob with Angled Layers

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair
  2. Ask your stylist to cut your hair into a bob, ending just below your chin
  3. Ask your stylist to add angled layers, starting at your cheekbones and ending at your chin
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, creating volume at the crown of your head
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for round face females:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and don't be afraid to try something new

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about which hairstyle suits for round face females:

1. Can I have short hair if I have a round face?

Yes! Short hair can be a great option for round face females, as long as it is cut in a way that adds height and angles to your face.

2. Should I avoid center parts?

Center parts can make your face appear wider, so it is best to avoid them if you have a round face. Side parts or no parts are a better option.

3. Can I still have bangs?

Absolutely! Bangs can be a great option for round face females, as long as they are cut at an angle and swept to the side.

Hair Care

Lastly, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and ideas, you are sure to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your round face. Remember, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin!
