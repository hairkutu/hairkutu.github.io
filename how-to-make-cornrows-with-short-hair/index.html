How To Make Cornrows With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you struggling to find a hairstyle that works for your short hair? Look no further than cornrows! Cornrows are a traditional African hairstyle that involve braiding your hair close to your scalp, creating a neat and stylish look. In this guide, we’ll show you how to make cornrows with short hair, step by step. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Cornrows with Short Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Making Cornrows with Short Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Cornrows

How to Make Cornrows with Short Hair

Making cornrows with short hair is not as difficult as it may seem. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Rat-tail comb
  • Hair clips
  • Hair ties
  • Styling gel or cream

With these tools in hand, you’re ready to start making cornrows with short hair. Follow our step-by-step guide below.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Once your hair is clean, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it. Apply some styling gel or cream to your hair to make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, use a rat-tail comb to section your hair. Make a part down the middle of your head, from the front to the back. Then, make another part from ear to ear. You should now have four sections of hair.

Step 3: Create Your Cornrows

Starting with one of the front sections, take a small section of hair and divide it into three equal parts. Begin braiding your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down. As you braid, add more hair to each section until you reach the end of your hair. Secure the end with a hair tie. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Finish Your Cornrows

Once you’ve made all of your cornrows, use a styling gel or cream to smooth down any flyaways or frizz. You can also accessorize your cornrows with beads or hair clips for a finishing touch.

Tips for Making Cornrows with Short Hair

Making cornrows with short hair can be challenging, but these tips can help:

  • Use a rat-tail comb to create neat, even parts in your hair.
  • Apply styling gel or cream to your hair before braiding to make it easier to work with.
  • Start with small sections of hair and add more as you go.
  • Use hair clips to keep your sections of hair separated and organized.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don’t get discouraged if your first few attempts don’t turn out perfectly.

FAQs

Can you make cornrows with really short hair?

Yes, you can make cornrows with very short hair. The key is to start with small sections of hair and add more as you go. You can also use hair extensions to add length to your hair if needed.

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last for up to two weeks with proper care. To extend the life of your cornrows, wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night and avoid getting your hair wet.

Hair Care for Cornrows

To keep your cornrows looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid getting your hair wet, as this can cause your cornrows to unravel.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair every few days to keep it moisturized.
  • When it’s time to take out your cornrows, use a detangling spray or conditioner to make the process easier.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to make cornrows with short hair in no time. Give it a try and see how you like this stylish and practical hairstyle!
