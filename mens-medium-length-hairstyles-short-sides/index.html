Mens Medium Length Hairstyles Short Sides A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, mens medium length hairstyles with short sides may be the perfect option. This look has been popular for several years now and shows no signs of slowing down in 2023. In this guide, we'll go over how to achieve this look, offer some tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

Mens medium length hairstyles with short sides typically involve leaving the hair on top longer while trimming the sides and back short. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides. This look can be achieved with a variety of hair types, from straight to curly, and can be styled in many different ways.

How to Achieve Mens Medium Length Hairstyles Short Sides

To achieve this look, you'll want to start by finding a good stylist who is experienced in creating mens medium length hairstyles with short sides. Once you've found someone you trust, you can work with them to create a customized look that fits your face shape and hair type.

When it comes to styling, you'll want to use a good quality pomade or styling product to help you achieve your desired look. If you're going for a more casual look, you can simply comb your hair back and let it fall naturally. For a more polished look, you can use a blow dryer and brush to create volume and texture.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve mens medium length hairstyles with short sides:

  1. Find a good stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut.
  2. Discuss your desired look with your stylist and work together to create a customized cut.
  3. Use a good quality pomade or styling product to help you achieve your desired look.
  4. Comb your hair back and let it fall naturally for a more casual look.
  5. Use a blow dryer and brush to create volume and texture for a more polished look.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Medium Length Hairstyles Short Sides

To maintain your mens medium length hairstyles with short sides, it's important to keep your hair clean and well-conditioned. You'll also want to visit your stylist regularly to keep your cut looking fresh and to trim any split ends.

When styling your hair, be sure to use a good quality product that won't weigh your hair down or leave it looking greasy. You can also experiment with different styles to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

What face shapes work best with mens medium length hairstyles with short sides?

Mens medium length hairstyles with short sides can work well with a variety of face shapes, but they tend to look best on those with oval or square faces.

Can this look be achieved with curly hair?

Yes! Mens medium length hairstyles with short sides can be achieved with curly hair. In fact, this look can work particularly well with curly hair, as the longer hair on top can help weigh down and control the curls.

How often should I visit my stylist to maintain this look?

You should visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your mens medium length hairstyles with short sides. This will help keep your cut looking fresh and prevent any split ends from forming.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and strong, it's important to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type. You should also avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as these can damage your hair over time.

Additionally, you should try to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins that are essential for healthy hair growth. And don't forget to stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water!

In conclusion, mens medium length hairstyles with short sides are a stylish and versatile option that can work well with a variety of hair types and face shapes. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve a look that is both trendy and timeless.
