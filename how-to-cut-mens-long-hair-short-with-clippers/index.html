How To Cut Men s Long Hair Short With Clippers A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi May 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a man with long hair who wants to switch to a shorter style? Do you want to save money by cutting your hair at home with clippers? If you answered yes to either of these questions, then this article is for you! In this post, we'll discuss how to cut men's long hair short with clippers in a relaxed English language. Let's dive in!

Description

Cutting men's long hair short with clippers can be a daunting task, especially for those who have never done it before. However, with the right tools, techniques, and a little bit of practice, it's not as difficult as you might think. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know to successfully cut your long hair short with clippers.

How to

Before we begin, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a set of hair clippers with various guards, a comb, scissors, a mirror, and a towel. Once you have all of the necessary items, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it before starting the haircut. This will make it easier to cut and give you a better idea of how your hair falls naturally.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Clippers come with different guard sizes, which determine how short your hair will be cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the length you like. It's always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down, as you can't add hair back once it's been cut.

Step 3: Start at the Sides

Begin by using the clippers on the sides of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the guard to guide the clippers. Keep the clippers flat against your head and move them in an upward motion. Repeat this process until you've cut all the hair on the sides.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Once you've finished the sides, move to the back of your head. Use the same technique, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Make sure to keep the guard level and move the clippers in an upward motion. It's important to use a mirror to see the back of your head and ensure you're cutting evenly.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move to the top of your head. Start at the front and work your way to the back, using the comb to guide the clippers. Keep the guard level and move the clippers in an upward motion. Again, use the mirror to ensure you're cutting evenly.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you cut your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down
  • Use a mirror to see the back of your head
  • Take breaks if you need them
  • Use scissors to trim any areas the clippers missed
  • Don't rush the process

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's long hair short with clippers:

How short should I cut my hair?

The length of your haircut is a personal preference. However, it's always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much off.

Can I cut my hair wet?

It's recommended to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on the length of your hair and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you're unsure about how to style your new haircut, ask your barber or stylist for advice.

Conclusion

Cutting men's long hair short with clippers is a simple process that can save you time and money. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a great-looking haircut at home. Remember to take your time and practice, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
