How To Control Hair Fall Home Remedies In Hindi A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 15, 2023 · 7 min read

Hair fall has been a common problem faced by many individuals, and it can be caused due to various reasons such as hormonal changes, pollution, stress, and genetics. While there are several products available in the market that promise to control hair fall, many people prefer home remedies as they are natural and cost-effective. In this blog post, we will discuss some of the most effective home remedies to control hair fall in Hindi.

Table of Contents

  • How to Control Hair Fall with Home Remedies
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Hair Fall
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Routine

How to Control Hair Fall with Home Remedies

Here are some effective home remedies to control hair fall:

1. Amla

Amla, also known as Indian gooseberry, is rich in vitamin C, which helps in strengthening hair follicles and preventing hair fall. You can either consume amla juice or apply amla oil on your hair to see results.

2. Onion Juice

Onion juice contains sulfur, which helps in promoting hair growth and preventing hair fall. You can extract onion juice and apply it on your scalp for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is rich in medium-chain fatty acids, which help in nourishing hair follicles and preventing hair fall. You can massage your scalp with warm coconut oil and leave it overnight before washing it off the next day.

4. Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds are rich in proteins and help in strengthening hair follicles. You can soak fenugreek seeds in water overnight and grind them into a paste the next day. Apply the paste on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes before washing it off.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to control hair fall with home remedies:

Step 1: Choose the Right Home Remedy

Depending on your hair type and the severity of your hair fall, choose the right home remedy that suits you.

Step 2: Prepare the Remedy

Follow the instructions to prepare the home remedy of your choice.

Step 3: Apply the Remedy on Your Scalp

Apply the remedy on your scalp and massage it gently. Leave it on for the recommended time.

Step 4: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in proteins, vitamins, and minerals
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using chemical products on your hair
  • Manage your stress levels

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about controlling hair fall:

1. Can stress cause hair fall?

Yes, stress can cause hair fall as it can disrupt the hair growth cycle.

2. Can genetics cause hair fall?

Yes, genetics can play a role in hair fall.

3. Can diet affect hair fall?

Yes, a balanced diet that is rich in proteins, vitamins, and minerals can help in preventing hair fall.

Hair Care Routine

Here is a hair care routine that you can follow to prevent hair fall:

  • Wash your hair twice a week with a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Massage your scalp with warm oil once a week
  • Use a hair mask once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly

In conclusion, hair fall can be a stressful problem, but with the right home remedies and hair care routine, you can prevent it. Follow the tips mentioned in this blog post to achieve healthy and strong hair.
