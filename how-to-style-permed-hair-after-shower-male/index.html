How To Style Permed Hair After Shower Male A Guide For Effortless Curls

Written by Robby May 17, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

If you've recently gotten a perm, you're probably wondering how to style your hair after a shower. It can be intimidating to figure out how to maintain your curls, but with the right techniques, it's easy to style permed hair after shower male. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to get the perfect style for your permed hair.

How to Style Permed Hair After Shower Male

Styling permed hair after a shower is all about minimizing frizz and defining your curls. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Use a Microfiber Towel

After you shower, use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze excess water out of your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel, as this can cause frizz and damage your curls.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair while it's still damp. This will help moisturize your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help maintain your curl pattern and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a curl cream or gel to define your curls. You can also use a wide-tooth comb to gently separate your curls and create more volume.

Tips for Styling Permed Hair After Shower Male

Here are a few tips to help you style your permed hair after a shower:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help maintain your curls overnight.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to your curls.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ About Styling Permed Hair After Shower Male

How often should I wash my permed hair?

It's best to wash your permed hair no more than once or twice a week. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Can I brush my permed hair?

It's best to avoid brushing your permed hair, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently separate your curls.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

To keep your permed hair healthy and looking great, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible, as this can cause damage to your curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to style your permed hair after a shower with ease. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
