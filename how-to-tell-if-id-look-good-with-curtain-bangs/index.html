How To Tell If You d Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Robby Aug 11, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you considering getting curtain bangs but unsure if they would suit you? Curtain bangs are a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can add volume and frame your face, but they are not for everyone. In this article, we will guide you on how to tell if you'd look good with curtain bangs.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Tell if You'd Look Good with Curtain Bangs
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips to Consider
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted in the middle and styled to frame the face. They are inspired by the hairstyles of the 60s and 70s and have made a comeback in recent years. Curtain bangs are ideal for those with oval, heart, and square-shaped faces, but they may not suit everyone. The key to pulling off curtain bangs is to find the right length, texture, and shape that complements your features.

How to Tell if You'd Look Good with Curtain Bangs

There are a few factors to consider when deciding if curtain bangs would suit you:

  • Face Shape: Curtain bangs look best on oval, heart, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round or long face, you may want to opt for a different style.
  • Hair Texture: Curtain bangs work well on straight and wavy hair textures. If you have curly hair, you may need to straighten your bangs daily to maintain the look.
  • Forehead Size: If you have a larger forehead, curtain bangs can help balance out your features. If you have a smaller forehead, you may want to opt for a different style.
  • Hair Length: Curtain bangs look best on medium to long hair lengths. If you have short hair, you may need to grow out your hair before getting curtain bangs.

Overall, the best way to tell if you'd look good with curtain bangs is to consult with a hairstylist who can assess your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Step by Step Guide

If you're still unsure if curtain bangs are right for you, here's a step-by-step guide to help you decide:

  1. Take a photo of yourself with your hair pulled back and your face fully visible.
  2. Print out the photo and draw a line down the middle of your face.
  3. Cut out a paper template of curtain bangs and hold it up to your face, making sure it covers the area from your hairline to your eyebrows.
  4. Assess how the curtain bangs look on your face. Do they complement your features, or do they overwhelm them?
  5. If necessary, adjust the length and shape of the paper template until you find the right style.
  6. Show the template to your hairstylist and ask for their opinion.
  7. If you're still unsure, start with a shorter length and gradually go longer until you find the right style.

Tips to Consider

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting curtain bangs:

  • Communicate with your hairstylist: Make sure your hairstylist understands your desired length, shape, and texture. Bring photos to show them the exact style you want.
  • Consider maintenance: Curtain bangs require regular trimming and styling to maintain their shape. Make sure you have the time and patience to style them daily.
  • Invest in quality hair products: Use hair products that suit your hair type and texture, such as a volumizing mousse or a smoothing serum.
  • Play around with styling: Experiment with different ways of styling your curtain bangs, such as parting them to one side or sweeping them back.
  • Be confident: Rock your new look with confidence and embrace your unique features!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

  • Can I still wear my hair up with curtain bangs? Yes, you can still wear your hair up with curtain bangs. Simply part your bangs in the middle and pin them back with bobby pins.
  • Can I get curtain bangs if I have curly hair? Yes, but you may need to straighten your bangs daily to maintain the look. Talk to your hairstylist about the best products and techniques for your hair type.
  • How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs? It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs lose their shape. Typically, you may need to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Blow-dry your bangs: Use a round brush and a blow dryer on a low heat setting to style your bangs.
  • Use a heat protectant: Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your bangs before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Avoid touching your bangs: Don't touch or brush your bangs too often, as this can cause them to lose their shape.
  • Get regular trims: Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to trim your bangs and maintain their shape.

Now that you know how to tell if you'd look good with curtain bangs, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out this trendy hairstyle. With the right length, texture, and shape, curtain bangs can enhance your features and give you a fresh new look.
