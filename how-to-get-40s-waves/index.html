How To Get 40S Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking to achieve the classic 40s wave hairstyle? Look no further! This guide will provide you with easy-to-follow steps to achieve the perfect 40s wave look. From the right products to use to the proper technique, we’ll cover everything you need to know to get those glamorous waves that were all the rage during the 1940s.

Table of Contents

  • What You’ll Need
  • How to Get 40s Waves: Step-by-Step
  • Tips for Perfecting Your 40s Waves
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for 40s Waves

What You’ll Need

Before diving into the technique, make sure you have the right tools and products on hand:

  • Heat protectant
  • Curling iron with a 1-inch barrel
  • Clips to section hair
  • Hairspray
  • Comb

How to Get 40s Waves: Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect 40s wave:

Step 1: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, make sure to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections. Start with the bottom layers and work your way up. Use clips to keep each section in place.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting at the root and working your way down. Hold for a few seconds and then release. Repeat this process for each section of hair, alternating the direction of each curl.

Step 4: Comb Out Your Curls

Using a comb, gently comb out your curls to create a wave-like shape.

Step 5: Set with Hairspray

Once you have achieved your desired wave shape, set your hair with hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips for Perfecting Your 40s Waves

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect 40s wave:

  • Use a curling iron with a 1-inch barrel to achieve the perfect wave shape.
  • Alternate the direction of each curl to create a more natural-looking wave.
  • Don’t be afraid to use hairspray to set your waves in place.

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about achieving 40s waves:

Can I achieve 40s waves with short hair?

Yes! Simply adjust the size of the curling iron barrel to fit the length of your hair.

How long does it take to achieve 40s waves?

The time it takes to achieve 40s waves depends on your hair length and thickness. On average, it takes about 30-45 minutes to achieve the perfect 40s wave.

Hair Care Tips for 40s Waves

To keep your 40s waves looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect 40s wave hairstyle. Whether you’re channeling your inner pin-up girl or simply want to try out a new look, 40s waves are a classic and timeless style that will never go out of fashion. Happy styling!
