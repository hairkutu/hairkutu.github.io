How To Trim Your Own Hair In The Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 30, 2023 · 6 min read

Trimming your own hair can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to the back of your head. But with a little patience and practice, you can achieve a salon-worthy look right at home. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps to trim your own hair in the back, along with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Trim Your Own Hair in the Back

Before we get started, here’s what you’ll need:

  • A mirror
  • A comb
  • A pair of sharp hair-cutting scissors
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. Once it’s dry, comb it out to remove any tangles or knots. Then, use your spray bottle to mist your hair lightly with water. This will help to make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is even.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Use your comb to part your hair down the middle, creating two equal sections. Then, use your comb to create a part at the back of your head, dividing each section in half. You should now have four equal sections of hair.

Step 3: Trim the Bottom Section

Starting with the bottom section, comb your hair straight down and hold it between your fingers at the length you want to cut it. Use your scissors to make small, vertical cuts into the hair, working your way around the section. Be sure to cut small amounts at a time, and check your progress in the mirror frequently.

Step 4: Move to the Next Section

Once you’ve finished trimming the bottom section, unclip the next section and repeat the process. Continue working your way up until you’ve trimmed all four sections of hair.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Once you’ve finished trimming, use your comb and mirror to check for evenness. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them until you’re happy with the result.

Tips for Trimming Your Own Hair in the Back

Here are a few tips to help make the process of trimming your own hair in the back easier:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a clean cut.
  • Take your time. Don’t rush through the process, or you may end up with an uneven haircut.
  • Check your progress frequently. Use your mirror to check your progress as you go, and make adjustments as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming your own hair in the back:

  • Can I use regular scissors to trim my hair?
  • It’s best to use hair-cutting scissors, as they are specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors may not be sharp enough, and can damage your hair.
  • How often should I trim my hair?
  • It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • What if I mess up?
  • If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always go to a salon and have a professional fix it for you.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet, rich in fruits and vegetables, to promote healthy hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to trim your own hair in the back with confidence. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and check your progress frequently. And if you do make a mistake, don’t worry – hair grows back!
