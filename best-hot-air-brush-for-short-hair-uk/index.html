Best Hot Air Brush For Short Hair Uk The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 20, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you looking for the best hot air brush for short hair in the UK? Look no further! In this article, we will review the top hot air brushes for short hair and provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to choose the right one for you. Read on to learn more!

Description

Hot air brushes are an excellent tool for those with short hair looking to achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home. They combine the benefits of a hair dryer and a styling brush in one, making it easier and quicker to achieve a polished look. With so many options on the market, it can be challenging to choose the right one for your hair type and styling needs.

How to Choose the Best Hot Air Brush for Short Hair UK

When choosing the best hot air brush for short hair in the UK, there are several factors to consider:

Barrel Size

The barrel size of the hot air brush is an essential factor to consider. For short hair, a smaller barrel size is ideal as it allows you to get closer to the roots and achieve more precise styling.

Bristles

The bristles on the hot air brush should be gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for brushes with ceramic or tourmaline bristles, as they emit negative ions that help reduce frizz and static.

Heat Settings

Choose a hot air brush with adjustable heat settings to ensure you can tailor the temperature to your hair type and styling needs. Lower temperatures are ideal for fine or damaged hair, while higher temperatures are best for thicker or coarser hair.

Top Hot Air Brushes for Short Hair UK

1. BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Rotating Hot Air Brush

The BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Rotating Hot Air Brush is an excellent option for short hair. It features a 2-inch barrel size, a ceramic and ionic technology, and three heat settings. The rotating barrel helps create volume and gives your hair a polished finish.

2. Remington Pro-Air Shine Hot Air Styler

The Remington Pro-Air Shine Hot Air Styler is a budget-friendly option that still delivers salon-worthy results. It features a 1-inch barrel size, ceramic and ionic technology, and two heat settings. The brush also has a cool shot button to help set your style in place.

3. Dyson Airwrap Styler

The Dyson Airwrap Styler may be on the pricier side, but it's worth the investment. It comes with multiple attachments, including a 1.2-inch barrel size, a pre-styling dryer, and a smoothing brush. The Airwrap uses air to style your hair, eliminating the need for extreme heat, making it ideal for those with damaged or fine hair.

Step by Step Guide to Using a Hot Air Brush for Short Hair UK

Using a hot air brush for short hair is simple and straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair until it's about 80% dry. Apply a heat protectant to prevent heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Step 3: Start Styling

Take a section of hair and place the hot air brush at the roots. Gently glide the brush down to the ends, rotating as you go to create volume and shape.

Step 4: Repeat

Continue working in sections until you've styled all of your hair.

Step 5: Finish

Finish with a cool shot to set the style in place.

Tips for Using a Hot Air Brush for Short Hair UK

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your hot air brush:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage.
  • Always start with slightly damp hair.
  • Section your hair to make styling easier.
  • Use a lower heat setting for finer or damaged hair.
  • Use a higher heat setting for thicker or coarser hair.
  • Don't overload the brush with hair.
  • Rotate the brush as you go to create volume and shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair UK

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the best hot air brush for short hair in the UK?

The best hot air brush for short hair in the UK depends on your hair type and styling needs. Some top options include the BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Rotating Hot Air Brush, Remington Pro-Air Shine Hot Air Styler, and Dyson Airwrap Styler.

How often should I use a hot air brush on my short hair?

You should avoid using hot tools on your hair every day. Aim to use a hot air brush no more than three times a week to prevent heat damage.

Can I use a hot air brush on wet hair?

No, you should avoid using a hot air brush on wet hair. Always start with slightly damp hair and use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage.

Choosing the best hot air brush for short hair in the UK can be challenging, but with our guide, you'll be able to find the perfect one for your needs. Remember to follow our step-by-step guide and hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful. Happy styling!
