Best Medium Length Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Daniel May 12, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. The wrong cut can make your face look even rounder, while the right one can help to balance out your features and create a more flattering look. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best medium length hairstyles for round faces, along with tips for styling and care.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  3. Step by Step Guide to Styling
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Description

Medium length hairstyles are perfect for round faces because they create a flattering frame around the face without adding too much volume. They can also be versatile and easy to style, making them a great choice for busy women who want to look their best without spending hours in front of the mirror.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to look for a cut that will help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. This can be achieved through layers, bangs, and a side part.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, you may want to opt for a cut with layers to help add dimension and reduce bulk. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, you may want to go for a sleeker look with fewer layers.

Some of the best medium length hairstyles for round faces include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to create a more elongated look, which can be especially flattering for round faces. They can also add volume and movement to your hair, making them a great choice for those with thin or fine hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a longer face, while also drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. They can be worn with a variety of hairstyles, from long layers to a sleek bob.

3. Asymmetrical Bob

The asymmetrical bob is a modern and chic hairstyle that can be a great choice for round faces. The longer side of the bob can help to elongate your face, while the shorter side can add a touch of edginess.

4. Layered Lob

The layered lob is a versatile and easy-to-style cut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. By adding layers throughout the length of your hair, you can create a more balanced look that flatters your round face.

Step by Step Guide to Styling

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your round face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Before you begin styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help to ensure that your style lasts longer and looks its best.

2. Apply a styling product.

Depending on your hair type and the style you're going for, you may want to apply a styling product such as mousse, gel, or hairspray to help hold your style in place.

3. Use a round brush to create volume.

If you want to add volume to your hair, use a round brush while blow-drying to create lift at the roots.

4. Finish with a heat protectant.

If you're using heat styling tools such as a flat iron or curling wand, be sure to apply a heat protectant first to help prevent damage to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've styled your hair, there are a few things you can do to help maintain your look:

1. Use a silk pillowcase.

Using a silk pillowcase can help to reduce friction and prevent hair breakage while you sleep.

2. Avoid over-washing your hair.

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day.

3. Get regular trims.

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look frizzy and unkempt.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that helps to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. Some of the best choices include long layers, side-swept bangs, and an asymmetrical bob.

Can I wear a blunt bob with a round face?

A blunt bob can be a tricky style for those with round faces, as it can make your face look even rounder. However, if you want to try a blunt bob, you can make it work by adding layers and texture throughout the length of your hair.

Can I wear my hair up with a round face?

Absolutely! There are plenty of updo styles that can be flattering for round faces, such as a messy bun or a topknot. Just be sure to keep some softness around your face to balance out your features.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

When it comes to caring for your hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Use a gentle shampoo.

Choosing a gentle shampoo can help to prevent damage to your hair and scalp, which can lead to dryness and breakage.

2. Condition regularly.

Conditioning your hair regularly can help to keep it soft and smooth, while also protecting it from damage.

3. Protect your hair from the sun.

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. To protect your hair, wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray.

In conclusion, finding the best medium length hairstyle for your round face can be a challenge, but with the right cut and styling techniques, you can create a flattering and versatile look that suits your unique features. By following these tips for styling and care, you can keep your hair looking its best and feeling healthy and vibrant.
