How To Undercut My Own Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a change? An undercut might be just what you need! Not only is it trendy and edgy, but it's also a great way to keep cool during the hot summer months. In this article, we'll show you how to undercut your own hair from the comfort of your own home.

Description:

An undercut is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back of the head is shaved or cut very short, while the hair on top is left longer. This creates a dramatic contrast between the two lengths and can be styled in a variety of ways.

How To:

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to undercut your own hair:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a clipper or trimmer with guards, scissors or shears, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide how short you want your undercut to be. You can choose a number on the guard for your clipper or trimmer, or you can use scissors or shears to cut the hair to the desired length.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Separate the hair on top of your head from the hair on the sides and back using the comb. Clip or tie the top section out of the way so it doesn't get in the way.

Step 4: Start Trimming

Using the clipper or trimmer, start cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head. Use the guard or scissors to achieve the desired length. It's important to go slowly and carefully, checking your work in the mirror as you go.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've finished trimming the sides and back, use the scissors or shears to blend the hair into the longer hair on top. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Now that your undercut is complete, it's time to style your hair. You can use a variety of products, such as pomade or wax, to create different looks. Experiment with different styles to see what works best for you.

Tips:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut:

  • Start with a longer guard or length and work your way down gradually
  • Go slowly and carefully to avoid mistakes
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercutting your own hair:

Is it hard to undercut your own hair?

It can be tricky, but with the right tools and some patience, anyone can do it.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! You can always go shorter or try to blend the hair to hide any mistakes.

How often do I need to undercut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep the undercut. On average, most people need to trim their undercut every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair after undercutting it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and avoid split ends

Now that you know how to undercut your own hair, it's time to give it a try! Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products.
