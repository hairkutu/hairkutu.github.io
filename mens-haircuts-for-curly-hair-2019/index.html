Mens Haircuts For Curly Hair 2019 How To Choose The Best Style

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. It looks great when it's well-styled, but it can be a nightmare to deal with on a daily basis. If you're a man with curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right haircut. In this article, we'll show you the best mens haircuts for curly hair 2019 and give you tips on how to style and care for your hair.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Curly Hair

The first step in choosing the best haircut for curly hair is to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you'll want to avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your head. If you have a square face, you can go for a more angular haircut that adds some height to your hair.

The second step is to consider your hair type. If you have thick curly hair, you'll want to choose a haircut that allows you to manage your hair without adding too much volume. If you have thin curly hair, you'll want to choose a haircut that adds volume and texture.

Finally, consider your lifestyle. If you're a busy guy who doesn't have time to style your hair every day, you'll want a low-maintenance haircut that looks good even when you don't have time to style it. If you're someone who likes to experiment with different styles, you can go for a more adventurous haircut that requires more maintenance.

Top Hairstyles for Curly Hair

There are many mens haircuts for curly hair 2019 that are stylish and easy to maintain. Here are some of the top hairstyles for curly hair:

1. The Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that looks great on men with curly hair. It's a low-maintenance style that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

2. The Undercut

The undercut is a popular mens haircut for curly hair 2019 that involves shaving the sides of your head and leaving the top long. It's a great way to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a casual hairstyle that's perfect for guys who want to keep their hair out of their face. It's easy to do and looks great with a beard.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be tricky, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a great look. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair:

1. Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair with a towel, but don't rub it too hard. You want to leave some moisture in your hair.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product like a curl cream or gel to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly.

3. Style your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Cup the diffuser around your hair and use a low heat setting. Don't move the diffuser around too much or it will create frizz. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to style it.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Maintaining curly hair requires some extra care and attention. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner

Curly hair can be dry and brittle, so it's important to use a shampoo and conditioner that adds moisture to your hair.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and create frizz. Avoid using them if possible.

3. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs About Mens Haircuts for Curly Hair

1. Can I use a hair straightener on curly hair?

Yes, you can use a hair straightener on curly hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash curly hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

3. What's the best way to detangle curly hair?

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots to prevent breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

To keep your curly hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to add moisture to your hair.

2. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

A silk pillowcase will prevent your hair from getting tangled and will help prevent frizz.

3. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry

Brushing your hair when it's dry can create frizz and breakage. Only brush your hair when it's wet and use a wide-tooth comb.

Choosing the right mens haircut for curly hair 2019 can be a challenge, but with the right tips and techniques, you can achieve a great look. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut. Use the right products and techniques to style and care for your hair, and you'll have healthy, stylish curls in no time!
