How To Control Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have thick, wavy hair that seems impossible to control? Don't worry, you are not alone. Many people with thick, wavy hair struggle to find a hairstyle that works for them. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks on how to control thick wavy hair.

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Controlling Thick Wavy Hair
  • FAQs

How to Control Thick Wavy Hair

The first step in controlling thick wavy hair is to find the right cut. A layered cut can help to thin out the hair and make it more manageable. A long bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers can work well for thick wavy hair.

Another way to control thick wavy hair is to use the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy hair, such as a curl-defining cream or a frizz-control serum. These products can help to tame frizz and define your waves.

It is also important to use the right tools when styling your hair. A wide-tooth comb can help to detangle your hair without causing damage. A diffuser attachment on your hairdryer can help to enhance your natural waves without causing frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to control thick wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining cream or frizz-control serum.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  4. Flip your head upside down and diffuse your hair with a hairdryer.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose waves.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Controlling Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for controlling thick wavy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun to help prevent tangles.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Try air-drying your hair instead of using a hairdryer to avoid heat damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your thick wavy hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and a high-quality flat iron to avoid heat damage.

Q: Can I color my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can color your thick wavy hair. However, be sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your color looking fresh.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Proper hair care is essential for controlling thick wavy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can finally take control of your thick wavy hair. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles and products until you find what works best for you.
