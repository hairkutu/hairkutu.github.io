How To Find The Right Hair Color For Your Skin Tone

Written by Frank Jul 07, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will look best on you. But with a little bit of knowledge and guidance, you can find the perfect hue that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and stylish. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about finding the right hair color for your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair color. The right shade can enhance your complexion, while the wrong one can make you look washed out or unnatural. That's why it's important to understand your skin tone and how it relates to hair color.

There are two main categories of skin tones: warm and cool. Warm skin tones have a yellow or golden undertone, while cool skin tones have a pink or blue undertone. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear greenish, you have warm undertones, while if they appear bluish, you have cool undertones.

How to

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start exploring different hair colors that will complement it. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm undertones, look for hair colors that have warm tones as well. These include shades of red, copper, honey, and golden blonde. Avoid cool or ashy colors, as they can clash with your skin tone and make you look sallow or tired.

Cool Skin Tones

If you have cool undertones, go for hair colors that have cool tones as well. These include shades of ash blonde, platinum, and cool brown. Avoid warm or golden colors, as they can make your skin look ruddy or sunburned.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a hair color based on your skin tone:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist.
  2. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.
  3. Consider your natural hair color and how drastic of a change you want to make.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist or use online tools to visualize the color on your hair.
  5. Prepare for maintenance and upkeep, as some hair colors require more frequent touch-ups.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your eye color and natural makeup shades when choosing a hair color.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain the vibrancy of your hair color.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage and heat styling to prevent fading and damage.
  • If you're unsure about a hair color, start with a semi-permanent or temporary dye to test it out.

FAQ

What hair color looks best on warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with shades of red, copper, honey, and golden blonde.

What hair color looks best on cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with shades of ash blonde, platinum, and cool brown.

Can I dye my hair a color that doesn't complement my skin tone?

You can dye your hair any color you like, but it may not look as flattering or natural as a color that complements your skin tone.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hair color for your skin tone, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Protect your hair from heat styling and sun damage with a heat protectant spray and a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it soft and shiny.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect hair color for your skin tone and enjoy a stylish and flattering new look.
