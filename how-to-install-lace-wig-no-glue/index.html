How to Install Lace Wig No Glue - Step by Step Guide

If you're tired of using glue to install your lace wig, there is another way. In this step by step guide, we'll show you how to install a lace wig without using glue. This method is not only easy but also a great way to protect your hairline. Let's get started!

How to Install Lace Wig No Glue

Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1 - Preparing your Hair

Before installing your lace wig, it's essential to prepare your hair properly. First, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Then, braid your hair into small cornrows or twist them into small sections. This will help to create a flat surface for the wig to lay on.

Step 2 - Prepare the Wig

Next, prepare the wig by cutting the lace as close to the hairline as possible. Be sure not to cut any of the hair. Then, apply a wig grip to the perimeter of the wig to keep it in place. You can also use bobby pins to secure the wig grip.

Step 3 - Place the Wig

Place the wig on your head and adjust it to fit properly. You can use the adjustable straps on the wig to tighten or loosen it as needed. Once you've positioned the wig, use a rat-tail comb to create a part where you want it to be.

Step 4 - Secure the Wig

To secure the wig, use a small amount of hair gel or edge control along your hairline. Then, use the comb to smooth your hair down and place the wig over it. Use bobby pins to secure the wig to your hair. You can also use hair clips to hold the wig in place while you're styling it.

Step 5 - Style the Wig

Now that the wig is in place, you can style it as desired. You can use a flat iron or curling wand to add some curls or waves. You can also use a brush or comb to style it into a ponytail or bun.

Tips for Installing a Lace Wig without Glue

Here are some tips to ensure your wig looks great:

  • Use a wig grip to keep the wig in place.
  • Apply a small amount of gel or edge control along your hairline to secure the wig.
  • Use bobby pins or hair clips to hold the wig in place while you're styling it.
  • Be sure to cut the lace as close to the hairline as possible.
  • Always style the wig after it's in place.

FAQ

Can I sleep with a lace wig without glue?

Yes, you can sleep with a lace wig without glue if you secure it properly. Use a wig grip and bobby pins to keep the wig in place while you sleep.

How long will the wig stay in place?

The wig should stay in place for a few days if you secure it properly. You can use bobby pins to adjust the wig as needed.

Hair Care

To maintain your wig, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wig brush or comb to detangle the hair.
  • Wash the wig with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Let the wig air dry on a wig stand.
  • Avoid using heat on the wig as it can damage the hair.

With these tips, you can install and maintain your lace wig without glue easily. Try it out and see how it looks!
