Curtain Bangs 2020 For Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face and you're looking for a trendy new hairstyle, curtain bangs 2020 for round face might be just what you need. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

Curtain bangs are a style of bangs that are parted in the middle and swept to the sides, creating a "curtain" effect. This style has been popular for several years, and it's still going strong in 2020. Curtain bangs are versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Get Curtain Bangs 2020 for Round Face

If you want to get curtain bangs for your round face, it's important to choose a style that flatters your features. Here's how to get the perfect curtain bangs:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The length of your curtain bangs will depend on your face shape and the look you're going for. For round faces, it's best to go for longer bangs that hit just below the cheekbones. This will help elongate your face and create a more flattering shape.

Step 2: Choose the Right Parting

The parting of your curtain bangs is crucial to the overall look. For round faces, a center parting is ideal as it will help create the illusion of length. However, if you have a high forehead, you can also go for a side parting to balance out your features.

Step 3: Get the Cut

Once you've chosen the right length and parting, it's time to get the cut. Your hairstylist will take into account your face shape and the look you're going for to create the perfect curtain bangs for your round face. Be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist to ensure you get the desired result.

Styling Tips

Once you have your curtain bangs, it's important to style them properly to achieve the desired look. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Blow-dry Your Bangs

To create a smooth, sleek look, blow-dry your curtain bangs with a round brush. This will help create volume and keep the bangs in place.

Tip 2: Use a Flat Iron

If you want to create a more polished look, use a flat iron to straighten your curtain bangs. This will help create a sleek, sophisticated look that's perfect for any occasion.

Tip 3: Add Texture

If you want to add some texture to your curtain bangs, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. This will help create a more tousled, effortless look that's perfect for everyday wear.

FAQ

Q: Are curtain bangs suitable for all face shapes?

A: Curtain bangs can be customized to suit different face shapes. However, it's important to choose the right length and parting to flatter your features.

Q: How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

A: It's important to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I style my curtain bangs with a side parting?

A: Yes, you can style your curtain bangs with a side parting if you have a high forehead. This will help balance out your features and create a more flattering look.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that's free from sulfates and harsh chemicals. This will help keep your hair and scalp healthy and prevent damage.

Tip 2: Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft, smooth, and manageable. Use a conditioner that's specifically designed for your hair type.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. This can cause damage to your hair and make it more difficult to style.

Now that you know everything there is to know about curtain bangs 2020 for round face, it's time to try this trendy hairstyle for yourself. With the right cut, styling, and hair care, you can achieve the perfect look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and fabulous.
