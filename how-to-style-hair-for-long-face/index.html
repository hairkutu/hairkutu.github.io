How To Style Hair For Long Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a long face, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that complements your features. However, with the right styling techniques and hair care, you can achieve a look that flatters your face shape. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style hair for long face, including tips and tricks, and answer frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Hair for Long Face
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Long Face

How to Style Hair for Long Face

The key to styling hair for a long face is to create the illusion of width and volume. You want to avoid hairstyles that make your face look even longer. Here are some hairstyles that work well for long face:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves or curls
  • Braids and twists
  • Pixie cut with volume on top

Hairstyles to Avoid

Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make your face look even longer. Also, avoid center parts, blunt bangs, and severe updos. These hairstyles can accentuate the length of your face and make it look narrow.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what hairstyles work well for long face, here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair:

  1. Start by applying a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting at the roots to create volume.
  3. If you have long layers, curl them away from your face with a curling iron or wand. If you have short hair, add volume on top with a texturizing spray.
  4. Part your hair to the side and sweep your bangs to the side.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your hair for long face:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for recommendations on hairstyles that would complement your face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a long face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a long face. However, make sure to add volume on top to balance out the length of your face.

2. What is the best length for hair if I have a long face?

The best length for hair if you have a long face is shoulder-length or shorter. Longer hair can make your face look even longer.

3. Should I get bangs if I have a long face?

Yes, you can get bangs if you have a long face, but make sure they are side-swept and not blunt. Blunt bangs can accentuate the length of your face.

Hair Care for Long Face

In addition to styling your hair, it's essential to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care for long face:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you know how to style hair for long face, try out these hairstyles and tips to achieve a look that flatters your face shape. Remember, confidence is the key to pulling off any hairstyle, so wear it with pride!
