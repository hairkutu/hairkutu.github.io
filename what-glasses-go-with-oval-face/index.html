What Glasses Go With Oval Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oval face shape, consider yourself lucky! This face shape is versatile and can rock almost any style of glasses. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right pair that suits your personality and style. In this guide, we'll break down what glasses go with oval face, how to choose the perfect frame, and offer tips on how to take care of your glasses.

Table of Content

  1. How to Choose the Right Frame
  2. Best Glasses for Oval Face
  3. Tips on Wearing Glasses
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Choose the Right Frame

The first step in finding the perfect glasses for your oval face is to determine the shape of your face. Oval faces are characterized by balanced proportions, symmetrical features, and a slightly rounded jawline. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are high.

When choosing glasses, look for frames that are wider than the broadest part of your face. Round, square, and rectangular frames are all great choices for oval faces, but avoid frames that are too small or oversized.

Another factor to consider is the color of the frame. Neutral colors like black, brown, and tortoiseshell are timeless and work well with any outfit. However, if you want to make a statement, consider frames in bold colors or patterns.

Best Glasses for Oval Face

Now that you know how to choose the right frame, let's take a look at some of the best glasses for oval faces.

1. Aviator Glasses

Aviator glasses are an iconic style that looks great on oval faces. The teardrop shape of the lenses complements the natural shape of the face, and the thin metal frame adds a touch of sophistication to any outfit.

2. Wayfarer Glasses

Wayfarer glasses have been a popular style for decades, and for good reason. The square frame works well with oval faces and adds a touch of retro charm to any look.

3. Round Glasses

Round glasses are a great choice for oval faces because they add softness to the face's natural angles. Choose a pair with a thin metal frame for a modern twist on a classic style.

Tips on Wearing Glasses

Now that you've found the perfect pair of glasses, here are some tips on how to wear them with confidence:

  • Make sure your glasses fit properly. Ill-fitting glasses can cause discomfort and headaches.
  • Keep your glasses clean. Use a microfiber cloth to wipe away smudges and oil.
  • Experiment with different styles. Don't be afraid to try new shapes and colors!

FAQ

What shape glasses are best for oval faces?

Oval faces can rock almost any style of glasses, but round, square, and rectangular frames are particularly flattering.

What color glasses look best on oval faces?

Neutral colors like black, brown, and tortoiseshell are timeless and work well with any outfit. For a bolder look, consider frames in bright colors or patterns.

Hair Care

Finally, don't forget to consider your hairstyle when choosing glasses. If you have long hair, consider frames that won't get tangled or caught in your hair. If you have short hair, choose frames that complement the shape of your face.

Now that you know what glasses go with oval face, it's time to find the perfect pair for you. Remember to choose a frame that complements your personal style and fits properly for maximum comfort. Happy shopping!
