What Hair Tone Suits Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on what hair tone suits you best! Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but fear not, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we will go through everything you need to know to make an informed decision on what hair tone suits you the most. Let’s get started!

Description

Your hair color is one of the first things people notice about you, which is why choosing the right hair tone is crucial. Your hair tone should complement your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. There are various factors to consider when choosing the perfect hair tone for you. With so many options out there, it can be challenging to know where to begin. Whether you’re considering going blonde, brunette, or somewhere in between, we’ve got you covered with this comprehensive guide.

How to Choose the Right Hair Tone

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair tone is to determine your skin tone. There are three main types of skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. If you have a cool skin tone, you may have pink, red, or blue undertones. If you have a warm skin tone, you may have yellow, peach, or golden undertones. Those with a neutral skin tone have a balance of cool and warm undertones. Knowing your skin tone will help you choose the right hair tone that complements your complexion.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

It’s essential to consider your natural hair color when choosing a new hair tone. Generally, it’s easiest to stick to a hair tone that is within two shades of your natural hair color. This will give you a more natural look and make the transition less drastic.

Step 3: Think About Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair tone. For example, those with blue or green eyes may want to consider warmer hair tones, such as golden blonde or caramel. Those with brown eyes may want to opt for cooler hair tones, such as ash brown or platinum blonde.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Tone

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair tone:

  • Consider your lifestyle: If you’re low maintenance, stick to a hair tone that requires minimal upkeep.
  • Think about your wardrobe: Your hair tone should complement your wardrobe and personal style.
  • Consult a professional: If you’re unsure about what hair tone suits you, consult a professional hairstylist for advice.

FAQ

What hair tone suits cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with cool hair tones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or ash brown.

What hair tone suits warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with warm hair tones, such as golden blonde, caramel, or auburn.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, but it’s best to use hair dye specifically formulated for sensitive skin. Always do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.

Hair Care Tips

Once you’ve chosen the right hair tone, it’s essential to take care of your hair to maintain the color and shine. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from UV rays when outdoors.

That’s it for our guide on what hair tone suits you best. Remember, choosing the right hair tone is all about finding a color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be well on your way to finding the perfect hair tone for you.
