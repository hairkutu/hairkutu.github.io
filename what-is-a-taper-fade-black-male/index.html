What Is A Taper Fade For Black Males

Written by William May 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish haircut that is both versatile and easy to maintain, a taper fade might be just what you need. A taper fade is a type of haircut that gradually fades from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back. This style is particularly popular among black males, who often sport a clean, polished look with a tapered cut.

How to Get a Taper Fade Haircut

If you want to get a taper fade haircut, you should start by finding a barber who specializes in this style. You can ask for recommendations from friends or look online for reviews of local barbershops. Once you've found a barber you trust, you can ask them to give you a taper fade haircut.

The barber will typically start by using clippers to cut your hair very short on the sides and back. They will then gradually blend the longer hair on top into the shorter hair on the sides and back, creating a tapered effect. The final result is a stylish, polished look that is perfect for any occasion.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Taper Fade Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a taper fade haircut:

  1. Find a barber who specializes in taper fades.
  2. Decide on the length you want for your hair on top.
  3. Sit in the barber's chair and let them know what you want.
  4. The barber will use clippers to cut your hair very short on the sides and back.
  5. They will then use scissors to trim the longer hair on top to the desired length.
  6. The barber will use a comb and clippers to gradually blend the longer hair on top into the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  7. They will keep blending until they achieve the desired tapered effect.
  8. The barber will then style your hair and make any final adjustments.
  9. You're done!

Tips for Maintaining Your Taper Fade Haircut

Once you've gotten a taper fade haircut, you'll want to maintain it so that it looks great for as long as possible. Here are some tips for keeping your taper fade looking fresh:

  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups.
  • Use hair products, like hair gel or pomade, to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from sunlight and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

FAQs About Taper Fade Haircuts for Black Males

Here are some frequently asked questions about taper fade haircuts for black males:

Q: How much does a taper fade haircut cost?

A: The cost of a taper fade haircut can vary depending on where you live and the experience level of your barber. In general, you can expect to pay anywhere from $20 to $50 for a taper fade.

Q: Can you get a taper fade with long hair?

A: Yes, you can get a taper fade with long hair. However, the longer your hair is on top, the more difficult it may be to achieve a clean, polished look.

Q: How often do you need to get a taper fade haircut?

A: This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your haircut. In general, you should plan on getting a touch-up every 2-4 weeks to keep your taper fade looking fresh.

Hair Care for Black Males with Taper Fade Haircuts

If you have a taper fade haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for hair care for black males with taper fade haircuts:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

A taper fade haircut is a stylish and versatile option for black males who want a polished, clean-cut look. With the right care and maintenance, your taper fade can look great for weeks to come.
