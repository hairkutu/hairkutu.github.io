How To Play Basketball With Long Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Mar 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair can be a hassle to manage while playing basketball, but it doesn't have to be a hindrance to your game. With the right techniques and products, you can play basketball with long hair without worrying about it getting in your face or tangling up.

  • How to Play Basketball with Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Hair Care

Before you hit the court, it's essential to take care of your hair to prevent damage and tangling. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Detangle your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Start from the ends and work your way up to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider wearing your hair in protective styles like braids or twists to minimize tangles while playing basketball.

How to Play Basketball with Long Hair

Playing basketball with long hair requires a bit of preparation and some strategic styling. Here's what you need to know:

Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make all the difference when playing basketball with long hair. Here are a few options to consider:

  • Ponytail: A classic ponytail can keep your hair out of your face and off your neck while playing.
  • Bun: A bun is another option for keeping your hair secure and out of the way. Make sure it's a tight, high bun to minimize movement.
  • Braids: Braids are an excellent choice for long hair, as they keep your hair neat and tangle-free. Consider French braids or cornrows for a more secure style.

Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can help keep your hair in place while playing basketball. Here are a few to consider:

  • Headbands: A headband can keep your hair out of your face and absorb sweat.
  • Hair Ties: Use a strong hair tie that won't slip or break during the game.
  • Bobby Pins: Bobby pins can help secure flyaway hairs or keep your bun in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to playing basketball with long hair:

  1. Wash and detangle your hair before the game.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that works for you.
  3. Secure your hair with a hair tie or pins.
  4. Put on a headband if desired.
  5. Apply a sweat-resistant hair product like gel or hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  6. Check your hair throughout the game and adjust as needed.
  7. After the game, take time to detangle your hair gently to prevent breakage.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few additional tips and tricks for playing basketball with long hair:

  • Use a sweatband on your forehead to prevent sweat from reaching your hairline.
  • Consider wearing a bandana or hat to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Wrap your hair in a scarf or silk bonnet while sleeping to prevent tangling.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner before playing basketball to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQs

Can I wear my hair down while playing basketball?

We don't recommend wearing your hair down while playing basketball, as it can get in your face and obstruct your vision.

Can I use hair extensions while playing basketball?

Yes, you can use hair extensions while playing basketball. Just make sure they're securely attached and won't come loose during the game.

How often should I wash my hair after playing basketball?

You should wash your hair after every basketball game to remove sweat and dirt buildup. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.

Playing basketball with long hair doesn't have to be a challenge. By choosing the right hairstyle, using hair accessories, and taking care of your hair, you can focus on your game without worrying about your hair getting in the way.
