How To Make Hair Fall Down Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly hair that always seems to stand up straight? Do you want to know how to make hair fall down guys and achieve a sleek, polished look? Look no further! In this article, we'll provide step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the hair of your dreams.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

Having hair that falls down is a popular style choice for many guys. However, achieving this look can be challenging, especially if you have thick or curly hair. Fortunately, there are several ways to make your hair fall down naturally, without resorting to harsh chemicals or damaging heat styling tools.

How to:

There are several techniques you can use to make your hair fall down naturally:

1. Blow drying:

Using a blow dryer on your hair can help weigh it down and make it fall naturally. Start by towel-drying your hair to remove excess moisture, then use a round brush to blow dry your hair in sections. Aim the blow dryer downwards, towards the ends of your hair, to help it fall down naturally.

2. Use a styling product:

Using a styling product like pomade, paste, or wax can help add weight to your hair and make it fall down. Apply a small amount of product to your fingertips, then work it through your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.

3. Use a straightening iron:

If you have curly or wavy hair, using a straightening iron can help make it fall down naturally. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair, then use the straightening iron to straighten small sections of hair at a time, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step-by-Step:

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair fall down naturally:

Step 1:

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.

Step 2:

Towel-dry your hair to remove excess moisture.

Step 3:

Apply a styling product to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.

Step 4:

Blow dry your hair using a round brush, aiming the blow dryer downwards towards the ends of your hair.

Step 5:

Use a straightening iron to straighten small sections of your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 6:

Finish with a hairspray or styling spray to hold your hair in place.

Tips:

  • Choose a styling product that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections to ensure that it dries evenly.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a straightening iron.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ:

Q: Can I make my hair fall down naturally without using any products?

A: Yes, blow drying your hair using a round brush can help make it fall down naturally, without the need for any styling products.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, washing your hair every 2-3 days is sufficient for most guys.

Q: Can using a straightening iron damage my hair?

A: Yes, using a straightening iron can damage your hair if you don't use a heat protectant spray or if you use it too frequently. Be sure to follow the manufacturer's instructions and use the lowest heat setting possible.

Hair Care:

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools as much as possible. Be sure to also eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to make your hair fall down naturally and achieve the sleek, polished look you've always wanted. So go ahead and try them out, and get ready to turn heads with your amazing hair!
