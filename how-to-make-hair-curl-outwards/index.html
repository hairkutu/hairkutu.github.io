How To Make Hair Curl Outwards

Written by William Mar 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your flat and lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and style to your locks? Look no further! In this article, we will teach you how to make your hair curl outwards. Whether you have straight or wavy hair, these tips and tricks will help you achieve the look you desire.

Description

Curling your hair outwards can be a great way to add some bounce and volume to your hair. This style is perfect for those who want to achieve a more voluminous and bouncy look. By following these steps, you can easily create this style at home without having to spend a lot of money at the salon.

How to Make Hair Curl Outwards

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to prep your hair first. This means washing your hair and conditioning it with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat styling tools.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into manageable sections. Use hair clips to hold the sections of your hair in place. This will make it easier for you to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Now it's time to curl your hair. Use a curling iron or a curling wand to curl your hair outwards. Start at the roots of your hair and wrap the hair around the curling iron or wand. Hold the curling iron or wand for a few seconds and release the hair. Repeat this process until you have curled all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair

Once you have curled all sections of your hair, it's time to brush your hair. Use a wide-toothed comb or a paddle brush to gently brush your hair. This will help to separate the curls and give your hair a more natural look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your curls in place, finish off with a light hold hairspray. Hold the can of hairspray about 10 inches away from your hair and spray lightly. Avoid using too much hairspray as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect outwards curl:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a larger barrel to create bigger curls.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly as this can make your hair look unnatural.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid breaking the curls.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place without making your hair stiff.

FAQ

Can I curl my hair outwards if I have short hair?

Yes, you can curl your hair outwards even if you have short hair. Use a smaller curling iron or wand to create tighter curls.

Do I need to use a specific type of curling iron or wand?

No, you can use any type of curling iron or wand to curl your hair outwards. Just make sure to choose a barrel size that is appropriate for the length of your hair.

How long will the curls last?

The length of time that your curls will last depends on the texture of your hair, the products you use, and the weather conditions. Generally, the curls should last for several hours to a day.

Hair Care

Curling your hair can cause damage if you don't take care of your hair properly. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair when brushing or styling as this can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily make your hair curl outwards and achieve a more voluminous and bouncy look. Just remember to take care of your hair properly to prevent damage and keep your locks healthy and shiny.
