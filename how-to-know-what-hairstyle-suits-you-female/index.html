How To Know What Hairstyle Suits You Female A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 18, 2023 · 12 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Or are you simply unsure of what hairstyle would suit you best? Whatever the reason may be, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task. But fret not, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to know what hairstyle suits you female. From face shape to hair texture, we’ll cover all the important factors you need to consider before making the big chop.

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Understanding Your Hair Texture
  • Considering Your Lifestyle
  • Choosing the Right Hair Length
  • Picking the Perfect Hair Color
  • Selecting the Right Haircut Style

  • FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

Before you decide on a hairstyle, it’s important to determine your face shape. The right hairstyle can help accentuate your best features and balance out any imperfections. Here are the different face shapes and the hairstyles that suit them best:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck as almost any hairstyle will suit you. From pixie cuts to long, flowing locks, you can experiment with different styles and lengths without worrying about looking unflattering.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should opt for hairstyles that create the illusion of length. Long, layered cuts, and asymmetrical bobs work well for this face shape. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs as they can make your face appear rounder.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you should aim for hairstyles that soften your strong jawline. Soft curls, layered cuts, and side-swept bangs work well for this face shape. Avoid straight, blunt cuts as they can make your face look boxy.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you should aim for hairstyles that balance out your forehead and narrow chin. Long, side-swept bangs, and shoulder-length cuts work well for this face shape. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy, straight-across bangs as they can accentuate your forehead.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you should aim for hairstyles that soften your cheekbones and narrow forehead. Long, layered cuts, and side-swept bangs work well for this face shape. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy, straight-across bangs as they can accentuate your cheekbones.

Understanding Your Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair texture. Your hair texture can affect how a certain hairstyle looks on you. Here are the different hair textures and the hairstyles that suit them best:

Straight Hair

If you have straight hair, you can experiment with different lengths and styles. Bob cuts and blunt bangs work well for this hair texture. Avoid adding too many layers as they can make your hair look flat.

Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, you should aim for hairstyles that accentuate your natural waves. Shoulder-length cuts and long layers work well for this hair texture. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy layers as they can make your hair look frizzy.

Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, you should opt for hairstyles that help define your curls. Layered cuts and long bobs work well for this hair texture. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy layers as they can make your hair look bushy.

Kinky Hair

If you have kinky hair, you should aim for hairstyles that help define your curls and add volume. Short cuts and layered styles work well for this hair texture. Avoid straightening your hair too often as it can damage your hair.

Considering Your Lifestyle

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your lifestyle. Your hair should complement your lifestyle and be easy to maintain. Here are some questions to ask yourself:

  • Do you have a busy schedule and little time to style your hair?
  • Are you frequently exposed to the sun or harsh weather conditions?
  • Do you participate in sports or physical activities?

Based on your answers, you can choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle. For example, if you have a busy schedule, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance style like a messy bun or a sleek ponytail. If you’re frequently exposed to the sun, you may want to avoid coloring your hair too often as it can damage your hair. If you participate in sports or physical activities, you may want to opt for a short, easy-to-manage style.

Choosing the Right Hair Length

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, it’s time to choose the right hair length. Here are the different hair lengths and the hairstyles that suit them best:

Short Hair

Short hair is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style. Pixie cuts, bobs, and shags work well for this hair length. Short hair can help accentuate your facial features and give you a bold, edgy look.

Medium Hair

Medium hair is versatile and can be styled in many ways. Shoulder-length cuts, long bobs, and layered styles work well for this hair length. Medium hair can help soften your facial features and give you a youthful, fresh look.

Long Hair

Long hair is perfect for those who want a feminine, romantic look. Straight, wavy, and curly styles work well for this hair length. Long hair can help balance out your facial features and give you a soft, ethereal look.

Picking the Perfect Hair Color

Your hair color can also affect how a certain hairstyle looks on you. The right hair color can complement your skin tone and bring out your best features. Here are some tips on picking the perfect hair color:

  • Determine your skin tone. Are you warm-toned or cool-toned?
  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. For warm-toned skin, opt for warm, golden hues. For cool-toned skin, opt for cool, ashy hues.
  • Consider the maintenance. Do you want a low-maintenance or high-maintenance hair color?

Based on these tips, you can choose a hair color that suits you best. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair color.

Selecting the Right Haircut Style

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, hair length, and hair color, it’s time to select the right haircut style. Here are some popular haircut styles:

  • Bob cuts
  • Layered cuts
  • Pixie cuts
  • Shags
  • Asymmetrical cuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Blunt bangs
  • Long layers
  • Short bobs
  • Sleek ponytails
  • Messy buns
  • Half-up, half-down styles

Based on your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, hair length, and hair color, you can choose a haircut style that suits you best. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Once you’ve chosen the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy
