How To Choose Hair Color To Match Skin Tone

Written by Frank Aug 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hair color, it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone. The right hair color can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to choose hair color to match skin tone.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose Hair Color to Match Skin Tone
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can be a daunting task, but with a little knowledge and some research, you can find the perfect shade. Your skin tone is determined by the amount of melanin in your skin, which affects how light or dark your skin appears. When choosing a hair color, you want to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Keep reading to learn how to choose hair color to match skin tone.

How to Choose Hair Color to Match Skin Tone

When choosing a hair color, it's important to consider your skin tone. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair color:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories: warm, cool, and neutral. If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow, peach, or golden hue. If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink, red, or blue hue. If you have neutral undertones, your skin will have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

2. Choose a Hair Color Based on Your Skin Tone

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair color that complements it. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have warm undertones, choose hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, copper, auburn, or warm brown.
  • If you have cool undertones, choose hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, burgundy, or black.
  • If you have neutral undertones, you can choose hair colors from either the warm or cool categories, but avoid extremes.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing a hair color to match your skin tone:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist or the inside of your arm.
  2. Choose a hair color based on your skin tone.
  3. Consider your natural hair color and whether you want to go lighter or darker.
  4. Take into account your eye color and natural makeup tones.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to determine what shades will work best for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance the color will require.
  • Choose a shade that complements your skin tone, eye color, and natural makeup tones.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual makeover tools to get an idea of how different shades will look on you.
  • Experiment with temporary hair color before committing to a permanent change.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing hair color to match skin tone:

What hair colors are best for warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with golden blonde, honey blonde, copper, auburn, or warm brown hair colors.

What hair colors are best for cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, burgundy, or black hair colors.

How do I know if I have warm or cool undertones?

You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist or the inside of your arm. If your veins appear greenish, you have warm undertones. If your veins appear bluish, you have cool undertones. If your veins appear both green and blue, you have neutral undertones.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it looking its best.

Choosing the right hair color can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and beautiful. By following these tips and guidelines, you can choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and makes you feel your best.
