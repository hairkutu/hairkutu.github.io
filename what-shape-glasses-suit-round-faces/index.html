What Shape Glasses Suit Round Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia May 30, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect pair of glasses can be a challenge. The wrong shape can make your face look even rounder, while the right shape can highlight your best features. In this article, we’ll explore what shape glasses suit round faces, as well as some tips for choosing the perfect pair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Shapes for Round Faces
  • Tips for Choosing Glasses for Round Faces
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the perfect pair of glasses, you need to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and look at your face in the mirror. The four main face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Oval

If your face is widest at the cheeks, with a rounded chin and forehead, you have a round face shape.

Best Shapes for Round Faces

The best glasses for round faces are those that add angles and definition to your face. The following shapes are a great place to start:

  • Rectangular
  • Square
  • Wayfarer
  • Browline

These shapes will help balance out the curves of your face and make it appear slimmer.

Tips for Choosing Glasses for Round Faces

When choosing glasses for round faces, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Avoid round glasses, which will only accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Choose glasses with angular or geometric shapes to add structure to your face.
  • Look for glasses with a wider frame to balance out the width of your face.
  • Avoid glasses with a low bridge, which will make your face appear even rounder.

FAQ

Q: Can round faces wear aviator glasses?

A: Yes, but make sure to choose an aviator style with a more square or rectangular frame to add definition to your face.

Q: Can round faces wear cat-eye glasses?

A: Yes, but choose a cat-eye style with a more angular shape to balance out the curves of your face.

Q: What color glasses should I choose for a round face?

A: Darker colors can help slim down your face, while lighter colors can make it appear wider. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair color.

Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

Wearing glasses can sometimes cause your hair to get caught in the frames or lose volume where the glasses sit on your ears. Try these hair care tips for glasses wearers:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume where the glasses sit on your ears.
  • Use a hair tie or clip to keep your hair away from your face and glasses while putting them on or taking them off.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair smooth and prevent it from getting caught in the frames.

By following these tips and choosing the right glasses for your face shape, you can find the perfect pair to highlight your best features and make you feel confident and stylish.
