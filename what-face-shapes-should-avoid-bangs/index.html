What Face Shapes Should Avoid Bangs

Written by Daniel Jun 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're considering bangs as your next hairstyle, it's important to know what face shapes look best with them. While bangs can look great on some people, they can also be a disaster for others. In this article, we'll go over what face shapes should avoid bangs and why.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Face Shapes to Avoid Bangs
  • Hair Care Tips for Bangs
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out what face shapes should avoid bangs is to determine your own face shape. Here are the basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face from your hairline to your chin. You can also use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, congratulations! You can rock almost any hairstyle, including bangs. Oval faces have a balanced proportion and can handle any length and style of bangs.

Face Shapes to Avoid Bangs

While bangs can look great on some people, they can also be a disaster for others. Here are the face shapes that should avoid bangs:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, avoid blunt bangs that fall straight across your forehead. This will make your face appear shorter and wider. Instead, go for side-swept bangs that are longer at the sides to create the illusion of length.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, avoid thick, heavy bangs that end at your eyebrows. This will make your face appear more boxy. Instead, go for soft, wispy bangs that are longer at the sides to soften your features.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, avoid bangs that are too short or too heavy. This will make your chin appear more narrow and pointy. Instead, go for soft, layered bangs that are longer at the sides to balance out your face.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, avoid heavy, blunt bangs that end at your eyebrows. This will make your forehead appear wider and your chin appear more narrow. Instead, go for side-swept bangs that are longer at the sides to soften your features.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

If you do decide to get bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your bangs frequently to prevent oil buildup.
  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

FAQs

Can I still get bangs if I have a face shape that should avoid them?

Yes, you can still get bangs if you have a face shape that should avoid them. Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape and consult with your hairstylist before making any drastic changes.

What type of bangs look best with an oval face shape?

Oval face shapes can handle any length and style of bangs. Try blunt, side-swept, or layered bangs to switch up your look.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent them from getting too long.

Now that you know what face shapes should avoid bangs, you can make an informed decision about whether or not to get them. Remember, the key to a great hairstyle is choosing one that flatters your unique features.
