How Long Do Braids Usually Last A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you considering getting braids but wondering how long they will last? Braids are a popular and versatile hairstyle that can last anywhere from a few days to several weeks or months, depending on various factors. In this article, we’ll break down everything you need to know about how long do braids usually last.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Braids
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Braiding
  • Tips for Maintaining Braids
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Braids

How to Get Braids

Getting braids involves sectioning your hair into small parts and weaving in extensions or your natural hair to create a pattern. The process can take anywhere from 2 to 8 hours, depending on the size and complexity of the braids. It's essential to find a skilled stylist who knows how to braid correctly and not too tightly, as tight braids can lead to hair damage and breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide to Braiding

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieve braids that will last for a long time:

  1. Start with clean and moisturized hair. Detangle your hair with a wide-toothed comb or your fingers.
  2. Section your hair into small parts and secure each section with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Take one section and split it into three equal parts. Begin braiding by crossing the right section over the middle, then the left section over the right, and so on.
  4. Add extensions or weave in your natural hair as you braid. Make sure to pull the extensions or natural hair tightly to prevent slipping.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section. Secure the end with a hair tie or rubber band.
  6. Repeat the process until all sections are braided.

Tips for Maintaining Braids

Here are some tips to help you maintain your braids and make them last longer:

  • Wrap your hair with a silk or satin scarf or bonnet at night to prevent frizz and tangling.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh your braids down and cause buildup.
  • Avoid getting your braids wet, especially for the first few days after installation, as this can cause the braids to loosen and unravel.
  • When washing your hair, use a diluted shampoo and focus on your scalp, avoiding the braids as much as possible.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or braid spray to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do braids usually last?

Braids can last anywhere from a few days to several weeks or months, depending on various factors such as the size and complexity of the braids, how well you take care of them, and your hair type.

Can you wash your hair with braids?

Yes, you can wash your hair with braids, but it's essential to be gentle and avoid getting the braids too wet, especially for the first few days after installation. Use a diluted shampoo and focus on your scalp, avoiding the braids as much as possible.

Can you swim with braids?

It's not recommended to swim with braids as the chlorine or saltwater can damage the braids and cause them to unravel. If you must swim, wear a swim cap or cover your braids with a scarf or hat to protect them.

Hair Care Tips for Braids

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy hair while wearing braids:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly with a light oil, such as jojoba or grapeseed oil.
  • Avoid wearing braids for too long, as this can cause hair damage and breakage.
  • Take breaks between braiding styles to allow your hair to rest and recover.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or rubber bands, as these can cause hair breakage and damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth and maintenance.

Overall, how long do braids usually last depends on several factors, including the size and complexity of the braids, how well you take care of them, and your hair type. By following the tips and guidelines in this article, you can help your braids last longer and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
