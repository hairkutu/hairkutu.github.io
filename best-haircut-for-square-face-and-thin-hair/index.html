Best Haircut For Square Face And Thin Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 08, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your square face and thin hair? Look no further! In this article, we will share the best haircut options for your face shape and hair type. From short to long, curly to straight, we've got you covered. Read on to discover the perfect hairstyle for you.

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and straight sides. If you also have thin hair, you may find it challenging to find a haircut that flatters your face shape and adds volume to your hair. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can make your hair look fuller and accentuate your best features.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Square Face and Thin Hair

The key to finding the best haircut for your face shape and hair type is to create balance and proportion. You want to choose a haircut that softens your angular features and adds volume to your hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume on top of your head, as they may make your face look wider.
  • Choose a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair, such as layers or waves.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or part, as they can soften your strong jawline.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your hair look flat and lifeless.
  • Choose a haircut that frames your face and draws attention to your best features.

Step by Step Guide to the Best Haircuts for Square Face and Thin Hair

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a great option for square faces and thin hair, as they can add volume and texture to your hair. Here are some of the best short haircuts for your face shape:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that adds texture and volume to your hair. It's a great option if you have thin hair, as it can make your hair look fuller and thicker. A pixie cut also draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones, which are often the best features of a square face.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. For a square face and thin hair, opt for a layered bob that adds volume and texture to your hair. You can also add a side-swept fringe to soften your strong jawline.

Medium Haircuts

Medium haircuts are a versatile option for square faces and thin hair. They can add volume and texture to your hair without being too short or too long. Here are some of the best medium haircuts for your face shape:

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option if you have thin hair, as it can add volume and texture to your hair. For a square face, opt for a layered cut that frames your face and draws attention to your best features. You can also add a side-swept fringe to soften your strong jawline.

Wavy Lob

A wavy lob is a long bob that adds texture and volume to your hair. It's a great option if you want to keep your hair long but still add some volume and texture. For a square face, opt for a wavy lob that frames your face and adds volume to your hair.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts can be a bit trickier for square faces and thin hair, as they can make your face look longer and narrower. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can still rock long hair. Here are some of the best long haircuts for your face shape:

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option if you have long, thin hair. It can add volume and texture to your hair without being too heavy. For a square face, opt for a layered cut that frames your face and adds volume to your hair. You can also add a side-swept fringe to soften your strong jawline.

Beach Waves

Beach waves are a great way to add texture and volume to your long, thin hair. You can achieve beach waves by using a curling iron or a hair straightener. For a square face, opt for beach waves that frame your face and add volume to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your square face and thin hair, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a square face and thin hair?

The best haircut for a square face and thin hair is one that adds volume and texture to your hair while softening your strong jawline. Some of the best haircuts for your face shape include the pixie cut, layered cut, bob cut, wavy lob, and beach waves.

Should I get a fringe if I have a square face and thin hair?

A fringe can be a great way to soften your strong jawline and add some volume to your hair. However, not all fringes are created equal. If you have a square face and thin hair, opt for a side-swept fringe that frames your face and draws attention to your best features.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face and Thin Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for square face and thin hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look flat.
  • Use a dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it's gentler on your hair than a brush.

Now that you know the best haircut for square face and thin hair, you can confidently head to the salon and ask for the perfect style for you. Remember to keep these tips in mind and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
