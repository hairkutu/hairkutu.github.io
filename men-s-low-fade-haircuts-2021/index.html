Men s Low Fade Haircuts 2021 A Guide

Written by Frank Aug 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy new haircut this year? Look no further than the men's low fade haircut! This style has been gaining popularity in recent years and shows no signs of slowing down in 2021. Keep reading for a guide on how to get and maintain this stylish haircut.

What is a low fade haircut?

A low fade haircut is a style where the hair gradually tapers from a longer length at the top to a shorter length at the bottom. This creates a clean, crisp look that's perfect for any occasion.

How to get a low fade haircut

If you're interested in getting a low fade haircut, the first step is to find a reputable barber or stylist who is experienced in this style. Once you've found someone you trust, you'll need to communicate exactly what you want. Bring in a photo or describe the look you're going for in detail to ensure you're both on the same page.

During the haircut, your stylist will use clippers to gradually fade your hair from the longer length at the top to the shorter length at the bottom. The exact length and placement of the fade will depend on your preferences and the shape of your head.

Step-by-step guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a men's low fade haircut:

Step 1: Choose your desired length for the top of your hair.

Some popular lengths for the top of a low fade haircut include 2-4 inches. Communicate your desired length to your stylist.

Step 2: Decide on the length of the fade.

There are various options for the length of the fade, including a bald fade, mid fade, and high fade. Choose the length that best suits your style preferences and facial features.

Step 3: Sit back and let your stylist fade your hair.

Your stylist will use clippers to gradually fade your hair from the longer length at the top to the shorter length at the bottom. This process may take some time, but the end result will be worth it!

Tips for maintaining a low fade haircut

Once you have your low fade haircut, you'll need to take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular touch-ups from your stylist to keep the fade looking fresh.
  • Invest in quality hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, which can damage your hair and make it harder to style.

Frequently asked questions

How often should I get a low fade haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your fade starts to look overgrown. Generally, you should plan to get a touch-up every 2-4 weeks.

Can I style my hair differently with a low fade?

Absolutely! The beauty of a low fade haircut is that it's versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find a look that works for you.

Hair care for men's low fade haircuts

Proper hair care is important for maintaining a healthy and stylish low fade haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair aggressively, as this can cause breakage and damage.

Conclusion

Men's low fade haircuts are a stylish and versatile option for anyone looking to switch up their look in 2021. Whether you're going for a bold and edgy look or a more classic style, a low fade is sure to turn heads. Talk to your stylist today to see if this haircut is right for you!
