What Does A 1 On The Side Look Like

Written by Frank Apr 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Welcome to 2023, where a 1 on the side is still a popular haircut for men. If you're not familiar with this haircut, it's a style where the hair on the sides and back of the head is cut to a length of 1/8 inch. The hair on the top of the head can be styled in various ways, depending on personal preference. In this article, we'll go over what a 1 on the side looks like, how to get it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Description

A 1 on the side haircut is a short, clean-cut style that's perfect for men who want a low-maintenance look. The hair on the sides and back of the head is cut to a length of 1/8 inch using clippers. This length is short enough to create a clean, streamlined look, but not so short that the scalp is visible. The hair on the top of the head can be left longer or styled in various ways, such as a crew cut, a quiff, or a slicked-back style.

How to Get a 1 on the Side Haircut

If you're interested in getting a 1 on the side haircut, here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose a Barber

Choose a barber who is experienced in giving short haircuts. Look for a barber who has a good reputation and reviews. Ask for recommendations from friends and family.

Step 2: Show a Picture

Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to your barber. This will help them understand exactly what you're looking for.

Step 3: Get Your Hair Cut

Sit back and let your barber work their magic. They'll use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head to a length of 1/8 inch. They may use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head, depending on the style you want.

Tips for Maintaining a 1 on the Side Haircut

Now that you have a 1 on the side haircut, here are some tips for maintaining it:

Tip 1: Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh, get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will prevent your hair from getting too long and losing its shape.

Tip 2: Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair looking and feeling its best. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Use a styling product, such as pomade or gel, to create your desired look.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair From the Sun

The sun can damage your hair and scalp, causing dryness and breakage. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

FAQ

Q: Is a 1 on the side haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a 1 on the side haircut can be adapted to suit any face shape. Your barber can adjust the length and style of the hair on the top of your head to complement your face shape.

Q: Can I style my 1 on the side haircut in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your 1 on the side haircut in various ways, such as a crew cut, a quiff, or a slicked-back style. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Q: Does a 1 on the side haircut require a lot of maintenance?

A: No, a 1 on the side haircut is low-maintenance. Regular trims and quality hair products are all you need to keep your haircut looking and feeling its best.

Hair Care

When getting a 1 on the side haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp and hair clean and healthy.

Tip 2: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair. A hair mask will help prevent dryness and breakage.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as a hair dryer or straightener, too often. Heat can damage your hair and cause breakage.

Now that you know what a 1 on the side haircut looks like, how to get it, and how to maintain it, you can confidently rock this stylish and low-maintenance look. Remember to choose a good barber, use quality hair products, and take care of your hair to keep it looking and feeling its best.
