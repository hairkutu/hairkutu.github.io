How To See How I Would Look With Short Hair

Written by Oliver Mar 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you thinking of cutting your hair short but not sure if it will suit you? Well, there is a way to see how you would look with short hair without actually cutting it. In this article, we will guide you on how to see how you would look with short hair using different methods.

Description

Short hair is a popular hairstyle that suits many face shapes and hair textures. However, not everyone feels confident enough to make the chop without knowing how it would look on them. Luckily, there are many ways to try out short hair without committing to it.

How to

Method 1: Virtual Makeover Tools

One of the easiest ways to see how you would look with short hair is by using virtual makeover tools. These tools allow you to upload your photo and try on different hairstyles, including short hair. Here's how to use it:

  1. Find a virtual makeover tool online, such as Modiface or Taaz.
  2. Upload a photo of yourself facing forward with your hair pulled back.
  3. Select the "short hair" option and browse through the different styles available.
  4. Adjust the length and color to find the perfect short hairstyle for you.
  5. Save the photo and share it with friends or family to get their opinion.

Method 2: Hair Extensions

If you want a more realistic idea of how you would look with short hair, you can try using hair extensions. Here's how to do it:

  1. Purchase a set of clip-in hair extensions that match your hair color.
  2. Trim the extensions to the length you want for your short hair.
  3. Clip in the extensions starting from the bottom of your head and working your way up.
  4. Style the extensions to create a short hairstyle that suits you.
  5. Take photos from different angles to see how you would look with short hair.

Method 3: Hair Cutting Apps

If you want a more hands-on approach, you can try using hair cutting apps. These apps allow you to take a photo of yourself and "cut" your hair to see how it would look. Here's how to use it:

  1. Download a hair cutting app, such as Hair Style.
  2. Take a photo of yourself facing forward with your hair pulled back.
  3. Select the "short hair" option and choose a style you like.
  4. Use the tools in the app to adjust the length and color of the hair.
  5. Save the photo and share it with friends or family to get their opinion.

Tips

  • When using virtual makeover tools or hair cutting apps, try to find ones that use realistic hair images.
  • Make sure to use clip-in extensions that are made with real hair for a more realistic look.
  • Take photos from different angles and in different lighting to get a better idea of how you would look with short hair.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to get their opinion on what short hairstyle would suit you best.

FAQ

Is it possible to try on short hair without cutting it?

Yes, there are many ways to see how you would look with short hair without actually cutting it. You can use virtual makeover tools, hair extensions, or hair cutting apps.

How do I choose the right short hairstyle for me?

It's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a short hairstyle. Consult with a hairstylist to get their opinion on what would suit you best.

Can I use these methods to try on other hairstyles?

Yes, virtual makeover tools, hair extensions, and hair cutting apps can be used to try on different hairstyles, not just short ones.

Hair Care

If you do decide to cut your hair short, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling. Regular trims will also help to keep your short hairstyle looking fresh and healthy.
