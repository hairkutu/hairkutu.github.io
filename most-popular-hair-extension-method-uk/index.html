The Most Popular Hair Extension Method In Uk Everything You Need To Know

Written by Eveline Mar 23, 2023 ¬∑ 7 min read

If you're thinking about getting hair extensions, you're not alone. Hair extensions have become increasingly popular in the UK over the past decade, with more and more women (and men!) opting for this hair transformation method. In this article, we'll discuss the most popular hair extension method in the UK, how to get them, tips, FAQs, and hair care. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Hair Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Hair Extensions
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Hair Extensions

Description

The most popular hair extension method in the UK is the tape-in hair extension method. This method involves attaching small wefts of hair to your natural hair using double-sided tape. The tape is then heated to secure the weft in place. Tape-in hair extensions are popular because they are easy to install, can be styled just like your natural hair, and are affordable compared to other hair extension methods.

Another popular hair extension method in the UK is the clip-in method. This method involves attaching small wefts of hair to your natural hair using clips. Clip-in hair extensions are popular because they are easy to install and remove, can be styled just like your natural hair, and are affordable compared to other hair extension methods. However, clip-in hair extensions are not recommended for daily wear as the clips can cause damage to your natural hair over time.

How to Get Hair Extensions

The first step in getting hair extensions is to find a reputable salon that specializes in hair extensions. Look for salons that have experience with the tape-in or clip-in method and have positive reviews from previous clients. Once you've found a salon, book a consultation appointment to discuss your options and any concerns you may have.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting tape-in hair extensions:

  1. Book a consultation appointment with a reputable salon.
  2. Choose the length, color, and texture of your hair extensions.
  3. On the day of your appointment, your natural hair will be washed and blow-dried.
  4. The stylist will section your hair and apply the double-sided tape to the roots of your natural hair.
  5. The stylist will attach the tape-in wefts to the tape on your natural hair.
  6. The stylist will blend the tape-in wefts with your natural hair and style your hair as desired.
  7. You're ready to show off your new hair extensions!

Tips for Maintaining Hair Extensions

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair extensions:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair extensions.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair extensions.
  • Brush your hair extensions regularly to prevent tangles.
  • Avoid going to bed with wet hair extensions.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to prevent tangling and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair extensions:

How long do hair extensions last?

Hair extensions can last up to six months with proper care and maintenance.

Can I dye my hair extensions?

It is not recommended to dye your hair extensions as the chemicals can damage the hair. If you want to change the color of your hair extensions, it's best to purchase new extensions in the desired color.

Can I swim with hair extensions?

Yes, you can swim with hair extensions, but it's important to rinse your hair thoroughly with fresh water after swimming in a pool or the ocean to prevent damage from chlorine or saltwater.

Hair Care for Hair Extensions

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the health and longevity of your hair extensions. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair extensions.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair extensions.
  • Brush your hair extensions regularly to prevent tangles.
  • Avoid going to bed with wet hair extensions.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to prevent tangling and breakage.

And there you have it ‚Äì everything you need to know about the most popular hair extension method in the UK. Whether you opt for tape-in or clip-in hair extensions, make sure to follow the tips and care instructions to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
