Shoulder Length Hair With Bangs For Chubby Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of having the same old hairstyle that doesn't flatter your chubby face? Look no further, because we have the perfect solution for you. Shoulder length hair with bangs is a great option for those with a rounder face shape. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as some tips and FAQs to help you maintain it.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs for Chubby Face

Before we get into the styling process, it's important to note that shoulder length hair with bangs is a versatile look that can be customized to suit your specific face shape and hair type. With that said, here are the general steps to achieve this style:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to determine the ideal length for your hair. Shoulder length hair should fall just below the shoulders, while the bangs should hit at or above the eyebrows. This length will help to elongate your face and draw attention away from any roundness.

Step 2: Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing the right style for your face shape, it's important to consider your features. For a rounder face shape, opt for side-swept bangs that will help to create a more angular appearance.

Step 3: Cut and Style Your Hair

Once you have determined the length and style of your hair, it's time to get it cut and styled. We recommend going to a professional hair stylist who can help you achieve the perfect look.

During your appointment, your stylist will likely use a razor or scissors to create layers that will add volume and movement to your hair. They may also suggest adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut and styled, it's time to maintain the look. To style shoulder length hair with bangs, start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume. Next, use a flat iron or curling wand to add waves or curls. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs

Now that you have achieved the perfect shoulder length hair with bangs for your chubby face, it's important to maintain the look. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

To keep your hair looking its best, use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have thick hair, use a smoothing serum to help tame frizz.

Tip 2: Trim Your Bangs Regularly

Bangs can be high maintenance, so it's important to trim them regularly to keep them looking their best. We recommend trimming your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their length and shape.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

To avoid damage to your hair, protect it from heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray. You should also avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair with bangs for chubby face:

Q: Can shoulder length hair with bangs be styled in different ways?

A: Yes, shoulder length hair with bangs is a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways. You can wear it straight, curly, or wavy, and you can also experiment with different hair accessories like headbands and clips.

Q: Is shoulder length hair with bangs suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, shoulder length hair with bangs can be customized to suit all hair types. Your hair stylist can help you determine the best length and style for your specific hair type and face shape.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: We recommend washing your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently, while those with dry hair can go longer between washes.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hair with bangs looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

To avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils, use a gentle shampoo that is free from harsh chemicals. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients like argan oil and coconut oil.

Tip 2: Condition Regularly

To keep your hair moisturized and tangle-free, use a conditioner after every wash. Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots, and leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. To protect your hair, wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner that contains SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect shoulder length hair with bangs for your chubby face and keep it looking its best. So why wait? Book an appointment with your hair stylist today and start rocking the perfect hairstyle!
