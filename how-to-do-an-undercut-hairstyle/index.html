How To Do An Undercut Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Luffy Apr 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that's trendy and low maintenance? Look no further than the undercut! This versatile hairstyle has been popular for years, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this article, we'll go over how to do an undercut hairstyle step by step, as well as provide tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to keep your new do looking fresh.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do an Undercut Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Do an Undercut Hairstyle

First, let's go over what an undercut is. An undercut is a hairstyle where the sides and back of the head are shaved or cut very short, while the top is left longer. This creates a contrast between the short sides and longer top, and allows for a variety of styling options.

To do an undercut hairstyle, you'll need clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back short, and a comb and scissors to shape the top. You can do this at home, but it's recommended to go to a professional stylist if you're not confident in your cutting skills.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to do an undercut hairstyle:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair with a clip, leaving the sides and back exposed.
  3. Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back short, depending on how short you want it.
  4. Blend the sides and back into the top using scissors or clippers with a guard.
  5. Unclip the top section of your hair and comb it to the desired length.
  6. Use scissors to shape the top, keeping in mind the length you want to maintain.
  7. Style the top as desired, using products like pomade or wax to hold it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut

Now that you have your new undercut, here are some tips to keep it looking fresh:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back to keep the contrast between the short sides and longer top.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Consider getting touch-ups from a professional stylist every few weeks to keep your undercut looking sharp.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercuts:

  • Will an undercut work for my face shape?

    • Yes! An undercut can work for any face shape, as long as you choose the right length and shape for the top. Talk to your stylist about what will work best for you.

  • Can I still style my hair with an undercut?

    • Yes! An undercut allows for a variety of styling options, from slicked back to messy and textured. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works for you.

  • Is an undercut high maintenance?

    • No! An undercut is actually low maintenance, as long as you keep up with regular trims and use quality hair care products.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your undercut looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to keep your undercut looking fresh.

Congratulations, you're now an expert on how to do an undercut hairstyle! Whether you're going for a bold, edgy look or a more subtle style, an undercut is a great choice for anyone looking for a low maintenance, trendy hairstyle.
