How To Style Long Hair Boy A Guide To Perfecting The Look

Written by Frank Aug 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Long hair for boys has become increasingly popular in recent years. It's a versatile look that allows for a range of styles, from messy and carefree to sleek and polished. If you're a boy with long hair, you may be wondering how to style it to achieve the look you want. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style long hair boy, along with tips and tricks to help you perfect your look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Long Hair Boy
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Style Long Hair Boy

The first step to styling long hair boy is to determine the look you want to achieve. Do you want a messy, carefree style or a sleek, polished one? Once you've decided on the look, you can start working on achieving it.

There are several ways to style long hair for boys, including:

  • Ponytail
  • Man Bun
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Side Part
  • Undercut

Each of these styles requires a different technique, so it's important to know which one you want to achieve before you begin. Once you've decided on the style, you can follow the step-by-step guide below to achieve it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ponytail

To achieve a ponytail, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck.
  3. Tie it with a hair elastic.
  4. Adjust the ponytail to your desired height and tightness.

You can also add accessories such as a headband or hair tie to add some flair to your look.

Man Bun

To achieve a man bun, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head.
  3. Tie it with a hair elastic.
  4. Twist the ponytail into a bun.
  5. Secure it with another hair elastic or bobby pins.

You can also leave some hair out around your face for a more relaxed look.

Half-Up Half-Down

To achieve a half-up half-down style, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather the top section of your hair, from your temples to the crown of your head.
  3. Tie it with a hair elastic.
  4. Adjust the ponytail to your desired height and tightness.

You can also add accessories such as a headband or hair tie to add some flair to your look.

Side Part

To achieve a side part, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Choose which side you want your part on.
  3. Using a comb, create a part on your chosen side.
  4. Style your hair as desired.

You can also add some hair product such as gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Undercut

To achieve an undercut, follow these steps:

  1. Choose which side you want your undercut on.
  2. Shave or buzz the hair on the sides and back of your head.
  3. Leave the top section of your hair long.
  4. Style the top section of your hair as desired.

You can also add some hair product such as gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect long hair boy look:

  • Use a quality hairbrush to remove any tangles and knots.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to add some accessories such as headbands, hair ties, or hats to add some flair to your look.
  • If you're growing out your hair, be patient and keep it healthy with regular trims and conditioning.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long hair for boys:

Can boys have long hair?

Yes, boys can have long hair. Long hair for boys has become increasingly popular in recent years.

What are some popular long hair styles for boys?

Some popular long hair styles for boys include ponytails, man buns, half-up half-down, side parts, and undercuts.

How do I keep my long hair healthy?

To keep your long hair healthy, it's important to wash and condition it regularly, avoid using too much heat on it, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Long hair requires proper care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for long hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangler brush to remove any tangles or knots.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your long hair boy look with ease. Whether you prefer a messy, carefree style or a sleek, polished one, there's a long hair style for every boy. So go ahead and experiment with different looks until you find the one that suits you best!
