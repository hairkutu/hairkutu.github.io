Mens Haircuts Long On Top Wavy A Guide To Stylish And Easy To Manage Hair

Written by Aprilia Apr 05, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and low-maintenance haircut, mens haircuts long on top wavy can be a great option. This style is characterized by longer hair on the top of the head, with wavy or curly texture that adds volume and movement. Whether you prefer a messy, bohemian look or a sleek, polished finish, this haircut can be adapted to suit your personal style.

Description

Mens haircuts long on top wavy are a popular choice for men of all ages and hair types. This style typically involves leaving the hair on the top of the head longer than the sides and back, with layers added to create texture and movement. The hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy and tousled to slick and polished, depending on the occasion and personal preference.

How to

If you're interested in trying out a mens haircut long on top wavy, here are the basic steps to achieve the look:

  1. Start by growing out your hair on the top of your head, leaving it longer than the sides and back. You can ask your barber or stylist to help you determine the ideal length for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Request layers to be added to your hair to create movement and texture. This will help to enhance the wavy or curly texture of your hair.
  3. To style your hair, apply a small amount of styling product (such as a pomade or wax) to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the top and sides. You can use a comb or your fingers to create your desired look.
  4. For a more polished finish, use a hair dryer and a round brush to blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. This will create a sleeker, more groomed look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place throughout the day.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving a mens haircut long on top wavy:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting your hair cut, schedule a consultation with your barber or stylist. Discuss your desired length, texture, and styling preferences, and ask for their recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Haircut

Once you've agreed on a plan, your barber or stylist will begin cutting your hair. They may start by trimming the sides and back, leaving the hair on top longer. They may also add layers to create texture and movement.

Step 3: Styling

After your haircut, your stylist will show you how to style your hair at home. They may recommend a specific styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to enhance the wavy texture of your hair. They may also demonstrate different styling techniques, such as using a comb or your fingers to create a messy or polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens haircut long on top wavy:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or stylist for advice on how to maintain your haircut and style.

FAQ

What face shapes suit mens haircuts long on top wavy?

Mens haircuts long on top wavy can suit a variety of face shapes, but they tend to work particularly well for those with oval or heart-shaped faces. The longer hair on top can help to balance out a wider jawline or forehead, while the wavy texture adds volume and movement.

Can mens haircuts long on top wavy work for all hair types?

Mens haircuts long on top wavy can work for many different hair types, but they tend to be most effective on those with naturally wavy or curly hair. If you have straight hair, you may need to use styling products or heat tools to create the desired texture.

How do I style mens haircuts long on top wavy?

To style mens haircuts long on top wavy, start by applying a small amount of styling product to your fingertips and working it through your hair. You can use a comb or your fingers to create your desired look, whether that's a messy, tousled style or a sleek, polished finish. You can also use a hair dryer and a round brush to create more volume and movement.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut long on top wavy looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This can include using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy, as well as using styling products that are designed to enhance your hair's natural texture. You may also want to consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and free of split ends.

Overall, mens haircuts long on top wavy can be a stylish and low-maintenance option for men who want a versatile and easy-to-manage hairstyle. Whether you prefer a messy, bohemian look or a sleek, polished finish, this haircut can be adapted to suit your personal style and preferences. With the right care and styling techniques, you can keep your hair looking its best and enjoy a great-looking haircut that makes you feel confident and stylish.
