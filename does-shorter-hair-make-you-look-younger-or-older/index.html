Does Shorter Hair Make You Look Younger Or Older

Written by Daniel May 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering cutting your hair short, you may be wondering if it will make you look younger or older. The truth is, it can go either way. While short hair can give you a fresh and youthful look, it can also make you appear older if not done correctly. In this article, we'll explore the question of whether shorter hair makes you look younger or older and provide tips on how to achieve a youthful look with a shorter hairstyle.

Description

Short hair has been a popular trend for many years, and it's not hard to see why. It's low maintenance, versatile, and can give you a fresh and youthful look. However, not all short hairstyles are created equal. Depending on the cut and styling, shorter hair can either make you look younger or older. It's important to understand the different factors that come into play when choosing a short hairstyle that will flatter your features and make you look youthful.

How to Achieve a Youthful Look with Short Hair

If you're looking to achieve a youthful look with short hair, there are a few things to consider:

1. Choose the Right Cut

The first step in achieving a youthful look with short hair is to choose the right cut. A pixie cut or a bob that sits just above the chin can be a great choice for many people. These cuts can help to open up your face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Avoid styles that are too severe or angular, as they can make you appear older.

2. Add Layers

Adding layers to your short hairstyle can help to create movement and volume. This can give your hair a more youthful and playful look. Talk to your stylist about adding layers that will flatter your face shape and hair texture.

3. Experiment with Color

Adding color to your short hair can also help to create a youthful look. Highlights or lowlights can add dimension and depth to your hair, while a bold color can add a fun and playful touch. Talk to your stylist about which colors will best complement your skin tone and eye color.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving a Youthful Look with Short Hair

1. Choose the right cut for your face shape and hair texture.

2. Add layers to create movement and volume.

3. Experiment with color to add dimension and depth to your hair.

4. Use hair products that are designed to add volume and texture.

5. Style your hair in a way that flatters your features and complements your outfit.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

1. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

2. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.

3. Use styling products sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.

4. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Short hair can make you look older if the cut and styling are too severe or angular. However, if you choose the right cut and add layers and color, short hair can have a youthful and playful look.

Q: How often should I get my short hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your short hair looking fresh and healthy.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in an updo with short hair?

A: Yes, there are many options for styling short hair in an updo. Talk to your stylist about which styles will work best for your hair length and texture.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair can be low maintenance, but it still requires proper care to stay healthy and stylish. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type, and get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy. Use styling products sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair, and protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools. With proper care and styling, short hair can be a great choice for a youthful and playful look.
