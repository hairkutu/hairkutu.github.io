How To Style Curly Hair Wet Look A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Jun 13, 2023 ¬∑ 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's unique and beautiful, but on the other, it can be difficult to style. The wet look is a popular style that can be achieved with curly hair. In this guide, we'll go over how to style curly hair wet look in 2023.

Description

The wet look is a style that has been around for decades. It's a popular choice because it's easy to achieve and looks great on all hair types, including curly hair. The wet look is achieved by using products that give the hair a wet appearance. This can be done with gel, mousse, or even water.

How To

The wet look is an easy style to achieve. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in achieving the wet look is to wash your hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair. This will help define your curls and keep them looking great. Use a dime-sized amount of product and work it through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 3: Add More Product

If your hair is very curly, you may need to add more product to achieve the wet look. Apply a small amount of gel or mousse to your hair and work it through with your fingers. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair. This will help distribute the product and define your curls. Be sure to comb your hair gently to avoid damaging your curls.

Step 5: Add Water

If you want a more dramatic wet look, you can spray your hair with water. Use a spray bottle to mist your hair lightly. Be sure to distribute the water evenly throughout your hair.

Step 6: Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat to dry your hair. Avoid using a blow dryer on high heat, as this can damage your curls and cause frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect wet look:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Apply product to damp hair for best results.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Avoid using a blow dryer on high heat to dry your hair.
  • Use a diffuser on low heat to dry your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curly hair wet look:

Q: Can I achieve the wet look with natural hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve the wet look with natural hair. Use a curl enhancing product and mist your hair with water for a more dramatic wet look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use gel or mousse on dry hair?

A: Yes, you can use gel or mousse on dry hair, but it's best to use it on damp hair for best results.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your curly hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips and using the steps outlined in this guide, you can achieve the perfect wet look for your curly hair in 2023. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
