Short Haircuts For Naturally Curly Hair And Round Face

Written by Bardi Aug 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of dealing with long curly hair that just won't behave? Do you have a round face and are struggling to find a haircut that flatters your features? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for naturally curly hair and round face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts for naturally curly hair and round face can be tricky to find. You want a haircut that complements your natural curls while also highlighting your features. It's important to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and brings out the best in your curls. In this article, we will explore some of the best short haircuts for naturally curly hair and round face.

How to

The first step to finding the perfect short haircut for your naturally curly hair and round face is to determine your face shape. Measure the widest part of your face, which is typically across the cheekbones. If this measurement is roughly the same as the length of your face, you likely have a round face shape.

Next, consider the following factors:

  • Your hair's texture
  • The thickness of your hair
  • Your personal style preferences

Step by Step

Once you have determined your face shape and considered the above factors, it's time to choose a short haircut that will flatter your naturally curly hair and round face. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Research short haircuts for naturally curly hair and round face online.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair.
  3. Consider trying out a few different haircuts to see which one you like best.
  4. Experiment with styling techniques to find the best way to highlight your curls.

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for naturally curly hair and round face, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long.
  • Choose a haircut with layers to add dimension to your curls.
  • Consider adding bangs to frame your face.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your curls.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for naturally curly hair and round face?

The best short haircut for naturally curly hair and round face is one that flatters your face shape and highlights your curls. Consider a layered bob, a pixie cut, or a curly shag.

Should I add bangs to my short haircut?

If you have a round face, adding bangs can help to frame your face and make it appear slimmer. However, if you have a high forehead, you may want to avoid bangs.

How can I style my short curly hair?

Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your curls. Consider using a diffuser to enhance your curls, or using a curling iron to create loose waves.

Hair Care

Caring for your naturally curly hair is important to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your curls.

Now that you have some tips on choosing the best short haircut for your naturally curly hair and round face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can embrace your natural curls and feel confident in your new look.
