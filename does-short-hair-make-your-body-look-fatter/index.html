Does Short Hair Make Your Body Look Fatter

Written by Oliver May 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are thinking of getting a short haircut, you may be wondering if it will make your body look fatter. This is a common concern, especially among women who have a round or fuller face. In this article, we will explore this question and provide you with tips and advice to help you make the best decision for your hair and body.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The idea that short hair makes your body look fatter is a common myth. It is not the length of your hair that makes your body appear larger, but rather the style and cut of your hair. If you choose a hairstyle that complements your features and body type, you can look fabulous regardless of hair length.

How to

Choosing the right short haircut can be a daunting task, especially if you are not sure what style will suit your face and body shape. Here are a few tips to help you choose the right short haircut:

1. Consider your face shape

The first step in choosing the right short haircut is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for a short haircut that adds height to your crown, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any short haircut you like.

2. Think about your body shape

Your body shape can also influence the type of short haircut that will suit you. If you have a fuller figure, you may want to opt for a short haircut that adds volume to the top of your head and keeps the sides and back relatively short. This will create a more balanced look and make your body appear slimmer.

3. Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a short haircut, it is important to consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule or are always on the go, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on your hair, you may want to choose a more high-maintenance haircut that requires regular trims and styling.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when getting a short haircut:

1. Find inspiration

Before you head to the salon, it is a good idea to find inspiration for your new haircut. Browse magazines, websites, and social media to find pictures of short haircuts that you like.

2. Consult with a hairstylist

Once you have found some inspiration, book a consultation with a hairstylist. They will be able to advise you on the best short haircut for your face and body shape and offer suggestions on how to style your new haircut.

3. Get the haircut

Once you have decided on your new haircut, it is time to get it done. Your hairstylist will cut your hair to the desired length and style it to suit your face and body shape.

4. Maintain your haircut

To keep your short haircut looking great, it is important to maintain it. This means getting regular trims and styling it on a daily basis. Your hairstylist will be able to advise you on how often to get trims and how to style your new haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you rock your short haircut:

1. Experiment with different styles

Short haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

2. Use the right products

Using the right products can help you achieve the perfect short haircut. Invest in a good quality shampoo, conditioner, and styling products.

3. Embrace your new look

Getting a short haircut can be a big change, but it can also be a great opportunity to embrace a new look. Be confident and rock your new haircut with pride.

FAQ

Q: Does short hair make your body look fatter?

A: Short hair does not make your body look fatter. It is the style and cut of your hair that can influence how your body appears.

Q: What is the best short haircut for a round face?

A: The best short haircut for a round face is one that adds height to the crown of your head and keeps the sides and back relatively short. A pixie cut or a short bob with layers can be a great option for a round face.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

1. Get regular trims

To keep your short haircut looking fresh and neat, it is important to get regular trims. Your hairstylist can advise you on how often to get trims based on your hair type and style.

2. Use the right products

Using the right products can help you achieve the perfect short haircut. Invest in a good quality shampoo, conditioner, and styling products.

3. Style your hair daily

To keep your short haircut looking its best, you will need to style it on a daily basis. Your hairstylist can advise you on how to style your hair based on your hair type and style.

In conclusion, short hair does not make your body look fatter. It is the style and cut of your hair that can influence how your body appears. By choosing the right short haircut for your face and body shape and maintaining it properly, you can look and feel great with short hair.
