How To Change Hair In Animal Crossing Pocket Camp A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby May 20, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're an Animal Crossing Pocket Camp player, you probably know that one of the most exciting things about the game is customizing your character's appearance. One of the most significant parts of your character's look is their hair, and changing it up can be just the thing you need to refresh your gameplay experience. Here's how to change hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp.

Description

Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is a mobile game that allows players to create and decorate their own campsite, meet animal friends, and complete tasks to earn rewards. One of the most exciting things about the game is customizing your character's appearance, which includes changing your hair.

How to Change Hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

Changing your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is a straightforward process. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Open the Menu

Start by opening the game's menu. You can do this by tapping the icon in the bottom right corner of the screen.

Step 2: Go to the Settings Menu

In the menu, you'll see a gear icon in the top right corner. Tap on that to open the settings menu.

Step 3: Tap on the "Edit Character" Button

In the settings menu, you'll see an option called "Edit Character." Tap on that to open the character customization screen.

Step 4: Tap on the "Hair" Icon

In the character customization screen, you'll see a series of icons along the bottom. Tap on the "Hair" icon to open the hair customization screen.

Step 5: Choose a New Hair Style

In the hair customization screen, you'll see a variety of hair styles to choose from. Scroll through them to find one you like, and then tap on it to select it.

Step 6: Confirm Your Selection

After you've chosen a new hair style, you'll be taken back to the character customization screen. Tap on the "Save" button in the bottom right corner to confirm your selection.

Tips for Changing Hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp:

  • Try on different styles before choosing one
  • Consider matching your hair to the season or current events
  • Don't worry, you can always change your hair back later

FAQ

How much does it cost to change your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp?

Changing your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is free.

Can I change my hair color?

No, you cannot change your hair color in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp.

Can I change my hair style more than once?

Yes, you can change your hair style as many times as you like in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

While changing your hair style in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is a fun way to update your character's look, it's important to remember to take care of your hair, just like you would in real life. Here are some tips:

  • Use shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair (like from a hair dryer or straightener)

Now that you know how to change hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp, you can mix up your character's look whenever you like. Have fun!
