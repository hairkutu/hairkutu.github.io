How To Cut Long Hair Short With Layers

Written by Oliver Aug 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're thinking of cutting your long hair short, adding layers can give it movement and texture. But before you pick up the scissors, it's important to know how to do it properly. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about cutting long hair short with layers.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Long Hair Short with Layers
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Long Hair Short with Layers
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Long Hair Short with Layers

Description

Long hair can be beautiful, but it can also be heavy and difficult to manage. Cutting your hair short with layers can give it a fresh look and make it easier to style. Layers can add volume, texture, and movement to your hair, which is why they are a popular choice for those who want to update their look. However, cutting your hair short with layers is not a decision to be taken lightly. It's important to understand the process and know what to expect before you make the cut.

How to Cut Long Hair Short with Layers

Cutting long hair short with layers can be done at home or by a professional stylist. If you're doing it yourself, you'll need a few basic tools, including sharp scissors, a comb, and clips. You'll also need to decide on the length and style you want, whether it's a blunt cut or a more textured look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut long hair short with layers:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural texture and movement of your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back.
  3. Take the top section of your hair and clip it out of the way.
  4. Decide on the length you want for your hair and cut straight across. This will be your guide for the rest of your hair.
  5. Take the left side section of your hair and comb it straight down. Use your guide to cut the hair straight across.
  6. Use your guide to cut the right side section of your hair in the same way.
  7. Next, take a small section of hair from the back and comb it straight up. Use your guide to cut the hair straight across.
  8. Continue to take small sections of hair from the back and cut them in the same way, making sure to use your guide to keep the length even.
  9. Once you've cut all of your hair to the desired length, it's time to add layers. Take small sections of hair and hold them straight up. Cut into the hair at a slight angle to create layers.
  10. Continue to cut small sections of hair at a slight angle to create layers throughout your hair.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair Short with Layers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting long hair short with layers:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it may look shorter when dry.
  • Take breaks if you need to, and step away from the mirror to get a fresh perspective.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long hair short with layers:

Can I cut my own hair short with layers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair short with layers, but it's important to have the right tools and know what you're doing. If you're unsure, it's best to go to a professional stylist.

What's the best way to add layers to short hair?

The best way to add layers to short hair is to take small sections of hair and cut into them at a slight angle. This will create texture and movement in the hair.

How do I know if short hair with layers will suit me?

Short hair with layers can suit many face shapes and hair types. It's best to consult with a professional stylist to determine if it's the right look for you.

Hair Care After Cutting Long Hair Short with Layers

After cutting your long hair short with layers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims will also help maintain the shape of your cut and prevent split ends.

Cutting long hair short with layers can be a big change, but it can also be a refreshing and exciting way to update your look. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and flattering cut that suits your face shape and hair type.
