Best Hair Color For Brunettes Over 60 A Guide To Flattering And Age Appropriate Hair Color

Written by Aprilia Apr 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a brunette over 60 looking for the best hair color that will flatter your skin tone and make you look younger? With so many hair color options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we’ll share our top picks for the best hair color for brunettes over 60, how to choose the right shade, and hair care tips to maintain your color. Let’s dive in!

Why Choosing the Right Hair Color Is Important

As we age, our skin tone tends to change. This means that the hair color that worked for us in our 20s may not be as flattering in our 60s. Choosing the right hair color can help enhance your natural features, complement your skin tone, and make you look more youthful.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

When choosing a hair color, consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm colors like golden brown or caramel. If you have cool undertones, choose cool colors like ash brown or burgundy. If you’re not sure what your undertones are, look at the veins on your wrist – if they appear blue, you have cool undertones, and if they appear green, you have warm undertones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Choose a color based on your skin tone (warm or cool)
  3. Consider your natural hair color and eye color
  4. Consult with a hair stylist to determine the best shade for you

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you’ve chosen the right hair color, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain your color

FAQs

1. Can I dye my hair at home?

While it’s possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting with a professional stylist to ensure you achieve the best results. A stylist will be able to choose the right shade for you and apply the color evenly.

2. Will hair dye damage my hair?

Hair dye can cause damage to your hair if it’s not applied correctly or if you don’t take care of your hair after coloring. To minimize damage, use color-safe products and avoid heat styling tools.

3. How often should I touch up my hair color?

The frequency of touch-ups will depend on the type of hair color you have and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, touch-ups are recommended every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips

Aside from using color-safe products and avoiding heat styling tools, there are other hair care tips you can follow to maintain the health of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair color for brunettes over 60 is all about finding a shade that complements your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. By following our tips and taking care of your hair, you can maintain a youthful and vibrant look. Remember to consult with a professional stylist to achieve the best results.
