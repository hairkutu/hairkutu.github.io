How Do I Know What Kind Of Haircut Suits Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't suit you? Do you find yourself confused about what kind of haircut suits your face shape and hair type? Look no further, as we have created a comprehensive guide to answer your question: How do I know what kind of haircut suits me?

Description

The right haircut can do wonders for your appearance, making you look younger, fresher, and more confident. However, choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, especially if you have no idea what suits you. In this article, we'll show you how to determine your face shape and hair type, and provide tips on finding a haircut that suits you best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining the right haircut for you. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Start by measuring the length and width of your face with a tape measure. Record the measurements in inches or centimeters.

Step 2: Determine Your Jawline

Look at your jawline and determine its shape. Is it round, square, or pointed?

Step 3: Determine Your Forehead Shape

Look at your forehead and determine its shape. Is it wide or narrow?

Step 4: Determine Your Cheekbones Shape

Look at your cheekbones and determine their shape. Are they high or low?

Step 5: Determine Your Face Shape

Use the following guide to determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Your face length is greater than your face width, and your jawline is slightly rounded.
  • Round: Your face length and width are almost equal, and your jawline is rounded.
  • Square: Your face length, width, and jawline are almost equal.
  • Heart: Your face is widest at the temples and narrows at the jawline.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline.
  • Pear: Your face is widest at the jawline and narrows at the forehead.

How to Determine Your Hair Type

Knowing your hair type is crucial in determining the right haircut for you. Here are the most common hair types:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Coily

Determine your hair type by looking at the shape of your hair strands. Straight hair is cylindrical, while curly hair is more oval-shaped. Wavy hair is a mix of both.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Once you have determined your face shape and hair type, it's time to choose the right haircut. Here are a few tips:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a haircut.
  • Choose a haircut that suits your lifestyle.
  • Bring a picture of the haircut you want to show your stylist.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice.

FAQ

Q. How often should I get a haircut?

A. It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Q. Can I get a haircut that's not recommended for my face shape?

A. Yes, you can get any haircut you like. However, it may not be the most flattering for your face shape.

Q. How do I know if a certain haircut will suit me?

A. You can use virtual haircut apps or try on wigs to see how you'd look with a certain haircut.

Hair Care

Once you have your new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh your hair down.

With these tips, you're sure to find a haircut that suits you best. Remember, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable with your new look.
