Can You Make Faux Locs Permanent

Written by William Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our blog post about whether you can make faux locs permanent. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to make your faux locs permanent, as well as tips, FAQ, and hair care. If you're wondering whether you can keep your faux locs in for a longer period, then you've come to the right place.

Description

Faux locs are a protective style that can last up to four weeks, depending on how well you maintain them. However, some people prefer to keep their faux locs for a more extended period, and that's where making them permanent comes in. By making your faux locs permanent, you can keep them in for up to six months, giving you a more extended period of time to enjoy your protective style.

How To Make Faux Locs Permanent

Making your faux locs permanent involves using a perm solution to set the hair. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your faux locs permanent:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step is to prepare your hair. Make sure your hair is clean and free from any oil or product buildup. You should also ensure that your hair is completely dry before you start the process.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small parts, and then twist each part into a faux loc. Make sure you twist each part tightly to ensure that the locs are well defined.

Step 3: Apply the Perm Solution

Apply the perm solution to each faux loc, ensuring that it's evenly distributed. You can use a small brush to apply the perm solution, but make sure you don't oversaturate the hair.

Step 4: Set the Hair

Once you've applied the perm solution, cover your hair with a plastic cap and allow it to set for the recommended time. The time will depend on the strength of the perm solution you're using, so make sure you read the instructions carefully.

Step 5: Rinse Your Hair

After the recommended time has elapsed, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure you remove all the perm solution from your hair.

Step 6: Dry Your Hair

Once you've rinsed your hair, dry it with a towel or blow dryer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you remove the faux locs.

Step 7: Remove the Faux Locs

After your hair is dry, carefully remove the faux locs. You should notice that your hair has a permanent wave, which will give you a more extended period to enjoy your protective style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your faux locs permanent:

  • Use a perm solution that's suitable for your hair type and texture.
  • Don't oversaturate your hair with the perm solution.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start the process.
  • Read the instructions carefully before you start the process.
  • Keep your hair moisturized to prevent breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making faux locs permanent:

Can I make my faux locs permanent if I have relaxed hair?

Yes, you can make your faux locs permanent if you have relaxed hair. However, you should be careful not to damage your hair by using a strong perm solution.

How long does the process take?

The process can take up to three hours, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

How long will my faux locs last after I make them permanent?

Your faux locs can last up to six months after you make them permanent, depending on how well you maintain them.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to care for your hair after you make your faux locs permanent:

  • Keep your hair moisturized to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin bonnet or scarf.
  • Don't use heat on your hair too often.

Now that you know how to make your faux locs permanent, you can enjoy your protective style for a more extended period. Just make sure you follow the tips and take good care of your hair to prevent breakage and damage.
