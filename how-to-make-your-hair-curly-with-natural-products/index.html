How To Make Your Hair Curly With Natural Products

Written by Eveline Sep 02, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're tired of using heat tools to curl your hair, or if you're looking for a more natural approach to styling, there are plenty of ways to make your hair curly using natural products. In this article, we'll go over some of the best ways to naturally curl your hair, from using ingredients you probably already have in your kitchen to incorporating natural hair care products into your routine.

Description

Natural hair care has become increasingly popular in recent years, as people look for ways to avoid harsh chemicals and synthetic ingredients. If you have straight or wavy hair and want to add some curls, there are plenty of natural products that can help. From coconut oil to flaxseed gel, there are a variety of ingredients that can help enhance your natural curl pattern and add volume and texture to your hair.

How to

There are several ways to make your hair curly with natural products. Here are some of the best methods:

Method 1: Use a Curl Enhancing Product

One of the easiest ways to make your hair curly with natural products is to use a curl enhancing product. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like aloe vera, coconut oil, or shea butter, which can help define your natural curl pattern and add moisture to your hair. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation.

Method 2: Use Flaxseed Gel

Flaxseed gel is a natural hair styling product that can help define your curls and reduce frizz. To make flaxseed gel, boil 1/4 cup of flaxseeds in 2 cups of water for about 10 minutes, or until the mixture thickens. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve and let it cool. Apply the gel to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation.

Method 3: Use a Curling Cream

Curling creams are a great way to enhance your natural curl pattern and add moisture to your hair. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like shea butter, argan oil, or coconut oil. Apply the cream to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair curly with natural products:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water.

Step 2: Apply a curl enhancing product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your damp hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Use your hands to scrunch your hair and encourage curl formation.

Step 3: Dry your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. This will help enhance your natural curl pattern and reduce frizz.

Step 4: Apply flaxseed gel

If you want more defined curls, apply flaxseed gel to your damp hair instead of a curl enhancing product. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation.

Step 5: Air dry or diffuse

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Avoid touching your hair too much while it dries to prevent frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your hair curly with natural products:

- Experiment with different natural products to find what works best for your hair type - Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water from your hair after washing - Avoid brushing your hair while it's wet, as this can cause breakage and frizz - Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently - Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

FAQ

Can natural products really make your hair curly?

Yes! Natural products like flaxseed gel, shea butter, and coconut oil can help enhance your natural curl pattern and add volume and texture to your hair.

Will my hair be as curly as if I used a curling iron or wand?

It depends on your hair type and the natural products you use. While natural products can help enhance your natural curl pattern, they may not create the same tight, defined curls that a curling iron or wand can.

How often should I use natural products to make my hair curly?

It's up to you! You can use natural products every time you wash your hair, or you can use them once a week or as needed to enhance your natural curl pattern.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy, curly hair, it's important to follow a consistent hair care routine. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner - Deep condition your hair once a week - Avoid using heat tools too often - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends - Protect your hair from the sun and wind

With the right natural products and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful, curly hair without relying on heat tools or harsh chemicals. Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for your hair type and enjoy your new, natural curls!
