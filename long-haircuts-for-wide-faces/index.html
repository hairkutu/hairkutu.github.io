Long Haircuts For Wide Faces Flattering Styles For 2023

Written by Luffy Apr 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a wide or round face shape, you may feel limited when it comes to hairstyles. But fear not! Long haircuts can actually be very flattering for wide faces. In this article, we'll explore some of the best long haircuts for wide faces, as well as tips for maintaining and caring for your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Long Haircut for a Wide Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to a Long Haircut for a Wide Face
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Long Haircut

How to Choose a Long Haircut for a Wide Face

When choosing a long haircut for a wide face, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Length: Longer hair can help elongate a wide face, so consider styles that hit at or below the collarbone.
  • Layers: Layers can also help add dimension and movement to a wide face, so look for styles with face-framing layers or long layers throughout.
  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can help soften a wide forehead and draw attention to the eyes.

Some great long haircut options for wide faces include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help add movement and dimension to a wide face. Ask your stylist to add long layers throughout your hair, focusing on the ends for a soft, feathered effect.

2. Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers can help draw attention to your best features and soften the edges of a wide face. Ask your stylist for layers that start around the chin and frame your face.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out a wide forehead and draw attention to the eyes. Ask your stylist for bangs that start at the side part and sweep across your forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide to a Long Haircut for a Wide Face

Ready to try a long haircut for your wide face? Follow these steps:

  1. Find a stylist who specializes in long haircuts and has experience working with wide face shapes.
  2. Discuss your desired length, layers, and bangs with your stylist.
  3. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  4. Sit in the stylist's chair and let them work their magic!
  5. Once your haircut is complete, ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new 'do.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Haircut

Now that you have a long haircut that flatters your wide face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims: Even if you're trying to grow your hair out, regular trims (every 6-8 weeks) can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Use the right products: Invest in high-quality shampoo, conditioner, and styling products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling: Too much heat styling can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Try to air-dry your hair whenever possible, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use hot tools.

FAQs About Long Haircuts for Wide Faces

Q: Will a long haircut make my face look even wider?

A: No! In fact, a long haircut can actually help elongate a wide face and make it look more balanced.

Q: What if I don't want bangs?

A: That's totally fine! Bangs aren't a requirement for a long haircut that flatters a wide face. Just focus on adding layers and length to your hair for a flattering effect.

Q: How can I style my long haircut?

A: There are endless ways to style a long haircut! Try beachy waves, a sleek blowout, or a messy bun for a casual look. For a more formal occasion, consider an updo or half-up, half-down style.

Hair Care Tips for Long Haircuts

Caring for long hair can be a bit more challenging than caring for shorter hair, but with the right products and techniques, it's totally doable. Here are some tips:

  • Invest in a high-quality brush: A boar bristle brush can help distribute your scalp's natural oils throughout your hair, keeping it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week: Long hair can become dry and damaged over time, so it's important to give it a little extra TLC. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles: Tight ponytails, braids, and buns can put stress on your hair and scalp, leading to breakage and thinning. Stick to looser styles whenever possible.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a long haircut that flatters your wide face and keeps your locks looking luscious and healthy. Happy styling!
