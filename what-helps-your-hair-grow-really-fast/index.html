What Helps Your Hair Grow Really Fast A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi May 23, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're looking for ways to make your hair grow faster, you're not alone. Many people struggle with slow hair growth, which can be frustrating when you're trying to achieve a certain look or style. Fortunately, there are several things you can do to promote hair growth and get the results you're looking for. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about what helps your hair grow really fast.

  • How To Make Your Hair Grow Faster
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Faster Hair Growth
  • FAQ

How To Make Your Hair Grow Faster

The key to fast hair growth is taking care of your scalp and hair. This means keeping your scalp clean and healthy, avoiding damage from heat styling and chemical treatments, and nourishing your hair with the right vitamins and nutrients. Here are some tips to help you make your hair grow faster:

1. Keep Your Scalp Clean

A healthy scalp is essential for fast hair growth. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. Avoid using hot water, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

2. Avoid Heat Styling and Chemical Treatments

Heat styling and chemical treatments can cause damage to your hair, making it more prone to breakage and slower growth. Try to limit your use of hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, and avoid harsh chemical treatments like bleaching and perming.

3. Nourish Your Hair with Vitamins and Nutrients

Healthy hair starts from the inside out. Make sure to eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients like biotin, Vitamin D, and iron. You can also take supplements to help promote hair growth.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're ready to take action and start growing your hair faster, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

The first step to promoting fast hair growth is understanding your hair type. This will help you choose the right products and treatments for your hair. Determine whether your hair is straight, wavy, curly, or coily, and whether it's fine, medium, or thick.

Step 2: Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. Look for products that are free of sulfates and parabens, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Step 3: Use a Scalp Massager

A scalp massager can help improve blood flow to your scalp, which can promote hair growth. Use a massager to massage your scalp in circular motions for a few minutes each day.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling and Chemical Treatments

As we mentioned earlier, heat styling and chemical treatments can cause damage to your hair, making it more prone to breakage and slower growth. Try to limit your use of hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, and avoid harsh chemical treatments like bleaching and perming.

Step 5: Eat a Balanced Diet

A healthy diet is essential for fast hair growth. Make sure to eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients like biotin, Vitamin D, and iron. You can also take supplements to help promote hair growth.

Tips for Faster Hair Growth

Here are some additional tips to help you promote fast hair growth:

1. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help protect your hair from damage and breakage, which can promote faster growth. Look for a lightweight, nourishing formula that won't weigh your hair down.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down hair growth. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks, or as needed to keep your ends healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase can help prevent friction and breakage, which can promote faster hair growth. You can also wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet to protect it while you sleep.

FAQ

1. How long does it take for hair to grow?

On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. However, this can vary depending on factors like genetics, age, and overall health.

2. Can stress affect hair growth?

Yes, stress can affect hair growth. When you're stressed, your body produces more cortisol, which can disrupt the hair growth cycle and cause hair loss.

3. What vitamins are good for hair growth?

Some vitamins that are good for hair growth include biotin, Vitamin D, and iron. You can also take supplements like Viviscal, which is specifically designed to promote hair growth.

Hair Care for Fast Growth

To promote fast hair growth, it's important to take care of your hair and scalp on a daily basis. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy, growing hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.

Choose a gentle shampoo that's appropriate for your hair type, and wash your hair regularly to keep your scalp clean and healthy.

2. Use a nourishing conditioner.

Use a conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type to nourish and protect your hair from damage.

3. Avoid heat styling and chemical treatments.

As we mentioned earlier, heat styling and chemical treatments can cause damage to your hair, making it more prone to breakage and slower growth. Try to limit your use of hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, and avoid harsh chemical treatments like bleaching and perming.

4. Eat a balanced diet.

A healthy diet is essential for fast hair growth. Make sure to eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients like biotin, Vitamin D, and iron. You can also take supplements to help promote hair growth.

5. Protect your hair from damage.

Protect your hair from damage by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside in the sun, and avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's wet, which can cause breakage.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair and scalp, you can promote fast hair growth and achieve the look you're after. Remember, healthy hair starts from the inside out, so make sure to eat a balanced diet and take care of your overall health as well.
