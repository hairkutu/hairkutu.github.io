What Does Kim Kardashian Use To Slick Your Hair Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering how Kim Kardashian always manages to look perfect with her slicked-back hair? Well, wonder no more as we spill the beans on what she uses to achieve that sleek look. In this article, we're going to share with you the secret to Kim Kardashian's perfect slicked-back hair and provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the look yourself.

Description

Kim Kardashian's hair has always been a topic of discussion. She is known for her sleek and stylish look, and her slicked-back hair is one of her signature styles. Her hair is always perfectly styled, and it seems like there's never a strand out of place. So, what's her secret?

What Does Kim Kardashian Use to Slick Your Hair Back?

The secret to Kim Kardashian's perfect slicked-back hair is a combination of products. She uses a combination of a hair gel, a hair oil, and a hairspray. This combination of products helps to create a sleek and polished look that stays in place all day.

How to Achieve the Look: Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what Kim Kardashian uses to slick her hair back, let's dive into how you can achieve the look yourself. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

It's important to start with clean hair before you begin styling. This will help the products to work better and will make it easier to achieve the look you want.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel

Apply a small amount of hair gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply Hair Oil

Next, apply a small amount of hair oil to your hair. This will help to add shine and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair Back

Using a fine-tooth comb, brush your hair back to create the sleek look. Make sure to brush your hair in the direction that you want it to stay.

Step 5: Apply Hairspray

Finally, apply a generous amount of hairspray to your hair to hold the style in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Slicked-Back Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hair:

  • Use a pea-sized amount of hair gel to avoid making your hair look greasy
  • Use a small amount of hair oil to avoid making your hair look greasy
  • Brush your hair back in small sections to ensure that it stays in place
  • Use a strong hold hairspray to keep your hair in place all day

FAQs

Can I achieve the same look with different hair types?

Yes, you can achieve the same look with different hair types. However, you may need to adjust the amount of product that you use depending on your hair type.

Can I use different products to achieve the same look?

Yes, you can use different products to achieve the same look. However, the key is to use a combination of products that work well together to create the perfect style.

How long will the style last?

The style should last all day if you use a strong hold hairspray. However, if you have particularly fine or oily hair, you may need to touch up the style throughout the day.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looks its best. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in great condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, and you'll be able to achieve the perfect slicked-back hair look like Kim Kardashian.
