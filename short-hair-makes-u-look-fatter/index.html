Short Hair Makes U Look Fatter The Truth About This Hair Myth

Written by Aprilia May 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short hair has been a popular hairstyle for many people, but there's a misconception that it makes you look fatter. In this article, we'll explore the truth behind this hair myth and provide you with helpful tips on how to style your short hair to make you look your best. So, let's dive in!

Description

It's a common belief that short hair makes you look fatter, but this simply isn't true. There are many factors that contribute to the way we look, including our body type, clothing, and posture. While certain hairstyles may accentuate certain features or create an illusion of more or less volume, hair length alone doesn't determine our weight or appearance.

That being said, there are ways to style short hair that can flatter your face shape and help you feel confident and beautiful. With the right cut, color, and styling products, short hair can be a versatile and chic choice for anyone, regardless of their body type or weight.

How to Style Short Hair

The key to styling short hair is to find a cut that works for your face shape and hair texture. Some popular short haircuts include the pixie cut, bob, and lob (long bob). These styles can be customized to suit your individual features and preferences, so it's important to work with a stylist who understands your needs.

Once you have a cut you love, you can experiment with different styling products and techniques to create different looks. For example, adding volume to the crown of your head can create the illusion of a longer, leaner face, while sweeping your hair to one side can add asymmetry and interest to your look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Here's a basic guide to styling short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant and styling product, such as mousse or gel
  3. Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume to the crown of your head
  4. Use a flat iron or curling wand to create waves or curls
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray or texturizing spray

Tips for Making Short Hair Work for You

Here are some tips for making short hair work for your body type and style:

  • Choose a cut that flatters your face shape and features
  • Experiment with different styling products to create different looks
  • Use color to add dimension and interest to your hair
  • Try different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, or scarves
  • Pay attention to your posture and body language to exude confidence and grace

FAQs About Short Hair and Weight

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair and weight:

Does short hair really make you look fatter?

No, short hair alone doesn't make you look fatter. There are many factors that contribute to our appearance, including our body type, clothing, and posture.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I want to look slimmer?

There are no hard and fast rules about what hairstyles to avoid, but some people find that certain styles, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs, can accentuate roundness in the face. However, this depends on your individual features and preferences.

Can short hair be slimming?

Short hair can be slimming if it's styled in a way that flatters your face shape and features. Adding volume to the crown of your head or sweeping your hair to one side can create a longer, leaner look.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut

In conclusion, short hair doesn't make you look fatter. It's a common myth that can be debunked with the right styling techniques and confidence. With the right cut, color, and care, short hair can be a chic and versatile option for anyone, regardless of their body type or weight. So go ahead and rock that pixie cut or bob, and feel confident and beautiful!
