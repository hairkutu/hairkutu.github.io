What Kind Of Hairstyles Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by William May 07, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, you may be wondering what kind of hairstyles can make your face look slimmer. The good news is that there are many hairstyles that can create the illusion of a slimmer face. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape, as well as provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles for a Slimmer Face
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for a Slimmer Face

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a hairstyle that will make your face look slimmer is to determine your face shape. There are four main face shapes: round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the descriptions below:

  • Round: Your face is roughly the same width and length, with full cheeks and a rounded chin.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and a slightly narrow chin.
  • Square: Your face is roughly the same width and length, with a strong, angular jawline.
  • Heart-shaped: Your face is wider at the forehead and temples, with a pointed chin.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can begin to choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Hairstyles for a Slimmer Face

There are many hairstyles that can make your face look slimmer, regardless of your face shape. Some popular options include:

  • Long Layers: Long, face-framing layers can create the illusion of a slimmer face, especially when they are styled with a deep side part.
  • Side Swept Bangs: Side swept bangs can help to elongate the face and draw attention away from round cheeks.
  • Bob: A chin-length bob can be a great option for a round face, as it can create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.
  • Pixie Cut: A short, choppy pixie cut can help to balance out a round face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.
  • Updo: An updo can create the illusion of a longer face, especially when it is styled high on the head.

When choosing a hairstyle, it is important to consider not just your face shape, but also your hair texture and personal style. A skilled hairstylist can help you choose a haircut that flatters your features and complements your overall look.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you have chosen a hairstyle, there are several styling tips that can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face:

  • Use a Deep Side Part: A deep side part can help to create the illusion of a longer face, especially when paired with face-framing layers.
  • Avoid Center Parts: Center parts can make a round face appear even rounder, so it is best to avoid them.
  • Add Volume at the Crown: Adding volume at the crown of your head can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.
  • Avoid Volume at the Sides: Adding volume at the sides of your head can make a round face appear wider, so it is best to avoid this.
  • Consider Highlights: Adding highlights to your hair can create depth and dimension, making your face appear slimmer.

FAQs

Q. Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

A. Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail. Just make sure to keep it high on your head, as this can help to elongate your face.

Q. What if I have curly hair?

A. Curly hair can be a bit more difficult to style, but the same principles apply. Look for hairstyles that add volume at the crown and avoid volume at the sides.

Q. Do I need to get a haircut to make my face look slimmer?

A. Not necessarily. You can create the illusion of a slimmer face through styling techniques like a deep side part or face-framing layers.

Hair Care for a Slimmer Face

Finally, it is important to take care of your hair in order to keep it looking healthy and radiant. Some tips for hair care include:

  • Wash Your Hair Regularly: Washing your hair regularly can help to remove dirt and oil, keeping your hair looking fresh and clean.
  • Use a Quality Shampoo: Using a high-quality shampoo can help to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid Heat Styling: Heat styling can damage your hair, so it is best to avoid it whenever possible.
  • Use a Heat Protectant: If you do need to use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.
  • Get Regular Haircuts: Getting regular haircuts can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face and feel confident and beautiful.
