As we age, our face shape changes and we may need to reconsider our hairstyles to flatter our current features. For square face shape, the key is to soften the angles and add some height and volume to elongate the face. In this article, we will share some hairstyle ideas, tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice for square face older women.

Hairstyle Ideas for Square Face Older Women

There are many hairstyle options that can flatter square face older women, here are some ideas:

Soft Waves

Soft waves can soften the angles of a square face and add some volume to the crown. You can achieve this by using a curling iron or hot rollers, or opt for a perm if you want a more long-lasting result.

Layered Bob

A layered bob can also work well for square face older women as it creates movement and texture around the face. You can choose a chin-length or shoulder-length bob depending on your preference.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to balance out a square face by adding some asymmetry. You can choose to have long or short bangs depending on your hair length and face shape.

Updo

An updo can be a great option for special occasions as it can elongate the face and create a more formal look. You can opt for a low bun, a top knot, or a French twist depending on your preference.

How to Style Hair for Square Face Older Women

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair for square face older women:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant if you plan on using heat tools.
  3. If you have short hair, apply a styling product such as mousse or gel and blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add some volume.
  4. If you have long hair, you can choose to air-dry your hair or blow-dry it with a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.
  5. Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create soft waves or curls.
  6. If you prefer a sleek look, you can use a straightening iron to smooth out your hair and add shine.
  7. Finish off with a hairspray or hair oil to set your hairstyle and add some extra shine.

Tips and Tricks for Square Face Older Women

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for square face older women:

  • Avoid blunt cuts or straight bangs as they can emphasize the angles of the face.
  • Opt for layers, waves, or curls to add some movement and texture.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your hair type and lifestyle.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ About Hairstyle for Square Face Older Woman

What is a square face shape?

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features.

What hairstyles should square face older women avoid?

Square face older women should avoid blunt cuts, straight bangs, and hairstyles that emphasize the angles of the face.

What hairstyles are best for square face older women?

Soft waves, layered bobs, side-swept bangs, and updos are some of the best hairstyles for square face older women.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Older Women

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often and always use a heat protectant.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyle options that can flatter square face older women, and it's important to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Soft waves, layered bobs, side-swept bangs, and updos are some of the best hairstyles for square face older women, and you can follow our step-by-step guide and tips to style your hair at home. Don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant!
