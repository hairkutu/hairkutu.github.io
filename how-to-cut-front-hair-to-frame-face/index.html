How To Cut Front Hair To Frame Face A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your boring hairstyle and want to add some dimension to your look? Cutting your front hair to frame your face is a great way to achieve a new and fresh style. In this article, we will show you how to cut front hair to frame face in a relaxed English language. We will cover everything from why you should go for this style, how to cut it, and hair care tips to maintain your new look. So, let's get started!

Why Cut Front Hair to Frame Face?

Adding layers to your front hair can add a lot of dimension to your face and hair. It can make your hair look voluminous and give the illusion of a fuller head of hair. Moreover, it can highlight your best features and make your face look more symmetrical. This style is perfect for those who want to add a new and fresh touch to their overall look.

How to Cut Front Hair to Frame Face

Cutting your front hair to frame your face isn't rocket science. However, it's important to do it right to avoid any disasters. Here's how you can cut your front hair to frame your face:

Step by Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to cut your front hair to frame your face:

  1. Wash and dry your hair completely to make sure it's tangle-free.
  2. Part your hair down the middle or on one side, depending on your preference.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your face, and comb it straight down.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length. It's better to start with a longer length and cut more if needed.
  5. Repeat the same process for the other side of your face.
  6. Blend the front layers with the rest of your hair to give a natural look.

It's always a good idea to seek professional help if you're not confident in cutting your hair yourself.

Tips for Cutting Front Hair to Frame Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting your front hair to frame your face:

  • Invest in good quality scissors to avoid any damage to your hair.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more as needed.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as the length may appear shorter when it dries.
  • Make sure your hair is tangle-free before cutting it.
  • Take small sections of hair to avoid any mistakes.
  • Blend the front layers with the rest of your hair to avoid a choppy look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting front hair to frame the face:

1. How often should I cut my front hair to maintain the style?

You should trim your front hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and avoid split ends.

2. Can I cut my front hair myself?

Yes, you can cut your front hair yourself, but it's always better to seek professional help if you're not confident enough.

3. Will cutting my front hair damage my hair?

No, cutting your front hair to frame your face won't damage your hair if you use good quality scissors and cut it properly.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to maintain your new look:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and smoothness to your hair.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly as it can cause breakage.

Cutting your front hair to frame your face is a great way to add a new and fresh dimension to your look. Follow the steps and tips mentioned above to achieve the perfect look. Don't forget to maintain your hair and take good care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. We hope this article has helped you in cutting your front hair to frame your face.
