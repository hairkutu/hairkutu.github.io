How To Shave Side Of Head Male Step By Step Guide In 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Shaving the side of your head can give you a bold and edgy look. It’s a popular style among men who want to stand out and express their individuality. However, shaving the side of your head requires the right tools, techniques, and care to achieve the desired results. In this blog post, we’ll show you how to shave side of head male in relaxed English language.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Shave Side of Head Male
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Shaving Side of Head Male
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care After Shaving Side of Head Male

Description

Shaving the side of your head involves removing hair from the temple area to the ear, creating a shaved or buzzed look. This style can be achieved with a razor, clippers, or trimmers. It’s important to have a clear idea of the length and style you want before you start shaving.

Shaving the side of your head can be a bold and edgy look that requires maintenance to keep it looking sharp. It’s important to take good care of your hair and scalp after shaving to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs.

How to Shave Side of Head Male

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to shave side of head male:

  1. Wash your hair and scalp with warm water and shampoo. This will help soften your hair and make it easier to shave.
  2. Decide on the length and style you want for your shaved side. Use a clipper or trimmer to remove the bulk of the hair in the temple area.
  3. Use a razor to shave the remaining hair in the temple area. Use short, gentle strokes and shave in the direction your hair grows.
  4. Use a hot towel or warm water to rinse your scalp and remove any remaining hair.
  5. Apply a moisturizing lotion or oil to soothe your scalp and prevent irritation.

Tips for Shaving Side of Head Male

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when shaving the side of your head:

  • Invest in a high-quality razor or clipper to ensure a smooth and even shave.
  • Use shaving cream or gel to lubricate your scalp and prevent razor burn.
  • Shave in a well-lit area to avoid missing any spots.
  • Take your time and use short, gentle strokes to avoid cuts and nicks.
  • Ask for help from a friend or professional if you’re unsure about how to shave the side of your head.

FAQs

How often should I shave the side of my head?

The frequency of shaving depends on the rate of hair growth and the style you want to maintain. Some men prefer to shave their side every few days, while others do it once a week or less.

Will shaving the side of my head cause hair loss?

No, shaving the side of your head won’t cause hair loss. However, it’s important to take good care of your hair and scalp after shaving to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs.

Hair Care After Shaving Side of Head Male

After shaving the side of your head, it’s important to take good care of your hair and scalp to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp with a hair oil or lotion to prevent dryness and flakiness.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair for at least a week after shaving.
  • Wear a hat or sunscreen when outside to protect your scalp from the sun.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair and scalp clean and healthy.

Shaving the side of your head can give you a bold and edgy look that requires maintenance to keep it looking sharp. By following these tips and using the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the style you want and take good care of your hair and scalp.
