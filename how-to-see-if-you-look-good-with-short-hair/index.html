How To See If You Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering chopping off your locks and going for a short hairstyle, you may be wondering if it will suit you. It's important to know how to determine if you'll look good with short hair before making such a drastic change. In this article, we'll go over several ways to assess if a short hairstyle is right for you.

Table of Contents

  • Check Yourself Out in the Mirror
  • Consider Your Face Shape
  • Think About Your Hair Texture
  • Try on Short Wigs
  • Trust Your Confidence

Check Yourself Out in the Mirror

The easiest way to see if you'll look good with short hair is to simply look at yourself in the mirror. Try pulling your hair back and pinning it up to get an idea of what you'd look like without your usual long locks. If you have a friend who is good at styling hair, consider asking them to create a faux short hairstyle for you to try out. This can give you a good idea of how you'll look with a shorter cut.

Consider Your Face Shape

Another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair will suit you is your face shape. Certain face shapes, such as oval or heart-shaped, tend to look good with short hair, while others, such as round or square, may not be as flattering. Do some research on which hairstyles tend to look good with your specific face shape and use that as a guide.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in how well a short hairstyle will suit you. If you have fine hair, a shorter hairstyle can add volume and make your hair look fuller. However, if you have thick, curly hair, a shorter cut may be more difficult to maintain and style. Consider your hair texture before making the decision to chop it all off.

Try on Short Wigs

If you're still not sure if you'll look good with short hair, consider trying on some short wigs. This can give you a realistic idea of how you'll look with a shorter cut without committing to the change. Make sure to choose a wig that is similar in color and texture to your natural hair for the most accurate representation.

Trust Your Confidence

Ultimately, the best way to determine if you'll look good with short hair is to trust your confidence. If you feel good and confident with a shorter cut, that's all that really matters. Remember that hair grows back and you can always experiment with different styles and lengths until you find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

If you do decide to go for a shorter hairstyle, there are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Short hair requires more frequent trims to maintain its shape and style.
  • Invest in quality styling products to help keep your hair in place.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Consider getting a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

FAQ

What face shapes look good with short hair?

Oval and heart-shaped faces tend to look good with short hair. Round and square faces may not be as flattering with a shorter cut.

How can I tell if a short hairstyle will suit me?

You can tell if a short hairstyle will suit you by looking at yourself in the mirror, considering your face shape and hair texture, trying on short wigs, and trusting your confidence.

What hair care tips should I follow if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, make sure to get frequent trims, invest in quality styling products, use gentle shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent damage to your hair.
