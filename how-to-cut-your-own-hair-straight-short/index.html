How To Cut Your Own Hair Straight Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read
Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts every few weeks? Do you want to save some money and try cutting your hair at home? If yes, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut your own hair straight short in the most relaxed way possible.

Description

Cutting your hair at home can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, you can achieve professional-looking results. Whether you want to trim your hair or go for a complete haircut, we will provide you with all the information you need to get started.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Straight Short

The first step in cutting your hair straight short is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, a mirror, and some hair clips. Once you have all these tools, follow the steps below:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly and dry it completely before you start cutting. Wet hair is harder to cut, and you may end up cutting more than you intended.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to separate your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair in the middle and then dividing it into two sections. Pin one section up and start working on the other section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start by trimming the ends of your hair. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut off the split ends. Once you have trimmed the ends, start cutting the length of your hair. Use the comb to make sure you are cutting straight.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

After you have cut the back of your hair, move on to the sides. Use the same technique as before and make sure you are cutting straight.

Step 5: Check the Length

Once you have finished cutting your hair, it's time to check the length. Use your fingers to feel around your hair and make sure it's even.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Now that you have cut your hair, it's time to style it. Blow-dry your hair and use some hair products to style it the way you like.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair:
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's been cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Make sure to cut your hair in a well-lit area.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and cut small sections at a time.

FAQ

1. Is it easy to cut your own hair straight short?

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, but with the right guidance, you can do it easily.

2. Can I use any type of scissors to cut my hair?

No, you should use scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. Using regular scissors can damage your hair.

3. How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair frequently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

In conclusion, cutting your own hair straight short can be an easy and cost-effective way to maintain your hair. With the right guidance, you can achieve professional-looking results from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the steps outlined in this article. Happy cutting!
