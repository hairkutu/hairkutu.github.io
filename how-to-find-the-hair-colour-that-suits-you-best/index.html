How To Find The Hair Colour That Suits You Best

Written by Robby Aug 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking to change up your hair colour but aren't sure which shade will suit you best? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to figure out what will look good on you. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the hair colour that suits you best, from determining your skin tone to choosing the right shade. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Choosing the Right Shade
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the right hair colour is to determine your skin tone. This will help you choose a shade that complements your complexion and enhances your natural beauty. Here are some tips to help you figure out your skin tone:

Warm Skin Tone

If you have a warm skin tone, your complexion will have yellow, peachy, or golden undertones. You may also have green veins on your wrist. The best hair colours for warm skin tones are golden blonde, honey blonde, copper, auburn, and warm brown shades.

Cool Skin Tone

If you have a cool skin tone, your complexion will have pink, red, or bluish undertones. You may also have blue veins on your wrist. The best hair colours for cool skin tones are ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown shades, and black.

Neutral Skin Tone

If you have a neutral skin tone, your complexion will have a mix of warm and cool undertones. You may also have both blue and green veins on your wrist. Lucky for you, you can pull off a wide range of hair colours! Try warm or cool shades to see what works best for you.

Choosing the Right Shade

Once you have determined your skin tone, it's time to choose the right shade for you. Here are some tips to help you pick the perfect hair colour:

Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

If you want a natural-looking result, it's best to choose a shade that is close to your natural hair colour. This will also make it easier to maintain your hair, as you won't have to touch up your roots as often.

Think About Your Lifestyle

It's important to choose a hair colour that fits your lifestyle. If you lead an active lifestyle, you may want to choose a low-maintenance shade that doesn't require frequent touch-ups. If you work in a conservative environment, you may want to choose a more natural-looking shade.

Consult with Your Stylist

Your hairstylist is an expert in hair colour and can help you choose a shade that will look great on you. They can also recommend hair care products to help maintain your colour and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

Use Colour-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

To keep your hair colour from fading, it's important to use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for colour-treated hair. Look for products that are free from sulfates and parabens.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade faster. Try to limit your use of these tools or use a heat protectant spray before styling.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can cause your hair colour to fade. Wear a hat or use a hair product with UV protection when you're spending time outdoors.

FAQs

What if I'm not sure which shade to choose?

If you're not sure which shade will look best on you, consult with your hairstylist. They can help you choose a shade that complements your skin tone and fits your lifestyle.

How often should I touch up my roots?

This will depend on the shade you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Your hairstylist can give you a recommendation for touch-ups based on your hair type and colour.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's best to leave it to the professionals. A hairstylist can ensure that your hair is evenly coloured and that the shade looks great on you.

Now that you know how to find the hair colour that suits you best, it's time to make an appointment with your hairstylist and get started! Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
