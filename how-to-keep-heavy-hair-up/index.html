How To Keep Heavy Hair Up Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 05, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you have thick, heavy hair that always seems to fall flat? Keeping it up can be a challenge, but it’s not impossible. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for keeping your heavy hair up and out of your face.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Heavy Hair Up
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Heavy Hair Up
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Heavy Hair

How to Keep Heavy Hair Up

The key to keeping heavy hair up is finding the right hairstyle and products that work for you. Here are some popular options:

Braids

Braids are a great option for heavy hair because they distribute the weight evenly and keep your hair out of your face. Try a French braid, Dutch braid, or fishtail braid.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that work well for heavy hair. Try a high ponytail, low ponytail, or side ponytail to switch up your look.

Buns

Buns are another great option for heavy hair. Try a messy bun, top knot, or braided bun for a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how to keep heavy hair up in a few simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots to add lift.
  3. Choose your hairstyle (braid, ponytail, or bun).
  4. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  5. If you’re doing a braid, start at the top of your head and work your way down, securing with a hair tie at the end.
  6. If you’re doing a ponytail, you can leave it as is or wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to conceal it.
  7. If you’re doing a bun, twist your hair into a bun shape and secure with bobby pins.
  8. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Keeping Heavy Hair Up

Here are some additional tips for keeping heavy hair up:

  • Use hair accessories like bobby pins, hair ties, and clips to keep your style in place.
  • Invest in a good quality hair tie that won’t break or stretch out easily.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down.
  • Consider getting layers cut into your hair to reduce the weight.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Can I keep heavy hair up without using hair ties?

Yes, you can use hair clips or bobby pins to keep your hair in place instead of a hair tie.

How often should I wash my heavy hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Generally, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Will braids damage my heavy hair?

No, as long as you’re gentle when braiding and take care when taking the braids out, they shouldn’t damage your hair.

Hair Care for Heavy Hair

Keeping heavy hair up is just one part of hair care. Here are some additional tips for taking care of your heavy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for thick or heavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Brush your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling and breaking your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to keep your heavy hair up and looking great all day long.
