What Hair Color Fits Me Best Quiz Tips And Tricks

Written by William May 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your hair color and want to switch it up? Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure which one will suit you best. Fortunately, there's a hair color quiz that can help you determine the perfect shade for your skin tone, eye color, and hair type. In this article, we'll show you how to take the "what hair color fits me best quiz" and provide you with some tips and tricks for maintaining your new hair color.

How to Take the Hair Color Quiz

Before taking the quiz, it's important to know that the results are just a guide. The final decision on your hair color should always be made with the help of a professional stylist. However, the quiz can give you a good starting point and help you narrow down your options.

To take the quiz, simply search for "what hair color fits me best quiz" on your preferred search engine. You'll find several quizzes available, so choose one that seems the most comprehensive. Answer the questions honestly, and be sure to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Once you've completed the quiz, you'll receive a list of recommended hair colors that will complement your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color

Now that you've taken the quiz and have a general idea of what hair color will suit you best, it's time to choose the perfect shade. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a crucial role in choosing the right hair color. If you have warm undertones, choose warm shades like honey blonde, caramel, or copper. If you have cool undertones, go for cool shades like ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy.

Step 2: Look at Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help you determine the best hair color for you. If you have blue or green eyes, try blonde or light brown shades. If you have brown eyes, opt for warmer shades like caramel or chestnut.

Step 3: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also guide you in choosing the right shade. If you have dark hair, consider going a few shades lighter. If you have light hair, try darker shades like chocolate brown or auburn.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional Stylist

Once you've narrowed down your options, it's time to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose the perfect shade based on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. They can also give you tips on how to maintain your new hair color.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Now that you have your new hair color, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Regular shampoos and conditioners can strip your hair of its color. Use color-safe products to keep your new hair color vibrant.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and fade your new hair color. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can also fade your new hair color. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun's rays.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting with a professional stylist. They can ensure that the color is applied correctly and that your hair remains healthy.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: This depends on the rate of your hair growth and the color you've chosen. Consult with your stylist to determine how often you need to touch up your roots.

Hair Care

Don't forget to take care of your hair after coloring it. Use a hair mask once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy. Avoid using harsh chemicals and hair products that can damage your hair. With proper care, your new hair color will stay vibrant and beautiful for weeks to come!

Now that you know how to take the "what hair color fits me best quiz," it's time to start exploring your options. Remember to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color when choosing a new shade. Consult with a professional stylist to ensure that your new hair color is applied correctly and that your hair remains healthy. And don't forget to take care of your hair after coloring it with regular hair masks and gentle hair products. With these tips and tricks, you'll have a gorgeous new hair color that complements your features perfectly.
