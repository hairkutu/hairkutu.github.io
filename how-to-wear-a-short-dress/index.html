How To Wear A Short Dress Tips Tricks And Style Advice

Written by Bardi Apr 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you feel intimidated by the idea of wearing a short dress? Do you worry that you won't be able to pull it off or that it's not appropriate for your body type? Fear not! In this article, we'll give you all the tips and tricks you need to confidently wear a short dress and look fabulous doing it.

How to Wear a Short Dress

Before we dive into the specifics, let's go over the basics of how to wear a short dress:

  • Choose a dress that flatters your body type
  • Accessorize appropriately
  • Wear the right shoes
  • Confidence is key!

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear a short dress like a pro:

  1. Choose a dress that flatters your body type. If you're petite, opt for a dress with a higher hemline to elongate your legs. If you're curvy, choose a dress that cinches at the waist to accentuate your curves.
  2. Accessorize appropriately. For a casual look, pair your dress with sandals and a denim jacket. For a dressier occasion, add heels and statement jewelry.
  3. Wear the right shoes. If you're wearing a flirty sundress, sandals or sneakers are a great choice. For a more formal look, wear heels or dressy flats.
  4. Confidence is key! Wear your dress with confidence and you'll look and feel amazing.

Tips for Wearing a Short Dress

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing a short dress:

  • Balance out a short hemline with a higher neckline or longer sleeves
  • Consider wearing tights or leggings underneath for added coverage
  • Choose the right undergarments to avoid any wardrobe malfunctions
  • Experiment with different styles and silhouettes to find what works best for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a short dress:

Is it appropriate to wear a short dress to a formal event?

It depends on the dress code of the event. If it's a black tie affair, a short dress may not be appropriate. However, if the dress code is cocktail or semi-formal, a short dress can be a great option.

What shoes should I wear with a short dress?

The shoes you wear with a short dress will depend on the occasion and the style of the dress. For a casual look, sandals or sneakers are a great choice. For a dressier occasion, wear heels or dressy flats.

How should I style my hair with a short dress?

Your hairstyle will depend on the neckline of the dress and the occasion. For a dress with a high neckline, a sleek updo can be a chic option. For a dress with a lower neckline, wearing your hair down in loose waves can be a great choice.

Hair Care

When wearing a short dress, it's important to make sure your hair is styled appropriately. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

Now that you know how to wear a short dress, go forth and rock that mini! Remember, confidence is key, so wear your dress with pride and let your inner fashionista shine.
