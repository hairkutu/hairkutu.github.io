Men s Long Tied Back Hairstyles The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair has been a symbol of masculinity for centuries, and men's long tied back hairstyles are no exception. Whether you're a rocker or a hippie, a businessman or a creative, there is a long tied back hairstyle that will suit your style and personality. This guide will take you through everything you need to know about men's long tied back hairstyles.

Description

A men's long tied back hairstyle is simply long hair that is pulled back and tied at the nape of the neck. This style can be achieved with any hair type, whether straight, wavy, or curly. The tied back hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their long hair out of their face while still showing off their luscious locks. It is a versatile style that can be worn in a variety of settings, from the office to a night out on the town.

How to

To achieve the men's long tied back hairstyle, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition

Begin by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and strong, which is essential for achieving a long tied back hairstyle.

Step 2: Comb and Brush

Next, comb and brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to tie your hair back and keep it in place.

Step 3: Tie Back

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it back using a hair tie or elastic band. Make sure the hair is tight and secure, but not so tight that it feels uncomfortable.

Step 4: Style

Once your hair is tied back, you can style it in a variety of ways. You can leave it straight and sleek, or add some texture and volume with a hair dryer and styling products.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect men's long tied back hairstyle:

  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and strong
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits your personality and lifestyle
  • Don't tie your hair back too tightly, as this can damage your hair and cause headaches
  • Use a hair tie or elastic band that matches your hair color to create a seamless look

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's long tied back hairstyles:

Q: Can I still wear a long tied back hairstyle if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear a long tied back hairstyle if you have thinning hair. Just make sure to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and add some texture and volume with styling products.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a long tied back hairstyle?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out regularly, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair or don't sweat much, you may be able to wash your hair every other day or even less frequently.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for achieving and maintaining a men's long tied back hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, which are essential for healthy hair

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect men's long tied back hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and strong for years to come.
