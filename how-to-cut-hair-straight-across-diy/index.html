How To Cut Hair Straight Across Diy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but if you’re up for it, then there’s no better way to save time and money. One of the most popular techniques for cutting your own hair is the straight across method. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps to achieve a perfect straight across cut.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure to gather all the necessary equipment. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair ties.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure to comb your hair thoroughly, so that there are no knots or tangles.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections, one at the front and one at the back. Use hair ties to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section. Take the hair tie off and comb your hair down so that it’s straight. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it straight across.

Step 4: Cut the Front

Move onto the front section. Take the hair tie off and comb your hair down so that it’s straight. Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it straight across, making sure to match the length of the back section.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. If there are any uneven spots, trim them carefully.

Tips

Here are some tips to make sure that you achieve the perfect straight across cut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Cut your hair when it’s dry to avoid any surprises when it dries.
  • Take your time and don’t rush the process.
  • If your hair is thick, consider dividing it into more sections to ensure an even cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair straight across:

Is it easy to cut hair straight across?

Cutting hair straight across is relatively easy, but it does require some practice. Start by cutting a small amount of hair, and gradually work your way up to the desired length.

Can I use any scissors to cut my hair?

No, it’s important to use sharp scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Using dull scissors can cause split ends and damage your hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Cutting your hair straight across DIY can be a great way to save time and money, but it does require some practice. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve the perfect cut every time.
