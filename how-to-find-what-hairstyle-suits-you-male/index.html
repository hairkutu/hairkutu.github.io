How To Find What Hairstyle Suits You Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your old hairstyle and looking for a change? Or are you someone who has always struggled to find the right hairstyle that suits your face shape and features? Whatever the case may be, we have got you covered! In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect hairstyle that suits you, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type
  • Step 3: Look for Inspiration
  • Step 4: Consult a Barber or Hairstylist
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for you is to determine your face shape. There are a few common face shapes such as oval, round, square, and heart-shaped. The shape of your face will play a crucial role in determining which hairstyle will suit you the most. For example, if you have a round face, you should avoid styles that add volume on the sides and instead opt for a style that adds height on the top.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step is to consider your hair type. Is your hair straight, wavy, curly, or kinky? Different hair types require different cuts and styles. For instance, if you have curly hair, you can consider a short haircut that keeps the curls in check, while someone with straight hair can try a longer hairstyle.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Now that you have determined your face shape and hair type, it's time to look for inspiration. Browse through magazines, social media, and hairstyle websites to find styles that catch your eye. Save pictures of the hairstyles that you like, and take note of what you like about each style.

Step 4: Consult a Barber or Hairstylist

The final step is to consult a barber or hairstylist. They have professional expertise and can suggest styles that will suit you the best. Show them the pictures you have saved and discuss your preferences. They will take into account your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle before suggesting a style that will suit you.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when finding the right hairstyle for you:

  • Choose a style that complements your features and enhances your best attributes.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on styling your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new, but also keep in mind that not all styles will suit everyone.
  • Be realistic about the maintenance required for the style you choose.

FAQs

How do I know what hair type I have?

You can determine your hair type by looking at the thickness and texture of your hair. Straight hair is usually thin and has no curl, while curly hair is thicker and has a defined curl pattern. Wavy hair falls somewhere in between straight and curly hair.

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height on the top and keeps the sides and bottom relatively short. This will create the illusion of a longer face and balance out the roundness.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on the style you choose and how fast your hair grows. As a general rule, getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks will help maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style and keep it looking healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your style.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and steps, you should be able to find the perfect hairstyle that suits you. Remember, it's not just about following the latest trends, but finding a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Happy hairstyling!
