How To Style A Lob Haircut Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Apr 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain, a lob haircut might just be the perfect choice for you. A lob, or long bob, is a trendy and chic haircut that falls just above the shoulders. It's a great option if you want to switch up your look without going too short.

How to Style a Lob Haircut

Styling a lob haircut is easy and fun. There are many ways to style a lob, from sleek and straight to tousled and messy. Here are some popular ways to style a lob:

  • Straight and Sleek
  • Beachy Waves
  • Messy and Textured
  • Half-Up, Half-Down
  • Braided Lob

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style a lob:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner if you want to add some volume to your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smoothness.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair if you want a sleek and straight look.
  5. If you want beachy waves, use a curling iron or a wand to curl your hair. Alternate the direction of the curls to create a natural-looking wave.
  6. If you want a messy and textured look, use a texturizing spray or a dry shampoo to add some texture to your hair. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create a tousled look.
  7. If you want a half-up, half-down look, take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it into a small ponytail. Leave the rest of your hair down.
  8. If you want a braided lob, braid a small section of hair on one side of your head and pin it back with bobby pins.
  9. Finish off your look with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your lob:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some volume to your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some texture to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and looks.
  • Accessorize your lob with hair clips, headbands, or scarves for a fun and trendy look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about lob haircuts:

Can I style my lob with bangs?

Yes, you can definitely style your lob with bangs. Ask your hairstylist to give you some side-swept bangs or some blunt bangs to add some personality to your lob.

How often should I get a trim for my lob?

It's recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and length of your lob.

Can I color my lob?

Yes, you can definitely color your lob. Talk to your hairstylist about the best hair color options for your skin tone and hair type.

Hair Care for Lobs

Here are some hair care tips for lobs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair to prevent buildup and greasiness.

Now that you know how to style a lob, it's time to try out some new looks and have fun with your hair. Whether you prefer a sleek and straight look or a messy and textured look, there's a lob hairstyle for everyone.
