Does Curly Hair Make Your Face Look Rounder

Written by Oliver Aug 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair is beautiful, but many people wonder if it can make their face look rounder. If you have curly hair, you may have heard someone say that your hair makes your face look fuller or rounder. But, is it true? In this article, we will explore this question and give you some tips on how to style your curly hair to flatter your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that curly hair can make your face look rounder. This is because curly hair tends to add volume to your hair, which can create the illusion of a fuller face. However, this is not always the case. The way you style your hair can greatly affect how round or angular your face looks.

How to

If you have curly hair and you want to avoid making your face look rounder, there are a few things you can do. First, avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your head. This can make your face look wider and rounder. Instead, aim for hairstyles that add height to your hair, such as a high ponytail or top knot.

Top Knot

To create a top knot, gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins. This will create a sleek and stylish look that elongates your face.

High Ponytail

A high ponytail is another great hairstyle for curly hair that can elongate your face. To create this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic. Then, use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head to add height.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to create a top knot:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the ponytail.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Here are the steps to create a high ponytail:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic.
  2. Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head to add height.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your curly hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your head.
  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to enhance your natural curls.
  • Consider getting layers to add movement and dimension to your hair.
  • Use a lightweight hair oil to add shine and control frizz.

FAQ

Does curly hair make your face look rounder?

Curly hair can make your face look rounder if you style it in a way that adds too much volume to the sides of your head. However, if you style your hair in a way that adds height to your hair, such as a high ponytail or top knot, you can elongate your face.

How do you style curly hair to flatter your face shape?

To flatter your face shape with curly hair, avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your head. Instead, aim for hairstyles that add height to your hair, such as a high ponytail or top knot. You can also consider getting layers to add movement and dimension to your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to style your curly hair to flatter your face shape, you can rock your curls with confidence! Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
