Which Cutting Suits On Oval Face

Description

Having an oval face shape is considered as the most versatile one in terms of haircuts. Almost any hairstyle can suit this face shape, but it is still important to choose the right one that will flatter your features. In this article, we will discuss which cutting suits on oval face and provide you with tips and tricks to maintain your hairstyle.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. Oval face shape is identified by having a forehead that is slightly wider than the jawline, high cheekbones, and a rounded chin. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a photo of your face. Then draw lines around your face using an editing software or a pen and paper. Once you have identified your face shape, you can start choosing the right haircut that will suit you.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some tips for choosing the right haircut for oval face shape:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture and Length

It is important to choose a haircut that will work well with your hair texture and length. If you have fine hair, go for a layered haircut to add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a long bob or a blunt cut can work well. If you have curly hair, consider a layered cut that will enhance your curls.

2. Choose a Haircut that Highlights Your Best Features

Consider your best features and choose a haircut that will highlight them. If you have high cheekbones, consider a short haircut that will draw attention to your face. If you have a long neck, a bob or a pixie cut can work well.

3. Go for Soft Layers

Soft layers can add dimension and movement to your hair. They can also help frame your face and enhance your features. Avoid going for too many layers as they can make your face look longer.

4. Avoid Bangs that are Too Heavy

While bangs can work well with oval face shape, avoid going for bangs that are too heavy. Opt for soft, wispy bangs or side-swept bangs that will frame your face without overwhelming it.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you have chosen the right haircut, it is important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

1. Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, avoid heavy products that can weigh it down. If you have curly hair, use products that will enhance your curls.

2. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut. This will also prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair first.

4. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles. Try different updos, braids, and ponytails to change up your look.

FAQs

Q: Are there any haircuts that I should avoid if I have an oval face shape?

A: Avoid haircuts that are too short as they can make your face look longer. Avoid going for too many layers as they can also elongate your face.

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have an oval face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs if you have an oval face shape. Opt for soft, wispy bangs or side-swept bangs that will frame your face without overwhelming it.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shape

Here are some additional hair care tips for oval face shape:

1. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. This will also prevent split ends and breakage.

3. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy and shiny. Include foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals such as salmon, nuts, and leafy greens.

Choosing the right haircut for oval face shape can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find the perfect one that will flatter your features. Remember to maintain your hairstyle properly and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
